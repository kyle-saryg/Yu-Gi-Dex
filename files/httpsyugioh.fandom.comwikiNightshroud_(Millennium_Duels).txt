 Nightshroud , known as  Darkness  (ダークネス,  Dākunesu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Nightshroud , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
