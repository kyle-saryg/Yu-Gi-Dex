 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Roman Goodwin , known as  Rutger Godwin  (ルドガー・ゴドウィン ,  Rudogā Godowin ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Roman Goodwin , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
