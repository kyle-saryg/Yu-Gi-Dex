 Duelist Pack: Jesse Anderson  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the seventh set in the Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Jaden Yuki 3 . It is the last Duelist Pack for the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime series.
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Duelist Pack: Jesse Anderson  was first released on April 25, 2008 as  Duelist Pack: Jesse Anderson Special Edition , before being released in individual packs on May 17, 2008.

 Features 
 The set features many cards used by  Jesse Anderson  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , predominantly consisting of " Crystal Beast " monsters and support cards for the archetype.
 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Jesse Anderson  contains 5 cards and each box contains 15 packs. There are 30 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 2  Ultra Rares 
 4  Super Rares 
 7  Rares 
 17  Commons 
 In the  TCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Jesse Anderson  contains 6 cards and each box contains 30 packs. There are 25 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 2  Ultra Rares 
 4  Super Rares 
 6  Rares 
 13  Commons
