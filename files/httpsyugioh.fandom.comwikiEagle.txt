 Eagle  (鷲,  Washi ) is an avatar character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.

 Appearance 
 Eagle takes on the form of a  bald eagle , with a brown body and a white head. He also wears a device that resembles a  Duel Gazer  over his left eye.
 
 Biography 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 The Eagle appears before Frog and Pigeon when he says that is his spot for a viewpoint provoking he would eat both of them. Eagle was blown away by Ghost Girl disguised as Blue Angel.
