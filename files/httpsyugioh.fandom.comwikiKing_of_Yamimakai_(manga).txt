 ↑  1.0   1.1   In the original Japanese manga, this card's Level and ATK/DEF are 7 and 2600/2000 in one scene and 6 and 2600/2300 in a later scene. This was corrected in the  bunkoban  to use 7 and 2600/2300 in both instances. In the English manga, it was Level 7 with 2600/2300 ATK/DEF in the first scene and Level 6 with 2300/2300 ATK/DEF in the later scene.
