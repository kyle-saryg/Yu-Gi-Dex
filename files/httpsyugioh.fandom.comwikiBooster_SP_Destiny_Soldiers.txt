 Booster SP: Destiny Soldiers  is a Special  Booster  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Contains 3 different theme Decks based around  DARK - Attribute   monsters . 
 Contains new cards as well as reprints of cards that are difficult to obtain. 
 Includes cards used by Aster Phoenix ( GX  anime  and  ARC-V  anime  versions) and  Sylvio Sawatari . 
 Includes members/support for the " Destiny HERO " and " HERO " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Abyss Actor " and " Abyss Script " archetypes to the  OCG , as well as the first support for the " Darklord " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 45 cards, consisting of:
 
 3  Secret Rares
