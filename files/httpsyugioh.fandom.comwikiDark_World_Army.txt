        Dark World Army     
 The  Dark World Army  (暗黒界, Ankokukai) is a dictatorship of legendary monsters hailing from the world of the humans trapped in the third alternate dimension that  Jaden Yuki  and his companions visit in order to find  Jesse Anderson . Its army is composed of numerous advisers and foot soldiers, and is headed by Dark World's king,  Brron . The remainder of the army joins  The Supreme King's Army  after Brron's defeat. Though once Jaden gets free, the army disbands.
