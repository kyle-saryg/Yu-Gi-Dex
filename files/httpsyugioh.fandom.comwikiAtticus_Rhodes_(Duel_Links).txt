 Atticus Rhodes  is an upcoming Legendary Duelist in Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links. This is a video game depiction of  Atticus Rhodes , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime. Although he isn't seen visually, he is in the game files since the release of the GX world in 2017.
