 2012 Israeli National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Israel. Sixty four people attended the event and it was won by Roi D.
 
 Decks 
 The winner Roi D. used a " Dino Rabbit " Deck. The runner-up Marom DE. used a " Wind-Up " Deck. In the remaining top four, Ido M. used a " Ninja " Deck and Shahak DE. used an " Inzektor " Deck. In the remaining top eight, Santiago F. used a " Dark World " Deck, Eden Z. used an "Inzektor" Deck, Guy H. used an "Inzektor" Deck and Ben G. used a "Wind-Up" Deck.
