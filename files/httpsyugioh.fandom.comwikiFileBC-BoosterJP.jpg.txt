 Booster Chronicle 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Booster Chronicle  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) as a part of the  Booster series .

 Trivia 
 Yami Yugi 's appearance on the cover of this booster pack is taken from the omake page of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 149: " The Line of Fire " . This was also later used for  Duelist Legacy Volume.4 ,  Invasion of Chaos , and  Retro Pack 2 . 
 Of the 80 cards in this set, all but two (" Fairy of the Fountain ", " Wing Eagle ") have since been printed in the English TCG.
