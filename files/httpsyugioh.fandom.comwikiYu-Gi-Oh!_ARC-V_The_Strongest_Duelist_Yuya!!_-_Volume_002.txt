 Bring Smiles With Your Entertainment!!  (エンタメで笑顔を!!,  Entame de Egao o!! ) is the second Volume of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!!  spin-off.
 
 Promotional card 
 Main article:  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! Volume 2 promotional card 
 The volume includes a promotional card, " Odd-Eyes Revolution Dragon ", used by  Yuya Sakaki  in this volume.
 
 
 Chapters 
 
 
 Image
 
 Number
 
 English title
 
 Japanese title
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 8 
 
 
 
 占い少女は、なんでもお見通し!!
 
 
 Uranai Shōjo wa, Nandemo Omito'oshi!!
 
 
 The Fortune-Telling Girl can See Through Everything!!
 
 
 Yuya   Duels  a  young fortuneteller  for his next match of the tournament.
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 9 
 
 
 
 リベンジマッチ！ オレのネオエンタメでぶっ倒すぜ!!
 
 
 Ribenji Macchi! Ore no Neo Entame de Buttaosu ze!!
 
 
 Revenge Match! I'll Beat You With My Neo Entertainment!!
 
 
 Shingo  stands before  Yuya  once again as next opponent in the Duel School Tournament, resolved to show Yuya his "Neo Entertainment" style.
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 10 
 
 
 
 タッグデュエル勃発！ 友情の力で勝利をつかめ!!
 
 
 Taggu Dyueru Boppatsu! Yūjō no Chikara de Shōri o Tsukame!!
 
 
 A Tag Duel Surges! Grasp Victory With the Power of Friendship!!
 
 
 Yuya  is deceived by a young boy,  Sora Shiun'in , who sets him up against  Syun Kurosaki . However,  Gongenzaka  learns about their scheme and interfers, and he ends up tagging alongside Yuya against the two.
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 11 
 
 
 
 喰うか喰われるか!? 甘くておいしいタッグデュエル!!
 
 
 Ku'u ka Kuwareru ka!? Amakute Oishī Taggudyueru!!
 
 
 To Eat or to be Eaten!? A Deliciously Sweet Tag Duel!!
 
 
 Yuya  and  Gongenzaka  continue to struggle against  Sora Shiun'in  and  Syun Kurosaki  in the " Sweet Candy World ", where the players have to eat candies to obtain  Action Cards . Meanwhile,  Reiji Akaba  also starts to move.
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 12 
 
 
 
 決戦バトルロイヤル!! ４龍激突!!
 
 
 Kessen Batoru Roiyaru!! 4 Ryū Gekitotsu!
 
 
 A Decisive Battle Royal!! The Four Dragons Clash!
 
 
 As the Duel School Tournament approaches its climax,  Yuya  ends up having to confront the masters of the three  Summoning  methods from the other Schools:  Yugo ,  Yuto  and  Yuri .
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 13 
 
 
 
 決着の時来たる!! 優勝は誰の手に!?
 
 
 Kecchaku no Toki Kitaru!! Yūshō wa Dare no Te ni!?
 
 
 The Conclusion Draws Near!! Who Will be the Winner?!
 
 
 Yuya  confronts his final adversary in the Duel School Tournament, the ruthless  Yuri , who turns out to be a big obstacle before he can reach  Reiji .
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 14 
 
 
 
 頂上対決！ 大会に隠された真実とは!?
 
 
 Chōjō Taiketsu! Taikai ni Kakusareta Shinjitsu to wa!?
 
 
 Ultimate Showdown! What is the Truth Behind the Tournament?!
 
 
 The final confrontation between  Yuya  and  Reiji  begins in an outer space field. Amidst this battle of quick Summons, Reiji reveals that the true purpose of that Duel is stopping a meteor coming towards Earth - turning this Duel in a Duel that has the fate of humanity on stake.
 
 
 
 
 Chapter 15 
 
 
 
 エンタメで笑顔を!!
 
 
 Entame de Egao o!!
 
 
 Bring Smiles With Your Entertainment!!
 
 
 Combining the strength of everyone's  Dragons ,  Yuya  pulls the ultimate Entertainment Duel against  Reiji , making the latter's plan succeed and saving everyone from the meteor.
