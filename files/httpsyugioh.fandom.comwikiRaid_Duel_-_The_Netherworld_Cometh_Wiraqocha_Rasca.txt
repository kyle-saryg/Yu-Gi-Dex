 Raid Duel - The Netherworld Cometh: Wiraqocha Rasca
 
 
 Raid Duel - The Netherworld Cometh: Wiraqocha Rasca!  is an  event  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It allows the player to unlock  Dark Signer Rex Goodwin  as a playable character.
 
 Event details 
 The player must be at Stage 4 or higher. 
 Collect Dark Signer Vestiges by defeating Duelist in Duel World and Ranked Duels. 
 Use Dark Signer Vestiges to Duel Rex Goodwin. 
 Synchro Monsters do double damage in Raid Duels, Signer Dragons do Triple, so use a Synchro deck that can deal a lot of damage in a short mount of turns!
