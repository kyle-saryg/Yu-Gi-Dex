Daimon  may refer to any of the following:
 
 Daimon : The Kaiba family butler. (Known as  Hobson  in the English anime.) 
 Kogoro Daimon : A member of  the Big Five . (Known as  Leichter  in the English anime.) 
 Taro Daimon : A fake identity used by Yusei Fudo. (Known as  Yusei Adams  in the English anime.) 
 Daimon Area : A dueling ground in  New Domino City .
