 Ai , " Wind Pegasus @Ignister ", " Light Dragon @Ignister ", and " Earth Golem @Ignister " appear in this card's artwork.
 The three " @Ignister " monsters mentioned above were the monsters summoned by this card's effect during this card's  first appearance . 
 This card's name and artwork is a parody of the  You Show Duel School  and  Pendulum Summoning  from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . 
 The Three Monsters in this Artwork are referenced to Three  D/D/D  Monsters used by  Declan Akaba . ( Fusion ) " Flame King Genghis ", ( Synchro ) " Gust King Alexander ", & ( Xyz ) " Wave King Caesar ".
