 Takahiro Kagami  is a Japanese animation director.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions , one of the  KaibaCorp researchers  is named after him.
 
 Work 
 Kagami is credited as character designer and animation director in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time . [1] 
 
Kagami is credited as animation director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh!  episodes. #001 " The Heart of the Cards " #007 " Attack from the Deep " #019 " Double Trouble Duel, Part 1 " #030 " Duel Identity, Part 2 " #042 " The Ties of Friendship " #054 " Obelisk the Tormentor " #066 " Mime Control, Part 2 " #079 " Shadow of a Duel " #091 " Mind Game: Mai Vs. Marik, Part 2 " #097 " Showdown in the Shadows, Part 2 " #103 " Freeze Play, Part 2 "
