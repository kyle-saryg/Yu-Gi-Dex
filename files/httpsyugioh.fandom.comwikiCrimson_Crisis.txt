 Crimson Crisis  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the third set of the  OCG' s sixth series. It is followed by  Raging Battle .
 This set's  Sneak Peek  event and English release in America was originally delayed due to the  Konami  and  Upper Deck   legal dispute . The Sneak Peek event was originally scheduled for February 7–8, 2009. [1]  However, it was finally released approaching March 10, 2009. During release, the set was shown at a Sneak Preview in both Europe and America by Upper Deck International and Konami Digital Entertainment.

 Features 
 Crimson Crisis  features many new cards and  Synchro Monsters  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. It introduces the " Reactor ", " Blackwing ", and " /Assault Mode "  archetypes . Many cards included that work together with already existing theme cards.  Synchro Monsters  gain more and more power-ups. Cards used by  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Greiger ,  Crow Hogan  and  Sayer  appear in this set. The TCG version also includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Reiko Kitamori ,  Depre Scott ,  Reggie MacKenzie ,  Professor Frank  and  Syrus Truesdale .
