 Structure Deck R: Surge of Divine Light  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the fifth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck R series, following  Structure Deck R: Tyranno's Rage .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck R: Surge of Divine Light  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 40 cards
 1  Ultra Rare 
 3  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 30  Commons 
 1 Ultra Rare promotional card 
 1 Common  Token 
 1 Rulebook 
 1 Double-sided  Playmat /Playing Guide 
 Features 
 The "Power Up with Packs" supplement on the back of the included play mat suggests combining this Deck with the following cards from these sets.
 
 " Gaia Saber, the Lightning Shadow ", " Heavy Storm Duster ", and " Back to the Front " from  Code of the Duelist 
 " Akashic Magician ", " Metaverse ", and " Broken Line " from  Circuit Break 
 " Kamion, the Timelord ", " Time Maiden ", and " Number 41: Bagooska the Terribly Tired Tapir " from  Collectors Pack 2017 
 " Maxx "C" ", " Upstart Goblin ", and " Rivalry of Warlords " from  Deck Build Pack: Spirit Warriors
