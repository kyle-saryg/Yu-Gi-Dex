 The  male hippie  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  used for comic relief. He and  his partner  are often seen as tourists.
 
 Biography 
 The two hippies were flying over the  Nazca Lines  when the spider lines disappeared. They were later interviewed on television about what happened. [1] 
 Later, the hippies were seen driving behind  Jack Atlas  and the  imposter Jack Atlas . Jack flipped his  Duel Runner  over their car to avoid crashing into them. [2]  The next day they met  Carly  and told her that they had seen two  Phoenix Whirlwinds  that night and showed her photos, which Carly was able to use as evidence against crimes Jack was accused of committing. [3] 
 He and his partner ate at  Cafe la Geen  once. He tried ordering food, but the waitress,  Stephanie  ignored him in favor of serving and staring at  Jack Atlas . [4] 
 
       The hippies swerving to avoid Yusei and Aporia.     
 When  Yusei Fudo   Turbo Dueled   Aporia  in the  World Racing Grand Prix , the two hippies were driving on the road near the race track, just before Aporia broke the track, forcing him and Yusei to continue the Duel on the normal roads. They nearly hit the hippies, but the male hippie swerved the car away from them, crashing into a wall. [5] 
 As  New Domino City  was evacuated while the  Ark Cradle  descended, the two hippies were caught in a squeezed crowd as they tried leaving the city. [6]
