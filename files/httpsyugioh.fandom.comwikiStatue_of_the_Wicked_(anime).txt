Main card page: " Statue of the Wicked " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Statue "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「スタチュー」 .   As its English name contains "Statue", it  may  be treated a member of the "Statue" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 This card's written lore in the anime was not shown, was too small to read, or was partially or completely obstructed; the lore listed here is a reconstruction based on how it was used, character descriptions of the card, and/or its  OCG / TCG  lore. It may not be accurate or complete.
