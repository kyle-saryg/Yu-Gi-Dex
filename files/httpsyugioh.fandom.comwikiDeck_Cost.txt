Deck Cost  is a system used in some of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  video games  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses . Each card is given a numeric cost. If the combined cost of all cards in a Deck exceeds a certain amount, the player will not be able to use it.
