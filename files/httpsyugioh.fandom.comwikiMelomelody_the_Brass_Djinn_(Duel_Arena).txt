 Melomelody the Brass Djinn  is a character version of the card " Melomelody the Brass Djinn ". 
 She appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P105. She also appears as an opponent at Stage 5-2 under Quest mode.
