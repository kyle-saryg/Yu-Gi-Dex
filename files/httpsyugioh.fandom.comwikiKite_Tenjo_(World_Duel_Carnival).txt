 Kite Tenjo , known as  Kaito Tenjo  ( 天 城  カイト,  Tenjō Kaito ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Kite Tenjo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
