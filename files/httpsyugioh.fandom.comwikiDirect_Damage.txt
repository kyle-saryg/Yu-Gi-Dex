Direct Damage  is an old terminology used in old  card texts . It may have referred to either of the following, depending on the context:
 
 Effect damage 
 Battle damage  resulting from a  direct attack . 
 Newer versions of cards using the term Direct Damage have received  errata  to change it to one of the above two.
