 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 Activate  this card by  targeting  1  Level  4 or lower  Cyberse   monster  in your  GY ;  Special Summon  it in  Defense Position , and if you do,  equip  this card to it. When this card  leaves the field ,  destroy  that monster. Once per Duel, if this card is in your GY, you can activate the above  effect  by  paying  half your  LP .
