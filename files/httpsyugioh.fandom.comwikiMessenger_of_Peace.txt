 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Monsters  with 1500 or more  ATK  cannot  declare  an  attack .  Once per turn , during your  Standby Phase ,  pay  100  LP  or  destroy  this card.

   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
You must pay 100 Life Points at each of your Standby Phases. If you cannot pay, this card is destroyed. All monsters with an ATK of 1500 points or more cannot attack. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
A  spell  cast by a pacifist fairy. It immobilizes all  monsters  on the foe's  field  with an  ATK  of 1,500 or higher for one  turn .
