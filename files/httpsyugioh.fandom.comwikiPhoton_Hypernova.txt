 Photon Hypernova  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the eleventh set in the  OCG ' s  11th series , following  Darkwing Blast .

 Features 
 In the first print runs of Photon Hypernova boxes, includes an  Photon Hypernova +1 Bonus Pack 
 Includes new members/support for the " Photon ", " Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes ",  " Kuriboh ", " Number ", " Number C ", " Bystial ", " Swordsoul ", " Kashtira ", " Tearlaments ", " Chaos ", " Abyss Actor ", " Ninja ", " Ninjitsu Art ", " Gishki ", " Generaider ", " Evil Eye ", " roid ", " Dogmatika ", " Icejade ", " Despia ", " Vaylantz ", " Tri-Brigade ", " Archfiend ", " Plunder Patroll ", " Therion ", " Springans ", " Numeron ", " Branded ", " Scareclaw ", " Abyss Script " and " Labrynth "  archetypes , as well as the  "C"  and  Evigishki  series. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Aquamirror " archetype.
