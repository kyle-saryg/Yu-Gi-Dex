 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  sent  to the  GY  as  Link Material  for a  Cyberse   monster : You can  add  1 " Cynet "  Spell / Trap  from your  Deck  to your  hand , then  discard  1 card. You can  banish  this card from your GY; during the  End Phase  of this  turn ,  shuffle  1 Cyberse monster from your GY into the Deck. You can only use each  effect  of "Firewall Phantom"  once per turn .
