 " glory " ( ｇｌｏｒｙ ,  Gurōrī ) is the fourth Japanese ending theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime, performed by  BAND-MAID . It replaces  BOY  in  episode 071 .

 Changes 
 From  episode 74  onwards,  Gore  is featured in his slimmer appearance. 
 From  episode 77  onwards,  Blue Girl  is replaced with Blue Maiden. 
 From  episode 82  onwards,  Firewall Dragon  and  Firewall eXceed Dragon  fly out of the wormhole right before  Cyberse Clock Dragon
