During either player's  Battle Step , when this card  battles  an opponent's monster: You can  target  the monster this card is battling;  banish  both the opponent's monster and this card. At the end of the  Battle Phase ,  return  any monsters banished by this  effect  to the  field , and if the other monster was an  Xyz Monster , this card gains 500 ATK for each  Xyz Material  it had when it was banished.
