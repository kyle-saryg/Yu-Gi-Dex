 Structure Deck R: Tyranno's Rage  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the fourth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck R series, following  Structure Deck R: Machine Dragon Re-Volt .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck R: Tyranno's Rage  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 40 cards
 1  Ultra Rare 
 3  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 30  Commons 
 1 Ultra Rare promotional card 
 1 Common  Token 
 1 Rulebook 
 1 Double-sided  Playmat /Playing Guide 
 Features 
 The "Power Up with Packs" supplement on the back of the included play mat suggests combining this Deck with the following cards from these sets.
 
 " Performapal Seal Eel ", " Pot of Acquisitiveness ", and " Dimensional Barrier " from  Invasion: Vengeance 
 " Summoner Monk ", " Edge Imp Sabres ", and " Phoenix Wing Wind Blast " from  Booster SP: Fusion Enforcers 
 " Apprentice Piper ", " Foolish Burial Goods ", and " Lost Wind " from  Raging Tempest 
 " Baobaboon ", " The Phantom Knights of Lost Vambrace ", and " Phantasm Spiral Crash " from  Maximum Crisis
