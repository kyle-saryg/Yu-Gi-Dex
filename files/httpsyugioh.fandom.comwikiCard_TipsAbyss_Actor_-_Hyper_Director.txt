 " Abyss Actor - Curtain Raiser " and " Abyss Actor - Extras " can Special Summon themselves from the  Pendulum Zone , allowing this card to be  Link Summoned  without using up your  Normal Summon . 
 This card helps ease the summoning of high-Level  Pendulum Monsters  unless their  Pendulum Effects  restrict  Special Summoning , such as those of the " Qli " archetype.
