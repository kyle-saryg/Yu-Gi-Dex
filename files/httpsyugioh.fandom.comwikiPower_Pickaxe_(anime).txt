 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 Once per  turn , you can  select  and  remove from play  1  monster  in your opponent's  Graveyard  whose  Level  is less than or equal to the  equipped  monster's. If the equipped monster  battles  during the same turn that this effect was used, it gains  ATK  equal to half of the removed monster's ATK, during the  Damage Step  only.
