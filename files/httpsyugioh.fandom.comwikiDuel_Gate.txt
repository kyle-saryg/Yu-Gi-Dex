      The Duel Gate.     
 The  Duel Gate  ( 決闘門 ,  Dyueru Gēto ) is a location in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . It is an entrance on  Saiko Island , and is both the starting line and goal for the  D1 Grand Prix 's final stage. It can only be opened if the participant acquires  Star Ticket (s) which have a value of 12 or above; when he does so, by placing the Star Tickets in his  Duel Disk , the small door inside the big gate will open, letting the participant enter. The  Aerial Fortress Seibal  is located beyond this gate.
