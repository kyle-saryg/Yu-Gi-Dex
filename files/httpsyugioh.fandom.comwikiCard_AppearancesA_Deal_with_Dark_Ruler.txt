 Anime 
 
 In  episode 113 ,  the Big Five  use this card during their Tag Duel against  Yami Yugi  and  Joey Wheeler . After " Mirage Knight " (whose ATK was 7800 due to its own effect) attacks and destroys " Five-Headed Dragon ",  Leichter  activates this card to pay half his Life Points and nullify the Battle Damage he would have taken as well as Special Summon " Berserk Dragon " (who became the Big Five's new Deck Master). 
 In the anime, this card had a different artwork.
