 Greek - Αρχαίο Εργαλείο ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards  lore) 
 Μια μηχανή που ανακαλύφθηκε στα ερείπια ενός αρχαίου πολιτισμού. Φαίνεται πως κατασκευάστηκε μόνο για να καταστρέφει. 
 
 
 In the English version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "A destructive machine discovered in the Ruins of the Ancients." 
 In the French version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Machine destructrice découverte dans les ruines des anciens." 
 In the German version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Eine zerstörerische Maschine, die in den Ruinen der Alten entdeckt wurde." 
 In the Italian version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Una macchina dall'altissimo potere distruttivo scoperta tra le Rovine degli Antichi." 
 In the Spanish version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Una máquina de destrucción descubierta en las Ruinas de los Antiguos."
