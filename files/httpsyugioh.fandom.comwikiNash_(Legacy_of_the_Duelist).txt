 Nash , known as  Nasch  ( 神 ,  Nasshu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . He is the Barian alter-ego of  Reginald Kastle . This is a video game depiction of  Nash , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
