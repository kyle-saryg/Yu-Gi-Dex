 Maximillion Pegasus  is a video game depiction of  Maximillion Pegasus , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  anime.
 He appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. His partner ID is P126.
 He was only obtainable by winning at least 30 times in Arena mode (Single Round only), by the end of the time period of November 10, 2014, 00:00 (UTC) through November 16, 2014, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received him in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on November 19, 2014.

 Description 
 The president of  Industrial Illusions . He is also a powerful  duelist  and loves  Toon  so much.
 
 Deck 
 Deck  consisting of Toon which can  direct-attack  your opponent. Let's add suitable Toon on your hands by " Toon Table of Contents ".  Ritual Monster  " Relinquished " has strong  effect  even though its  ATK / DEF  is 0.

 Trivia 
 " Mind Haxorz " in this character's Deck is a reference to the mind-reading power of the  Millennium Eye  that Pegasus wielded in the anime and manga. 
 " Reshef the Dark Being " in this character's Deck is a reference to how that monster possessed Pegasus in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction .
