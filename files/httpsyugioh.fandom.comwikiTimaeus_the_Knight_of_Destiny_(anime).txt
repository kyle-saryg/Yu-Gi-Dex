 " Legendary Knight Timaeus " 
 This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except from your  Fusion Deck  by having " Legendary Knight Timaeus " absorb " Legendary Knight Critias " and " Legendary Knight Hermos ". This card's  ATK  is equal to the combined ATK of the absorbed  monsters . When this card is  destroyed by battle , Special Summon 3 "Legendary Knight" monsters from your hand, Deck, or Graveyard.
