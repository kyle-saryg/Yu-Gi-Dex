 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament " and " Mermail Abyssmegalo ". 
 To maintain card advantage, activate this card when it would send at least 2 of your opponent's Spell/Trap Cards to the Graveyard. 
 This card's effect bypasses protection granted to Spell/Trap Cards by " White Dragon Ninja ", " Dark Magician the Dragon Knight ", " Number 66: Master Key Beetle " etc.
