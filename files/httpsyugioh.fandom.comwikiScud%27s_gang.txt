 Scud's gang  is a gang of misfits and bullies at  Domino High School , led by  Scud  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions .
 
 Biography 
 The gang was picking on  Aigami , making provocative remarks, but Aigami was defended by  Joey Wheeler . Knowing that he was in  a gang  before, they left, but after telling Aigami to meet them later. When they threatened to attack Aigami with weapons, Aigami used the  Quantum Cube  to disintegrate the gang.
