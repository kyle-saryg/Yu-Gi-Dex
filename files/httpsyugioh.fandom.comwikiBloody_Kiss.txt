 Bloody Kiss  is  Akiza Izinski 's  Duel Runner . [1] 
 It was built by  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas  and  Crow Hogan , and given to Akiza as a gift, when she took-up  Turbo Dueling . [2] 
 
 History 
       The Duel Runner before being modified for the Turbo Dueling exam.     
 Bloody Kiss  was originally built by Yusei as a regular motorcycle that Akiza could use to practice for the first part of the exam, to obtain her  Turbo Duel  license. After she passed it, Yusei, Jack, and Crow worked hard on modifying it completely for Akiza's Turbo Dueling exam. 
 
 
 Features 
 Bloody Kiss  possesses two bladewings which are located at each side in the front part of the Runner. These bladewings are normally deployed whenever Akiza is riding it or Dueling. However, when not in use, these wings can retract and lower themselves. It is also shown that it possesses a hybrid  Duel Disk  for Akiza to use in regular Duels.    
 During the  World Racing Grand Prix ,  Bloody Kiss  has the  Team 5D's  logo on it.
