The  Grandpa Cup  is a Calendar event in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Eternal Duelist Soul . It takes place at the beginning of June and offers two special sets, one for the Qualifying Round and another for the Final. The Final round special set includes the three  promotional cards  and  alternate artwork  versions of cards.
