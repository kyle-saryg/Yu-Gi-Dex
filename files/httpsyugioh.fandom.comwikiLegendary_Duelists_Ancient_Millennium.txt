 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Weevil Underwood ,  Bandit Keith ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Vellian Crowler ,  Jesse Anderson ,  Rudolph Heitmann ,  Duel Academy  students' personnel, such as  Yuri ,  Dennis McField ,  Jean-Michel Roget  and the  Obelisk Force  members. 
 Includes members and/or support for the  Ancient Gear ,  Armed Dragon ,  Crystal Beast ,  Ojama  and  A-to-Z  archetypes.
