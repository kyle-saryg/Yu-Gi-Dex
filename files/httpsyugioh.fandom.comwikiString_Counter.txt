 A  String Counter  is a type of  counter  used exclusively by " Number 40: Gimmick Puppet of Strings " and " Number C40: Gimmick Puppet of Dark Strings ". They are used to mark monsters for destruction at a later point (if ever), with effect damage inflicted at that time.
