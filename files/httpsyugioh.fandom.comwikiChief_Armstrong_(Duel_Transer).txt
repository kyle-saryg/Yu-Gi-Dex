 Chief Armstrong , known as  Takasu  ( 鷹栖 ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Duel Transer . This is a video game depiction of  Mr. Armstrong , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. He is encountered in the Yggdrasil Entrance.

 Trivia 
 This character's Deck is largely built around the theme of "Chain" cards (Japanese: チェーン  chēn ).
 " Dark Prisoner ", " Oppressed People " and " People Running About " are most likely included in this Deck as references to prisoners, which reflects this character's position as a  prison warden  in the  anime .
