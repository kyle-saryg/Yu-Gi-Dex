 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
4  Level  10  monsters Cannot be  destroyed by battle . When a monster you  control  inflicts  battle damage  to your opponent: You gain  LP  equal to the  damage  they took. If this card has " Number 92: Heart-eartH Dragon " as an  Xyz Material , it gains this  effect . ●  Once per turn : You can  detach  1 material from this card;  negate  the effects of all  face-up  cards your opponent currently controls, until the end of this  turn . This  activation  and effect cannot be negated.
