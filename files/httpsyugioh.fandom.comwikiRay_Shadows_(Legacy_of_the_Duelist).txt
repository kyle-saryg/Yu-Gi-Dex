 Ray Shadows  known as  Rei Shingetsu  ( 真 月   零   Shingetsu Rei ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Ray Shadows ,  Vector 's human form, a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.

 Trivia 
 Ray's Utopian V Deck is based on Ray and  Yuma Tsukumo 's  Battle Royal  Duel against  Girag  and is both their Decks combined.  Rank-Up-Magic Limited Barian's Force  is the only card belonging to Ray however, so nearly all of the cards in the Deck are Yuma's. Presumably Ray was used instead of Yuma so the game would have an extra character for Challenge Mode.
