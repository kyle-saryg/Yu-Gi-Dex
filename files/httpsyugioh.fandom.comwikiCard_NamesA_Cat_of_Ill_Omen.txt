 Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 The given approximated Arabic name (هرّة النّحس السّوداء) translates to  Black Cat of Misfortune . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Μια Γάτα Κακού Οιωνού ") is an exact translation of the  TCG  name of this card.
 An unofficial translation of its  OCG  counterpart (" Black Cat Informs of Misfortune ") in Greek could be " Μαύρη Γάτα Προοιωνίζει τη Δυστυχία ".
