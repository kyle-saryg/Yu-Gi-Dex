The  Pro League  is a circle of the highest ranking Duelists in the world. It is the goal of many  Duel Academy  students to one day compete in the Pro League.
 The Pro League has a ranking system, though how it works is never divulged. At the time of the  Genex Tournament , the 8th, 9th and 10th ranked Duelists in the league are  Maitre' D ,  Elroy Prescot  and  Mathmatica , respectively. In addition, it is mentioned earlier that  Howard X Miller  is ranked higher than  Aster Phoenix , although the numerical ranks that they hold are not mentioned. In the anime,  The D  has been the champion for more then 10 years, while in the manga the current champion is  Koyo Hibiki .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , there are many professional leagues for  Turbo Dueling  around the world ( Ride Ace ,  IFS Duel League ,  Shingon League ...).
