 Vetrix , known as  Tron  (トロン  Toron ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Vetrix , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
