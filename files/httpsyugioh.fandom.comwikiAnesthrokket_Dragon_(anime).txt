 [  Dragon  /  Effect  ] When a  Link Monster 's  effect  is  activated  that  targets  this  face-up  card on the  field  ( Quick Effect ): You can  destroy  this card, then make 1 face-up  monster  on the field unable to  attack , also it has its effects  negated . During the  End Phase , if this card is in the  GY  because it was  destroyed  on the field  by battle  or card effect and  sent  there this  turn : You can  Special Summon  1 " Rokket " monster from your  Deck , except "Anesthrokket Dragon". You can only use each effect of "Anesthrokket Dragon"  once per turn .
