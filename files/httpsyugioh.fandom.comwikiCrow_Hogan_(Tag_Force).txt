 Unlockables 
 Tag Force 4 
 
 By completing Crow's storyline, the player receives three copies of the card, " Blackwing - Silverwind the Ascendant ". 
 The player can unlock the packs,  Black Paint  and  Beast Kingdom '87 , by entering the shop with Crow. 
 Tag Force 5 
 
 By completing Crow's storyline, the player receives three copies of the card, " Black-Winged Dragon ". 
 The player can unlock the packs,  Power Brawlers ,  Rule the Field ,  Once a Beast, Always a Beast  and  Soaring Blackwings , by entering the shop with Crow. 
 Tag Force 6 
 
 By completing Crow's storyline, the player receives three copies of the card, "Black-Winged Dragon".
