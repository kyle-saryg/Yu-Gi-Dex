   English 
 
 
( Quick Effect ): You can  target  1  face-up   monster  on the  field ;  equip  this card from your  hand  or field to it as an  Equip Spell  that gives it 100  ATK / DEF . You can only use this  effect  of "Silver-Winged AXE-Sarry"  once per turn .
