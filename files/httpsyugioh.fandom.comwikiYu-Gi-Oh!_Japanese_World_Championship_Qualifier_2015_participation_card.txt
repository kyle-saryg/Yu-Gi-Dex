 Yu-Gi-Oh! Japanese World Championship Qualifier 2015 participation card 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Japanese World Championship Qualifier  participation card  is a  Super Rare   replica  of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2014 prize card , " The Twin Kings, Founders of the Empire ". It was distributed at the Japanese World Championship Qualifier 2015 that was held on June 27, 2015 (Tokyo) and on July 4, 2015 (Osaka).
