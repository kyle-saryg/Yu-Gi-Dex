 Lore
 
 
 The enigmatic man that holds the  Millennium Key . He is after  Yugi . 
 Ability
 
 
 
 Millennium Key 
 This card is defeated in battle, but you can look at your opponent's hand and choose the next 2 cards that they will play and use to battle. 
 
 Rule 
 
 
 
 ~Rule ➇~ 
 Each turn will continue like this, until both players' Decks are empty.
