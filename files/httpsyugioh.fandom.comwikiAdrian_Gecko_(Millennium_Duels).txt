 Adrian Gecko , known as  Amon Garam  (アモン・ガラム,  Amon Garamu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Adrian Gecko , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
