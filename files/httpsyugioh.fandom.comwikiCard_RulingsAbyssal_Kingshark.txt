 Mentions in Other Rulings 
 Makiu, the Magical Mist : The destruction effect of “ Makiu, the Magical Mist ” does not  target , so it will not destroy “ Abyssal Kingshark ”, etc. 
 OCG Rulings 
 If this card's effect of " This card is not destroyed by the effect of a card which does not target " is applied, and then this card is destroyed, and then this card is Special Summoned back to the Field, then you can use the effect again. [1]
