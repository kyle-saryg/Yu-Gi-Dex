 War of the Giants Reinforcements 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 War of the Giants Reinforcements  is an expansion pack, designed specifically to be used in combination with  Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants  to adapt the latter for the  Draft environment . It was only released in Europe; its North American equivalent is  War of the Giants: Round 2 , which is missing 1 card (" Zubaba General ").
