 "Harto Tenjo's Theme"  (天城ハルトのテーマ,  Tenjō Haruto no Tēma ) is an instrumental musical composition from the Japanese version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL .
 It is 2:42 long and has been released on  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL Sound Duel 1 .
 
 Appearances 
 "Harto Tenjo's Theme" was played in the following episodes:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  episode 13: " The Number Hunter, Part 1 " 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  episode 22: " The Shark Hunter " 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  episode 40: " About Hart "
