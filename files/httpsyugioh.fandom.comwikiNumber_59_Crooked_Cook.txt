 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  4  monsters While you  control  no other cards on the  field , this card is  unaffected  by other cards'  effects .  Once per turn  ( Quick Effect ): You can  detach  1  material  from this card;  destroy  as many other cards you control as possible, then this card gains 300  ATK  until the end of this turn for each monster destroyed by this effect and  sent  to the  GY .
