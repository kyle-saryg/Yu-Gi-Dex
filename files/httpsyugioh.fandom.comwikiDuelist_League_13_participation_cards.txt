 Duelist League 14 
 
 
 
 
 
 These cards were handed out to participants of the 2012 Duelist League, open only to those aged 14 and under. All cards were available in purple, red, green, and blue  Rare  versions. This set is followed by  Duelist League 14 participation cards .
 
 Breakdown 
 All 20 cards are  Rare , available in blue, green, purple and red.
