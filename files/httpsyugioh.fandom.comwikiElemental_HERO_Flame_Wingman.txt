" Elemental HERO Avian " + " Elemental HERO Burstinatrix " Must be  Fusion Summoned . When this card  destroys  a  monster   by battle  and  sends  it to the  GY : Inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the  ATK  of the destroyed monster in the GY.
