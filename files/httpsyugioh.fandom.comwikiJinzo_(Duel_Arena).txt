 Jinzo  is a character version of the card " Jinzo ". 
 He appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P030. He is also available as an opponent at Stages 6–3 and 12-5 under Quest mode.

 Partner 
 A Deck that has the potential to completely shut down traps using the  Trap Cards  " Royal Decree " and " Jinzo ". The  ATK  of these monsters isn't all that high. Handle powerful opponent monsters using  Effect Monsters  and  Spell Cards !

 Trivia 
 This is the only character to appear twice as an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena  under Quest mode. 
 Each opponent of Stage 12 is based on a member of  the Big Five . This character is based on  Leichter , who took the form of this card's  card counterpart . Its quote is a reference to Leichter using a Deck focused on restoring his  Life Points .
