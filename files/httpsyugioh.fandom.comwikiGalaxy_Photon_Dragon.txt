 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  4  monsters All other  LIGHT  monsters you  control  gain 500  ATK . You can only use each of the following  effects  of "Galaxy Photon Dragon"  once per turn . You can  detach  1  material  from this card; take 1 " Photon " or " Galaxy " card from your  Deck , and either  add  it to your  hand  or  send  it to the  GY . If a LIGHT monster(s) is  Special Summoned  to your  field : You can  target  1 of them that has a Level; its Level becomes 4 or 8 until the end of this  turn .
