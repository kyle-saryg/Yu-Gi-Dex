 Micky Starlett , known as  Mikiyo Naname  ( 斜 芽   美 技 代   Naname Mikiyo ) in the Japanese version, is a participant in the "Junior Youth" class of the  Arc League Championship  and is an  Idol  Duelist belonging to the Idol Group " Dueling Dynamos " in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. [1] [2]
