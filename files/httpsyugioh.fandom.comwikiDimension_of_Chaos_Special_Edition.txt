 Dimension of Chaos: Special Edition  is a  Special Edition , containing 2  Super Rare  cards and 27 cards equivalent to 3  Dimension of Chaos   Booster Packs .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Dimension of Chaos: Special Edition  is comprised of 29 cards:
 
 27 cards equivalent to 3  Dimension of Chaos  Booster Packs 
 1 of 2  Super Rares  (" Ultimaya Tzolkin " or " Frightfur Tiger ") 
 1 of 2 Super Rares of preview cards from  Breakers of Shadow
