 Taku Yashiro (born January 06, 1993) is a Japanese voice actor who was affiliated in VIMS in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 
 Work 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , he voices  Battle Beast .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS , he voices  Flame .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS , he voices  Tatsuhisa Kamijo  (Luke).
