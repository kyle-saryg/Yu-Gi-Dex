When this card is  destroyed  and  sent  to the  Graveyard  while it has Xyz Material: You can Special Summon this card from your Graveyard, then gain  LP  equal to the  original ATK  of this card. " Number 101: Silent Honor ARK " must be in your Graveyard to  activate  and to  resolve  this  effect . If this card is Special Summoned this way, it cannot  attack  for the rest of this  turn .
