 Duelist Entry Deck VS  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the tenth deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2014 .  The  TCG  North American equivalents of this deck are  Saber Force Starter Deck  and  Dark Legion Starter Deck . The  TCG  European equivalent of this deck is  2-Player Starter Deck Yuya & Declan . The Korean  OCG  equivalents of this deck are  Duelist Entry Deck VS: Saber Force  and  Duelist Entry Deck VS: Dark Legion .
