 Mai Valentine , known as  Mai Kujaku  ( 孔雀 舞 ,  Kujaku Mai ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Mai Valentine , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
