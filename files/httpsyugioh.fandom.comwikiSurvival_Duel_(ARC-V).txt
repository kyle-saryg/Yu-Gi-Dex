A  Survival Duel  is a type of  Duel  used by  Duel Academy 's instructor  Sanders  as a final test for his students to become full-fledged Duel soldiers. It involves pitting five of his elite students,  Ted ,  Ethan ,  Bernie ,  Jacob  and  Emma , against five  Lancers  (and unbeknownst to all, the  Battle Beast ) in the  Field Spell  " Predator Jungle " in a Free-For-All fight that will end with only one Duelist emerging victorious and alive, with everyone else being turned into cards, via the  Human Sealing Technology .
