 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Activate  only when a  monster (s) is  destroyed  by a  card effect . Toss a coin. If Heads, apply the Right Side Up effect, and if Tails, apply the Upside Down effect. ● Right Side Up: The  controller  of the destroyed monster(s) gains  Life Points  equal to the total  ATK  of the destroyed monster(s). ● Upside Down: Inflict  damage  to the controller of the destroyed monster(s) equal to the total ATK of the destroyed monster(s).
