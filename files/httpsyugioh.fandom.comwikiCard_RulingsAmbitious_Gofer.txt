 TCG Rulings 
 This effect  targets  the opponent's monsters. [1] 
 Your opponent chooses whether or not to reveal a monster when this effect resolves. [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Ambitious Gofer "  targets  one to two of your opponent's monsters. [2] 
 Your opponent reveals one Monster Card in his hand when the effect of " Ambitious Gofer " begins resolving. [2] 
 The effect of " Ambitious Gofer " may or may not destroy a monster at resolution, so the effect of " Stardust Dragon " cannot be Chained to it. [3]
