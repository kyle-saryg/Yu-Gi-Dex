 Names 
 
 The Japanese name of this card ( Alien Psycho ) can be translated in Greek as " Τρελός/Πνευματικά Ασταθής Εξωγήινος ". 
 The Spanish name of this card is " Psíquico Alien ". In the Spanish TCG, the name of the " Alien " archetype is " Alienígena ". Therefore, the name of this card should be " Psíquico Alienígena ".
 "Psíquico Alienígena" is its given name in the Card Database.
