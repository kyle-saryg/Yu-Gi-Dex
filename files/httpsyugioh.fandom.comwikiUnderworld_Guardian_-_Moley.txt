 Underworld Guardian - Moley  is a character appearing in some video games.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , Moley appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for December 2009.
 
 Deck 
 In World Championship 2008, Moley's Decks are pared with Gigori's Decks since all the duels taking place in their world are Tag Duels. Surprisingly, unlike the other Guardians, both Guardians' Decks all share a recurring theme - they are usually focused around DARK and/or Fiend monsters.
 In Stardust Accelerator, Moley uses a  DARK  Deck, titled "Black Bomb".
