      Zorc Arm Eyeball.     
 "Zorc Arm Eyeball"  was an unnamed  Monster World  piece that spawned alongside  Zorc Arm Dragon , from  Zorc's  arm, after  Hiroto  shot it off. The eyeball was easily defeated by  Joey , after  Dark Yugi  rolled a critical hit.
