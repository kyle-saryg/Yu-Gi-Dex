 Penguin Soldier  is a character version of the card " Penguin Soldier ". 
 He appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. His partner ID is P063. He also appears as an opponent at Stage 6-1 under Quest mode.

 3.2   Opponent 
 
 
 
 
 Description 
 An unexpectedly reliable penguin solider  [ sic ] . Spent a long while without any allies, which has made him quite sad and lonely.
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Hi there. I'm Penguin Soldier. I often get called "cowardly" or "cheap," but all I'm looking to do is avoid unnecessary fighting. I can't abide all these hot-heads.
 
 Decks 
 Partner 
 A  Deck  that combines "Bounce", which returns your opponent's cards to their  hand , and "Hand Destruction", which destroys your opponent's hand. Monsters individually do not have especially high  ATK , but each monster does have very powerful  effects . Use " Salvage " over and over to clear out your opponent's  field !
