 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Quinton , known as  V  ( Ⅴ ,  Bui ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Quinton , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
