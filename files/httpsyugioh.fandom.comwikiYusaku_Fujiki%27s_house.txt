 Yusaku Fujiki's house  is the residence of  Yusaku Fujiki  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. It is located within  Den City . The house seems to be rather small and the walls are shown to be somewhat worn off. Yusaku appears to live there by himself. It is currently unknown if his family live with him, but he has  Roboppi  to clean it. Hidden behind a wall near the stairway is a room for Yusaku to log into  LINK VRAINS , similar to the room located in  Café Nom .
