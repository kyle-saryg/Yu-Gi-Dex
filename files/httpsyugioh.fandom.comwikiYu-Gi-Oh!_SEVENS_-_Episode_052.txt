 " The Last Rush Duel " is the fifty-second episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on June 13, 2021.
 Rush Duels  will be eradicated by a Rush Duel via "Real Time Rush Duel Uninstallation."  Luke  and  Yuga  are faced with a tough decision: will they continue their  Duel , or cancel it? However, the two of them simply smile, and declare that they won't just let the best Duel ever end this way! This shall be the final Rush Duel!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Tatsuhisa "Luke" Kamijo 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
