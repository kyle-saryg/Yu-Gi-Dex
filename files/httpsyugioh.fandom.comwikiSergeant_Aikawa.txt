 Sergeant Aikawa  is a character appearing in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 . In the English version, he is called  Lioside , who is in fact a character from previous games.
 He is based on  Aikawa , an editor of  V Jump , who used same Deck type as him in the  The Third Charisma Duelist Determining Fight Tournament . The two Aikawas spell their names in different Japanese writing systems.
