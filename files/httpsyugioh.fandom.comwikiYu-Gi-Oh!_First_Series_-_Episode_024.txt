 " Now! The Time of Decision - The Miracle of Friendship " is the twenty-fourth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! (Toei anime) .

 Featured Duels 
 Dark Yugi vs Seto Kaiba 
 Both players start with 2000  Life Points .
 Dark Yugi's turn 
 Dark Yugi  Summons " Winged Dragon, Guardian of the Fortress 1 " in  Attack Position .
 Kaiba's turn 
 Seto Kaiba  blocks it by Summoning " Hitotsu-Me Giant " in Attack Position.
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi  attacks  and  destroys  "Hitotsu-Me Giant" with "Winged Dragon, Guardian of the Fortress" (Kaiba: 2000 → 1800  Life Points ).
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba  draws  and Summons " The Wicked Worm Beast " in Attack Position and attacks "Winged Dragon, Guardian of the Fortress". "The Wicked Worm Beast" is destroyed, but Kaiba takes no damage because they had the same ATK.
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi's turn is not shown.
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba  Sets  " Saggi the Dark Clown "
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi Sets " Chimera ".
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba  Flip Summons  "Saggi the Dark Clown" and  activates  " Dark Energy " to triple its ATK ("Saggi": 600 → 1800 ATK). "Saggi" attacks and destroys "Winged Dragon, Guardian of the Fortress" (Dark Yugi: 2000 → 1600 Life Points).
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi draws " Right Leg of the Forbidden One ". He Sets " Koumori Dragon ".
 Kaiba's turn 
"Saggi the Dark Clown" attacks and destroys both "Chimera" and "Koumori Dragon". (Dark Yugi: 1600 → 1400 Life Points).
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi draws and Summons " Gaia The Fierce Knight " in Attack Position. "Dark Knight Gaia" attacks  and destroys "Dark Clown Saggi" (Kaiba: 1800 → 1300 Life Points).
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba draws and Summons " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ". "Blue-Eyes" attacks and destroys "Gaia" (Dark Yugi: 1400 → 700 Life Points).
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi Sets a monster.
 Kaiba's turn 
"Blue Eyes White Dragon" attacks and destroys the Set monster.
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi Sets " Gremlin ".
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba draws and Summons another "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" in Attack Position.
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi draws and  activates  " Swords of Revealing Light ".
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba Sets a monster.
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi has "Right Leg of the Forbidden One", " Left Arm of the Forbidden One ", " Left Leg of the Forbidden One " and " Dark Mammoth " in his  hand . Dark Yugi draws  Right Arm of the Forbidden One "
 Kaiba's turn 
The effect of "Sealing Swords of Light" ends. Kaiba draws and Summons his last "Blue-Eyes White Dragon".
 Dark Yugi's turn 
Dark Yugi draws " Exodia the Forbidden One ". " Exodia " is summoned and destroys every monster Kaiba  controls , and drops his Life Points to 0 (Kaiba: 1300 → 0 Life Points).
 Dark Yugi wins.
