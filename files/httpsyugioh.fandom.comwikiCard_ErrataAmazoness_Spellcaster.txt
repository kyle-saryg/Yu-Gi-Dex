 Second erratum 
 
 
 Until  the end of this turn,  switch  the original ATK  of 1 face-up  monster   on your side of field that includes  "Amazoness"  in its card name or is named "Amazon Archer", with the original ATK of  1 face-up monster  on  your  opponent's   side of the field . 
 Select  1  face-up  "Amazoness"  monster   you control and  1 face-up monster your  opponent   controls .  Switch  the original ATK   of  these monsters   until  the end of this turn . 
 Target  1 "Amazoness" monster you control and 1 face-up monster your opponent controls;  switch  the original ATK of  those targets  until the end of this turn. 
 
 
       MFC-084 Magician's Force     
       TU04-EN018 Turbo Pack: Booster Four     
       LCJW-EN100 Legendary Collection 4: Joey's World Mega Pack

 First erratum

 Escolha 1 monstro "Amazoness" que você controla e  1 monstro com a face para cima que  seu oponente controla; troque  o ATK original  desses alvos até o final deste turno .
