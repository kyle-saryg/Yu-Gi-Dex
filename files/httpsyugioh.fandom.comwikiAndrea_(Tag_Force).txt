 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
       Andrea, in  Tag Force 3 .     In  Tag Force 1-3 , she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 Tag Force 1 
 Upon acquiring Andrea's  Deck Recipe , she also gifts the player with a " Gravedigger Ghoul " card.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 During  Tyranno 's fourth-to-last story event, Andrea and  Jewels  Tag Duel against the player and Tyranno.
 Andrea and Tyranno can Tag Duel against the player and their partner as part of an optional random event.

 Decks 
 Andrea uses  Dinosaur  Decks  in every  Tag Force  game. From  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4  onwards, she uses Decks dedicated to the  " Jurrac "  and  " Evol "  archetypes as well.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , her decks accommodate sauropods like " Bracchio-raidus " because the Level 7 and higher theropods (like " Ultimate Tyranno ") are used by  Tyranno Hassleberry .
