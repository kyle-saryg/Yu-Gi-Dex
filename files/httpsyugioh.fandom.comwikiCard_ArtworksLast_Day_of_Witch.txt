 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 The International version of "Last Day of Witch" is completely redrawn to avoid conflict with Pagans and Neopagans due to the artwork being based on the events of the Salem Witch Trials. Also, in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , "Last Day of Witch" was drawn with a different artwork.
