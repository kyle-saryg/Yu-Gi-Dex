 Duel Terminal - Sacred Star Knights!!  is the thirteenth installment of the  OCG 's  Duel Terminal  series, following  Duel Terminal - Xyz Startup!! , and followed by  Duel Terminal - Ouroboros, Wicked Dragon of Destruction!! .

 Features 
 Includes further support for known archetypes: " Gem-Knight ", " Laval ", " Gusto ", and " Gishki " 
 Introduces the " Constellar " and " lswarm " archetypes. 
 Introduces  Aporia ,  Slifer Red Blair Flannigan  and  Kite Tenjo  as duelable opponents. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards (consisting of 30 New Cards and 20 Reprint Cards), or 54 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares  (all available as Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares) 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 10  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 30  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
