 The  May 11, 2021 Forbidden and Limited List  was the twenty-second Forbidden and Limited List to go into effect in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links , on May 11, 2021.
 A player's Deck may only contain one  Limited  card and two  Semi-Limited  cards, in total. This contrasts to the  TCG  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game , where the player can have one copy of each Limited card and two copies of each Semi-Limited card.
