 Voice actor 
 
 
 Gregory Abbey [1] , also known under the aliases  Frank Frankson  and  John Campbell , is an American voice actor for the English dubs of the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime.
 
 Roles 
 Abbey's first major role was portraying  Tristan Taylor , replacing  Sam Riegel . He also voices  Jean-Claude Magnum .
 Abbey portrays  Damon  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . 
 Abbey's most notable role was portraying  Yusei Fudo  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
