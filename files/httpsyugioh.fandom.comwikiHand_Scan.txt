 Hand Scan  is a psychic ability  Sherry LeBlanc  possesses in the  manga . Using it she is able to see cards in her opponent's hand by reading their mind, [1]  similar to  Pegasus '  Mind Scan .
 
 Usage 
 During her  Turbo Duel  with  Akiza Izinski , Sherry used this ability with " Name Erasure " and " Mokusatsu " to constantly make Akiza discard her cards. [1]  Akiza ultimatelly countered Sherry's Hand Scan with her own psychich ability:  Foresee Draw . Akiza foresaw her draw and kept her eyes closed until her  Main Phase ; since Akiza herself didn't see the card she drew, Sherry weren't able to scan it. [2] 
 
 Trivia 
 Technically this is cheating. But Sherry doesn't get disqualified.
