 Starter Deck 2010  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the fifth deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2009 . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Starter Deck: Duelist Toolbox .

 Features 
 The deck features 5 new cards used by  Yusei . 
 Includes many reprints to utilize new cards from the start. 
 Includes cards that work together with the latest anime expansions. 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Starter Deck 2010  contains 43 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:

 2  Super Rares
