 Carly Carmine , known as  Carly Nagisa  (カーリー渚,  Kārī Nagisa ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Carly Carmine , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Decks 
 Her first deck, "Lost Fortune" is the one used before she was a Dark Signer. Her other deck is the one used as a Dark Signer.

 Trivia 
 The only time Carly is ever seen without her glasses, aside from being a Dark Signer, is after she loses to Sayer. 
 Though her first duel is as her regular self (before becoming a Dark Signer) when the duel starts her duel icon uses her Dark Signer appearance rather than her regular appearance which spoils the game's next duel and is a plot hole as she isn't supposed to be a Dark Signer yet.
