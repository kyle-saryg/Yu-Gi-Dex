 The  2012 Danish National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Denmark. It took place in Kolding on June 16–17 and was won by by August Kauffmann. [2]  The tournament was attended by 107 people, 18 of whom got to qualify for the World Championship Qualifier continentals that year. [2] 
 The entry fee was 160  kr  for the main event and 100 kr for the  Dragon Duel . [1] 
 
 Decks 
 The winner, August Kauffmann used a " Final Countdown " Deck. [2]
