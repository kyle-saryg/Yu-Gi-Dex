 " Heroic Champion " ( Ｈ － Ｃ   Hiroikku Chanpion , written as  Ｈ－Ｃ  in the anime) is an  archetype  of  Warrior   Xyz Monsters , which are part of the " Heroic " archetype, that are used by  Nistro  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. Its  support  cards were retooled into a "Heroic" support when released in the  OCG / TCG .
 All of the "Heroic Champion" monsters are based on mythological weapons and require  Level  4 Warrior monsters to be  Summoned .
 
 Trivia 
 All members of this archetype are  Rank  4 monsters, except for the Rank 1 " Heroic Champion - Jarngreipr ".
