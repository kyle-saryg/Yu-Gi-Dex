 Ancient Prophecy  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fifth set of the  OCG ' s sixth series. It is followed by  Stardust Overdrive .
 The North American Sneak Peeks for this set took place from August 15–16, 2009.
 Packs of the  TCG  set were also printed in the  Ancient Prophecy: Special Edition .

 Features 
 Ancient Prophecy  contains cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime, such as " Earthbound Immortals ", along with cards used by  Yusei ,  Luna ,  Leo ,  Crow ,  Rally ,  Lazar ,  Carly  and  Greiger . The TCG version also contains cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Yubel ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Yako Tenma ,  Bastion Misawa  and  Marcel Bonaparte 
 The set introduces the " Fishborg ", " Fortune Lady " archetypes and includes support for the " Koa'ki Meiru " archetype as well as further more for " Blackwing ", " Infernity " and " XX-Saber " cards.
