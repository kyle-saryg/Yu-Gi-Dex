 Levia-Dragon - Daedalus  is a character version of the card " Levia-Dragon - Daedalus ". This  Duel Monster Spirit  appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .
