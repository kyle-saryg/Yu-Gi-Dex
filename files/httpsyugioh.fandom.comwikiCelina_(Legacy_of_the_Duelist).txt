 Celina , known as  Serena  in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Celina , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
