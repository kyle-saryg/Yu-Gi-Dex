 Veronica Taylor  is an American voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Taylor's first role in Yu-Gi-Oh! history was portraying  Kenta , a boy that helps  Serenity Wheeler  root for her brother  Joey  as he Duels  Weevil Underwood  during Battle City. Taylor would later go on to portray  Chris  in the  Waking the Dragons  arc.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Taylor provides the voice of  Crystal Beast Amethyst Cat ,  Dark Scorpion - Meanae the Thorn  and  Princess Rose  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . She also did the voice of  Fonda Fontaine , replacing  Bella Hudson .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Taylor's most notable role is portraying  Carly Carmine  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's . She also provides the voices for  Ancient Fairy Dragon  and  Haluna .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , Taylor voices  Jen ,  Ponta ,  Meredith , and Mira Tsukumo.
 
 Other 
 Taylor later relocated to California, where she resides as a California-based voice actress. 
 Veronica became the voice of Setsuna Meioh/Sailor Pluto of  Sailor Moon   and  Sailor Moon Crystal . She also voiced young Speckles in the English dub of  The Dino King . She is also very well known for having done the original voices of Ash Ketchum, May and other roles of  Pokemon
