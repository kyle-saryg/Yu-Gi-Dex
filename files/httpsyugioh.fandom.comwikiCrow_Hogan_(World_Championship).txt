 Crow Hogan  (クロウ・ホーガン ,  Kurou Hōgan ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Crow Hogan , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.

 Unlockables 
 Reverse of Arcadia 
 The player can unlock the  Black Bird Disk  by defeating Crow in 10  Turbo Duels . 
 The player is given the  Security Uniform  by Crow in chapter 2. 
 The player can unlock Crow's  Duel Runner ,  Blackbird  by unlocking every other Duel Runner frame. 
 The player can unlock Crow as a Tag Partner by defeating  Team Shooting Birds  in 3 Duels. 
 The player can partially unlock the  Ruler of Chaos!!   Booster Pack  by defeating Crow 3 times. 
 Over the Nexus 
 The player can partially unlock the  Starstrike Blast  Booster Pack by defeating Crow 3 times in each of  Single Duels ,  Tag Duels  and  Tag Turbo Duels . 
 The player can unlock the Black Bird Disk by defeating Crow in 10 Duels.
