 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Warrior "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ウォリアー」 .   As its English name contains "Warrior", it  may  be treated a member of the "Warrior" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The  Croatian, Greek  and  Thai                      names given are not official.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
This card cannot be  destroyed by battle . When a card on the  field  is  destroyed  by battle or by a  card effect ,  return  this  face-up  card to its  owner 's  hand .

 Esta carta no puede ser destruida en batalla. Cuando una carta en el Campo es destruida en batalla o por un efecto de carta, devuelve esta carta boca arriba a la mano de su propietario.
