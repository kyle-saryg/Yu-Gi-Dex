 Treeborn Frog  is a character version of the card " Treeborn Frog ". It appears briefly in one of the  Duel Monsters Spirit World  dimensions, attacking a group of  Garuda the Wind Spirits  that had been harassing weaker monsters. [1] 
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 Treeborn Frog appears in the video game Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  as a Level Two Theme Duelist. Tt challenges the player to  Special Summon  10 or more times from the  Graveyard  during a  Duel . By beating it, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Three Theme Duelists and a new  Booster Pack .
