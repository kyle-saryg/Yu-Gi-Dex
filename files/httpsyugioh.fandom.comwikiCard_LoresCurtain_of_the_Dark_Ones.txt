 Translated Japanese - Curtain of Black Demon Tribe 
Curtain has made by magician. That increae the power of magicians.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Greek - Αυλαία των Σκοτεινών 
Μια κουρτίνα φτιαγμένη απο ένα μάγο που λέγεται πως ενδυναμώνει τα σκοτεινά τέρατα.
 
 
 In the English version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "A curtain that a spellcaster made, it is said to raise a dark power." 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "On dit que ce rideau fabriqué par un enchanteur invoque une puissance des ténèbres." 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Ein von einem Hexer hergestellter Vorhang, der angeblich die Mächte der Dunkelheit herbeiruft." 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Questo sipario che si dice essere stato creato da un mago incantatore genera una potenza oscura." 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Una cortina hecha por un lanzador de conjuros, se dice que incrementa el poder oscuro."
