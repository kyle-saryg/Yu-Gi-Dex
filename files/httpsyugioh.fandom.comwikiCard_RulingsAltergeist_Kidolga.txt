 OCG Rulings 
 If " Altergeist Kidolga " declared an attack that turn, the monster Special Summoned by its effect can declare an attack even if that ' Altergeist Kidolga " is no longer on the field. During subsequent turns, the monster Special Summoned by this effect will not be able to attack.
