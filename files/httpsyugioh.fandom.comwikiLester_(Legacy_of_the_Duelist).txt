 Lester , known as  Lucciano  (ルチアーノ,  Ruchiāno ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution .. This is a video game depiction of  Lester , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
