 Legendary Duelists 
 Legendary Duelists: Ancient Millennium 
 
 
 
 
 
 Legendary Duelists  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Joey Wheeler ,  Mai Valentine ,  Mako Tsunami ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Nightshroud ,  Tania ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Paradox Brothers ,  Axel Brodie  and  Broder . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Amazoness ", " Cyber ", " Darkness ", " Gearfried ", " Red-Eyes ", " roid " and " Vehicroid " archetypes, as well as the  Chemical ,  with Chain  and " Umi " series.
 Introduces the first support for the " Bonding " and " Cyberdark " archetypes to the  TCG .
