 The  Heartland City  is the fifth arc of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . It covers  Yuya ,  Gong ,  Shay , and  Sylvio  going to the  Xyz Dimension  through a wormhole after a dimensional transport glitch from  Roger 's  dimensional transporter  in order to find  Zuzu  who's been sent to the  Fusion Dimension .
