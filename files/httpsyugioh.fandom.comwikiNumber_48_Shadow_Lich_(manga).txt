When this card is  attacked , would be  destroyed , or would be  banished  by your opponent's card:  Special Summon  as many " Phantom Tokens " ( Rank  3/ ATK  500/ DEF  500) as possible instead, then, until the  End Phase , hide this card among them, with the same name, ATK and DEF as them. You take no  battle damage  from  battles  involving a "Phantom Token". When a "Phantom Token" is destroyed: Inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to its ATK. Once per  turn : You can  activate  this  effect ; this card gains ATK equal to the combined ATK of all "Phantom Tokens" you currently control, until the end of this turn.
