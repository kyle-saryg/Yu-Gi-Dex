 " She's That One Other Nanahoshi " is the seventieth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on October 17, 2021.
 Among the weirdly-dressed members of  Goha 5th Elementary 's Insect Duel Club is  Nanaho Nanahoshi , a girl with a ladybug hat. She seems to know  Yuga .  Swirly  brings her to where Yuga is so that she can pay him a visit. However, when Yuga sees Nanaho, he starts to act strangely...
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Nanaho Nanahoshi 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
