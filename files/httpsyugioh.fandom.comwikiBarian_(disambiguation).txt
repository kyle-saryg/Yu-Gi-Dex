Barian  can refer to:
 
 The  Barian World , a world in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. 
 Barian , the species which lives in that world. 
 Vector , a being initially and mistakenly identified as  Barian . 
 " Barian ", an  archetype  of cards. 
 " Barian's ", another archetype. 
 The  BARian , a bar on Earth visited by the Barians  Girag  and  Alito . 
 Barian's Guardian , a term coined by Vector, in his guise as Ray Shadows.
