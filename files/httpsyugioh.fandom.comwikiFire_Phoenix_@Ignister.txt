 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2+  Cyberse   monsters During  damage calculation , if this card  attacks : You can inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to this card's  ATK , and if you do, your opponent takes no  battle damage  from that battle. If this card is  destroyed  by card  effect : You can destroy 1 monster your opponent  controls . During the next  Standby Phase  after this  Link Summoned  card was destroyed and sent to the  GY : You can  Special Summon  this card from your GY. You can only use each effect of "Fire Phoenix @Ignister"  once per turn .
