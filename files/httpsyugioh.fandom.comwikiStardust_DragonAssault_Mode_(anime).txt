 [  Dragon  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except with its own  effect  or with " Assault Mode Activate ". You can  Tribute  this card to  negate  the  activation  of a  Spell Card ,  Trap Card , or  Effect Monster 's effect, and  destroy  that card. If you negate an effect this way, you can Special Summon this card from your  Graveyard  during the  End Phase . When this card on the field is destroyed, you can Special Summon 1 " Stardust Dragon " from your Graveyard.
