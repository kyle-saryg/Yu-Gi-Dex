 Code of the Duelist  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ), It is the first set in the OCG's  10th series , following  Maximum Crisis [1]  and followed by  Circuit Break .

 Features 
 The first  Booster Pack  in  Series 10  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS . 
 Includes cards used by  Yuya Sakaki ,  Declan Akaba ,  Yuri ,  Z-ARC ,  Yusaku Fujiki ,  George Gore ,  Skye Zaizen ,  Varis , and the  Knights of Hanoi .
 The TCG version includes cards used by  Yusei Fudo ,  Rex Goodwin ,  Kalin Kessler ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Kite Tenjo  and  Yugo . 
 Includes members/support for the " Dark Contract ", " Dark World ", " D/D ", " D/D/D ", " Lightsworn ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Predaplant ", " Saber ", " Supreme King Dragon ", " Traptrix ", and " Trap Hole " archetypes, as well as the  Attribute Booster ,  Captain ,  Dark counterpart  and  Rescue Squad  series.
 The TCG version includes members/support for the " Abyss Actor ", " Ancient Gear ", " Destiny HERO ", " Galaxy ", " Gogogo ", " Infernity ", " Junk ", " Performapal " and " Speedroid " archetypes. 
 Introduces:
 Three new archetypes: " Gouki ", " Trickstar ", " World Chalice " and " World Legacy " archetypes to the OCG/TCG. 
 Two new TCG-only archetypes: " F.A. " and " Vendread ". 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box. The set contains 80 cards, or 103 cards in the  Master Set :
 
 1  Holographic Rare  (Japanese version only) 
 6  Ultimate Rares 
 16  Secret Rares 
 6  Ultra Rares   (also available as  Ultimate Rare  and  Secret Rare ) 
 10  Super Rares   (also available as  Secret Rare ) 
 18  Rares 
 46  Commons 
 In the  TCG , there are 9 cards per pack and 24 packs per box. The set contains 100 cards:
 
 8  Secret Rares 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 14  Super Rares 
 20  Rares 
 48  Commons
