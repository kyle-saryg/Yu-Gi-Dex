 Machina Mayhem Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixteenth deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Warriors' Strike Structure Deck . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Machiners Command  in the  OCG .
 It was reprinted in the  Machine Madness Value Box  in February 28, 2014, which reprinted the cards with text fitting the  Problem-Solving Card Text  standards at the time.

 Features 
 The deck introduces the " Machina "  archetype  for the first time to the  TCG ; it focuses partially on  Union Monsters .
 The "Power Up With Packs!" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Shreddder " and " Machine Assembly Line " from  Absolute Powerforce , " Koa'ki Meiru Powerhand " from  Raging Battle , " Seal of Wickedness " and " Lord British Space Fighter " from  Stardust Overdrive , " Scrubbed Raid ", " Road Synchron " and " Road Warrior " from  Duelist Pack: Yusei 2  and additional copies of " Solidarity ", " Cyber Valley " and " Heavy Mech Support Platform " from  Machina Mayhem Structure Deck . Additionally, various Tuner monsters and Equip Spells are recommended from  Power Tool Dragon 2009 Collectible Tin  in support of its main card, " Power Tool Dragon ", along with " Break! Draw! " from  Absolute Powerforce  (featured in the tin).
