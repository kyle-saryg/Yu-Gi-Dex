 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1  Tuner  + 1+ non-Tuner  monsters During your opponent's  Main Phase , you can ( Quick Effect ): Immediately after this  effect   resolves ,  Synchro Summon  using  materials  you  control , including this card. If this card is in your  GY : You can  target  1 other  Synchro Monster  in your GY;  return  it to the  Extra Deck , and if you do,  Special Summon  this card, but  banish  it when it  leaves the field . You can only use each effect of "Shamisen Samsara Sorrowcat"  once per turn .
