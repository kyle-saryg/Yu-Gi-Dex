 These cards were handed out to members that participated in the Duelist League during 2010. This set of 20 cards were printed in in a  Rarity  that is similar to  Rare , although the text color for this set is; Blue, Bronze, Light Green and Light Blue. This set is followed by  Duelist League 2 participation cards  in 2011.
