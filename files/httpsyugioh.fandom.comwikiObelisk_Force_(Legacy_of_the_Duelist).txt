 Obelisk Force , known as (オベリスク・フォース,  Oberisuku Fōsu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of a member of the  Obelisk Force , a group from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. It also is a video game depiction of one of the members of the  Juvenile Officers , as they are replaced in their duel with  Alexis Rhodes (ARC-V)  with a member of the Obelisk Force. The Obelisk Force member represented is specifically one with a red jewel.
