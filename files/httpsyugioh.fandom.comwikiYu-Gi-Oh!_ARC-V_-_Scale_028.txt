 "The Enemy's Hideout!"  is the twenty-eigth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga .
 This chapter was first printed in Japanese in the January 2018 issue of  V Jump , released on November 21, 2017, and in English in the November 27, 2017 issue of  Weekly Shonen Jump .
 
 Summary 
 With  Isaac  defeated,  Reiji  returns to Earth.  Yuya  and  Yuzu  join him and his associates and travel to their enemy's lair - located at Antarctica itself.
