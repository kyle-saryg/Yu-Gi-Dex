 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] The  controller  of this card  pays  500  Life Points  during each of his/her  Standby Phases  (this is not optional). When this card is  targeted  by the  effect  of a card controlled by your opponent, when  resolving  the effect, roll a six-sided die. If the result is 1, 3, or 6,  negate  the effect and  destroy  the opponent's card.
