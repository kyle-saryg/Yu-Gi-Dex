 Deck Build Pack: Genesis Impactors  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Introduces the " Ki-sikil ", " Lil-la ", " Evil★Twin ", " Live☆Twin " " Drytron " and " Magistus " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Includes new members/support for the " Endymion " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards. The set contains 45 cards.
