 Structure Deck: Blaze of Destruction  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the third deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (eleventh including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Zombie Madness . In the  TCG , it is the third deck.
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Structure Deck: Blaze of Destruction  was first released on April 29, 2006 with  Structure Deck: Fury from the Deep  as  Structure Deck: Special Set 2 , before being released on May 10, 2006.
 
 It's a struggle of the  elements  with the two new  Structure Decks . Control the waves and crush your opponents with " Fury from the Deep " or leave them standing in a cloud of smoke with the  FIRE  monsters of "Blaze of Destruction". The  Structure Decks  are ready to play with out of the box, so you can master their new strategies. Each  Deck  has 40 cards with 1 brand new  Ultra Rare   Monster Card , rulebook, and Dueling guide which assists a Duelist in taking his game to the next level. 
 —  Upper Deck Entertainment

 Features 
 The Deck includes  FIRE  monsters capable of inflicting  effect damage . Featured cards include " Infernal Flame Emperor ", " Solar Flare Dragon " and " Level Limit - Area B ".
