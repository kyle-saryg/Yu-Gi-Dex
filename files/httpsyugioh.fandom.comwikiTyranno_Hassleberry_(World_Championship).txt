 Tyranno Hassleberry , known as  Tyranno Kenzan  (ティラノ剣山,  Tirano Kenzan ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Tyranno Hassleberry , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
