 Chumley Huffington , known as  Hayato Maeda  (前田隼人,  Maeda Hayato ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Chumley Huffington , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
