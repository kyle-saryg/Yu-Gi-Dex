 Effect 
 When exactly 1  monster  your opponent  controls  is  destroyed : You can  activate  the following  effect . ● If the destroyed monster is Level 5 or 6:  Draw  1 card. ● If the destroyed monster is Level 7 or higher: Draw 2 cards.
