 Number Hunters 
 Dragons of Legend 
 
 
 
 
 
 Number Hunters  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game   Booster Pack . It follows the style of the  Hidden Arsenal  packs in terms of rarity distribution and set size.

 Features 
 Contains cards used by  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Shark ,  Trey ,  Quattro ,  Vetrix ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Nistro ,  Dextra ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Alito ,  Fender ,  Carlyle Chesterton ,  Lotus Hanazoe ,  Devon Knox ,  Art Stanley ,  Mayday Walker ,  Brooke Walker ,  Mach ,  Shadow Giant ,  Cologne ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Weevil Underwood  and  Jack Atlas . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Butterspy ", " Chronomaly ", " CXyz ", " Gagaga ", " Gimmick Puppet ",  " Gogogo ", " Heraldry ", " Heroic Challenger ", " Number ", " Photon " and " Zubaba "  archetypes , and the " Overlay " series.
