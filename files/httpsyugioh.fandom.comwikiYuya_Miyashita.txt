 Yuya Miyashita  is a Japanese voice actor and member of the band  RUN&GUN . [1]  He provides the voice of  Yusei Fudo  in the Japanese versions of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's [2]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time . [3]
