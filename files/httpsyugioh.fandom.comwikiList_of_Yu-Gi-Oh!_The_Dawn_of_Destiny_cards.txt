This is a  list of cards in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dawn of Destiny .
 There are a total of 1017 cards that can be won, and are listed below in the order they appear (note that some of the cards' names on this list are different to their  TCG  counterparts).
 The card list in  The Dawn of Destiny  is very similar to that used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  video games; most of the differences are cards that were removed for  The Dawn of Destiny .
