 The  2012 Honduran National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Honduras. It took place in the Hotel Alameda in Tegucigalpa, Honduras on May 27, 2012 [1]  and was won by Alejandro Andres Aguiluz. [2]
