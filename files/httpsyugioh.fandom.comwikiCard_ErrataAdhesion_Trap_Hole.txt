 First erratum 
 
 
 You can only activate this card when your opponent successfully Normal Summons, Flip Summons, or Special Summons a  monster . As long as  that  monster remains  face-up  on the field, the original ATK of  that monster  is halved. 
 You can only activate this card  when your opponent  successfully Normal Summons, Flip Summons, or Special  Summons a  monster(s) .  As long as  the  monster ( s )   remains  on the field,  the  original ATK  of  the monster(s)  is halved.
