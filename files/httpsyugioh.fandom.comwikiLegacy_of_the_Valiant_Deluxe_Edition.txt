 Legacy of the Valiant: Deluxe Edition  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  deluxe edition box. It is the second in the  Deluxe Edition  series.

 Breakdown 
 Each  Legacy of the Valiant: Deluxe Edition  is comprised of:
 
 9  Legacy of the Valiant   Unlimited Edition  Booster Packs 
 2  Ultra Rare  versions of  Common  or  Rare  cards from  Legacy of the Valiant 
 1 pack of 50  card sleeves 
 1 of 2  Super Rare  preview cards from  Primal Origin
