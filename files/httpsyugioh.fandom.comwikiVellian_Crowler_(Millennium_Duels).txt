 Vellian Crowler , known as  Cronos de Medici  (クロノス・デ・メディチ,  Kuronosu de Medichi ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Vellian Crowler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
