 Collection Pack: Duelists of Revolution  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Leon von Schroeder ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Carly Carmine ,  Vizor ,  Shinji Weber  and  Gansley . 
 Includes members/support for the " Number ", " T.G. " and " Vision HERO " archetype. 
 Introduces the " Battlewasp " and " Fortune Fairy " archetype to the OCG. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 46 cards:
 
 1  20th Secret Rare  (1  Extra Secret Rare  in the Korean version) 
 4  Ultra Rares 
 5  Super Rares 
 10  Rares 
 26  Commons 
 In the Japanese version, all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Collector's Rare . 
 In the Korean version, all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Secret Rare .
