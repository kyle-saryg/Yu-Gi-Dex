 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Iron Blacksmith Kotetsu ", " Hidden Armory ", " Armory Call ", " Power Tool Dragon ", " Vylon Cube ", " Vylon Delta " and " Mermail Abyssmegalo ".
 "Armory Call" (or " Swords at Dawn " targeting this card in your Graveyard) can equip this card to a " Mermail " monster in response to your opponent's Spell Card, in order to negate it.
