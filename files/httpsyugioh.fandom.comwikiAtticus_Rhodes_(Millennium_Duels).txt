 Atticus Rhodes , known as  Fubuki Tenjouin  (天上院 吹雪,  Tenjōin Fubuki ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Atticus Rhodes , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
