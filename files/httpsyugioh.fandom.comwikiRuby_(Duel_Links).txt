 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 
 
 
 Ruby  is a  Standard Duelist  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . She only appears in the  ZEXAL  Duel World. She is an opponent and cannot be unlocked as a playable character.

 Dialogue 
 When " Number Possessed Duelist " Ruby starts a Duel, she says "I have so much power! I feel like I can do anything!"
 When she wins the Duel, she says "" 
 When she loses the Duel, she says "...... Umm... What am I doing here?" 
 When " Barian Possessed Duelist " Ruby starts the Duel, she says ""
 When she wins the Duel, she says "" 
 When she loses the Duel, she says "...... Umm... What am I doing here?" 
 When Ruby summons a " Number " card, she says "You're in for it now! Because my Number is here to fight!"
 When she activates the effect of that card, she says "I activate my Number's effect!" 
 When she declares an attack with that card, she says "My Number attacks!" 
 When Ruby activates " Rank-Up-Magic Barian's Force ", she says "Rank-Up-Magic Barian's Force!" followed by "No one can stop the power of the Barians!" 
 Trivia 
 Ruby, along with the  three   other   student  Standard Duelists in ZEXAL World, have specific voice lines when they are possessed by  Numbers  or the  Barians , as shown above. 
 Her design is based on  Sophie Davison .
