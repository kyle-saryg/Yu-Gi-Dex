 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Magician "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 魔 術 師 」 .   As its English name contains "Magician", it  may  be treated a member of the "Magician" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The  Croatian  and  Greek                       names given are not official.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When using this card you  control  as a  Synchro Material  for a " T.G. "  Synchro Monster , you can use "T.G."  monsters  in your  hand  as non- Tuner  Synchro Materials. During the  End Phase , if this card is in the  Graveyard  because it was  destroyed  on the  field  and  sent  there this  turn : You can  add  1 "T.G." monster from your  Deck  to your hand, except "T.G. Cyber Magician".

Durant la End Phase, si cette carte est dans le Cimetière parce qu'elle a été détruite sur le Terrain et y a été envoyée ce tour : vous pouvez ajouter 1 monstre "T.G." ("Magicien Cyber T.G." exclu) depuis votre Deck à votre main.

   5D's Tag Force 5 
 
 
This card cannot be used as a  Synchro Material Monster , except for the  Synchro Summon  of a " Tech Genus "  monster . When using this card as a Synchro Material Monster, the other Synchro Material Monster is 1 monster in your  hand . This card is also treated as a  Machine - Type  monster. When this card on the  field  is  destroyed  and  sent  to the  Graveyard , you can add 1 "Teach Genus Cyber Magician SC-01" from your  Deck  to your hand.
