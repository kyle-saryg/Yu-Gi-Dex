 In the international dub, this monster's outfit has been edited to remove the visibility of her cleavage. 
 When this card was released, it was redrawn with a different shading and background, the flowing of the hair was slightly altered, and it was zoomed out. 
 In the  TCG , this monster's breasts were partially recolored black, slightly reduced in size, and their separation was made less pronounced.
