 Takeshi Maeda  is a Japanese voice actor.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ), he voiced  Valon . [1] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ), he voiced Ryo Marufuji ( Zane Truesdale ). [2]
