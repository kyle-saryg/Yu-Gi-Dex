 Hirutani  is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is the series incarnation of  Hirutani  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . His appearance in the anime was an amalgamation of his two manga appearances.
 Hirutani is the leader of a gang of teenage extortionists that wield super-powered yo-yos. Hirutani tried to force  Katsuya Jonouchi  into joining his gang.
