   Equip 
 
 This card can only be  equipped  to  Warrior - Type   monsters . Increases the  ATK  of the equipped monster by 800 points and decreases the ATK of all  WATER  monsters on the  field  by 500 points. [note 1]

 Notes 
 
 ↑   Despite having the Japanese lore of its  OCG  counterpart , "Lightning Blade" did not function that way in the original series anime. It could be equipped to a  Beast-Warrior -Type monster, and did not decrease the ATK of WATER monsters.
