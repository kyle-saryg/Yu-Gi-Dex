 Card sleeves  (カードプロテクター [Note 1]   kādo purotekutā  "card protector") are protective plastic covers used to prevent cards from getting bent or scratched. [1]  Most card sleeves are made by third-party manufacturers, though  Konami  distributes a few styles of their own sleeves. [2] [3] [4] [5] 
 If a player uses card sleeves, they must all be the same so their cards aren't  marked . [1]  Also, if a player use a card sleeve on their  Extra Deck , it must differ from the  Main Deck . In case of  Side Deck , the card must have the same backing as the respective deck they belong in (Main/Extra).
 In the  OCG , it is possible for a player's  Deck  to contain cards with different  backings . In cases like this, it is mandatory for the player to use sleeves to prevent their cards from being marked. [6] 
 In video games, card sleeves serve no practical purpose and only serve as customizable features.

 These card sleeves were only available during the "Summer Go! Go! Carnival" promotion campaign that was celebrated on 2014.
 
 If you buy 5 packs of  The New Challengers  between July 19 and August 8, you will receive 5 card sleeves of 1 type of the following 3. 
 Dark Magician Girl Dark Magician Rescue Hamster Ghostrick 
 If you buy 5 packs of  The New Challengers  or  Raging Masters  after August 8 (until supplies run out), you will receive 5 card sleeves of 1 type of the following 3. 
 World Championship 2014 Solemn Judgment Black Luster Soldier - Envoy of the Beginning 
 There's a seventh card sleeve design that is available during both periods, but it can only be obtained in some convenience stores.
