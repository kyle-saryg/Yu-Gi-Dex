 Oliver Wyman , regularly credited as  Pete Zarustica , is an American voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment . He has voiced a variety of characters in the English dubbed version of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Wyman's first major role in  Yu-Gi-Oh!  history was voicing  Zigfried von Schroeder . He also voiced  Dr. Alex Brisbane ,  Alexander the Great , an unidentified kid Joey Duels in episode 133, and  Aknadin  within the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Wyman's most notable role was the voice of  Aster Phoenix [1] . He also voiced  Brier ,  Neo-Spacian Aqua Dolphin [1] ,  Freed the Brave Wanderer , and  Guardian of the Labyrinth's  shield within the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , Wyman voiced  Alex .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Wyman reprises the role of Aster [1] , voicing an  alternate universe version  of the character.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 Wyman reprises the role of  Aster Phoenix .
