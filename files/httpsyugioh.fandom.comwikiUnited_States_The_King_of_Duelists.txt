 The  United States The King of Duelists  was a  Duel Monsters  tournament in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime , won by  Maximillion Pegasus , who humiliated  Bandit Keith  in  the final . Its finals took place in the  New York Duel Stadium .
 In the English dub of the anime, the events from this tournament are said to be the Intercontinental Championship.
 This tournament finals are based on an event from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga , where Keith challenged Pegasus to  a Duel .
 
 Events 
 In  the final , Pegasus did not touch any of his cards. He instead performed a  Mind Scan  on Keith, learning all his strategies and then wrote down instructions on how to counter them. He handed them to a child from the audience,  Sam , whom he got to Duel in his place. With the instructions, Sam defeated Keith.
