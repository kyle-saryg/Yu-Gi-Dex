 Aki  (アキ  Aki ) [1]  is a student at  Heartland Academy  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL .

 Design 
 Appearance 
 Aki wears the Heartland Academy girls' uniform (In the dub, her skirt was lengthened). She is fair skinned and has gray blue eyes. She has light blue hair with crown shaped hairpieces.
 
 History 
 Deck 
 Aki has been shown to use " Madolche Chateau ".
