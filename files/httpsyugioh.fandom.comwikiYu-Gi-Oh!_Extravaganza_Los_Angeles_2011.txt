The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Extravaganza Los Angeles 2011  was the first  Yu-Gi-Oh! Extravaganza . It was a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  event which was held in Los Angeles on the weekend January 22–23, 2011. It featured a number of events with various prizes. Each event also granted players participation points and winners prize points. At the end of the second day the four players with the most points and four random players compete for a  Super Rare  set of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series Prize Cards . [1] 
 
 Events 
 The following events took place at the tournament. Winners received both the participation and winning prizes. [1]

 Playoffs 
 There were two playoffs, with four players in each. The first consisted of four selected at random. The second consisted of the four players who had accumulated the most points over the two days. Had there been a tie for points, players would have been chosen at random from those tying. [1] 
 The winners from each, received a set of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series Prize Cards  printed in  Super Rare . [1]
