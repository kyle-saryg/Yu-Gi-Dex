 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " and another "Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King". 
 Use the  Pendulum Effect  of " Abyss Actor - Mellow Madonna " to set up your  Pendulum Scales  and Special Summon two " Abyss Actor " monsters from your hand, including one that is Level 7 or higher. That way, this card can destroy 2 other face-up cards and your opponent cannot respond to it (except with the destroyed cards'  floating effects ).
