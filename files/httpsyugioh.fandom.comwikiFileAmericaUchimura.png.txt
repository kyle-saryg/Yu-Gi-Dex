 America Uchimura , known as  Yonehito Uchimura  ( 内村   米人 ,  Uchimura Yonehito ) in the Japanese version, is a  Ra Yellow  student.
 America appears several times in the anime:
 
 In the 1st season, he watches  Jaden Yuki  and  Dimitri 's Duel. [1] 
 In the 2nd season, he is seen being beaten by  Chazz Princeton  along with  Ishiki Erishi  and another Ra Yellow member when Chazz is in  Sartorius 's  Society of Light . [2] 
 In the 3rd season, he is one of many students who are transported to the second alternate dimension. [3] 
 In the 4th season, he comes to Jaden's Graduation Ceremony after  Nightshroud 's defeat. [4] 
       In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller .     
 America appears in the game  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  as an opponent.
