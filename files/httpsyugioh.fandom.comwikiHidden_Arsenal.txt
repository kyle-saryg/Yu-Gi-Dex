 Features 
 Introduces the " Ice Barrier ", " Mist Valley " and " Worm "  archetypes  to the  TCG  as well as further support for the " Ally of Justice ", " Neos ", " Kuri ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Flamvell " and " X-Saber " cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 foil cards: 1  Secret Rare  and 4  Super Rares  and each box contained 36 packs in the  TCG  and 30 packs in the  OCG .
 This set contains 30 cards, consisting of:
 
 15  Secret Rares
