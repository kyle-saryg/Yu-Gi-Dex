 Goha 7th Elementary  (ゴーハ７ 小   Gōha Nana Shō ) is the elementary school in  Goha City  that  Yuga Ohdo  attends. Like most areas of the city, the laws there are enforced by  Goha Corporation drones , including the lesson times. [1]  The school's anthem is " Goha 7th Elementary School Song ". [2]
