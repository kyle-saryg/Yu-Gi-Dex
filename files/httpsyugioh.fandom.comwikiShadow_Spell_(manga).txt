 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile>  Select  1  face-up   monster  on your opponent's side of the  field . When the selected monster is  destroyed  or removed from the field, this card is also destroyed.
