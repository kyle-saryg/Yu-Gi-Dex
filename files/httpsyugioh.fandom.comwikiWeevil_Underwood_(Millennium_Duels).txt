 Weevil Underwood , known as  Insector Haga  (インセクター羽蛾,  Insekutā Haga ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Weevil Underwood , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
