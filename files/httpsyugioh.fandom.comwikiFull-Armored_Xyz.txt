 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Red "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「レッド」 .   As its English name contains "Red", it  may  be treated a member of the "Red" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Effect 
 Condition 
 Condition 
 Target  1  face-up   Xyz Monster  that was  Special Summoned  to your side of the  field ; once per  turn ,  Xyz Summon  1 Xyz Monster, and if you do,  equip  that target to the Xyz Monster you Xyz Summoned by this  effect . The equipped monster gains  ATK  equal to the ATK of the monster equipped to it by this effect. If the equipped monster would be  destroyed , you can  send  the monster equipped to it with this effect to the  Graveyard  instead.
