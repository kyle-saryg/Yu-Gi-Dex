 Black Rose Dragon  is a character version of the card " Black Rose Dragon ". 
 It appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P044. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 9-4 under Quest mode.
