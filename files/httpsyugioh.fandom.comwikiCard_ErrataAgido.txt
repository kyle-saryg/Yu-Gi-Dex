 First erratum 
 
 
 When this card is destroyed and sent to the Graveyard as a result of battle, roll a six-sided dice. You can Special Summon 1 Fairy-Type monster from your Graveyard whose Level  Stars  is equal to the number rolled. If the result is 6, you can Special Summon a Level 6 or higher monster.
