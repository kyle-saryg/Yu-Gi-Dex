 Stardust Acceleration  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on September 25, 2018.
 
 Description 
 Witness the power of Synchro Summoning! Take flight,  Stardust Dragon ! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Stardust Dragon ",  Yusei Fudo 's ace monster, "Stardust Dragon" is here! When a card or effect that would destroy a card on the field activates, you can  Tribute  this monster to negate and destroy it! If you Tribute "Stardust Dragon" for its effect, it will return to the field at the end of your turn! 
" XX-Saber Hyunlei ", A  Synchro Monster  that can destroy up to 3 Spell/Trap Cards when successfully Synchro Summoned! Get rid of your opponent's Spell/Trap Cards with this monster before an all-out attack! 
" Azure-Eyes Silver Dragon ", This  Synchro Monster  is a powerful ally of " Blue-Eyes White Dragon "! When it is successfully Special Summoned,  Dragon - Type  monsters you control cannot be targeted or destroyed by card effects! It also lets you  Special Summon  1  Normal Monster  from your  Graveyard  at the beginning of your turn, meaning you can bring back " Blue-Eyes White Dragon "! 
" Unexpected Dai ", A  Spell Card  that  Special Summons  1  level  4 or lower  Normal Monster  from your Deck! Use this card to Special Summon  Tuner Monsters  included in this BOX such as " Genex Controller " and " Galaxy Serpent " to set up a Synchro Summon! 
 
A total of 90 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 42  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Stardust Acceleration  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Interceptomato ", " Pinecono ", " Variety Comes Out ", " Shooting Star ", and " Stardust Re-Spark ".
