 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Target  1  monster  on the  field  or in the  GY  to  activate  1 of these  effects ; ●  Attach  it to your  Rank  2  monster  as  material . ● If your opponent  controls  it, place it in your  zone  your  Link -2 monster  points to  and take control of it. ● If it is in the GY,  Special Summon  it to your zone your Link-2 monster points to. You can only activate 1 "Spright Double Cross" per  turn .
