 Premium Pack Vol.9  is a Korean pack, containing a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 40 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 6  Secret Rare 
 8  Ultra Rare 
 26  Super Rare
