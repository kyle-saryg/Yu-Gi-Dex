 Structure Deck: Surge of Radiance  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the eleventh deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (nineteenth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Machine Re-Volt .

 Features 
 The deck features  LIGHT - Attribute   Fairy - Type  monsters, with effects involving  drawing  cards, and increasing a player's  Life Points . Featured cards include " Neo-Parshath, the Sky Paladin ", " Nova Summoner ", and " Voltanis the Adjudicator ".
