 During your  Draw Phase , you can  add  1  random  " Malefic "  Monster Card  from your  Deck  to your  hand  instead of conducting a  normal draw . If you  control  a  face-up  "Malefic" monster, no monsters you control can  attack , except "Malefic" monsters.
