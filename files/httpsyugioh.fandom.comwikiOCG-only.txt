An  OCG -only  card is a card currently released in the  OCG , but not the  TCG .
 OCG -only cards can be eventually released in the  TCG  through a number of different methods, such as in  Booster Packs , as  Shonen Jump promotional cards , in a  Gold Series , or as other types of promotional cards.
