 " I'm Gonna Bash It Into 'Em! " is the fifty-sixth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on July 11, 2021.
 Hearing that a pro baseball team will be established in  Goha City ,  Luke  is determined to become the Base-Stealing King. When Yuga and his friends come to the tryout, they encounter  Yuka Goha , the only girl of the Goha Siblings. She challenges the athletic  Romin  to a Duel. Baseball Rush Duel, play ball!
 
 Featured Duel: Romin Kirishima VS. Yuka Goha 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
