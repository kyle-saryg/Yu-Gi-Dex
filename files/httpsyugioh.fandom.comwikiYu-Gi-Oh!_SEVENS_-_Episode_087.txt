 " Kirishima Melon " is the 87th episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It aired in Japan on February 20, 2022.
 Princess G  challenges  Romin  to a match that'll decide her future path. If Princess G wins, Romin will have to give up on Dueling and pursue music. If Romin wins, she will walk on the path she chose for herself. Romin responds to Princess G's powerful feelings during the  Duel . After their ultimate Guitar Battle Duel, Romin decides the path that she will walk on, and that is...
 
 Featured Duel: Romin Kassidy VS. Princess G 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
