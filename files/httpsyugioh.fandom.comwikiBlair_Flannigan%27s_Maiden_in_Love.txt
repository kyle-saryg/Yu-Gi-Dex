 Blair Flannigan's Maiden in Love
 
 
 Blair Flannigan's Maiden in Love  is an  event  in Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links. When in effect,  Blair Flannigan  appears as a temporary opponent at the Gate, who can be Dueled in order to claim multiple rewards, including the ability to unlock her as a  playable character.
