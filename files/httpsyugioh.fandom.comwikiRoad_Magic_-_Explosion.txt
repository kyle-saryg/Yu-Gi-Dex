 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 [Requirement] : If you  control  a  face-up   monster  ( Level  7 or higher  Spellcaster ),  send  1 monster ( FIRE ) from your  hand  to the  GY . [Effect] : Inflict 400  damage  to your opponent [for each card they control].
