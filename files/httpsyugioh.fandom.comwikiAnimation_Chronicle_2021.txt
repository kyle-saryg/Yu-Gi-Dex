 Animation Chronicle 2021  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Yami Yugi ,  Gansley ,  Crump ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  The D ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Frost ,  Sky Scout ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Tenzen Yanagi ,  Grady ,  Sherry LeBlanc ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Reginald Kastle ,  Mr. Kay ,  Chironex ,  Don Thousand ,  Cologne ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Yugo ,  Yuri ,  Sora Perse ,  Lulu Obsidian ,  Ai  and  Roboppi . 
 Includes new members/support for the " @Ignister ", " roid ", " A.I. ", " Appliancer ", " Cipher ", " Destiny HERO ", " Frightfur "," Ice Barrier ", " Kuriboh ", " Lyrilusc ", " Noble Knight ", " Number ", " Number C ", " Number C39 ", " Penguin ", " Speedroid ", " Utopia ", " Utopic ", " Chaos ", " Numeron ", " Zexal ", " ZW - " and " ZS - " archetypes. 
 Introduces the first support for " Numerounius " archetype to the  OCG . 
 Introduces the " Stealth Kragen " archetype to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 50 cards:
 
 1  Prismatic Secret Rare 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 6  Super Rares 
 38  Commons 
 The Super Rare and 4 of the Ultra Rare cards also come in  Collector's Rares , and all Common cards also come in  Normal Parallel Rares .
