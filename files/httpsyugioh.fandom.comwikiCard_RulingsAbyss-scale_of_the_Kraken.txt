 OCG Rulings 
 The effect that negates effects doesn't start a chain. When a monster the opponent controls activates its effect, at the time that effect will resolve, this effect is applied immediately. [1] 
 After this card is equipped, when a monster the opponent controls activates its effect, this effect must be applied mandatorily when that effect resolves. [1] 
 This effect can be applied during the  Damage Step  and during  Damage Calculation . [1] 
 The effect of " Abyss-scale of the Kraken " is applied even if an effect is activated on the opponent's side of the field during the  Damage Step . Furthermore, the effect is also applied even if an effect is activated on the opponent's side of the field during  Damage Calculation . [2] 
 The effect of " Abyss-scale of the Kraken " that negates an effect activated on the opponent's side of the field and then sends itself to the  Graveyard  doesn't start a chain. When an effect activated on the opponent's side of the field would be applied, its effect is applied immediately. [3]
