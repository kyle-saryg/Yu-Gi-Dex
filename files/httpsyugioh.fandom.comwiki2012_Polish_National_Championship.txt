 The  2012 Poland National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Poland. It took place in Łódź on the weekend, June 16–17, and was won by Krzysztof Duda. The tournament was attended by 37 people, 8 of whom got to qualify for the World Championship Qualifier continentals that year. [2] 
 The entry fee was 40  zł  for the main event. [1]
