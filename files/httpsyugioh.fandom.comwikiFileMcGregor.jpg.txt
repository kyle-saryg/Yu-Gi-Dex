 Detective Paul McGregor  is an  English  Duelist who dresses like  Sherlock Holmes . He beats  Shane Jordan  in Round 1 but loses to  Balfry Ginger  in Round 2 at the Music Hall, even though he claimed that he was "clued in" on Balfry's strategy at the beginning of their Duel.
