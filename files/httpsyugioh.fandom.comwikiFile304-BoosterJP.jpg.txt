 Power of the Guardian  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the fourth set in  Series 3 .
 In other languages, it is amalgamated with  Threat of the Dark Demon World  to form the contents of  Dark Crisis .

 Features 
 The set introduces the " Guardian " monsters and a contains support for  Equip Spell Cards  to back them up. It contains numerous cards from the  Virtual World  arc and a few from  Battle City . It also reprints " Black Luster Soldier " as an  Ultimate Rare .
 Highly sought after cards include " D.D. Warrior Lady ", " Butterfly Dagger - Elma " and " Skill Drain ".
 Yami Yugi 's appearance on the cover of this booster pack is taken from the omake at the end of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 126: " Awaken the Gods!! "  (also present on the title page of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 130: " Ultimate Summon!! " ).
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards and each box contains 30 packs.
