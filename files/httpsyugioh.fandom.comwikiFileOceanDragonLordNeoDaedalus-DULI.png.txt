 Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus  is a character version of the card " Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus ". This  Duel Monster Spirit  appears in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  series of video games and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .

 It appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , this monster and  Abyss Soldier  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents, by having at least 10000  DP .

 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator 
 Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus uses an " A Legendary Ocean " Deck titled, "Do the Daedalus".
 Many of its monsters effects revolve around "A Legendary Ocean", particularly " Levia-Dragon - Daedalus " and " Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus ", who are capable of clearing the field.
 " Gravity Bind " and " Level Limit - Area B " block the opponent's  Level  4 or higher monsters from attacking, while "A Legendary Ocean" downgrades Daedalus' monsters' Levels, allowing them to attack.
