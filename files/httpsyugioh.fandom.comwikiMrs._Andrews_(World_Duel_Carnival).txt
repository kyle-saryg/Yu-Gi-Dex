 Mrs. Andrews , known as  Mrs. Okudaira  in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival . This is a video game depiction of  Mrs. Andrews , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
