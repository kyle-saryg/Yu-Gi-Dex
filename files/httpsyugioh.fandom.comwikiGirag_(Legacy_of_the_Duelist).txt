 Girag  (ギラグ  Giragu ), known as  Souhachi Kiraku  (喜楽 壮八  Kiraku Sōhachi ) in his past life is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Girag , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
