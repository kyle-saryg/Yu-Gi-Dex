 Yu-Gi-Oh! Volume 31 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh!  Volume 31 promotional card  is a " Judgment of Pharaoh ", included in Volume 31 of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga. The English equivalent of this volume is  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist   Volume 24 , although the English version did not contain this promotional card.
