 Batteryman D  is a character version of the card, " Batteryman D ". 
 Batteryman D appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , it appears as a  Duel Monster Spirit . By beating it a few times, it can be unlocked for use by the player. It can be found at the Power Plant.
 In  World Championship 2007 , it appears as a Level One Limited Duelist and it challenges the player to a 1000  Life Points   duel . By beating it, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Two Limited Duelists and a new booster pack.
 
 Ability 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , Batteryman D has the ability to allow the player to get unique cards easier. To improve this ability, the player has to level up the spirit by  Summoning  the corresponding card in a Duel and winning.
 
 Deck 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , Batteryman D play a  Zombie  Deck titled, "Super Full Energy". In  World Championship 2007 , he play a  Beatdown  Deck titled, "A Sudden Snap".
