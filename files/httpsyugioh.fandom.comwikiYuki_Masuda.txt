 Yuki Masuda  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices Daichi Misawa ( Bastion Misawa ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ). [1]  and  Breo  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's . [2]
