 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 In  episode 73 ,  Kite Tenjo  uses this card during his Duel against  Yuma Tsukumo . He uses this card to  Special Summon  " Daybreaker " since he controlled no monsters at that time. Kite then activates the effect of "Daybreaker" to Special Summon another "Daybreaker". 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 In  chapter 21 , this card is seen in  Kaito Tenjo 's hand during his duel against  Fusion Mask .
