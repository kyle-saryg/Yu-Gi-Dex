 Translated Japanese - Beheading Beautiful Woman 
Contrary to her good looks, a woman has beheaded many by katana.
 
 
 
 Greek - Όμορφη Κεφαλοκυνηγός 
Ένα βίαιο πλάσμα που έχει αποκεφαλίσει πολλά εχθρικά τέρατα.
 
 
 
 Greek - Όμορφη Κεφαλοκυνηγός  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards  lore) 
 Μια γοητευτική Γιαπωνέζα κοπέλα που φορά κιμονό. Παρά την ομορφιά της, η χατζάρα της έχει κόψεί πολλά κεφάλια. 
 
 
 In the French version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Créature redoutable ayant décapité de nombreux monstres ennemis." 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Eine bösartige Kreatur, die schon zahllose feindliche Monster geköpft hat." 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Creatura feroce che ha decapitato moltissimi mostri avversari." 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Una criatura maligna que ha decapitado a numerosos monstruos enemigos."
