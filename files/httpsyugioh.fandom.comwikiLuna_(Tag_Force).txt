 Luna , known as  Luca  or  Ruka  ( 龍 可 ,  Ruka ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's   Tag Force  video games. This is a video game version of the anime character,  Luna .

 Unlockables 
 Tag Force 4 
 
 By completing Luna's storyline, the player receives three copies of the card, " Ancient Fairy Dragon " and unlocks  Devack  as a Tag Partner. 
 The player can unlock the packs,  Mark of the Red Dragon ,  Bound to the Mortal World  and  Joker's Full House , by entering the shop with Luna.
