 Appliancer Reduce 
 
 Japanese:   機塊 リデュース 
 Kana:   きかい リデュース 
 Romaji:   Kikai Ridyūsu 
 Translated:  Appliance Reduce 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 If you  control  an " Appliancer "  Link Monster :  Banish  as many  monsters  your opponent controls as possible that were  Special Summoned  this turn, then  draw  cards equal to the number of "Appliancer" Link Monsters you control with different names. During  damage calculation , if an opponent's monster  attacks : You can banish this card from your  GY ; any  battle damage  you take from that  battle  is halved. You can only  activate  1 "Appliancer Reduce" per  turn .
