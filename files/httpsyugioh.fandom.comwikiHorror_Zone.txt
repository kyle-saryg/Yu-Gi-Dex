 The  Horror Zone  was the second stage of  Death-T . It comprised three games.
 The first game was the  Electric Chair Ride , known as the  Electric Chair Ride of Death  in the Japanese version. While playing this, the group were transported to the  Murderer's Mansion .
 The second game was the  bllood puzzle  riddle played inside the Mansion, in order to find an exit.
 The third game was the  deathmatch  with the  Chopman  played in the basement.
