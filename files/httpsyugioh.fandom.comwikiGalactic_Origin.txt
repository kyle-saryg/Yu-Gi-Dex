 Galactic Origin  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on November 7, 2017, 9:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Wielding the powers of the forest and the ocean, the defender of earth has arrived! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Elemental HERO Terra Firma ," "Secrets Guards of the Ice Barrier" An Elemental HERO Fusion Monster has arrived with its power of earth! It has an effect that increases it ATK by Tributing other " Elemental HERO " monsters. 
" Exiled Force ," while this monster has a low ATK and DEF, it has an effect that destroys another monster on the field by banishing itself. It's a useful effect that is easy to use, but keep in mind that you will be losing a monster on your side of the field as well. 
" Cosmic Cyclone ," a Quick Spell Card that can banish 1 Spell/Trap Card on the field. It can also banish a Set card, so try banishing your Opponents card at the end of the turn it is Set! 
" First-Aid Squad ," A Trap Card with the effect that adds 1 Normal Monster Card from the Graveyard to your hand, as well as an effect that Special Summons it from the Graveyard as a monster when a Normal Monster is destroyed in battle. Even if the Normal Monster you added to your hand gets destroyed in battle, you can use the second effect to continue fighting. 
A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Galactic Origin  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
