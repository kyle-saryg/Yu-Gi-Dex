 Limited Edition 10: Chaos Black  is one of the two packs that make up  Limited Edition 10 . It was released alongside  Limited Edition 10: Holy White . 
 The pack contains cards used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set consists of 4 cards: 4  Ultra Rares .
