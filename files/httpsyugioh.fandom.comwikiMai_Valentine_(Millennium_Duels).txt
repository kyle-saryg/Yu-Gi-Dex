 Mai Valentine , known as  Mai Kujaku  (孔雀舞,  Kujaku Mai ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Mai Valentine , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
