      Saiko Island.     
 Saiko Island  is an island located in the northwest of  Satellite  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . Uninhabited, the island holds traces of many ancient  Duels . The finals of the  D1 Grand Prix  were held here. [1]  Throughout the island, there are 12  Duel Zodiacs , each of which harbor a  Star Ticket . The  Duel Gate  is also located on this island.
