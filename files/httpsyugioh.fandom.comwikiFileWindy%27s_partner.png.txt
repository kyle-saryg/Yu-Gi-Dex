 Windy's partner  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. He is one of the victims of the  Hanoi Project  and the human that  Windy  was based on. He is the only victim of the Lost Incident whose status varies in different dubs: in the Japanese version his current status is unknown, while in the English version he "isn't a part of this world anymore" (according to Windy), implying that he is deceased.
 
 Biography 
 History 
 Windy's partner was one of the six children that got kidnapped and used as a test subject to create the  Ignis  during the events of the  Hanoi Project . [1] [2] 
 
       Windy injures his own partner.     
 Years after the Lost Incident, he was involved in a reckless driving accident caused by his corrupted Ignis  Windy , [3]  on the night of the red moon. [2]
