 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1  Tuner Synchro Monster  + 1+ non-Tuner  monsters Must be either  Synchro Summoned , or  Special Summoned  by  banishing  1 Tuner Synchro Monster and 1 " Black-Winged Dragon " from your  field  and/or  GY . Each time your opponent  activates  a  monster effect , place 1  Black Feather Counter  on this card, and if you do, inflict 700  damage  to them. During your opponent's  turn  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Tribute  this card with 4 or more Black Feather Counters;  destroy  all cards on the field.
