 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Beast ,  Beast-Warrior , and/or  Winged Beast   monsters During your  Main Phase : You can  Special Summon  1  Level  4 or lower Beast, Beast-Warrior, or Winged Beast monster from your  hand , also you cannot use monsters as  Link Material  for the rest of this  turn , except Beast, Beast-Warrior, and Winged Beast monsters. If this card is  sent  to the  GY : You can  draw  1 card, then place 1 card from your hand on the bottom of the  Deck . You can only use each  effect  of "Tri-Brigade Ferrijit the Barren Blossom"  once per turn .
