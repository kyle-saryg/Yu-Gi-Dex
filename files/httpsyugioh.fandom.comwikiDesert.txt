 Desert , known as  Wasteland  in the English manga and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses , is a field  terrain  that was shown in  Duelist Kingdom . It was used by  Yami Yugi  to power-up his monsters like " Mammoth Graveyard ".
 In early video games, the field can be changed to Desert by activating " Wasteland ", which grants the  ATK  and  DEF  changing effects of "Wasteland" to the affected area.
