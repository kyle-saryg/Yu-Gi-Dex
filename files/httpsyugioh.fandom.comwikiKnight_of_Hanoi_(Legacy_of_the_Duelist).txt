 Knight of Hanoi , known as (ハノイの 騎 士   Hanoi no Kishi ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of a member of the  Knights of Hanoi , a group from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
