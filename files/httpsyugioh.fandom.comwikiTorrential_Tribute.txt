   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
A  trap card  that has dire consequences to anyone that trips it. If  attacked , it  destroys  all  monsters  on the foe's  field . 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
You can  activate  this card when a  monster  is  Summoned  (including  Flip Summon  and  Special Summon ).  Destroy  all monsters on the  field .
