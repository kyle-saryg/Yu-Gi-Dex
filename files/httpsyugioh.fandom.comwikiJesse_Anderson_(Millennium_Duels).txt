 Jesse Anderson , known as  Johan Anderson  (エド・フェニックス,  Yohan Anderusen ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Jesse Anderson , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
