 Monster Cannon モンスター・キャノン 
 
 Japanese:   モンスター・キャノン 
 Romaji:   Monsutā Kyanon 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Type
 
 
 Item 
 
 Level
 
 
 4  
 
 
 When a  monster  passes through this  item , the monster's  controller  can  sacrifice  1 of their own monsters to  destroy  1 opponent's monster in the dungeon. Then, this item is also destroyed.
