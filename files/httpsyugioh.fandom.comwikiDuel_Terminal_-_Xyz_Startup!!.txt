 Duel Terminal - Xyz Startup!!  is fourth installment of the  OCG 's  Duel Terminal  series, following  Duel Terminal - Judgment of Omega!! , and followed by  Duel Terminal - Sacred Star Knights!! .
 
 It also includes further support for known archetypes. 
 Introduces the new  Xyz Monsters  and their game mechanic.
 This is where  Synchro Monsters  begin to pass on the torch to  Xyz Monsters , meaning archetypes with Synchro mechanics, such as " Gusto " and " Laval ", will later instead use Xyz Monsters. 
 Introduces  Odion Ishtar ,  Amnael ,  Antinomy ,  Sherry Leblanc ,  Yuma Tsukumo  and  Reginald Kastle  as duelable opponents. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards (consisting of 30 New Cards and 20 Reprint Cards), or 54 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares  (all available as Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares) 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 10  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 30  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
