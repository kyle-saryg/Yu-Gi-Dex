 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 139 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  uses this card during the  Lancers' duel against   Z-ARC . He activates this card to set " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King " from his Graveyard by  Tributing  " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady ". After the effect of "Rise of the Abyss King" fails, Sylvio uses this card's effect to Set "Rise of the Abyss King" from his Graveyard again, by Tributing " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ". This card remains on the field until Sylvio is removed from the duel.
