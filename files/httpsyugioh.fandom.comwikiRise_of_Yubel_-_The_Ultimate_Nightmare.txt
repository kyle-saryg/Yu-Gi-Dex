 Rise of Yubel - The Ultimate Nightmare
 
 
 Rise of Yubel - The Ultimate Nightmare  is an  event  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . When in effect,  Yubel  appears as a temporary opponent at the Gate, who can be Dueled in order to claim multiple rewards, including the ability to unlock her as a playable character.

 Event details 
 The player must be at Stage 4 or higher. 
 Collect Shattering Hearts by defeating Duelist in Duel World and Ranked Duels. 
 Use Shattering Hearts at the Gate to Duel Yubel.
