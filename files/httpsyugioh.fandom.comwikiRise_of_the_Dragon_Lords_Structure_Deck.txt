 Rise of the Dragon Lords Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the eleventh deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Machine Re-Volt . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Revival of the Great Dragon  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 Until recently,  Structure Decks  have been limited to only utilize either  Attribute  or  Type . This new  Deck  consists of theme and strategy; it focuses on summoning  Dragon - Type   monsters  from the  Graveyard . The Deck features 7 new cards.
