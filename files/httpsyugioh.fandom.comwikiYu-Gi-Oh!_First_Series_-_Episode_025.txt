 Summary 
 Yugi  and his friends meet  Ryo Bakura , a gentle new student with an interest in role-playing games. Little do they know the danger that dwells within him... Bakura's  Millennium Ring  contains an evil spirit whose main goal is to collect all of the  Millennium Items . He also extracts people's souls and traps them inside miniature figurines.
 One day, Yugi and his friends come to Bakura's house so they can play  Monster World  together. They are welcomed by  Dark Bakura , who traps Yugi and his friends inside a  Shadow Game  so he can steal Yugi's  Millennium Puzzle . However,  Dark Yugi  emerges to challenge him...
