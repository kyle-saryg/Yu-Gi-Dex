 Jack Atlas  (ジャック・アトラス,  Jakku Atorasu ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Jack Atlas , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock Jack and his Deck recipe in the Free Duel Mode if they defeat him in the 5D's cup in both Normal and Expert Modes. 
 If the player uses " Red Dragon Archfiend " against Jack, they unlock the "Back at Ya" achievement.
