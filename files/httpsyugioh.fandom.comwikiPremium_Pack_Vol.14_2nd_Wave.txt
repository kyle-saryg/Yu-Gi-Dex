 Premium Pack Vol.14 2nd Wave  is a pack in the Korean  OCG , containing most of the cards that were released in the Japanese  Promotion Pack 2019 ,  Jump Festa Limited Pack 2019 ,  20th Anniversary Legendary Dragons  and most of the missing  V Jump promotional cards  up to date. As well as several other Japanese  OCG  promos, that were previously unreleased in Korean. Plus a single reprint card  Hero Spirit , which had a previous Korean printing, but a very limited one.
