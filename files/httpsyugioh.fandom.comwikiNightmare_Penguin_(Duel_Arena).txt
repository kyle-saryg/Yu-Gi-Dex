 Nightmare Penguin  is a character version of the card " Nightmare Penguin ". 
 He appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. His partner ID is P068. He also appears as an opponent at Stage 12-2 under Quest mode.

 Decks 
 Partner 
 An  Xyz  Deck built around  monsters  with the "Penguin" archetype. Use the  effect  that  returns  your opponent's cards to their  hand  to completely expose their  field . Use the effect of " The Great Emperor Penguin " to instantly gather your "Penguin" cards for an Xyz Summon!

 Trivia 
 Each opponent of Stage 12 is based on a member of  the Big Five . This character is based on  Crump , who used its  card counterpart  as his  Deck Master  in the  Virtual World  arc. This character's quote is a reference to Crump's dream of building a penguin theme park before  Seto Kaiba  rejected the idea.
