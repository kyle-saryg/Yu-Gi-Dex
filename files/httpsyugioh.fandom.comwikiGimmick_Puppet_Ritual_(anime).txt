Main card page: " Puppet Ritual " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Gimmick Puppet "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ギミック・パペット」 .   As its English name contains "Gimmick Puppet", it  may  be treated a member of the "Gimmick Puppet" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
