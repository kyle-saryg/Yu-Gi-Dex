 Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time Movie Pack , known in Japan as  Yu-Gi-Oh! Super Fusion! Bonds That Transcend Time Movie Pack , is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features
