 Features 
 3 cards per Booster Pack, 50 packs per display. 
 50 different cards (all reprints):
 38 cards from the  ZEXAL  anime (each card appears in at least one episode). 
 12 legacy cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game ' s rich past, such as " Odin, Father of the Aesir " and " Thor, Lord of the Aesir ". 
 Includes cards used by  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Reginald Kastle ,  Bronk Stone ,  Cody Callus ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Lillybot ,  Nistro ,  Dextra ,  Cameron Clix ,  Charlie McCay ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Paradox ,  Halldor ,  Dragan  and  Broder . 
 Breakdown 
 All 50 cards are available in 2 Versions,  Common  and  Starfoil . Each  Booster  contains 2 Common cards and 1 Starfoil card.
