 Marik Ishtar  (マリク・イシュタール,  Mariku Ishutāru ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Marik Ishtar , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
