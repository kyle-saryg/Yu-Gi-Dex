 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Χαώδης Βασιλιάς Καρχαρίας ") is an exact translation of the TCG name of this card in Greek. A possible translation of the OCG name (" Deep-Sea King Devil Shark ") could be " Διαβολικός Καρχαρίας Βασιλιάς του Βυθού ".
