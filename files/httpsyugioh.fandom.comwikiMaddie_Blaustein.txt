 Madeleine Joan Blaustein  (commonly credited as  Maddie Blaustein , formerly credited as  Addie Blaustein ) was an American voice actress for the  English dubbed version of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series. She passed away on December 11, 2008, in her sleep from a short illness at the age of 48. [3]

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 In the  second series anime,  Blaustein's first and most notable role was the voice of  Dr. Solomon Muto . She also voiced  Shimon Muran  and  Zygor  within the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , Blaustein's most notable role was the voice of  Sartorius  during season 2. She also reprised her role of  Solomon Muto  during that same season. She also voiced  Wheeler's doctor ,  Taiyou Torimaki , and  Kozaky  within the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , Blaustein voiced  Larry .
