 Biography 
 After defeating  Shark  and winning the  Team Duel Tournament ,  Yuma , along with  his team , gain a club room for them. However, when they get to the room, they find Raizo, who claims that room to be his. He and Yuma then begin a  Duel  for the room, with Yuma defeating Raizo. [1] 
 Later,  Kento Naito  reveals that the room originally belonged to him and the Fencing Club, but Raizo took the room to himself by force. [2]
