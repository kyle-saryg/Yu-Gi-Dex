 Trigger-like 
 If a " Blackwing "  Synchro Monster (s) you  control  is  destroyed :  Target  1 of those  monsters ;  Special Summon  it, but it has its  effects   negated . During the  Battle Step  or  Damage Step , when a "Blackwing" monster you control  attacks : You can  banish  this card from your  Graveyard ; until the end of the Damage Step, all  Spell Cards  your opponent controls have their effects negated, also they cannot  activate  Spell Cards.
