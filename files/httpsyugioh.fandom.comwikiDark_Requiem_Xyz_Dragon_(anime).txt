 2  Level  5  monsters 
 If this card has a " Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon " as  Xyz Material , it gains these  effects . ●  Once per turn : You can  detach  1 Xyz Material from this card, then  target  1 Level 5 or higher monster your opponent  controls ; until the end of this  turn , that monster's ATK becomes 0, and if you do, this card gains ATK equal to its  original ATK .  ● During either player's turn, if a card or effect is  activated  that would  destroy  a card(s): You can detach 1 Xyz Material from this card;  Special Summon  1  Xyz Monster  from your  Graveyard , and if you do,  negate  its effects.
