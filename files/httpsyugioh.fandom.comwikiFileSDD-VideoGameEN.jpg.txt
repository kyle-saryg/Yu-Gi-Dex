 Yu-Gi-Oh! Worldwide Edition - Stairway to the Destined Duel - Bundles 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Worldwide Edition: Stairway to the Destined Duel  promotional cards  are a set of 3 cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Worldwide Edition: Stairway to the Destined Duel .
 
 Breakdown 
 In North American English:
 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares :
 2  Effect Monsters 
 1  Spell Card 
 In European English, French, and Italian:
 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares :
 1  Normal Monster 
 1  Spell Card 
 1  Trap Card
