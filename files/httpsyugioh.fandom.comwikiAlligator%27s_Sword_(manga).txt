Main card page: " Alligator's Sword " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Alligator "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「アリゲーター」 .   As its English name contains "Alligator", it  may  be treated a member of the "Alligator" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
