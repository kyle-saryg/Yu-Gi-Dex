 Premium Pack Vol.13  is a Korean pack, containing a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean.

 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards. Each box contains 15 packs. The set contains 55 cards. There are 126 cards in the  Master Set .
 
 2  Ultimate Rares 
 16  Ultra Rares  also available as  Secret Rares 
 37  Super Rares 
 All the cards are also available as  Normal Parallel Rares
