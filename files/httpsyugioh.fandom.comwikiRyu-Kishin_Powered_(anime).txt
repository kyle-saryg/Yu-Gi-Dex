Main card page: " Ryu-Kishin Powered " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Red "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「レッド」 .   As its English name contains "Red", it  may  be treated a member of the "Red" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
