 Dark Magician Girl , known as  Black Magician Girl  in the Japanese version, is a  Duel Spirit  version of the card " Dark Magician Girl ". This is a video game version of  Dark Magician Girl , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Dark Magician Girl appears during the festival event, and can be dueled by  the player . After the duel, she can be found behind the waterfall in the forest at any point of Part 1, and can be selected as a partner once the player gives her a  Golden Eggwich . She does not otherwise participate in the Tag Force tournament.
 
 
 Tag Force 2 
 Dark Magician Girl duels against the player in the first story event for every Tier 1 character.
 
 Decks 
 Dark Magician Girl uses  Spellcaster  Decks  that feature  her own card  and  her master's .
