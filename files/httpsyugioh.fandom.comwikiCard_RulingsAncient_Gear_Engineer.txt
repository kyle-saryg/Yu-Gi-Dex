 OCG Rulings 
 The effect " Negate any Trap effects that target this card, and if you do, destroy that Trap Card. " is a  Continuous Effect . [1] 
 The effect " If this card attacks, your opponent cannot activate any Spell/Trap Cards until the end of the Damage Step. " is a  Continuous Effect . [1] 
 The effect " At the end of the Damage Step, if this card attacked: Target 1 Spell/Trap Card your opponent controls; destroy that target. " is a  Trigger Effect . (It targets 1 Spell/Trap Card your opponent controls. It is always activated at the end of the  Damage Step  in which " Ancient Gear Engineer " attacked.) [1] 
 Even if " Ancient Gear Engineer " attacks and is destroyed by battle, the effect " Target 1 Spell/Trap Card your opponent controls; destroy that target. " is activated at the end of the  Damage Step . [1] 
 The effect " Target 1 Spell/Trap Card your opponent controls; destroy that target. " is activated at the end of the Damage Step in which " Ancient Gear Engineer " attacked. However, if " Ancient Gear Engineer " attacks a face-down Defense Position " Shaddoll Squamata ", and " Ancient Gear Engineer " is destroyed and sent to the Graveyard by the effect of " Shaddoll Squamata " after damage calculation, then the effect " Target 1 Spell/Trap Card your opponent controls; destroy that target. " is not activated. [2] 
 Previously Official Rulings 
   These TCG rulings were issued by  Upper Deck Entertainment  and have since been deemed unofficial by Konami. [3]  They were previously considered official and can still be correct, as long as they follow the official gameplay rules, or unless Konami has issued a ruling that says otherwise. References:  [4] [5] [6] [7] [8] [9] [10] [11] [12] 
 
 " Ancient Gear Engineer 's" effect  activates  at the end of the  Damage Step  (when monsters  destroyed by battle  are sent to the Graveyard). If it is  destroyed by battle , or removed from the field by a card effect before then, its effect will not  activate .
