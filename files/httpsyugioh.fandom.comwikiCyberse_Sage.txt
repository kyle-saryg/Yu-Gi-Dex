 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  Ritual Summon  this card with " Cynet Ritual ". During your  Main Phase : You can  Fusion Summon  1  Fusion Monster  from your  Extra Deck , by  banishing  Fusion Materials mentioned on it from your  field  and/or  GY , including a  Cyberse  monster. If this card is  sent  to the  GY  as  Synchro Material : You can  target  1 Cyberse monster or 1  Ritual Spell  in your GY;  add  it to your  hand . You can only use each  effect  of "Cyberse Sage"  once per turn .
