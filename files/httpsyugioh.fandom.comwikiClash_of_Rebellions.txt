 Clash of Rebellions  (クラッシュ・オブ・リベリオン  Kurasshu Obu Riberion ) is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fifth set in the  OCG ' s  9th series , following  Crossed Souls , and followed by  Dimension of Chaos .

 Features 
 Introduces cards used by  Yuya Sakaki ,  Gong Strong ,  Zuzu Boyle ,  Sora Perse ,  Declan Akaba ,  Julia Krystal ,  Shay Obsidian , and  Dennis McField .
 The  TCG  version also includes cards used by  Sylvio Sawatari  and  Yugo . 
 Includes members/support for the " Archfiend ", " Cyber Dragon ", " D/D ", " Fluffal ", " Gem-Knight ", " Infernoid ", " Magician ", " Melodious ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Performapal ", " Raidraptor ", " Rank-Up-Magic ", " Red-Eyes ", " Toon ", and " Void " archetypes.
 The  OCG  version includes new members/support for the " Aether ", " Empowered Warrior ", " Geargia ", and " Lightsworn " archetypes. 
 The  TCG  version includes a new member of the " Draconia " series. 
 Introduces the " Aroma ", " Dracoslayer ", " Igknight ", and " Performage " archetypes.
 The  TCG  version introduces the " Kozmo " and " Kaiju " archetypes. 
 The Korean  OCG  version introduces the " Paleozoic " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 In the Japanese  OCG , there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box. The set contains 90 cards, or 113 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 1  Holographic Rare  (also available as Ultra Rare, Secret Rare, and Ultimate Rare) 
 6  Ultimate Rares 
 16  Secret Rares 
 6  Ultra Rares  (all available as Secret Rares and Ultimate Rares) 
 10  Super Rares  (all available as Secret Rares) 
 20  Rares 
 54  Commons 
 In the  TCG , there are 9 cards per pack, 24 booster packs per box, and 12 display boxes per case. The set contains 100 cards, or 106 in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 1  Ghost Rare 
 5  Ultimate Rares 
 8  Secret Rares 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 14  Super Rares 
 20  Rares 
 48  Commons
