This Property entry details every card that  Special Summons  a monster as part of a  card effect  and treats this Special Summon as a  Synchro Summon .
 Since the Special Summon occurs during the resolution of the card effect, it cannot be  negated . Because it is a Special Summon treated as Synchro Summon, rather than a Synchro Summon, it does not use  Synchro Materials .
