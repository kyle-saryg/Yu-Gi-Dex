 Pikeru and Friends 
 
 White Magician Pikeru  is a character version of the card, " White Magician Pikeru ". She appears in several Yu-Gi-Oh!  video games .
 In  GX Spirit Caller , she appears as a  Duel Monster Spirit . By beating her a few times, she can be unlocked for use by the player. She can be found at the  Abandoned Dorm ,  Dried Well , and Coliseum.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , the player first encounters Pikeru in the World of Grace, where she asks for a "cute" card. (Cards that qualify include  Kuriboh ,  Baby Dragon , and  Dark Magician Girl .) If the player gives her a card she likes, the player is then able to duel  Ebon Magician Curran  on the same map. In order to duel Curran again, the player must find Pikeru again and give her another card. Repeating this process five times unlocks Pikeru as an opponent in Free Duel Mode.
 
 Ability 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , Pikeru has the ability to allow the player get a wonderful encounter. To improve this ability, the player has to level up the spirit by  Summoning  the corresponding card in a Duel and winning.
