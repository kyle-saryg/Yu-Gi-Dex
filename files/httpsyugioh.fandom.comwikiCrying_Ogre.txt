 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] During your opponent's  turn , when your opponent  declares  a second  direct attack  during the same  Battle Phase , you can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . When you do, this card's  ATK  and  DEF  becomes equal to the amount of  Battle Damage  you took from that direct attack. The  monster  that declared the second direct attack must then  attack  this card.
