 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  4  monsters If this card is  Special Summoned : You can  target  1 " Number "  Xyz Monster  in your  GY , except "Number 39: Utopia Rising"; Special Summon it in  Defense Position , then you can  attach  all of this card's  material  to it. If you  Xyz Summon : You can Special Summon this card from the GY, but it  banish  it when it  leaves the field . You can only use each effect of "Number 39: Utopia Rising"  once per turn .
