 Condition 
 [  Cyberse  /  Effect  ] If a  Cyberse   monster  you  control  would be used as  Link Material  for a " Code Talker " monster, this card in your  hand  can also be used as material. If this card is  sent  from the hand or  field  to the GY as material for the  Link Summon  of a "Code Talker" monster: You can either  add  1 "Cynet"  Spell / Trap  from your  Deck  to your hand, or, if this card on the field was used as material, you can add 1  Level  4 Cyberse monster instead. You can only use each  effect  of "Micro Coder"  once per turn .
