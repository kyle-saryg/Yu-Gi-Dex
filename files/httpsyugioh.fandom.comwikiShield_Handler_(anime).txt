 When a  card effect  is  activated  that would  destroy  a  monster(s)  on the  field :  Target  1  Link Monster  on each player's field;  negate  the effects of your opponent's target, and if you do,  equip  this card to your target. The equipped monster cannot be destroyed by card effects.
