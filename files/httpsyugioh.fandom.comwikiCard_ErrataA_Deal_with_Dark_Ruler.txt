 You can only activate this card during a turn in which a  monster with  8 or  more Level stars  on your side of the field was sent to the Graveyard. Special Summon 1 "Berserk Dragon" from your hand or Deck. 
 You can only activate this card during a turn in which  a  Level  8 or  higher monster   on  your  side of the field  was sent to the Graveyard .  Special Summon 1 "Berserk Dragon" from your hand or Deck. 
 (This card is always treated as an "Archfiend" card.) If  a Level 8 or higher monster  under  your  control  was sent to the Graveyard  this turn:  Special Summon 1 "Berserk Dragon" from your hand or Deck.
