 Male

 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Sora Perse , known as  Sora Shiunin ( 紫 雲 院   素 良 ,  Shiunin Sora ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Sora Perse , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
