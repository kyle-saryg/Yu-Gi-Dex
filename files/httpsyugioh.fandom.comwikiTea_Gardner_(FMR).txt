 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 Tea Gardner , known as  Anzu Mazaki  (真崎杏子  Mazaki Anzu ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . This is a video game depiction of  Téa Gardner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  and  anime .
 Tea works part-time at the card shop, during the  Duel Monsters  World Championship tournament. When the player enters her shop, they are given the options, "save", "build deck", "return to title" and "leave shop".
 Tea appears in the present day events of the game, where she takes the same role as the  card shop owner , from the Ancient Egyptian events. However her Ancient Egyptian counterpart is  Teana .
