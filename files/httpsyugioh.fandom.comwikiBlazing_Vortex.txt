 Blazing Vortex  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the third set in the  OCG ' s  11th series , following  Phantom Rage  and followed by  Lightning Overdrive .

 Features 
 The first print runs of  Blazing Vortex  boxes include 1  Blazing Vortex +1 Bonus Pack . 
 Includes new members/support for the " Armed Dragon "," LV ", " Fabled ", " Dream Mirror ", " Metalfoes ", " Eldlich ", " Golden Land ", " Dogmatika ", " Tri-Brigade ", " Constellar ", " Ojama ", " Dual Avatar ", " Live☆Twin ", " Ki-sikil ", " Lil-la ", " Evil★Twin ", " Ancient Warriors ", " Dragunity ", " Machina ", " Archfiend ", " Virtual World ", " Virtual World Gate " and " Windwitch " archetypes, as well as  The Fabled ,  Digital Bug ,  Greed ,  Paladins of Dragons  and  Wight  series.
 Introduces the " Springans " and " S-Force " archetypes to the  OCG/TCG . 
 Introduces the " War Rock " and " Materiactor " archetypes for  TCG . 
 TCG  version also includes new members/support for " Pendulum " and " Polymerization " archetypes, as well as  Synchro  series. 
 Breakdown
