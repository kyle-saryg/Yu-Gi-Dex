 This card's  OCG  and  TCG  rulings contradict each other. 
 
 Differences 
 
In the TCG, the effect " Your opponent skips their next Main Phase 1. " starts a Chain Link. In the OCG, it does not start a Chain Link.

 OCG Rulings 
 The effect " If this card is Ritual Summoned: Your opponent skips their next  Main Phase 1 . " is not an  Ignition ,  Trigger ,  Quick , or  Continuous Effect . [1] 
 Applying " Your opponent skips their next  Main Phase 1 . " does not start a  Chain Link . [1] 
 The effect " Negate  the  effects  of  face-up   Fusion ,  Synchro , and  Xyz Monsters  while they are on the  field . " is a  Continuous Effect . [1] 
 The effect " If this card is  sent  from the field to the  Graveyard : You can  add  1 " Dracoverlord "  monster  from your  Deck  to your  hand , except "Amorphactor Pain, the Imagination Dracoverlord". " is a  Trigger Effect  that is activated from the Graveyard. (It does not target. It can be activated during the  Damage Step .) [1]
