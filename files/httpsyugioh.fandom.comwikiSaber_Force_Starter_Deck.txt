 Yu-Gi-Oh! Saber Force Starter Deck  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Dark Legion Starter Deck  in North America, they are the ninth and tenth Decks in the  TCG ' s annual Starter Deck series (nineteenth and twentieth overall), following  Super Starter: Space-Time Showdown . This Deck is the  TCG  equivalent of one half of  Duelist Entry Deck VS  in the  OCG . For the European equivalent see  2-Player Starter Deck Yuya & Declan .
