 This card can be searched by " Ice Barrier ", " Crystal Girl ", " Gizmek Uka, the Festive Fox of Fecundity ", " Gizmek Makami, the Ferocious Fanged Fortress ", " Dragodies, the Empowered Warrior ", " Arnis, the Empowered Warrior ", " Magician's Circle ", " Spellbook of Judgment ", " The Grand Spellbook Tower ", " Spellbook Star Hall ", " Lucky Loan ", " Lemon Magician Girl ", " Eria the Water Charmer, Gentle ", " Mother Grizzly ", " Mermail Abyssalacia ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Chaos-End Master ", " Umbramirage the Elemental Lord ", " Sangan " and " Witch of the Black Forest ". 
 This card's stat gain also counts your  Field Zone , therefore, counting only your own cards, its ATK can climb to 4600 on its own. 
 A " Crystal Beast " Deck can make use of this card's on-field effect to set up the summon of " Rainbow Dragon " (and by extension, " Rainbow Overdragon ").
