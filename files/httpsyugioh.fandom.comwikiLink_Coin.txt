 Spell   
 
 Send  1  Link Monster  you  control  to the  GY ;  excavate  cards from the top of your  Deck , equal to the sent monster's  Link Rating ,  add  1 excavated card to your  hand , also place the remaining cards on top of your Deck in any order.
