 Heartland Academy  (ハートランド 学 園   Hātorando Gakuen ) [1]  is the middle school for students who reside in  Heartland City . The school is a large, circular building with many facilities for the students who attend there. 
 Due to the popularity of the  Augmented Reality Duels , the school's central field area is a perfect place for the students to relax and Duel during their breaks. [2]
