 A  Cubic Counter  is a type of  counter  associated with the " Cubic " archetype. When in effect, the monster(s) in question gain a grainy, fossilized look and lose their effects and cannot attack.
 In the original Japanese version of  The Dark Side of Dimensions , when a monster gains a Cubic counter,  Aigami  refers to them as being "un-dimensionalized".
