 Wikipedia  has an article about  Anime . 
 
 
 Anime  may refer to any of the following:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! , a series by  Toei Animation 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! , a series by  NAS , known as  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  in Asia 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , a spin-off series by NAS, known as  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  in Asia 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , another spin-off series by NAS 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , another spin-off series by NAS 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , another spin-off series by NAS 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS , another spin-off series by NAS 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS , another spin-off series by Bridge 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Go Rush , another spin-off series by Bridge 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! the Movie , a film based on the Toei series 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light , a film based on the first NAS series 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , a film based on first three NAS series 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions , a film based on the first NAS series
