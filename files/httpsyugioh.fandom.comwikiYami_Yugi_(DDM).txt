 Yami Yugi , known as  Dark Yugi  in the Japanese version, is a character in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters . He is based on  Dark Yugi  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Description 
 The Ultimate in name and deed, this man-child wields miraculous power. His mastery is already a legend, making the title King of Games his own.
