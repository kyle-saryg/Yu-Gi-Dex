 Mako Tsunami , known as  Ryota Kajiki  (梶木漁太,  Kajiki Ryōta ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Mako Tsunami , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
