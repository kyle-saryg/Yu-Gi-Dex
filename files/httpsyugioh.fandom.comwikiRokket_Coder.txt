 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is used for the  Link Summon  of a " Borrel "  monster , it is treated as a  Dragon  monster. You can only use each of the following  effects  of "Rokket Coder"  once per turn . If a  Cyberse  monster you  control  would be used as  Link Material  for a " Code Talker " monster, this card in your  hand  can also be used as material. If this card you control would be used as Link Material for a "Borrel" monster, you can also use 1  DARK  monster in your hand as material.
