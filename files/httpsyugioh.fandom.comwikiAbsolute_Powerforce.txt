 Absolute Powerforce  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the seventh set of the  OCG ' s sixth Series after  Stardust Overdrive . It is followed by  The Shining Darkness .
 The North American Sneak Peeks for this set took place from February 6–7, 2010.
 Packs of the  TCG  set were also printed in the  Absolute Powerforce: Special Edition .

 Features 
 Contains many cards from characters like  Jack Atlas ,  Yusei ,  Akiza ,  Misty ,  Leo ,  Rex Goodwin ,  Roman Goodwin ,  Devack  and  Trudge . The TCG version also contains cards from  Hunter Pace ,  The Supreme King ,  Marik Ishtar  and  Yugi Muto .
 The set also includes further support for the " Majestic ", " X-Saber ", " Synchron ", " Reptilianne ",  Djinn of Rituals , " Spider ", " Cyber Dragon ",  Umi , " Koa'ki Meiru ", " Earthbound Immortal " and " Gravekeeper's "  archetypes  and  series .
 TCG exclusives  include further support for the "Gravekeeper's", "Koa'ki Meiru" and "X-Saber" archetypes, support for  Beasts ,  Plants  and  Spellcasters , as well as cards that will work together with the  Machina Mayhem Structure Deck .
