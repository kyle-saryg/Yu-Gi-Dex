 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters: Pyramid of Light  is a novel based on the film,  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light , known as  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters: Pyramid of Light  in the Japanese version.
 
 Contents 
 Story 
 The story is divided into a prologue, 5 chapters and an epilogue.
 
 Prologue 
 The Omen 
 The Three Mythic Gods Appear 
 The Pyramid of Light 
 Shining Dragon 
 Anubis, King of Destruction 
 Epilogue 
 While the storyline follows that of the film, a number of changes are made. It states that the  Pyramid of Light  was made by  Akhenaden  in a failed attempt to recreate the  Millennium Puzzle  for his son,  Seto .
 
 Character analysis 
 The book contains a character analysis section, which gives profiles on the characters. It goes into detail on the Decks of  Pegasus ,  Yugi  and  Kaiba , including their complete Decklists, ratings for attack, defense, speed and combo, along with various strategies.
