 Reaper on the Nightmare  is a character version of the card, " Reaper on the Nightmare ".
 
       Reaper on the Nightmare in  Over the Nexus .     
 Reaper on the Nightmare appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus . In  World Championship 2008 , it can be unlocked if the player has beaten  Nightmare Penguin  five times. The player can then unlock it as a tag partner by beating it ten times. In  World Championship 2011 , Reaper on the Nightmare and  Dark Blade the Dragon Knight  can be unlocked in Free Turbo Tag Duel if the player can beat the team known as " Down to the Sea " three times. Their team name is " Unified in Darkness ".
 
 
 Deck 
 In World Championship 2008 and 2011, Reaper on the Nightmare uses Decks titled "Perishing Darkfest" and "Nightmare Hunter".
