 Sonia was  Rafael 's sister. She was killed by a tidal wave along with  Julian  and their parents while on a cruise ship piloted by  Dartz . 
 
 Background 
 When Rafael survived the tidal wave and the years he spent on a deserted island, he was devastated to return to civilization and learn that his family was gone. After his second Duel with  Yami Yugi , Sonia and the rest of Rafael's family appeared to him next to his " Guardians ". When  The Great Leviathan  was destroyed, she was seen again with Julian and their parents, promising Rafael that they would always be in his heart.
