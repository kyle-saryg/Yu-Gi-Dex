 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " and " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ". 
 This card's first effect can restore up to 3000 LP at once. 
 If you open with this card, " Abyss Actor - Comic Relief ", " Shien's Spy " and two other " Abyss Actor " monsters (including one that is Level 4 or lower so that you can use it to  Link Summon  " Abyss Actor - Hyper Director "), you will be able to activate this card's second effect (via "Comic Relief") and draw 5 cards.
