 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Painful Escape ", " Sangan ",  " Witch of the Black Forest " and " Frontline Observer ". 
 Use this card to  Link climb  in a  Beast-Warrior  Deck.
 " Rescue Rabbit " is a one-card means of enabling this card's summon, by bringing out two Beast-Warrior  Normal Monsters  that can be used as  Link Material . 
 Use this card to dump " Manticore of Darkness " into the graveyard to set it up to be revived. 
 Since this card's first effect doubles its current  ATK , increase its ATK (for example, with " Beast Rising ", " Megamorph " or " Wild Nature's Release ") before activating the effect.
