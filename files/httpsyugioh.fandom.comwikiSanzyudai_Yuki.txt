 Design 
 Name 
 The name Sanzyudai appears to be a pun on Judai, which is  Jaden Yuki 's given name in the Japanese version. The name 十代 (jūdai) literally means "teenage" or "ages 10-19", befitting Jaden Yuki's youth. On the other hand, Sanzyudai appears to be significantly older than Jaden, which is reflected in the name 三十代 (sanjūdai), meaning "the thirties" or "ages 30-39".
 
 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Sanzyudai Yuki asks for the player's aide in his,  Senjome  &  Sironos ' aide in their feud against  Emperor.Umiuma  and the rest of  UmiumaCorp . He appears in the morning at the cafeteria of the  Slifer Red  Dorm after the player has completed the game with 3 or more Page 1 characters. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  , to Sanzyudai's annoyance, he is partnered with Emperor.Umiuma, and in order to be allowed to change partners, he must duel at least once. He challenges the player and his partner to a Duel, and upon victory, he allows the player to partner with him. He appears during a  Random Event  at the Main Gate after the player has completed the Story Mode with 4 or more Page 1 Characters, and have beaten both Jaden and Yusei Fudo 15 or more times each.
 
 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Sanzyudai uses a  Stall  Deck  focused on dealing effect damage while skipping his opponent's Battle Phases with  D.D. Borderline .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Sanzyudai uses  Monster Mash Decks  to quickly power up " Chaos Necromancer " with " Magical Merchant ".
