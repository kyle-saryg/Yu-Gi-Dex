 This card can be searched by " The Melody of Awakening Dragon ", " Tempest, Dragon Ruler of Storms ", " Starliege Seyfert ", " Draconnection ", " Minerva, Lightsworn Maiden ", " Wavering Eyes ", " Luster Pendulum, the Dracoslayer ", " Magical Abductor ", " Majester Paladin, the Ascending Dracoslayer ", " Performapal Celestial Magician ", " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Performapal Handsamuraiger " and " Chaos Zone ". 
 An easy way to summon this card is to have " Rescue Rabbit " call out two " Master Pendulum, the Dracoslayer " or " Vector Pendulum, the Dracoverlord ". 
 If your opponent controls two Attack Position monsters whose combined ATK is 1000 or less, this card's  Pendulum Effect  can cause an OTK alongside " Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon " or " Supreme King Dragon Odd-Eyes ".
 This card's monster effect can cause an OTK if it attacks an opposing Attack Position monster with 2000 or less ATK and you activate " Action Magic - Full Turn ".
