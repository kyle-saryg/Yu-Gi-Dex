 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During your  turn :  Target  1 " Blackwing "  monster  with 2000 or less  ATK  in your  Graveyard ;  Special Summon  that  target . You cannot  Normal Summon / Set  during the  turn  you  activate  this card.
