 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
3 or more  Level  7  monsters You can also  Xyz Summon  this card by using 1 monster you  control  with  "Number C101" through "Number C107"  in its name as the  Xyz Material . (Xyz Materials  attached  to that monster also become Xyz Materials on this card.) This card gains 1000  ATK  for each Xyz Material attached to it. You can  target  1 "Number" monster in your  Graveyard ; until your opponent's next  End Phase , this card's name becomes that monster's, and replace this  effect  with that monster's original effects. You can only use this effect of "CXyz Barian Hope"  once per turn .
