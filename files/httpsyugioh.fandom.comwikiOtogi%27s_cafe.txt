 Otogi's cafe  is a food vendor managed by  Mr. Clown . His son  Ryuji Otogi  also works here as a server, as did  Katsuya Jonouchi . It appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions .
 
 Events 
 Yugi Mutou ,  Anzu Mazaki ,  Hiroto Honda  and  Ryo Bakura  were eating here and being served by Ryuji, when they heard news of  KaibaCorp 's new model  Duel Disk .
