 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  negate  the  activation  of a  Spell / Trap Card , or  monster effect :  Add  1  LIGHT   Fairy   monster  from your  Deck  to your  hand . You can only use this  effect  of "Power Angel Valkyria"  once per turn .

   German
