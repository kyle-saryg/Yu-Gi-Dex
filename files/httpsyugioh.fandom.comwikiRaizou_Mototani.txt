 Though canonically never given a name,  Raizou Mototani  is an  Obelisk Blue  student. In the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  video game, he was dubbed as "Raizou Mototani". Likewise, in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Duel Academy  video game, he was dubbed as "Conklin".

 Biography 
       Taiyou Torimaki  with Raizou Mototani.     
 He was also a friend of  Chazz Princeton  before Chazz left  Duel Academy . He is seen next to Chazz while  Jaden Yuki  dueled  Dr. Crowler . He watched Jaden and  Syrus Truesdale  face the  Paradox Brothers  while noticing Chazz all agitated. When Chazz left Duel Academy he wasn't worried unlike Jaden believing he left because of his loss to  Bastion Misawa  and Jaden When Chazz goes to the  Obelisk Blue  dorm when in the  Society of Light , Raizou was one of the first to be defeated and join the Society led by  Sartorius . He is always seen with  Taiyou Torimaki . He was presumably turned into a  Duel Ghoul  with the rest of the Academy.
 
 Deck 
 Spirit Caller 
       In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller , Raizou plays a  Rock  Deck called  Giant Enshrinement .
