 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , when the player receives Alvaro's  Deck Recipe , they also receive the card " Royal Guard ".
 Character-wise, he seems to miss the point of Dueling and thinks it's all about physical strength.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2 , he has supposedly got larger muscles, but his sprite and model remain the same.
 Alvaro can sometimes appear in a special random event at the Red Dorm, where he will ask the player for 20,000 DP. If the player agrees, Alvaro will in exchange give him several rare cards.

 Decks 
 Alvaro uses  Insect  Decks  focused on protecting " Petit Moth " as it evolves into more powerful stages, keeping it on the field by increasing its  DEF  or stopping attacks altogether. In later installments, Alvaro also includes some easily-summoned high-ATK Insect-Type monsters as support.
 His third Deck in both the second and third games, instead of being his usual defensive "Moth" Decks, are  Demise/Doom OTK  Decks .
