 TCG Rulings 
 Even if “ Alien Ammonite ” is no longer on the field, the monster it Summoned will still be destroyed in the  End Phase . [1] 
 The effect that destroys the Summoned monster in the End Phase does not start a  chain . [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The effect which  Special Summons  one " Alien " Monster in your  Graveyard  is a  Trigger Effect . [2] 
 The effect  targets  one Level 4 or lower " Alien " Monster in your  Graveyard .  After the monster is  Special Summoned , the effect does not continue to  target  the Monster. [2] 
 Special Summoning  the " Alien " monster from the Graveyard and destroying it during the  End Phase  are the same effect.  It does not start a new  Chain  during the  End Phase . [2] 
 If " Alien Ammonite " is not on the field, the  Special Summoned  monster is still destroyed during the End Phase. [3]
