Crow  plays a  " Blackwing " Deck  which focus upon swarming the field as quickly as possible, the  Synchro Summoning  of " Blackwing Armor Master ", " Blackwing - Silverwind the Ascendant ", " Blackwing Armed Wing ", or his  Signer Dragon , " Black-Winged Dragon ". Most "Blackwing" monsters resemble Crow's namesake, black-winged birds or birdlike humanoids. In the  Tag Force  games, Crow focuses more on a swarming strategy with his "Blackwings", as well as cards that destroy the opponent's Deck.
 Crow has two Decks, one for Turbo Duels and one for normal Duels.
