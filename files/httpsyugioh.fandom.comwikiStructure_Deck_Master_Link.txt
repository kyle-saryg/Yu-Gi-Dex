 Structure Deck: Master Link  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game [1] . It is the 36th Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Powercode Link .

 Features 
 Contains cards used by  Yusaku Fujiki . 
 Introduces the first support for the " Cynet " archetype to the OCG.
