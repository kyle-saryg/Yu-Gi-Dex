 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Single Purchase ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Sangan ", " Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon ", " Supreme King Dragon Odd-Eyes ", " Rescue Hamster ", " Pendulum Rising ", " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Luster Pendulum, the Dracoslayer ", " Magical Abductor ", " Majester Paladin, the Ascending Dracoslayer ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Soul Rope ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Dark General Freed ", " Creeping Darkness ", " Chaos Zone ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Abyss Actor - Sassy Rookie ", " Abyss Actor - Extras ", " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ", and " Abyss Script - Abysstainment ".
 On the same turn that you Special Summon this card from the  Pendulum Zone  and use its effect, use "Abyss Script - Abysstainment" to Tribute the other " Abyss Actor " monster you Normal Summoned, so that this card's ATK is doubled and you gain access to the " Abyss Script " Spell Card you had sent from the Deck to the Graveyard.
