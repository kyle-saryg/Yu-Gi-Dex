 Kokurano  ( 孤 蔵 乃 [1]  or  孤 蔵 野 [2]   Kokurano ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime , by  Toei Animation . He is the series version of  Kokurano  from the  manga . He is a self-proclaimed psychic at  Domino High School .
 
 Biography 
   This section is  missing information  from  Yu-Gi-Oh!   episode 13 . 
 Kokurano has a slight dislike for  Miho Nosaka  because she would not get a prediction from him, for, in his words, she "mocks life with her complete innocence".
 He was defeated by  Dark Yugi , in  a game  involving bottles falling from the ceiling.
