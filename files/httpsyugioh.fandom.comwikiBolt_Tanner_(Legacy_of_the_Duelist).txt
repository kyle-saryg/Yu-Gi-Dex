 Bolt Tanner , known as  Jin Himuro (氷室仁) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Bolt Tanner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
