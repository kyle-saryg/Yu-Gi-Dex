 Collectors Pack: Duelist of Legend Version  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 The second "Concept Pack" that contains cards from both the animated series and the manga! 
 This pack is the equivalent to the  TCG 's  Dragons of Legend , with the exception of a few cards - while the Japanese and Japanese-Asian sets includes " Number 106: Giant Hand ", and the Korean set includes " Ruffian Railcar ", the TCG includes " Number 73: Abyss Splash " and " Number C106: Giant Red Hand " (the latter two were already released in the Japanese  OCG ), thus this set contains 1 fewer card than its  TCG  equivalent.
 Another difference within the  OCG  releases, is that while the Japanese and Japanese-Asian sets (as well as Dragons of Legend) includes " Guardian Eatos ", the Korean set includes " Number 58: Burner Visor ". 
 The " Super Rare " and " Ultra Rare " cards are also available as " Collector's Rare " in the Japanese set, and as " Secret Rare " in the Japanese-Asian and Korean sets. 
 Brand new cards from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . 
 Includes cards used by  Yami Yugi ,  Rafael ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Franz ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Crow Hogan ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Vector ,  Girag , and  Nash .
