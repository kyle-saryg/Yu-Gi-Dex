 Insect Queen  is a character version of the card " Insect Queen ". 
 She appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P064. She is also available as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 2 under Quest mode, and under  Duel Monsters Arena  mode.
 In the beta, she had a default of 4 Rental Points as a partner, and her partner ID was P028.
