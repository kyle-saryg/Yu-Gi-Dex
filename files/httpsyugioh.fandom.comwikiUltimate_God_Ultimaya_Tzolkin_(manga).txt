 When you  Set  a card(s),  Special Summon  1  Duel Dragon  from your  Extra Deck  for each card Set. Each player can only Set cards once per  turn . If there is a Duel Dragon on your  field , your opponent cannot  attack  this card. Once per turn, if this card would be  destroyed , you can  negate  its destruction. If this card  battles  while there is no Duel Dragon on your field, its  ATK  becomes equal to the ATK of the monster it is battling, also destroy 1 of your opponent's Set cards.
