 The turn player can choose two  types  of monsters to be converted into ectoplasm, with half the  ATK . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> Choose 2  types  of  monsters .
