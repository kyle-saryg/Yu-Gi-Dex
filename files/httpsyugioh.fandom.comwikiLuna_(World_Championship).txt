 Luna , known as  Ruka  or  Luca  ( 龍可 ,  Ruka ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Luna , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.

 Biography 
 Stardust Accelerator 
 Luna and her brother Leo participate in a Tag Duel tournament in the game's storyline. They are the third opponents of the player and their partner. 
 
 Reverse of Arcadia 
 Due to the player's connection with the  Spirit World , Luna plays a more important role in this game's storyline. The player rescues Luna from  Zeman's castle  in Chapter 3, then helps free her and Leo from the  Arcadia Movement  cells in Chapter 4. In the process, Luna and the player are engaged in a Tag Duel with  Liquid  and  Okita .
 In Chapter 5, Luna asks the player to go to the Spirit World with her and help her unseal  Ancient Fairy Dragon . They return to their world at the control unit where Leo was dueling  Devack . The player and Luna team up again to defeat Devack. Post-story, Luna can be found at New Domino City, and must be talked to in order to access the Spirit World.
 
 Over the Nexus 
 Luna and her brother are  New Domino Academy  students who make cameos in the storyline. They happened to be looking for  Yusei  at his garage in the Fountain Plaza when they spot the player. They are then seen cheering the player on prior to the final  WRGP  match against  Team New World .

 Unlockables 
 Stardust Accelerator 
 
 The player can unlock Luna as a Tag Partner by defeating her ten times. 
 The player can unlock  Leo's custom Disk  by defeating Luna and Leo ten times in  Tag Duels . 
 Reverse of Arcadia 
 
 The player can unlock Luna as a Tag Partner by defeating her and Leo three times in Tag Duels. 
 The player can unlock  Leo's custom Disk  by defeating Luna and Leo ten times in  Tag Duels . 
 The player can partially unlock  Whirlwind of Dragunity!!  by defeating Luna three times. 
 Over the Nexus 
 
 The player can unlock the  Duel Academia uniform  by defeating her and Leo ten times in Tag Duels. 
 The player can partially unlock  Whirlwind of Dragunity!!  by defeating Luna three times.
