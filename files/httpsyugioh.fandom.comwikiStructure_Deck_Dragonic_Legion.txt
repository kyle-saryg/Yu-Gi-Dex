 Structure Deck: Dragonic Legion  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the twenty-second Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (thirtieth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Devil's Gate . The  TCG  equivalent of this Deck is  Dragons Collide Structure Deck .

 Features 
 This is the first Structure Deck with 2 cover-cards. This Deck features  LIGHT ,  DARK  and  Dragon -Type monsters.
It also includes new Dragon-Type monsters, as well as support cards. There are 5 new cards and many reprints.
