 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 104 ,  Queen  uses this card during her  Master Duel  against  Ai . She controls this face-up card when the duel is first shown. Queen links this card with " Allure Queen LV3 " and " Allure Queen LV7 " to  Link Summon  " Golden Allure Queen ". 
 Video games 
 Allure Queen LV5  and  Allure Queen LV7  use this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . 
 Giry  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 .
