 Yugi Muto  is a video game depiction of  Yugi Muto , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 He is available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena  as a Super Partner, the first partner any player receives when they create an account, with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P001.
 He also appears as an opponent, under  Duel Monsters Arena  mode.

 Description 
 A game loving, timid but loving teenager that solved the  Millennium Puzzle . He possesses another personality,  Yami Yugi , inside himself and calls him "the other me".
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Are you here to duel with me?
 
 Decks 
 Built around  EARTH   monsters  like the " Magnet Warrior " and " Big Shield Gardna ".  Summon  powerful monsters like " Summoned Skull " while blocking  attacks  with cards like " Swords of Revealing Light " and " Mirror Force "!

 Trivia 
 This Partner's Deck name, "Block-Puzzle-Demolition", is a reference to the  attack name  of " Toy Magician ", which is "Block Demolition".
 "Toy Magician" was a unique monster used by  Yugi Muto  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! R , which  Yami Yugi  never used. 
 This Partner's Deck (post-October 22, 2014 maintenance) was specifically modified to comply with the restrictions of  Duel Monsters Arena  mode, released on the same day of its modification.
