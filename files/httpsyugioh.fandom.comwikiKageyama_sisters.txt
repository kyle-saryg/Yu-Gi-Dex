 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (Toei) 
 
 
 The  Kageyama sisters  are three witches in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime , who want to have the three " Hecate " cards. The sisters include,  Risa , " Sister B " and " Sister C ".
 The sisters are beautiful seemingly-innocent girls. However their obsession drives them to do cruel things to get the cards that they desire.

 History 
 Risa Kageyama tries to take  Yugi Mutou 's " Violet Hecate " card, while pretending to be his friend. Yugi, with the  Millennium Puzzle  taken away from him, is forced into a  Magic & Wizards  game with the Kageyama sisters. Yugi is about to lose, until  Kaiba  appears and takes over. Kaiba defeats the Kageyama sisters, explaining that he should be the one to defeat Yugi.
 
 Deck 
 The Kageyama sisters collectively play a  Hecate  Deck focused on slowly summoning their " Gorgon " card.
