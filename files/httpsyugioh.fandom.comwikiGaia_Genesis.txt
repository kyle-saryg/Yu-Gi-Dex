 Gaia Genesis  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on August 27,2018, 8:00 AM (EST). 
 
 Description 
 New EARTH and WATER archetypes added! Koa'ki Meiru and Mermail! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Elemental HERO Gaia ," An  Elemental HERO  monster that can halve the ATK of the opponent's monster and then add that to its ATK! If you face a monster with high ATK, use this effect to defect it. 
" Mermail Abyssmegalo ," A monster that can be Special Summoned from your hand by discarding 2 WATER monsters from your hand to the Graveyard. When this card is Special Summoned this way, you can add 1 " Abyss- " Spell/Trap Card from your Deck to your hand! 
" U.A. Midfielder ," Newly added " U.A. " monsters can battle while trading places with monsters on your side of the field and in your hand. "U.A." mmonsters with powerful effects can easily be Special Summoned from your hand. 
" Galaxy Cyclone ," A Spell Card that can destroy 1 Spell/Trap Card that is Set on the field! You can also banish this card from your Graveyard to destroy 1 face-up Spell/Trap Card! Be aware that this effect can only be activated when the card is sent to your Graveyard! 
A total of 40 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 48  N .
 
 
 Each box of  Gaia Genesis  contains 100 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
