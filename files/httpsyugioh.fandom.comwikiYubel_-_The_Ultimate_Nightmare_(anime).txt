 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except with the  effect  of " Yubel - Terror Incarnate ". This card cannot be  destroyed by battle . You take no  Battle Damage  from  battles  involving this card. If this  face-up   Attack Position  card battles with an opponent's  monster , inflict  damage  equal to its  ATK  to your opponent and  destroy  that monster at the end of the  Damage Step . Your  hand size limit  becomes 10.
