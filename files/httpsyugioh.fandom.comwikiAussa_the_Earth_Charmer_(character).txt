 Aussa the Earth Charmer  is a character version of the card, " Aussa the Earth Charmer ". 
 Aussa the Earth Charmer appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . In  World Championship 2006 , she appears as a Theme Duelist and she challenges the player to take  control  of her monsters 10 or more times during a Duel. In  World Championship 2007 , she appears as a Level Six Theme Duelist and she challenges the player to do the same challenges in 2006. By beating her, the player gets closer to unlocking a new Booster Pack.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2006 , Aussa the Earth Charmer plays an  EARTH  Deck titled, "Spirits of the Earth". In  World Championship 2007 , she plays a  Female  Deck titled, "Come on Over".
