 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 42 ,  Halil  plays this card during his duel against  Zuzu Boyle  at the  Arc League Championship   Battle Royal . When Zuzu activates " Pendulumstatue White Flower " on her  Pendulum Zone , Halil activates this face-down card, negating "White Flower" and adding it to his hand. In the  following episode , Halil's duel with Zuzu merges with  Olga 's duel with  Gong Strong , such that Halil and Olga are on the same team. After Gong  Synchro Summons  " Superheavy Samurai Ogre Shutendoji ", its effect destroys this card, forcing Halil to return "White Flower" to Zuzu's hand.
