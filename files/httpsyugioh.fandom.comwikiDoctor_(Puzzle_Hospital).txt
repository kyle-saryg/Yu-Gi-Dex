 The  doctor  at  Puzzle Hospital  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga .
 
 Biography 
 She treated  Sect Ijuin  and  Yusei Fudo  after they were injured from the  Skeleton Knight  and Dueling  Jack Atlas . Yusei refused to tell her what happened the night before they arrived injured and disregarded her advice to rest. [1]
