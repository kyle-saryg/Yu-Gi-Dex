 This card can be  Xyz Summoned  by using " Rank-Up-Magic Astral Force " on " Number C43: High Manipulator of Chaos ", " Number C65: King Overfiend " or " Number C96: Dark Storm ". 
 This card can be Xyz Summoned by using " The Phantom Knights' Rank-Up-Magic Launch " on a  Rank 4   DARK  monster. 
 You can use " Overlay Regen " to replenish this card's material.
