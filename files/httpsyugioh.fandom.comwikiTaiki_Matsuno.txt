 Taiki Matsuno  ( 松野   太紀 ,  Matsuno Taiki ), born  Tatsuya Matsuno  ( 松野   達也 ,  Matsuno Tatsuya ) is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices Jun Manjome ( Chazz Princeton ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ). [2]
