 " The Two Ideals " is the -th episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on January 30, 2022.
 Roa  has been practicing like mad for a secret  RoaRomin  concert. Like  Gavin  and the others, Roa seems to have lost all his memories about  Rush Duels .  Yuga  challenges Roa to a  Duel  in order to restore his memories. And so, they Duel each other once again. The day of the Seven Hells Hall concert will now be re-created...!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Roa Kirishima 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
 Duel continues in the next episode .
