 The  2019 Gold Sarcophagus Tin  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game   collectible tin .

 Breakdown 
 Each 2019 Gold Sarcophagus Tin contains the following:
 
 Three 16-card  Mega Packs . Each  Mega Pack  contains cards from  Flames of Destruction ,  Dark Saviors ,  Cybernetic Horizon  and  Soul Fusion :
 1  Prismatic Secret Rare 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 1  Super Rare 
 1  Rare 
 12  Commons 
 5 Promotional  Prismatic Secret Rare  variant cards:
 2 of 6 new cards with art by  Kazuki Takahashi 
 2 of 5 cards from the  original Yu-Gi-Oh! animated series 
 1 of 3 new World Premiere cards
