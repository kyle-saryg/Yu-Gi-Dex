 [  Beast  /  Effect  ] Special Summon  this card by stacking it on top of 1 " Cubic Seed "  monster  you  control . This card gains 1000  original ATK  for each monster stacked underneath it. If this card  destroys  a monster  by battle , you can  return  this card to your  Deck  to  add  1 " Blade Garoodia the Cubic Beast " from your Deck to your  hand . In addition, Special Summon the "Cubic Seed" monsters underneath this card to your  field . [1] [2]
