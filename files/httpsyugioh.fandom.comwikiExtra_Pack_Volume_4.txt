 Extra Pack Volume 4  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -exclusive cards to the  OCG .

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive  cards from the  Booster Packs   The Shining Darkness ,  Duelist Revolution ,  Starstrike Blast , and  Storm of Ragnarok . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 10 cards per pack and 10 packs per Japanese box. Or 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per Korean box.
 The set includes 40 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 2  Ultra Rare 
 4  Super Rare 
 6  Rare 
 2  Normal Rare 
 26  Common
