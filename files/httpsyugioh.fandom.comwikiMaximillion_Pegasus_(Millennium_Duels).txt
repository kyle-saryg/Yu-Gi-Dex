 Maximillion Pegasus , known as  Pegasus J. Crawford  (ペガサス・ Ｊ ・クロフォード,  Pegasasu Jē Kurofōdo ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Maximillion Pegasus , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
