 Spell 
 
 
 
 The moment this magic is  activated , the game jumps to the  battle phase  three turns ahead. 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> Move the  turn count  forward by 6  turns  (3 turns for each player). All cards on the  field  are treated as having had their  effect(s)  active for three full turns for both players. The turn in which this card is  activated  continues as normal. ( Yu-Gi-Oh! R )
