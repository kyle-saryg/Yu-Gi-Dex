 "Kattobingu!!"  is the thirteenth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  manga. It was first published April 3, 2013 in the  Saikyō Jump  magazine.

 Summary 
 Yuma 's  Duel  against  Shark  for the finals of the  Team Duel Tournament  concludes.
 
 Featured Duel: Team ZEXAL vs. Team Shark 
 This is a Team Duel which uses the rules of the  Team Duel Tournament ; check the article for rules.
 
 Duel 3: Yuma Tsukumo vs. Shark 
 Duel continues from  the previous chapter .
 Turn 3: Shark 
 Shark   attacks  and  destroys  " Number 39: Utopia " with " Tri-Edge Levia " (Yuma: 4000 → 3900), with the latter's  effect   banishing  "Utopia". Shark  Sets  a card.
 Turn 4: Yuma Tsukumo 
 Yuma   Normal Summons  " Gagaga Caesar " (1800/600). As he  controls  a " Gagaga "  monster , he  Special Summons  " Gagaga Clerk " (400/800) from his  hand  in  Attack Position . He then  activates  the effect of "Gagaga Caesar",  Banishing  " Kagetokage " from his  Graveyard  to change all "Gagaga" monsters he controls to  Level  4. He then overlays his two Level 4 monsters to  Xyz Summon  " Zubaba General " (2000/1000). Shark activates his set " Splash Capture ", banishing two  Fish - Type  monsters from his Graveyard to take control of "Zubaba General". Yuma Sets a card.
 Turn 5: Shark 
Shark  detaches  both  Xyz Materials  from "Zubaba General" to Special Summon " Xyz Remora " (800/800) from his hand. He then activates its effect, Special Summoning two Level 4 Fish-Type monsters from his Graveyard in Defense Position, with their effects negated. He Special Summons " Shocktopus " (1600/800) and " Unshaven Angler " (1500/1600). Shark overlays his 3 level 4 monsters to Xyz Summon " Number 32: Shark Drake " (2800/2100) in Attack Position. He attacks Yuma directly with "Tri-Edge Levia", but Yuma activates his set " Damage Diet ", halving the damage he takes this turn (Yuma 3900 → 3000). Shark attacks Yuma directly with "Zubaba General" (Yuma 3000 → 2000) and "Shark Drake" (Yuma 2000 → 600).
 Turn 6: Yuma 
Yuma Normal Summons " ZW - Ultimate Shield " (0/2000) and activates its effect, allowing him to Special Summon his banished "Utopia". Yuma then activates " Rank-Up-Magic Limited Barian's Force ", using "Utopia" as an Overlay Unit to Special Summon " Number C39: Utopia Ray V " (2600/2100) in Attack Position. Yuma activates its effect, detaching an Xyz Material to destroy a monster on the field and and inflict its original ATK as damage to Shark. Yuma destroys "Shark Drake" (Shark 2900 → 100). "Utopia Ray V" attacks and destroys "Tri-Edge Levia" (Shark 100 → 0).
