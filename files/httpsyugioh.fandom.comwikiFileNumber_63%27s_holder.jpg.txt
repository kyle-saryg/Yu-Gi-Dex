 Number 63's holder  is an unnamed character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga . He was the original owner of " No. 63: Shamoji Soldier ". He created a cooking store, which was going well because of the power of the " Number ". However, one night,  Ryoga Kamishiro  showed up and  Dueled  him. Ryoga defeated him easily, and took his "Number" card. [1] 
 
 Deck 
 The only known card in this Duelist's Deck was " No. 63: Shamoji Soldier ".
