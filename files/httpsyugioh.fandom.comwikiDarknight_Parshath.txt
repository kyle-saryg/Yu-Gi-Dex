 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card  destroys  an opponent's  monster   by battle : You can  banish  1  DARK  monster from your  Graveyard ;  draw  1 card. This card gains 100  ATK  for each DARK monster in your Graveyard.
