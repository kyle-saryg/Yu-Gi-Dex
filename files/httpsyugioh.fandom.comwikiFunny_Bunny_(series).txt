 Funny Bunny , known as  Funny Rabbit  in the  manga  and Japanese version, is the name of a comic and cartoon read and watched by  Maximillion Pegasus .
 It was aired on every Saturday morning, when Pegasus was growing up in America. According to Pegasus, there were 3,013 episodes of his cartoon along 26,000 bullets shot at him. 
 
 Characters 
 Funny Bunny  ( Funny Rabbit ): The main character, who is forever evading the law. 
 Ruff Ruff McDogg : The police officer antagonist to Funny Bunny. 
 A pig and a crocodile are also seen when Pegasus is reading the comic. 
 Influence 
 Pegasus uses Funny Bunny illustrations in his computer security system.
 Pegasus based some of his cards, including the  Toon monsters , on the series. He explained that  Toon monsters  were able to dodge attacks in a similar fashion to how Funny Bunny could dodge Ruff Ruff McDogg's bullets.
 The card " Dark Rabbit " may have been based on this character. The card's flavor text even mentions that "Dark Rabbit" is a  "funny bunny" .
