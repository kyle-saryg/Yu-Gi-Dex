 Mr. Armstrong , also called  Facility Chief  and known as  Takasu  (,  Takasu ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Mr. Armstrong , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Biography 
 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock " Trap of Board Eraser " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Straight Flush " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the second time. 
 The player can unlock " Mass Driver " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the third time. 
 The player can unlock " Iron Chain Snake " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the fourth time. 
 The player can unlock " Restructer Revolution " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the fifth time. 
 The player can unlock " Harpie's Feather Duster " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the sixth time. 
 The player can unlock " Reload " by defeating Mr. Armstrong for the seventh time. 
 The player can unlock  Mr. Armstrong's Duel Runner  by defeating him for the first time. 
 The player can unlock Mr. Armstrong's Decklist by winning the  Silver Cup .
