 " Irreplaceable Days " is the eighty-first episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on January 9, 2022.
 It's a typical morning, but  Gavin  and  Romin  are acting more distant than usual. As if to provoke  Yuga , who is already bewildered and confused,  Yuga Goha  challenges  Otes  to a  Duel . Yuga Goha also has  Monster Reborn  with him.
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Goha VS.Otes 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
