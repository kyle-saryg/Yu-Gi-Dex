 Alito  (アリト  Arito )is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Alito , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
