 Alexis Rhodes , known as  Asuka Tenjoin  ( 天 上 院   明日 香 ,  Tenjōin Asuka ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Alexis Rhodes (ARC-V) , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
