 " Agonizing Heart " (せつない心,  Setsunai Kokoro ) is an instrumental musical composition from the Japanese version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
 It is 1:20 long and has been released on the soundtrack  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX Sound Duel Vol II .
 
 Appearances 
 The composition is played in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  in:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 6: " The Shadow Duelist, Part 2 "  when  Jaden Yuki  gives  Alexis Rhodes  a picture of  Atticus Rhodes . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 22: " The Duel Off, Part 2 "  when Jaden defeats  Bastion Misawa  and  Gerard  decides not to disclose story about the missing students. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 26: " The School Duel, Part 2 "  when  Chazz Princeton  realizes what is the dueling about and decides to go back to Duel Academy. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 41: " A Reason to Win "  when Alexis recalls time of her childhood with Atticus. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 44: " The Seventh Shadow Rider "  when Jaden, Syrus and Chazz are talking about  Lyman Banner  at a campfire. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 49: " Rise of the Sacred Beasts, Part 2 "  when  Kagemaru  stood up without using power of the Sacred Beasts. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 64: " Camaraderie Contest "  when Syrus states he believes Jaden will come back. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 93: " Heart of Ice, Part 1 "  when Atticus recalls time of his childhood with Alexis. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 161: " Shall We Duel? An Invitation to a Pair Duel "  when Alexis is looking through pictures on her computer. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 162: " Judai VS Asuka! A Set Card Full of Hidden Feelings " 
 when Jaden and Alexis come round in the tag duel. 
 when Alexis suppresses telling Jaden how she feels about him. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 163: " A Challenge from Psycho Shocker "  when  Syrus Truesdale  recalls  Zane 's graduation match against Jaden. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 179: " Farewell, Judai! A Tearful Graduation Ceremony "  during Alexis' graduation speech. 
 The composition is also played in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time  during the rooftop conversation.
