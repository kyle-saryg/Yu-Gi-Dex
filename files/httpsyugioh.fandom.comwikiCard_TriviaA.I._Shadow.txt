 Anime 
 
 This card's name is a pun on  eye shadow . 
 This card's effect of forcing the opponent's monster(s) to attack the controller's monster is a reminiscent of the anime effect of " Shadow of Eyes ". Both cards' artworks also have a seduction motif.
 The background of this card's artwork is the same as "Shadow of Eyes".
