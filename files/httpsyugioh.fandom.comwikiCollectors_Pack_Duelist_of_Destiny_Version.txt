 Collectors Pack: Duelist of Destiny Version  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Yami Yugi ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Alister ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Aura Sentia , and  Ally . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Ancient Gear ", " Legendary Dragon ", " Legendary Knight ", " Red-Eyes ", and " Toon " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Prediction Princess " and " Aquaactress " archetypes to the OCG. 
 The " Super Rare " and " Ultra Rare " cards are also available as " Collector's Rare " in the Japanese set, and as " Secret Rare " in the Korean set.
