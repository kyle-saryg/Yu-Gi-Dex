 Sealed Play Battle Kit 3 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Sealed Play Battle Kit 3  is a  Battle Kit  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the third Battle Kit in the  TCG ' s Battle Kit series, following  Sealed Play Battle Kit 2 . The kit includes 10 booster packs of " Battle Pack 3: Monster League ", and 1 of 5  playmats .
 
 Breakdown 
 Main article:  Set Card Ratios:Battle Pack 3: Monster League (TCG) 
 Each  Sealed Play Battle Kit 3  contains 50 cards in total. 10 packs per box and 5 cards per pack. The number of cards of each rarity in each booster pack are as follows: 
 
 1  Shatterfoil Rare 
 1  Rare 
 3  Commons
