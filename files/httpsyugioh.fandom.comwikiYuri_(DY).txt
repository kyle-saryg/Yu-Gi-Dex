 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 
 
 
 Yuri  is a character featured the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Yuri , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Yuri is a  Fusion  Master from the Neo Fusion Lab.
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Yuri runs a  " Predaplant " Deck .
