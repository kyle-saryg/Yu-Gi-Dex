 This card is based on the fairy tale  Sleeping Beauty , in which the last good fairy who yet to give the princess her gift and uses it to partially reverse the wicked fairy's curse, proclaiming that the princess will instead fall into a deep sleep for 100 years and be awoken by a king's son.
