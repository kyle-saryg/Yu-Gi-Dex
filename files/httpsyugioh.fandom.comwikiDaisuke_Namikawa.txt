 Daisuke Namikawa  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices Ryota Kajiki ( Mako Tsunami ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) [2] ,  Leo Akaba  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  and  Otes  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS .
