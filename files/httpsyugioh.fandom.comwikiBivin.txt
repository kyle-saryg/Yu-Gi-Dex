 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In  Tag Force 1-3 , he is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy  who is lacking in self-confidence.
 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , upon acquiring his  Deck Recipe , he also gifts the player with a " Freed the Matchless General " card.
 
 Tag Force 4 
 In his own story, he competes with the Player against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the  Player  will receive a photo of himself,  Terence ,  Simon ,  Rio  and Bivin called "City Guy".
