 Kenjiro Tsuda  is a Japanese voice actor.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ), he voices  Seto Kaiba ,  Priest Seto  and  Legendary Knight Critias [2] .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ), he voices Seto Kaiba and  Kaibaman . [3]  He reprised the role of Seto Kaiba in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions   in 2016.
