 Kyoji Mikado  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . He is  Futoshi 's private tutor.
 
 Etymology 
 The name "Mikado" is a play on the kanji character 帝 (tei, i.e. monarch), which can be read as "Mikado".
