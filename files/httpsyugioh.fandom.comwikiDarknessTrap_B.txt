   English 
 
 
 Activate  only by the effect of " Darkness/Trap A ". Your opponent cannot activate  Spell / Trap Cards  in response to this card's activation. If you  control  "Darkness/Trap A", apply the following effect. ● Activate the first effect of all  Set  " Darkness/Trap C ", " Darkness/Trap D " and " Darkness/Trap E " in your  Spell & Trap Zones  in any order. -I activate " Infinity "!-
