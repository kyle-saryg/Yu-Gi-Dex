 Jack Atlas  (ジャック・アトラス,  Jakku Atorasu ) is a character in the  Duel Terminal  arcade games. This is a video game depiction of  Jack Atlas , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 The background music " Jack Battle " plays when the player Duels Jack.
