Toon  terrain is a kind of  terrain  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses . It is created by activating " Toon World ". All  Toon monsters  gain 500 ATK and DEF, while non-Toon monsters lose 500 ATK and DEF. 
 The following monsters that are powered up on this terrain: " Dark Rabbit ", " Parrot Dragon ", " Manga Ryu-Ran ", " Bickuribox ", " Stuffed Animal ", " Hungry Burger ", " Saggi the Dark Clown ", " Niwatori ", " Genin ", " Toon Mermaid ", " Toon Alligator ", and " Toon Summoned Skull ".
