 Weekly Shōnen Jump 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Weekly Shōnen Jump  promotional cards  are  OCG  promotional cards included with issues of the  Weekly Shōnen Jump  magazine.
 One  TCG  card, the  JMP-001  " Blue-Eyes White Dragon "  Shonen Jump  Vol. 1, Issue 1 promotional card , was released with  Weekly Shōnen Jump  2003, Issue 3–4 . It is not included in this list.
 
 Breakdown 
 Includes member of the " Amazoness ", " Archfiend ", " Black Luster Soldier ", " Cubic ", " D/D ", " Dark Magician ", " Dododo ", " Elemental HERO ", " Fire Fist ", " Meklord ", " Malefic ", " Number ", " Red Dragon Archfiend ", " Speedroid ", " T.G. " and " ZW - " archetypes.
