 Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule GB 
 
 
 
 Imori  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule GB . He is the game's incarnation of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  character,  Imori .
 The player encounters Imori on the second and third floors.
