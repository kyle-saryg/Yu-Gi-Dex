 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 116 ,  Varis  uses this card during his second  Master Duel  against  Soulburner . He Special Summons this card from his hand while he controls a face-up " Anesthrokket Dragon ". Varis then links both monsters to  Link Summon  " Quadborrel Dragon ". This card's effect then activates, adding " Rokket Synchron " from Varis' Deck to his hand, which he Normal Summons, activating its effect to revive this card, before tuning both to  Synchro Summon  " Borreload Savage Dragon ".
