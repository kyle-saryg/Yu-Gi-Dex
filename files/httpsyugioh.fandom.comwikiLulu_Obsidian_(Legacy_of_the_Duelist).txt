 Lulu Obsidian , known as  Ruri Kurosaki  in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Lulu Obsidian , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
