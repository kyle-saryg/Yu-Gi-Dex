 Duelist Pack: Jaden Yuki 2  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the third set in the Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Chazz Princeton .

 Features 
 The set features cards used by  Jaden Yuki  from the Yu-Gi-Oh! GX series. The cards in the set are mostly reprints with a few new cards.
 These new cards are  Card Trooper ,  Light Laser ,  Burial from a Different Dimension , Edge Hammer and  Kid Guard .
 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Jaden Yuki 2  contains 5 cards and each box contains 15 packs. In the  TCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Jaden Yuki 2  contains 6 cards and each box contains 30 packs. There are 30 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
