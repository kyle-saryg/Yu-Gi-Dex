 KENN  ( ＫＥＮＮ   Ken ), born  Kenichiro Ohashi  ( 大 橋   賢 一 郎   Ōhashi Ken'ichirō ) is a Japanese voice actor.
 He provides the voice of Judai Yuki ( Jaden Yuki ) and  The Supreme King  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . [2] 
 He provides the vocals in the song " Wake Up Your Heart ", while the band The NaB's provide the music. [3]
