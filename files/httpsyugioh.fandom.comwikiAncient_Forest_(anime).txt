 Ancient Forest 
 
 Japanese:   古の森 
 Romaji:   Inishie no Mori 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Field 
 
 When you  activate  this card, change any  Defense Position   monster (s) on the  field  to  face-up   Attack Position .  Flip Effects  are not activated at this time. Monsters can be  Normal  or  Special Summoned  in Defense Position, but cannot be Set or changed to Defense Position. If a monster  attacks ,  destroy  it at the end of the  Battle Phase .
