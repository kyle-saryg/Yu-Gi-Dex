 Koji Ishi , ( 石井   康嗣 ,  Ishī Kōji ), born  Kozo Ishi  ( 石井   浩三 ,  Ishī Kōzō ) is a Japanese voice actor.
 In Toei's  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series, he voices  Hirutani . [1] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , he voices  Rare Hunter 3  and Mask of Darkness ( Umbra ). [2] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters: Pyramid of Light , he voices  Anubis . [3] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , he voices Takasu ( Mr. Armstrong ). [4]
