 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
A player  controlling  this  monster  loses 300  Life Points  during each of his/her  Standby Phases  when this card is  face-up  on the  field . Control of this card is shifted to your opponent by  paying  500 Life Points at your own  End Phase . 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Tant que cette carte reste face recto sur le Terrain, elle inflige à son contrôleur 300 points de dommages durant chacune de ses Standby Phases. Si le contrôleur paye 500 Life Points durant sa End Phase, le contrôleur de ce monstre est changé. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Wenn diese Karte offen auf dem Spielfeld liegt, verursacht sie bei dem Spieler, der sie kontrolliert, in jeder seiner Standby Phasen 300 Punkte Schaden. Die Kontrolle dieser Karte geht auf den Gegner über, wenn der Spieler, der sie kontrolliert, in seiner End Phase 500 Life Points bezahlt. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Fino a quando questa carta resta scoperta sul Terreno, infligge al suo controllore 300 punti di danno durante ogni sua Standby Phase. Se il controllore paga 500 Life Points durante la sua End Phase, il controllore di questa carta viene scambiato. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Portuguese 
 
 
 Enquanto este card estiver no Campo com a face para cima, o controlador deste monstro perde 300 Life Points durante cada uma das suas Standby Phases. O controle deste card passa para o seu adversário se você pagar 500 Life Points na sua End Phase.

This card inflicts 300 points of damage to the Life Points of this card's controller during each of his/her Standby Phases. Control of this card is shifted to your opponent by paying 500 Life Points at your own End Phase.
