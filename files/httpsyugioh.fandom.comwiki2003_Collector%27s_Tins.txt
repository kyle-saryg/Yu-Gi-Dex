 The  2003 Collector's Tins  are six  collectible tins , released in 2003.
 Each tin contains one promotional card, which is depicted on the tin and one of each of the following  Booster Packs ;  Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon ,  Metal Raiders ,  Spell Ruler  (known as  Magic Ruler  at the time),  Pharaoh's Servant  and  Labyrinth of Nightmare . In North America, this was the first release for the 5 booster packs to show  Magic Card s  renamed to  Spell Card s . However, the  Spell Cards  in the  Spell Ruler  packs still had a MRL set abbreviation because the set was still printed as  Magic Ruler . 
 
 Breakdown 
 There are six  Secret Rare  promotional cards, consisting of two  Normal Monsters , three  Effect Monsters  and one  Fusion Monster . Two of the cards are used by  Yugi Muto , two are used by  Seto Kaiba  and two are used by  Joey Wheeler .
