 " Armor Ninja " ( 機 甲 忍 者   Kikō Ninja ) is a " Ninja " sub- series  used by  Kaze  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , consisting of four  Effect Monsters  (one for each elemental  Attribute , making this a Four Elements series) and two  Xyz Monsters .
 Their main strategy is to  swarm  the  field  quickly and modify  Levels  in order to  Xyz Summon  almost instantly. They are supported by cards such as " Ninja Grandmaster Hanzo " and " Upstart Golden Ninja ".
 " Earth Armor Ninja " is the main monster. Combine it with " Air Armor Ninja " to Xyz Summon " Blade Armor Ninja " or combine with " Flame Armor Ninja " to Xyz Summon " Number 12: Crimson Shadow Armor Ninja ".
 
 " Blade Armor Ninja " can still use its effect on itself for a 2200 point double attacker, making it great for clearing the opponent's field. 
 " Number 12: Crimson Shadow Armor Ninja " is designed to protect the monsters ninjas. Cannot be destroyed by battle or by card effects.
