 Yugi's Uncut Card Sheet promotion 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yugi's Uncut Card Sheet promotion  was a lottery in the Korean  OCG  held from August to November 2018. To enter, players had to mail in 20 empty and undamaged Booster Pack sleeves from  Code of the Duelist ,  Circuit Break ,  Extreme Force ,  Flames of Destruction  and/or  Cybernetic Horizon . During the 3rd round,  Soul Fusion  packs were also allowed to be entered, due to its release during that time. [1]  Prizes were sent out at the end of November.
 The promotion ran in three rounds, with the following mail-in periods.
 
 August 14 – September 16, winners announced September 21 [2] 
 September 17 – October 14, winners announced October 23 [3] 
 October 15 – November 18, winners announced December 12 [4]
