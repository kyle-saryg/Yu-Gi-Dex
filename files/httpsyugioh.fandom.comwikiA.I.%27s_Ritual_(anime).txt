 Effect 
 This card can be used to  Ritual Summon  any " @Ignister "  Ritual Monster . You must also  Tribute  "@Ignister"  monsters  from your  hand  or  field  whose total  Levels  equal or exceed the Level of the Ritual Monster you Ritual Summon. If you control a  WATER  "@Ignister" monster at  activation , you can also  banish  "@Ignister" monsters from your  GY .
