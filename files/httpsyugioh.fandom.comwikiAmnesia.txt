 Continuous 
 Continuous 
 Trigger-like 
 Activate  this card by  targeting  1  face-up  card on the  field ; that card's name becomes "Amnesia" while this card is face-up on the field. If that card  leaves the field ,  destroy  this card. If this  Set  card in your  Spell & Trap Zone  leaves the field: The card name of all face-up monsters your opponent currently controls becomes "Amnesia" until the end of this  turn .
