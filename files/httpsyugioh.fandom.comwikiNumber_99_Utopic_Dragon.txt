 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
3  Level  10  monsters You can also  Xyz Summon  this card by  discarding  1 " Rank-Up-Magic "  Spell Card  and using a " Utopia " monster you  control  as the  Xyz Material . (Xyz Materials  attached  to that monster also become Xyz Materials on this card.)  Once per turn : You can  target  1 " Number " monster in your Graveyard;  Special Summon  it in  Defense Position , but its  effects  are  negated . During either player's  turn , when a monster effect is  activated  that targets this  face-up  card: You can  detach  1 Xyz Material from this card; negate the activation, and if you do,  destroy  that card.

(Les Matériels Xyz attachés au monstre deviennent aussi des Matériels Xyz de cette carte.) Während des Spielzugs eines beliebigen Spielers, wenn ein Monstereffekt aktiviert wird, der diese offene Karte als Ziel wählt: Du kannst 1 Xyz-Material von dieser Karte abhängen; annulliere die Aktivierung und falls du dies tust, zerstöre die Karte.
