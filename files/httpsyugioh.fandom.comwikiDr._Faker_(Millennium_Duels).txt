 Dr. Faker  ( Ｄｒ． フェイカー  Dokutā Feikā ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Dr. Faker , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
