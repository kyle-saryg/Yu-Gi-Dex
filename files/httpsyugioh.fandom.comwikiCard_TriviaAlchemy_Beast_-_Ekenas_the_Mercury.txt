 An  Echeneis  is a legendary creature described in medieval bestiaries. It is a described as a fish that is half a foot in length. It is said to latch on to ships, holding them back. They are said to be found in the Indian Ocean. 
 Despite mercury being a metal, its attribute is  WATER  because mercury is a liquid at room temperature, similar to water.
