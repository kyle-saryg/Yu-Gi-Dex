 Dark Revelation Volume 3  is a combined reprint set in the  TCG .
 The  OCG  equivalent of this Booster Pack is  Expert Edition Volume 3 .
 The rarities duplicate exactly their parent sets, but do not include  Ultimate Rares . This set was initially released only with the  Ultimate Edition 2   Special Edition  blister pack.
