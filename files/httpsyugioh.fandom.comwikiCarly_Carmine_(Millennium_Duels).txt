 Carly Carmine , known as  Carly Nagisa  (カーリー渚,  Kārī Nagisa ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Carly Carmine , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
