 Dark Master Zorc  is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He was the avatar of  Dark Bakura , used in his game of  Monster World  against  Yugi ,  Jonouchi ,  Honda ,  Anzu  and  Miho Nosaka .
 
 Biography 
 Dark Bakura  played the role of Zorc in a game of  Monster World  against  Yugi ,  Jonouchi ,  Honda ,  Anzu  and  Miho Nosaka , who played the adventurers, using the minatures;  Yugi ,  Jonouchi ,  Anzu ,  Hiroto , and  Miho .
