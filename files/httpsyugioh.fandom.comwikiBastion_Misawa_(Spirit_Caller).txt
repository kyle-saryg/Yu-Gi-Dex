 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller 
 
 
 
 Bastion Misawa , known as  Daichi Misawa  (三沢大地,  Misawa Daichi ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Bastion Misawa , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
