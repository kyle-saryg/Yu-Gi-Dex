 Aster Phoenix , known as  Edo Phoenix  (エド・フェニックス,  Edo Fenikkusu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Aster Phoenix , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
