 Property 
 
 
   Quick-Play 
 
 If an opponent's  monster  is  destroyed by battle , or leaves the  field  because of a  card effect :  Target  1  Link -1  Link Monster  in each  GY ;  banish  them, then  Special Summon  1 " Appliancer " monster from your GY, and if you do, your opponent Special Summons 1 of their  banished  Link Monsters, and the Special Summoned monsters cannot be destroyed by battle until the end of the  turn . You can only  activate  1 "Appliancer Recycle" per turn.
