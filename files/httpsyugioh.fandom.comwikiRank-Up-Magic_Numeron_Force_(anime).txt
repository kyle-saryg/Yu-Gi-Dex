 Until the end of the  turn ,  negate  the  effects  of any currently  face-up  cards on the  field  other than this card.  Target  1 face-up  Xyz Monster  you  control ;  Xyz Summon  from your  Extra Deck , 1 " C " Xyz Monster that is 1  Rank  higher than that  monster  you control, by using it as the  Xyz Material . (Xyz Materials  attached  to it also become Xyz Materials on the  Summoned  monster.) The Xyz Monster that was Xyz Summoned by this effect negates any "cannot be  destroyed by battle " effects.
