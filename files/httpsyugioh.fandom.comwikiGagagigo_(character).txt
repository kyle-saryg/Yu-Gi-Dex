 Gagagigo  is a character version of the card, " Gagagigo ".
 In the manga, Gagagigo appeared as an Egyptian monster spirit. In the  OCG / TCG , his  card illustration history  depicts him as a tragic hero, who fights many battles, but loses sense of his original self after a body reconstruction. He appears as a free Duel opponent in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . Other video games also featured him, at  different stages  of his life, as Free Duel opponents.

 Manga biography 
       Gagagigo in the manga.     
 During the final battle against  Zorc Necrophades ,  Hiroto Honda  summoned Gagagigo alongside  Yugi Mutou 's  Dark Magician  and  Katsuya Jonouchi 's  Red-Eyes Black Dragon  to fight Zorc. [1] 
 
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 Gagagigo appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . He is tag partners with  Goblin Elite Attack Force .
 
 Deck 
 Gagagigo uses a Deck titled, "Ga-ga Goo-goo".
