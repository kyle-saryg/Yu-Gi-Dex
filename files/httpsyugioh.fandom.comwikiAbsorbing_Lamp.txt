 Continuous 
 When your opponent  activates  a  Spell Card : Activate this card by  targeting  that Spell Card;  negate  the activation, and if you do, add that target to your  hand . While this card is  face-up  on the  field , that card is treated as your own card. If this card  leaves the field , return that card to your opponent's hand.
