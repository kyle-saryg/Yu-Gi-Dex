 The Ultimate Rising  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was available in-game from the onset of the video game's main release, as well as its beta release.
 
 Description 
 The Ultimate Dragon is Here! Unleash its Formidable Power!! 
 Seto Kaiba 's strongest signature monster " Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon " and other mighty monsters such as " Axe Raider ". 
 " Sonic Bird " and " Fusion Gate ", which are great cards for  Ritual Summoning  and  Fusion Summoning  respectively. 
 " Twister ", which can destroy  Spell  and  Trap Cards . 
 Handy Spell/Trap cards such as " Mask of the Accursed " and " Reinforcements ". 
 Total of 100 types: 10  Ultra Rares , 12  Super Rares , 32  Rares , and 46  Normals 
 
 Each box of  The Ultimate Rising  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, with these consisting of either 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card; OR 1 Common card, 1 Rare card, and 1 Super Rare, Ultra Rare, or (additional) Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Malice Ascendant ",  King of Yamimakai ", " Faith Bird ", " The Snake Hair ", " Card Rotator ", and " 7 ".
