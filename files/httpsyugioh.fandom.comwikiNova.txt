 Decks 
 Nova runs two types of Decks in the Tag Force series, both of which aim to  deck out  his opponent.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Nova uses a Deck based on the  Exchange of the Spirit OTK .
 In the following games, as " Exchange of the Spirit " is  Forbidden  in the default banlists, his Decks become  Remove from play / Mill  Decks  focusing on " Necroface ".
