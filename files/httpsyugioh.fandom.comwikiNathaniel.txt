 Nathaniel , known as  Isomaki  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. He is a chauffeur for  Akira Zaizen .

 Biography 
 Season one 
 Nathaniel drove Akira to the meeting point with  Emma Bessho , and opened the door. [2] 
 Following an anonymous tip from Emma, Nathaniel waited in a car outside of  SOL Technologies  in time to collect Akira when he came running out. He told Akira to get in and that he would take him to the hospital that  Skye Zaizen  was in. [1] 
 
 Season two 
 Nathaniel drove Akira to a gazebo, where he was to meet with Emma and Skye. [3]
