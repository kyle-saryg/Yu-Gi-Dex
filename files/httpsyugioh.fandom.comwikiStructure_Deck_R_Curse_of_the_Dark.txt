 Structure Deck R: Curse of the Dark  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the sixth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck R series, following  Structure Deck R: Surge of Divine Light .

 Features 
 This Deck continues the theme of  Structure Deck: Curse of Darkness , using  DARK   monsters  in battle, but with new cards that can be used in the current environment.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck R: Curse of the Dark  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 40 cards
 1  Ultra Rare 
 3  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 30  Commons 
 1 Ultra Rare promotional card 
 1 Common  Token 
 1 Rulebook 
 1 Double-sided  Playmat /Playing Guide
