 Dimension Box Limited Edition  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . [1]

 Features 
 Includes new members and/or support for the " Abyss Actor ", " D/D/D ", " Dark Contract ", " Edge Imp ", " Frightfur ", " Lunalight ", " Magician ", " Melodious ", " Phantom Knights ", " Predap ", " Predaplant ", " Raidraptor ", " Speedroid ", " Superheavy Samurai ", " The Phantom Knights " and " Windwitch " archetypes. 
 Includes new artworks of the  Four Dimension Dragons . 
 Includes members/support for the " Blackwing ", " D/D ", " Galaxy ", " HERO ", " Red Dragon Archfiend " and " Superheavy Samurai " archetypes. 
 Includes cards used by  Aster Phoenix ,  Jack Atlas ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Gong Strong ,  Yuto ,  Yugo , and  Yuri .
 According to promotional material, it includes cards used by  Zuzu Boyle ,  Declan Akaba ,  Sylvio Sawatari ,  Sun Shadow  and  Moon Shadow ,  Rin ,  Jack Atlas ,  Crow Hogan ,  Chojiro Tokumatsu ,  Lulu Obsidian ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Celina ,  Sora Perse ,  Dennis McField ,  Aster Phoenix , and  Alexis Rhodes .

 1 Preconstructed Deck of 45 cards
 15  Ultra Parallel Rares
