 Extra Pack 
 Extra Pack Volume 2 
 
 
 
 
 
 Extra Pack  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -exclusive cards to the  OCG .

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive  cards from the  Booster Packs   Tactical Evolution ,  Gladiator's Assault , and  Phantom Darkness . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 10 cards per pack and 10 packs per box.
 The set includes 32 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 2  Ultra Rare 
 4  Super Rare 
 6  Rare 
 1  Normal Rare 
 19  Common
