 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Printed Effect Once per  turn , this card cannot be  destroyed  by  card effects . When this card is  activated :  Set  1 " Darkness/Trap A ", " Darkness/Trap B ", " Darkness/Trap C ", " Darkness/Trap D " and " Darkness/Trap E " directly from your  hand  or  Deck . The activation and effect of  Continuous Trap Cards  you control cannot be  negated . -I activate " Darkness "!- Hidden Effect Once per turn: You can  return  all other  Spell / Trap Cards  on the  field  to the hand.
