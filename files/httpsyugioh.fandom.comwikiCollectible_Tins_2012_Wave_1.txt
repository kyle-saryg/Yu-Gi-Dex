 Breakdown 
 Every tin contains 1  Super Rare  " Wind-Up Zenmaines " and five  Booster Packs ; included packs depend on the region:
 
 North American 
 3  Photon Shockwave  packs 
 2  Galactic Overlord  packs 
 French 
 2  Order of Chaos  packs 
 3  Galactic Overlord  packs 
 United Kingdom, German, Italian, and Spanish 
 1  Photon Shockwave  pack 
 2  Order of Chaos  packs 
 2  Galactic Overlord  packs 
 In addition, each tin type includes a different set of promos:
 
 "Evolzar Dolkka" 
 1  Secret Rare  " Evolzar Dolkka " 
 3  Super Rare  monsters: " Genex Neutron ", " Scrap Dragon " and " Dark Highlander " 
 "Heroic Champion - Excalibur" 
 1  Secret Rare  " Heroic Champion - Excalibur " 
 3  Super Rare  monsters: " Blizzard Princess ", " Wind-Up Rabbit " and " Evolzar Laggia "
