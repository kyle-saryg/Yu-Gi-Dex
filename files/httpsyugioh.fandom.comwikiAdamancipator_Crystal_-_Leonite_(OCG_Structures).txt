 Condition 
 
 
 If this card is  Special Summoned  by the  effect  of an " Adamancipator " card: You can place 1 "Adamancipator" card from your  hand  or  GY  on the top of the  Deck . If this card is in your  GY : You can  target  1  FIRE   Synchro Monster  you  control  or in your GY;  return  it to the  Extra Deck , and if you do, place this card on the top of the Deck. You can only use each effect of "Adamancipator Crystal - Leonite"  once per turn .
