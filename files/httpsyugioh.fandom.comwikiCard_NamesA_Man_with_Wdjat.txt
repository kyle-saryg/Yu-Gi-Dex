 The unofficial  Greek  name given translates to " A Man with the Eye of Wdjat ".
 A possible translation of the OCG name of this card (" Man Holding the Eye of Wdjat ") in Greek could be " Ο Άντρας που Κρατά/Κατέχει το Μάτι του Wdjat ".
