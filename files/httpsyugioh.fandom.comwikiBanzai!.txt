 Banzai!
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Frequency
 
 
 Monthly (2001–2005) 
 
 Banzai!  was the monthly German language adaptation of the popular Japanese manga anthology  Weekly Shōnen Jump . 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  ran in  Banzai!  from September 2002 to January 2005.
