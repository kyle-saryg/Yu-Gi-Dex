 [  Fairy  /  Effect  ] Select  2 " Cubic Seed "  monsters  you  control , stack them on top of each other, and stack this card on top of them to  Special Summon  this card. This  effect  can be  activated  during the  Battle Phase . When this card is Special Summoned, inflict 800  damage  to your opponent.This card gain 800  original ATK  for each monster stacked underneath it. After this card  battled ,  return  it to the  Deck  to  add  1 " Indiora Doom Volt the Cubic Emperor " from your Deck to your  hand . During this  turn , Special Summon the "Cubic Seed" monsters stacked underneath this card to your  field . [1] [2]
