 Yuki Nakao  is a Japanese voice actress.
 She voices various minor characters.

 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) she voices Ryota Kajiki ( Mako Tsunami ) in his childhood, Black Magician Girl ( Dark Magician Girl ),  Sister , computer audio, a synthetic voice,  Rick ,  KC operator 2 ,  Boy 5  and  Mana . [1] 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ) she voices Black Magician Girl ( Dark Magician Girl ) and  Echo . [2]
