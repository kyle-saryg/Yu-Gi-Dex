 Structure Deck: Sacred Beasts of Chaos  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  (OCG). It is the 40th Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Rebirth of Shaddoll .
 The theme for this Structure Deck is based on a poll conducted by Konami on its Official Card Database site during June 21 to July 3, 2019. [1] . The " Sacred Beasts " theme achieved second place on that poll.
