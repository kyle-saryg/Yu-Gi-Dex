 Ishizu Ishtar  (イシズ・イシュタール,  Ishizu Ishutāru ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Ishizu Ishtar , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
