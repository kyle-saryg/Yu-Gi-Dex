 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  monsters , including a  Level / Rank / Link -2 monster Cannot be used as  Link Material  the  turn  it was  Link Summoned . You can only use 1 of the following  effects  of "Spright Sprind" per turn, and only  once that turn . If this card is Link Summoned: You can  send  1 Level 2 monster from your  Deck  to the  GY . If another monster(s) is  Special Summoned  while this monster is on the  field : You can  detach  1  material  from your  Xyz Monster , then  target  1 monster on the field;  return  it to the  hand .
