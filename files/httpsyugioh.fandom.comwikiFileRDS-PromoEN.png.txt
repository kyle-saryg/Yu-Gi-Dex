 The Rise of Destiny: Special Edition  is a  Special Edition  box, which contained three  Rise of Destiny  packs and one of four  Ultra Rare  cards: " Diffusion Wave-Motion ", " Dark Magician Girl ", " Judgment of Anubis " or " Vampire Lord ".
