 The  2012 European Championship  is a continental  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . Players who had been successful in other European tournaments get to compete to qualify to become the European champion and qualify for the World Championship. It took place in Milan, Italy from June 6−8, 2012. [1] 
 
 Participants 
 The following players get to participate in the 2012 European Championship
 
 Players who earned a qualification at a regional  World Championship Qualifier . 
 Players who earned a qualification at a national World Championship Qualifier. 
 The top four players from the  2011 European Championship . 
 The top thirty two players from each  Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series  since July 2011. 
 The winner was  Stefano Memoli  from Milan with a "Wind-Up" Deck.The top 4 were Stefano Memoli (first place), Michail Paraschos (second place with Chaos Dragons), Joshua Schmidt (third place with Wind-Ups) and Angel Matas (fourth place with Inzektors). [ citation needed ]
