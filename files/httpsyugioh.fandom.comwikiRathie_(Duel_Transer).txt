 Rathie , known as  Kurumizawa  (志崎) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Duel Transer . This is a video game depiction of  Rathie , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime, whose English name was unknown until his appearance in this video game. He is encountered as the final opponent in the Yggdrasil Entrance, informing the players of his  PDA Netshop  and awarding the player a " Dark Magician Girl " card upon his defeat.
 
 
 Decks 
 "Idol Love" is a fan Deck consisting of various  female  monsters and cards with females in their artworks, which reflects Rathie's interests within the anime and the  Tag Force  series. It even includes a copy of " Novox's Prayer ", but lacks the corresponding Ritual Monster, " Skull Guardian ".
