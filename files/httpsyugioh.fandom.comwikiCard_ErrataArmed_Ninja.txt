 English 
 
 
 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 FLIP:  Destroys  1  Magic  Card on the field. If this  card's target  is  face-down ,  flip it face-up . If  the card  is a  Magic  Card, it is destroyed. If  not ,  it is returned  to its  face-down  position.  The flipped card is not activated. 
 FLIP:  Select  1  Spell  Card on the field  and destroy it . If the  selected  card  is  Set ,  pick up and see  the card . If  it is a  Spell  Card , it is destroyed . If  it is a Trap Card ,  return it  to its  original  position.

 Second erratum 
 Third erratum 
 
 
 FLIP:  Destroy  1 Spell Card on the field. If the  card  is  face-down ,  look at  the card   and destroy it if  it is a Spell Card.  If it is a Trap Card , return it to its original position. 
 FLIP:  Target  1 Spell Card on the field ; destroy that target .  ( If the  target  is  Set ,  reveal it,  and destroy it if it is a Spell Card.  Otherwise , return it to its original position. )
