 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 14 ,  Ghost Gal  uses this card during her  Speed Duel  against  Playmaker . She  Sets  this card from her hand on the first turn. On Ghost Gal's next turn, she activates this card to revive " Altergeist Marionetter ". 
 In  episode 30 , Ghost Gal uses this card during her Speed Duel against  Varis . She Sets this card directly from her Deck using the effect of "Altergeist Marionetter". In the  following episode , Ghost Gal activates this card to revive " Altergeist Kidolga ", before Setting another copy of this card. The first copy is destroyed when the equipped "Kidolga" is destroyed by Varis' (unseen) " Mirror Force ". On Varis' next turn, the effect of his " Topologic Trisbaena " banishes the second copy of this card.
