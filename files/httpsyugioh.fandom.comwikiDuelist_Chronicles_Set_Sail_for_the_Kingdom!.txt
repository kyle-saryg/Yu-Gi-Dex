 Event details  
 Collect Dice Fragments by dueling in Duel World and Ranked Duels. 
 Use Dice Fragments to roll 1-3. 
 Move along the board earning Millenium Coins. 
 Collect Millenium Coins and try you luck with the Card Lottery. 
 Earn Zone Reward for each zone that you clear.
