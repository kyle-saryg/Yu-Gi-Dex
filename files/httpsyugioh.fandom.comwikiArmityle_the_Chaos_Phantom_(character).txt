 Armityle the Chaos Phantom  is a character version of the card, " Armityle the Chaos Phantom ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , this character and  Phantom of Chaos  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents during Story Mode (clear chapter 4). Their Tag Team name is  Armityle Summoning .
