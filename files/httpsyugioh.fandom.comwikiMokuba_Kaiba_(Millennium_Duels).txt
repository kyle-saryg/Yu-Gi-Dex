 Mokuba Kaiba  (海馬モクバ,  Kaiba Mokuba ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Mokuba Kaiba , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
