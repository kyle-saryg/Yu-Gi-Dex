Mimic  is an unofficial term used to describe a  card effect  that can copy another card's  ATK / DEF ,  Level / Rank , effects, or name, or a card with such an effect. Most name mimic effects are  Continuous Effects  that usually help get the stronger monsters of its  archetype  out, such as " Proto-Cyber Dragon " or " Elemental HERO Neos Alius ".
