 Zexal  (ゼアル  Zearu ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Zexal , a character who is the combined form of  Yuma Tsukumo  and  Astral  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
