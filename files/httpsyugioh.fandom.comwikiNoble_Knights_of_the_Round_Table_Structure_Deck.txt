 Noble Knights of the Round Table Structure Deck 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Noble Knights of the Round Table Structure Deck  is an original product in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena . It was only available after the "urgent maintenance" on November 21, 2014 until after maintenance on December 17, 2014.
 Each product costs 20,000 Miniclip Credits.
 This Structure Deck is composed of the contents of  Noble Knights of the Round Table Box Set  and  Noble Knights of the Round Table Power-Up Pack , with a " Token Thanksgiving " card included to celebrate the upcoming  Thanksgiving  after its release.
 
 Description 
 Noble Knights have grown steadily stronger since their debut, but have lacked the guiding focus of Merlin, the fabled wizard of Arthurian legend! The Noble Knights for challenging tournament play by adding key cards to this already popular Deck theme. Merlin has now arrived, seeking the once and future king, and has the power to take a Noble Knight from your Deck, and turn him into a leader of men.
