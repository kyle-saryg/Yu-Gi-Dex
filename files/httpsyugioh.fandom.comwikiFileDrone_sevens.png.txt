 Goha Corporation  (ゴーハ コーポレイション,  Goha Kōporeishon ), usually shortened simply to " Goha " is a corporate entity that controls  Goha City .

 Authorities 
 Goha Corp control various aspects of citizens' lives, including food, clothing, shelter, and  Duel Monsters , even down to restricting how Duelists can trade cards. Breaking these rules awards a penalty to a Duelist; after accumulating six penalties, a Duelist's account is banned and their  Duel Disk  is locked down. [1]
