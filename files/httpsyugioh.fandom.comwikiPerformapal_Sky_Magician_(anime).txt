Main card page: " Performapal Sky Magician " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Magician "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 魔 術 師 」 .   As its English name contains "Magician", it  may  be treated a member of the "Magician" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.       
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Sky "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 天 」 .   As its English name contains "Sky", it  may  be treated a member of the "Sky" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
