 " Catharsis " (カタルシス,  Katarushisu ) is a musical composition from the Japanese  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 Catharsis (κάθαρσις) is a Greek word meaning "cleansing" or "purging".
 It is 1:26 long and has been released on the soundtrack  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Sound Duel Vol II .
 
 Appearances 
 "Catharsis" is played in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time  when the  Crimson Dragon  appears to help  Yusei Fudo  travel back in time.
