 The Japanese name of this card ( Iron Heart ) can be translated in Greek as " Σιδερένια Καρδιά ". 
 In the French version of  Card Almanac , this card name was  "Outil ancien" . 
 In the German version of  Card Almanac , this card name was  "Antike Maschine" . 
 In the Italian version of  Card Almanac , this card name was  "Antico Attrezzo" . 
 In the Spanish version of  Card Almanac , this card name was  "Herramienta antigua" .
 The Spanish printed card of this name has a small typo, as it's " Herr e mienta Antigua " instead of " Herr a mienta Antigua ".
