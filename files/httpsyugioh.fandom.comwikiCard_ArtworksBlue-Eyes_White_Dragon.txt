 This card is given different artworks in all media it has been released in, featuring it in different positions and with different backgrounds. Also, in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , this monster was drawn with a different artwork. Its  Rush Duel  variant is drawn in the style of this card's first  OCG  artwork, but with more details.
