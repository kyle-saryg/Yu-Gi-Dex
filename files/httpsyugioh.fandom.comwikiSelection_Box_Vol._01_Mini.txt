 Selection Box Vol. 01 Mini  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was only available during a limited period between May 11, 2018 and Jun 11, 2018 and can no longer be purchased in-game. The majority of the cards in the set are reprints of those in other sets, though a handful of new cards are also included.
 The box was later available again from January 12 2019 to January 24 2019.
 
 Description 
 Each box of  Selection Box Vol. 01 Mini  contains 36 packs. Each pack contains 12 different cards, which will always consist of 7 Normal (aka Common) cards, 4 Rare cards, and 1 Super Rare or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 8 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 12 copies 
 Recommended Cards 
 
 " Sangan " When this monster is sent from the field to the Graveyard, you can add a monster with 1500 ATK or less from your Deck to your hand. It is a highly versatile card, as you can search for any monster as long as it has a low ATK. 
" Offerings to the Doomed " A Quick Spell that destroys 1 face-up monster. Try to finish the Duel within the turn you use this card, as it will skip your next Draw Phase. 
" King of the Swamp " This monster lets you add " Polymerization " to your hand, and it can also be used as a substitute Fusion Material. It will be a key card in Decks that use Fusion Summoning. 
" Fire Formation - Gyokkou " A Continuous Spell that prevents your opponent from activating the targeted Set Spell/Trap Card. Your opponent cannot chain the Set card that was targeted, so this card will hardly ever be wasted.
