 Structure Deck: Marik  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the eighth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Kaiba Volume 2 . It is the last Structure Deck in the  OCG  to be based off characters from the anime series.

 Features 
 The deck features cards used by  Yami Marik , and also some of  Strings ' " Slime " cards with their corresponding support cards. It also contained a lot of cards from  Labyrinth of Nightmare ,  Invasion of Chaos  and  Pharaonic Guardian , including a few " Gravekeeper's " monsters and support cards, representing  Marik Ishtar 's personal history. Two new cards are released for the first time, those being " Magical Stone Excavation " and " Malevolent Catastrophe ".
 The first 40 cards are in their own set, while a separator divides the last bit from the rest.
