The  Arabic, Croatian, Greek  and  Thai                     names given are not official.  
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Warrior "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ウォリアー」 .   As its English name contains "Warrior", it  may  be treated a member of the "Warrior" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . Must first be  Special Summoned  (from your  hand ) by  Tributing  1 " Alpha The Magnet Warrior ", " Beta The Magnet Warrior ", and " Gamma The Magnet Warrior " from your  hand  and/or  field . You can Tribute this card, then  target  1 "Alpha The Magnet Warrior", "Beta The Magnet Warrior", and "Gamma The Magnet Warrior" in your  GY ; Special Summon them.
