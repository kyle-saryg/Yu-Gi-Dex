 Attribute Mastery 
 
 Japanese:   アトリビュート・マスタリー 
 Romaji:   Atoribyūto Masutarī 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 When this card is  equipped  to a  monster ,  declare  1  Attribute . At the start of the  Damage Step , if the equipped monster  battles  a monster of that Attribute,  destroy  that monster (without  damage calculation ).
