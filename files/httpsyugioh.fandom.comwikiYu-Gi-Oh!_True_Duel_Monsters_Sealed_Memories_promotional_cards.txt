 Yu-Gi-Oh! True Duel Monsters: Sealed Memories promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! True Duel Monsters: Sealed Memories  promotional cards  (遊戯王真デュエルモンスターズ 封印されし記憶 同梱カード  Yugiō Ma Dyuerumonsutāzu Fūin Sa Reshi Kioku Dōkon Kādo ) are a set of five cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! True Duel Monsters: Sealed Memories , released on December 9,  1999 . A  game guide  was later released on December 14 with a bundled  promotional card .
