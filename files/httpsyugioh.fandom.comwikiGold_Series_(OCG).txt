 The  Gold Series  set is specifically designed to be desirable to all aspects of the hobby with never before seen " gold technology ".
 This is the first set in the  Gold Series  series in the  OCG , and is followed by  Gold Series 2010 .

 Features 
 While the Japanese set includes " Jinzo ", " Exodia the Forbidden One ", and " Sangan ", the Korean set includes " Banisher of the Radiance ", " Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon ", and " Snipe Hunter ". 
 Breakdown 
 7 cards per pack, 10 packs per box.
 The set includes 20 cards, 40 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 2  Normal Rares 
 18  Commons 
 20  Gold Rares
