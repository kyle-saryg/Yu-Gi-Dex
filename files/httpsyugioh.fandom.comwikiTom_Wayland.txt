 Birth

 Gender

 Occupation 
 Voice actor 
 
 
 Tom Wayland is an American musician, actor, voice actor, ADR director, producer and dubbing supervisor who works for 4Kids Entertainment, DuArt Film and Video, NYAV Post, Real Recording LLC, Matlin Recording LLC, Beatstreet Studios and Audioworks Producers Group. Tom is also the founder of TripWire Productions, which has produced more than 200 different anime titles and overseen the voice director of many animation titles. He is also a graduate from the NYU's Tisch School Of Arts.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Wayland voiced  Abidos the Third  and  Chumley Huffington [1]  (standing in for  Ted Lewis  in the latter role in  Episode 85 ).
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Wayland voiced  Crow Hogan  from episode 94 onwards, replacing  Christopher C. Adams . He also provides the voice of  Blister ,  Lester ,  Syd  and younger  Aporia .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC V 
 Wayland voiced  Skip Boyle , before being replaced by  Billy Bob Thompson  after being falsely accused of sexual harassment.
