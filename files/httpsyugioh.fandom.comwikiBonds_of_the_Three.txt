" Bonds of the Three " (３人の絆) is an instrumental musical composition from the Japanese  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 It is 3:00 long and has been released on  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Sound Duel Vol III .
 
 Appearances 
 "Bonds of the Three" was played in the following episodes:
