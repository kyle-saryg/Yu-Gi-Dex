 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Utopia "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 希 望 皇 ホープ」 .   As its English name contains "Utopia", it  may  be treated a member of the "Utopia" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
