 Condition 
 Activate  this card by  targeting  1 " Galaxy "  monster  you  control . Once per  battle , during either player's  turn , when an  attack  is  declared  involving that target and an opponent's monster with an  ATK  equal to your monster's ATK: You can  banish  1 ??? from your  Deck ; that target gains 1500 ATK, that opponent's monster's  effects  are  negated , also that opponent's monster becomes  unaffected  by card effects. These effects are applied until the end of the  Damage Step .
