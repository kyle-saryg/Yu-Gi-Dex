 Jonouchi's father  is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is the anime incarnation of  Jonouchi's father  from the  manga .
 Mr. Jonouchi is father of  Katsuya Jonouchi  and  Shizuka Jonouchi . He suffers from alcohol and gambling addictions.
 
 Biography 
 Yugi Mutou ,  Hiroto Honda ,  Anzu Mazaki  and  Miho Nosaka  went to visit Katsuya at his apartment. Thinking them to be Katsuya coming home, Mr. Jonouchi fired a bottle at them, demanding to know where Katsuya had been, causing them to flee. [1]
