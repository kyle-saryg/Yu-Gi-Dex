 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  Normal  or  Special Summoned : You can reduce the  Level  of all  Insect   monsters  you currently  control  by 1. If this card's  battle position  is changed: You can  Special Summon  1 "Oily Cicada" from your  hand ,  Deck , or  GY . You can only use each  effect  of "Oily Cicada"  once per turn .
