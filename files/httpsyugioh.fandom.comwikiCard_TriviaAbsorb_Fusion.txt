 " Gem-Knight Master Diamond " appears in this card's artwork.
 This card represents the effect of "Gem-Knight Master Diamond" to acquire the effect(s) of the other " Gem-Knight "  Fusion Monsters . 
 This card possibly depicts the transformation of "Master Diamond" into " Cairngorgon, Antiluminescent Knight ".
