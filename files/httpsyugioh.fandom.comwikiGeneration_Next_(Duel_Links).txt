 Generation Next  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on September 21, 2017, 9:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 The future is in your hands! Call forth the Arcana Knight! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Arcana Knight Joker " can negate the effect of monster effect or Spell/Trap Card that targets it. 
" King of the Swamp " has two effects, one which adds " Polymerization " from your Deck to your hand, and another that allows it to be used as a a substitute for Fusion Material. 
" Fusion Recycling Plant " has two effects, one which adds " Polymerization " from your Deck or Graveyard to your hand, and another that allows you to add 1 monster from your Graveyard that was used as Fusion Material to your hand 
" Typhoon " can be activated from your hand under certain conditions to destroy 1 face-up Spell/Trap Card. 
A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Generation Next  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
