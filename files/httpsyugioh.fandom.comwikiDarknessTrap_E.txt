   English 
 
 
 Activate  only by the  effect  of " Darkness/Trap B ". When this card is activated: Inflict 2000  damage  to 1 opponent player. Each time another " Darkness "  Continuous Trap Card  is activated, inflict 2000 damage to 1 opponent player. -I activate " Darkness 3 "!-
