 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival Duel Champion Guide! promotional card , is a set of 1 card, included with the  strategy guide  for  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival . The card, " Ruffian Railcar ", was used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime by  Anna Kaboom .
