 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Chumley Huffington , known as  Hayato Maeda  ( 前田   隼人 ,  Maeda Hayato ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Chumley Huffington , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
