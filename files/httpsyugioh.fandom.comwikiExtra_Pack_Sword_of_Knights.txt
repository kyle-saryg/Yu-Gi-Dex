 Extra Pack: Sword of Knights  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -exclusive cards to the  OCG .

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive  cards from  Booster Packs   Galactic Overlord ,  Return of the Duelist ,  Abyss Rising ,  Cosmo Blazer  and  Lord of the Tachyon Galaxy ; as well as reprints of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2012 prize cards  as  Extra Secret Rare  cards. 
 All  Ultra Rare  cards in the set are also available as  Secret Rare . 
 Breakdown 
 5 cards per pack, 15 packs per Japanese box. 30 packs per Korean box.
