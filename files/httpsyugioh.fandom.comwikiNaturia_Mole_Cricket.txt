 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During the  Main Phase  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Tribute  this card;  Special Summon  1 " Naturia "  monster  from your  Deck . If your opponent  controls  a  face-up   monster(s)  with the highest  ATK  on the field, you can Special Summon 2 instead. If your opponent Special Summons a monster(s) from the  Extra Deck , or you Special Summon a " Naturia " monster(s) from the Extra Deck, while this card is in your  GY : You can Special Summon this card. You can only use each  effect  of "Naturia Molcricket"  once per turn .
