 Legendary Dragon Decks  is a  TCG -exclusive set that contains 3 pre-constructed Decks, and is the third set in the  Legendary Decks  series. [1] [2] [3]

 Breakdown 
 The set contains a total of 153 cards:
 
 40-card Main Deck and 11-card Extra Deck based on  Yugi ,  Kaiba , and  Joey 's " Legendary Dragon " archetype and their  Fusion Monsters . 
 40-card Main Deck and 11-card Extra Deck based on  Zane Truesdale 's " Cyber Dragon " archetype. 
 41-card Main Deck and 10-card Extra Deck based on  Yuya Sakaki 's " Odd-Eyes " archetype, as well as the other members of the  Four Dimension Dragons . 
 Each Deck includes:
 
 1 new ( TCG exclusive ) card for its respective theme. 
 5 foil cards.
