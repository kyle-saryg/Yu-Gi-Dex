 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  control  a " Pegasus/Spell A ", this card can attack your opponent directly. When this card inflicts  battle damage  to your opponent:  Look  at your opponent's  hand , also  discard  1 card from their hand. -I  Summon  " Toon Gemini Elf "!-
