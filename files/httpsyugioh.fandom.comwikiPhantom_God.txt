 Phantom God  is an  OCG -only reprint set, it contains reprints of various cards from the  Volume  pack series mostly from  Vol.2  and  Vol.3 .
 In other languages this pack was combined with the Japanese  Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon  into another set, also called  Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contained 5 cards and each box contained 30 packs.
 The set consisted of 65 cards, or 66 counting cards printed in more than one rarity. It contained:
 
 5  Secret Rare 
 2  Ultra Parallel Rare 
 4  Ultra Rare 
 5  Super Rare 
 11  Rare 
 40  Common
