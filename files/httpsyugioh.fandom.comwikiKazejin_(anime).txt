 [  Spellcaster  /  Effect  ] This card can be  Set  in your  Spell & Trap Card Zone  as a  Spell Card . It cannot be played  face-up  without a " Sanga of the Thunder " and " Suijin " on your  field . If so, you can  Special Summon  this card. Once per turn when this card is  attacked  by your opponent's  monster , you can  negate  the attack.
