 Starter Deck 2014  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the ninth deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2013 . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Super Starter: Space-Time Showdown .
 Deck Custom Pack 01  is intended to supplement this Deck.

 Features 
 Starter Deck 2014  includes:
 
 1  Enhancement Pack  to power up the Starter Deck. 
 A Special Download Code for downloading an "OCG Teaching Software" for the Nintendo 3DS that explains how to perform a  Pendulum Summon .
