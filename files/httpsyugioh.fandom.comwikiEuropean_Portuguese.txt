European Portuguese  cards are  Portuguese  cards distributed from Portugal, under the English brand  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game . They were printed using the spelling of Portugal. The ID was  P , then changed into  PT . European Portuguese cards ceased to be distributed after the release of  Cybernetic Revolution . From  Cosmo Blazer  and onwards, Portuguese cards are distributed from South America under a new brand,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Estampas Ilustradas , although the European edition still keeps the English brand.
