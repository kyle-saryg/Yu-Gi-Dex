 El Shaddoll Construct  is a character version of the card " El Shaddoll Construct ". 
 She appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P131.
 She was only obtainable by having 15 or more of the following " Shaddoll "-related cards in one's trunk (including multiple copies of 1 or more cards) before maintenance occurred on January 7, 2015: " Shaddoll Falco ", " Shaddoll Hedgehog ", " Shaddoll Squamata ", " Shaddoll Dragon ", " Shaddoll Beast ", " El Shaddoll Winda ", "El Shaddoll Construct", " Shaddoll Fusion ", " Curse of the Shadow Prison ", " Sinister Shadow Games ", and/or " Shaddoll Core ". The reason for this limited section of cards was because  The New Challengers  and newer packs had not yet been released in-game.
 Applicable players received her in their Gift Boxes on January 9, 2015.

 Description 
 The enormous shadow commands those wandering earthbound phantoms that escaped the fates of NEMESIS, in search for something unknown, Their hidden code is said to be found in the word "FOREST".
 
 Opponent Quote 
 I will make a pretty doll out of you. And you shall live forever...
 
 Decks 
 A  DARK   Deck  that focuses on  Fusion Summon  and  Flip Effect . It contains cards that  activate  their  effect  even if they are  sent  to the  Graveyard  by effect, which could easily give you an opportunity to turn things around.
