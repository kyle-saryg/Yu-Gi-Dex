 " Lofty Providence " is the sixty-fifth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on September 12, 2021.
 Yuga  and co. head to  Maximum Mountain  to stop  Tiger 's excessive training. They look after Tiger, who was just about to pass out. However, the Sixross then shows up!  Yuo  insists on a  Duel , and Tiger is willing to fight, but she's still not in good shape, so  Asana  decides to Duel in her place. The one Yuo chooses as Asana's opponent is...
 
 Featured Duel: Asana Mutsuba VS. Neil Saionji 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
