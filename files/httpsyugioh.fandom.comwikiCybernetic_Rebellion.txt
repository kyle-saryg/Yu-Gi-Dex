 Cybernetic Rebellion  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on July 11, 2019.
 
 Description 
 Cyber Dragon , activate!  Machine -Type monsters are stronger than ever with the new  Crystrons ! 
 
 Featuring: 
 Cyber Twin Dragon 
A powerful Fusion Monster that can attack twice, used by  Zane Truesdale  in the anime! If your opponent is the only one controlling a monster, you can Special Summon " Cyber Dragon " and Normal Summon " The Light - Hex-Sealed Fusion " to Fusion Summon it immediately. 
 Crystron Quariongandrax 
A giant  Synchro Monster  that can be Synchro Summoned by using 2 Tuner monsters and 1 non-Tuner monster! When Synchro Summoned, it can banish cards from your opponent's field and Graveyard, up to the number of non-Tuner monsters you used to Synchro Summon it. 
 Deep Sea Diva 
A Tuner monster that can Special Summon 1 Level 3 or lower  Sea Serpent -Type monster from your Deck when Normal Summoned! Put it in your Deck alongside Sea Serpent-Type monsters to Synchro Summon Level 3 - 5 Synchro Monsters! 
 White Aura Dolphin 
When this monster is destroyed by your opponent and sent to the Graveyard, you can banish another WATER monster from your Graveyard to Special Summon this monster as a Tuner monster from your Graveyard! It also has an effect that lets you halve the ATK of one of your opponent's monsters until the end of the turn. Use these two effects to keep it on the field as long as possible! 
A total of 90 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 42  N .
 
 
 
 
Each box of  Cybernetic Rebellion  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Shore Knight ", " Alien Kid ", " Mecha Sea Dragon Plesion ", " Abyss-scale of Cetus ", and " Underworld Egg Clutch ". 
 The following cards are reprints from previous packs: " Alien Overlord ", " Spined Gillman ", " Mermail Abyssgunde ", " Alien Kid ", " Underworld Egg Clutch ", " Shore Knight ", " Mecha Sea Dragon Plesion ", and " Abyss-scale of Cetus ".
