 Summoning 
 Special Summon  a Level 4  Spellcaster  with 1500 or less ATK, for example, " Aqua Madoor " via " Unexpected Dai ", then follow up with " Inferno Reckless Summon ". 
 " Summoner Monk " can Special Summon another Level 4 Spellcaster from your Deck. Or, " Rescue Rabbit " can bring out two Level 4 Spellcaster  Normal Monster s . Then Special Summon " Performage Hat Tricker " from your hand. 
 " Onomatopickup " can allow a " Gagaga " deck to easily meet this card's summoning requirements. 
 You can  Xyz Summon  this card by using " Rank-Up-Magic Astral Force " on " Ghostrick Socuteboss ". 
 Using this card 
 This card's effect is useful for quickly getting  Quick-Play Spell Cards . 
 This card can be used with "Rank-Up-Magic Astral Force" to summon " Magi Magi ☆ Magician Gal ".
