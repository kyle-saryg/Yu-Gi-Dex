 Joey Wheeler  is a video game depiction of  Joey Wheeler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 He is available as a Legend Partner, with a default of 6 Rental Points. His partner ID is P004.
 He was also available as an opponent, under  Duel Monsters Arena  mode.

 Description 
 Yugi's  classmate, and eventually his best friend. Although at the time he was only starting out as a  Duelist , fights with many rivals have forged him into a force to be reckoned with.
 
 Opponent Quote 
 I'm boosting my Duelist level to the max for you!
 
 Decks 
 A  Deck  centered around  Warrior - Types , offering high-return but at a high risk. As it does not use the  Extra Deck , you will need to fight using  Equip Cards  in support. Don't be scared of failure - just keep on using those gamble cards!

 Trivia 
 Although  Joey Wheeler  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime and manga never used " White Elephant's Gift ", this card is included in 1 version of this character's Side Deck as a reference to  Seto Kaiba  frequently insulting him as a "horse bone" in the Japanese anime and manga. The Japanese name of "White Elephant's Gift" translates to "Value of a Horse's Bone".
 The same applies to " Heart of the Underdog " in some of this character's Side Decks: Kaiba has sometimes referred to Joey as "ordinary" in the Japanese versions of the anime/manga. The Japanese name of "Heart of the Underdog" translates to "Willpower of the Ordinary".
