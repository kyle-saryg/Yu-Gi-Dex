 OCG Rulings 
 If a face-down " Ally of Justice Reverse Break " is attacked by a  LIGHT  monster, then " Ally of Justice Reverse Break " is destroyed after  Damage Calculation  when Battle Damage is inflicted. [1] 
 The effect of " Ally of Justice Reverse Break " activates after entering the  Damage Step , before flipping the monster face-up.  After you destroy a monster with this effect, you do not perform  Damage Calculation . [2]
