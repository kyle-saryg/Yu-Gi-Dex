 Mentions in Other Rulings 
 Kinka-byo : If you Special Summon " Arcana Force 0 - The Fool " with " Kinka-byo 's" effect and the coin toss for " Arcana Force 0 - The Fool " is heads, " Kinka-byo 's" effect is negated and it is destroyed. " Arcana Force 0 - The Fool " remains on the field.
