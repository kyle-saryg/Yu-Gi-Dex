 Features 
 Contains 3 different theme Decks based on Xyz Summoning 
 Contains new cards as well as reprints of cards that are difficult to obtain. 
 Includes cards used by  Yuto  and  Shay Obsidian 
 Includes members/support for the " Raidraptor ", " Rank-Up-Magic ", and " Trap Hole " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Super Quant " archetype, and the first support for the " Phantom Knights " and " The Phantom Knights " archetype.
