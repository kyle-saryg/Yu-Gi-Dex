 Structure Deck: Master of Pendulum  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the twenty-ninth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Synchron Extreme .

 Features 
 The Deck is focused on Pendulum Summons. 
 It includes two new ace dragons as well as many shiny cards, to appeal to the beginner and lower-age group demographics! 
 The reprinted cards inside the Deck meet the needs of players, both new and old. 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Magician ", " Performapal " and " Odd-Eyes " archetypes.
