 OTS Tournament Pack 20 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OTS Tournament Pack 20  is a booster pack that is available as a Tournament Prize or Participation Pack released to the  TCG  tournament locations. It is the twentieth  OTS Tournament Pack , following  OTS Tournament Pack 19 .
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 3 cards per pack.The set contains 27 cards:
 
 3  Ultimate Rares 
 10  Super Rares 
 14  Commons 
 The Portuguese version contains 11 more Common cards, bringing the total to 38 cards
