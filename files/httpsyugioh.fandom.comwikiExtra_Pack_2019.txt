 Extra Pack 2019  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG - exclusive  cards to the  OCG . [1]

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive cards from  Cybernetic Horizon ,  Soul Fusion ,  Savage Strike ,  Dark Neostorm  and other products. 
 Introduces the " Danger! ", " Time Thief " and " Valkyrie " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Includes the  WCS2018 prize card  (replica) of " Noritoshi in Darkest Rainment " and " Amatsu-Okami of the Divine Peaks " as  Extra Secret Rare .
