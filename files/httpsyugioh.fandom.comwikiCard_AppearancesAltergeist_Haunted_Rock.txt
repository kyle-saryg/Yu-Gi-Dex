 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 77 ,  Ghost Gal  uses this card during her  Speed Duel  against  The Shepherd . She Sets this card directly from her Deck using the effect of " Altergeist Marionetter ". This card can be activated on the same turn if Set by an " Altergeist " monster, so Ghost Gal activates this card to send " Altergeist Fifinellag " from her hand to the Graveyard. Then, using " Altergeist Marionetter 's" effect, she sends this card to the Graveyard to revive "Fifinellag". 
 In  episode 106 , Ghost Gal uses this card during her  Tag-Team  Speed Duel with The Shepherd against  Roboppi . She Sets this card directly from her Deck using the other effect of "Altergeist Marionetter". This card can be activated on the same turn if Set by an "Altergeist" monster, so Ghost Gal activates this card to send " Altergeist Pookuery " from her hand to the Graveyard. This card remains on the field until the duel ends in the  following episode .
