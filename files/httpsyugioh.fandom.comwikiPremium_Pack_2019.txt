 Premium Pack 2019  is the 21st  Premium Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game [1]  ( OCG ), following  Premium Pack 20 . While it was first available at a Jump Festa event held during December 22-23, 2018, this set did not go on sale until March 9, 2019.

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Akiza Izinski ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Crow Hogan ,  Eve ,  Sora Shiunin , Yuma Tsukumo ( anime  and  manga ),  Yuri ,  Yugo ,  Yuto ,  Yusei Fudo  and  Yuya Sakaki . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Frightfur ", " Gimmick Puppet ", " Number ", " Ojama ", " Performapal " , " Power Tool ", " Salamangreat ", " Speedroid ", " Superheavy Samurai " and " Utopic ". 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 4 cards. The set contains 20 cards:
 
 3  20th Secret Rares 
 17  Commons 
 All cards are also available as  Ultra Rare  and  Secret Rare .
