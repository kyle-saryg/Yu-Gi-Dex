 Members 
 
 
 
 
 Flame Swordsman  ( 炎 の 剣 士   Honō no Kenshi ) is a  series  of  FIRE   Warrior   monsters  used by  Joey Wheeler  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime  and  manga  that consists of the original " Flame Swordsman " and its  retrains .
 " Flame Swordsman " appears as a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters  anime.
