 Yugi's Legendary Decks 
 Legendary Decks II 
 Legendary Dragon Decks 
 
 
 
 
 
 Legendary Decks II  is a  TCG -exclusive set that contains 3 pre-constructed Decks, and is the second set in the  Legendary Decks  series.

 Breakdown 
 Each set contains:
 
 A 40-card Deck based on  Yugi 's " Exodia the Forbidden One " and " Dark Magician ", plus 3  Ultra Rare  promotional cards (the tournament-legal versions of the  Egyptian God  cards) and an Ultra Rare Yugi Token card 
 A 43-card Deck based on  Kaiba 's " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ", plus an Ultra Rare Kaiba Token card 
 A 43-card Deck based on  Joey 's " Red-Eyes Black Dragon " and  Gemini monsters , plus an Ultra Rare Joey Token card 
 3  Secret Rare  promotional cards: " Eternal Soul ", " Dark Burning Attack ", and " Dark Burning Magic ". 
 The Kaiba and Joey Decks each include the following:
 
 3  Ultra Rare  cards 
 40  Common  cards 
 1 Token 
 The Yugi Deck includes the following:
 
 6  Ultra Rare  cards 
 37  Common  cards 
 1 Token
