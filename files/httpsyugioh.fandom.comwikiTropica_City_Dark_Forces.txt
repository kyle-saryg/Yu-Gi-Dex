 Tropica City Dark Forces  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from April 25, 2013. Each pack costs   15.
 This collection includes Gusto, Evoltile, Watt, Dragunity, Naturia, Koa'ki Meiru and Crystal Beast Monsters and Spells, as well as the Elemental Lords.
