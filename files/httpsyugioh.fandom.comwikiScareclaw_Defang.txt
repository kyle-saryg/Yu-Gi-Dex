 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Your opponent cannot  target  " Scareclaw "  Link Monsters  and " Visas Starfrost " you  control  with  card effects , also they cannot be  destroyed  by your opponent's card effects.  Monsters   destroyed by battle  with your "Scareclaw" Link Monster or "Visas Starfrost" are  banished  instead of being  sent  to the  GY . You can  banish  1 "Scareclaw" Link Monster from your  field  or GY, then target 1 card your opponent controls;  destroy  it. You can only use this effect of "Scareclaw Defang"  once per turn .
