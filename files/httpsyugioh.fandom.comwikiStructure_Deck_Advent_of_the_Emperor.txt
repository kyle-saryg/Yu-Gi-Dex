 Structure Deck: Advent of the Emperor  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the fourteenth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-second including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Undead World . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  The Dark Emperor Structure Deck .

 Features 
 The deck features the " Monarch " series, including a new "Monarch", " Caius the Shadow Monarch ". It features 5 cards new to the  OCG  on its release, the other 4 being " Dimensional Alchemist ", " Samsara Kaiser ", " D.D.R. - Different Dimension Reincarnation " and " By Order of the Emperor ".
