 " The Team Battle Royale Begins! " is the fourty-third episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on April 11, 2021.
 The Goha Rush Duel Team Battle Royale tournament has finally begun!  Yuga  comes to the venue to cheer on  Luke ,  Gavin , and  Romin . Luke then urges Yuga to take  Asana 's place, since she wasn't able to take part in the tournament. That's when a  man  from the crowd challenges Yuga to a Duel!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS Otes 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
