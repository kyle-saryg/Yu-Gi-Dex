 In the International version of "Exile of the Wicked" the Bible and cross are removed to avoid religious connotation. Also, in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , "Exile of the Wicked" was drawn with a different artwork.
