 Hunter Pace , known as  Mukuro Enjo  (炎城 ムクロ,  Enjō Mukuro ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Hunter Pace , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
