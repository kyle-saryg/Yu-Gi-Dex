 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
       Masha, in  Tag Force 2-3 .     
 In Tag Force 1-3, she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , after the player gets her  Deck Recipe , they also get the card " Reinforcement of the Army ".
 Throughout the GX Tag Force games, she has changed hairstyles. In Tag Force, her hair was tied in two pigtails, like  Maki . In Tag Force 2, she changed it to look like  Yumi 's, although her sprite is in a smiling gesture to differentiate between the two as they both have the same hair color.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 In the second-to-last event of  Tyranno 's story, Masha and  Disa  Tag Duel against the player and Tyranno.
 
 Tag Force 5 
 In Tag Force 5, Masha can be found in the Coastal Region. She mentions  her older sister  somewhat frequently.

 Decks 
 Masha uses Decks that aim to lock down the opponent's  Spell  and  Trap Cards  with  " Horus the Black Flame Dragon "  and  " Royal Decree " . During the earlier installments she complements it exclusively with " Jinzo ", but from  Tag Force 4  onwards her Decks feature  Dragon - Type  monsters other than "Horus" as well.
