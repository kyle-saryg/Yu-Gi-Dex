 Generation Force  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fifth set of the  OCG ' s 7th series. It is followed by  Photon Shockwave .
 The North American Sneak Peeks for this set took place on August 6, 2011.
 Packs of the  TCG  set were also printed in the  Generation Force: Special Edition .

 Features 
 Generation Force  introduces the  Xyz Summon  mechanics to the game. It contains many cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime, including cards used by  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Reginald Kastle ,  Mr. Kay  and  Lillybot . The  TCG  version of the set also includes cards used by  Jaden Yuki  and  Aster Phoenix . 
 The set introduces the " Geargiano ", " Wind-Up "  archetypes  and " Crashbug ", " Keeper "  series . It also includes support for " Gusto ", " Gishki ", " Laval ", " Gem-Knight "/" Gem- ", " Vylon ", " Number ", " Penguin ", " HERO ", and " Steelswarm " archetypes, as well as support for  Psychic - Type  monsters.
