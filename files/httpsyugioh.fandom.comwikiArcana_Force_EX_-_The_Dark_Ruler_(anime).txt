 [  Fairy  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned , except by  Tributing  3  monsters  you  control  or with its own  effect . When this card is Summoned, toss a coin. If Heads, this card gains the Right Side Up effect, if Tails, this card gains the Upside Down effect. ● Right Side Up: This card can  attack  twice during the same  Battle Phase . If this card  battles  twice using this effect, it is changed to  Defense Position  at the end of the Battle Phase. This card's  battle position  cannot be changed until the end of your next  turn . ● Upside Down: If this card is  removed from the field ,  destroy  all cards on the  field , then Special Summon it during your next  Standby Phase .
