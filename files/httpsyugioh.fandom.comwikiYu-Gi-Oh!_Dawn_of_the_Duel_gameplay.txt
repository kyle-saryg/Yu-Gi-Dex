Dawn of the Duel gameplay 
 The  Dawn of the Duel  arc  continues to follow the Yu-Gi-Oh! gameplay set by the  Battle City Rules . The main duel featured in this arc is the final  Duel  between  Yami Yugi  and  Yugi Muto . In this Duel, the special rule was added that at the start, each player started with 6 cards in their hand (before the first draw). This was to match the manga.
