 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can only  control  1 "Life Shaver".  Once per turn , during your opponent's  End Phase : Place 1  counter  on this card. During the  Main  or  Battle Phase , if this card has a counter: You can  send  this card to the  GY , then your opponent  discards  as many cards as possible from their hand, up to the number of counters that were on this card.
