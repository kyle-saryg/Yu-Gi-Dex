 Crystal Beast Ruby Carbuncle , known as  Gem Beast Ruby Carbuncle  in the Japanese version and often shortened to  Ruby  in the anime, is a character version of the card, " Crystal Beast Ruby Carbuncle ".

 Appearances 
 Anime 
 Ruby is the  Spirit Partner  of  Jesse Anderson .
 
 Reverse of Arcadia 
 Crystal Beast Ruby Carbuncle appears an unlockable opponent in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia .
 Crystal Beast Ruby Carbuncle and  Earthbound Immortal Wiraqocha Rasca  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents by summoning " Rainbow Dragon " in a Duel. Their Tag Team name is  Earthbound Crystal .
 
 
 Deck 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , Ruby Carbuncle uses a " Crystal Beast " Deck, titled "Duel Crystals".
