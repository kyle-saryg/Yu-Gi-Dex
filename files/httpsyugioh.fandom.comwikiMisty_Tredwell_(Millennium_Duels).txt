 Misty Tredwell , known as  Misty Lola  (ミスティ・ローラ,  Misuti Rora ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Misty Tredwell , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
