 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Nelson Andrews , known as  Fuya Okudaira (奥平風也,  Okudaira Fūya ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Nelson Andrews , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
