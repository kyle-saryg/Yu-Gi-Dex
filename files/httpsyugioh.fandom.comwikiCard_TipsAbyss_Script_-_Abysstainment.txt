 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Watch Dog ", " The Despair Uranus ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady ", and " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ". 
 If you manage to activate this card's second effect, Special Summon as many " Abyss Actor - Evil Heel " and " Abyss Actor - Funky Comedian " as possible, in order to make full use of their ATK modulation effects. Each "Evil Heel" would decrease an opposing monster's ATK by 5000, while each "Funky Comedian" would gain 1500 ATK.
