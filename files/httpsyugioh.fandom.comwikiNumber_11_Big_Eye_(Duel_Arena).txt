 Number 11: Big Eye  is a character version of the card " Number 11: Big Eye ". 
 It appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P129.
 It was only obtainable by achieving any Rank from 1 to 100 on the Arena Weekly Win Ratings (Single) for the time period of December 8, 2014, 00:00 (UTC) through December 14, 2014, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received it in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on December 17, 2014.

 Description 
 One of the  monsters  with the " Number "  archetype . This is a one-eyed monster that is capable of manipulating its enemies!
 
 Deck 
 This  deck  contains  Spellcaster - type  cards. Base your strategy around  Pendulum Summon  as well as  Xyz Summon .  Pendulum Monsters  normally  return  to your  Extra Deck  when  destroyed . Please note, however, they will be  sent  to the  Graveyard  after being used as  Xyz Materials .

 Trivia 
 This partner, along with  Yami Marik , were likely released in commemoration of " Snatch Steal " becoming  Limited  in the  TCG  on January 1, 2015, the news of which was coincidentally revealed during the same day these partners were released. This is because the two partners share the theme of "mind control."
 It is also possible that this partner was released to celebrate its reprint in  Astral Pack Six , instead.
