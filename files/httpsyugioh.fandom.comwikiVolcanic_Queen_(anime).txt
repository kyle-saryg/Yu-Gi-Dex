 [  Pyro  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card can only be  Special Summoned  from your  hand  to your opponent's side of the  field  by  Tributing  1  monster  your opponent  controls . If you Special Summon this card, you cannot Normal Summon or Set this  turn . Once per  turn , you can send 1 other card you control to the  Graveyard  to inflict 1000  damage  to your opponent. During your  End Phase , either  Tribute  1 other  monster  or take 1000 damage.
