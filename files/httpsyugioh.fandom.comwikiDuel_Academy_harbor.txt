      The harbor in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Duel Academy .     
 The  harbor  area is a location on the  Duel Academy  island in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . The  lighthouse  and  warehouse  are located here. 
 In the anime, this was the location where  Sartorius  Dueled and defeated  Prince  Ojin , obtaining the  keys  to  SORA .  Maximillion Pegasus  Dueled  Vellian Crowler  and  Jean-Louis Bonaparte  here so that they may both join  Industrial Illusions  , they lost due to Pegasus turning Bonaparte and Crowler against each other by claiming that the person to win first place in the Duel would get the most payment.
 In the anime, this was also the place where the Duelists  Jesse Anderson ,  Axel Brodie ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Thelonious Viper  and  Adrian Gecko  first arrived on the island by the means of a boat.
