 Yumi Kakazu , (かかず ゆみ,  Kakazu Yumi ; born with the kanji  嘉数   由美 ) is a Japanese voice actress.
 She voices  Anzu Mazaki  in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . [2]
