 Appearances 
 World Championship 2008 
       Embust in World Championship 2008     
 Green Guardian - Embust guards the exit to the Duel World " Grace ", where the player must solve 3 out of the 5 challenges presented to progress to the next  Duel World , " Sunlight ".
 
 
 Stardust Accelerator (World Championship 2009) 
 Embust appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for September 2009.
 
 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , Embust uses the following Decks:
 
 Open and Close : a " Skill Drain "-based Deck with Normal Monsters,  Life Point  Recovery cards and  Mask of Restrict . 
 Wiggling Beings : an  Insect -based deck that uses cards that benefit from the opponent's monsters also counting as  Insect  via " DNA Surgery ", such as " Insect Barrier ". Once you are paralyzed, it uses cards like " Swarm of Scarabs " and " Swarm of Locusts " to take out key cards and clear the field. 
 Calm Spirits : a  Charmers -based deck, with " Waboku " and " Chorus of Sanctuary " to help preserve the Charmers long enough to take control of opposing monsters and then send them to the Graveyard for their Familiar-Possessed upgrade.  Also features the minor monsters shown alongside the various Charmer card arts. 
 Silent Memory : A Removal deck based around " Macro Cosmos ", " Banisher of the Light " and also traps that return cards to the opponent's Deck or Hand, like " Compulsory Evacuation Device " or " Forced Back ". Embust also tries to lock your spells down using " Spell Canceller ", while not running any spells himself. 
 Silent Victory : A Deck based around the Silent Warrior level group
