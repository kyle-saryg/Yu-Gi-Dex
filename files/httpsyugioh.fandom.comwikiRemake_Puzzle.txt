 Normal 
 
 Activate  only during your opponent's  Battle Phase .  Destroy  1 " Morphtronic "  monster  you  control  and end the Battle Phase. Then,  Special Summon  1 "Morphtronic" monster from your  Graveyard  whose  Level  is less than the Level of the monster destroyed and  sent  to the Graveyard by this  effect .
