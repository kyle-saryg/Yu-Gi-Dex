 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Ancient Gear Soldier " is a  Continuous Effect . [1] 
 If " Ancient Gear Soldier " is destroyed by battle, the " your opponent cannot  activate  any  Spell / Trap Cards  until the end of the  Damage Step . " effect is no longer applied. [1] 
 Even if the effect of " Ancient Gear Soldier " is being applied, the effects of face-up  Continuous Trap Cards  can still be activated. [1]
