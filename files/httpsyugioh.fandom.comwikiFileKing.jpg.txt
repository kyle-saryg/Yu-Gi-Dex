 This  King  (前世の世界王) [1]  is a mysterious figure. He is the ruler of an unnamed kingdom. In the dub, he is the father of  Jaden Yuki 's past incarnation.
 
 Biography 
 Knowing that the power of  the Gentle Darkness  within  the Supreme King  was the only thing that could save his people from the invading  Light of Destruction , he had  Yubel , with her consent, turned into a dragon, to act as a guardian until he was old enough to use his power wisely. [2]
