 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When you  activate  this card, you can also  detach  up to 3  materials  from your  Xyz Monsters .  Add  1 " Springans "  monster  from your  Deck  to your  hand , then, if you detached any material(s) when you activated this card, you can  Special Summon  the same number of "Springans" monsters from your hand and/or  GY . If this card is in your GY: You can  target  1 Xyz Monster on the  field ; detach 1 material from it, and if you do, add this card to your hand. You can only use either  effect  of "Tallyho! Springans!"  once per turn .
