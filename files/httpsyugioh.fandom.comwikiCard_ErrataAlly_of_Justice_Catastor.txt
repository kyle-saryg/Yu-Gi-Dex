 English 
 
 
 Original 
 First erratum 
 Second erratum 
 
 
 1 Tuner + 1 or more non-Tuner monsters If this card  battles  a non-DARK monster, destroy that monster without  applying  damage calculation. 
 1 Tuner + 1 or more non-Tuner monsters If  this card  attacks or is attacked by  a non-DARK monster , destroy  that monster  at the start of the Damage Step ( without damage calculation ) . 
 1 Tuner + 1 or more non-Tuner monsters At the start of the Damage Step, if  this card  battles  a non-DARK monster : Destroy  that monster  immediately  (without damage calculation) . 
 
 
       DTP1-EN031 Duel Terminal - Preview Wave 1     
       DT01-EN035 Duel Terminal 1

 Fourth erratum   
 
 
 1 Tuner + 1  or more  non-Tuner monsters At the start of the Damage Step, if this card battles a non-DARK monster: Destroy that monster. 
 1 Tuner + 1 +  non-Tuner monsters At the start of the Damage Step, if this card battles a face-up non-DARK monster: Destroy that monster. 
 
 
         CT10-EN006 2013 Collectible Tins Wave 1     
         DUDE-EN007 Duel Devastator
