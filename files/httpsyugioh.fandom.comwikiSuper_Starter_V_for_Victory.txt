 Super Starter: V for Victory  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the seventh deck in the  TCG ' s annual Starter Deck series (fifteenth overall), following  Starter Deck: Xyz Symphony . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Starter Deck 2013  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 Each  Super Starter: V for Victory  contains 2  additional packs  (1 pack contains 5 cards out of 15 different cards) in total. 
 The European release of this Starter Deck contains only 1 Super Starter Power-Up Pack, but it contains 10 cards instead of 5. 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Super Starter: V for Victory  contains 42 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 1  Super Rare 
 40  Commons
