   English 
 
 
 Special Summon  this card as an  Effect Monster  ( Thunder - Type / LIGHT / Level  5/ ATK  1900/ DEF  0). (This card is also still a  Trap Card .) If  Summoned  this way, this card cannot be  destroyed by battle .
