 Structure Deck: Pendulum Evolution  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the thirty-third Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Seto Kaiba . [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Z-ARC  and  Yuya Sakaki 
 Includes members/support for the " Magician " and " Pendulum Dragon " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Pendulumgraph " archetype to the OCG. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Fusion Dragon ", " Synchro Dragon ", and " Xyz Dragon " archetypes.
