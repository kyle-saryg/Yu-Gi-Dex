 Structure Deck: Rebirth of Shaddoll  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  (OCG). It is the 39th Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Revolver .
 The theme for this Structure Deck was decided by a poll conducted by Konami on its Official Card Database site during June 21 to July 3, 2019. [1]
