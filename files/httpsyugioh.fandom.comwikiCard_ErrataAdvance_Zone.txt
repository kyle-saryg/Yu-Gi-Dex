 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 Once per turn, during the End Phase of any turn in which you Tribute Summoned a monster(s): You can apply these effects,  depending  on the number of monsters you Tributed for Tribute Summons this turn. ● 1 or more: Destroy 1 Set card your opponent controls. ● 2 or more: Draw 1 card. ● 3 or more: Add 1 monster from your Graveyard to your hand. 
 Once per turn, during the End Phase of any turn in which you Tribute Summoned a monster(s): You can apply these effects,  in sequence, based  on the number of monsters you Tributed for Tribute Summons this turn. ● 1 or more: Destroy 1 Set card your opponent controls. ● 2 or more: Draw 1 card. ● 3 or more: Add 1 monster from your Graveyard to your hand.
