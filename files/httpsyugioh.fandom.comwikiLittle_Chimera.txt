   Korean 
 
 
 이 카드가 필드 위에 앞면 표시로 존재하는 한, 필드 위의 화염 속성 몬스터의 공격력은 500 포인트 올리고, 물 속성 몬스터의 공격력은 400 포인트 내린다. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
As long as this card remains face-up on the field, increase the ATK of all FIRE monsters by 500 points and decreases the DEF of all WATER monsters by 400 points. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
 A monster that came from an experiment to grow wings on a beast. Despite its appearance, it is not very strong. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
As long as this card remains face-up on the field, increase the ATK of all FIRE monsters by 500 points and decrease the ATK of all WATER monsters by 400 points.
