 Battles of Legend: Armageddon  is is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Franz ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Tetsu Trudge , Crow Hogan ( manga  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V ),  Don Thousand  and  Olga . 
 Includes new members/support for the " Fusion " and " Polymerization " archetypes. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Fossil " and " Numeron " archetypes to the  TCG . 
 Includes a special reprint of " Number 39: Utopia " written in  Astral glyphs . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack, 4  Ultra Rares  and 1  Secret Rare . The set contains 94 cards:
 
 1  10000 Secret Rare 
 1  Starlight Rare 
 33  Secret Rares 
 59  Ultra Rares
