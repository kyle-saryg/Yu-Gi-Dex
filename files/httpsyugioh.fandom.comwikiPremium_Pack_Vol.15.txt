 Premium Pack Vol.15  is a pack in the Korean  OCG  that contains a number of cards released in the Japanese  OCG  that were previously unreleased in Korean.

 2   Breakdown

 4   List

 Features 
 Includes cards from the Booster Packs  Jump Festa Limited Pack 2020  and  Premium Pack 2020 , as well as the unreleased Ritual Spell Cards from  Premium Pack 2 . 
 Includes the promotional cards from  Victory Carnival 2019 ,  V Jump  (VJMP-JP166 to VJMP-JP181),  Saikyō Jump ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V Volume 7 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! OCG Structures Volume 1 ,  Master Guide 6 , and  The Valuable Book 22 . 
 Includes the alternate art of " Red-Eyes Darkness Metal Dragon " from  Perfect Rulebook 2020 . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 53 cards.
 
 4  Extra Secret Parallel Rares 
 13  Ultra Rares  /  Secret Rares 
 36  Super Rares 
 All the cards are also available as  Normal Parallel Rare .
