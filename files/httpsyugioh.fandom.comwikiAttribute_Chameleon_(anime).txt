 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Once during your opponent's  turn , you can  select  1  face-up   monster  your opponent  controls  and  declare  1  Attribute . That monster's Attribute becomes the declared Attribute until the  End Phase .
