 Starter Deck Kaiba Reloaded  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Starter Deck Yugi Reloaded , they are the sixteenth and seventeenth Decks in the  TCG ' s Starter Deck series, following  Super Starter: V for Victory .
