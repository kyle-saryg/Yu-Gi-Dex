 Names 
 
 This card can be searched by " Ahrima, the Wicked Warden ", " Scarm, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss ", " Tour Guide From the Underworld ", " Resonator Command ", " Frightfur Meister ", " Sky Scourge Cidhels ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Chaos Zone ", " Sphere of Chaos ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Piri Reis Map ", " Umbramirage the Elemental Lord " and " Sangan ". 
 This card's effect upon Normal Summon does not negate the revival target; it can either set up a  Rank 3   Xyz Summon  or the revival target's effect that requires a Tribute, the latter of which in turn trigger's this card's search effect.
