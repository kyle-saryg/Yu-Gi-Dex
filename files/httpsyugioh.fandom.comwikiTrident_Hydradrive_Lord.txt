 3 " Hydradrive "  Link Monsters 
 If this card is  Link Summoned : You can  target  all "Hydradrive" Link Monsters used as  Link Material ; this card is treated as having the same  Attribute (s) as that target(s).  Monsters  your opponent controls with the same Attribute as this card's have their  effect (s)  negated .  Once per turn  ( Quick Effect ): You can target 1  face-up  monster your opponent controls; the Attributes of all face-up monsters your opponent currently controls become the same as the targeted monster's, until the end of this  turn .
