 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Leon von Schroeder ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Slade Princeton ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Paradox ,  Aigami ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Crow Hogan ,  Yuto ,  Sora Shiunin ,  Yuri ,  Yugo ,  Eve ,  Yusaku Fujiki  and  Light Tsukiko . 
 Includes members/support for the " Alien ", " Ancient Gear ", " Artifact ", " Blackwing ", " Bujin ", " Buster Blader ", " Charmer ", " Chaos ", " Crystron ", " Cyber ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Cynet ", " Cubic ", " D/D ", " D/D/D ", " Darklord ", " Destiny HERO ", " Destruction Sword ", " Edge Imp ", " Endymion ", " Evolution Pill ", " Familiar-Possessed ", " Fluffal ", " Frog ", " Frightfur ", " Fusion ", " Harpie ", " HERO ", " Hole ", " Gusto ", " Madolche ", " Magical Musket ", " Malefic ", " Mayakashi ", " Mecha Phantom Beast ", " Melodious ", " Number ", " Ojama ", " Orcust ", " Paleozoic ", " Parshath ", " Phantom Beast ", " Polymerization ", " Predaplant ", " PSY-Frame ", " Raidraptor ", " Reptilianne ", " roid ", " Rose ", " Rose Dragon ", " Salamangreat ", " Scrap ", " Secret Six Samurai ", " Simorgh ", " Six Samurai ", " Sky Striker ", " Sky Striker Ace ", " Speedroid ", " Tenyi ", " Traptrix ", " Vehicroid ", " Watt ", " Witchcrafter ", and " World Legacy " archetypes, as well as the  Fairy Tale ,  Flame Swordsman ,  Herald ,  Vanity's ,  Wight  and  Xtra HERO  series. 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Duel Overload  box contains:
 
 6 Booster Packs of 5 Ultra Rare cards. The set contains 100 Ultra Rare cards (30 new Links and a total of 56 new cards new to the  TCG  and 44 reprints).
