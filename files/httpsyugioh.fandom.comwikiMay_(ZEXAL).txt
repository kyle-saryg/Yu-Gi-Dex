 May , known as  Mayumi  in the Japanese and Italian versions, is  Charlie McCay 's niece which appears in the  second arc . She is currently ill.

 Design 
 May appears as a young girl with blue hair and purple eyes. She wears a yellow shirt.
 
 Biography 
 World Duel Carnival 
 May was going into surgery for an unspecified condition and was nervous.  Charlie McCay , her uncle, gives her " Number 7: Lucky Straight " as a token of good luck and tells her to do " kattobingu ". [1]  The card comes later into  Yuma 's possession. [2]
