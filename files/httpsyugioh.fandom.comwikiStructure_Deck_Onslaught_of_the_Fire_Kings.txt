 Structure Deck: Onslaught of the Fire Kings  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the twenty-fourth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (thirty-second including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Roar of the Sea Emperor . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Onslaught of the Fire Kings Structure Deck .

 Features 
 The deck features the  FIRE  attribute. Cards from this deck can be combined with card(s) included in  Cosmo Blazer . It also includes many reprints, as well as introducing the " Fire King "  archetype  to the  OCG .
