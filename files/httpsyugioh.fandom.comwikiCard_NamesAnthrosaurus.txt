 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Ανθρώπινος Δεινόσαυρος ) is a translation of the Japanese name of this card ( Dinosaur Man ). 
 In the French, German, and Spanish version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card name was  "Anthrosaurus" . 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card name was  "Antrosauro" .
