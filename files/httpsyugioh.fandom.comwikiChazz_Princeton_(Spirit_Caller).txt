 Chazz Princeton , known as  Jun Manjome  (万丈目 準,  Manjōme Jun ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Chazz Princeton , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
