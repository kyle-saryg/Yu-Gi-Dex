 With  Hiroto Honda 's support,  Miho Nosaka  enters a popularity contest against arrogant school beauty  Kaoruko Himekoji . Soon Kaoruko resorts to cheating, but when she hurts Miho, she incurs the wrath of  Dark Yugi .
 Ryo Bakura  and  Mokuba Kaiba  each have a brief appearance in this episode.
