 Members 
 
 
 
 
 Super Magical  ( 超 魔 導   Chōmadō ) is a  series  of  Level  8  DARK   Spellcaster   Fusion Monsters  used by  Yugi Muto  and  Yami Yugi  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime and manga. Every monster in this series lists " Dark Magician " as one of its  Fusion Materials , and are all visually a fusion of it and another famous monster used by Yugi throughout the series.
