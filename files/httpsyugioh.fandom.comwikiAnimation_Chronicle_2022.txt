 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto / Yami Yugi ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Rex Raptor ,  Duke Devlin ,  Nezbitt ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Jesse Anderson  (possessed by  Yubel ), Leo ( anime  and  manga ),  Luna ,  Tenzen Yanagi ,  Reginald Kastle/Nash ,  Nistro ,  Erazor ,  Scritch ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Frederick ,  Nagi ,  Gloria Tyler ,  Grace Tyler ,  George Gore  and  Earth . 
 Includes new members/support for for the " Amazoness ", " Crystal ", " Crystal Beast ", " Elemental HERO ", " Gadget ", " Heroic ", " Morphtronic ", " Neo-Spacian ", " Ninja ", " Ninjitsu Art ", " Number ", " Performapal ", and " Odd-Eyes " archetypes, as well as the  Three Musketeers of Face Cards  series to the OCG. 
 Introduces the " Advanced Crystal Beast ", " Doodle Beast ", " Doodlebook " and " G Golem " archetypes, as well as the  Ancient Treasure  series to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards:
 1  Prismatic Secret Rare 
 6  Ultra Rares 
 7  Super Rares 
 36  Commons 
 All  Ultra Rare  (except " Neo-Spacian Aqua Dolphin ") and  Super Rare  cards are also available as  Collector's Rares  ( Secret Rares  in Korean set). 
 All  Common  cards are also available as  Normal Parallel Rares .
