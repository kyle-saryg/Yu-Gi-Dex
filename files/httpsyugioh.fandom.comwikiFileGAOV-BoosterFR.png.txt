 Galactic Overlord  is the eighth and final set of the  OCG ' s  7th series . It follows  Order of Chaos  and is followed by  Return of the Duelist .
 It has been released in the  OCG  in Japanese and Korean and in the  TCG  in English, French, German, Italian and Spanish.
 This set's name was written as  Galactic Overload  at one point, due to a mistake caused by the words "Load" and "Lord" having the same pronunciation in Japanese,  rōdo .

 Features 
 The set features cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime series, including cards used by  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Reginald Kastle ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Dextra ,  Nistro ,  Trey ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Cameron Clix  and  Cody Callus . The  TCG  version of the set also includes cards used by  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Fortuno  and  Charlie McCay .
 It includes support for  Xyz Monsters , the  archetypes  " Photon ", " Gagaga ", " Evol ", " Watt ", and " Inzektor ", the  Types   Spellcaster ,  Dragon  and  Zombie  and the  Attributes   WATER ,  WIND  and  LIGHT .
 It introduces the " Bounzer ", " Butterspy " and " Hieratic " archetypes and the " Lightray " series. It also includes new cards from the " Number " archetype.
