 Team lswarm  is a  Dueling Team  in Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , which is named after the " lswarm "  archetype .
 Each member of the team is represented by a different mask. When they first appeared before  Team ZEXAL , they appear only as flying masks, until one of them transforms into  Dark Mask of Laughter  to start the  Duel . They also seem to like horror elements.

 Biography 
 Team Duel Tournament 
 Team lswarm enters the  Team Duel Tournament  and face  Team ZEXAL  in the  Heartland Theme Park  Horror Tower. They being the match with  Dark Mask of Laughter  vs.  Yuma . After a tough battle, Yuma defeats Laughter.
 Crying Mask  and  Angry Mask  used tricks to decieve their opponents,  Takashi  and  Tetsuo , being able to reduce their  Life Points  to under 1000 as they were distracted. However, when  Tokunosuke  reveals their tricks, they are defeated by Takashi and Tetsuo.
