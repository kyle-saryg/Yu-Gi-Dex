 A  Wedge Counter  ( 楔 カウンター  Kusabi Kauntā ) is a type of  counter  used by the  Synchro Monster  " Blackwing Armor Master " and its upgraded form " Blackwing Full Armor Master ". It can be removed from a monster to reduce that monster's  ATK  and  DEF  to 0 until the end of a turn by the former, or to take control of a monster(s) by the latter. There can be only be a maximum of 1 Wedge Counter on a monster. Wedge Counters are the first counters to be represented by  official cards .
