 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Magnet "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 磁石 」 .   As its English name contains "Magnet", it  may  be treated a member of the "Magnet" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.       
 This card's written lore in the anime has not yet been transcribed; if you are able to read Japanese, please help out by adding an accurate Japanese lore from  this image  to this page. The current anime lore listed here is only a reconstruction and may not be fully correct.
