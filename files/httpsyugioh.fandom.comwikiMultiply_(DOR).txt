 Card type 
 
 
   POWER UP 
 
 Grants  Kuriboh  the power to resurrect on any  Summoning Area  other than the one it's defeated in. Also, when the  monster   flips   face-up , another Kuriboh is generated  face-down  in your own Summoning Area.
