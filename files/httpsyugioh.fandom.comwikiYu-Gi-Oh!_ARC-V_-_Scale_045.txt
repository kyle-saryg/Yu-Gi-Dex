 "Arc of Destiny!!"  is the forty-fifth and final chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga .
 This chapter was first printed in April in the June 2019 issue of  V Jump , released on April 19, 2019.
 
 Summary 
 As their last act,  Yuya  and  Reiji  sprint to obtain the final  Action Card  that will determine everyone's arc of destiny.
 
 Featured Duel: Yuya Sakaki vs. Reiji Akaba 
 Duel continues from the previous Scale . 
 Turn 10: Yuya 
 Yuya  draws " Odd-Eyes Phantom Dragon ". He and  Reiji  run to obtain an  Action Card . Yuya obtains and activates it: " Mini Bomb ", which inflicts 100  Effect damage  to Reiji (Reiji: 100 → 0 LP).
