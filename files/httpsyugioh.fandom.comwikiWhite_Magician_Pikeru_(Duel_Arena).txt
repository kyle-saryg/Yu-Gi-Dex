 White Magician Pikeru  is a character version of the card " White Magician Pikeru ". 
 She appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P069. She is also available as an opponent at Stage 2-2 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, her partner ID was P031 instead.

 A young  magician  fated to undertake  terrible trials . Skilled at healing spells.

 I'm going to become a wonderful  princess ! You'd better watch out for my royal  Equip Spell Cards . I'll quickly become too strong for you to handle.
 
 Decks 
 Partner 
 A " Princess Pikeru "  Summon   Deck . Basically, you should battle using "White Magician Pikeru" equipped with " Trial of the Princesses ". Use your recovered  Life Points  to sustain " Mirror Wall " in order to hold up a powerful defense.
