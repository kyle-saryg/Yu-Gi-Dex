   English 
 
 
 [Requirement] : During the  turn  this card is  Normal Summoned , if there are no other  monsters  on your  field , you can  activate  this. [Effect] : Choose 1  Spellcaster   Normal Monster  in your  GY  and  Special Summon  it to your field  face-up .
