 Darkness Gimmick   is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on June 30, 2021.
 
 Description 
 The puppet that controls the strings of fate!
Raise the curtain of darkness!
 
 List 
 The set contains 90 cards. 9 of them are  UR , 11 of them are  SR , 28 of them are  R  and the remaining 42 of them are  N . 24 of them are  reprints  from other sets.
