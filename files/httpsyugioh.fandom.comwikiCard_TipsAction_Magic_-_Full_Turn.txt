 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Mythical Bestiamorph ", " Spell Calling ", " Quick Booster ", and " Mecha Phantom Beast Jaculuslan ". 
 This card is best used while you control a monster that can  attack multiple monsters  and deal damage. Add the ability to inflict  piercing  damage if necessary. 
 If used during your opponent's turn, it is best used while you control a monster that  inflicts battle damage on your opponent  regardless of matchup (the most notable example being " Amazoness Swords Woman "). 
 Traditional Format 
 " Supreme King Dragon Starving Venom " can copy the effect of a monster that affects the amount of battle damage it inflicts(A great example being " Borrelsword Dragon "), while allowing your monsters in general to inflict piercing damage in the same turn.
