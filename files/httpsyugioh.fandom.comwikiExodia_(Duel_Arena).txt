 Exodia  is a character version of the card " Exodia the Forbidden One ". 
 He appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P026. He also appears as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 6 under Quest mode.
 Defeating it for the first time as an opponent earns the player 3000 Duel Points.
 In the beta, he had a default of 5 Rental Points instead, and his partner ID was P015.

 Description 
 Exodia's seal has been broken. Infinite power brings victory in  Duels .
 
 Opponent Quote 
 I am Exodia. When the seal is broken, an end to all cometh. Show me your fearlessness and then we shall fight.
 
 Upon defeat:
 
 Impressive. My seal remains unbroken.
 
 Decks 
 An Exodia  Deck  where holding all five cards detailed on "Exodia the Forbidden One" in your  hand  will lead you to victory. Use  draw  support and Battle avoidance  effects  to break Exodia's seal!
