 First erratum 
 
 
 By Tributing this card, inflict 500  points of  damage to your  opponent's Life Points , and then neither player can activate Trap Cards  until the End  Phase of this turn. 
 By Tributing  this card ,  inflict 500 damage to your  opponent , and then neither player can activate Trap Cards  during the Battle  Phase of this turn.
