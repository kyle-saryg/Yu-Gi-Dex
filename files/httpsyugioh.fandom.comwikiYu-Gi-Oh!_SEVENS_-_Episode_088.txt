 " The Truth Behind the Kamijo Gear Company " is the eighty-eighth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on February 27, 2022.
 The new Duel Rules have been damaging the Duel Program on the moon. Nail suggests a complete shutdown of the control system in order to prevent the destruction of the Duel Program. To shut down the system, the power lurking inside Luke will be necessary.
