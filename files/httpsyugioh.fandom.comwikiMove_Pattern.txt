        A Move Pattern.     
 A  Move Pattern  is a mechanic from  Capsule Monster Chess  and Mattel's  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game . It shows the movement capabilities of a monster.
 
 Mattel's  Capsule Monsters 
 In Mattel's game, the movement pattern is indicated on the cards with a green arrow.
 
 Can move horizontally and vertically. Can move diagonally. Can move in all directions. 
 The monsters also have a  Move Range  to show how many spaces they can move, and a  Move Cost .
 
 Capsule Monster Chess 
 The Move Patterns in  Capsule Monster Chess  in the manga are similar to Mattel's version. They were made beforehand and not fully elaborated on.
