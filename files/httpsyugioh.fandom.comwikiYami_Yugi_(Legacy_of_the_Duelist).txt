 Yami Yugi 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 English name 
 Yami Yugi 
 
 
 Japanese translated 
 Dark Yugi 
 
 
 Japanese name  
 闇 遊戯 闇遊戯 やみゆうぎ Rōmaji: ( Yami Yugi ) 
 
 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Yami Yugi , known as  Dark Yugi  ( 闇 遊戯 ,  Yami Yugi ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yami Yugi , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 
 Decks 
 Yami Yugi's Decks are based on his Duels throughout the anime.
 
 "Exodia Obliterate" -  Yami Yugi and Seto Kaiba's first Duel 
 "The Wasteland" -  Yami Yugi and Weevil Underwood's Duel 
 "Attack the Moon" -  Yami Yugi and Mako Tsunami's Duel 
 "Yugi's Friends" -  Yami Yugi and Yami Bakura's Duelist Kingdom Duel 
 "Fire the Catapult" -  Yami Yugi and PaniK's Duel 
 "Kuriboh Power" -  Yami Yugi and Seto Kaiba's Duelist Kingdom Duel 
 "Black Luster Soldier" -  Yami Yugi and Mai Valentine's Duel 
 "The Skull Dragon" -  Yami Yugi and Joey's Duelist Kingdom Duel 
 "Magician of Chaos" -  Yugi Muto, Yami Yugi and Maximillion Pegasus' Duelist Kingdom Duel 
 "A Chain Destruction" -  Yami Yugi and Seeker's Duel 
 "Dark Magician Girl" -  Yami Yugi and Arkana's Duel 
 "Deck Destruction" -  Yami Yugi and Strings' Duel 
 "Dark Magicians" -  Yami Yugi and Yami Bakura's Battle City Duel 
 "The Sky Dragon" -  Yami Yugi and Seto Kaiba's Battle City Duel 
 "The Fiend's Sanctuary" -  Yami Yugi and Yami Marik's Duel 
 "The Legendary Timaeus" -  Yami Yugi and Rafael's second Duel  (DLC) 
 "Heart of the Dragons" -  Yami Yugi vs Dartz  (DLC) 
 "The Ceremonial Duel" - The  Ceremonial Battle 
 "The Legendary Duelist" -  Jaden Yuki and Yami Yugi's Duel  (DLC)
