 Schrödinger Nekoyama  ( 猫 山 シュレディンガー  Nekoyama Shuredingā ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. He works for  Nail Saionji .

 Etymology 
 Combined to his cat motif, the name Schrödinger is likely a reference to the    Schrödinger's cat , a thought experiment which presents an hypothetical cat that may be simultaneously both alive and dead.
 
 Deck 
 Schrödinger plays a  " Cat " Deck,  with monsters based on "X-Eyes Y-Dragon" monsters from previous Yu-Gi-Oh anime series.
