 Notes 
 
 ↑   Despite having the Japanese lore of its  OCG  counterpart , prior to its errata, "Toon World" did not function that way in the anime. Instead, it had the following effective effect: 
" Monsters  you  control  become  Toon monsters . When  selected  as  attack targets , they can dodge the  attack  or counterattack without being  destroyed . At the end of your turn, you can hide Toon monsters in this card, where they cannot be selected as attack targets."
