 Normal 
 
 Select  1  face-up   monster  on the  field  and toss a coin. If Heads, apply the Right Side Up  effect , and if Tails, apply the Upside Down effect. ●Right Side Up: Inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the selected monster's  ATK . ●Upside Down: You take damage equal to the selected monster's ATK.
