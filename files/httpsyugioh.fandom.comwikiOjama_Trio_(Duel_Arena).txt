 Ojama Trio  is a character version of the card " Ojama Trio ". 
 They collectively appear as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Their partner ID is P067.
 In the beta, they had a default of 4 Rental Points, and their partner ID was P030.
