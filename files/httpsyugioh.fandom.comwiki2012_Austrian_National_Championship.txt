 The  2012 Austrian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Austria and was won by Deniz Mailänder. [2] 
 The entry fee was 8€ for the main event and 4€ for the  Dragon Duel . [1]  155 people took part in the main event. [3]

 Main event finals
 
 Main event 
 After seven rounds of Swiss, the format switched to single-elimination. [4] 
 In the top sixteen, Damir Ramic defeated Daniel Lieb, Bernhard Hoeger defeated Michael Gstöttenmayr, Deniz Mailänder defeated Sebastian Pichler, Patrik Rieder defeated Michael Letonja, Claudio Kirchmair defeated Stephan Reither, Bernhard Apfelthaler defeated Daniel Angerer, Manuel Egger defeated Manuel Zöschg and Paul Ablöscher defeated Ying Jun Qi. [5] 
 In the quarter finals, Bernhard Hoeger defeated Damir Ramic, Deniz Mailänder defeated Patrik Rieder, Bernhard Apfelthaler defeated Claudio Kirchmair and Paul Ablöscher defeated Manuel Egger. [5] 
 In the semi finals, Deniz Mailänder defeated Bernhard Hoeger and Bernhard Apfelthaler defeated Paul Ablöscher. [5] 
 In the final, Deniz Mailänder defeated Bernhard Apfelthaler. [2]

 Decks 
 The winner  Deniz Mailänder used a " Dino Rabbit " Deck. The runner-up Bernhard Apfelthaler used a " Wind-Up " Deck. In the remaining top four Bernhard Hoeger used a " Dark World " Deck and Paul Ablöscher used a "Dino Rabbit" Deck. In the remaining top eight, Damir Ramic used a " Six Samurai " Deck, Patrik Rieder used a "Wind-Up" Deck, Claudio Kirchmair used a "Dino Rabbit" Deck and Manuel Egger used an " Inzektor " Deck.
