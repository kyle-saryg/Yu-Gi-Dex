 Gladiator's Assault  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixth set in the OCG's 5th series, following  Tactical Evolution . It is followed by  Phantom Darkness .

 Features 
 Gladiator's Assault  includes enhanced support for " Six Samurai ", " Alien " and " Gemini " Decks, introduced the " Gladiator Beast "  archetype , and features cards used by the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  characters  Jaden Yuki  and  Adrian Gecko . It also delves into the anime's past, creating real-life counterparts for  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  season 2 cards like " Swing of Memories " and " No Entry!! ". 
 The TCG set contains the most  Secret Rares  of any booster set to date. It also includes cards used by  Yami Bakura ,  Yami Marik ,  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba  and  Rebecca Hawkins .
