 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament ", " Trap Trick ", " Traptrix Myrmeleo " and " Traptrix Sera ". 
 You can chain this card to effects like that of " Nobleman of Crossout ". Though your monster may be destroyed by "Acid Trap Hole" (if it has less than 2000 DEF), any  flip effect  it has will activate. Thus, it works well with cards like " Morphing Jar ". 
 Force your opponent's monster with low DEF into face-down Defense Position via a card effect like " Book of Moon " or " Book of Eclipse ", then use this card to destroy it. 
 Use cards like " Chorus of Sanctuary " or " Yellow Luster Shield " to increase the range of monsters that this card will flip up and back down without destroying.
