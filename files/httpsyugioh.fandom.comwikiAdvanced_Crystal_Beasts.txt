 " Advanced Crystal Beast ", known as " Advanced Gem Beast " ( Ａ 宝玉獣 ,  Adobansudo Hōgyokujū ) in Japan, are an  archetype  of cards used by  Yubel  while she possessed  Jesse Anderson .
 They are wild, more feral, and more armored versions of their  original forms , complete with their own ace card, " Rainbow Dark Dragon " who rivals " Rainbow Dragon ".
 " Crystal Beast "-orientated  Spell  and  Trap Cards  also work on "Advanced Crystal Beasts" on the field, since their names are treated as the same as their normal "Crystal Beast"  counterparts . But, they have a weakness: they can only be face-up on the field with the  Field Spell Card  " Advanced Dark " in play, otherwise they are  destroyed . "Rainbow Dark Dragon", in the anime, can't exist without all of the "Advanced Crystal Beasts" on the  field , or in the Graveyard, otherwise it will be  banished .
 The archetype was released to the  OCG  in  Animation Chronicle 2022  and in the  TCG  in  Battles of Legend: Crystal Revenge . Unlike in the anime,  OCG/TCG  "Advanced Crystal Beast" monsters are distinct from their original counterparts and are never treated as having their respective original counterpart's names. All of them have stronger effects than their original counterparts. Another distinction from their anime counterparts is that they are sent to the Graveyard instead of destroyed while "Advanced Dark" is not on the field, although this only applies while they are monsters, rather than in the  Spell & Trap Zones .
