 Breakdown 
 Each  Savage Strike Special Edition  contains:
 
 3  Savage Strike  Booster Packs 
 1 of 2  Super Rares , either " White Rose Dragon " or " Trickstar Crimson Heart " 
 1 of 2 Super Rare preview cards from  Dark Neostorm , either " Grid Sweeper " or " Yuki-Musume, the Ice Mayakashi "
