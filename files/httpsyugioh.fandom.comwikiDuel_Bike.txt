Duel Bike  is one of  Yuga 's "Roads", which he uses to travel to and from school.
 
 Overview 
 The Duel Bike is an electric bicycle that can be attached to  Yuga 's  Duel Disk , allowing Yuga to pedal in order to power it. [1]
