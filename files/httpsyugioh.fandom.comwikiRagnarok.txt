 Property 
 
 
   Quick-Play 
 
 Activate  only while there are any 2 of  Dark Sage ,  Dark Magician ,  Dark Magician Girl , or  Magician of Black Chaos  on the  field .  Remove from play  all  monsters  from your  hand ,  Deck  and  Graveyard .  Destroy  all monsters on your opponent's side of the field.
