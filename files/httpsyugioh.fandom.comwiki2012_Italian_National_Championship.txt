 The  2012 Italian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Italy. It took place in Rimini on the weekend, June 23–24, and was won by Vasilis Koltoukis. The tournament was attended by 510 people, 85 of whom got to qualify for the World Championship Qualifier continentals that year. [2] 
 The entry fee was €20 for the main event. [1]
