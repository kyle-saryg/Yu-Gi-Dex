 Akihiro Tomonaga  is a Japanese manga assistant and story composer.
 
 Work 
 Tomonaga is credited for the following works [1] [2] :
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! (manga) : Assistant 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL : Story and art 
 Tomonaga is also credited as an art assistant for the  Outlaw Star  manga series.
