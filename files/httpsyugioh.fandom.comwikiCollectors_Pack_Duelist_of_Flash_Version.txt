 Collectors Pack: Duelist of Flash Version  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards from the animated series and manga. 
 Includes cards used by Alexis Rhodes ( GX  anime  and  ARC-V  anime  versions),  Jesse Anderson ,  Axel Brodie ,  Adrian Gecko ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Jack Atlas ,  Jinbei Tanigawa ,  Taro Yamashita ,  Yoshizo Hayashi ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Mizar ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Shun Kurosaki ,  Chojiro Tokumatsu , and Kite Tenjo ( ZEXAL  anime  and  ARC-V  anime  versions). 
 Includes members/support for the " Cyber ",  Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes ", " Number ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Performapal " and " Raidraptor " archetypes, as well as the " Legendary Planet " series. 
 Introduces the " Cardian " and " Cyber Angel " archetypes to the OCG. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Cipher " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack:
 
 15 packs per Japanese box 
 30 packs per Korean box 
 The set contains 45 cards, including:
 
 4  Ultra Rares 
 5  Super Rares 
 10  Rares 
 26  Commons 
 In the Japanese version all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Collector's Rare . 
 On the Korean version all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Secret Rare .
