 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto / Yami Yugi ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Jesse Anderson ,  Yubel ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Nash ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Yuto ,  Yugo ,  Yuri ,  Z-ARC ,  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Ai  and  Skye Zaizen . 
 Include new members and/or support for the " Chaos ", " Black Luster Soldier ", " Red-Eyes ", " HERO ", " Elemental HERO ", " Crystal Beast ", " Synchron ", " Resonator ", " Infernity ", " Barian's ", " CXyz ", " Number ", " Number 10X ", " Number C ", " Utopia ", " Utopic "," Rank-Up-Magic ", " Polymerization ", " Fusion ", " Pendulum Dragon ", " Stardust ", " Xyz Dragon ", " Synchro Dragon ", " Performapal"  and " Fusion Dragon " archetypes, as well as the  Signature move ,  Synchro ,  Overlay  and  Firewall  series. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards:
 
 3  Ultra Rares 
 24  Super Rares 
 23  Normal Parallel Rares 
 All Super Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rares ,  Prismatic Secret Rares  and  Ultimate Rares , and all Normal Parallel Rare cards are also available as Secret Rares and Ultimate Rares.
