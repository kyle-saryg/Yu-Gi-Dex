 Yuri , known as  Joeri  (ユーリ,  Yūri ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yuri , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
