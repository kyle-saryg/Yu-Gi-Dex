 Sugoroku's friend  is an unnamed character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime , based on  Sugoroku's friend , from the  manga .
 
 Biography 
 Sugoroku Mutou 's friend is a gamer from America. He gave his " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " card to Sugoroku. Sugoroku kept a photo of him in  Kame Game  and held the card with high sentimental value, refusing to sell it to  Seto Kaiba . [1]
