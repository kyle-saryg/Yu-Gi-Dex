 Takanori Hoshino  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He provides the voice of  Jack Atlas  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's [2] , a role he reprises in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V [3]  by voicing an  alternate universe version  of the character. He also voiced  Fake Jack Atlas  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , and  Honest  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . [4] 
 He also participates as a guest in multiple Yu-Gi-Oh! events, including Jump Festa presentations and OCG Times livestreams.
