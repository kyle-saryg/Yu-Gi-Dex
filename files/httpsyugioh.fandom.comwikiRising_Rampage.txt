 Rising Rampage  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) [1]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the ninth set in the  OCG ' s  10th series , following  Dark Neostorm  and followed by  Chaos Impact .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Theodore Hamilton ,  George Gore ,  Varis ,  Skye Zaizen  and  Ren 
 Includes members/support for the " B.E.S. ", " Borrel ", " Dinowrestler ", " Fortune Lady ", " Reptilianne ", " Charmer ", " Salamangreat ", " Witchcrafter ", " World Legacy ", and " Yosenju "  archetypes , as well as the " Link "  series . 
 Introduces the first support for the " Simorgh " archetype. 
 Introduces the " Marincess " and " Tenyi " archetypes to the  OCG / TCG .
 The  TCG  version includes members and/or support for the " Dragunity ", " F.A. ", " White "and " Number " archetypes. 
 The  TCG  version also introduces the " Dream Mirror " archetype. 
 Breakdown
