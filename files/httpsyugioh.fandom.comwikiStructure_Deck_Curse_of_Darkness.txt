 Structure Deck: Curse of Darkness  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the twelfth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twentieth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Surge of Radiance .

 Features 
 The deck features  DARK - Attribute  monsters, with the deck strategy involving  Beatdown   ATK -strength  monsters  and  damage /card  destruction  by  Continuous   effects . It includes 8 new cards to the OCG.
