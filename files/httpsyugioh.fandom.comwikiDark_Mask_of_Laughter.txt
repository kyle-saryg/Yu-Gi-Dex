 Dark Mask of Laughter  is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  manga. He is a member of  Team lswarm , who enters the  Team Duel Tournament .

 Biography 
 Team Duel Tournament 
 When  Team ZEXAL  entered the  Heartland Theme Park  Horror Tower, Laughter and  his team  appeared before them as flying masks. Then, they introduced themselves, and the upper mask transformed into Laughter, who introduced himself as "Dark Mask of Laughter". The match then began with Laughter and  Yuma 's  Duel . Although Laughter gave Yuma a hard time, he and his team were eventually defeated. [1]
