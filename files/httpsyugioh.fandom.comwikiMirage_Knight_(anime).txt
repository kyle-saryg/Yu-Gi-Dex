 [  Warrior  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except with " Dark Flare Knight ". If this card  battles  an opponent's  monster , it gains  ATK  equal to the ATK of that monster during  damage calculation  only. At the end of the  Battle Phase , if this card  attacked  or was attacked,  remove this card from play  and if " Dark Magician " and " Flame Swordsman " are in your  Graveyard ,  Special Summon  them to your side of the  field .
