 White Dragon Ritual  
 
 [  Dragon  /  Ritual  /  Effect  ] This  monster  can only be  Ritual Summoned  with the  Ritual Spell Card , " White Dragon Ritual ." You must also offer monsters whose total  Level  Stars equal 4 or more as a  Tribute  from the  field  or your  hand . When this monster  attacks  a  face-down   Defense Position  monster,  destroy  the face-down monster immediately with this card's  effect  without  flipping  it  face-up  or  damage calculations . You can  Special Summon  1 " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " from your hand or your  Deck  by offering this card as a Tribute during your  Main Phase . (Blue-Eyes White Dragon cannot attack during that  turn .)
