 LINK VRAINS Box 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LINK VRAINS Box  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Skye Zaizen ,  George Gore  and  Varis . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Gouki ", " Rokket " and " Trickstar "  archetypes . 
 Breakdown 
 Each  LINK VRAINS Box  contains:
 
 4  Extra Secret Rare  promotional cards 
 1 Set of 20  Ultra Rare  cards 
 1 of each of the following  Booster Packs :
 Code of the Duelist 
 Circuit Break 
 Extreme Force 
 1 Storage Box 
 1 Card Case 
 1 Set of 60  Card sleeves 
 1 of 4 Sets of 60 Card sleeves
