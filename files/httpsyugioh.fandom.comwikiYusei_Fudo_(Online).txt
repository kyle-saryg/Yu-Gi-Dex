 Yusei Fudo  ( 不動遊星 ,  Fudō Yūsei ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Online 3: Duel Accelerator . This is a video game depiction of  Yusei Fudo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Profile 
 Yusei is a  Signer  with the  Mark of the Dragon  on his arm.
 He is the son of  Professor Fudo , a researcher of  Enerdy . With his knowledge of machines and computers, he was able to create his own  Duel Runner . He seems calm and collected, but wields a great passion for the bond he shares with his friends. In terms of Dueling, he cherishes the close relationship he shares with his cards.
 
 Unlockables 
 By defeating Yusei 14 times, the player receives the card, " Scrap-Iron Scarecrow ". 
 By defeating Yusei 20 times, the player receives the Deck recipe, "Power of Bonds". 
 By defeating Yusei 45 times, the player receives the card, " Debris Dragon ". 
 By defeating Yusei 55 times, the player receives the Deck recipe, "Regained Bonds". 
 By defeating Yusei 90 times, the player receives the card " Assault Beast ". 
 By defeating Yusei 105 times, the player receives the the Deck recipe, "Evolving Bonds" and unlocks  Yusei Fudo's garms , in the shop. 
 Decks 
 Yusei's strategy revolves around  Synchro Summons , with " Stardust Dragon " being his main weapon of choice.
