 Starter Deck Yugi Reloaded  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Starter Deck Kaiba Reloaded , they are the sixteenth and seventeenth Decks in the  TCG ' s Starter Deck series, following  Super Starter: V for Victory .

 Each  Starter Deck Yugi Reloaded  contains 50 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:

 Features 
 The "Power Up With Packs!" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Silent Swordsman LV7 " from  Duelist League 17  and  " The Seal of Orichalcos " and " Magician of Black Chaos " from  Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World . Additionally, other Legendary Collections ( The Duel Academy Years  and  Joey's World ),  2013 Collectors Tins , other Starter Decks ( Super Starter: V for Victory ,  Starter Deck: Xyz Symphony , and  Starter Deck: Dawn of the Xyz ),  Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants ,  Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants Sealed Box , and  Judgment of the Light: Deluxe Edition  are featured, but no specific cards are mentioned.
