 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 
 
 
 Shingo Sawatari  is a recurring character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate version of  Sylvio Sawatari , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime . Shingo is a student from the famous  Leo Duel School .
