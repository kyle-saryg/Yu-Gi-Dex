 In  episode 158 ,  Rafael  uses this card during his Duel against  Yami Yugi . When " Dark Magician " (whose ATK was 3000 due to it being affected by " The Seal of Orichalcos ") attacks " Backup Gardna ", Rafael activates this card from his hand to discard two cards (" Wicked-Breaking Flamberge - Baou " and " Kishido Spirit ") from his hand and end the current turn immediately.
