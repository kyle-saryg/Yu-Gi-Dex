 [  Cyberse  /  Effect  ] If this card was used as material for a  Link Summon : You can  Special Summon  this card onto your  field . If this Special Summoned card would  leave the field ,  banish  it instead. When an  attack  is declared by a  Link Monster  you  control  against a Link Monster your opponent controls, you can banish this card from your  GY :  Negate  that attack, and the  targeted  Link Monster's ATK becomes 0 until end of  turn , and cannot target other Monsters with  effects  other than itself. You can only use each effect of "Firewall Guardian"  once per turn .
