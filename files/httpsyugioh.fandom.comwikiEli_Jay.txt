 Voice actor 
 
 
 Eli Jay  is an English language voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment . 
 
 Roles 
 Jay's first major role in  Yu-Gi-Oh!  history was being the voice of  Halldor  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 Jay's most notable role in  Yu-Gi-Oh!  history was providing the voice of  Yuma Tsukumo  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . [1]
