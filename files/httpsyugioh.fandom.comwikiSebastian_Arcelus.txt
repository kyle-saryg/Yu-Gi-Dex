 Sebastian Arcelus  is a voice actor for the English dubs of the Yu-Gi-Oh! anime. 
 
 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Arcelus's first role was the voice of  Espa Roba . He was also the voice of  Rex Raptor  starting from episode 145, replacing  Sam Riegel  until he was replaced in season 5 by  Tony Salerno . 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Arcelus portrays  Marcel Bonaparte  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
