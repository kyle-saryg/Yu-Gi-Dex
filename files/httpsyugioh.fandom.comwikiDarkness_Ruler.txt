 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 Darkness Ruler  is the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  incarnation of  PaniK . He is a member of the White Roses located at  St. Albans .
 Players can obtain the card " Exile of the Wicked ", using the  find  ability on B6, while playing against Darkness Ruler.
 
 Deck 
 Darkness Ruler uses a  Fiend Deck  with " King of Yamimakai " as his Deck Leader. All his monsters are  Fiend-Type , therefore automatically getting a  Field Power Bonus  due to the  Darkness field  when he is faced in story mode.
