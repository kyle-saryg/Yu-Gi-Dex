   Quick-Play 
 
 If you would take  damage  from the  effect  of " Sargasso the Different Dimension Battlefield ", you can  send  this card from your  hand  to the  Graveyard  instead. While this card is in your Graveyard, you are unaffected by "Sargasso the Different Dimension Battlefield".
