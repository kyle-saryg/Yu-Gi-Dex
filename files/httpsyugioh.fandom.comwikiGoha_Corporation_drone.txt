 Drones  are robots featured in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. They serve as a common method for the  Goha Corporation  to enforce their laws, and they have the authority to penalize citizens for infractions. Drones are commonplace across  Goha City , even in schools.

 Design 
 Appeareance 
       Other Goha drones TO-3 (yellow, red, blue)     
       Black Goha AI robot drone.     
 Goha drones are spherical drones who can be in various colors and shapes. Currently there are known Pink Drone, Yellow Drone, Red Drone, Black Drone and Blue drone. All have blue screens with Goha logo who serves as both a display and their faces. Their expressions are yellow (smiley faces or letters or numbers).They all have black circles under and on top of them and two cylindrical appendages with white rims, in addition to retractable limbs concealed at the bottom of the casing. Some Drone can operate human-sized bodies as well. They are capable of combining into other forms as well. 
 
 Personality 
 Goha drones are strict who punish everyone who breaks the rules or tell the people to do things according to the rules. If someone refuses to listen to them, they duel them (case with Kaizo).
