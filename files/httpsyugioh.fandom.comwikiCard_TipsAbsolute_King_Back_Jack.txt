 Manga 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Painful Escape ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Mimiclay ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Where Arf Thou? ", " Wolf in Sheep's Clothing ", " One for One ", " Zombowwow ", " Chaos Zone ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Stygian Security " and " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ". 
 Use cards like " Fiend Griefing ", " Foolish Burial ", " Mathematician ", " Armageddon Knight " and " Dark Grepher " to send this card from your Deck to the Graveyard.
 If you send this card to the Graveyard during your opponent's turn, immediately follow up with its other effect, either to Set the  Normal Trap Card  you placed on top of your Deck, or activate the effect of the card you would send to the Graveyard. 
 Discard this card for " A Feather of the Phoenix " to essentially Set any Normal Trap Card that was in your Graveyard. 
 This card's best applications are in " Sylvan ", " Burning Abyss " and " Paleozoic " Decks. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Last Will ". 
 In a " Last Turn " Deck, you can use "A Feather of the Phoenix" to place a Trap from your Graveyard on top of your Deck, which you can use to catch your opponent by surprise. If your Deck focuses on boosting your opponent's LP as well as fulfilling this card's LP cost, that Trap can be " Self-Destruct Button ".
