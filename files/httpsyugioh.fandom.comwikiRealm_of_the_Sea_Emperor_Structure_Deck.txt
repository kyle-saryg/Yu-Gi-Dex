 Realm of the Sea Emperor Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the twenty-third Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Samurai Warlords Structure Deck . This Deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Roar of the Sea Emperor  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 This deck features cards with the  WATER   Attribute  and introduces support for the " Atlantean " archtype, as well as reprints of past cards.
 The "Let's Power Up This Deck! (With Booster Packs)" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " The Seal of Orichalcos ," " Beast King Barbaros ," " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation ," " Blue Dragon Ninja ," " Moulinglacia the Elemental Lord ," and " Lemuria, the Forgotten City ."
