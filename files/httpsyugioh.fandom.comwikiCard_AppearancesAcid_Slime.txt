 In  episode 65 ,  Mad Dog  uses this card during his  Duel  against  Zane Truesdale . He Normal Summons this card on the first  turn  of the Duel. When Zane Special Summons " Cyber Dragon " through its own effect, Mad Dog  activates  " Last Machine Acid Virus " to Tribute this card, destroy "Cyber Dragon", and inflict 500 damage to Zane. Later " Proto-Cyber Dragon " attacks " Clone Slime ", but Mad Dog activates the effect of " Clone Slime " to Tribute the latter and revive this card as well as make this card " Proto-Cyber Dragon 's" attack target. "Proto-Cyber Dragon" then destroys this card, but this activates this card's effect, inflicting 800 damage to Zane.
