Link: https://yugioh.fandom.com/wiki/Anti-support

Anti-support  is an unofficial term for an interaction in which a card specifically affects cards with a particular property in some way harmful to the player who  possesses  the supported card, in contrast with  support . For example, " System Down "  banishes  all  Machine - Type  monsters from the opponent's field and Graveyard, thus it anti-supports Machine-Type monsters.
 As with  support , anti-support can be applied to  archetypes ,  Attributes ,  Types , or monsters with a certain  Level ,  Rank ,  ATK , or  DEF , among other groups of cards.
Exclusions, such as the  Xyz Materials  for " Number F0: Utopic Future " being required to not be " Number " monsters, are not considered anti-support. However,  Unclassified Effects  that require that there be only 1 monster with a particular property on the field or on a side of the field, such as with " Earthbound Immortal " and " Kaiju " monsters, are considered anti-support.

 Examples 
 " Goblin Pothole Squad " provides anti-support for the " Trap Hole " archetype. 
 " Ally of Justice " monsters provide anti-support for  LIGHT  monsters. 
 " Last Day of Witch " provides anti-support for  Spellcaster - Type  monsters. 
 " Ritual Sealing " provides anti-support for  Ritual Monsters . 
 " White Hole " provides anti-support for " Dark Hole ".
