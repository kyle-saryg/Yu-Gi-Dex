 " Take Back the Mutsuba Name's Pride! " is the thirty-ninth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on March 6, 2021.
 Magnum Over Road  clashes against  Wurm Ex-Cavator  in an intense battle!  Yuga  and  Asana  are having fun with their Duel. However, Asana's beloved R6 eventually breaks down. Even so, Asana still won't back down, as she wants to take back the Mutsuba name's pride.
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo vs. Asana Mutsuba 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
