 Activation requirement 
 Effect 
 Activate  only when your opponent  declares  an  attack  while you  control  an " Amazoness "  monster . All monsters your opponent controls are changed to  face-up   Attack Position  ( Flip Effects  are not activated), and lose 600  ATK  as long as they remain face-up on the  field . Your opponent must attack with all of their monsters.
