 This card's artwork is based on a scene from  episode 48  of  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS , just before  Playmaker  activates " Cynet Ritual ". 
 This card has three  counterparts .
 Cynet : "Cynet Ritual" 
 Fusion : " A.I. Love Fusion "
