 Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon  is a character version of the card, " Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon ". 
 Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . In  World Championship 2006 , it appears as a Theme Duelist and it challenges the player to forms 5 or more  Chain  during a Duel. In  World Championship 2007 , it appears as a Level Four Theme Duelist and it challenges the player to successfully  summon  "Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon" and win the duel. By beating it, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Five Theme Duelists and a new Booster Pack.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2006 , Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon plays a  Dragon  Deck titled, "Shining Dragon Awakes!". In  World Championship 2007 , it plays a  Blue-Eyes  Deck titled, "Shining Dragon".
