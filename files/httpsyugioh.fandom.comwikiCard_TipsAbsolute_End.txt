 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick " and " Lilith, Lady of Lament ". 
 Use this card when you have " Spirit Barrier " active, to take no battle damage and protect your monsters for a turn.
 Or, use " Des Counterblow " to discourage your opponent from attacking directly, otherwise their monster gets destroyed.
