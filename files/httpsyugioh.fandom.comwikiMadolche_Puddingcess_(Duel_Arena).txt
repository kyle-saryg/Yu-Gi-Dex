 Madolche Puddingcess  is a character version of the card " Madolche Puddingcess ". 
 She appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P057.

 Description 
 The kind Princess - or perhaps "Puddingcess" - of the Confection Kingdom. Doesn't like her  manner lessons  very much.
 
 Decks 
 An  Xyz  Deck centered around  EARTH  Monsters with the " Madolche "  archetype . The effect of "Madolche Puddingcess" is particularly strong. In terms of Summoning, start with a  Special Summon  from " Madolche Mewfeuille ". Use  Field Spell Cards  to enhance the otherwise low  ATK !
