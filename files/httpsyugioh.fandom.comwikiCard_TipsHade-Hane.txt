 Names 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Transmodify ", " Broken Blocker ", " Prediction Princess Coinorma ", " Spirit of the Fall Wind ", " Subterror Behemoth Apparition ", " Chaos-End Master ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Giant Rat ", " BOXer ", " Frontline Observer " and " Ninjitsu Art of Transformation ". 
 " Prediction Princess Tarotrei " and " Tindangle Angel " can both Special Summon this card from your hand or Graveyard face-down. 
 Unless this card would be destroyed by battle after being flipped face-up, you can use this card's  Flip effect  to bounce itself, as well as two monsters your opponent controls. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Last Will ".
