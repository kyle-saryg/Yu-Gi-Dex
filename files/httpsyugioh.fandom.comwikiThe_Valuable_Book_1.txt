 An official Catalog of the hit Card Game based on the super popular "Yu-Gi-Oh!" franchise! Covering everything from Volumes 1 to 4 and Boosters 1 to 4 we'll introduce all 451 cards, including the various Rare cards! 
 — Translated blurb from Shueisha's website [1] 
 
 Official Card Catalog: The Valuable Book 1  (公式カードカタログ　ザ・ヴァリュアブル・ブック　１,  Kōshiki Kādo Katarogu: Za Varyuaburu Bukku 1 ), [2]  originally known as  Official Card Catalog: The Valuable Book  and commonly known as  The Valuable Book 1 , is the first book in  The Valuable Book  price catalog series. It was published by  Shueisha  on August 20,  1999 , and covers 451 cards, including those in  Vol.1 ,  Vol.2 ,  Vol.3 ,  Vol.4 ,  Booster 1 ,  Booster 2 ,  Booster 3 , and  Booster 4 . [1] [3] 
 
 Promotional cards 
 Main article:  The Valuable Book 1 promotional cards 
 The book came with two promotional cards, " Dokurorider " and " Revival of Dokurorider ".
