 " Pendulum Dragon " (ペンデュラム・ドラゴン  Pendyuramu Doragon ) is an  archetype  used by  Yuya Sakaki  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. It is a sub-archetype of the " Pendulum " archetype. All members of this archetype are  DARK   Dragon   monsters , except for the following:
 
 EARTH  - " Beast-Eyes Pendulum Dragon " 
 Spellcaster  - " Double Iris Magician "
