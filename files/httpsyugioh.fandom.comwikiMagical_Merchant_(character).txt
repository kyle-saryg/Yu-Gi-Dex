 Magical Merchant  is a character version of the card, " Magical Merchant ". 
 Magical Merchant appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  as a Level Four Theme Duelist who challenges the player to activate 10 or more  Continuous Spell / Trap cards . By beating it, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Five Theme Duelists and a new Booster Pack.
 Magical Merchant also appears as an opponent in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .
