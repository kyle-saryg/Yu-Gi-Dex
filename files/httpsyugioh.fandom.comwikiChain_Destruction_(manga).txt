 Romaji:   Chēn Desutorakushon

 Card type
 
 
 Trap 
 
 
 
 Activate  when an ally or enemy  monster  with 2000 or less  ATK  appears!! All copies of the card in the  Deck  are  destroyed . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> You can  activate  this card when a  monster  with an  ATK  of 2000 points or less is  summoned  (including  Special Summon ). This monster is  destroyed , together with all Monster Cards of the same name in the summoning player's  hand  and  Deck . The summoning player's Deck is then shuffled.
