 Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS - Luke! Explosive Supremacy Legend  is a  manga  series and spin-off to the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  series. It was published in the  V Jump  magazine from September 19, 2020 to March 19, 2022 with Sugie Tasuku doing the illustrations and Masahiro Hikokubo scripting the Duels.
 
 Plot 
   This summary is currently incomplete. 
 The manga centralizes on  Tatsuhisa "Luke" Kamijo , the boy who ambitions to be the Duel King, and ruler of the Rush Duels along with his friends; Yuga, Romin and Gakuto.
