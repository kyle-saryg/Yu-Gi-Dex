 The  2012 Irish National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Ireland. It took place in University College Dublin in Ireland on June 2, 2012 [1]  and was won by Adam Hussein. [2] 
 
 Events 
 The entry fee was €15 for the main event and €5 for the  Dragon Duel . The timetable of events was as follows: [1]

 Decks 
 The winner and runner-up both used " Dino Rabbit " Decks with the exact same Decklist. [2] 
 The other top eight used a " Wind-Up ", an " Inzektor ", a " Malefic   Drain ", a " Sea Lancer ", a " Monster Mash " and a " Chaos Dragon " Deck. [2]
