 Princess Pikeru  is a character version of the card " Princess Pikeru ".
 She appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 , as a Theme Duel Duelist. In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , she appears as a Free Duel opponent.
