 With " Alien Skull ", " Alien Warrior ", " Alien Kid ", " Alien Telepath ", " Alien Grey " or " Alien Overlord " on the field, activate this card when a monster declares an attack to potentially destroy the attacker and deal damage to your opponent. 
 This card can be useful late game if your opponent has several monsters on the field. Activate its effect to spread A-Counters, then  suicide  it to summon more powerful Reptile monsters from the Graveyard.
 You can discard " Ogdoabyss, the Ogdoadic Overlord " to distribute 10  A-Counters  on opponent monsters. Then when M-Frame does crash into opponents monsters, summon it along with other monsters. Then you can activate its effect to send opponents monsters to the GY while the monsters summoned by M-Frame stay on the field.
