 Kodou Kinomiya  (来宮虎堂, Kinomiya Kodō) is a character appearing within the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga  and was a finalist in the  D1 Grand Prix .

 Biography 
 Kodou entered the  D1 Grand Prix  and advanced to the second stage. An omake from the tankōbon  volume 5  revealed that he Dueled  Jean  for the  Level  1 Star Ticket and lost. Later, he is shown to have headed to the Level ?  Duel Zodiac .  Crow  later goes there as well, and when he arrives, he finds Kodou on the ground, covered in injuries, saying that he can't win against "him", shortly before he passes out and retires from the tournament. [2]
