 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie Ani-Manga  is a 288 page, full color ani-manga based on the film,  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light .
 The French version only focuses on the beginning of the Duel between  Yugi Muto  and  Seto Kaiba . The preceding and following chapters were excluded.
 
 Promotional cards 
 Main article:  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie Ani-Manga promotional card 
 The English release of this book came with a promotional " Slifer the Sky Dragon " card. The French version included a " Theinen the Great Sphinx ". The Italian version included a " Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon ".
