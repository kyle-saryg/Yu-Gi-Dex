 Leo , known as  Rua  ( 龍亞 ,  Rua ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Leo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Biography 
 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock " Morphtronic Celfon " by defeating Leo for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Smashing Ground " by defeating Leo for the second time. 
 The player can unlock " Stumbling " by defeating Leo for the third time. 
 The player can unlock " Morphtransition " by defeating Leo for the fourth time. 
 The player can unlock " Fighting Spirit " by defeating Leo for the fifth time. 
 The player can unlock  Leo's Duel Runner  by defeating Leo for the first time. 
 The player can unlock Leo's Decklist by winning the  Gold Cup .
