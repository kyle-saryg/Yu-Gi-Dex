 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In Tag Force 1-3, he is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy . He is usually seen spacing out.
 
 Tag Force 
 Mathew can usually be found with  Craig , a  Ra Yellow  student, at the volcano. They also team up for the Tag Force tournament.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 In an optional random event, Mathew and Craig Tag Duel against the player and their partner.
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 From  Tag Force 4  onwards, he is a resident of  Tops .
 
 Tag Force 4 
 In the third event of  Luna 's story, Mathew and  Terence , under  Devack 's control, Duel against the  player  and Luna.
 In his own story, he competes with the player against  Carly Carmine  and Luna in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the Player will receive a photo of himself, Mathew and  Enzo  called "Tops Guy".
 
 Tag Force 5 
 In Poncho  Yusei Fudo 's third story event, Mathew and  Kalin Kessler  Tag Duel against the player and Yusei.

 Decks 
 In the first three  Tag Force  installments, Mathew uses Decks with low-Level high-ATK monsters that are forced into  Defense Position  upon attacking in tandem with " Final Attack Orders " to circumvent those effects.
 In the  Tag Force 5D's  games, Mathew also uses other Decks centered around monsters whose battle positions change constantly, such as  Clown Control  Decks  and  Karakuri  Decks .
