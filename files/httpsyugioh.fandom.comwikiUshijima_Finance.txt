      Ushijima Finance     
 Ushijima Finance  is a location in  Domino City  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  first series anime . [1] 
 Tetsu Tasaki  tried robbing Ushijima Finance, only to find the safe had been emptied earlier. The next day the attempted robbery was mentioned on  Morning Asa  after a report of a string of successful robberies, which were believed to have been committed by Tetsu Tasaki. However the other robberies were committed by  Jiro . [1]
