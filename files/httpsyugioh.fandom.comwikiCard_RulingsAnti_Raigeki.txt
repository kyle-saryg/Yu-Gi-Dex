 OCG Rulings 
 It does not  target . [1] 
 This card is activated directly in  Chain  to the activation of the opponent's " Raigeki ". [1] 
 If you activated " Anti Raigeki " in Chain to your opponent's " Raigeki ", your opponent cannot then activate another "Anti Raigeki" in Chain. [1] 
 If you activated " Anti Raigeki " in Chain to your opponent's " Raigeki ", and next your opponent activated " Royal Decree " in Chain, you cannot activate another "Anti Raigeki" in Chain. [1] 
 " Anti Raigeki " finishes resolving by  destroying  all monsters in the opponent's  Monster Zones . After the entire  Chain  has resolved, the activated card "Anti Raigeki" is sent to the Graveyard. (The card "Anti Raigeki" itself is not destroyed.) [1]
