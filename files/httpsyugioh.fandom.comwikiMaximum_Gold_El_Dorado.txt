 Maximum Gold 
 Maximum Gold: El Dorado 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Maximum Gold: El Dorado  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ), following  Maximum Gold  in the  Gold Series  series.

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Seto Kaiba ,  Yugi Muto / Yami Yugi ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Rare Hunter ,  Gozaburo Kaiba ,  Adrian Gecko ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Don Thousand ,  Shoma Yusa ,  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Lucien Grimley ,  Prince Ojin ,  Titan, the Rock Spirit ,  Reginald Kastle ,  Mako Tsunami ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Noah Kaiba ,  Rex Raptor ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Jack Atlas ,  Yuto ,  Quinton ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Emma Bessho ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Don Piero  and  Rex Goodwin . 
 Introduces the first support for " Numerounius " archetype to the  TCG . 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Maximum Gold  box contains 4 packs with 7 cards each: 2  Premium Gold Rares  and 5 gold-letter  Rares .
