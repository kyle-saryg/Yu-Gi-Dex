 Celebrity Twins  is the name of a  Tag Duel  team in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , consisting of  Leo  and  Luna .
 After defeating Celebrity Twins three times, the  player  can ask Leo or Luna to be their Tag Partner. After defeating them ten times, the player unlocks  Leo's custom Disk .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus  after defeating them tree times, the  player  unlocks the  Duel Academia uniform .
