 Ayumi Kinoshita  is a Japanese actress, voice actress and model.
 She provides the voice of Aki Izayoi ( Akiza Izinski ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's . [2]  In a press release, Kinoshita stated, "This is my second time as a voice actress, so, like Aki, I will be very lost. I hope I can grow."  [3] 
 In tokusatsu, she is best known for portaying  Marika "Jasmine" Reimon/DekaYellow  and  Yuko Fukui/Kyoryu Cyan  in the  Super Sentai  series,  Tokusou Sentai Dekaranger  and  Zyuden Sentai Kyoryuger .
