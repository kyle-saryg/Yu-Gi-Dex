 Deck

 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Yuya Sakaki , known as ( 榊   遊 矢 ,  Sakaki Yūya ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yuya Sakaki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
