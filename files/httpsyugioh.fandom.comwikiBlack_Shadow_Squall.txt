 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When a  Spell / Trap Card  is  activated : You can remove 1  Black Feather Counter  from your  field ;  negate  the activation, and if you do,  destroy  that card. If you  control  " Black-Winged Dragon ": You can  Set  this card from your  GY  to your  field , but  banish  it when it  leaves the field . You can only use 1 of the preceding  effects  of "Black Shadow Squall" per  turn , and only  once that turn .
