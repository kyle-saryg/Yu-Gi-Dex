 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Special Summon  this card as a  Normal Monster  ( Fiend / Tuner / EARTH / Level  2/ ATK  0/ DEF  0). (This card is also still a  Trap .) You can only  activate  1 "Shapesister" per  turn .
