 "The Menace of "Mist"!!"  is the fourteenth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! R  manga. It was first printed in Japan on May 20, 2005 in  V Jump  magazine. It was later reprinted in volume 2 of Yu-Gi-Oh! R.
 Kaiba  and  Willa Mette  begin their confrontation on the roof of the  KaibaCorp  building.
 
 Featured Duel: Seto Kaiba vs. Willa Mette 
 Turn 1: Kaiba 
 Kaiba   Sets  " Shrink " and  Normal Summons  " Blade Knight " (1600/1000) in  Attack Position .
 Turn 2: Willa 
 Willa  Sets a card and Normal Summons " Lancer Dragonute " (1600/1800) in  Defense Position .
 Turn 3: Kaiba 
Kaiba Normal Summons " Vorse Raider " (1900/1200) in Attack Position. It  attacks  "Lancer Dragonute", but Willa  activates  his  face-down  " Mist Body ",  equipping  it to "Lancer Dragonute" It prevents its  destruction in battle . Kaiba then Sets " Crush Card ".
 Turn 4: Willa 
Willa Sets two cards and Normal Summons " Axe Dragonute " (2000/1000) in Attack Position. He  activates  " Card Hexative ", preventing both of Kaiba's Set cards from being activated. "Axe Dragonute" attacks and  destroys  "Blade Knight" (Kaiba 4000 → 3600).
 Turn 5: Kaiba 
Kaiba Sets a card. He  Tributes  "Vorse Raider" to  Tribute Summon  " Swordstalker " (2000/1600) in Attack position. Its effect increases its  ATK  by 20% of the ATK of the "Blade Knight" in his  Graveyard  to 2320. "Swordstalker" attacks Axe Dragonute", but Willa activates his  face-down  " Synchronic Ability ", copying the effect of "Mist Body" and preventing "Axe Dragonute" from being destroyed in battle.
 Duel continues in  the next chapter .
