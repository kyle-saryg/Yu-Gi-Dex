 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 The official  Greek  name given (" Ιπτάμενος Ιππότης Parshath ") is an exact translation of the English name of this card.
 A possible translation of the Japanese name (" Angel Knight Perseus ") could be " Περσέας, Άγγελος Ιππότης " or " Αγγελικός Ιππότης Περσέας ".
 A transliteration of the name " Parshath " in Greek could be " Πάρσχαθ ".
