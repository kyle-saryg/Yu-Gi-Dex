 Tania  (タニヤ,  Taniya ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Tania , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Tania appears in Part 3, teaming up with a brainwashed  Bastion Misawa . After beating Part 3, she can be found in the Forest after 10:00 P.M in Part 1. If the player defeats her 10 times, she will give them her  Deck Recipe  and 3 copies of the  Amazoness Arena  card.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Tania makes a cameo during the second story event for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters, where the player and their partner duel  Atticus Rhodes  and  Yasmin . However, if the player is partnering with either of those characters at the time, Tania will take their place in the opponent's team.
