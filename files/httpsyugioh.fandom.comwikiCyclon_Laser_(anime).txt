   Equip 
 
 This card can only be used to  equip  " Gradius ". Increases the  ATK  of "Gradius" by 300 points. When "Gradius"  attacks  with an ATK that is higher than the  DEF  of opponent's  Defense Position   monster , inflict the difference as  Battle Damage  to your opponent's  Life Points .
