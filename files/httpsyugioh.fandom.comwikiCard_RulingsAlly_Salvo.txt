 TCG Rulings 
 This effect  targets  2 cards on the field. [1] 
 If one of the  targets  is missing when this effect  resolves , the other is still  destroyed . [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 This card's effect  targets  two cards. [2] 
 If one of the targets is removed from the field before " Ally Salvo "'s effect resolves, then the other target is still destroyed. [3]
