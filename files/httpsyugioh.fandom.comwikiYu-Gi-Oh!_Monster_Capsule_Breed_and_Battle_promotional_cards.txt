 Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle  promotional cards  are a set of ten  Konami's  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  cards released with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle .
 These cards are not part of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  as they were released before the game was created. This set and the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  promotional cards  are the only sets to contain such cards.
