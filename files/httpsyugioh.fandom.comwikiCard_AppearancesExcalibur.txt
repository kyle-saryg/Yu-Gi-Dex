 In  episode 164 ,  Yami Yugi  uses this card during his Duel against an  Orichalcos Soldier . He equips this card onto " Dark Magician Girl the Dragon Knight ", doubling its ATK. "Dark Magician Girl the Dragon Knight" then attacks and destroys " Orichalcos Gigas " (whose ATK was 2900 due to its own effect and " The Seal of Orichalcos "), winning Yami Yugi the duel.
