 Walter , known as  Takafumi Kai  in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Slifer Red  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 - 6 , his name is given to  Senshi Teshima .

 Decks 
 In the first two Tag Force games, Walter uses  Alien  Decks . Compared to  Jaime , however, Walter's Decks as a whole are more oriented towards  locking down the field  and protecting his cards.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Walter switches to  Worm  Decks .
