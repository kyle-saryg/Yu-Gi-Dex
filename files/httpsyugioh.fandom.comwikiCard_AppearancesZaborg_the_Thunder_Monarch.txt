 Anime 
 
 In  episode 75 ,  Thunder  uses this card during his Tag Duel with  Frost  against  Syrus Truesdale  and  Tyranno Hassleberry . He  Tribute Summons  this card by Tributing " Thunder Knight ". This card's effect then activates, destroying " Truckroid ". This card then attacks Syrus directly, but Frost activates " Revolving Stage " to redirect the Battle Damage that Syrus would take to Hassleberry. In  the next episode , this card attacks Syrus directly. This card is later destroyed by the effect of " Jurassic Impact ". 
 In  episode 77 ,  T-Bone  uses this card during his Duel against  Jaden Yuki  (this card originally belonged to Thunder, but Sarina forcibly placed this card into T-Bone's deck along with Frost and Blaze's signature cards before T-Bone dueled Jaden). He adds this card from his Deck to his hand via the effect of " Lightning Crash " T-Bone then Sets a card and Tributes " Granmarg the Rock Monarch " to Summon another "Granmarg the Rock Monarch". The effect of "Granmarg" then activates, allowing T-Bone to destroy his Set " Dowsing Point ". The latter's effect then activates, allowing T-Bone to add " Mobius the Frost Monarch " from his Deck to his hand. T-Bone then activates " Shadow Moon " to send this card, " Thestalos the Firestorm Monarch ", "Mobius", and "Granmarg" from his hand and field to the Graveyard in order to activate " Blue Moon " and Special Summon " Demiurge Ema ". 
 In the anime this card's effect was called  Space Thunder  ( Thunder Crush  in the English dub) and its attack was called  Rolling Thunder .
