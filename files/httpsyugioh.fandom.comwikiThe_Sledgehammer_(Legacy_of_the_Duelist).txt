 The Sledgehammer , known as  Strong Ishijima  (ストロング 石 島   Sutorongu Ishijima ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  The Sledgehammer , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
