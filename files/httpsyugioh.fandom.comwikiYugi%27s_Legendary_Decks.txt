 Yugi's Legendary Decks 
 Legendary Decks II 
 
 
 
 
 
 Yugi's Legendary Decks  is a preconstructed set released in the  TCG , and the first set in the  Legendary Decks  series. Its card contents are essentially identical to the three  Memories of the Duel King  sets from the  OCG  consolidated into one set, with the sole exception of the  Token  cards.

 Breakdown 
 The set contains:
 
 A 41-card  Deck  representing  Yugi 's Deck in the first season of the original  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime, including his initial Duel with  Seto Kaiba  and his adventures in  Duelist Kingdom , featuring " Exodia " and including the first  TCG  printing of " Griffore ". 
 A 41-card Deck representing Yugi's  Battle City  Deck from the second and third seasons of the anime, including the first  TCG  printing of  Arkana 's "red" " Dark Magician " artwork. 
 A 41-card Deck representing Yugi's Deck used against  Yami Yugi  in the  Ceremonial Battle , featuring " Gadget " monsters and the "Silent" series of " LV " monsters. 
 3  Secret Rare  cards new to the  TCG : " Electromagnetic Turtle ", " Dark Renewal ", and " Black Illusion ". 
 3  Ultra Rare  non-playable historical cards: " Glory of the King’s Hand ", " Set Sail for The Kingdom ", and " Duelist Kingdom " 
 3 Ultra Rare non-playable  Egyptian God  Cards: " Slifer the Sky Dragon ", " Obelisk the Tormentor ", and " The Winged Dragon of Ra ". 
 1 Ultra Rare "Yugi" Token Card.
