 In  chapter 44 ,  Atticus Rhodes  uses this card in his duel against  Jim Crocodile Cook . He Normal Summons this card on the first turn. On Jim's turn, his " Lion Alligator " attacks and destroys this card. In the  following chapter , Atticus uses the effect of " Synthesize Sphere " to revive this card. On Jim's next turn, Jim activates " Regressing Wings " to negate the effects of Atticus' monsters until the end of the turn, which enables Jim to attack this card with " The Tyrant Neptune ", but Atticus activates " Unbroken Atmosphere " to prevent this card's destruction. On Atticus' next turn, he Tributes this card, "Synthesize Sphere" and " Transforming Sphere " to Tribute Summon " The Atmosphere ". 
 In  chapter 53 , Atticus uses this card during his duel against  Aster Phoenix . He Special Summons this card from his Deck via " Atmospheric Transference ". Atticus then summons another " Sphere " monster to enable this card's effect to prevent Aster from attacking. On Atticus' next turn, he Tributes this card, "Synthesize Sphere" and " Breeze Sphere " to Tribute Summon "The Atmosphere".
