 Continuous 
 When an opponent's  monster  with an original  ATK  of 3000 or more  declares  an  attack :  Target  that monster;  negate  the attack, that target's ATK becomes 0, and its  effects  are negated. When the target  leaves the field ,  destroy  this card.
