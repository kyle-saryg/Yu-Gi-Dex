 Kerry Williams  is a voice actress for  4Kids Entertainment  whose provided voices for the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime series.
 
 Roles 
 Williams' major role in the anime was portraying  Rebecca Hawkins  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , Williams voices  Sadie [ citation needed ]  and background female  Obelisk Blue  students. [ citation needed ]
