 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can only play this card if you were present at the 2007 Yu-Gi-Oh! TRADING CARD GAME World Championship. The  ATK  of all your " Elemental Hero " and " Neo-Spacian "  monsters  on the field is doubled.
