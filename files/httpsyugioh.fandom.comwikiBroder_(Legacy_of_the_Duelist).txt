 Broder , known as  Brave  (ブレイブ,  Bureibu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Broder , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
