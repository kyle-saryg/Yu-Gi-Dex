 Kalin Kessler , known as  Kyosuke Kiryu  (鬼柳京介 ,  Kiryū Kyōsuke ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Kalin Kessler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
