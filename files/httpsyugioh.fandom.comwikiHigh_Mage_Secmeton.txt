 High Mage Secmeton  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is located at the Ocean Shrine.  The Prince  faces him after defeating the  Ocean Mage . The Prince duels and defeats him, taking the  Millennium Necklace .
 He also appears as an enemy Marshal in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom , named  Secmayton .
