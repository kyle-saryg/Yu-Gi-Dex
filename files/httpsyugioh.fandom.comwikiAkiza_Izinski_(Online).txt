 Akiza Izinski , known as  Aki Izayoi  ( 十六夜 アキ,  Izayoi Aki ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Online 3: Duel Accelerator . This is a video game depiction of  Akiza Izinski , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Profile 
 Akiza is a  Signer  with the  Mark of the Dragon  representing the Front Claw. She was once feared as the  Psychic Duelist  "Black Rose", but her encounter with  Yusei Fudo  and his friends gradually saw her opening up to them. After the battle against the  Dark Signers , she joined Yusei's group of friends. Despite being a top student at  Duel Academy , she seeks Yusei for help in Physics and his attention.
 
 Unlockables 
 By defeating Akiza 14 times, the player receives the card, " Wall of Thorns ". 
 By defeating Akiza 20 times, the player receives the Deck recipe, "Chilling Rose". 
 By defeating Akiza 45 times, the player receives the card, " Black Garden ". 
 By defeating Akiza 55 times, the player receives the Deck recipe, "Whispers of the Black". 
 By defeating Akiza 90 times, the player receives the card " Tytannial, Princess of Camellias ". 
 By defeating Akiza 105 times, the player unlocks  Akiza Izinski's garms  in the shop. 
 Deck 
 Akiza uses a  Plant - Type  Deck, centered around " Black Rose Dragon ". 
 Her level 1 Deck, is titled "Chilling Rose".
