 Anime 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 73 ,  Damon Lopez  uses this card in his  Turbo Duel  against  Sergey Volkov . The full duel is not shown, but this card is shown to have destroyed several of Sergey's (unseen) monsters before being destroyed itself. 
 In  episode 95 , Damon summons this card when he,  Shinji Weber  and  Tony Zomboni  are confronted by a  Sector Security  line during the  Commons ' riots. It is destroyed by an officer's " Goyo King ". 
 In  episode 145 , this card appears in  Shay Obsidian 's memories of teaching his younger sister  Lulu  how to duel. 
 Video games 
 Alien Shocktrooper  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . 
 Alien Overlord  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . 
 Jaime  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 .
