 Sherry LeBlanc , known as (シェリー・ルブラン,  Sherī Ruburan ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution .. This is a video game depiction of  Sherry LeBlanc , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
