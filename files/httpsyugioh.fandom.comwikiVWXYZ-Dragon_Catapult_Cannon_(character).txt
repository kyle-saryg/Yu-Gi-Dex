 VWXYZ-Dragon Catapult Cannon  is a character version of the card, " VWXYZ-Dragon Catapult Cannon ".
 VWXYZ-Dragon Catapult Cannon appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , by beating it, it can unlock  Chazz Princeton .
 
 Deck 
 In World Championship 2006 and 2008, VWXYZ-Dragon Catapult Cannon uses a  VWXYZ  Deck titled, "Machine 5 Deck" and "Combined Orders".
