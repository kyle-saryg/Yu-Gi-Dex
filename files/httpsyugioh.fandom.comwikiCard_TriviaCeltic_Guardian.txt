 This card has a  retrained  counterpart: " Obnoxious Celtic Guard ". 
 Either this monster or "Obnoxious Celtic Guard" appears in the artworks of " Hero's Guild ", " Summoning Clock ", " Superior Protector " ," Triggered Summon ", and " Mirage Ruler ". 
 The  Bandai  version of this card has 1300 ATK, 2200 DEF and is Level 5, while the Konami version has 1400 ATK, 1200 DEF and is Level 4. 
 Some versions of the LOB-007 print have the Japanese version of the logo on the back bottom right corner of the card, where "Yu-Gi-Oh!" should be written in English. 
 This card's second  OCG  artwork is taken from the title page for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 66: " Seen Through!? " . 
 When  Atem  summons this monster for the first time during  his first duel with Pegasus , he refers to this monster as  Elf Swordsman,  its Japanese name.
