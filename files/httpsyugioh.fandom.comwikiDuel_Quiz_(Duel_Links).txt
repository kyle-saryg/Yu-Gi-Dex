Duel Quiz  is a minigame in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It is accessible through the Duel School. All of them have a preset field configuration and requires the player to win in that turn. Level 1 Quizzes are unlocked at the beginning of the game, Level 2 Quizzes are unlocked at Stage 10 while Level 3 Quizzes are unlocked at Stage 15.
 Each Duel Quiz that is successfully cleared awards the player a certain number of Gems or a card.
