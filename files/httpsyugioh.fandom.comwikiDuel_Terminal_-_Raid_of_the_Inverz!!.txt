 Duel Terminal - Raid of the Inverz!!  is the tenth installment of the  Duel Terminal  series which follows  Duel Terminal - Vylon Descends!! . It is followed by  Duel Terminal - Judgment of Omega!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. 
 Introduces the new " Steelswarm ", " Gishki " and " Gusto " archetypes. 
 Introduces new  Ritual  and  Synchro Monsters . 
 Introduces  Ishizu Ishtar ,  Axel Brodie ,  Greiger  and  Sayer  as duelable opponents.
