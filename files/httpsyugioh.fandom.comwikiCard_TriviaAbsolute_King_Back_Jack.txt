 This monster is based on  Jack Atlas , who owns this card and holds the title of king (in Japanese, his title can be read as "absolute king"). The design of the monster's backpack is based on Jack's  Duel Runner , the  Phoenix Whirlwind . 
 This card's name "Back Jack" may be a pun on "Blackjack", "Backpack" or "The Back of Jack Atlas". 
 This card is similar to " Kaibaman ", " Numeral Hunter ", and " Go! - D/D/D Divine Zero King Rage " in that they are all personifications of their users, the aforementioned being  Seto Kaiba ,  Kite Tenjo , and  Declan Akaba  respectively.
