 Anime 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (القرد البهلوان), literally  Acrobat Ape . This would be anatomically incorrect, as apes do not have tails. It would also place it in the  Ape  archetype. 
 The given approximated Arabic name (السّعدان البهلوان) translates to  Acrobat Monkey .
