 Duel Terminal 1  is made up of the OCG sets  Duel Terminal - Synchro Awakening!!  and  Duel Terminal - Invasion of Worms!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 100 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 60 of the cards are new, while 40 are reprints. Introduces the " Genex " archetype to the TCG. Includes further support for the " Ice Barrier ", " Mist Valley ", " X-Saber ", " Ally of Justice ", " Worm " and " Flamvell " archetypes.
