 Elemental Hero Flame Wingman  is a character version of the card " Elemental Hero Flame Wingman ".

 Appearances 
 Anime 
 It appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  as a Duel Monster Spirit of  Jaden Yuki 's ace card (Until Elemental Hero Neos became his new ace card). In  episode 151 , it is alongside Jaden's monsters which encouraged Jaden to play " Polymerization " card, after the latter was unable to play it due to  the Supreme King 's actions. It made a reappearance in  episode 172 , alongside both Jaden's and  Jesse 's familiar monsters, when Jaden was freed from  Sartorius ' curse.
 
 Video games 
 It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus . This character and  Supply  can be unlocked as Turbo Tag Duel opponents after successfully Synchro Summoning " Scrap Twin Dragon " in a Duel. Their Tag Team name is  You-Go! .
