 Duel Terminal 6a  is the first part of the  TCG   Duel Terminal 6 . It contains cards from the  OCG  set  Duel Terminal - Judgment of Omega!!  and was released February 3, 2012.

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. Includes further support for " Vylon ", " Laval ", " Gem-Knight ", " Steelswarm ", " Gishki " and " Gusto " archetypes.
