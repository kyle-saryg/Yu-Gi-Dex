 Dark Master - Zorc  is a  Duel Monster Spirit  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links , serving as an event opponent for  The Shadow Memories: Bakura  event.

 The Shadow Memories: Bakura 
 Both players possess the following skill: "Success or Failure" (Can be used if you control a face-up monster at the beginning of your Battle Phase. Roll a ten-sided dice two times and apply the appropriate effect based on the result.
 
 00: Your monster(s) gain 1000 ATK/DEF until the end of the turn and battle damage they inflict to your opponent is doubled. 
 01-09: Your monster(s) gain 500 ATK/DEF until the end of the turn. 
 10-49: Your monster(s) gain 200 ATK/DEF until the end of the turn. 
 50-98: Your monster(s) gain 100 ATK/DEF until the end of the turn. 
 99: The ATK/DEF of your monster(s) become 0 until the end of the turn.)
