 Naoyuki Kageyama  is the author of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga .
 He was born on April 12, 1969 and is originally from Tokyo, Japan. He is the recipient of an honorable mention for the 1990  Weekly Shōnen Jump  Hop Step Award for his work,  Mahō no Trump  (Magic Trump) and started drawing  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  for  V Jump  in February 2006. Kageyama is a baseball fan and his favorite team is the Seibu Lions. [1] 
 He also mentions in his manga cracks that he dislikes  Synchro Monsters  and promised himself to never use one, as he prefers  Fusion Monsters  (namely " Elemental HERO " Fusions), much to his dismay when he found out that " Dark End Dragon " and " Light End Dragon " were going to be released as Synchro Monsters.
