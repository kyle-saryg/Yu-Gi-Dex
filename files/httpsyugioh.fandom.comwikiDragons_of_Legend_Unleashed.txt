 Dragons of Legend: Unleashed  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It includes dozens of cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!   manga  and  TV series . [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yami Yugi ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Rafael , Alexis Rhodes ( GX  anime  and  ARC-V  anime  versions),  Jesse Anderson ,  Axel Brodie ,  Adrian Gecko ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Franz ,  Jack Atlas ,  Tetsu Trudge ,  Jinbei Tanigawa ,  Taro Yamashita ,  Yoshizo Hayashi ,  Yuma Tsukumo , Kite Tenjo ( ZEXAL  anime  and  ARC-V  anime  versions),  Mizar ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Shun Kurosaki  and  Chojiro Tokumatsu . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Cyber ", " Dark Magician ", " Galaxy ", " Guardian ", " Kuriboh ", " Legendary Dragon ", " Legendary Knight ", " Number ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Performapal ", " Raidraptor ", " Red Dragon Archfiend " and " Red-Eyes "  archetypes , as well as the  Legendary Planet  and " Orichalcos "  series . 
 Introduces the " Cipher ", " Cyber Angel " and " Flower Cardian " archetypes to the  TCG .
