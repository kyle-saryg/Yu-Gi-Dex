 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Pay  1000  Life Points .  Declare  a  Type  and an  Attribute . Your opponent must  send  1  monster  with both the declared Type and Attribute from their  hand  or  Deck  to the  Graveyard .
