 Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon  is a character version of the card " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon ".
 It appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P054. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 9-5 under Quest mode.
