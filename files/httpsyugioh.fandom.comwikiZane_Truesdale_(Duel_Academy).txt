 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Duel Academy 
 
 
 
 Zane Truesdale , known as  Ryo Marufuji  (丸藤 亮,  Marufuji Ryō ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Duel Academy  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Zane Truesdale , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
 
 Deck 
 Zane uses a Deck focused on " Cyber Dragons ", supplemented by other  Machine - Type   monsters  and the " Chaos " monsters. 2 Main Deck cards; 1 Fusion Deck card are missing from this list.
