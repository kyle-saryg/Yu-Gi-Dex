 Red Rose 
 
 
 
 
 
 The Rose Card is "Seacret Key Card", 5 Red Roses & 5 White Roses clect fall into the "GOD". 
 Description
 
 
 8 members of the Lancasterians organization, the Red Roses, each had a Red Rose card. The objective of the game was to collect all 16 Rose Cards Red and  White . If the player sided with the Lancasterians, they automatically had all 8 Red Rose cards, if they sided with the Yorkists, the objective was to win each of the Red Rose cards off the Lancasterians.
