 Burning Nova  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on July 09, 2018, 8:00 AM (EST).
 
 Description 
 A new star is born! The flames of justice rekindled! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Elemental HERO Nova Master ," An " Elemental HERO " monster that lets you draw a card every time it destroys a monster by battle. It requires a FIRE monsters as a Fusion Materiel, so you can use "Fire Fist" and "Laval" cards available from this BOX! 
" Batteryman Industrial Strength ," A monster that can be Special Summoned by banishing 2 Batteryman monster from your Graveyard. In addition, you can banish 1 Thunder-Type monster from your Graveyard to destroy 1 monster and 1 Spell/Trap Card on the field! 
" Magician of Faith ," A Flip monster that lets you add a Spell Card from your Graveyard to your hand. Recycle powerful Spell Cards and use them again! 
" Dust Tornado ," A Trap Card that lets you destroy 1 of your opponent's Spell/Trap Cards, then allows you to set a Trap Card. Destroy your opponent's Spell/Trap Card during your opponent's turn and set your own Trap Card. Then, you can activate the Trap Card you set during your own turn! 
A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Burning Nova  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
