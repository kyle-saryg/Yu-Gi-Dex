 Duel Terminal - Judgment of Omega!!  is eleventh installment of the  Duel Terminal  series which follows  Duel Terminal - Raid of the Inverz!! . It is followed by  Duel Terminal - Xyz Startup!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. 
 It also includes further support for " Vylon ", " Laval ", " Gem-Knight ", " Steelswarm ", " Gishki " and " Gusto " archetypes. 
 Introduces  Sartorius  and  Sherry LeBlanc  as duelable opponents. 
 Trivia: This box features a reference to the "Enemy Controller" QTE game where the red button shown at the bottom right was known as the "action button"
