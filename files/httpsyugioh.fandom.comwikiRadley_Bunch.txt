The  Radley Bunch , known as the  Ramon Group  in the Japanese version is a group of people led by  Radley  who lived in  Crash Town  and opposed the  Malcolm Crew .
 The  Duelist  who always Dueled for  Radley  was  Kalin Kessler , who won fifty times in a row before losing to  Yusei Fudo  from the Malcolm Crew.
