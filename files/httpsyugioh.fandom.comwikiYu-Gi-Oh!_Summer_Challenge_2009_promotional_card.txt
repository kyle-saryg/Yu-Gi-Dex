 Introduction
 
 
 
 The Summer Challenge 2009 was held throughout Japan from August 9 . " Gaia Knight, the Force of Earth " was the promo card given to who collected 3 or more wins, while the participation promo was " Sonic Chick ". Other cards were given at random as promos.

 OCG Set Prefix:  YSD4-JPP 
 OCG Set Release:  August–September, 2009
