 Kaiba Kōporeishon Urutora Rea
 
 
 Kaiba Corporation Ultra Rare  ( 海馬 コーポレイションウルトラレア  Kaiba Kōporeishon Urutora Rea ), is an  OCG -exclusive rarity introduced in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Movie Pack . 
 The cards are similar to  Ultra Rares  (gold card name and a holofoil image), but also have a  Kaiba Corporation Rare  coating.
