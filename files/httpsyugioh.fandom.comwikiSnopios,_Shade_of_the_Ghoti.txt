 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During the  Main Phase  ( Quick Effect ): You can  banish  2  Fish   monsters  from your  hand  and/or  GY ;  Special Summon  this card from your hand. If this card is  Special Summoned : You can  target  1  face-up  card on the  field ; banish it when it  leaves the field . If this card is banished: You can banish 1 Fish monster from your GY;  add  this card to your hand. You can only use each  effect  of "Snopios, Shadow of the Ghoti"  once per turn .
