 2017 Mega-Tin Mega Pack  or  Mega Tin 2017 Mega Pack  is a  Mega Pack  set released with the  2017 Mega-Tins .
 The set contains cards, made up of a year's worth of  Booster Packs  from the sets  Shining Victories ,  The Dark Illusion ,  Invasion: Vengeance , and  Raging Tempest .
