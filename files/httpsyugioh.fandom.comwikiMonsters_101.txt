 Monsters 101 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Monsters 101  is a  Booster Pack  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 , containing basic  Monster Cards .
 It is available from the beginning of the game. Each pack costs 80  DP  and contains 5 cards.
