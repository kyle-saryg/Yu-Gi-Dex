 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  6  monsters You can also  Xyz Summon  this card by using a " Constellar "  Xyz Monster  you  control  as  material , other than "Constellar Ptolemy M7". ( Transfer  its materials to this card.) If  Summoned  this way, the following  effect  cannot be  activated  this turn.  Once per turn : You can  detach  1 material from this card, then  target  1 monster on the  field  or in either  GY ;  return  that target to the  hand .

   Japanese
