 The " Sealed Play Battle Kit " is a Yu-Gi-Oh! set that was released exclusively at Wal-Mart stores (and in Imaginaire stores in Qu√©bec, Canada). It has since been available in other retailers. The set includes 10 booster packs of " Battle Pack: Epic Dawn ", and 1 "Battle Pack: Epic Dawn"  playmat . The playmat, being made of thick and durable rubber, is styled after the mats featured in the  Gold Series 4: Pyramids Edition .
