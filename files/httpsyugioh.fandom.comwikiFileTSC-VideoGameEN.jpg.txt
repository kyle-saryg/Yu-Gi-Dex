 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards Bundles 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards  promotional cards  are a set of 3 real-life cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards .
 
 Breakdown 
 In North American English.
 
 1  Effect Monster 
 1  Spell Card 
 1  Trap Card 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 In European English, German, French and Italian.
 
 2  Spell Cards 
 1  Trap Card 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 In Spanish.
 
 2  Effect Monsters 
 1  Trap Card 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares
