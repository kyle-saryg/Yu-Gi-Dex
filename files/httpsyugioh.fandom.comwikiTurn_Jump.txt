 Turn Jump 
 
 Japanese:   時の飛躍－ターンジャンプ－ 
 Romaji:   Toki no Hiyaku -Tān Janpu- 
 Translated:  Leap of Time -Turn Jump- 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Quick-Play 
 
 You can only  activate  this card during the  Battle Phase . For the next 3  turns , each player must pass their turn. (Also, do not  draw  during the  Draw Phase .) 6 turns after activation, resume play from the Battle Phase.
