 Duelist Pack: Dimensional Guardians 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duelist Pack: Dimensional Guardians  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixteenth set in the  TCG ' s Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Rivals of the Pharaoh . [1] [2]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yuya Sakaki ,  Yugo ,  Yuri ,  Sora Perse ,  Shun Kurosaki , Alexis Rhodes ( GX  and  ARC-V ), Jack Atlas ( 5D's  and  ARC-V ) and Kite Tenjo ( ZEXAL  and  ARC-V ). 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Archfiend ", " Cipher ", " Cyber ", " Cyber Angel ", " Cyber Girl ", " Frightfur ", " Fusion ", " Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes ", " Pendulum ", " Performapal ", " Raidraptor ", " Rank-Up-Magic ", " Red Dragon Archfiend ", " Resonator " and " Speedroid " archetypes, as well as " Signature move " and " Synchro " series.
