 Jack Atlas  (ジャック・アトラス,  Jakku Atorasu ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Decade Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Jack Atlas , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
