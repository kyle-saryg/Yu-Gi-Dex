 TCG Rulings 
 The effect to  Special Summon  can still be used during the  Damage Step , even if your monster is destroyed by a card effect instead of battle. [1] 
 You can  Special Summon  a monster with this effect when you destroy your opponent’s “ Amazoness ” monster and send it to the  Graveyard . [1] 
 If a  face-down  “ Amazoness ” monster is destroyed, you can activate the effect to  Special Summon . [1] 
 You refer to the printed  Level  of the “ Amazoness ” monster when deciding on a monster to  Summon . [1]
