 TCG Rulings 
 If “ Ancient Crimson Ape ” is destroyed and sent to the Graveyard, its effect does not activate. [1] 
 If you control your opponent’s monster and it is destroyed and sent to the Graveyard, the effect of “ Ancient Crimson Ape ” activates. [1]
