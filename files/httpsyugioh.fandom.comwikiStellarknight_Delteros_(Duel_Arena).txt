 Stellarknight Delteros  is a character version of the card " Stellarknight Delteros ". 
 He appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. His partner ID is P133.
 He was only obtainable by having 15 or more of the following " tellarknight "-related cards in one's trunk (including multiple copies of 1 or more cards) before maintenance occurred on January 7, 2015: " Satellarknight Deneb ", " Satellarknight Altair ", " Satellarknight Vega ", " Satellarknight Alsahm ", " Satellarknight Unukalhai ", "Stellarknight Delteros", " Stellarknight Alpha ", " Satellarknight Skybridge ", " Stellarnova Wave ", and/or " Stellarnova Alpha ". The reason for this limited section of cards was because  The New Challengers  and newer packs had not yet been released in-game.
 Applicable players received him in their Gift Boxes on January 9, 2015.

 Trivia 
 This character's partner description refers to the fact that  this monster  is composed of the bodies of " Satellarknights Deneb ", " Altair " and " Vega ", as seen in the artwork of " Stellarknight Alpha ". 
 When this partner was first released, its Deck name was "Threads of Stars" instead. This was a mistake, one that was likely related to the fact that the silhouette of  Baxia, Brightness of the Yang Zing  was accidentally used in promotions for the "1-week Arena Challenge!" event of January 2015, instead of that of  Saffira, Queen of Dragons . Baxia's Deck name was supposed to be "Thread of Stars" instead of "Queen's Blessings", which was supposed to be Saffira's Deck name. These mix-ups were fixed during the same maintenance that released Saffira as a partner, and this partner was assigned a new Deck name. 
 This partner's Deck name is a joke based on combining the names of the comedy play,  A Midsummer Night's Dream , and the astronomical asterism, the  Summer Triangle .
 The latter name is used since  Delteros itself is a reference to the Summer Triangle .
