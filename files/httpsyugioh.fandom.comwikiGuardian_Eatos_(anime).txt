Main card page: " Guardian Eatos " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Guard "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 Ｇ 」 .   As its English name contains "Guard", it  may  be treated a member of the "Guard" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 [  Fairy  /  Effect  ] This card can only be  Summoned  while you  control  a  face-up  " Celestial Sword - Eatos ". If you have no  monsters  in your  Graveyard , you can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . You can  destroy  a "Celestial Sword - Eatos"  equipped  to this card to  remove from play  Monster Cards from the top of your opponent's Graveyard until a non-Monster Card is revealed, then have this card gain  ATK  equal to the combined ATK of the monsters removed. Monsters with 0 ATK cannot be removed from play by this  effect .
