 Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (الرامية الأمازونيّة) literally  Amazonian Archer  or  Archer of the Amazon . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (الأمازونيّة الرامية) literally  Archer Amazon . 
 The most recent reprint of this card in GLD3 names it  Amazoness Archer ; formerly its official name was  Amazon Archer . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Αμαζόνα Τοξότρια ) translates to " Amazon Archer ". 
 Its first Spanish printed name was " Amazoness Arquera ". Its GLD3 reprint name is " Arquera Amazoness ".
