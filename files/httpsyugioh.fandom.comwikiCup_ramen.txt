      Cup ramen.     
 Cup ramen  is a Japanese noodle meal sold in cups. It is particularly popular in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. [1]  Cup ramen was an important part of  Lazar ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas  and  Crow Hogan 's youths because of their underprivileged roots. [2] 
 Brands include:
 
 Oshiruko Noodles [1] 
 Red Demon's Noodle [1] 
 Kiiroi Itachi [2]
