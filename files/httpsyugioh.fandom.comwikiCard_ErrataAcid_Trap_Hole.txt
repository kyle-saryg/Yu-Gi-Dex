 English 
 
 
 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 Flip  1 face-down Defense Position monster  face-up .  If  the  monster's  DEF is 2000 points or less, the monster  is destroyed . If the DEF is more than 2000 points, return the monster to  its  face-down Defense Position. 
 Select  1 face-down Defense Position monster .   Flip  it face-up , and   if  the DEF  of the monster  is 2000  points  or less,  destroy   the monster . If the DEF is more than 2000  points ,  return  the monster to face-down Defense Position. 
 
 
       DDS-005 Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories promotional cards     
       SOI-ENSE1 Shadow of Infinity: Special Edition     
 
 
 Second erratum 
 Third erratum 
 
 
 Select  1 face-down Defense Position monster  and   flip  it face-up .   If   the  monster's  DEF is 2000 or less , destroy  it .   If   the  monster's  DEF is more than 2000 ,  flip  the monster to face-down Defense Position . 
 Target  1 face-down Defense Position monster  on the field;  flip it face-up , then destroy it   if   its  DEF is 2000 or less , or return it face-down   if   its  DEF is more than 2000. 
 
 
       SDMA-EN029 Structure Deck: Marik     
         LCYW-EN283 Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World Mega Pack
