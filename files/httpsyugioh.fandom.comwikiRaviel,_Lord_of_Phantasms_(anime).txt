 [  Divine-Beast  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except by  Tributing  3  monsters . When your opponent  Summons  a monster(s), Special Summon 1 " Phantasm Token " ( Fiend - Type / DARK / Level  1/ ATK  1000/ DEF  1000) for each of those monsters. "Phantasm Tokens" cannot  declare  an  attack . You can Tribute 2 monsters to have this card gain ATK equal to the combined ATK of the Tributed monsters, until the  End Phase .
