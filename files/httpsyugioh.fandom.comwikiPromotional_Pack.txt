 Promotional Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Promotional Pack  is a Spanish  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game , containing cards from  Starter Deck: Yugi .
 Each pack contains two  TCG  cards and one informational card.
