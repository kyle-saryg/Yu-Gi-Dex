 Martial Arts Spirit  is a formerly  TCG-exclusive   series  of  Level  5  WIND   Warrior   Pendulum   Spirit monsters  with 2400  ATK  and 1000  DEF .

 Design 
 The members of this series are based off of a different form of  martial arts , with their names also referring to the highest rank of their depicted martial art.
 
 Playing style 
 The members of this series share the same Pendulum Effect:
 
 If a monster(s) is Pendulum Summoned: Return this card from the Pendulum Zone to the hand. 
 In addition to the typical Spirit monster effect, each member has an effect to send to the GY cards your opponent controls that are in the same column as the card(s) in your Pendulum Zones that activates when Normal Summoned.
