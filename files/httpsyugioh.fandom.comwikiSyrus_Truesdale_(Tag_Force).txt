 Syrus Truesdale , known as  Sho Marufuji  (丸藤翔,  Marufuji Shō ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Syrus Truesdale , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Syrus is a Tier 1 character in every GX-era  Tag Force  game.
 
 
 Tag Force 
 If Syrus is not the player's partner for the Tag Force tournament, he will partner with his brother  Zane  for Part 2. During Part 3, he is brainwashed by the  Shadow Riders  and is  Abidos the Third 's partner.
 
 
 Tag Force 2 
 Syrus is dueled alongside  Tyranno  in the first event for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Syrus has three stories in this game: one for each dorm. However, the stories for Slifer Red and Ra Yellow are completely identical.
