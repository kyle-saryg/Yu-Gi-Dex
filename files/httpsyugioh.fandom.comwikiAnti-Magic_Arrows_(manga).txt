Main card page: " Anti-Magic Arrows " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Anti "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「アンチ」 .   As its English name contains "Anti", it  may  be treated a member of the "Anti" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
