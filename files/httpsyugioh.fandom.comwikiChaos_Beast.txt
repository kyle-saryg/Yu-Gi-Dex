 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1  LIGHT   Tuner  + 1+ non-Tuner  DARK   monsters Gains 1000  ATK  during the  turn  a card(s) is  banished . You can only use each of the following  effects  of "Chaos Beast"  once per turn . You can  target  1 of your banished LIGHT or DARK monsters;  add  it to your  hand . If this is in your  GY , except the turn it was  sent  there: You can banish 1 LIGHT and 1 DARK monster from your hand and/or GY, except this card;  Special Summon  this card.
