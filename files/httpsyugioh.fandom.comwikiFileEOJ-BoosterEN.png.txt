 Enemy of Justice  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the eighth Booster Pack in the  OCG   Series 4 .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Alexis Rhodes ,  Amnael ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Jaden Yuki , and  Zane Truesdale . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Batteryman " and " Elemental HERO " archetypes, as well as the  Cyber Girl  and  Konami Arcade Games  series. 
 Introduces the " Destiny HERO " archetype, as well as the  Counter Fairy ,  Helios ,  Herald , and  Majestic Mech  series, to the OCG/TCG. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Harpie " archetype to the OCG/TCG.
