 " The Cards with Teeth " is the second volume of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Summary 
 Seto Kaiba , the world  Duel Monsters  champion discovers  Yugi 's  grandfather  has a " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " card, and will stop at nothing to get it.
 Jonouchi  rejoins  Hirutani's gang  and ignores his friends. Yugi is suspicious of this choice, so he  Anzu  and  Honda  investigate.
 Shadi , the keeper of the  Millennium Items  sees Yugi as his rival. He puts him to the test to see if he is the true "King of Games".
