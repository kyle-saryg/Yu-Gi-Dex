 Fox Fire  is a character version of the card, " Fox Fire ".
 Fox Fire appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 .
 
 Deck 
 In World Championship 2007 and 2008, Fox Fire uses a  FIRE  Deck titled "Unforgiving Flame" or "Fiery Chariot", which uses many  Pyro  Monsters that inflict  Effect Damage  to the opponent.
