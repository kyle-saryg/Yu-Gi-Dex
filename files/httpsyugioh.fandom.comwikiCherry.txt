 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2-3 
 In Tag Force 2-3, she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 In Tag Force 4-6, she is a student at  Duel Academy  in  New Domino City .
 
 Tag Force 4 
       Cherry in  Tag Force 4 .      In her own story, she competes with the Player against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the  Player  will receive a photo of himself, Cherry,  Rayna ,  Hana ,  Reyna ,  Odelia ,  Yuma ,  Tasha ,  Nadia  and  Maggie  called "City Student".
 
 
 Tag Force 5 
 In  Leo 's second story event, Cherry and Duel Academy  Akiza  Tag Duel against the player and Leo.
 
 Tag Force 6 
 In Duel Academy Akiza's third story event, Cherry and  Tasha  Tag Duel against the player and Akiza.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Cherry uses Decks based around the " Pyramid of Light " monster series, which feature a number of  EARTH  and  Beast -type monsters as backup and support.
 In later installments, Cherry also uses Decks with a large number of high-Level monsters that can summoned easily through " Mausoleum of the Emperor " and other card effects.
