 Limited Edition: Kaiba Pack  is one of the three packs that make up the first  Limited Edition  set. It was released alongside  Limited Edition: Yugi Pack  and  Limited Edition: Joey Pack , all three of which were available by mail order through the  Weekly Shonen Jump  magazine.
 The set features  Seto Kaiba  on the packet and contains three  Ultra Secret Rare   Normal Monsters .
