 [Requirement] : If you  control  a  face-up   monster  ( Warrior ). [Effect] :  Send  the top card of your  Deck  to the  GY . If a monster was  sent  to the GY by this  effect , choose 1 face-up monster (Warrior) you control. It gains  ATK  equal to (the  Level  of the monster sent to the GY by this effect) x 300, until the end of this  turn , and you take that much damage.
