 " Cubic Seed " is an  archetype  used by  Aigami  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions . It is a sub-archetype of the " Cubic " archetype. Its only member is " Vijam the Cubic Seed ", which is used as the foundation to bring out the formidable "Cubic" monsters.
