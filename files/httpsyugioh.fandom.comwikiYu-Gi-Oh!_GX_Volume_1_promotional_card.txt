 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  Volume 1 promotional card  is a " Light and Darkness Dragon ", included in  volume 1  of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga . Despite this volume's official English release date of November 6, 2007, copies were available for sale as early as October 29 of the same year. [1]
