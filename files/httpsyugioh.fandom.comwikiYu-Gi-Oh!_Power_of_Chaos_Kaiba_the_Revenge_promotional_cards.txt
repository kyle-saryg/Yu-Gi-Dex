 Yu-Gi-Oh! Power of Chaos: Kaiba the Revenge 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Power of Chaos: Kaiba the Revenge  promotional cards  are three promotional cards, " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ", " Aqua Chorus " and " Seal of the Ancients ", that came with the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Power of Chaos: Kaiba the Revenge .
