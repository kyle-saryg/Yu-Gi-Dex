 Members 
 
 
 
 
 Attribute Summoner  is a  series  of six  Level  4  Effect Monsters  with 1400  ATK  who, when they're  destroyed by battle  and  sent  to the  Graveyard , allow the player to  Special Summon  a monster with 1500 or less ATK and the same  Attribute  as themselves, including a copy of themselves, from the  Main Deck .

 TIPS :
 Since there is no Level restriction, "Mystic Tomato" can Summon very powerful  DARK  monsters like " Yubel " or " Earthbound Immortal Wiraqocha Rasca " (though it will only have 100 ATK). You can also Summon " The Dark - Hex-Sealed Fusion " to use as a  Fusion Material .
 Since both  WIND  and  FIRE  monsters have Level 4  Tuner monsters  with less than 1500 ATK (" Top Runner " and " Flamvell Magician "), both "Flying Kamakiri #1" and "UFO Turtle" are good to build a Synchro Deck around.
 "Shining Angel" can Summon " Honest ", which can return itself to your hand. Special Summoning " Silent Swordsman LV3 " and  Leveling Up  to " Silent Swordsman LV5 " on the next Standby Phase would easily net a 2300 ATK monster that's unaffected by the opponent's  Spell Cards .
 "Mother Grizzly" is good in " Gishki ", " Cloudian " and " Ice Barrier " Decks.
 "Giant Rat" can Summon " Ancient Gear Engineer " or " Enraged Muka Muka ", which are Level 5 monsters, and " Castle Gate ", a Level 6 monster, meaning it could be useful for  Xyz Summons . It can also summon " Superheavy Samurai Big Benkei ", whose effects would switch it to defense immediately and give you a 3500 beater.
