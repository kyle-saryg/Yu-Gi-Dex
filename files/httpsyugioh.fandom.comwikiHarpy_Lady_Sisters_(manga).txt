 Harpy Lady Sisters 
 
 Japanese:   ハーピィ・レディ三姉妹 
 Romaji:   Hāpyi Redi Sanshimai 
 Translated:  Three Harpie Lady Sisters 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ATK  /  DEF 
 
 
 2700 / ???
 
 
 
 
 This is not a physical card, but a set of " Harpy Lady 1 ", " Harpy Lady 2 " and " Harpy Lady 3 " created with " Kaleidoscope ". They normally function as three separate  monsters , but can  attack  together with 2700  ATK .
