 Double Coston  is a character version of the card, " Double Coston ". 
 Double Coston appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . It appears as a Level Four Limited Duelist and it challenges the player to only included  Zombie - Type  monsters in the Deck. By beating it, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Five Limited Duelists and a new Booster Pack. It also appears as a downloadable ghost.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2007 , Double Coston play a Zombie Deck titled, "I Don't Die" and a  Wicked God  Deck titled, "Tri-Dark".
