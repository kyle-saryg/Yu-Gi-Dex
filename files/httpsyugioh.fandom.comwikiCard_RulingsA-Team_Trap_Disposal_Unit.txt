 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " A-Team: Trap Disposal Unit " is a  Quick Effect  that can be activated during either player's turn. (It does not  target . Tributing " A-Team: Trap Disposal Unit " is a cost to activate this effect.) [1] 
 The effect of " A-Team: Trap Disposal Unit " can be activated during the  Damage Step . [1] 
 This effect must be chained to the activation of an opponent's  Trap Card . (It cannot be chained to the activation of the effect of a Trap Card.) [1] 
 Previously Official Rulings 
   These TCG rulings were issued by  Upper Deck Entertainment  and have since been deemed unofficial by Konami. [2]  They were previously considered official and can still be correct, as long as they follow the official gameplay rules, or unless Konami has issued a ruling that says otherwise.
