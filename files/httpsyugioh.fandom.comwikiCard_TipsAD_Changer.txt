 This card can be  searched  by  " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Downbeat ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Freed the Matchless General ", " Field-Commander Rahz ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Mimiclay ", " Where Arf Thou? ", " One for One ", " Wolf in Sheep's Clothing ", " Reinforcement of the Army ", " Reinforce Truth ", " Shining Angel ", " Little Trooper ", " Road Warrior ", " Red Sparrow Summoner ", " Dragodies, the Empowered Warrior ", " Chaos Zone ", " Photon Veil " and " Exchange of Night and Day ". 
 This card can be used to Summon " Rocket Hermos Cannon " with " The Claw of Hermos ", then its effect can be used to change a monster to Defense Position, preferably one with low DEF, so that the equipped monster can inflict piercing battle damage. 
 This card is a key card in  Empty Jar  Decks, in order to activate the  Flip effect  of " Morphing Jar ". 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Summon Sorceress " and " Last Will ".
