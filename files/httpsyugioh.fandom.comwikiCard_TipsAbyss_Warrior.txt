 This card can be  searched  by " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Summoner Monk ", " Resonator Engine ", " Soul Rope ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Underworld Egg Clutch " and " Shark Cruiser ". 
 This card can be used to discard monsters like " Friller Rabca ", " Graydle Slime ", " Citadel Whale ", " Atlantean " or " Fishborg " monsters, triggering or setting up their effects. 
 You can put on top of your Deck the same  WATER  monster you discarded, in order to set up cards that involve either  excavating  (like " Monster Gate "), drawing and revealing a card (like " Gishki Zielgigas ") or simply declaring a card name (like " Reversal Quiz "). 
 You can use this card to cause a  dead draw  for your opponent's next turn.
