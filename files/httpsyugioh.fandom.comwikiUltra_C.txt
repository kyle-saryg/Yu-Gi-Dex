 Condition 
 When your opponent  Xyz Summons  a " Baby Tiragon ":  Special Summon  1  Xyz Monster  from your opponent's  Extra Deck  to your side of the  field , and if you do,  equip  it with this card. (This Special Summon is treated as an Xyz Summon.) When this card  leaves the field ,  control  of that monster switches to your opponent. When control of that monster switches,  destroy  this card.
