 Duelist Edition Volume 4 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duelist Edition Volume 4  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in the  Duelist Edition  series.

 Features 
 A variety of cards have been specially selected, all of which have been renewed with a new card and text format. 
 Reprint cards from Booster Packs series:  Ancient Prophecy ,  Stardust Overdrive ,  Absolute Powerforce , and  The Shining Darkness . 
 Includes one new card, " Steam Synchron ".
