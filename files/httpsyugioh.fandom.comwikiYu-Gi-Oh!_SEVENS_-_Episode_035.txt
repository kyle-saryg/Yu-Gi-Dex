 " Sound! Gohanium " is the thirty-fifth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on January 30, 2021.
 While  Yuga  is gone, a giant machine with caterpillar track closes in on his Road Laboratory. The one who is driving it is  Caterpillio Zomyoji , the mechanic of the Machine Cavalry Duel Club! Caterpillio uses his demolition techniques to get closer to the laboratory. However, his outrageous actions shall not go unpunished! For the beautiful girl with a strong sense of justice,  Haruka Kamijo , is here!
 
 Featured Duel: Haruka "Tiger" Kamijo vs. Caterpillio Zomyoji 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
