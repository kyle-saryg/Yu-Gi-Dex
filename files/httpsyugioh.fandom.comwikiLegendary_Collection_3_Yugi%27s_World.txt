 Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  collector's set, and the third in the  Legendary Collection  series. The set contains a collapsible hard-backed double-sided game board, seven promotional cards, including the first legal release of " The Seal of Orichalcos ", and five  Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World Mega Packs .

 Trivia 
 The  1st Edition  printing of this set features a transparent window on the top of the box where the actual " The Seal of Orichalcos " card is placed. The  Unlimited Edition  printing instead only prints an  image  of the card on the box, and the actual card is located along with the other six promotional cards inside, likely to save on production costs.
