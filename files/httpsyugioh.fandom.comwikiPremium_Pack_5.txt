 Premium Pack 5  is the fifth Premium Pack in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  (OCG), following  Premium Pack 4 . This set went on sale on December 22, 2001.
 Of the cards in  Premium Pack 5 , all have been released in the TCG. " Kunai with Chain " was the last to be released in the TCG, appearing as a  Super Rare  in " The Duelist Genesis ".
 " Red-Eyes Black Dragon " ( P5-01 ), " Harpie's Feather Duster " ( P5-02 ) and " Crush Card Virus " ( P5-03 ) were released in the July 2001 issue of  V Jump  magazine as preview of the Premium Pack. The three cards were distributed in a number of 1000 sets (3 in 1) with a raffle.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 6  Ultra Rare  cards, with  Ultra Parallel Rares  randomly inserted in every 2 to 4 packs.
 The entire set includes 9 cards, 18 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
