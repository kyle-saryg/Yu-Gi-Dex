 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Cannot be  destroyed by battle  or by an opponent's  card effect , also you take no  battle damage  from  attacks  involving this card. At the end of the  Battle Phase , if this card attacked or was attacked: Inflict 4000  damage  to 1 opponent player. -I  Summon  " Sandaion, the Timelord "!-
