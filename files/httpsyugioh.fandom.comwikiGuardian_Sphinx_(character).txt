 Guardian Sphinx  is a character version of the card, " Guardian Sphinx ". He appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 , as a Theme Duel Duelist. In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , he appears as the guardian of the pyramid in World 2 Sunlight.And in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , as a Duel opponent.
 
 Deck 
 Guardian Sphinx uses a Deck titled "Everlasting Kingdom" and "Go Home Already!".
