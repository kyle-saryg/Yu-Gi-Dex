 Yuma Tsukumo  ( 九十九   遊 馬 ,  Tsukumo Yūma ), is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Yuma Tsukumo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
