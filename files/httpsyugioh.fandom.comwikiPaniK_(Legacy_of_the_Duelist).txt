 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 PaniK , known as  Player Killer of Darkness  (「闇」のプレイヤーキラー,  Yami no Pureiyā Kirā ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  PaniK , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
