 Structure Deck: Fury from the Deep  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fourth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twelfth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Blaze of Destruction . In the  TCG , it is the fourth deck.
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Structure Deck: Fury of the Deep  was first released on April 29, 2006 with  Structure Deck: Blaze of Destruction  as  Structure Deck: Special Set 2 , before being released on May 10, 2006.
 
 It's a struggle of the  elements  with the two new  Structure Decks . Control the waves and crush your opponents with "Fury from the Deep" or leave them standing in a cloud of smoke with the  FIRE  monsters of " Blaze of Destruction ". The  Structure Decks  are ready to play with out of the box, so you can master their new strategies. Each  Deck  has 40 cards with 1 brand new  Ultra Rare   Monster Card , rulebook, and Dueling guide which assists a Duelist in taking his game to the next level. 
 —  Upper Deck Entertainment

 The deck features  WATER  monsters. Featured cards include " Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus ", " Gravity Bind ", and " Tribe-Infecting Virus ".
