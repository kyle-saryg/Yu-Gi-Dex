 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival 
 
 
 
 Alito  (アリト,  Arito ), known as  Alit  in the Italian version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival . This is a video game depiction of  Alito , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
