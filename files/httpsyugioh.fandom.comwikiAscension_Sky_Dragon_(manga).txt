Main card page: " Ascension Sky Dragon " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Celestial "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 天 輪 」 .   As its English name contains "Celestial", it  may  be treated a member of the "Celestial" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
