 The  desert girl  is an NPC girl in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters . She appears in  Episode 5 .
 
 History 
 She appears in the midst of  Mystical Sand 's sandstorm as part of a village, alongside the silent village elder and other NPCs. She reveals that  Alexander the Great  passed the five trials a long time ago, gives Tea a pendant that turns out to unlock the final challenge, and gives the group a clue on how to defeat Mystical Sand. At the end of the episode, it's revealed she and all the other villagers were statues and came to life to help the players win.
