 Duelist Pack: Duelists of Whirlwind  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugo ,  Lulu Obsidian  and  Sherry LeBlanc . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " roid ", " Speedroid ", " Clear Wing ", " Synchro Dragon ", " Synchron " and " Lyrilusc " archetypes, as well as the  Fleur  and  Synchro  series. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 46 cards:
 
 1  Holographic Rare  ( Extra Secret Rare  in Korean set) 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 7  Super Rares 
 9  Rares 
 24  Commons 
 The Ultra Rare and Super Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rare .
