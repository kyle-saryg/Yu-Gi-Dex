 The  striped sweater Duelist  was an unnamed character in the manga and second series anime.
 
       The striped sweater Duelist in the manga.     
 He was a participant in the  Duelist Kingdom  tournament, but lost to  Mai Valentine  at an early stage, as she defeated him using her "aroma tactics". After Mai takes away his  Star Chips , he shouts out that she'd been using some sort of psychic powers, mocked by Mai he went off weeping.
