 Names 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (نداء الأمازون) literally  Call of the Amazon  referring to the  Amazon Rainforest  or the  Amazon River . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (نداء الأمازونيّة) translates to  Call of the Amazon  referring to the  Amazons  of  Greek mythology . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Κάλεσμα Αμαζόνας ) translates to " Amazon's Call ". 
 This card was named " Llamada de las Amazonas " (" Amazons' Call ") in the Spanish dub and " Llamada de la Amazona " (" Amazon's Call ") in the American Spanish one.
