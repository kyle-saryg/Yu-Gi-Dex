 [  Machine  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This  monster  can only be  Special Summoned  by  selecting  1  face-up  " Gradius " on your side of the  field . The  ATK  and  DEF  of this card becomes the same as the  original ATK and DEF  of the selected "Gradius". This card also gains the  effects  of any  Equip Cards  equipped to "Gradius". If the selected "Gradius" is  removed from the field ,  destroy  this card.
