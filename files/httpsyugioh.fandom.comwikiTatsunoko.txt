   English 
 
 
1  Tuner  + 1 or more non-Tuner  monsters If this  Synchro Summoned  monster would be used as a  Synchro Material , 1 monster in your  hand  can be used as 1 of the other Synchro Materials.  Unaffected  by other monsters'  effects .
