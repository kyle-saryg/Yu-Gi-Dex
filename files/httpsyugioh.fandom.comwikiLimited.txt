 In the  OCG  and the  TCG   Advanced  and  Traditional Formats ,  Limited  (制限  Seigen ) cards are cards that players are allowed only one copy of in their  Main Deck ,  Side Deck , and  Extra Deck  combined.
 These cards are Limited as of October 1, 2019 for the  OCG , and April 14, 2021 for the  TCG  Advanced Format.

 Anime and manga 
 During the  Battle City  tournament, it is stated that players can only have one copy of " Monster Reborn " in their Decks. This influences  Yami Yugi and Yami Marik's Duel , in which Yami Marik is forced to recycle his only copy of "Monster Reborn" in order to Special Summon " The Winged Dragon of Ra " from the Graveyard.
