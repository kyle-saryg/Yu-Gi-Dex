 Vellian Crowler , known as  Cronos de Medici  (クロノス・デ・メディチ  Kuronosu de Medichi ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Vellian Crowler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
