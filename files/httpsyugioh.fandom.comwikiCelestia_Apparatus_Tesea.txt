 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  control  " Adventurer Token ": You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . You can  reveal  1 card that mentions "Adventurer Token" in your  hand ;  add  1 non- Normal Spell  that mentions "Adventurer Token" from your  Deck  to your hand. You cannot Special Summon  monsters  the  turn  you  activate  this  effect , except "Adventurer Token" and monsters that mention it. You can only use each effect of "Celestia Apparatus Tesea"  once per turn .
