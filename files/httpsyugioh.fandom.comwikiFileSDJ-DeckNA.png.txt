 Starter Deck: Joey  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Starter Deck: Pegasus , they are the third and fourth deck in the  TCG ' s Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck: Yugi  and  Starter Deck: Kaiba . It is also released in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in Korean.
