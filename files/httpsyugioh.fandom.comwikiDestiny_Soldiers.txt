 Destiny Soldiers  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by Aster Phoenix ( GX anime  and  ARC-V anime  versions) and  Sylvio Sawatari . 
 Includes members/support for the " Destiny HERO " archetype. 
 Introduces the " Abyss Actor " and " Abyss Script " archetypes to the  TCG . 
 Introduces the first support for the " Darklord " archetype to the  TCG . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack, 4 Super Rares and 1 Secret Rare. The set contains 60 cards, including:
