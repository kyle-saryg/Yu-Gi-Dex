 Kalin Kessler , known as  Kyosuke Kiryu  (鬼柳京介, Kiryū Kyōsuke) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Kalin Kessler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
