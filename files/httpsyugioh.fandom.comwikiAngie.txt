 Angie  is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  video games. She is always found in the Underground Duel Arena, with a different Tag Partner - and boyfriend - in each game.

 Appearances 
 Stardust Accelerator 
 The player can find Angie partnering with  Graton . They can accept Angie as a Tag Partner after defeating her 10 times.
 
 Reverse of Arcadia 
 The player can accept Angie as a Tag Partner after defeating her 5 times. Her partner in this game is  Morrison .
 
 Over the Nexus 
 The player can accept Angie as a Tag Partner after beating her and  Nelson  in Tag Duels 3 times.
 Angie is involved in a quest in this game. If the player partners with Angie, after leaving  Bootleg , they are stopped by Graton and Morrison, her former boyfriends, who force the player and Angie into a Tag Duel. After beating them, Angie tells them that the player is only her current Tag Partner and that her boyfriend is waiting at the Underground Duel Arena. Graton and Morrison decide to find and beat him, and Angie goes after them, worried for Nelson's safety.
 The player and Angie find that Nelson is safe and sound, having beaten Graton and Morrison while getting them to promise not to stalk Angie anymore. Nelson notes that the player protected Angie earlier, and rewards them with  Hardened Armed Dragon  as a token of gratitude.
 Graton and Morrison do not appear in-game until this quest is carried out.
