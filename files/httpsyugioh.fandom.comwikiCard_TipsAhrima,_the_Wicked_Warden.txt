 Names 
 
 This card can be searched with " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Dogu ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Chaos Zone ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Transmodify ", " Downbeat " and " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ". 
 Use this card's second effect with " Lair of Darkness " itself to Tribute your opponent's monster and enable a search. 
 If you decide to search a " Darkest Diabolos, Lord of the Lair " with this card's effect, you can immediately summon it from your hand.
