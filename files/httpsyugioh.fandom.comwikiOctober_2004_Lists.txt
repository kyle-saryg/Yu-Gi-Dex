 The  October 2004 Forbidden and Limited Lists  were Forbidden and Limited lists in the  TCG , going into effect on October 1,  2004 . [1] 
 This list updated the previous  August 2004 list  which had announced in advance that 13 cards were going to change status from  Limited  to  Forbidden , starting October 1. But in addition to that, once October 1 came by, another 5 cards where declared Limited as well. This list was followed by the  April 2005 Forbidden and Limited List.
