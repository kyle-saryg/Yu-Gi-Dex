 Structure Deck: Masters of the Spiritual Arts Spirit Art Awakening Pack  is an  Enhancement Pack  released with the  Structure Deck: Masters of the Spiritual Arts .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck: Masters of the Spiritual Arts Spirit Art Awakening Pack  contains 5 cards: 4  Super Rares  and 1  Secret Rare . 
 All 5 cards are available as both Super Rare and Secret Rare.
