 Missy , known in Japan as  Ran Kochou  (胡蝶蘭  Kochō Ran ), is an  Obelisk Blue  student infatuated with  Zane Truesdale  to the point where she wears a locket with his picture in it. In the beginning of the second season, Missy Duels Zane's younger brother,  Syrus  in hopes of pursuing her love interest in the  Pro League , and if Syrus won the Duel, which he does, he gets promoted to  Ra Yellow . She lost to Syrus' " Super Vehicroid Jumbo Drill ", thereby missing out on the chance to go pro much to her sadness.

 Voice/Mannierisms 
 She tends to call people "hon" when she Duels.
 
 Etymology 
 "Ran Kochou" is a pun on the flower  Phalaenopsis aphrodite , which is called  Kochōran  in Japanese.
 
 Deck 
 Missy plays an  Insect  Deck , primarily centered around Summoning her " Insect Princess " and increasing its strength by forcing  control  of her weaker Insect- Type   monsters  over to her opponent's side of the  field  through the  Field Spell  " Insect Garden ", then forcing them to  attack  using " Insect Pheromone ". Aside from her anime-only cards, all of her cards seen in her Duel against Syrus are from  Invasion of Chaos .
