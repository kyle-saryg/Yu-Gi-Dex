For the  Structure Deck , see  Structure Deck: Curse of Darkness .      
 The  Croatian  and  Greek                       names given are not official.  
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Darkness "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ダークネス」 .   As its English name contains "Darkness", it  may  be treated a member of the "Darkness" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Each time a  Spell Card  is  activated , immediately after it resolves, the  player  that activated it takes 1000  damage .

 À chaque fois qu'une Carte Magie est activée, le contrôleur de cette Carte Magie reçoit 1000 points de dommages.

 Immer wenn eine Zauberkarte aktiviert wird, erhält der kontrollierende Spieler dieser Zauberkarte 1000 Punkte Direktschaden.
