 Scotch  un-named within the anime, is a resident of  Satisfaction Town  and previously a member of the  Radley Bunch .
 
 Biography 
 Scotch was originally a member of the  Radley Bunch , who attended the duels against the  Malcolm Crew . He along with  Radley  were very impressed with the dueling skills of  Kalin Kessler  and continously boasted to the rival gang when Kalin continously defeated their members. When  Lawton  took the town over with  Barbara , Scotch began to doubt his belief in the duels they carried out there as the two began to take it more seriously and made members of the same group duel each other for entertainment as the  Dyne Mine  continously needed new miners due to the old ones dying out. Barbara set Scotch and another member of their group to paint the town sign to  Lawton Town  but as they were doing it, they overheard  Kalin Kessler 's melody through his harmonica and  Yusei Fudo 's  Duel Runners  engine roaring. He cheered the two on when they were dueling Lawton for the towns freedom and helped re-build the town when Lawton set off the explosives he stored around. He is currently living there under no ownership of a duel gang and no worries of being sent off to the Dyne Mine.
