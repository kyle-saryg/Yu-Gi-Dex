 Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle  ( 遊☆戯☆王 モンスターカプセル ブリード＆バトル  Yūgiō Monsutā Kapuseru Burīdo ando Batoru ), is the first  Yu-Gi-Oh!  video game, and was released for the PlayStation by  Konami  on July 23,  1998 . It features a more well-developed variant of the  Capsule Monster Chess  game from the  manga .
 Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle  is the first video game to feature  Capsule Monster Chess , with  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule GB  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum  also featuring the game.

 Plot 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle  primarily features characters from the  Toei anime . The player raises and trains Capsule Monsters to defeat opponents in a tournament. Opponents include  Yugi Mutou ,  Katsuya Jonouchi ,  Anzu Mazaki ,  Mokuba Kaiba ,  Ryo Bakura ,  Sugoroku Mutou ,  Seto Kaiba  and  Dark Yugi .
