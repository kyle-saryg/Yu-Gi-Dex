 The  2012 Spanish National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Spain. It took place in the Hotel Princess in Madrid on the weekend, June 16–17, and was won by Antonio Ripoll Castell. The tournament was attended by 119 people, 20 of whom got to qualify for the World Championship Qualifier continentals that year. [2] 
 The entry fee was €12 for the main event and entry for the  Dragon Duel  was free. [1]
