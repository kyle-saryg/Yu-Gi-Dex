 Memories Of a Friend
 
 
 Memories Of a Friend  is an  event  in Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links. When in effect,  Yami Yugi  and  Joey Wheeler (DSOD)  appear as  temporary opponents at the Gate, who can be Dueled in order to claim multiple rewards, including the ability to unlock  Joey Wheeler (DSOD)  as a playable character.
