 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you have a  face-up  card in your  Spell & Trap Zone , you can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . You can only Special Summon "Adularia of the Moonlit Water"  once per turn  this way. Gains 600  ATK / DEF  for each face-up  Spell / Trap  on the  field . You can  send  2 face-up cards from your Spell & Trap Zone to the  GY ; send 1  Level  4 or lower monster from your  Deck  to the GY. You can only use this  effect  of "Adularia of the Moonlit Water" once per turn.
