 Strike of Neos: Special Edition  is a  Special Edition  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).
 Two different variants were available: a blister pack variant and a box variant.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Strike of Neos: Special Edition  blister pack contains:
 
 2  Strike of Neos   Booster Packs 
 1   Enemy of Justice  Booster Pack 
 2  Super Rare  cards (" Cyber End Dragon " and " Shrink ") 
 Each  Strike of Neos: Special Edition  box contains:
 
 2  Strike of Neos   Booster Packs 
 1   Enemy of Justice  Booster Pack 
 1 of 2  Super Rare  cards (" Cyber End Dragon " or " Shrink ")
