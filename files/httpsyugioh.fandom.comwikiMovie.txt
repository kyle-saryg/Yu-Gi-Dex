Movie  may refer to any of the following:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! the Movie , a movie released in 1999, based on the  Toei Animation anime . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light , a movie released in Japan in 2005 and internationally in 2004, based on the  NAS anime . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , a movie released in Japan in 2010 and internationally in 2011, celebrating the 10th anniversary of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions , a movie released in Japan in 2016 and internationally in 2017, with the story set after the events of the  original manga .
