 Character design 
 The Dark Scorpion Burglars's outfits consist of the standard  Dark Scorpion . Each Dark Scorpion Burglars member have a difference in the outfit.
 When the Dark Scorpions were brought to life by the power of  Don Zaloog 's eyepatch  Shadow Charm , they went undercover as civilians. Zaloog was disguised as a police detective,  Chick  as a  Slifer Red  student,  Cliff  as a security guard,  Gorg  as a janitor, and  Meanae  as one of  Duel Academy 's school nurses.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , they are brought to life by the power of the  Shadow Charm . However, since they fail to obtain the keys in a Duel, they are unable to unlock the spirit gate of the  Sacred Beasts . When Zaloog and his gang are discovered, he and  Chazz Princeton  duel with all the remaining keys on the line. In the end, he and his cohorts are defeated, and the power of his Shadow Charm is nullified. After reverting back to their spirit forms, the Dark Scorpions join the many weak monsters in Chazz's care, to the young duelist's annoyance. He never uses them however, and they haven't been seen since.

 Non-canon appearances 
       In Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007     
 Dark Scorpion Burglars appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . In  World Championship 2006 , they appears as a Limited Duelist and they challenges the player to only include one copy of each card in the Deck. In  World Championship 2007 , they appears as a Level Two Limited Duelist and they challenges the player to do the same challenge in 2006. By beating them, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Three Limited Duelists and a new Booster Pack.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2006 , Dark Scorpion Burglars play a  Chaos  Deck titled, "Single Deck". In  World Championship 2007 , they play a  Mill  Deck titled, "Only One".
