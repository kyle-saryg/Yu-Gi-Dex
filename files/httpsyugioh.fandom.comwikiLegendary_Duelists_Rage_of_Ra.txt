 Features 
 Includes support for the Deck themes of  Yami Marik ,  Primo ,  Jakob ,  Lester ,  Aporia ,  Espa Roba ,  Makoto Inotsume ,  Franz  and  Ren . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Meklord ", " Slime ", and " Jinzo " archetypes as well as the  The Winged Dragon of Ra  series.
