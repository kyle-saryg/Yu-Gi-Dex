 This is the first  Scale  9  Pendulum Monster  that is not a " Qli " monster. 
 This monster is wearing a hat resembling the hair of " Abyss Actor - Superstar ". 
 This monster's name may refer to the popular English lullaby  Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star . 
 This monster appears in the artwork of " Set Rotation ", " Abyss Playhouse - Fantastic Theater ", " Abyss Actors' Curtain Call ", and " Stand In ".
