 Ryko, Lightsworn Hunter  is a character version of the card " Ryko, Lightsworn Hunter ". 
 He appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P041. He also appears as an opponent at Stage 10-3 under Quest mode.

 Decks 
 A  Deck  centered around LIGHT  monsters  with the "Lightsworn"  archetype . Use the  effect  of each "Lightsworn" monsters to  send  cards to the  Graveyard  and then  Special Summon  " Judgment Dragon "!
