 Biography 
 When  Yuma ,  Bronk  and  Tori  break in to  Heartland  to find Yuma's Deck, they sounded an alarm which made this robot appear and challenge them to a  Dance Dance Revolution -like game using  Xyz Monsters . Bronk said that Yuma should go ahead to find  Hart  and although this robot tried to stop him, apparently Yuma still got away. [1] 
 
 Abilities 
 Its arm can expand and can become a hook to catch intruders getting away.
 
 Deck 
 The Dueling robot uses a  " Scrap " Deck .
