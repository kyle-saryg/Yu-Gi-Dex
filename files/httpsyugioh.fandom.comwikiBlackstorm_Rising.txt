 Blackstorm Rising  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on November 12, 2018.
 
 Description 
 Get ready to be blown away! Rise,  Black-Winged Dragon ! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Black-Winged Dragon ",  Crow 's ace monster, "Black-Winged Dragon" is here! When you would take Effect Damage, you can place a Black Feather Counter on this monster instead. Removing all Black Feather Counters from this card will reduce the ATK of an opponent's monster and inflict Effect Damage! 
" Breaker the Magical Warrior ", "Breaker the Magical Warrior" which was used by  Yami Yugi  is here! When successfully Summoned, this monster gains 1 Spell Counter which increases its ATK. It can also destroy 1 Spell/Trap Card on the field by removing 1 Spell Counter. 
" Dragunity Knight - Vajrayana ", This  Synchro Monster  can equip 1 Level 3 or lower Dragon-Type Dragunity monster in your Graveyard when it is successfully Synchro Summoned! In addition, you can send 1 equipped Equip Card to the Graveyard to double its ATK until the end of the turn. By equipping " Dragunity Phalanx ", you can even Synchro Summon a Level 8 Synchro Monster! 
" Cards of Consonance ", A  Spell Card  that lets you draw 2 cards by discarding one Dragon-Type Tuner monster with 1000 ATK or less! This BOX contains monsters that can be used as this card's cost, so put them in your deck together! 
 
A total of 90 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 42  N .
 
 
 Each box of  Blackstorm Rising  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Bujingi Raven ", " Bujingi Swallow ", " Mist Valley Windmaster ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", and " Shadow Impulse ".
