 Property 
 
 
 Equip [note 1] 
 
 
 
 Activate  when you have taken a  Direct Attack . Increases  ATK  by the same amount of  damage  you've taken. At each turn's  End Phase , this amount is decreased by half. 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile>  Activate  only during your opponent's  Battle Phase , and only if an opponent's  monster  has inflicted  Battle Damage  to your  Life Points  from a  Direct Attack  in this  turn . The  equipped  monster gains  ATK  equal to the total Battle Damage that has been inflicted to your Life Points in this turn from Direct Attacks. At each turn's  End Phase , this amount is decreased by half.
