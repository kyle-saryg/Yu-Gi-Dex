 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (الراميات الأمازونيّات) literally  Amazonian Archers  or  Archers of the Amazon . It was also once mistakenly referred to as  Amazon Archer . 
 The given unofficial Arabic name (الأمازونيّات الراميات) translates to  Archer Amazons . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Αμαζόνες Τοξότριες ) translates to " Amazon Archers ".
