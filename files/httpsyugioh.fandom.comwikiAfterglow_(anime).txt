 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Remove from play  all "Afterglow" in your  Deck . Then,  shuffle  this card into the Deck. During your next  Draw Phase , if you  draw  this card that was  added  to your Deck by its own  effect , you can inflict 4000  damage  to your opponent's  Life Points .
