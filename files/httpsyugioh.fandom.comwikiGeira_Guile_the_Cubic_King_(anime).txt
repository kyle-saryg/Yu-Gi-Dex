 [  Fairy  /  Effect  ] Special Summon  this card by stacking it on top of 1 " Cubic Seed "  monster  you  control . When this card is Special Summoned, inflict 800  damage  to your opponent. This card gains 800  original ATK  for each monster stacked underneath it. After this card  battled ,  return  this card to the  Deck  to  add  1 " Vulcan Dragni the Cubic King " from your Deck to your  hand . During this time, Special Summon the "Cubic Seed" monsters stacked underneath this card to your  field . [1] [2]
