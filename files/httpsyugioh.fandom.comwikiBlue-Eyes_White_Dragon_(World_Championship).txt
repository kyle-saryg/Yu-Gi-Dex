 Blue-Eyes White Dragon  ( 青眼の白龍   Burūaizu Howaito Doragon ) is a character version of the card, " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ", appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  video games.

 Appearances 
       Blue-Eyes White Dragon in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 , Blue-Eyes White Dragon is a Limited Duel Duelist.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , Blue-Eyes White Dragon can be unlocked as an opponent, after  the  player  Summons " Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon " in a Duel.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , Blue-Eyes White Dragon can be unlocked as a Single Duel opponent by defeating  Darklord Desire  3 times. Blue-Eyes White Dragon and  Red Eyes B. Dragon  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents by defeating Tag Team  Love Reptiles?  3 times. Their Tag Team name is  Legend's Anniversary .
