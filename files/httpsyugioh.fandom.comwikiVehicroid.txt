 " Vehicroid " (ビークロイド  Bīkuroido ) is an  archetype  of  EARTH   Machine   Fusion Monsters  used by  Syrus Truesdale  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . They are a sub-archetype of the " roid " archetype. In the anime, the "roid" archetype is referred to as "Vehicroids". With the exception of " Super Vehicroid - Mobile Base ", all members of this archetype list " Drillroid " as one of their  Fusion Materials .
