 Urara Takano , ( 高乃麗 ,  Takano Urara ), born  Hisako Takayama  ( 高山 ひさ子,  Takayama Hisako  is a Japanese voice actress.
 She voices Insector Haga ( Weevil Underwood ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ). [2]
