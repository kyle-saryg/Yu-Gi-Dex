 Pierre The Gambler , known as  Mitsuo  in the japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Pierre the Gambler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 He is referred to simply as "The Gambler", with his in-game profiles being the only place where his real name, Pierre, appears.
 
 
 Tag Force 
 Pierre appears as an opponent during the Tag Force tournament, teaming with  Blair Flannigan . After completing the game, he appears during Part 1 at the pier, and can become the player's partner if they win at a minigame of guessing in which hand he hides a coin.
 
 
 Tag Force 2 
 Pierre duels against the player in the third story event for every Tier 1 character.
 
 Decks 
 Pierre uses  Gambling Decks .
