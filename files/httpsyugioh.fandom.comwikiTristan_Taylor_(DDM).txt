 Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters 
 
 
 
 Tristan Taylor , known as  Hiroto Honda  in the Japanese version, is a character in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters . He is based on  Hiroto Honda  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Description 
 His macho playing style emphasizes vigorous power and zesty spirit. He will take on all challengers fairly, without trickery.
