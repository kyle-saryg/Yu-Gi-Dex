 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Blu-ray & DVD promotional card 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Blu-ray & DVD  promotional card  is a card that is included in the Blu-ray and DVD of the film  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions . In Germany this card was only given out in Theathers and not in DVDs or Blu-rays.
