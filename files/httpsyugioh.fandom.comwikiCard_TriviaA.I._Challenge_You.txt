 Ai , a silhouette of  Playmaker  and the sword of " Dark Templar @Ignister " appear in this card's artwork. 
 This card references the moment when Ai declared an attack with "Dark Templar" in  " Journey's End " , though it depicts Ai in his Ignis form rather than humanoid appearance.
