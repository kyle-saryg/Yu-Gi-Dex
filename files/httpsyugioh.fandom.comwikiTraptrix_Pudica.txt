 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Unaffected  by the  effects  of " Hole "  Normal Traps . You can only use each of the following effects of "Traptrix Pudica"  once per turn . When this card is  Normal Summoned : You can  add  1 " Traptrix Orchard " from your  Deck  to your  hand . If this card is  Special Summoned : You can  target  1 opponent's Special Summoned  monster ;  banish  it, and if you do, during the next  Standby Phase , your opponent can Special Summon 1 of their banished monsters.
