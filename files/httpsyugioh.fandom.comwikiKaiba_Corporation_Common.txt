 Kaiba Kōporeishon Nōmaru
 
 
 Kaiba Corporation Common  ( 海馬 コーポレイションノーマル  Kaiba Kōporeishon Nōmaru ), is an  OCG -exclusive rarity introduced in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Duel Set  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Theater distribution card 1 . 
 These cards are just like  Commons , but have a hi-tech pattern and the "KC"  KaibaCorp  logo coating, with similar holo coloring as the  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rare  or  Millennium Rare  cards.
