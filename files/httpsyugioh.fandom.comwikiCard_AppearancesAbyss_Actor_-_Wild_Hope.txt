 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 101 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  plays this card during his duel against  Kite Tenjo . He Normal Summons this card on the first turn. On Kite's turn, one of his " Cipher Wing ", with its ATK doubled by " Cipher Interference ", attacks and destroys this card, triggering its effect which allows Sylvio to add " Abyss Actor - Funky Comedian " from his deck to his hand. On Sylvio's next turn, he sets the  Pendulum Scales  at 1 and 8 before  Pendulum Summoning  this card from his Extra Deck as well as " Abyss Actor - Superstar " from his hand. He then uses "Funky Comedian's"  Pendulum Effect  to return this card to his Extra Deck and allow "Superstar" to gain 1600 ATK.  
 In  episode 139 , Sylvio uses this card during the  Lancers' duel against   Z-ARC . On his turn after he joins the duel, Sylvio sets the Pendulum Scales at 1 and 8, then Pendulum Summons this card, " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " and "Abyss Actor - Superstar" from his hand. He tributes this card to use the effect of " Abyss Script - Abysstainment ", to Set " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King " from his Graveyard.  
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 In  chapter 9 ,  Shingo Sawatari  uses this card during his third duel against  Yuya Sakaki . He activates " Abyss Actors Back Stage " to add this card and " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " from his Deck to his Extra Deck face-up. On Shingo's next turn, with the Pendulum Scales set at 2 and 8, he Pendulum Summons this card and "Leading Lady" before Tributing both of them to  Tribute Summon  " Abyss Actor - Evil Heel ".
