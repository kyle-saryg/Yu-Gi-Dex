 Cassandra Lee Morris , often credited as  Cassandra Lee  and  Meghan McCracken , is a voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Morris' first role in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  franchise was the voice of  Alice . Her most notable role was  Yubel .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Morris voiced  Leo  and  Luna  for the first two seasons of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  before moving to Los Angeles, where she currently resides. These roles were succeeded by  Eileen Stevens .
