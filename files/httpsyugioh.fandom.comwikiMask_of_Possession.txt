 Mask of Possession 
 
 Japanese:   遺言の仮面 
 Romaji:   Yuigon no Kamen 
 Translated:  Mask of Testament 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This is not a physical card, but part of the effect of " Masked Beast of Guardius ". When "Masked Beast of Guardius" is  destroyed , "Mask of Possession" could be attached to a  monster , allowing the  controller  of "Mask of Possession" to control that monster.
