 The  shopping mall mother  (ショッピングモールの 母 親   Shoppingu mōru no hahaoya ) [1]  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions .
 
 Biography 
 The mother brought her daughter to the  Domino City shopping mall . They went to  Otogi's cafe , where  Katsuya Jonouchi  gave her daughter a balloon and she purchased ice cream. During a fight between Jonouchi and  Hiroto Honda , the daughter lost her balloon. [2]
