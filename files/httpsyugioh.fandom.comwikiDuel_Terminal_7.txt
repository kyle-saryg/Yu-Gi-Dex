 Duel Terminal 7 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duel Terminal 7  was released in two parts:  Duel Terminal 7a  is the first part, being made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Sacred Star Knights!!  and was released September 28, 2012;  Duel Terminal 7b  is the second part, being made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Ouroboros, Wicked Dragon of Destruction!!  and was released February 1, 2013.

 Features 
 The set includes 100 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. It also includes further support for known archetypes: " Gem-Knight ", " Laval ", " Gusto ", and " Gishki ". Introduces the " Constellar " and " lswarm " archetypes. Introduces  Aporia ,  Slifer Red Blair Flannigan  and  Kite Tenjo  as duelable opponents.
 Includes further support for the " Gem-Knight ", " Gishki ", " Constellar " and " lswarm "  archetypes . Introduces  Bronk Stone  and  Nelson Andrews  as duelable opponents. Includes cards used by  Bronk Stone  and  Nelson Andrews  in the  anime .
