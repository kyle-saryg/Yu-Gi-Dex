 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Target  1 of your  banished   Cyberse   monsters ;  Special Summon  it. During your  Main Phase , if you have 2000  LP  or less; You can  banish  this card from your  GY , then target 2 of your banished Cyberse monsters;  add  them to your  hand . You can only use each  effect  of "Cynet Rollback"  once per turn .
