        Arena icon     
 The  Arena  is a place to challenge other  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM  players up to 5 times a day for free to improve their score with each victory.
 Winning will increase the player's consecutive wins meter, rewarding them with a score in the order of +50, +100, +200, +400 and +800 per win and they receive   6.
 Losing will decrease the consecutive wins meter one stage, increase the player's score by 10 points and they receive   2.
 An option to reset the daily duels selection can be purchased with   5000, and an additional  2500 for each reset in any given day. This will not affect the consecutive wins meter for that day. Consecutively winning after purchasing will result in +800 per duel.
 The player's consecutive wins meter resets at the beginning of each day, along with the cost to reset duels, which reverts to   5000.
 The weekly leaderboard and scores are reset to 0 once a week on Monday at 7:00 a.m.  UTC .
