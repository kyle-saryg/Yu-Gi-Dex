 " Warrior " (ウォリアー  Woriā ) is an  archetype  of primarily  Warrior   monsters , which are used by  Yusei Fudo  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. All of their Japanese names are comprised of English words transliterated into Japanese followed by 「ウォリアー」 ("Warrior").
 In the anime, Yusei uses "Warrior"  Synchro Monsters  that require a corresponding " Synchron "  Tuner  to Summon (the first word of the Tuner's name is the same as the first word of the Synchro Monster's name). In the manga, he mostly uses  EARTH  "Warrior" Synchro Monsters that don't require a corresponding "Synchron" monster.
 The non-Synchro "Warriors" work in conjunction with the "Synchrons" to easily Synchro Summon Synchro Monsters, including the "Warrior" Synchro Monsters. For example, " Level Warrior " can change its  Level  to make a  Synchro Summon  easier.
 Each "Warrior" that requires a "Synchron" has the same  Attribute  as its counterpart, but follows its own distinctive color scheme. However, " Quickdraw Synchron " can be used in place of any "Synchron" to summon any "Warrior" that requires a "Synchron" Tuner. There is currently no  WATER  "Warrior" Synchro Monster that follows this pattern with " Steam Synchron ".
 Due to their Japanese names, a number of older cards, such as " Kunoichi " and " Ryu Senshi ", also fall into this archetype. Also, several older cards, such as " Aztekipede, the Worm Warrior " and " Beaver Warrior ", include "Warrior" in their English names but are not part of this archetype. However, because  TCG  and  OCG  support for this archetype only works on  Synchro Monsters , neither have been an issue.
