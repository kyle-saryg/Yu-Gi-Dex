 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2+  Cyberse   monsters  with different  Attributes Gains 500  ATK  for each monster it  points to . ( Quick Effect ): You can pay 1000  LP ;  draw  1 card, then if your LP are 2000 or less, you can apply this  effect . ●  Banish  this card from the  field , and if you do,  Special Summon  1  Link -3 or lower Cyberse monster from your  Extra Deck , except "Decode Talker Heatsoul". You can only use this effect of "Decode Talker Heatsoul"  once per turn .
