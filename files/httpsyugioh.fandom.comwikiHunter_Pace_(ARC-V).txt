 Hunter Pace , known as  Mukuro Enjo  ( 炎 城  ムクロ  Enjō Mukuro ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. He is an alternate universe version of the  Hunter Pace  that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.

 Biography 
 Friendship Cup 
 Hunter Pace was involved in a  Turbo Duel  against  Shay Obsidian  in the  Underground Dueling Arena . He was defeated by " Raidraptor - Rise Falcon ". [1] 
 
 Friendship Cup Finals 
 Hunter was among the  Commons  in the  Underground Labor Facility  when  Shinji Weber  declared their revolution. [2]
