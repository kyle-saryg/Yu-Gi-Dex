 Rie Tanaka  is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She provides the voice of  Vivian Wong  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ). [ citation needed ] 
 
 Trivia 
 From 2012 to 2018, she was married to fellow voice actor  Kōichi Yamadera .
