 OCG Rulings 
 If you activate the card "Amorphage Lysis" during the Damage Step, it can be activated from the start of the Damage Step until before damage calculation. [1] 
 The effect " All monsters on the field, except "Amorphage" monsters, lose 100 ATK and DEF for each "Amorphage" card on the field. " does not make a  Chain Link . (It applies to not only monsters you control other than "Amorphage" monsters, but also monsters your opponent controls other than "Amorphage" monsters.) [1] 
 Each face-up monster in the Monster Zone other than "Amorphage" monsters has its ATK and DEF decreased by 100 for each "Amorphage" card on the field, including "Amorphage Lysis". [1] 
 If the number of "Amorphage" cards on the field changes, the amount ATK and DEF are decreased by is also changed. [1] 
 The effect " If a card(s) in your Pendulum Zone(s) is destroyed (except during the Damage Step): You can place 1 "Amorphage" Pendulum Monster from your Deck in your Pendulum Zone. " makes a  Chain Link . (It does not  target . It cannot be activated during the  Damage Step .) [1] 
 Except during the  Damage Step , the card and the effect " You can place 1 "Amorphage" Pendulum Monster from your Deck in your Pendulum Zone. " can be activated in the same  Chain Link . [1]
