 " Buster Blader " (バスター・ブレイダー  Basutā Bureidā ) is an  archetype  of  Warrior   monsters  used by  Yugi Muto  to counter the " Blue-Eyes " cards used by  Seto Kaiba . All of its members are  Level  7  EARTH  monsters, except for the Level 8  LIGHT  " Buster Blader, the Dragon Destroyer Swordsman ". The archetype is related to the " Destruction Sword " archetype.
