 Yami Yugi , known as  Dark Yugi  ( 闇 遊戯 ,  Yami Yugi ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Yami Yugi , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
