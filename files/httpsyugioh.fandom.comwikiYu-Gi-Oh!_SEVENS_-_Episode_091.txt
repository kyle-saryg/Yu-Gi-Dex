 " Fierce Battle! Rush Robot Duel! " is the ninety-first episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on March 20, 2022.
 Borrowing the power of the Six  Goha Siblings ,  Yuga  and his friends activate the  Rush Duel Robot  and face off against the Otes Robot in a final trial in order to save the Duel Program! And thus, a large-scale, intense Rush Robot Duel began between two giant robots! In space!
