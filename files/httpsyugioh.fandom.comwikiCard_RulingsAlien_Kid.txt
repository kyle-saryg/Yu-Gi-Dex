 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Alien Kid " which places  A-Counters  on Special Summoned monsters does not start a  Chain Link . [1] 
 If " Alien Kid " is face-up on the field when multiple monsters are Special Summoned simultaneously, then each Special Summoned monster gains one  A-Counter . [2]
