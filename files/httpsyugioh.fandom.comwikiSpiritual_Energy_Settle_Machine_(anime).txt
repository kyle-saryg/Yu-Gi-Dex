Main card page: " Spiritual Energy Settle Machine " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Spirit "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 精霊 」 .   As its English name contains "Spirit", it  may  be treated a member of the "Spirit" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 As long as this card remains  face-up  on the  field , all  Spirit monsters  remain face-up on the field and do not  return  to their  owners '  hand  during the  End Phase  even if they are  Normal Summoned  or  flipped  face-up.  Discard  1 card from your hand to the  Graveyard  during each of your End Phases. If you do not,  destroy  this card.
