 Tadashi Miyazawa  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices Sugoroku "Grandpa" Muto ( Solomon "Grandpa" Muto ) and Simon ( Shimon ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) [2]  and continues to voice Grandpa in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ) [3]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters: Pyramid of Light  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light ).
