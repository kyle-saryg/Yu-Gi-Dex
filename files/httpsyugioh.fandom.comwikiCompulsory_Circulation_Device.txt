 Quick-Play 
 
 
 
 During the  Battle Phase :  Detach  any number of  Xyz Materials   attached  to an  Xyz Monster (s) you  control ;  banish  1  monster  your opponent controls for each Xyz Material detached. Monsters banished by this  effect   return  to the  field  at the end of the Battle Phase.
