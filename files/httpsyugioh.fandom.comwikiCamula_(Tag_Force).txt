 Camula  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  series. This is a video game depiction of  Camula , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Camula appears in Part 3 as the last  Shadow Rider , teaming up with a brainwashed  Zane Truesdale . If Zane is the player's partner, Camula does not appear and they face  Kagemaru  immediatly after defeating  Amnael .
 After beating her in Part 3, Camula will appear in Part 1 after 10:00 P.M. in the Obelisk Blue Girls' dorm. Defeating her 10 times will earn the player her  Deck Recipe  and a " Vampire Genesis " card.
 
 Tag Force 3 
 If the player is partnering with a Tier 2 or Tier 3 character, during their third story event, Camula and  Jesse Anderson  Tag Duel against the player and their partner. If the player is teaming up with Jesse at the time, Camula teams up with  Atticus Rhodes  instead. If Camula is the player's partner, they duel against Jesse and Atticus.
 
 Decks 
 Camula uses  Zombie  Decks  featuring a number of " Vampire " monsters and vampire-themed cards such as " Overpowering Eye ". Referencing her bios, Camula also includes " Illusion Gate " in some of her Decks even when the card is  Forbidden  in most in-game banlists.
