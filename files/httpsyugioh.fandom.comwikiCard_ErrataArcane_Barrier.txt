 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 Each time a Spellcaster-Type monster(s) on the field is destroyed, place 1 Spell Counter on this card (max. 4). You can send this card and 1 face-up Spellcaster-Type monster you control to the Graveyard  to  draw 1 card for each Spell Counter on this card. 
 Each time a Spellcaster-Type monster(s) on the field is destroyed  face-up , place 1 Spell Counter on this card (max. 4). You can send this card and 1 face-up Spellcaster-Type monster you control to the Graveyard ;  draw 1 card for each Spell Counter  that was  on this card.
