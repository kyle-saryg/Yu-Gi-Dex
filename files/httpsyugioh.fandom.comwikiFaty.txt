 Faty , known as  Toshihisa Furusawa  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Ra Yellow  student.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Faty uses a  Winged Beast  Deck  composed of  Normal Monsters  that are also  Fusion Material  for  Non-Effect   Fusion Monsters .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Faty uses two kinds of  Fusion  Decks . The first one uses a large amount of  Fusion Substitutes  with Normal Monsters, while his other Decks aim to summon " Dark Flare Knight " and, in turn, " Mirage Knight ".
