 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  Ritual Summon  this card with " Underworld Ritual of Prediction ". Must first be Ritual Summoned and cannot be Special Summoned by other ways except by " Prediction Princess Tarotrei ". FLIP : You can Special Summon 1  Flip monster  from your  Deck  in  face-down   Defense Position . ( Quick Effect ): You can  activate  1 of these  effects ; ● Change any number of face-down monsters you  control  to  face-up  Defense Position. ● Change any number of face-up monsters you control to face-down Defense Position. You can only use each effect of "Nether Prediction Princess Tarotreith"  once per turn .
