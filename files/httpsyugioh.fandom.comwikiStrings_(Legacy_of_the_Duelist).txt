 Strings , known as  Pantomimer  (パントマイマー,  Pantomaimā ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Strings , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
