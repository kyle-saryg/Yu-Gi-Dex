 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 Richard Slysheen of York  is the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  incarnation of  Heishin  and portrayal of  Richard III of England . He is a member of the White Roses.
 Players can obtain the card " Warrior Elimination ", using the  find  ability on B4, while playing against Richard Slysheen.
 
 Deck 
 Richard Slysheen plays a  Warrior/Beast-Warrior Deck . His Deck Leader " Battle Steer " has the abilities to reduce the Level Cost for Summoning same  Type  monsters, extend support
range and give movement boosts to same  Type  monsters. True to his status as King, he keeps his deck leader safely away from attacks and sends minions out to fight.
