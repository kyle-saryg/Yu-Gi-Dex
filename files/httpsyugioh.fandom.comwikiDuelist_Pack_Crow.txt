 Duelist Pack: Crow  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the eleventh set in the  OCG ' s Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Yusei 3 .  In the  TCG , it is the thirteenth set. It is the last Duelist Pack for the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime series.
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Duelist Pack: Crow  was also released as part of  Duelist Pack: Crow Special Edition .

 Features 
 The set features a lot of the latest cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime used by  Crow Hogan , as well as containing  support  for the " Blackwing "  archetype . It contains 5 new cards along with 25 reprints.
 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Crow  contains 5 cards and each box contains 15 packs. In the  TCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Crow  contains 5 cards and each box contains 36 packs. There are 30 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
