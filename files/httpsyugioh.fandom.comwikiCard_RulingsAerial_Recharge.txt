 The effect of this card can be activated in the same chain as the activation of this card. [1] 
 This card cannot be activated during the  Damage Step . [1] 
 Sending this card to the  Graveyard  or Tributing 1 " Mecha Phantom Beast " monster during the  End Phase  does not start a chain. [1] 
 You can  Tribute  1  face-down  " Mecha Phantom Beast " monster. [1]
