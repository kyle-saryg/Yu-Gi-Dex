 Necromancy 
 
 Japanese:   ネクロマンシー 
 Romaji:   Nekuromanshī 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Select  up to 4  random   monsters  from your opponent's  Graveyard , and  Special Summon  them in  face-up   Defense Position  on your opponent's side of the  field . If a monster(s) Special Summoned by this  effect  is  destroyed , all  face-up  monsters your opponent  controls  lose 600  ATK  for each of these monsters until the  End Phase .
