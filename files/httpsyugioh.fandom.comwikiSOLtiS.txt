 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  episode 103: " Reflections " 
 
 
 
 SOLtiS  are upcoming androids by  SOL Technologies  to help assist humans worldwide. However, before they were released to the public, two of them were reprogrammed by  Ai  and  Roboppi  to serve as avatars for them in the real world. This came in handy for tracking down and incapacitating members of SOL Technologies like  Queen  and  Akira Zaizen .
