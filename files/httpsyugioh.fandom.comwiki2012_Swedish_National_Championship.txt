 The  2012 Swedish National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Sweden. It took place in Stockholm, Sweden on May 26, 2012 [1]  and was won by Teodor Hurtigh Isaacs. [2]
