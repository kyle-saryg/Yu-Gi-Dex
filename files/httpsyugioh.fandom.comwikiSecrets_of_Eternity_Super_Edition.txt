 Secrets of Eternity Super Edition 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Secrets of Eternity Super Edition  is a  Super Edition , containing 3  Secrets of Eternity   Booster Packs  and 1  Super Rare  of 14 promotional cards.

 Breakdown 
 Each  Secrets of Eternity Super Edition  is comprised of:
 
 3  Secrets of Eternity  Booster Packs ( Unlimited Edition ) 
 1 of 14  Super Rare  cards 
 Each European  Secrets of Eternity Super Edition  is comprised of:
 
 1 Booster Pack with 28 cards equivalent to 3  Secrets of Eternity  Booster Packs + 1 Super Rare card
