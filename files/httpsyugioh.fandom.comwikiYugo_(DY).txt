 Yugo  is a character featured the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Yugo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Yugo is a  Synchro  Master from the Accel Seminary.
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Yugo runs a  " Speedroid " Deck .
