 Harrington Rosewood , known in Japan as  Mitsuru Ayanokouji  ( 綾小路  ミツル,  Ayanokōji Mitsuru ), is an  Obelisk Blue  student, who is head of  Duel Academy 's tennis team as well as the heir to Ayanokōji Motors. He fell in love with  Alexis Rhodes  at first sight and desires to date her, much to the jealousy of  Jasmine  and  Mindy . After seeing Alexis being friendly with  Jaden Yuki , he grows jealous and duels Jaden over who becomes Alexis' fiancee. He loses the Duel. In the dub he vows to always love Alexis while watching the sunset. In the original Japanese, he yells that he hates Dueling. He graduated with  Zane Truesdale .
 
 Deck 
 Harrington uses a  Tennis  Deck , which largely includes "Ace" cards that inflict  effect damage  to his opponents, and "Ball"  monsters  like " Mystical Shine Ball " and " Mega Thunderball ". The amounts of effect damage he inflicts, combined with the 4000-LP duel format in the anime, greatly resembles the tennis scoring system, and allows him to defeat an opponent in as few as three moves. His signature card, " Deuce ", causes the Duel to resemble a tennis match by restricting the amount of times an opponent can  attack , and giving the victory to the first Duelist to inflict damage twice.
