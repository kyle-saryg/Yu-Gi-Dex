 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Select  1  face-up   monster  you  control  and 1 face-up monster your opponent controls on the  field . The  ATK  of your monster becomes equal to the  original ATK  of selected monster; this change lasts until the  End Phase . If your selected monster  destroys  an opponent's monster  by battle  and  sends  it to the  Graveyard , inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the ATK of the destroyed monster.
