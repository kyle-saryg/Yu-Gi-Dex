 Crimson Kingdom  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on June 12, 2017, 9:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Kneel before the Dark Lord! He is the master of life and death! 
 
 Featuring: 
-" Vampire Lord ", who will be  Special Summoned  from the  Graveyard  during the  Standby Phase  if  destroyed  by a  card effect . 
-" Red-Eyes Wyvern ", a monster you can  banish  from your Graveyard to Special Summon a " Red-Eyes " monster from your Graveyard. 
-" Soul Resurrection ", which lets you Special Summon a  Normal Monster  from your Graveyard in  defense position . 
-" The Warrior Returning Alive ", which allows you to add a  Warrior - Type  monster to your  hand  from your Graveyard. 
-A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Crimson Kingdom  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
