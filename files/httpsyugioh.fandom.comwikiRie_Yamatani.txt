 Rie Yamatani , known as simply  Rie  in the English version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . She is an  Obelisk Blue  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 - 6 , her name is given to  Peta Kelvin .

 Appearances 
 Across her appearances in the games, Rie seems to be hyperactive.
 
 Tag Force 1 
 Rie partners with  Maki  for the Tag Force tournament.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 As part of an optional random event, Rie and Maki Tag Duel against the player and their partner.
