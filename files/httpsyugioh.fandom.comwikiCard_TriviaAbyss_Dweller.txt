 This is the first  TCG exclusive   Sea Serpent - Type   Xyz Monster , and the third  TCG  exclusive Sea Serpent-Type monster in general. 
 This card is a Xyz  counterpart  of " The Dragon Dwelling in the Deep ", having the same appearance and stats:  Level / Rank ,  Attribute ,  ATK , and  DEF .
 "The Dragon Dwelling in the Deep" was also a  TCG  exclusive monster. 
 This could be also be the  WATER  counterpart to " Dweller in the Depths "and " The Thing in the Crater ."
