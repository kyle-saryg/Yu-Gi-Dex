 20th Anniversary Pack 2nd Wave  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Include cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Rafael ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Mindy ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Crow Hogan ,  Greiger ,  Tetsu Trudge ,  Bruno ,  Sayer ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Vector  and  Dumon . 
 Include members/support for the " Artifact ", " Blackwing ", " Constellar ", " Dark Magician ", " Elemental HERO ", " Evolzar ", " Fire Formation ", " Guardian ", " HERO ", " Ice Barrier ", " Infernity ", " Inzektor ", " lswarm ", " Junk ", " Nekroz ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Qli ", " Shaddoll ", " Six Samurai ", " Skyblaster ", " Star Seraph ", " Stardust ", " Synchron ", " T.G. ", " Utopia " and " Wind-Up " archetypes, as well as the  Cosmic Synchro Monster ,  Yo-kai Girl ,  Goyo ,  Greed ,  Herald ,  Rescue Squad ,  signature move ,  Signer Dragon ,  Solemn ,  Vanity's  and  Zombie counterpart  series. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 51 cards:

 1  Extra Secret Parallel Rare  (Korean only) 
 3  Secret Parallel Rares 
 6  Ultra Parallel Rares 
 9  Super Parallel Rares 
 32  Normal Parallel Rares
