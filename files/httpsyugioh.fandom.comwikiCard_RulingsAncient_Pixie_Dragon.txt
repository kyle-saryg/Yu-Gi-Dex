 OCG Rulings 
 The " After  resolving  a  Field Spell Card  that was  activated  during your  turn :  Draw  1 card. " effect is a  Trigger Effect . [1] 
 This effect activates when a  Field Spell Card  resolves. It starts a  Chain Link . (This effect is not activated in a chain to the activation of a Field Spell Card.) [1] 
 This effect is not activated if the activation of a Field Spell Card is negated. [1] 
 The " Once per turn: You can  target  1  face-up   Attack Position  monster on the  field ;  destroy  that target. There must be a face-up Field Spell Card on the field to activate and to resolve this effect. " effect is an  Ignition Effect . (It targets 1 face-up Attack Position monster on the field.) [1]
