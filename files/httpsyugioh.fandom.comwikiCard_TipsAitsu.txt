 Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Sangan ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Summoner's Art ", " Dragoons of Draconia ", " UFO Turtle ", " Flamvell Firedog ", " Princess Cologne ", " Chaos-End Master ", and " Witch of the Black Forest ". 
 " Valhalla, Hall of the Fallen " can easily Special Summon this card from your hand. 
 Use " Cost Down ", " Star Blast " or " Mausoleum of the Emperor " to remove the Tribute requirement to Normal Summon this card.
 If you use "Cost Down", you can discard " Koitsu " and equip it to this card with " Roll Out! ".
