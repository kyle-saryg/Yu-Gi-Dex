 Structure Deck: HERO's Strike  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the twenty-seventh Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Blitzkrieg of the Mechlight Dragons . The  TCG  equivalent of this Deck is  HERO Strike Structure Deck .
