 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 J. Shadi Morton  is the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  incarnation of  Shadi  and a portrayal of  John Morton . He is a member of the Red Roses and located at  Paris . Reflecting his original incarnation's lack of emotion, Shadi barely comments on defeat or victory.
 
 Deck 
 J. Shadi Morton plays a  Millennium Deck . Among his strategies, he uses the Crush terrain to protect his monsters. It is best to pack as many Field Spell cards as possible in your strategies to make sure your Deck Leader is not at stake just to get a direct Attack on his "Millennium Golem".
