 Members Support 
 
 
 
 
 " Pendulum " (ペンデュラム  Pendyuramu ) is an  archetype  consisting of  Pendulum Monsters  and cards that support them. 
 This archetype contains two sub-archetypes, " Pendulum Dragon " and " Pendulumgraph ".
