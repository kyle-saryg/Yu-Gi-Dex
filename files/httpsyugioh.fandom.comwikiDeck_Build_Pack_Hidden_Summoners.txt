 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards. The set contains 45 cards.
 
 3  Ultra Rares  /  Secret Rares 
 12  Super Rares 
 30  Commons  /  Normal Parallel Rares 
 Features 
 Introduces the first support for the " Nephthys " archetype to the OCG. 
 Introduces the " Mayakashi " and " Prank-Kids " archetypes to the  OCG .
