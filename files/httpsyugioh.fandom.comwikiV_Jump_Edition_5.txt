 V Jump Edition 5  is the twenty-second installment of the newly renamed Limited Edition series, it could be pre-ordered with the July Issue of V Jump Magazine, on 2011. This pack contains monsters from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  and two new  Xyz Monsters . The previous set is  V Jump Edition 4 , and this is followed by  V Jump Edition 6 .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards: 5  Ultra Rares .
