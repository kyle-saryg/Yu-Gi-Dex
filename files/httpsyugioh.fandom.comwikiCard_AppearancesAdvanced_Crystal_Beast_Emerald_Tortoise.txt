 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  episode 147 ,  Jesse Anderson , while possessed by  Yubel , uses this card during his Duel against  Zane Truesdale . After " Cyber Twin Dragon " attacks and destroys " Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ", Jesse activates the second effect of " Advanced Dark " to nullify the Battle Damage he would take by sending this card from his Deck to the Graveyard. In  the next episode , " Cyber End Dragon " (whose ATK was 8000 due to " Power Bond ") attacks " Rainbow Dark Dragon ", but Jesse activates " Counter Gem " to take this card, " Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ", " Advanced Crystal Beast Amethyst Cat ", " Advanced Crystal Beast Ruby Carbuncle " and " Advanced Crystal Beast Topaz Tiger " from his Graveyard and place them in his Spell & Trap Card Zone as Continuous Spell Cards. Jesse then sends all Advanced Crystal Beasts in his Spell & Trap Card Zone to the Graveyard in order to increase the ATK of "Rainbow Dark Dragon" by 1000 for each monster sent to the Graveyard this way. With five sent this way, "Rainbow Dark Dragon" gains 5000 ATK. Zane then activates " Cybernetic Zone " to banish his "Cyber End Dragon" from the field until the End Phase. 
 In  episode 151 , Jesse, while possessed by Yubel, uses this card during his Duel against  Jaden Yuki . After " Elemental Hero Flame Wingman " attacks and destroys " Advanced Crystal Beast Cobalt Eagle ", Jesse activates the second effect of "Advanced Dark" to nullify the Battle Damage he would take by sending this card from his Deck to the Graveyard.
