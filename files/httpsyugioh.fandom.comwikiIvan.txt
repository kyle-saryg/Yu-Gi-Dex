 Ivan , known as  Yuta Nishino  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Slifer Red  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 - 6 , his name is given to  Mark Freedom .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , when the player receives his  Deck Recipe , they also receive the card, " Giganto ".
 Ivan usually hangs out with  Terence , and teams up with him for the Tag Force tournament.
