 Erica Schroeder , often credited as  Bella Hudson , is an American voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 
 Roles 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! , Schroeder's first major role in  Yu-Gi-Oh!  history was portraying  Mai Valentine  in season 4, replacing  Megan Hollingshead , [1]  who portrayed Mai in the first three seasons before she relocated to California. She also voiced  Mana [2]  and her  Monster Spirit , " Dark Magician Girl " [2]  (voicing the latter as early as  episode 103 ).
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , she voiced  Camula , [ citation needed ]   Sarina . [ citation needed ] ,  Fonda Fontaine  (before being replaced by  Veronica Taylor  in  episode 60 ) [ citation needed ]  and  Tania , after replacing  Kathleen Delaney  in  episode 148 . [ citation needed ]  She reprised her role as  Dark Magician Girl  in  episode 42 . Minor roles include the head of the disciplinary action squad in  episode 7 . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , Schroeder's most notable role was portraying  Akiza Izinski . [3]  She also provided the voices for  Rally Dawson , [2]   John [ citation needed ]  and  Zora . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , she reprised both  Akiza Izinski  and  Dark Magician Girl .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , she voiced  Cathy Katherine [4]  and  Mrs. Francis . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , she voices  Melissa Trail [5]  and  Ray Akaba  when possessing  Riley .
 
Schroeder reprises her role as  Mai Valentine  and  Akiza Izinski  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .
