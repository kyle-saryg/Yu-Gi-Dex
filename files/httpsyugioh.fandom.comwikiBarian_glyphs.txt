      Barian glyphs, describing a story from  Duel Terminal - Champion of Chaos!! .     
 Barian glyphs  are the writing system used by the  Barians  from the  Barian World  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
 In reality, the glyphs are a cipher of Japanese. Each glyph is a stylized version of a letter from the  Latin alphabet . A word written in Japanese phonology can be converted to Barian by converting the word to romaji and then replacing each letter with its corresponding Barian glyph. As the letters "c", "j", "l", "q", "v" and "x" are not used in standard  Japanese romanizations , they do not have Barian glyphs.

 The story from  Duel Terminal - Champion of Chaos!!  was written in the glyphs when they appeared on a slab and a screen in front of  Dr. Faker . [1]

 The glyphs appear when " Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon " uses its " Tachyon Transmigration "  effect . [2]
