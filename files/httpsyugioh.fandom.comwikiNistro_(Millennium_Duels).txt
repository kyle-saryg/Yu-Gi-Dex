 Nistro , known as  Gauche  (ゴーシュ  Gōshu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Nistro , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
