 When this card appeared in the  second series anime , it was completely redrawn, compared to its  first series anime  artwork. The second series anime artwork was also used as its  OCG / TCG  artwork. 
 This card's second series anime and  OCG / TCG  artwork is taken from  Yu-Gi-Oh!   Duel 10: " The Cards with Teeth (Part 2) " , specifically from the panel in which this monster is Summoned. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , this card was drawn with a different artwork.
