 Rex Raptor , known as  Dinosaur Ryuzaki  (ダイナソー竜崎,  Dainasō Ryūzaki ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Rex Raptor , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
