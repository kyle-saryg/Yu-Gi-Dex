 Starter Deck: Yugi  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Starter Deck: Kaiba , they are the first and second deck in the  TCG ' s Starter Deck series. It is also released in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in  Asian-English  and Korean.
 The set has been released in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ) in three different English prints—North American, European and Oceanic—as well as in European French, French-Canadian, German, Italian, Portuguese and Spanish. The European English, European French, German and Italian sets contain less cards than the other prints.

 Features 
 The deck contains cards selected from the Japanese  EX  and  EX-R  Starter Boxes.
 
 Breakdown 
 In the North American English, Oceanic English, French-Canadian, Portuguese, Spanish, Asian-English and Korean versions, each  Starter Deck: Yugi  contains 50 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 2  Super Rares 
 47  Commons 
 In the European English, French, German and Italian versions, each  Starter Deck: Yugi  contains 46 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 2  Super Rares 
 43  Commons
