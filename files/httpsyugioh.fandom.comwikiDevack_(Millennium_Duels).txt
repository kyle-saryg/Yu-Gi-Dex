 Devack , known as  Demak  (ディマク,  Dimaku ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Devack , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
