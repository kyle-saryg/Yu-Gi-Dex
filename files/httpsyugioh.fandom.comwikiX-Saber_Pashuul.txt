 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
This card cannot be  destroyed by battle . During each of your opponent's  Standby Phases : You take 1000  damage . You must  control  this card in  face-up   Defense Position  to  activate  and to  resolve  this  effect .
