 Blair Flannigan , known as  Rei Saotome  (早乙女レイ,  Saotome Rei ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Blair Flannigan , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Blair is a Tier 2 character. She can be found in the Slifer Red dorm disguised as a boy, either in the Cafeteria or in  Bawnji 's room, where she will mention  Zane Truesdale  and blush but not say much else.
 If the player shows Blair a barette, a bandanna and a hat obtained by having their relations with Zane,  Syrus Truesdale  and  Alexis Rhodes  reach at least 4 hearts, Blair will run off and disappear from the overworld for the most part. She can only be found during classtime, at the top-left of the Slifer Red dorm's empty area, and will be undisguised.
 Defeating Blair while undisguised at least 10 times will earn the player her second  Deck Recipe  and 3 copies of " Maiden in Love "
 Notably, the player cannot partner with Blair's disguised self for Part 2 and Part 3.
 
 
 Tag Force 2 
 Blair is a Tier 1 character in this game, being one of the possible partners for the player from the very beginning of the game.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Blair has four stories in this game: as a Slifer Red student, as an Obelisk Blue student, disguised as a boy, and as her younger self.

 Decks 
 Blair chiefly uses Decks based around her signature card " Maiden in Love ", alongside either " Cupid Kiss " to steal her opponent's monsters or with  Stall  cards for defensive purposes.
 In  Tag Force 3 , she uses  Lightsworn  Decks  as well.
 In  Tag Force  only, while disguised, Blair uses a Deck focused around the " Silent Magician " and " Silent Swordsman " " LV " series that also features a number of other silence-themed cards.
