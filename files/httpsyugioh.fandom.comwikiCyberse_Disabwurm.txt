 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1  Cyberse   Ritual ,  Fusion ,  Synchro ,  Xyz , or  Link Monster  + 1 Cyberse  monster During your  Main Phase : You can  Special Summon  1 Cyberse monster from your  hand , then you can make this card's  Level  become that monster's. When your opponent  activates  a  Spell / Trap Card  or  effect  and you  control  a  Link -4 or higher Cyberse monster ( Quick Effect ): You can  banish  this card from your  field  or  GY ;  negate  the activation. You can only use each effect of "Cyberse Disabwurm"  once per turn .
