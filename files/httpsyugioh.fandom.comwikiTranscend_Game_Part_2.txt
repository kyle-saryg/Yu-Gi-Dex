 Transcend Game Part 2
 
 
 Transcend Game Part 2 is an  event  in Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links. When in effect,  Prana  
 appears as a temporary opponent at the Gate, who can be Dueled in order
 to claim multiple rewards, including the ability to unlock  Sera  as a 
playable character.
