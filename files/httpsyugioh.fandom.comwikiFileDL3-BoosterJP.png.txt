 Duelist Legacy Volume.3  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in the  Duelist Legacy  series. It contains reprints of cards from  Thousand Eyes Bible ,  Spell of Mask  and  Labyrinth of Nightmare .
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack, and 30 packs per box.
