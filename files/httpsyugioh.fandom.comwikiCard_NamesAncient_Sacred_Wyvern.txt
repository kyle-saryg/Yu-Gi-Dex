 Names 
 
 In French, a misprinted version of this card names it as "Ancien Dragon Féerique" (Ancient Fairy Dragon) instead of "Ancienne Vouivre Sacrée". 
 The  Greek  name given translates to " Ancient Holy Serpent Dragon ". An alternate and far more accurate translation could be " Αρχαίος Ιερός Φτερωτός Δίποδος Δράκος με Διχαλωτή Ουρά " (Ancient Holy Winged Two-Legged Dragon with a forked tail).
