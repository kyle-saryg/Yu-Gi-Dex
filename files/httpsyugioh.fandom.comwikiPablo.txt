 Pablo , known as  Junjirou Takada  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is an  Obelisk Blue  student.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Pablo looks down on all  Slifer Red  students more than any of his fellow Obelisk Blue students in the games, calling the player a "lowly Slifer" no matter what the circumstance - even if the player has just defeated him or become his partner.
 
 Tag Force 1 
 Pablo partners with  Deron , a  Ra Yellow  student, for the Tag Force tournament.

 Decks 
 Pablo uses  Stall  Decks  with different goals in every game, but with one exception, all of his Decks share the trait of always using the maximum possible number of cards in the Main Deck - 80 in  Tag Force  and  Tag Force 2 , or 60 in  Tag Force 3 .
 In  Tag Force , his Deck uses a plethora of effects to gain  Life Points  and remove monsters from the field, be it by destroying them or by returning them to the hand.
 In  Tag Force 2 , Pablo's Decks load his Graveyard via overlapping  searcher  chains to power up " Chaos Necromancer " and activate " Blasting the Ruins ".
 In  Tag Force 3 , his first Deck is similar to the previous game's, but his second Deck aims to use either " Witch Doctor of Sparta " or " Junk Barrage " with his searchers to deal massive  effect damage  or outright  OTK  his opponent. Similarly, his third Deck is based on the  Colossal Fighter OTK , using "Junk Barrage" instead of " Armory Arm ". This Deck notably has only 40 cards in its Main Deck, instead of Pablo's usual maximum Main Deck size.
