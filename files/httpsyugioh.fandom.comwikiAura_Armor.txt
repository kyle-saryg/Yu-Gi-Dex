 Aura Armor 
 
 Japanese:   オーラアーマー

 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Activate  only when an opponent's  monster   declares  a  direct attack  while your  Life Points  are 2000 or less.  Negate  the  attack , and  Special Summon  1 " Player Token " ( Warrior - Type / EARTH / Level  4/ ATK  ?/ DEF  ?). When that "Player Token" is Special Summoned, halve your Life Points. The ATK and DEF of the "Player Token" are equal to your Life Points.
