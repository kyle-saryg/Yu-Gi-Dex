 This card's  Pendulum Effect  essentially sets up a  Pendulum Summon  of " Abyss Actor " monsters on the same turn. The number-one search target is " Abyss Actor - Twinkle Little Star ", which covers the range of Levels for all "Abyss Actor" monsters.
