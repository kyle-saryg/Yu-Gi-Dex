 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 In  episode 134 , this card is shown in the  Three Emperors of Yliaster 's visions of the future. It is shown as part of a  Synchro Summoning  example in a classroom, where " Marauding Captain " and " Plaguespreader Zombie " would have been tuned together to Summon it. It is also shown when a bunch of Turbo Duelists are riding together as well as on a hologram projection that was being shown. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 In  chapter 22 ,  Syndy Chross  uses this card during her duel against  Shark . She tunes " Junk Synchron " with " Doppelwarrior " to Synchro Summon this card. On Shark's turn, his " Number 73: Abyss Splash " attacks and destroys this card while avoiding its own destruction by this card's effect due to " Sea Lord's Amulet ".
