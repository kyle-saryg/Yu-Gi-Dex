 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  4  monsters You can  detach  2  materials  from this card, then  target  1  face-up   monster  your opponent  controls ; its  ATK  becomes half its current ATK, and if it does, this card gains that lost ATK.
