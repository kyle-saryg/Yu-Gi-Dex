 Hanto Goto  ( 後 藤 ハント  Gotō Hanto ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. He is a student at  Goha 2nd Elementary , and he is a member of the  Dueling & Dinosaur Research Club  under  Kan Hakubutsu .
