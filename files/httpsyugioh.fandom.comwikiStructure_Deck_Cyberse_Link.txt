 Structure Deck: Cyberse Link  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game [1]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game [2] . It is the thirty-fourth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Pendulum Evolution . It is the thirty-ninth Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Dinosmasher's Fury Structure Deck .

 Features 
 Contains cards used by  Yusaku Fujiki 
 The "Power Up with Packs" supplement on the back of the included play mat suggests combining this Deck with the following cards from these sets.
 " Trishula, Dragon of the Ice Barrier ", " The Phantom Knights of Break Sword ", and " PSY-Framelord Omega " from  Battles of Legend: Light's Revenge . 
 " Decode Talker ", " Link Spider ", and " RAM Clouder " from  Starter Deck: Link Strike 
 " Firewall Dragon ", " Heavy Storm Duster ", and " Salvagent Driver " from  Code of the Duelist 
 " Broken Line ", " Fuse Line ", and " Link Bumper " from  Circuit Break .
