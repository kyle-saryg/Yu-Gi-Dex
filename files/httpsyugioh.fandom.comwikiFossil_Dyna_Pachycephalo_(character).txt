 Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo  is a character version of the card " Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo ".
 It appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .

 Deck 
 
 Stardust Accelerator 
 Fossil Dyna Pachycephalo uses 2 Decks "Snapped Pachy" and "No Spcl. Summon" in Stardust Accelerator. 
 "Snapped Pachy" is its regular Deck, while "No Spcl. Summon" is used for Tag Duels.
