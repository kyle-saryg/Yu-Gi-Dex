 This card can be searched by “ Dogu ”, " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", “ Witch of the Black Forest ”, " Painful Escape ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Transmodify ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Resonator Engine ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Summoner Monk ", " Soul Rope ", " Tour Bus To Forbidden Realms ", " Red Nova " and " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ". 
 Use " Barrel Behind the Door " to inflict a total of 1000 damage to your opponent without taking any damage yourself, when this card is destroyed by battle. 
 Equip this card with " Black Pendant ", so that your opponent will take 1000 damage and you will take 500 when it's destroyed.
 Add " Backfire " to increase the damage your opponent takes to 1500. You can further add " Dark Room of Nightmare ", whose effect will trigger three times in this case, deal a total of 2400 damage to your opponent while taking only 500 yourself.
