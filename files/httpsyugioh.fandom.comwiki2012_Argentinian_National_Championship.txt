 The  2012 Argentinian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Argentina. It took place at Salon Basaldua in Buenos Aires, Argentina [1]  and was won by Santiago Vera. [2]
