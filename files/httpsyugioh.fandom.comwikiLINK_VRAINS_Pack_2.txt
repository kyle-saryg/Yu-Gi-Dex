 Features 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Alien ", " Black Luster Soldier ", “ D/D/D ”, “ Dark Contract ”, “ Darklord ”, " Dragunity ", " Fire Fist ", “ Gaia The Fierce Knight ”, " Harpie ", “ HERO ”, " Magical Musketeer ", " Nordic ", " Parshath ", “ PSY-Frame ”, “ Raidraptor ”, " Scrap ", " Six Samurai ", " Speedroid ", “ The Phantom Knights ”, “ Thunder Dragon ” and " Traptrix " archetypes, as well as the “ Railway ” series. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 100 cards:
 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 15  Super Rares 
 25  Rares 
 55  Commons 
 The Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rare .
