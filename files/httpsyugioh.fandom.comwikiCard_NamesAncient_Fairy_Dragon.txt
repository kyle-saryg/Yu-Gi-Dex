 Anime 
 
 In the American Spanish dub of the anime, this card is known as  "Antigua Hada Dragón" . Later, it was renamed as  "Dragón Hada Antigua"  (lit. "(Female) Ancient Fairy Dragon").

 تنين الجِنية القديمة
 
 The given approximated Arabic name (تِنِّين الجِنِّيَّة القَدِيمَة Tinniin Al-Jinniyya Al-Qadiima) translates to  Ancient Fairy Dragon .
