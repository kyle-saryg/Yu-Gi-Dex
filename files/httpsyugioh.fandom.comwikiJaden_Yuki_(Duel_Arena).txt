 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena 
 
 
 
 This is a video game depiction of  Jaden Yuki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime. He is available as a Legend Rare Partner, with a default of 6 Rental Points. His partner ID is P005.
 In the beta, he had a default of 4 Rental Points as a partner, and his partner ID was P003.

 Description 
 A young Duelist studying at " Duel Academy ". He belongs to " Slifer Red ," the lowest ranked of the dorms, but he is a top class Duelist. Has the mysterious power to see the  spirit of the cards .
 
 Decks 
 Partner 
 A  Fusion   Deck  created from " Elemental HERO " cards and " Neo-Spacian ". It isn't hard to perform  Fusion Summons , so you'll need a firm understanding of the  effects  of each  Fusion Monster  and the required  Fusion Materials  in order to  Summon  the required monster just when you need it!
