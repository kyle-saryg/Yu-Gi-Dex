 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 108 ,  Ai  uses this card during his  Speed Duel  against  The Gore . He activates this card to revive " Doshin @Ignister ". Ai then banishes this card from his Graveyard, and " Ignister A.I.Land " from his hand, to return " TA.I. Strike " in his Graveyard to his hand. 
 In  episode 109 , Ai uses this card during his  Master Duel  against  Pandor . He activates this card to revive " Fire Phoenix @Ignister ".
