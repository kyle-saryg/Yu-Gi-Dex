 Kakimoto  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Kakimoto , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Kakimoto is a student of  Leo Institute of Dueling  and is one of  Shingo 's friends.
