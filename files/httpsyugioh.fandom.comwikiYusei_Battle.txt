 " Yusei Battle " ( 遊星 バトル,  Yūsei Batoru ) is an instrumental musical composition from the Japanese  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 It is 4:07 long and has been released on  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Sound Duel Vol I .
 
 Appearances 
 "Yusei Battle" is played in the following episodes:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 021: " Second Round Showdown, Part 2 " , during  Yusei 's last turn 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 055: " A Score to Settle, Part 2 " , when  Yusei  Synchro Summoned  Junk Warrior  against  Kalin  and destroyed his  Hundred Eyes Dragon . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 101: " The Edge of Elimination, Part 2 " , when  Yusei  attacked  Breo  with  Junk Destroyer  and defeated him. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 102: " The Edge of Elimination, Part 3 " , after  Yusei  Fusion Summoned  Dragon Knight Draco-Equiste . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 108: " Primo's Plan, Part 3 " , when  Yusei  took over  Bruno  and  Primo's  Duel and Synchro Summoned  Junk Destroyer  against Primo. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 121: " The Miraculous Trump Card, Zushin the Sleeping Giant! " , when  Yusei Fudo  Synchro Summoned  Stardust Dragon  against  Taro Yamashita . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 128: " Yusei's Last Stand " , when  Yusei  surprised  Team Ragnarok  by Summoning  Majestic Star Dragon . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 145: " Faster than Light!! " , during  Yusei 's fourth turn in the episode. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 151: " Converging Wishes " , while Yusei explained the effect of " Converging Wishes " 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episode 153: " Clashing Souls! " , during Yusei's second turn. 
 It is also played in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time  during Yusei's first turn in the Duel against  Paradox .
