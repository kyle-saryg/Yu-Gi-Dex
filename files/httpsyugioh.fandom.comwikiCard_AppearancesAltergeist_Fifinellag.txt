 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 77 ,  Ghost Gal  uses this card during her  Speed Duel  against  The Shepherd . She activates " Altergeist Haunted Rock " to send this card from her hand to the Graveyard, then uses " Altergeist Marionetter 's" effect to revive this card. Ghost Gal then tunes this card with "Marionetter" to  Synchro Summon  " Altergeist Dragvirion ".
