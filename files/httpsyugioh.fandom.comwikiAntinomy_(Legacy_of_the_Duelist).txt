 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Antinomy  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Antinomy , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. His alter-ego,  Bruno , serves as a supporting character.
