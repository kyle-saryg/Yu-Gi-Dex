 " Assault Blackwing ", known as " Assault Black Feather " ( Ａ   ＢＦ   Asaruto Burakku Fezā ) in the Japanese version, is an  archetype  of  DARK   Winged Beast   monsters , as well as a sub-archetype of the " Blackwing " archetype. They are used by  Crow Hogan  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.

 Design 
 Appearance 
 Like the original "Blackwing" monsters, the "Assault Blackwing" monsters resemble either humanoid birds or humanoids in bird-like outfits. Additionally, the "Assault Blackwing" monsters have a half-mechanical appearance to them.
 
 Etymology 
 The "Assault Blackwing" monsters are named after legendary Japanese weapons and given a title relating to rain.

 Playing style 
 "Assault Blackwing" monsters tend to treat themselves as  Tuner monsters  if they are Special Summoned by using at least 1 "Blackwing" monster (either as a  Tribute  for the  Main Deck  members, or as a  Synchro Material  for the  Synchro  members), allowing their user to quickly gather up the total Levels needed to Summon powerful Synchro Monsters.
 
 Trivia 
 With the exception of "Assault Blackwing - Kunai the Drizzle", all members of this sub-archetype are  Synchro Monsters .
