 " Number C39 ", known as " Chaos Numbers 39 " ( ＣＮｏ. ３９  Kaosu Nanbāzu Sanjūkyū  or  CNo. 39  Kaoseu Neombeojeu 39 ) in Japan and Korea, is a sub- archetype  to the " Chaos ", " Number ", and " Number C " archetypes. All of them are upgraded versions of " Number 39: Utopia ", and their effects require a "Utopia" monster to promote the most effective. Also, all of them are from the " Utopia " or " Hope " archetype, thus gaining  support  from the " ZW - " archetype. They are used by  Yuma Tsukumo  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime and  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  manga.
