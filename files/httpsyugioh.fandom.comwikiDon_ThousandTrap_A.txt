   English 
 
 
When your opponent would  Special Summon  exactly 1 monster while you  control  no other cards:  Negate  the Special Summon, and if you do,  destroy  it, then choose 1 monster in your opponent's  Deck  and Special Summon it to their side of the  field . -I activate " Numeron Xyz Revision "!-
