 Dark Destruction  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from December 12, 2012. Each pack costs   90.
 This collection includes Zombie, Fiend and Warrior monsters as well as many complex spells.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 9 cards.
 The set contains 112 cards in total.
