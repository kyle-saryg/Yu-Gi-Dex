 Rio Kastle , known as  Rio Kamishiro  ( 神 代   璃 緒 ,  Kamishiro Rio ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Rio Kastle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
