Sector Security , known as  Security  or  Corrupted Security  (セキュリティー  Sekyuritī ) in the Japanese version, is an organization led by  Fusion Dimension 's ex-agent  Jean-Michel Roget , who attempts to maintain law and order in  New Domino City  as well as to arrest the  Commons  that they find in the  Topsiders -restricted areas, appearing in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . [1]  It is an alternate universe version of  Sector Security  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 A subsection of Sector Security are the  Duel Chasers , who engage criminals in  Turbo Duels [2]  and the  Stomptroopers , who catch fugitives that escape from the  Facility . [3]  After the events of the Friendship Cup,  Declan Akaba  reveals that both Commons and Topsiders are turning over a new leaf and working in unison, ultimately causing the organization to be disbanded. [4]

 Sector Security officers can be controlled by an operation called "King's Gambit" [5] ; Roget initiated this in order to wrest authority from the  High Council . During the first King's Gambit, hundreds of Security officers drove to the High Council's Hall of Dominion at once, putting it under siege. During the second King's Gambit, Security officers were called to quell several groups of rioting  Commons . The  Lancers  and  Chojiro Tokumatsu  defeated most of the Security officers  [6] [7] ; after  Sora Perse  and  Moon Shadow  infiltrated the Sector Security headquarters and forcibly shut down King's Gambit, they prevented Commons from clashing with the remaining officers. [8] . The remaining officers were then seen watching the  Friendship Cup  Final between  Yuya Sakaki  and  Jack Atlas [9] .

 Deck 
 The ground-based officers of Sector Security use " Vigilante " Decks , which are used to easily  Synchro Summon   monsters  like " Marksman Heiji Type 0 ", and exploit its  effect  to  destroy  as many monsters as possible and inflict  effect damage , which coupled with his  ATK  value, can result in a  OTK . If this monster  leaves the field  it can  Special Summon  the  Synchro Material Monsters , thus ensuring durability. The  Duel Chasers  and  Stomptroopers  play different Decks.
