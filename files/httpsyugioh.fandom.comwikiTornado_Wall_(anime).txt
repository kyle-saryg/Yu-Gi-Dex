 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 This card can only be  activated  when " Umi " is active on the  field . As long as "Umi" remains  face-up  on the field, any  damage  to your  Life Points  becomes 0. When "Umi" is  destroyed  or  removed from the field , this card is also destroyed.
