 Mai Kujaku  and the  Player Killer of Darkness  faced each other in  Duel Monsters , during the  Duelist Kingdom  tournament, in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Events 
 During the night after the first day of preliminaries at the  Duelist Kingdom  tournament, the  Player Killer  of Darkness searched  the island  for participants to eliminate from the tournament.
 Mai had been  camping  with  Yugi Mutou ,  Katsuya Jonouchi ,  Anzu Mazaki ,  Hiroto Honda  and  Ryo Bakura  was there too. Mai was left alone in her tent after getting Anzu to meet Yugi. While she was alone, the Player Killer snuck up on her and forced her into a Duel, where she had to bet all eight of her  Star Chips .
 The Player Killer made use of the  Darkness  to hide his monsters and easily overpower Mai.
 Yugi and Anzu heard Mai screaming as she lost and ran to the Duel. The Player Killer explained how Mai had been eliminated and that  Maximillion J. Pegasus  had listed taking out Yugi as a priority. Yugi then switched to  Dark Yugi , who  Dueled  the Player Killer to win back Mai's Star Chips.
