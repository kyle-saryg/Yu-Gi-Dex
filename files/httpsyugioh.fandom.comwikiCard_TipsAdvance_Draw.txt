 This card can be searched by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Mythical Bestiamorph ", " Cyberse Clock Dragon ", " Endymion, the Mighty Master of Magic " and " Left Arm Offering ". 
 This card is useful in a " Hieratic " deck to Tribute " Hieratic Seal of the Sun Dragon Overlord " or any other Level 8 Dragon monster summoned with 0 ATK/DEF. 
 This card is useful in a " Gimmick Puppet " deck to Tribute Level 8 monsters with low stats that aren't being used for  Xyz Summons . 
 In a " Lair of Darkness " deck, this card can either trigger " Darkest Diabolos, Lord of the Lair " or remove a Level 8 or higher monster your opponent controls. 
 Use this card to Tribute " Shaddoll Core " or " Metal Reflect Slime ", as they summon themselves as Level 9 and Level 10 monsters respectively. 
 Use this card to Tribute monsters that can change their own Levels to 8 or higher, such as " Flelf ", " Gagaga Magician " or " Galaxy Wizard ". 
 You can use " A Deal with Dark Ruler " after using this card to bring out " Berserk Dragon ". 
 Use with either " Elemental HERO Absolute Zero " or " Earthbound Immortal Aslla piscu " for a  nuke .
