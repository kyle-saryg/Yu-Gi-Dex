 Housewife  ( 主婦   Shufu ) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . She is based on the  manga  character,  Mrs. Hanasaki , in terms of appearance.
 
 Biography 
 The housewife was a customer at  Burger World  while  Jiro "the Jorogumo"  and  Tetsuo "the Hedgehog" Sasaki  were  on the loose . She was uncooperative when the  detective  and staff searched for Tetsuo among the customers. [1]
