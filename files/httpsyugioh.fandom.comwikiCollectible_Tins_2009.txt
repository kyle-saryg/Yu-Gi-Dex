 The  Collectible Tins 2009  are a set of Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game collectible tins. They were released in 2 waves:
 
 Collectible Tins 2009 Wave 1 
 Collectible Tins 2009 Wave 2 
 Additionally, the  Collectible Tins Exclusive 2009  were also released, which all feature  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  characters on their tins and include different  Secret Rare  promotional cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Collectible Tin 2009  contains 5  Booster Packs , 1 set of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  branded card sleeves, 1 set of  Deck partitions , and 1  Secret Rare  promotional card.
