 Tin Goldfish  is a character version of the card " Tin Goldfish ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P104. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 5-4 under Quest mode.

 Description 
 A  Machine - Type   monster  with a nostalgic feel. Not a  Fish -Type!
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Say, do you know about tinplate toys? Many old toys used to be made from tinplate. Why do I know this? Because I've got a long history of my own.
 
 Decks 
 Partner 
 A  Deck  comprised of cards that are easy to tie together for combos, centered around " Gadget " monsters. Use the  effect  of "Tin Goldfish" to  Special Summon  a "Gadget." Then use the effect of that "Gadget" to enhance your  hand ! There are many more combos to find and exploit!

 Trivia 
 The term "tinplate" in this character's opponent quote are references to its translated Japanese name: "Tinplate Goldfish". 
 " Tin Archduke " in this character's Decks is a reference to  Bronk Stone , who used that card and " Tin Goldfish " in the  anime .
