 Effect 
 Printed lore : During the  turn  when monsters with a  Level  of 7 or higher are  sent  to the  Graveyard  from both sides of the  field :  Banish  all monsters from the  Graveyards . You can  Special Summon  the  Spellcaster - Type  monster with the highest Level that was banished from your Graveyard by this card’s effect. Millennium Memory lore : During the turn a highest Level monster is sent to the Graveyard from both sides of the field: Banish all monsters from the Graveyards. You can Special Summon the Spellcaster-Type monster with the highest Level that was banished from your Graveyard by this card’s effect. [1] [2] 
 
 4K Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English card uses the same lore as the OCG/TCG card. The Japanese card and reference sources state the effect differently.):</mobile> If a  monster (s) with an original  Level  of 7 or higher is  sent  from each player's  field  to the  Graveyard  this  turn :  Banish  as many monsters from the Graveyards as possible, then you can  Special Summon  the 1 highest-Level  Spellcaster - Type  monster among those banished monsters to your field (your choice, if tied).
