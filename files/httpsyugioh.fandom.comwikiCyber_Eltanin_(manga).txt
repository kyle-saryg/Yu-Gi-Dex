 This card's  ATK  is equal to 500 times the number of  Cyber   monsters  that have been  removed from play . When this card is  Special Summoned , you can send a number of  face-up  monsters on your opponent's  Field  to the  Graveyard , equal to the number of Cyber monsters that have been removed from play.
