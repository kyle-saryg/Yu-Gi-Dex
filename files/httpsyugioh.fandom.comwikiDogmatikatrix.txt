 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When this card  resolves , you can  add  1 " Dogmatika "  Ritual Monster  or  Ritual Spell  from your  Deck  to your  hand , then, if your opponent  controls  a  monster , you can add 1 "Dogmatika" card from your Deck to your hand.  Once per turn , if you  control  a "Dogmatika" Ritual Monster: You can  look  at either  Extra Deck  and  send  1 monster from it to the  GY . You can only  activate  1 "Dogmatikatrix" per  turn .
