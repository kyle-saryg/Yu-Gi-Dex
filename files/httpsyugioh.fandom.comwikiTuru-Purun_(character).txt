 Mobile appearances 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 
 
 
 Turu-Purun  is a  Duel Monster Spirit  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It appears as a Tower Monster in the  D.D. Tower: Water Dimension   event  and also in the  D.D. Castle: Emergence  event as an occasional Castle Guard. This is the character version of the card " Turu-Purun ".
