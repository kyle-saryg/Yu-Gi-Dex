 Jasmine , known in Japan as  Junko Makurada  is an  Obelisk Blue  student, who is friends with  Mindy  and  Alexis Rhodes .

 Biography 
 Jasmine and is seen most of the time hanging around with  Alexis  and  Mindy , cheering the former on during her Duels.
She first appears when  Syrus Truesdale  snuck in the girls dorm because he thought Alexis liked him. The girls had to hide Syrus when  Fonda Fontaine  came to ask what was going on. Alexis decided to challenge Jaden to a duel with Mindy and Jasmine cheering her on. After Alexis loses, the girls let Syrus and Jaden leave without telling the staff.
 
       Jasmine being held hostage by Wheeler.     
 Early on in the first season, while searching for  Chazz Princeton , Jasmine is kidnapped by an experimental chimpanzee,  Wheeler , and taken hostage until  Jaden Yuki  won the Duel for her freedom. Despite the ordeal however, Jasmine bears no ill-will towards the chimpanzee and is the first to state that he should not be sent to the cruel lab he escaped from. When Wheeler is freed and able to return to his troop, Jasmine is happy for him and bids her former captor farewell on good terms. 
 In the second season, during the  Genex Tournament , she and Mindy were only seen Dueling once against a  Pro League  Duelest named  Maitre' D , and were losing badly until Alexis took over and defeated Maitre' D. Mindy and Jasmine try to convince Alexis to return to  Obelisk Blue , but Alexis declines saying her loyalty is to  Sartorius . They were two of the few Obelisk Blue students who did not join the  Society of Light . 
 In the third year, she got transported into the  alternate dimension  with everyone else, and in the fourth, she lost to  Tyranno Hassleberry  and  Blair Flannigan  during her first Duel in the  Pair Duel Tournament .  She was one of the last students at Duel Academy during the  Trueman  siege but lost along with Mindy, going to the  World of Darkness . She was revived when Jaden defeated  Nightshroud . 
 
       The Harpie Lady Sisters Cosplay.     
 It is interpreted both girls share many crushes in the anime. Their eyes turn into hearts whenever an attractive male walks by, as seen with  Harrington Rosewood ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Yugi Muto , and  Aster Phoenix . However, she seems to be a little more sensible than Mindy, as, despite initial infatuation with Harrington, she agreed with  Syrus Truesdale  about his somewhat boastful personality and sports clichés being repulsive as he bragged about himself to Alexis.
 
 Etymology 
 It is possible her name is a reference to the Japanese singer and actress  Junko Sakurada  from the 1970s.
 
 Anime Deck 
 The only known card in Jasmine's anime Deck is " Mermaid Knight ".
