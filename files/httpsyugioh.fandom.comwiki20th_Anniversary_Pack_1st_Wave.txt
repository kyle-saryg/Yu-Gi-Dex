 20th Anniversary Pack 1st Wave  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 All the reprints are cards from  DM  and  GX  that used to be, or still are, tournament-competitive. 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Mai Valentine ,  Téa Gardner ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Princess Rose ,  Yami Bakura ,  Kagemaru ,  Yubel , and  Battle Beast . 
 Includes members/support for the " Black Luster Soldier ", " Blue-Eyes ", " Chaos ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Elemental HERO ", " Exodia ", " Frog ", " Fusion ", " Forbidden One ", " Harpie ", " HERO ", " Gladiator Beast ", " Jinzo ", " Neos ", " Neo-Spacian ", " Polymerization ", and " Red-Eyes "  archetypes ; as well as the  Armed Dragon ,  Chimeratech ,  D.D. ,  Jar ,  Mirror Trap ,  Monarch ,  Royal ,  Sacred Beast , and  signature move  series. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack and 15 packs per Japanese box or 30 packs per Korean box. 
 The set contains 51 cards:
 
 1  Holographic Parallel Rare  (Japanese only) 
 1  Extra Secret Parallel Rare  (Korean only) 
 3  Secret Parallel Rares 
 6  Ultra Parallel Rares 
 9  Super Parallel Rares 
 32  Normal Parallel Rares
