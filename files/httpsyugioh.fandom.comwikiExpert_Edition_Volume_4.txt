 Expert Edition Volume 4 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Expert Edition Volume 4  is a  Booster Pack  in the  OCG .  Dark Revelation Volume 4  is its equivalent in the  TCG . This set combines all the cards in  Cybernetic Revolution ,  Elemental Energy ,  Shadow of Infinity  and  Enemy of Justice , as well as 5  Secret Rare  previously Out-of-Print cards. This set does not include  Ultimate Rares .
