 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution  is a 2019 remaster of the 2015 game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist .

 Features 
 Contains the entirety of  Legacy of the Duelist , including all previously released DLC, within the base game. This includes content from every series from the original series to  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , as well as Battle Pack Sealed and Draft gameplay. 
 Features new characters and events from the latest animated series  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS , with the continued option to take the role as the series' rivals and villains in "Reverse Duels". 
 Presents Link Monsters and the New Master Rule to the game. 
 Play against Duelists across the world using the Nintendo Online service. 
 In March 2020, the game received a free update to implement the April 1 rule revisions. This update also added over 1000 cards and over a dozen new characters originally from later seasons of  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS . 
 Gameplay 
 Position of cards now matter due to  Link Monsters . Player must manually choose which zone cards are played in. 
 The order of cards has changed, in the original  Spell Cards  were first, then  Trap Cards , then  Monster Cards . In Link Evolution, it's Monsters, then Spells, then Traps. 
 Cards 
 Includes 10,027 cards. Goes up to  Ignition Assault ,  Mystic Fighters ,  Legendary Duelists: Magical Hero ,  Structure Deck: Shaddoll Showdown , and  Duel Overload . 
 Includes more than 10000 cards up to around the release of  Structure Deck: Shaddoll Showdown . 
 Includes 3 exclusive cards for those who buy the physical copies of the game:  Cynet Codec ,  Micro Coder  and  Progleo 
 Campaigns 
 Includes all previously released Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist content, adjusted with new story scenes. 
 Has campaigns for all anime series of Yugioh up to  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS .
 This also expands the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  campaign with full narrative scenes and additional duelists.
 The Tutorial duel now is against  The Sledgehammer  instead of  Gong Strong .
 During the point where you summoned Judge Man and both Pendulum Monsters destroyed in the previous turn, you instead summon only one of the two. This is because of the above-mentioned  Pendulum Summon  restriction in the New Master Rules. 
 Gamemodes 
 Challenge other players with local-play or Nintendo Switch Online 
 Take on the roles of the animated series’ villains with Reverse Duels 
 Compete with Battle Packs for Sealed play and Draft play 
 New Rules/Format 
 This game uses the New Master Rules, introduced in  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS . 
 The  Pendulum Zone  is now on the Spell/Trap zone. 
 You may summon up to six monsters, with the  Extra Monster Zone  used only by  Extra Deck  monsters. Each duellist can only use one Extra Monster Zone.
 Prior to the March update, all  Extra Deck  monsters couldn't be summoned except in this zone or wherever a  Link Arrow  is pointing. 
 After the March update, only  Pendulum Monsters  that are Pendulum Summoned back to the field from the Extra Deck still retain this restriction. 
 Uses the April 2020 Forbidden and Limited List. 
 Uses a custom Forbidden and Limited list. 
 Graphics 
 Some of the character's Deck lists have changed.
 When sorted by "Duelist" decks are listed in alphabetical order, rather than by their order in the story. 
 Monsters have their attack animation play when summoned rather than when attacking. 
 Several attack animations have been added or revised. 
 Packs have sound effects when opened. 
 Some cards use different art, such as the  Egyptian Gods . 
 All cards in the original game had the Legacy of the Duelist logo in the bottom left corner of the cards. This is no longer the case in Link Evolution, so the cards are now identical their real life counterparts. 
 Consoles 
 PS4 version of Link Evolution has a Platinum Trophy. It is the only Yu-Gi-Oh! game to have one. 
 There are three new achievements/trophies on both XBOX One and PS4. 
 Glitches 
 Checking the card details of a Link Monster will correctly show it as not having a DEF value. However, checking the card details of any non-Link monster and then checking a Link Monster's details again will result in the last-loaded DEF value being shown. This will persist until either a non-Monster Card is checked, or until the game is closed. 
 The game (very rarely) may crash when attempting to load one of the 3D cutscenes.

 Trivia 
 The game does not include  Turbo Duel  or  Speed Duel  rules, modifying any duel using those rules to use the standard rule set. 
 The VRAINS campaign has no narrative scenes. 
 All VRAINS characters use never-before-seen artwork.
 Some characters, like  Alexis Rhodes  and  Aster Phoenix , have completely new artwork, exclusive to this title. 
 Alexis Rhodes duels a member of the  Obelisk Force  rather than the  Juvenile Officers . 
 There is no DLC or  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  content planned for the game at this time. 
 The ARC-V campaign features a special event with  Yuya Sakaki  dueling against  Yusho Sakaki . This duel did not happen in the anime.
