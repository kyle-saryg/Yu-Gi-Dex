 The Shining Darkness  is the eighth and final set of the  OCG 's sixth Series after  Absolute Powerforce . It is followed by  Duelist Revolution , the first set from Series 7.

 Features 
 The Shining Darkness  features the latest cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime, including cards used by  Crow Hogan ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Kalin Kessler ,  Leo  and  Luna .
 Includes further support for the " Blackwing ", " Koa'ki Meiru ", " Morphtronic ", " Infernity ", " Ice Barrier ", " Synchron ", " Archfiend ", " Frog ", " Batteryman ", " Worm ", and " Ally of Justice "  archetypes  and introduces the new " Watt " archetype.
 
 Breakdown
