 Saikyō Jump  ( 最強  ジャンプ  Saikyō Janpu ) is a Japanese magazine which publishes spin-off manga based on popular anime and manga series.
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  spin-off  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!!  is currently being serialized in  Saikyō Jump . The  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  spin-off  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  was also serialized until the series ended.
