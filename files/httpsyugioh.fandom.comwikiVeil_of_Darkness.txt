 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Darkness "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ダークネス」 .   As its English name contains "Darkness", it  may  be treated a member of the "Darkness" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During your  Draw Phase , if you  draw  a  DARK   monster (s): You can  reveal  any number of them;  send  them to the  GY , then you can draw 1 more card. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Durant votre Draw Phase, si vous piochez un ou plusieurs monstres TÉNÈBRES : vous pouvez en révéler un nombre de votre choix ; envoyez-les au Cimetière, puis vous pouvez piocher 1 carte supplémentaire.
