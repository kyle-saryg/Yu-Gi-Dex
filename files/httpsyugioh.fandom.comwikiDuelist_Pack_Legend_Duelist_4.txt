 Duelist Pack: Legend Duelist 4  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by various female protagonists over the course of the Yu-Gi-Oh! series, including  Mai Valentine ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Anna Kaboom , and  Celina . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Harpie ", " Cyber Angel ", and " Lunalight " archetypes, as well as the " Railway " series.
 Introduces the first support for the " Rose Dragon " and " Machine Angel " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 55+1 cards.
