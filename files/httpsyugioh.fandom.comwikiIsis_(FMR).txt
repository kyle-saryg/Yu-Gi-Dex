 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 Isis , known as  Naomi Ishizu  (石津ナオミ  Ishizu Naomi ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . She is the game's incarnation of  Ishizu Ishtar , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  and  anime . She has a fierce personality, contrasting her manga and anime depictions.
 Isis appears as one of the contenders in the Duel Monsters World Championship. After she lost to  Yugi Muto , her  Millennium Necklace  got absorbed into 1 of the  Millennium Item Cards .
 
 Deck 
 Isis uses mainly  WATER  monsters. Her strongest cards are " B. Skull Dragon " and " Twin-headed Thunder Dragon ". She can also use  Swords of Revealing Light .
