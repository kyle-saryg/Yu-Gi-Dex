 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Traptrix "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 蟲 惑 魔 」 .   As its English name contains "Traptrix", it  may  be treated a member of the "Traptrix" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Discard  1  Level  4  Insect  or  Plant   monster , or 1  Normal Trap ;  draw  2 cards. You can  banish  this card from your  GY , then  target  1 of your banished Level 4 Insect or Plant monsters, or 1 of your banished Normal Traps; place it on the bottom of the  Deck . You can only use the each  effect  of "Traptrix Allure"  once per turn .
