 Shell Guardian - Savan  is a character appearing in some video games. He first appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008  in the World of Sunlight, guarding the door to the World of Civilization. The player must defeat him 3 times, each time with a different Deck building condition, to unlock the World of Civilization.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , Savan appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for October 2009.
 
 Deck 
 In World Championship 2008, Savan's Decks are also designed along the same Deck building restrictions he imposes on the player.
 
 40 Cards only :  Midnight Thunder , a Thunder-themed beatdown Deck. 
 2000 LP :  Wanna Dance? , a defensive Burn Deck that attempts to destroy aggressive players with massive reflected damage and instant effect damage. 
 Monsters only :  Prehistoric Drum , a Monsters-only beatdown Deck which also has some effects that Special Summon more monsters to get around the Normal Summon limit. 
 No Spells :  Prohibited Trap , a Deck designed to circumvent the effects of Trap cards, which will likely be the player's only method of defense with this restriction. 
 3 Copies of Each Card :  Back, You Beast! , a Beast-themed beatdown Deck. 
 In Stardust Accelerator, Savan uses a  WATER  Deck, titled "Ocean's Saver".
