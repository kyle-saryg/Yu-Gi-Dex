 Chaos Hundred Universe

 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Special Summon  from your  Graveyard , all " Number C10X "  monsters  that were  destroyed  under your  control  this  turn , also Special Summon from your  Extra Deck  to your opponent's side of the  field , an equal number of "Number C10X" monsters in  face-up   Defense Position . (X is any number from 1 to 7.) The  effects  of all monsters Special Summoned by this effect are  negated .
