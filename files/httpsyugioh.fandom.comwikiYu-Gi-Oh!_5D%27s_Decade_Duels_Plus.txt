 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Decade Duels Plus  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh!  video game for the Xbox 360 and Playstation 3. The game boasts new features and functionality including:
 
 Voice and Text chat compatibility 
 Over 2000 playable cards, including cards featured in Yu-Gi-Oh!, Yu-Gi-Oh! GX and Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Hundreds of additional cards and packs for purchase through Xbox Live® Marketplace and the PlayStation® Store 
 Online Multiplayer with an up-to-four-player Tag Duel for total gameplay customization with user-created rules 
 Multiple game modes, including Quick Match, Rank Match, and Custom Match 
 Online Leader Boards to track your stats worldwide 
 Available cards ranging from  Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon  to  Ancient Prophecy . [ citation needed ]

 Future Fusion  should wait until the second standby phase after activation to Summon the monster, but the game will sometimes Summon it immediately.
