 The Desert Mage  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is located at the Desert Shrine.  The Prince  must defeat him before proceeding to  High Mage Martis , to duel for the  Millennium Scale . He also appears as an enemy in Kaiba's mode in The Falsebound Kingdom named Semmut.
 
 Deck 
 Desert Mage often uses " Labyrinth Wall " whose  DEF  rises to 3500 due to the Desert field.
