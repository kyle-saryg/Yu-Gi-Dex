 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  Normal  or  Special Summoned : You can  add  1  Spell / Trap  " Fur Hire " from your  Deck  to your  hand . During the  Main Phase , if you  control  a  monster  "Fur Hire" ( Quick Effect ): You can  banish  this card from your  GY , then  target  1 card "Fur Hire" in your GY;  add  it to your hand, or, if it is a monster, you can  Special Summon  it instead. You can only use each  effect  of "Rex, Freight Fur Hire"  once per turn .
