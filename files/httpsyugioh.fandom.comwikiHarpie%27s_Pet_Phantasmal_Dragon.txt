The  Croatian                        name given is not official.  
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Phantasm "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 幻 魔 」 .   As its English name contains "Phantasm", it  may  be treated a member of the "Phantasm" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
3  Level  4  WIND   monsters This card's  effects  can only be applied/ resolved  while it has  material . This card can  attack  your opponent  directly . Your opponent cannot  target  any " Harpie " monsters with effects or for attacks. During each of your  End Phases :  Detach  1 material from this card.
