 Structure Deck: Invincible Fortress  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the seventh deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (fifteenth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Spellcaster's Judgment . In the  TCG , it is the seventh deck.
 
 Create an unbreakable defense with the mighty  Earth   Monster  in the "Invincible Fortress"  Structure Deck ! These  monsters  can be  Summoned  to unleash their powerful  effects , and then returned to  face-down   Defense Position  to protect your  Life Points  with their incredible  Defense Points . "Invincible Fortress" is ready to play right out of the box and is great for new Duelists as well as experienced players. Each  Deck  contains 40 cards, including an all-new  Ultra Rare   monster , a rulebook, and a Dueling Guide to help Duelists improve the Deck and their game! 
 —  Upper Deck Entertainment 
 
 Although this Structure Deck was not released in Spanish, it is listed on the Spanish version of the official card database. [1]

 Features 
 The deck features  Earth  monsters,  Direct Damage  through  card effects , high defense monsters and returning the opponent's monsters to their hand. Featured cards include " Exxod, Master of The Guard ", " Great Spirit ", and " Canyon ".
