 Spellcaster Deck / Lv: 10 
 A Deck built with  Spellcaster -Type monsters. Cards such as “ Rogue Doll ” and “ The Illusory Gentleman ” have a higher ATK!
 This Deck also contains handy Spell Cards. If you face a monster with higher ATK, change your opponent’s monster to Defense Position using " Block Attack ", and then attack! In addition, you can gain Life Points with " Blue Medicine " even if you receive damage from your opponent.
