 Duel Terminal 4  is made up of the OCG sets  Duel Terminal - Charge of the Genex!!  and  Duel Terminal - Pulse of the Trishula!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 100 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 60 of the cards are new, while 40 are reprints. Introduces the " Genex Ally " and " The Fabled " archetype to the TCG. Includes further support for " Ice Barrier ", " Jurrac ", " Naturia " and " Dragunity " archetypes.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 100 cards all of which are  Parallel Rare . 60 of the cards are new, while 40 are reprints.
