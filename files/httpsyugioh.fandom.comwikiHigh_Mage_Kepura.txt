 High Mage Kepura  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is located at the Meadow Shrine.  The Prince  faces him after defeating the  Meadow Mage . The Prince duels and defeats him taking the  Millennium Eye .
 It is possible to win the card " Acid Trap Hole " after recieving a TEC rank for defeating High Mage Kepura.
 He also appears as an enemy Marshal in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom , named  Kepulia .
 
 Deck 
 High Mage Kepura's Deck consists mainly of cards that benefit from the  Meadow  Field. It also contains a lot of Dragons.
 His most powerful card is " Gate Guardian ", so it is helpful for the player to have a " Meteor Black Dragon ", which can beat it, while placed under the Sun  Guardian Star .
