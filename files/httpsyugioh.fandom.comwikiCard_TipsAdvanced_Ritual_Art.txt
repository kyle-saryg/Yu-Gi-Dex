 This card can be searched by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Herald of the Arc Light ", " Spiritual Whisper ", " Sonic Bird ", " Manju of the Ten Thousand Hands ", " Prediction Princess Arrowsylph ", " Beginning Knight ", " Gateway to Chaos ", " Cyber Angel Idaten ", " Ritual Sanctuary ", " Shinobird Power Spot ", and " Revendread Slayer ". 
 This card is part of the  Demise/Doom OTK . 
 You can use this card to mill 12 Level 1  Normal Monsters  to  Ritual Summon  " Herald of Ultimateness ". 
 You can also use this card to mill  Normal Tuner monsters  for " Variety Comes Out ". 
 This card can be used to send " Forbidden One " cards to the Graveyard to Summon a Level 4 Ritual Monster. They can then be retrieved with cards like " Backup Soldier ", " Dark Factory of Mass Production ", etc. 
 This card can be useful in Ritual Summoning " Lycanthrope " and/or " Cu Chulainn the Awakened ", since their effects require Normal Monsters in the Graveyard.
