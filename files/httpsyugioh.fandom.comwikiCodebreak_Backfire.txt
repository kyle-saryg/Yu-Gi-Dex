 Codebreak Backfire 
 
 Japanese:   コードブレイク・バックファイア 
 Romaji:   Kōdobureiku Bakkufaia 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Monsters  on the  field  cannot be  destroyed by battle  with " Codebreaker " monsters. If your "Codebreaker" monster(s) is  Special Summoned  to your opponent's field: Inflict 600  damage  to your opponent. "Codebreaker" monsters your opponent  controls  cannot  attack , they cannot be  Tributed  for a  Tribute Summon , also they cannot be used as  material  for a Special Summon.
