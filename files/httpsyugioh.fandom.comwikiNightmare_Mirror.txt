 Nightmare Mirror 
 
 Japanese:   悪夢の魔鏡 
 Kana:   あくむのまかがみ 
 Romaji:   Akumu no Makyō 
 Translated:  Nightmare's Demon Mirror 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Activate  when an opponent's  monster   attacks . By  discarding  1 card from your  hand , Nightmare Mirror reflects the opponent. It becomes the  attack target , causing the opponent to take 1000  damage .
