 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When your opponent  activates  a  Spell / Trap Card  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Tribute  1 " Naturia "  monster  and this card;  negate  the activation, and if you do,  destroy  that card.
