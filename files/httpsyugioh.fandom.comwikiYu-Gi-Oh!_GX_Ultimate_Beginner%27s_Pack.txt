 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Ultimate Beginner's Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Ultimate Beginner's Pack  is a gift bundle in the Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game (TCG).

 Features 
 This is the perfect gift for Yu-Gi-Oh! fans! If you have never played Yu-Gi-Oh! before, this is a great introduction into the world of dueling. This special package features  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Duel Academy  for the Game Boy® Advance, tutorial DVD, Starter Deck of trading cards, and exclusive trading cards. Includes Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Duel Academy (Game Boy® Advance), a  Starter Deck  of Trading Cards,  Jaden  and  Chazz  Duelist Packs, a Training DVD, and a set of  Exodia  cards
