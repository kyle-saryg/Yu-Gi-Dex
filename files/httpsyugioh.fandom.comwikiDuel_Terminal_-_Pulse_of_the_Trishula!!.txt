 Duel Terminal - Pulse of the Trishula  is the eighth installment of the  Duel Terminal  series which follows  Duel Terminal - Charge of the Genex!! . It is followed by  Duel Terminal - Vylon Descends!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. 
 It includes further support for the " Dragunity ", " Dragunity Knight ", " Fabled ", " The Fabled ", " Genex Ally ", " Naturia ", and " Ice Barrier " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Neo Flamvell "  archetype . 
 Introduces  Atticus Rhodes ,  Yubel  and  Kalin Kessler  as duelable opponents. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards (consisting of 30 New Cards and 20 Reprint Cards), or 54 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares  (all available as Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares) 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 9  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 31  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
