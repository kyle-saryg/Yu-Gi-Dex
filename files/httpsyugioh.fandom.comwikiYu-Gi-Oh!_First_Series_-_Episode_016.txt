 Summary 
   This summary is currently incomplete. 
 Jonouchi  has a crush on  Miyuki , the nurse taking care of his sister  Shizuka . But when a the corrupt doctor,  Kekeru Goyu , fires her, Jonouchi cannot expose him, or Shizuka will be kicked out of the hospital.
 
 Mistakes 
 At one point  Dark Yugi 's eyes were red then when he was talking to the doctor they were purple like Yugi's eyes. When he gave the penalty game his eyes were red again.
