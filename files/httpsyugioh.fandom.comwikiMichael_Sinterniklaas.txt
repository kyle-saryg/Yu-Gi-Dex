 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Sinterniklaas's first major role in Yu-Gi-Oh! history was portraying  Mahad / Dark Magician  in season 5.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Sinterniklaas's most notable role is portraying  Torunka  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  while he was affected by the  Minus Curse .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , Sinterniklaas reprised his role as   Dark Magician .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Sinterniklass portrays  Cody Callus  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL .
