 V Jump Edition 6  is the twenty-third installment of the newly renamed Limited Edition series, it could be pre-ordered with the December Issue of V-Jump Magazine, in 2011. This pack contains  Xyz Monsters  that appeared during the  World Duel Carnival  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. The previous set is  V Jump Edition 5 , and this is followed by  V Jump Edition 7 .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards: 5  Ultra Rares .
