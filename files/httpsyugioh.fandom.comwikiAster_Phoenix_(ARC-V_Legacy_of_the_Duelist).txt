 Aster Phoenix , known as  Edo Phoenix  (エド・フェニックス,  Edo Fenikkusu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Aster Phoenix (ARC-V) , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
