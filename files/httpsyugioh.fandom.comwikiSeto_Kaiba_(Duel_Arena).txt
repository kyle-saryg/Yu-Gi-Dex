 Seto Kaiba  is a video game depiction of  Seto Kaiba , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 He appears as a Legend Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 6 Rental Points. His partner ID is P003. He is also available as an opponent, under  Duel Monsters Arena  mode.

 3.2   Opponent 
 
 
 
 
 Description 
 High school student and the young CEO of  Kaiba Corporation . A skilled Duelist and  Yugi 's eternal rival. Loves " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ", and enjoys using tactics based on  brute force .
 
 Opponent Quote 
 My Blue-Eyes will smash you up!
 
 Decks 
 Partner 
 It goes without saying that this is a power Deck, built around " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " and also including " Obelisk the Tormentor ". Provide support for cards in your field with XYZ  Union Monsters  and aim to Summon a High  Level   Monster !
