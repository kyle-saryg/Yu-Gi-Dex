 During your  Main Phase : You can  excavate  the top 5 cards of your  Deck , and if you do, you can  add  1 excavated " Adamancipator " card to your  hand , also place the rest on the bottom of the Deck in any order. During your opponent's  turn , if a  FIRE  monster is in your  GY  ( Quick Effect ): You can  target  1  Rock  monster in your GY;  Special Summon  it. You can only use each  effect  of "Adamancipator Risen - Leonite"  once per turn .
