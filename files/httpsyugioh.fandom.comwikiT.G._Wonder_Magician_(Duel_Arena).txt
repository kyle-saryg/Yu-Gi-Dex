 T.G. Wonder Magician  is a character version of the card " T.G. Wonder Magician ". 
 She appears as Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P050. She also appears as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 4 under Quest mode.

 Trivia 
 This character's introduction quote is a reference to  Bruno 's  Summon chants  for his " T.G. " monsters in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. This is fitting, since Bruno uses this character's card counterpart. Furthermore, this character's introduction quote is the same as Bruno's Summon chant of "Wonder Magician", though it is reworded slightly.
