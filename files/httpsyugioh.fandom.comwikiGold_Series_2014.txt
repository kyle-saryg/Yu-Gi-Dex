 Features 
 This is one of the products for the 3rd "Rubber Duel Field Present Campaign" promotion. 
 Includes 3 cards chosen by online voting. 
 Includes new cards and cards released first in the TCG. 
 Includes popular cards that were widely used in Duels in the past. 
 Since each card is highly useful and will be helpful in building your Deck, this is a Product that appeals to a wide range of players. 
 While the Japanese and Chinese sets include " Tour Guide From the Underworld ", the Korean set includes " Exodia the Forbidden One ". 
 Breakdown 
 20 packs per box.
 The Japanese and Chinese version contains 4 cards per pack (1 card will be Gold Rare). 
 The Korean version contains 7 cards per pack (2 cards will be Gold Rare) 
 The set includes 20 cards, 50 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 14  Commons 
 16  Gold Rares 
 20  Gold Secret Rares
