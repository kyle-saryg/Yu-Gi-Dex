 Limited Edition 
 Limited Edition 2 
 
 
 
 
 
 Limited Edition  is a set of three packs available through mail order through the  Weekly Shonen Jump  magazine. The  Yugi Pack ,  Kaiba Pack  and  Joey Pack  contained a different set of three cards.
 All of these cards have since been released in the English  TCG  originally, in  McDonald's Promotional Cards .
 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains nine  Normal Monsters , all of which are  Ultra Secret Rare .
