 " Sky Striker Ace " ( 閃 刀 姫 ,  Sentōki , literally "Flash Sword Princess") is a small  archetype  of  female   monsters  with 1500  ATK  introduced in  Deck Build Pack: Dark Savers . It is part of the " Sky Striker " archetype. All of this archetype's  Extra Deck  members are  Machines  with various  Attributes , while its  Main Deck  members are  DARK  and  LIGHT   Warrior  monsters.

 Design 
 "Sky Striker Ace" Extra Deck Monsters are essentially the Main Deck Monsters wearing different suits of powered armor. The suits also have a serial number that starts with an "X-00" for " Sky Striker Ace - Raye " and "Z-00" for " Sky Striker Ace - Roze ".
 
 Etymology 
 The Japanese name of this archetype, literally "Flash Katana Lady", is a pun on the word 戦闘機  sentōki  "fighter aircraft", which is appropriate because "Sky Striker Ace" monsters wear armor that is composed of fighter aircraft parts. A part of this archetype name,  閃 刀 , is used for the Japanese name of " Sky Striker ".

 Playing style 
 The main theme of Sky Striker Ace monster is to utilize their Main Deck monster; Raye, to quickly summon the Sky Striker Ace Link monsters. Many of their Link Monsters are also highly dependant on Spell Cards to support them. In addition, many of their cards have powerful replenishing effects to maintain hand advantage. With the exception of Raye and Hayate, the other Sky Striker Ace monsters have effects that revolve around gaining or losing ATK and LP.
 Hayate also has the ability to send Sky Striker Cards from the Deck to the Graveyard to boost other Sky Striker Ace monsters or set up Raye from the Graveyard.
