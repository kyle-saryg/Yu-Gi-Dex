 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters International: Worldwide Edition promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters International: Worldwide Edition  promotional cards  are cards included with copies of the Japanese game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters International: Worldwide Edition .
 The first edition of this game contained  Secret Rare  cards, while the "normal edition" contained  Ultra Rare  cards. As such, there are more Ultra Rare cards in circulation.
 One promotional card related to this was included in a strategy guide about the game:  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters International: Worldwide Edition Game Guide .
