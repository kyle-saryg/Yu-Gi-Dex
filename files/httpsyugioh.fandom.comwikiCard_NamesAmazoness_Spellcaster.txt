 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (السحر الأمازونيّ) literally  Amazonian Magic , not referring to the  Amazons . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (الأمازونيّة الساحرة) translates to  Magician Amazon . 
 The  Greek  name given translates to " Amazon Spellcaster ".
