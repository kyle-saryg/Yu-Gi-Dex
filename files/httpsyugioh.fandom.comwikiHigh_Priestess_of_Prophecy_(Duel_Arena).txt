 High Priestess of Prophecy  is a character version of the card " High Priestess of Prophecy ". 
 She appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P056.
 In the beta, her partner ID was P024.
 
 Description 
 A  magician  who uses a " Spellbook " packed with magical secrets.
 
 Deck 
 A  Deck  comprised of  Spell Cards  with the "Spellbook"  archetype , and the Spellcaster- Types  who can wield them. Along with the innate effect of "High Priestess of Prophecy", this deck also incorporates a variety of other  Summoning  methods. Make good use of Spellbook cards to bring down your opponent!
