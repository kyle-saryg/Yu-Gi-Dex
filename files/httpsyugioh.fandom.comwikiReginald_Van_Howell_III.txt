 Reginald "Reggie" Van Howell III , known in Japan as  Houzan Gokaido  (五階堂宝山,  Gokaidō Hōzan ) or  "Mr. Elite"  (エリート君,  Elitekun ) is the top-ranking freshman of the second year, who was set up to Duel against  Chazz Princeton  as a challenge to prove if the latter is worthy of returning to  Obelisk Blue . He is commonly called Reggie as it is his nickname. 
 
 Personality 
 Reggie's personality greatly differs from the Japanese and English versions. In the Japanese version, he idolizes Chazz and is respectful, even during the Duel. In the English version, however, he insults Chazz without end, though he does state he formerly admired him when they were in secondary school. Also in the dub, he has a tendency to refer to things such as  Life Points  and  Attack Points  as simply "points", using phrases like "I  pay  800 points" or "Your  monster  has no points."
 
 Deck 
 Reggie plays a  Warrior Deck  that utilizes many different  Equip Spell Cards  to power up his  monsters . It seems to be based directly on  Structure Deck: Warrior's Triumph , since Reggie also uses " Gilford the Legend ".
