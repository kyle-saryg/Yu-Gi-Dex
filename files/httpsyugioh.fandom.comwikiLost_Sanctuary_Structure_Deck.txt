 Lost Sanctuary Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the nineteenth deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Dragunity Legion Structure Deck . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Lost Sanctuary  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 The deck features the  Fairy -Type Theme. It is a  Fairy -Type Deck orientated on utilizing the " Agents "; it includes 5 new cards and many reprints.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Lost Sanctuary Structure Deck  contains: 
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 40 cards
 1  Ultra Rare

 1  Rulebook
