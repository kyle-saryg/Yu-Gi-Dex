 Alexis Rhodes , known as  Asuka Tenjouin  (天上院 明日香,  Tenjōin Asuka ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Alexis Rhodes , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
