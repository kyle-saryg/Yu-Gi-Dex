 Melody , known as  Miho Nosaka  in the Japanese version, is a character in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters . She is the game's incarnation of  Miho Nosaka  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Description 
 A meek and quiet girl and one of  Yugi 's classmates. She is affectionately known as "Ribbon" for her trademark bows.
