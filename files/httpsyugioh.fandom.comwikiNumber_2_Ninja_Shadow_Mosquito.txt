 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2+  Level  2  monsters Cannot be  destroyed by battle . You take no  battle damage  from  battles  involving this card. Your opponent's monsters must  attack  if able. When a monster  declares  an attack: You can  activate  1 of these  effects ; ●  Detach  1  material  from this card, and if you do, place 1  Hallucination Counter  on a  face-up  monster your opponent  controls . (The effects of monsters with a Hallucination Counter are  negated ). ● Inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the  ATK  of 1 monster with a Hallucination Counter.
