 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Single Purchase ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Transmodify ", " Downbeat ", " Painful Escape ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Number 29: Mannequin Cat ", " Sangan ", " Broken Blocker ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Soul Rope ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Dark General Freed ", " Creeping Darkness ", " Chaos Zone ", " Dark Renewal ", " Startime Magician ", " Ebon High Magician ", " Magician Navigation ", " Dark Horizon ", " Lemon Magician Girl ", " The Grand Spellbook Tower ", " Spellbook Star Hall ", " Magician's Circle ", " Arnis, the Empowered Warrior ", " Dragodies, the Empowered Warrior ", " Fusion Reserve ", " Fusion Conscription ", " Ojamassimilation ", and " Magical Meltdown ". 
 This Normal Summoned card can be used as  Link Material  for " Artemis, the Magistus Moon Maiden " or " Salamangreat Almiraj ", allowing for a 1-card  Fusion Summon  (via the searched " Invocation ") of " Invoked Mechaba " or " Invoked Purgatrio ". 
 This card's  Quick Effect  helps  Fusion Monsters  whose effects activate when they destroy a monster by battle or inflict damage (see  Card Tips:Battle Fusion ). 
 This card can be added back to your hand with " Dark Eruption " in order to reuse its discard effect. 
 Use this Normal Summoned card with any other non-DARK, non-Spellcaster monster to  Link Summon  " Aleister the Invoker of Madness ", then use the searched "Invocation" to Fusion Summon an " Invoked " monster by banishing this card and the other monster, as well as trigger the effect of "Aleister the Invoker of Madness".
