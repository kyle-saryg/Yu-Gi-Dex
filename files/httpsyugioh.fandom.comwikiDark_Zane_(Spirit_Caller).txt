 Dark Zane , known as  Ryo Marufuji  (丸藤 亮,  Marufuji Ryō ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Zane Truesdale , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
