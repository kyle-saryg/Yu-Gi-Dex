 Premium Gold: Infinite Gold  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ), following  Premium Gold: Return of the Bling  in the  Gold Series  series.

 Features 
 21  Gold Secret Rare  cards new to the  TCG . 
 79  reprints , including mass releases of " Number 106: Giant Hand " and " Digvorzhak, King of Heavy Industry " in the  TCG  for the first time, previously being highly exclusive  YCS  prize cards. 
 The cards in the set can be organized into the following:
 
 Cards 1-20 are Gold Secret previously- OCG exclusive  imports from  Premium Pack 18 
 Card 21 is a Gold Secret previously- OCG  exclusive import from a  V Jump  promotional card 
 Cards 22-40 are Gold Secret reprints of popular tournament cards, being printed as one of the Gold Rare rarities for the first time 
 Cards 41-100 are Gold Rare reprints of popular tournament cards, being printed as a Gold Rare for the first time 
 Breakdown 
 Each English, Spanish, and Portuguese box of  Premium Gold: Infinite Gold  contains 3 mini-packs of 5 cards each: 2 Gold Secrets and 3 Gold Rares 
 Each French, German, and Italian box of  Premium Gold: Infinite Gold  contains 2 mini-packs of 5 cards each 
 The set includes a total of 100 cards:
 
 40  Gold Secret Rare  cards
