 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament " and " Mermail Abyssmegalo ". 
 This card can be returned to your hand by " Mermail Abyssleed ". 
 This card is effective bait for Spell/Trap removal that can also be triggered by your own cards, including " Scrap Dragon ", " Rokket Tracer ", " Anteatereatingant ", " Double Cyclone " and many others.
 If you do activate this card, its best target is " Mermail Abyssnose ".
