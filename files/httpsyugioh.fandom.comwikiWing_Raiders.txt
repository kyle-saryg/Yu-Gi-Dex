 Wing Raiders  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yuto ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Astral ,  Nash ,  Vector ,  Mr. Heartland , and  Eliphas . 
 Includes members/support for the " Bujin ", " Gagaga ", " Gem-Knight ", " Ghostrick ", " Harpie " " Number ", " Raidraptor ", " Rank-Up-Magic " and " Zubaba " archetypes and the  Forbidden  series. 
 Introduces the " Super Quant " archetype, and the first support for the " Phantom Knights " and " The Phantom Knights " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 9 cards per pack, consisting of 1 Holo card, 1 Rare card and 7 Common cards. The set contains 60 cards including.
