 Structure Deck: Pendulum Domination  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the thirtieth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Master of Pendulum .

 Features 
 This deck is based on the rival  Declan Akaba  and his Pendulum Summoning, but you also get to experience many other different kinds of Summoning methods. 
 Contains reprints of many high utility cards to satisfy the needs of new and old players alike. 
 Includes members/support for the " D/D " and " Dark Contract " archetypes.
