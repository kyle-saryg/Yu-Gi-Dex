      The Dome Duel Arena.     
 The  Dome Duel Arena  was the structure at the top of the first  Kaiba Land .
 
 Structure 
 The dome is in the center of a giant arena at the top of the Kaiba Land building, with the previous  Death-T  stages below it. Two bridges at either side lead into the it.
 Inside the dome, is a table and two chairs. As players play  Duel Monsters  on the table,  Solid Vision  holograms of the cards are projected inside the dome.
 
 History 
 The dome was the arena for the  Yugi Mutou  and  Seto Kaiba 's game of  Duel Monsters  in the final stage of  Death-T . Yugi faced Kaiba as  Dark Yugi , using his  Grandfather 's Deck. Yugi managed to win, using " Exodia " and inflicted the " Mind Crush "  Penalty Game  on Kaiba.
