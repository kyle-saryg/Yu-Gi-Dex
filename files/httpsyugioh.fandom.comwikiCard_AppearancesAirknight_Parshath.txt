 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 In  episode 186 , the  KC DuelTek 760  uses this card during its Duel against  Yami Yugi  while the latter was using  Rick 's  Deck . After the computer activates " Cost Down " to  discard  " Sinister Serpent " and reduce the Levels of all monsters on the computer's hand and field by two until the End Phase, the computer Normal Summons this card without Tribute. This card then attacks and  destroys  Yugi's  Defense Position  " Petit Dragon ". This card then inflicts piercing damage to Yami. The machine then uses this card's effect to  draw a card . Due to the effect of " Robbin' Goblin ", Yami Yugi was forced to discard one random card in his hand. Later " Luster Dragon " (whose ATK was 2200 due to it being equipped with " Dragon Treasure ") attacks and destroys this card. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  episode 49 , this card appears in the display case of the Kame Game shop. Due to the power of the Sacred Beasts, the cards are drained of their energy which two kids notice. All the cards are restored after Jaden defeats Kagemaru. 
 In  episode 75 , this card is shown in the  Kame Game  Display Case at  Solomon Muto 's game shop. 
 Video games 
 Damon ,  Mindy  and  Emperor.Umiuma  use this in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force . 
 Arcana Force 0 - The Fool  and  Sekizato  use this in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . 
 Virgil  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .
