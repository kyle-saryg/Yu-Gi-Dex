 Gagaga Girl  is a character version of the card " Gagaga Girl ". 
 She appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P102. She also appears as an opponent at Stage 7-4 under Quest mode.

 Description 
 A magical girl who works in tandem with " Gagaga Magician " to perform various  Ranks  of  Xyz Summons .
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Once I get with  my friend  we can  Summon  any  Rank  of  Xyz Monster . Top it off with a " Cell Phone Subtraction " and you're done.
 
 Decks 
 An  Xyz   Deck  focused on  monsters  with the " Gagaga "  archetype , capable of making use of their own  effects . The key is getting "Gagaga Magician" and "Gagaga Girl" on the  field  together!

 Trivia 
       The possible inspiration for this partner's pose.     
 Unlike most other  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena  partners whose poses are based on their card artworks, this partner's pose is unique, and possibly inspired from the moments in the  anime  when  its monster counterpart  activated its effect.
