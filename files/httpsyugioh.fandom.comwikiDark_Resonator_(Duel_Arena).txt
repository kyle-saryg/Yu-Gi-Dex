 Dark Resonator  is a character version of the card " Dark Resonator ".
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P089. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 4-5 under Quest mode.
