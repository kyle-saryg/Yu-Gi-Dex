 Jaden Yuki , known as  Judai Yuki  (遊城十代,  Yūki Jūdai ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Jaden Yuki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
