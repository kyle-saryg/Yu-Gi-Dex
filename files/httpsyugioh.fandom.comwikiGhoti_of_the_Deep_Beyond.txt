 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1+  Fish   Tuners  + 1+ non-Tuner  monsters The  original ATK  of this card becomes 500 x the number of  banished  monsters. If this card is  Synchro Summoned  during the opponent's  turn : You can banish all cards on the  field . During the  Standby Phase  of the next turn after this card was banished from the  Monster Zone : You can  Special Summon  this banished card.
