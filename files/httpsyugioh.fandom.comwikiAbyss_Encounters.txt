 Abyss Encounters  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on February 20, 2017, 6:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Gaze into the depths of the abyss. Darkness and terror lie in the realms below. 
 
 Featuring: 
" Water Dragon Cluster ,"  Bastion Misawa 's ace monster is back with new powers! Obtain "Water Dragon Cluster" and its support cards to wash away the competition! 
" Evigishki Levianima ," a Ritual Monster that lets you draw a card every time you declare an attack with it. In addition, if the card you drew was a " Gishki " monster, you will be able to look at a card in your opponent's hand. 
" Archfiend Emperor, the First Lord of Horror ," The mighty king of the Archfiends. This monster will cost you a lot of Life Points, but its powerful effects more than make up for it. 
" Ties of the Brethren ," a card that lets you Special Summon 2 monsters the same Type, Attribute, and Level but with different names. Put it in your Archfiend deck or Harpy Deck to make it even stronger! 
A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Abyss Encounters  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
