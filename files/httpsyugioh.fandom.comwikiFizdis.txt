 Fizdis  is a character appearing in some Yu-Gi-Oh! video games. She has the appearance of an Egyptian servant girl with the same hairstyle as an Egyptian princess or queen.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
       Seto  takes Fizdis captive.     
 Fizdis was a servant in the palace of the Amenhotep dynasty.
 When the palace was under attack, she hurriedly woke  the prince , informed him of  Heishin 's invasion and urged that they escape. However, she was captured by  Seto  and two other  Mages . Seto ordered them to take her away, while he spoke with the prince. She pleaded for help, but was taken away.
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom 
 Fizdis appears at the start of Yugi's campaign as one of his Marshals. She has three monsters to help out the cause of the resistance: " Kanikabuto ", " Horn Imp " and " Penguin Soldier ". Though she doesn't have as much dialogue as the other characters she is still pretty helpful.
