 Valon  (ヴァロン) is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Valon , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
