      Thunder Spark  challenges  Yuma Tsukumo  to a "Speed Duel".     
 A  Speed Duel  (スピードデュエル  Supīdo Dyueru ) is a new type of  Duel  introduced in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga  by  Thunder Spark . It is similar to a  Turbo Duel , but played in the  Speed Field . In a Speed Duel, both duelists Duel in a  Speed Loader  which is similar to a  Duel Runner , but with a most futuristic design [1] . The vehicle is also able to ride over the water. [2] 
 
 Rules 
 Both players begin from the starting line and ride towards the goal. 
 If a player's  Life Points  are reduced to 0, they lose. 
 If a player crashes, they lose. 
 The player who passes the first corner takes the first  turn . 
 The player who is ahead of the opponent can  Normal Summon  twice during their turn.
