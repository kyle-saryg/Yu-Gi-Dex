 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 FLIP : You can  declare  a  Level  from 1 to 9; this card becomes the declared Level until the end of this  turn . During the  Main Phase , if this card was  flipped   face-up  ( Quick Effect ): You can  target  1 other face-up  monster  on the  field ; immediately after this  effect   resolves ,  Synchro Summon  using only that monster and this card. You can only use 1 of these  effects  of "Turbo-Tainted Hot Rod GT19" per  turn , and only  once that turn .
