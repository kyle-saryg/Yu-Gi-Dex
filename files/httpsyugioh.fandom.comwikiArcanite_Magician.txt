 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Magician "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 魔 術 師 」 .   As its English name contains "Magician", it  may  be treated a member of the "Magician" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The  Arabic, Croatian, Greek, Thai  and  Irish                    names given are not official.  ( card names )

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1  Tuner  + 1+ non-Tuner  Spellcaster   monsters If this card is  Synchro Summoned : Place 2  Spell Counters  on it. This card gains 1000  ATK  for each Spell Counter on it. You can remove 1 Spell Counter from your  field , then  target  1 card your opponent  controls ;  destroy  that target.
