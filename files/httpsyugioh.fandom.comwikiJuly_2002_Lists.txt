 The  July 2002 Forbidden and Limited List  was the second Forbidden and Limited list in the  TCG , going into effect on July 1,  2002 . [1]  This list updated the previous  May 2002  list, adding four cards from the  Booster Pack   Metal Raiders , and was followed by the  October 2002 Forbidden and Limited List .
