Main card page: " Cybernetic Hidden Technology " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Cyber "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「サイバー」 .   As its English name contains "Cyber", it  may  be treated a member of the "Cyber" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

   Continuous 
 
 When an opponent's  monster   declares  an  attack , you can  send  1 " Cyber "  Machine - Type  monster you  control  to the  Graveyard  to  destroy  the attacking monster. Then, end the  Battle Phase . You can send this card and 1 "Cyber" Machine-Type monster from your  hand  to the Graveyard to destroy 1  face-up  monster your opponent controls.
