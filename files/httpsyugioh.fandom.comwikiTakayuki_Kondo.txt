 Takayuki Kondo  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He provides the voice of Hiroto Honda ( Tristan Taylor ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) [2] . He was later replaced with  Hidehiro Kikuchi [2]  after  episode 51 .
