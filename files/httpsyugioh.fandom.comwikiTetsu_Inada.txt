 Tetsu Inada  is a is a Japanese voice actor who works for Aoni Production.
 
 Roles 
 In the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime , he voiced  homeroom teacher [1]  and  Jonouchi's father . [2] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ), Inada voiced  Mike . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , he voiced  Hideo Izayoi  (Senator Izinski). [ citation needed ]
