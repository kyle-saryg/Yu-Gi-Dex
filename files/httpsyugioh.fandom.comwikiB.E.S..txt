 " B.E.S. ", short for " Bacterian Empire Ship " as well as known as " Giant Battleship " ( 巨 大 戦 艦   Kyodai Senkan ) in the  OCG , is an  archetype  of  Machine   monsters  with various  Attributes  (primarily  LIGHT  &  EARTH ) based on the bosses of the  Gradius series . They were used by  Lorenzo  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime, as well as by  Nathan  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  games.
 " B.E.S. Big Core " was previously known simply as "Big Core", and since no cards within the archetype have been reprinted since its name was changed all cards that support B.E.S. monsters mention "Big Core" specifically as well.

 Playing style 
 The main strategy of this Deck is to get out the field spell, " B.E.F. Zelos " to give all of your B.E.S monsters blanket protection and turn them all into budget " Kozmo Dark Destroyers ". Once they have multiple copies of " Boss Rush " on the field and the field spell, the deck starts to become a very real threat as the monsters have protection from many  conventional forms  of  removal  and immunity to  targetting , and special summon multiple ships to the board that can be used as either beatsticks or Extra Deck fodder.
