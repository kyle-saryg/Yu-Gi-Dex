 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 
 
 
 Lord Percius , known as  Bergman Sakata  in the Japanese version, is one of the finalists of the  World Duel Carnival  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. He is from Sweden, and is the winner of that country's most recent Championship [1]  Later in the tournament, he formed an alliance with  Pepe Perez  and  Count Crazy Fang  to defeat  Vetrix , but failed. [2] 
 
 Deck 
 Percius plays a  Bountiful Angel Permission Deck . [1]  The only card shown was " Master of Oz ".
