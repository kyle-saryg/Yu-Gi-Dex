 Zane Truesdale , known as  Ryo Marufuji  (丸藤 亮,  Marufuji Ryō ) in the Japanese version, is a mentioned character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. This is an alternate universe version of the  Zane Truesdale  that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
 His name and some data about him was shown on a blackboard in a  Duel Academy  classroom as a part of a lesson detailing the various cards capable of  Fusion Summoning . He is described as a legendary  Duelist  from Duel Academy's past and known to have played a card (possibly " Overload Fusion ") that allows for the Summoning of " Chimeratech Fortress Dragon ", " Chimeratech Rampage Dragon " and " Gatling Dragon ".
