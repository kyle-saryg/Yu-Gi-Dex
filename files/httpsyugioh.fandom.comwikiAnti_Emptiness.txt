 Anti Emptiness 
 
 Japanese:   虚無坑 
 Kana:   きょむこう 
 Romaji:   Kyomukō 
 Translated:  Nihility Hole

 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Activate  only when your opponent activates a  Spell  or  Trap Card  that would  destroy  a Spell or Trap Card on the  field .  Negate  the activation of that card, and destroy it. The  ATK  of 1  face-up   Attack Position  monster you  control  becomes 0 until the  End Phase .
