 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  sent  to the  GY  as  Link Material  for a  Cyberse   monster : You can  Special Summon  1 " Firewall " monster from your  Deck , except "Firewall Defenser". You cannot Special Summon monsters the  turn  you activate this  effect , except Cyberse monsters. If a "Firewall" monster(s) you  control  would be  destroyed  by card effect, you can  banish  this card from your GY instead. You can only use each effect of "Firewall Defenser"  once per turn .
