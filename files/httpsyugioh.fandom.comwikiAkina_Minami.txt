 Akina Minami  is a star from the tv show  Sakiyomi JumBANG! . She and her co-stars  Tetsuya Yanagihara  and  Yoshiyuki Hirai  make a brief appearance in the film  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time .
 
 Character Biography 
 Akina Minami and her co-stars Yoshiyuki Hirai and Tetsuya Yanagihara work as MCs at an event held by Maximillion Pegasus in Bonds Beyond Time.
 Later, three people resembling the trio appear in Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL to promote the World Duel Carnival.
