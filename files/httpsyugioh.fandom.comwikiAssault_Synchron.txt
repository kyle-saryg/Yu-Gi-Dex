 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During your  Main Phase : You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand , then take 700  damage . If  Summoned  this way, you cannot Special Summon  monsters  from the  Extra Deck  while you  control  this monster, except  Synchro Monsters . If a  face-up   Dragon  Synchro Monster(s) you control is  Tributed  or  banished : You can banish this card from your  GY , then  target  1 of those  monsters ; Special Summon it. You can only use each  effect  of "Assault Synchron"  once per turn .
