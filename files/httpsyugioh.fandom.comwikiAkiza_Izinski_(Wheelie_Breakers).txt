 Akiza Izinski , known as  Aki Izayoi  ( 十六夜 アキ,  Izayoi Aki ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Akiza Izinski , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Biography 
 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock " Ivy Wall " by defeating Akiza for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Doppelganger " by defeating Akiza for the second time. 
 The player can unlock " Anti-Spell " by defeating Akiza for the third time. 
 The player can unlock " Gold Sarcophagus " by defeating Akiza for the fourth time. 
 The player can unlock " Begone, Knave! " by defeating Akiza for the fifth time. 
 The player can unlock " Heavy Storm " by defeating Akiza for the sixth time. 
 The player can unlock  Akiza's Duel Runner  by winning the  Gold Cup . 
 The player can unlock Akiza's Decklist by winning the  Platinum Cup .
