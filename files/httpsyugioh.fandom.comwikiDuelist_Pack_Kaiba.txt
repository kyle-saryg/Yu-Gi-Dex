 Duelist Pack: Kaiba  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the eleventh set in the  TCG ' s Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Yusei 2 .
 It was reprinted in the  Silver Dragon Value Box  and  Duelist Pack: Yugi & Kaiba Special Edition  (with updated  Problem-Solving Card Text  on the cards). It was also reprinted in  Yugi's Collector Box , and  Kaiba's Collector Box .

 Features 
 The set features cards used by  Seto Kaiba . It contains the first TCG release of " Malefic Blue-Eyes White Dragon ", reprints of hard to get cards such as " Fiend's Sanctuary ", the  alternate artwork  " La Jinn the Mystical Genie of the Lamp ", the first TCG release of the alternate artwork " XYZ-Dragon Cannon " and " Vorse Raider ". 
 This is the first Duelist Pack to have  Ultimate Rare  cards.
