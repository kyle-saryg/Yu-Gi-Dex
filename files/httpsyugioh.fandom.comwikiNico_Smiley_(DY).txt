 Nico Smiley  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Nico Smiley , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime . He delivers  Reiji Akaba 's invitation for an exhibition  Duel  to  Yuya Sakaki , and afterwards, narrates said exhibition Duel.
