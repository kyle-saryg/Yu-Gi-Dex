 Design 
 Appeareance 
       Full-body image of Nick     
 Nick is portrayed in a Western style, both in physical appearance and attire. He is a fair-skinned boy with blue eyes and caramel-blonde hair topped with a brown cowboy hat. Nick wears a large cream-colored tall-collared shirt with a vest over it which has dark brown shoulders and a dark pink body with tan fringes at the bottom and separating said sections, a navy blue undershirt, a soft chartreuse ascot, light brown chaps over dark brown pants, brown boots with turned-up toes, and a black belt with a gold buckle. He also carries a comically large set of tongs at his belt.
 
 Etymology 
 Nick's full japanese name is a word play on the japanese grilled meat cuisine  Yakiniku .
 Nick dresses in cowboy attire, often associated with the  Western genre .
