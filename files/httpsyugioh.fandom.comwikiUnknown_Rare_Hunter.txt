 Unknown Rare Hunter 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Organization 
 Rare Hunters 
 
 
 
 
 Anime debut  
 Yu-Gi-Oh!   episode 88: " Awakening of Evil, Part 3 " 
 
 
 Anime appearances 
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (second series) 
 
 
 
 He was a member of  Marik Ishtar 's  Rare Hunters  and was chosen to do a trial run with a false copy of " The Winged Dragon of Ra ". His actions, even though committed on someone else's behalf, earned him the anger of God, who, in turn, struck him with lightning, resulting in this man's painful death.
