 Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus , known as  Gem Beast Sapphire Pegasus  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ".

 Appearances 
 Anime 
 He appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  as one of  Jesse Anderson 's Duel Monster Spirits. At one point, he was imprisoned by  Trapper , but Jesse ultimately freed him. When Duel Academy was transported into an alternate dimension, he fought the spirit of " Harpie Lady " and later  her sisters . Later,  Yubel  possessed Jesse and trapped all the  Crystal Beasts  into their  darker versions . While  Jaden  was Dueling Yubel-possessed Jesse, Sapphire Pegasus and the other Crystal Beasts contacted him with help of  Winged Kuriboh  and explained to him how to free them and Jesse from Yubel's influence. He made a reappearance when Jaden was freed from  Sartorius ' curse, telling him that Jesse can't be affected by  Nightshroud 's powers too.
 
 Video games 
 He appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator  as a Single Duel opponent and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus  as a Tag-Team partner for  Dragon Queen of Tragic Endings .
 
 
 Decks 
 Stadust Accelerator 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , Sapphire Pegasus uses a " Crystal Beast " Deck titled, "Duel Jewel". By gathering "Crystal Beasts" in the  Spell & Trap Card Zone , he is able to Summon " Hamon, Lord of Striking Thunder ". The Deck contains no  Trap Cards .
