 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Activate  only by the  effect  of " Darkness/Trap B ". When this card is activated: 1  monster  you  control  gains 4000  ATK . Each time another " Darkness "  Continuous Trap Card  is activated, 1 monster you control gains 4000 ATK. -I activate " Darkness 2 "!-
