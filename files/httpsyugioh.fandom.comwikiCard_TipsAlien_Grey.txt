 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Mimiclay ", " Broken Blocker ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Spirit of the Fall Wind ", " Subterror Behemoth Apparition ", " Shining Angel ", " Rai-Mei ", " Damage = Reptile ", " Snake Whistle ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Oshaleon ", " Flying Saucer Muusik'i ", " Crop Circles ", " "A" Cell Recombination Device ", " King of the Feral Imps ", " Chaos Zone " and " Photon Veil ". 
 It is best to let this card be attacked while face-down so that you can at least place the  A-Counter  on the attacking monster, then this card would be destroyed by battle and you get to draw a card. Follow up with cards that make use of the A-Counter on your opponent's monster, such as " Brainwashing Beam " or " Detonator Circle "A" ". 
 Because this card is  LIGHT   Reptile , " W Nebula Meteorite " can be used at the end of your opponent's turn to flip this card face-up and back down again, then Special Summon " Cosmic Horror Gangi'el " from your Deck to add more A-Counters to opposing face-up monsters during your turn. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Last Will ".
