 Legendary Collection 5D's Mega Pack  is a  Mega-Pack  released with  Legendary Collection 5D's  boxes. 
 As with previous  Legendary Collection  Mega-Pack sets, this set debuted previously  OCG exclusive  cards to the  TCG , with a total of 2: " Mono Synchron " and " Steam Synchron ".

 Features 
 The cards in the set can be broken down as follows:
 
 1 through 56 includes cards used by  Yusei Fudo . 
 57 through 84 includes cards used by  Jack Atlas . 
 85 through 109 includes cards used by  Akiza Izinski . 
 110 through 145 includes cards used by  Crow Hogan . 
 146 through 160 includes cards used by the  Dark Signers . 
 161 through 176 includes cards used by  Aporia . 
 177 through 204 includes cards used by  Team Ragnarok . 
 205 through 221 includes cards used by  Bruno . 
 222 through 256 are miscellaneous cards used by other characters in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime or otherwise related to that era, including cards used by  Leo ,  Luna ,  Tetsu Trudge ,  Sayer ,  Elsworth ,  Greiger ,  Devack ,  Rex Goodwin ,  Roman Goodwin , and  Z-one . 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains:
 
 1  Secret Rare 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 1  Super Rare 
 1  Rare 
 5  Commons 
 In the set, there are:
 
 44  Secret Rares 
 41  Ultra Rares 
 36  Super Rares 
 29  Rares 
 110  Commons 
 The above adds to 260, not 256, because four of the cards in the set appear as both Commons and Ultra Rares.
