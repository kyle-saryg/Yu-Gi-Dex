 This card can be searched by " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Soul Rope ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Snake Whistle ", " Damage = Reptile ", " King of the Feral Imps ", " Mysterious Triangle ", " Flying Saucer Muusik'i ", " Crop Circles ", " "A" Cell Recombination Device ", " Chaos Zone " and " Photon Veil ". 
 " Alien Psychic " and " Cosmic Slicer Zer'oll " can be used in tandem with this card to prevent your opponent's Special Summoned monsters from applying their effects or attacking. 
 While this card's effect is not enough to discourage your opponent from Special Summoning, it can enable various effects that require your opponent's monsters to have an  A-Counter (s), such as " Brainwashing Beam ", " Mass Hypnosis ", "Mysterious Triangle", " Cell Explosion Virus ", " Detonator Circle "A" " and " Interdimensional Warp ".
