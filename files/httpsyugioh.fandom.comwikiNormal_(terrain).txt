Normal terrain is a  terrain  in  Duelists of the Roses  that is primarily created by  Burning Land's  effect and  Yamadron's  effect. If there are Labyrinth squares on the field,  Earthshaker  and  Magical Labyrinth  can change Labyrinth squares to Normal squares. No monsters are powered up or down on this terrain.
