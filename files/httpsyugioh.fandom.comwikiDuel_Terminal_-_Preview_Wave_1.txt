 There were working Duel Terminals at the Comic Con in August, giving out English Language Duel Terminal cards, at the official Konami Booth. They only included the original reprints from the OCG set.
 This was the first wave (DTP1-EN001 to DTP1-EN020) released August 4, 2008.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 20 reprints cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare .
