 Lioside , known as  Kozo Kiba  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 , his name is given to  Hiroshi Kumakura .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 - 6 , his name is given to  Sergeant Aikawa .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 Lioside can be found at the back of the classroom before class begins each day. In  Tag Force 2  and  Tag Force 3 , he cannot be found on the field at any other time.
 
 Tag Force 1 
 During the Tag Force tournament, he teams with  Yumi , and the pair can be found at the  Abandoned Dorm .

 Decks 
 Lioside uses  Ritual Summon  Decks , using " Contract with the Abyss " and " End of the World " to quickly summon powerful  Ritual Monsters . In  Tag Force 2  and  Tag Force 3 , he can also use " Advanced Ritual Art " with  Normal Monsters  as well.
