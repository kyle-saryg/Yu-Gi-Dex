 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 6 promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 6 promotional cards   are a set of 3 cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 6 . The 3 cards; " Sonic Warrior ", " Influence Dragon " and " Crimson Blader ", were all used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
