 [  Warrior  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card can only be  Special Summoned  with " Legend of Heart ". When a  monster  you  control  is  selected  as an  attack target , you can switch the  target  to this card. During either player's  turn , you can  remove from play  1  Effect Monster  from your  Graveyard  to have this card gain that monster's  effects  until the  End Phase . Once per turn, during either player's turn, you can remove from play 3 monsters from your  Deck  to have this card's  ATK  triple each time it is attacked this turn.
