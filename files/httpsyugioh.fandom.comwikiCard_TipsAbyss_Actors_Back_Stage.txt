 This card can be  searched  by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Primathmech Alembertian ", " Trap Trick ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament ", " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope " and " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ". 
 If your opponent would destroy your Set " Abyss Script " cards without destroying your " Abyss Actor "  Pendulum Scales , activate this card to enable the effects of those "Abyss Script" cards that are destroyed while Set. 
 Use this card to set up the effect of " Pendulum Halt ".
