 Chazz Princeton , known as  Jun Manjome  ( 万丈目   準 ,  Manjōme Jun ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Chazz Princeton , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
