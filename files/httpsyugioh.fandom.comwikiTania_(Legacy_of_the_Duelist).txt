 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Tania , known as タニヤ,  Taniya  in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Tania , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
