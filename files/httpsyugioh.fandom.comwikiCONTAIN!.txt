 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
(This card is always treated as a " Rescue-ACE " card.) If you  control  a "Rescue-ACE"  monster :  Target  1  Effect Monster  your opponent controls; it cannot  attack , its  effects  are  negated , also, if you control " Rescue-ACE Hydrant ", it cannot be used as  material  for a  Fusion ,  Synchro ,  Xyz , or  Link Summon . These effects last until the end of this  turn .
