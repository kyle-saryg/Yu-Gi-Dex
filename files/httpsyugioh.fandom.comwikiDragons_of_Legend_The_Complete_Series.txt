 Dragons of Legend: The Complete Series 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Dragons of Legend: The Complete Series  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards from  Dragons of Legend ,  Dragons of Legend 2 , and  Dragons of Legend: Unleashed . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Harpie ", " Jinzo ", " Magician Girl ", and " Prophecy "  archetypes . 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Dragons of Legend: The Complete Series  box contains:
 
 1 of 3  collectible dice  featuring one of the  Legendary Dragons : Timaeus, Critias, or Hermos 
 1 of 7  Secret Rare   variant cards 
 2  Dragons of Legend: The Complete Series  Booster Packs. Each pack contains 1 standard  Ultra Rare , 2 colorful Ultra Rares, and 15  Commons . The set contains:
 30 Ultra Rares 
 113 Commons
