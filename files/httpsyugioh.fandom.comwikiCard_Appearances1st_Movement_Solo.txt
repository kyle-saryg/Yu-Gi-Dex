 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 10 ,  Zuzu Boyle  plays this card during her  Action Duel  against  Julia Krystal , using it to  Special Summon  " Aria the Melodious Diva " from her  hand .

 In  episode 21 , this card is in Zuzu's deck which she drops when pushed aside by  Shay Obsidian . 
 In  episode 77 , this card is in Zuzu's hand during her  Turbo Duel  against  Sergey Volkov .
