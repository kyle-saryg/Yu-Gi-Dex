 Rex Raptor and Weevil Underwood's Duel  was the final Duel of the the  Japanese National Duel Monsters Championship  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . In is based on  a Duel  from the  manga .
 The finalists were  Weevil Underwood  from East Japan and  Rex Raptor  from West Japan, aged 14 and 15 respectively at the time. One of them defeated  Mako Tsunami  in the semifinals.
