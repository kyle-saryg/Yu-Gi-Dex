 Legend of the Dragon  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from December 12, 2012. Each pack costs   99.
 This collection includes Dragon, Fairy and Beast-Warrior monsters as well as many of the most powerful spells.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains at least 9 cards.
 The set contains 112 cards in total.
