 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Téa Gardner , known as  Anzu Mazaki  ( 真崎 杏子 ,  Mazaki Anzu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Téa Gardner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
