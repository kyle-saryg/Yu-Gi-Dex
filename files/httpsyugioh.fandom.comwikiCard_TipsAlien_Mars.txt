 Names 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Damage = Reptile ", " UFO Turtle ", " Sphere of Chaos ",  Vampire Dragon ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Snake Whistle ", " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Horse Prince ", " King of the Feral Imps ", " Flying Saucer Muusik'i ", " Crop Circles " and " "A" Cell Recombination Device ". 
 This card is generally inferior to " Cosmic Slicer Zer'oll " except for the fact that "Zer'oll" still allows  Continuous Effects  of monsters with an  A-Counter (s) to apply. 
 " Planet Pollutant Virus " and " Alien Kid " both put A-Counters on monsters your opponent Special Summons. While either card's effect is active, you can chain a  Spell Speed 2  effect to Special Summon this card, should any of their Special Summoned monsters attempt to activate their effects. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Last Will ".
