 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 This card can be  searched  by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Freed the Matchless General ", " Field-Commander Rahz ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Sphere of Chaos ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " UFO Turtle ", " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Horse Prince ", " Red Sparrow Summoner ", " Dragodies, the Empowered Warrior ",  Reinforcement of the Army " and the " Igknight " monsters " Cavalier ", " Crusader ", " Gallant ", " Margrave ", " Paladin ", " Squire ", " Templar " and " Veteran ". 
 Note that this card's effect won't negate the damage inflicted, and doesn't care whether you or your opponent activated the effect, making it a good card for  Burn Decks . 
 Level 3 monsters that can combo with this card for a  Rank 3   Xyz Summon  include " Achacha Archer ", " Ancient Gear Hunting Hound ", " Fairy Archer ", " Gravekeeper's Curse ", " Gallis the Star Beast " (if its effect succeeds), and, if they were summoned on a previous turn, " Bowganian ", " Des Koala ", " Stealth Bird ", " Self-Destruct Ant " or " Princess of Tsurugi ".
