 [  Beast  /  Effect  ] When this card is  Summoned , you can place 1 " Crystal Beast "  monster  from your  hand ,  Deck , or  Graveyard   face-up  in your  Spell & Trap Card Zone . When this card is  destroyed , you can place it face-up in your Spell & Trap Zone, instead of  sending  it to the Graveyard. While this card is in the Spell & Trap Zone, it is treated as a  Continuous Spell Card .
