 Duelist Pack: Yusei 3  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the tenth set in the  OCG ' s  Duelist Pack  series, following  Duelist Pack: Yusei 2 . In the  TCG , it is the twelfth set, following  Duelist Pack: Kaiba .
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Duelist Pack: Yusei 3  was also released as part of  Duelist Pack: Yusei 3 Special Edition .

 Features 
 The set features 8 new cards used by the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  main protagonist  Yusei Fudo  in the anime.
 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Yusei 3  contains 5 cards and each box contains 15 packs. In the  TCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Yusei 3  contains 5 cards and each box contains 36 packs. There are 30 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 2  Ultra Rares 
 4  Super Rares
