 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Warrior "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ウォリアー」 .   As its English name contains "Warrior", it  may  be treated a member of the "Warrior" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
This  monster  cannot be  Special Summoned . When this card  destroys  a monster as a  result of battle  and  sends  it to the  Graveyard , inflict  damage  to your opponent's  Life Points  equal to the  ATK  of the destroyed monster. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Portuguese 
 
 
 Este card não pode ser Invocado por Invocação-Especial. Quando este card destrói um monstro como resultado de um combate e o envia para o Cemitério, inflija dano aos Life Points do seu adversário igual ao ATK do monstro destruído.
