 This Link Summoned card gains  ATK  equal to the combined  ATK  of all " Allure Queen "  monsters  it  points to .  Once per turn , when your opponent activates a card or effect that targets an "Allure Queen" monster(s) you control, or when your "Allure Queen" monster is targeted for an  attack , while " Allure Palace " is in a  Field Zone  (Quick Effect): You can make that monster unable to be destroyed by  battle  or card effects this turn, then you can  destroy  1 monster your opponent controls.
