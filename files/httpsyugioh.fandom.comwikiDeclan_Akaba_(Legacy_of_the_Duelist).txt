 Declan , known as  Reiji  in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist   and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Declan Akaba , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
