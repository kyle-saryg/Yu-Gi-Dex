 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is in your  hand ,  field , or  GY : You can  target  1 " Springans "  Xyz Monster  you  control ;  attach  this card to it as  material . You can  banish  this card and 1 "Springans"  monster  with a different name from your GY;  add  1 "Springans" card from your  Deck  to your hand. You can only use each  effect  of "Springans Branga"  once per turn .
