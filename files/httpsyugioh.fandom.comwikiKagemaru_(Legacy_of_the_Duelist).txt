 Kagemaru  (影丸)  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of the young  Kagemaru , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
