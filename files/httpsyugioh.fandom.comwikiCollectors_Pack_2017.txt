 Collectors Pack 2017  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Noah Kaiba ,  Rex Raptor ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Yubel ,  Z-one ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Gong Strong ,  Declan Akaba ,  Sylvio Sawatari ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Moon Shadow , Aster Phoenix ( GX  manga  and  ARC-V  anime  versions),  Gloria Tyler ,  Grace Tyler , the  Battle Beast , and  Ren . 
 Includes members/support for the " Abyss Actor ", " Abyss Script ", " Amazoness ", " D/D " " D/D/D ", " Destiny HERO ", " Gladiator Beast ", " Magician ", " HERO ", " Ninja ", " Number ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Performapal ", " Raidraptor ", " Superheavy Samurai ", " Timelord ", " Vision HERO "  archetypes , as well as the  Sacred Beast  and  Shark   series . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack:
 
 15 packs per Japanese box. 
 30 packs per Korean box. 
 The set contains 45 cards, including:
 
 4  Ultra Rares 
 5  Super Rares 
 10  Rares 
 26  Commons 
 In the Japanese version all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards come in  Collector's Rare . 
 In the Korean version all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards come in  Secret Rare .
