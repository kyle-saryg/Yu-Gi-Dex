 X-Saber Urbellum  is a character version of the card " X-Saber Urbellum ". 
 He appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P040.
 In the beta, he had a default of 5 Rental Points, and his partner ID was P021.

 Description 
 One of the  ten warriors  who fight with legendary swords, trained by the  Swordmaster .
 
 Decks 
 A  Synchro   Deck  based around  EARTH   monsters  with the " X-Saber "  archetype . Develop the "X-Saber" cards from " XX-Saber Boggart Knight ". Once you have the  Tuners , hit the Synchro Summons. You can also aim for a  Xyz Summons  [ sic ]  using two  Level  3 monsters!
