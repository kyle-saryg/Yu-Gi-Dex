Link: https://yugioh.fandom.com/wiki/Property:Effect_types

This property lists all effect types of cards. Unlike  Property:Effect type , it also lists duplicate effect types (e.g. "Summon, Continuous, Continuous" would be listed as that instead of just "Summon, Continuous"). It has type  text .
