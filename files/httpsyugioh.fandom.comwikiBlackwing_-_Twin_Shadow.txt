 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  control  2 or more " Blackwing "  monsters , you can  activate  this card from your  hand .  Return  2 of your "Blackwing" monsters that are  banished  and/or in your  GY  to the  Deck  and/or  Extra Deck  (1  Tuner  and 1 non-Tuner);  Special Summon  1 "Blackwing"  Synchro Monster  or " Black-Winged Dragon " from your Extra Deck whose  Level  exactly equals the total Level of those monsters. (This is treated as a  Synchro Summon .) You can only  activate  1 "Blackwing - Twin Shadow" per  turn .
