 Deck thinner  refers to a card that reduces the number of cards in your  Deck  so you have a greater chance of  drawing  what you need (At the risk of  Decking out  faster, especially against  mill  Decks). Most Deck thinners are also  searchers . In many cases, Deck Thinners also serve as easy Siders for Deck Siding. Examples of deck thinners include:
