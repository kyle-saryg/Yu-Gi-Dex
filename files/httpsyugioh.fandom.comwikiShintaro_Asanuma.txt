 Shintaro Asanuma  is a Japanese voice actor who provides the voice of  Crow Hogan  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's [2] . He reprises the role of Crow in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V [3] , voicing an  alternate universe version  of the character.
