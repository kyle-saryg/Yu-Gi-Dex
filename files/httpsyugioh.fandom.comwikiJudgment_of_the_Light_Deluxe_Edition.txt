 Judgment of the Light: Deluxe Edition  is a Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game deluxe edition box. It is the first in the  Deluxe Edition  series.

 Breakdown 
 Each  Judgment of the Light: Deluxe Edition  is comprised of:
 
 9  Judgment of the Light   Unlimited Edition  Booster Packs 
 2  Ultra Rare  versions of  Common  or  Rare  cards from  Judgment of the Light 
 1 pack of 50  card sleeves 
 1 of 2  Super Rare  preview cards from  Shadow Specters
