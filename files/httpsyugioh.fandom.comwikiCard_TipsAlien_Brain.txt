 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament " and " Flying Saucer Muusik'i ". 
 You can tribute the stolen monster to Tribute Summon " Spawn Alligator " and get the stolen monster back on your side of the field during the End Phase. 
 You can also Tribute Summon " Dark Alligator " using the stolen monster as the single Tribute.
