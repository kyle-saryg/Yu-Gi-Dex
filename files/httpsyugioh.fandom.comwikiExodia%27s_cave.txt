Exodia's Cave  is a cavern in  Duel Monsters Spirit World  that once contained a sealed  Exodia . In the events of  GX , Exodia was released from his containment through the use of a human sacrifice and used in battle, however, Exodia's controller lost a Duel and vanished along with Exodia's card
