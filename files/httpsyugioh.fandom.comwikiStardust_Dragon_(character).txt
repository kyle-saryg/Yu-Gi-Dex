 Stardust Dragon  is one of the servants of the  Crimson Dragon , commonly referred to as  Signer Dragons .
 Yusei Fudo  is the  Signer  associated with Stardust Dragon and as such possesses the " Stardust Dragon " card.
 Stardust Dragon also appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , as a Single Duel opponent. He also appears as a Tag Duel opponent, alongside  Dark Paladin  in a team called  Fusion & Synchro .
 
 Anime biography 
 Stardust served the Crimson Dragon and fought alongside him and the other Dragons in the ancient battle against the  Earthbound Immortals .
He was also with the Crimson Dragon and the other Signer Dragons in the battle against  Red Nova .
 
 
 Deck 
 Stardust Dragon uses a Deck titled "Stardust Shooting".
