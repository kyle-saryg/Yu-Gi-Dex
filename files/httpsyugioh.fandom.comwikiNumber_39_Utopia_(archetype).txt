 " Number 39: Utopia ", known as " Numbers 39: King of Wishes, Hope " ( Ｎｏ． ３９  希 望 皇 ホープ  Nanbāzu Sanjūkyū Kibō'ō Hōpu ) in Japan, is an anime-only sub- archetype  of the " Number ", " Utopia " and " Utopic " archetypes. The cards in this archetype are used by  Yuma Tsukumo  and  Astral  as their  signature cards .
 This archetype is solely supported by the anime effect of " ZW - Sylphid Wing ". The archetype's counterpart in the  TCG / OCG  is, for all intents and purposes, " Utopia ". The only difference between the two is " Number 39: Utopia Beyond ", which belongs to the "Utopia" archetype but not this one.
