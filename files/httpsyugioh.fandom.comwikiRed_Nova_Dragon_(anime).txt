 [  Dragon  /  Synchro  /  Effect  ] 
 2  Tuners  + " Red Dragon Archfiend " 
 This card gains 500  ATK  for each Tuner monster in your  Graveyard . This card cannot be  destroyed  by the  effects  of your opponent's  Spells ,  Traps , or  monsters . Once per opponent's  turn , you can  remove this card from play . If you do, you can  negate  1  attack  this turn from a monster your opponent  controls . During the  End Phase ,  return  this card that was removed by this effect to the  field .
