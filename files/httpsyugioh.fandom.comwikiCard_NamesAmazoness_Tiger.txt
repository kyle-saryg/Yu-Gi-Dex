 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (النمرة الأمازونيّة) literally  Amazonian Tigeress  or  Tigeress of the Amazon . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (الأمازونيّة النمرة) translates to  Tigeress Amazon . 
 The  Greek  name given translates to " Amazons' Tiger ".
