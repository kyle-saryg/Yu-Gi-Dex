 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> When this card is  sent  to the  Graveyard , you can  activate  its  effect . You can then  equip  it to a  monster  on the  field , and this card will be treated as an  Equip Spell Card  that decreases the  ATK  of the equipped monster by 500 points.
