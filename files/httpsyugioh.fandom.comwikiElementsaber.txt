 " Elementsaber " (エレメントセイバー  Erementoseibā ) is an  archetype  of  Warrior   monsters  debuting in  Flames of Destruction . It is a sub-archetype of the " Saber " archetype, and closely linked to the " Elemental Lord " archetype.

 Appearance 
 Design 
 The "Elementsaber" monsters released so far are shown to be humans wearing an armor with similar design to the "Elemental Lord" of their respective elements.

 Playing style 
 Elementsaber monsters focus on sending each other to the Graveyard and manipulating their Elements to easily fulfill the Elemental Lord's Summoning Condition.  Malo  and  Palace of the Elemental Lords  will quickly send the other Elementsaber monsters to the Graveyard.  Makani  and  Nalu  then adds the key monster to hand, such as  Moulinglacia  to disrupt your opponent's hand or  Phosphorage  to wipe all their monsters.  Lapauila Mana  provides the Elemental Lords defensive power, depending on which monsters were send to the Graveyard to activate its effect. If properly Summoned,  Aina  can bring back an Elemental Lord monster, recycling its effect once more, or another Elementsaber monster for defensive effect.
 Because of their ability to change Element in Graveyard, they can also be used to fulfill many cards' condition such as  Salvage  can be used to quickly return any Elementsaber monsters with 1500 Atk or lower back to hand.
