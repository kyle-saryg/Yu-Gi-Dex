 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Loggy uses a Deck packed with cards based on masks, many of which were released in  Labyrinth of Nightmare .
 In later installments, Loggy focuses more on the summon of " The Masked Beast " and on  Normal Monster  support.
