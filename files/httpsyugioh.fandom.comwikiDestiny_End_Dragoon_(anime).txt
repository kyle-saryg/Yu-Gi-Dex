 This card cannot be  destroyed  by  card effects . You can  destroy  1  monster  your opponent  controls  and inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to its  ATK . If you  activate  this effect, monsters you control cannot  battle  this turn. During your  Standby Phase , if this card is in your  Graveyard , you can  remove from play  1 " Destiny Hero " card from your Graveyard to  Special Summon  this card.
