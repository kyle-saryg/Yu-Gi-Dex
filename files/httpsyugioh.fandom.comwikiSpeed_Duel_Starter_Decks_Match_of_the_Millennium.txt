 Match of the Millennium 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Speed Duel Starter Decks: Match of the Millennium  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Twisted Nightmares , it is the 4th set of Decks in the TCG's  Speed Duel Starter Decks  series, following  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Ultimate Predators .

 Each  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Match of the Millennium  contains:
 
 1 31-Card  Yugi  Deck 
 1 31-Card  Pegasus  Deck 
 4  Skill Cards  (2 for Yugi and 2 for Pegasus) 
 2  Ultra Rare   variant cards
