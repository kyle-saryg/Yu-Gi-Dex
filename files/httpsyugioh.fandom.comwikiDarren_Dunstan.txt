 Darren Timothy Dunstan  is a Canadian born voice actor and voice director based in New York City. He has provided voice-over and voice direction on several properties of  4Kids Entertainment .

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Dunstan's first and most notable role was as  Maximillion Pegasus [1]  in the English dub version of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  second series  anime. He also voiced  Witty Phantom  and  Funny Bunny .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , Dunstan reprises his role as Pegasus on a number of occasions. He also provides the voices of  Adrian Gecko  and  Don Zaloog [1] . 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , Dunstan voices  Andre .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , Dunstan again voices Pegasus.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Dunstan provides the voices of  Mr. Kay  and  Number 96's holder  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Dunstan provides the voice for  Jean-Michel Roget [1] . 
 
 Trivia 
 In the international version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V - Episode 002 , Darren Dunstan is listed in  Yuya 's Duel list in the Leo Corporation data.
