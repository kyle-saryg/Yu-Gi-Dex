 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] You cannot  Normal Summon  or  Flip Summon  this card unless you have an  Archfiend   Monster Card  on your side of the  field . The  controller  of this card  pays  800  Life Points  during each of his/her  Standby Phases  (this is not optional). When this card is specifically designated as a  target  of the  effect  of a card controlled by your opponent, when  resolving  the effect, roll a six-sided die. If the result is 2 or 5,  negate  the effect and  destroy  the opponent's card. Also negate the effect of an  Effect Monster  that is destroyed by this monster  in battle .
