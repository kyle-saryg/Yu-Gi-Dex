 Abidos the Third  (アビドス３世,  Abidosu San-sei ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Abidos the Third , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 In  Tag Force , after  Don Zaloog  is defeated, Abidos the Third appears partnering with a brainwashed  Syrus Truesdale . After beating him, Abidos will appear in Part 1 after 10:00 P.M. in the Academy's Main Gate.
 
 Decks 
 Abidos uses  Zombie  Decks  revolving around " Spirit of the Pharaoh ".
