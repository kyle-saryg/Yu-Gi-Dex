 Lore
 
 
 The Harpie Lady 2 that was duplicated by the " Elegant Egotist ". 
 Rule 
 
 
 
 ~Special Rule~ 
 When this card is played simultaneously with " Elegant Egotist ", it becomes an  Equip Card  for a " Harpie Lady " in play, increasing its  Attack  and  Defense Strength  + 500.
