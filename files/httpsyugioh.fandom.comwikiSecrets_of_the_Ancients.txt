 Secrets of the Ancients  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on December 7, 2018, 8:00 AM (EST).
 
 Description  
 Many secrets lie within "Ancient Fairy Dragon"! Take control of the field with its forbidden power! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Ancient Fairy Dragon ",  Luna's  Ace monster "Ancient Fairy Dragon" is here! "Ancient Fairy Dragon" can Special Summon a Level 4 or lower monster from your hand at the expense of your Battle Phase. In addition, you can destroy all Field Spell Cards and add a Field Spell Card from your Deck to your hand! 
" Junk Synchron ", When this Tuner monster is Summoned, you can Summon a Level 2 or below monster in Defense Position from your Graveyard. Use this effect to Synchro Summon cards such as " Junk Warrior  and " Armades, Keeper of Boundaries "! 
" Delta Flyer ", A Tuner Monster that can increase the level of another monster you control by 1! It's Level 3, so if you control a Level 4 monster, you can Synchro Summon either a Level 7 or 8 monster! 
" Trident Warrior ", A monster that lets you Special Summon a Level 3 monster from your hand when Normal Summoned! If you choose to Special Summon a Tuner, you're all ready for a Level 7 Synchro Summon! 
A total of 40 types of Cards: 3  UR , 10  SR , 17  R , and 20  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Secrets of the Ancients  contains 100 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 9 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 10 copies of each instead: " Chain Dog ", " Valerifawn, Mystical Beast of the Forest ", " Self-Mummification ", " Summon Breaker ", and " Battle Instinct ".
