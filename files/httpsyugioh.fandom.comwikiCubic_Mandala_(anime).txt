 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Special Summon  all  monsters  with  Cubic Counters  that were  destroyed  during this  turn  to your opponent’s  field  with 0  ATK , and if you do, place 1 Cubic Counter on each of them. A monster with Cubic Counter has its  effects   negated , and it cannot  attack . If a monster Special Summoned by this effect is on your opponent's field, negate all monster effects in your opponent’s field,  hand , and  Graveyard . When all monsters Special Summoned by this card’s effect  leaves the field , destroy this card. [1] [2] 
 
 4K Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English card uses the same lore as the OCG/TCG card. The Japanese card and reference sources state the effect differently.):</mobile> If you  control  a " Cubic "  monster :  Activate  this card by  targeting  any number of monsters that are in your opponent's  Graveyard  because they were  destroyed  and  sent  there this  turn :  Special Summon  them to your opponent's  field , but each becomes  ATK  0 and has 1  Cubic Counter  placed on it (Monsters with a Cubic Counter cannot  attack , also  negate  their  effects ). While your opponent controls any of the monsters Special Summoned by this effect, negate any monster effects your opponent activates. When the last of those monsters  leaves the field , destroy this card.
