 Duelist Saga 
 Duel Power 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duelist Saga  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).
 This set introduces a new, revamped  Ultra Rare  foil which gives off horizontal reflections when moving the card around, with a shine similar to  Parallel Rares .

 Features 
 Includes 42 brand new cards and 58 reprints from the first five  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series:  Duel Monsters ,  GX ,  5D's ,  ZEXAL , and  ARC-V . 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Yami Yugi ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Téa Gardner ,  Yami Bakura ,  Mai Valentine ,  Alister ,  Espa Roba ,  Jinzo ,  Leichter ,  Leon von Schroeder ,  Rafael ,  Rebecca Hawkins ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Yubel ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Midori Hibiki ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Daigo Sorano ,  Dimitri ,  Doctor Collector ,  Gravekeeper's Chief ,  Kagemaru ,  Makoto Inotsume ,  Marcel Bonaparte ,  Mindy ,  Princess Rose ,  Trueman ,  Yusuke Fujiwara ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Crow Hogan ,  Rex Goodwin ,  Bolt Tanner ,  Tetsu Trudge ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Reginald Kastle ,  Rio Kastle ,  Vector ,  Roku ,  Thunder Spark ,  Kyoji Yagumo ,  Yuya Sakaki  and  Dipper O'rion . 
 Includes members/support for the " Black Luster Soldier ", " Blackwing ", " Blue-Eyes ", " Chaos ", " Constellar ", " Cyber ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Darklord ", " Darkness ", " Dark Magician ", " Elemental HERO ", " Evolzar ", " Frog ", " Fusion ", " Gladiator Beast ", " Guardian ", " Harpie ", " HERO ", " Ice Barrier ", " Infernity ", " lswarm ", " Jinzo ", " Junk ", " Lightsworn ", " Magician ", " Masked HERO ", " Necrovalley ", " Neos ", " Neo-Spacian ", " Number ", " Penguin ", " Polymerization ", " Red-Eyes ", " roid ", " Saber ", " Skyblaster ", " Stardust ", " Synchron " and " X-Saber " archetypes; as well as the  Armed Dragon ,  Chimeratech ,  Cosmic Synchro Monster ,  Dark counterpart ,  D.D. ,  Full Armor ,  Goyo ,  Greed ,  metal counterpart ,  Mirror Trap ,  Rescue Squad ,  Royal ,  Sacred Beast ,  signature move ,  Signer Dragon ,  Solemn ,  Synchro , and  Vision HERO  series.
 Introduces the first support for the " Skyscraper " archetype to the  TCG .
