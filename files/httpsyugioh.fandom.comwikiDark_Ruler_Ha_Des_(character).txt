 Dark Ruler Ha Des  is a character version of the card, " Dark Ruler Ha Des ". He appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .
 
 Deck 
 Dark Ruler Ha Des uses a Deck titled "Writhing in the Darkness" and "Lively Skill Drain".
