 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 
 
 
 Original Tournament  was a tournament featured in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
 Maximillion Pegasus  attended the tournament in Europe (Southern United States in the dub) a few years before  Jaden Yuki 's third year in  the academy . As soon as  Jesse Anderson  started to Duel, the " Crystal Beast " cards in Pegasus' briefcase began to shine. Understanding they chose Jesse as a master, Pegasus gifted them to Jesse.
