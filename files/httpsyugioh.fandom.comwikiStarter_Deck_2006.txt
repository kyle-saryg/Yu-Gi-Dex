 Starter Deck 2006  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the first deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series. In the  TCG , it is the first deck in the  TCG ' s annual Starter Deck series (seventh overall, following  Starter Deck: Yugi Evolution  and  Starter Deck: Kaiba Evolution ) . It is also the default set of cards players of the video game  Yugioh: World Championship 2006  begin with along with their choice of one of the first six structure decks.
