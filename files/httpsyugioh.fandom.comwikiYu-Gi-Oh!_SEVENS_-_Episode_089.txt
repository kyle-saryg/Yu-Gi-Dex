 " Tiger & Dragon " is the eighty-ninth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on March 6, 2022.
 Tiger  tells  Yuga  that he's just a useless grade-schooler since he couldn't figure out how to amplify the Pauli effect. Angered by what Tiger said,  Luke  challenges his ultra-strong sister to a Duel. Accompanied by a mysterious light, the fierce Duel between the Kamijo siblings ends in a way no one could have expected!
 
 Featured Duel: Lucidien "Luke" Kallister VS. Haruka "Tiger" Kamijo 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
