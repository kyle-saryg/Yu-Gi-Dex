 Anime 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Ninjitsu Art of Transformation " and " Swallow's Nest ". 
 This card's summon is easily enabled by " DNA Transplant ", " Ojama Trio ", " Nightmare Archfiends " or " Revival Gift ". 
 Use this card's effect to "turn off" a  floodgate  card hindering your plays, such as " Mask of Restrict " or " Imperial Iron Wall ". Then, if your opponent controls the floodgate, use this card as  Tribute fodder  or material for a monster that can remove the "turned off" card for good. 
 Even if you negate this card, you cannot control more than 1 "Alector, Sovereign of Birds", because a  Field-Unique  condition is not an effect. 
 Traditional Format 
 You can use this card's effect to negate the effect of " Royal Oppression ", Special Summon any Monster you want and, after the End Phase, you will be able to use "Royal Oppression"'s effect against your opponent's Special Summons.
