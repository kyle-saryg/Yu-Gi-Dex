 Kagemaru  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video games. This is a video game depiction of  Kagemaru , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Kagemaru is the final boss of the game, confronted at the Finals Arena after defeating all the Shadow Riders. The player must defeat him two times in a row, without editing or changing Decks, in order to finish Part 3.
 
 Tag Force 3 
 In the first event of Excited  Bastion 's story, Kagemaru and  Titan  Tag Duel against the player and Bastion.
 
 Decks 
 Kagemaru uses Decks revolving around the  Sacred Beasts .
