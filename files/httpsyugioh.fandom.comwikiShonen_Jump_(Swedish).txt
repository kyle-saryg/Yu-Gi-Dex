 Shonen Jump  is a Swedish language version of  Weekly Shōnen Jump . It is a sister publication to Manga Media AB's and Bonnier Carlsen's magazines Manga Mania and Shojo Stars.
 Manga Media AB was a joint venture company between Bonnier Carlsen & Schibsted.
 The magazine included chapters from various popular  Weekly Shōnen Jump  titles including  Rurouni Kenshin ,  Bleach ,  Naruto ,  Shaman King , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! . In January 2007, Bonnier was unable to renew its license with Shueisha for the magazine and had to cease publication.
 
 Promotional cards 
 The  JMP-EN001  and  JMP-EN002  " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " and " Red-Eyes Black Dragon " were released as promotional cards with this magazine.
