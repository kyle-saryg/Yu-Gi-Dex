 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] The  controller  of this card  pays  500  Life Points  during each of his/her  Standby Phases  (this is not optional). When this card is specifically designated as a  target  of the  effect  of a card controlled by your opponent, when  resolving  the effect, roll a six-sided die. If the result is 3,  negate  the effect and  destroy  the opponent's card. When a " Terrorking Archfiend " on your side of the  field  is destroyed and  sent  to the  Graveyard , you can send this card from your  hand  to the Graveyard to  Special Summon  the "Terrorking Archfiend".
