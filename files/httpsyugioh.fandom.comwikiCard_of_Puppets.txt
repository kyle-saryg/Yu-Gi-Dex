 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Puppet "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「パペット」 .   As its English name contains "Puppet", it  may  be treated a member of the "Puppet" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
