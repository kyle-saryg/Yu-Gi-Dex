 Booster SP: Tribe Force  is a Special  Booster  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). This pack will let you make a bunch of Deck Themes! It’s the latest type of Pack, which specializes in building specific decks! The included themes are 3 new themes:  Pendulum ,  Ritual  and  Fusion -based, and  Synchro Monsters  for an existing Deck Type.

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Sylvio Sawatari  and  Crow Hogan . 
 Introduces the " Nekroz ", " Ritual Beast " and " Yosenju " archetypes to the OCG. 
 Contains members/support for the " Blackwing " archetype.
