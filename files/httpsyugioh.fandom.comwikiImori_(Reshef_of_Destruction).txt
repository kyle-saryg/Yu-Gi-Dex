 Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction 
 
 
 
 Imori  (井守  Imori ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction . He is the game's incarnation of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  character,  Imori .
 The player can find Imori at the Egypt exhibition at the beginning of the game. He disappears after the player defeats the first  Millennium Items Keeper .
