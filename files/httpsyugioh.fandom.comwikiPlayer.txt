A  player  is a participant in a game and may refer to any of the following:
 
 Duelist , a player of  Duel Monsters  or the real world card or video games based on it 
 Player ( BAM ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 Player ( Tag Force ) , the character controlled by the player of the  Tag Force  games 
 Player ( World Duel Carnival ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival 
 Player ( WC08 ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 
 Player ( WC09 ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator 
 Player ( WC10 ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia 
 Player ( WC11 ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus 
 Player ( YDT1 ) , the character controlled by the player of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Duel Transer
