 Jean Michel Roget , known as (ジャン・ミシェル・ロジェ  Jan Misheru Roje ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Jean-Michel Roget , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
