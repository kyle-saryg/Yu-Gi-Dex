 " A Yell of Thirst " ( 渇 いた 叫 び,  Kawaita Sakebi ) is the Japanese opening theme to the  Toei Yu-Gi-Oh! anime . The song is performed by a Japanese group called  Field of View . It debuted in Japan on April 4, 1998 and was the opening theme for all the 27 episodes. It was also used for the  movie  as the opening theme.

 Trivia 
 Two versions of this song exist in TV Size. The first version is for the first 3 episodes of the anime and the movie, while the second version is used by subsequent episodes.
