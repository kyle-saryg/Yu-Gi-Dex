 " Exosister "(エクソシスター) is an  archetype  introduced in  Deck Build Pack: Grand Creators . The archetype is composed of  LIGHT   Spellcaster   monsters  and LIGHT  Warrior   Xyz Monsters .
 
 Trivia 
 This archetype's name is a portmanteau of "exorcist" and "sister".
