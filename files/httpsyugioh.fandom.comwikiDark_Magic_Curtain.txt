 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Pay  half your  LP ;  Special Summon  1 " Dark Magician " from your  Deck . You cannot  Summon  other  monsters  the  turn  you  activate  this card (but you can  Set ).

   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
When this card is activated, you cannot summon any monster in the same turn (including Flip Summon and Special Summon). You can Special Summon 1 "Dark Magician" from your Deck at the cost of half your Life Points. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Ultimate Masters: World Championship Tournament 2006 
 
 
When this card is  activated , you cannot  summon  any  monster  in the same  turn  (including  Flip Summon  and  Special Summon ). You can  Special Summon  1 " Dark Magician " from your  Deck  at the  cost  of half your  Life Points .
