 Structure Deck: Revival of the Great Dragon  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the thirteenth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-first including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Curse of Darkness . The  TCG  equivalent of this Deck is  Rise of the Dragon Lords Structure Deck .

 Features 
 Until recently,  Structure Decks  have been limited to only utilize either  Attribute  or  Type . The new  Deck  consists of theme and strategy. It focuses on  Special Summoning   Dragon - Type   monsters  from the  Graveyard . The deck features 5 new cards.
