 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
       Disa, in  Tag Force 1-3 .     
 In Tag Force 1-3, she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 Disa is  Masha 's older sister, and they team up during the Tag Force tournament in  Tag Force  and the Obelisk Blue Tournament in  Tag Force 2  and  3 . The player also faces them in a Tag Duel during the seventh event of  Tyranno 's story.
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
       Disa, in  Tag Force 4 .     
 Disa can be found in the City's Coastal Region, mentioning she has a job that she is not particularily fond of.

 "Emperor's Team 4"  is only used by Disa during the story event that features her.
