 Kuriboh  is a character version of the card " Kuriboh ".
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P062.
 
 Description 
 The  Duel Monsters  mascot, small but surprisingly reliable.
 
 Deck 
 A  Deck  all set with "Kuriboh" and the cards to support it. The " Kuriboh Tokens " that can be  Special Summoned  using " Multiply " can then be used for a variety of different purposes, including the Special Summons of High  Level   Monsters  and  Activation  of  Spell  &  Trap Cards !
