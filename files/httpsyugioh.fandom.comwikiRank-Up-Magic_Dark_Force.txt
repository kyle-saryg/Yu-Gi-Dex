 Condition 
 When an  Xyz Monster  you  control   destroys  an opponent's  monster   by battle :  Target  that  face-up  Xyz Monster you control;  send  that target to the  Graveyard , then  Special Summon  from your  Extra Deck , 1 Xyz Monster that is 1  Rank  higher than the sent monster, in face-up  Attack Position , also Special Summon from your Extra Deck, 1 Xyz Monster that is 2 Ranks higher than the sent monster, in face-up  Defense Position . Their  effects  are  negated . (This Special Summon is treated as an  Xyz Summon .)
