 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Single Purchase ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Sangan ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon ", " Supreme King Dragon Odd-Eyes ", " Rescue Hamster ", " Pendulum Rising ", " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Luster Pendulum, the Dracoslayer ", " Magical Abductor ", " Majester Paladin, the Ascending Dracoslayer ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Soul Rope ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Chaos Zone ", " Dark General Freed ", " Creeping Darkness ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Abyss Actor - Sassy Rookie ", " Abyss Actor - Extras ", " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ", and " Abyss Script - Abysstainment ". 
 To aid either of this card's effects, have the battling " Abyss Actor " monster be "Abyss Actor - Sassy Rookie", or control a face-up " Abyss Prop - Wild Wagon " so that your "Abyss Actor" monster is not destroyed by the first battle each turn.
 " Abyss Actor - Twinkle Little Star " also complements either of this card's effects, effectively being able to destroy any monster with higher ATK by attacking it twice in one turn, so long as you can take the damage.
