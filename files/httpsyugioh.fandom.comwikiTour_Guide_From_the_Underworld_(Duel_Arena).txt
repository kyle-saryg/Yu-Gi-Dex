 Tour Guide From the Underworld  is a character version of the card " Tour Guide From the Underworld ". 
 She appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P141.
 She was only obtainable by winning at least 30 times in Arena mode (Single Round only), during the time period of February 9, 2015, 00:00 (UTC) through February 15, 2015, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received her in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on February 18, 2015.

 Description 
 A cute guide on the " Tour Bus From the Underworld ". Her tour is very popular. Just be careful not to take the " Tour Bus To Forbidden Realms " by " Mistake ", or you will be sorry...
 
 Deck 
 This is a technical  deck  mainly consisting of  level  3  Fiend   type  cards. The deck offers many ways to  Special Summon   monsters . Enjoy a guided tour of " Burning Abyss "!

 Trivia 
 This character's description is a reference to the fate of " Sangan ", who  accidentally  boarded  the wrong bus , and  through   some unfortunate  events, became  jailed .
 This story deliberately parallels how "Sangan" became  Forbidden , until its effect was nerfed.
