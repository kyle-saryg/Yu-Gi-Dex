 Sarina's sanctuary
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Appears in (anime)
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 
 
 
 Appears in (episode)
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  episode 75: " Taken by Storm, Part 1 " 
 
 
 It is a small walled of cave in the forested hills. There is a small path through the forest leading to it. Inside, there are some lit candles.
Sarina gives her follower's their respective quests there.
