 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Number 96 , known simply as  Numbers 96  ( Ｎｏ． ９６,  Nanbāzu Kyūjūroku ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Number 96 , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. Both Duels with Number 96 are DLC in Legacy of the Duelist.
