 Millennium Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Millennium Pack  is a pack that was released for the twentieth anniversary of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 The  TCG  equivalent of this set is  Millennium Pack .

 Features 
 Contains cards from  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , including 10 new cards. 
 Contains cards that will work with cards from  Millennium Box Gold Edition . 
 Contains cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Yami Marik ,  the Big Five , and  Mai Valentine . 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards. Each Japanese box contains 10 packs and each Korean box contains 20 packs. The set contains 30 cards:
 
 2  Millennium Secret Rares 
 8  Millennium Ultra Rares 
 20  Millennium Super Rares
