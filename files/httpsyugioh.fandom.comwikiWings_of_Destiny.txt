 Wings of Destiny  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from November 13, 2013. Each pack costs   15.
 This collection includes Photon, Gishki, Blackwing, Lightsworn and Lightray Monsters and Spells..
