 Shonen Jump 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Shonen Jump  promotional cards are a series of special cards included in issues of the  Shonen Jump  magazine, given to users who order a subscription, available through mail-in orders or included in related tankoban.
 On December 7, 2018 Konami annouced the program would be discontinued, with the last 4 promotional cards to be delivered by July 30 and December 31, 2019 respectively.
 As the magazine is only distributed in North America, all cards in this set are  not legal  in other  TCG  regions such as Europe until they are reprinted in another set.

 Breakdown 
 The vast majority of  Shonen Jump  promotional cards are  Ultra Rare , with the exception of a few early cards which are  Secret Rare .
