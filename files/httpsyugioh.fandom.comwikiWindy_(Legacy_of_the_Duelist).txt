 Windy , known as (ウィンディ,  Windi ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Windy , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
