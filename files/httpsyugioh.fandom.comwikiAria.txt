 Aria  is a character appearing within the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga  and was a contestant in the  D1GP .

 Biography 
 Aria was one of the contestants participating in the  D1GP  Tournament [1] . He was shown to have lost to  Tetsu Ushio  on a screen. [2]  Later, he loses to  Leo  and  Luna , who act as Eliminators working for  Rex Goodwin  in the Tournament. [3] 
 
 Deck 
 The only shown card in Aria's Deck was " Alien Overlord ".
