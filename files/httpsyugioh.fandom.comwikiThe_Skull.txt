 Romaji:   Dokurā 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Monster Number
 
 
 89
 
 Group
 
 
 Undead
 
 Type
 
 
 Gutsy
 
 A wizard with a creepy mask. She has a cheerful personality, unlike her dreadful appearance, and she can't help but dance when her favorite music plays. 
 Special Ability: Create Zombie 
 She randomly revives one of her dead ally, which will get 1/2 of its full HP when revived, in a specific location.
