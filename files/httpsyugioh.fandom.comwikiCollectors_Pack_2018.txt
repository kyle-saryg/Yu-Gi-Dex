 Collectors Pack 2018  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Duke Devlin ,  Sam ,  Leon von Schroeder ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Lucien Grimley ,  Z-one ,  Bronk Stone ,  Vector ,  Iggy Arlo , and  Varis . 
 Includes new members/support for the " Borrel ", " Galaxy ", " Number ", " Photon ", and " Timelord " archetypes, as well as the  Fairy Tale ,  Railway , and " Topologic " series. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 45 cards:
 
 4  Ultra Rares 
 5  Super Rares 
 10  Rares 
 26  Commons 
 In the Japanese version, all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Collector's Rare . 
 In the Korean version, all Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Secret Rare .
