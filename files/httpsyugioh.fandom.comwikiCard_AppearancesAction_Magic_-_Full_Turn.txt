 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Manga 
 In  Scale 22 , during the  Action - Turbo Duel  between  Yuya Sakaki  and  Ren , Ren finds and activates a copy of this card to double any battle damage inflicted during the turn. The turn, however, is Yuya's, and his " Performapal Clay Breaker " is able to attack a weakened " White Aura Bihamut " controlled by Ren; the battle damage inflicted to Ren is doubled to 400.
