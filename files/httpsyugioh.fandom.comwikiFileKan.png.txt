 Kan Hakubutsu  ( 白 佛 カン  Hakubutsu Kan ) also known as  "Professor"  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS . He is a sixth-grade student at  Goha 2nd Elementary , and is the president of the  Dueling & Dinosaur Research Club .

 Personality 
 The president of Goha 2nd Elementary's Dueling & Dinosaur Research Club, who pursues the relationship between dinosaurs and duels. Show off romance tactics that image the evolution of life and a duel that summons powerful dinosaur monsters.
 
 Etymology 
 Kan's name is a word play on the japanese word for museum (博物館,  hakubutsukan ), where dinosaur fossils are often kept.
