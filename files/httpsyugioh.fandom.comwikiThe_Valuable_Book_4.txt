 The 4th Catalog of the Yu-Gi-Oh! Card Game: Duel Monsters, has appeared! It covers the 500+ cards released in 2001. With complete explanations of the New Expert Rules and a manual on Deck Construction, you'll have everything to master Dueling with this one book! 
 — Translated blurb from Shueisha's website [1] 
 
 Official Card Catalog: The Valuable Book 4  (公式カードカタログ　ザ・ヴァリュアブル・ブック　４,  Kōshiki Kādo Katarogu: Za Varyuaburu Bukku 4 ), [2]  commonly known as  The Valuable Book 4 , is the fourth book in  The Valuable Book  price catalog series. It was published by  Shueisha  on January 25,  2002 , and covers the 500+ cards released in  2001 , the  New Expert Rules , and a  Deck  construction manual. [1] [3] 
 
 Promotional cards 
 Main article:  The Valuable Book 4 promotional cards 
 The book came with two promotional cards, " Viser Des " and " Legendary Fiend ".
