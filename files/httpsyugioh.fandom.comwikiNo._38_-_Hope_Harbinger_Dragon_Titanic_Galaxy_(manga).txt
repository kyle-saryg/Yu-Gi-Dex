 Cannot be  destroyed by battle  except with " Number "  monsters . During either player's  turn , when your opponent  activates  the  effect  of a  Spell Card : You can  negate  that effect, then  banish  that Spell Card and this card. If this card banished by this effect is  Special Summoned :  Attach  the Spell Card banished by this card's effect to this card as an  Xyz Material . When an opponent's monster  declares  an  attack : You can  detach  1  Xyz Material  from this card; change the  attack target  to this card. When an  Xyz Monster  you  control  is  destroyed : This card gains  ATK  equal to the destroyed monster's  original ATK . When this card is  destroyed :  Target  1 Xyz Monster you control; it gains ATK equal to the ATK this card had on the  field .
