 This card's English name means "Red Ace" in Japanese;  Akaki  (赤き) means "red" and  eisu  is a phonetic rendering of "ace". 
 This card's mask resembles Hei's mask from  Darker Than Black .
 He is also manipulating what appears to be lightning, much like Hei. 
 This card's concept could have been inspired by the Japanese urban legend of  Aka Manto .
