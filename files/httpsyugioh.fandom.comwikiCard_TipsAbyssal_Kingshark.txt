 Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 This card can be  searched  by " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Transmodify ", " Downbeat ", " Whitefish Salvage ", " Beautunaful Princess ", " Superancient Deepsea King Coelacanth ", " Underworld Egg Clutch ", " Shark Cruiser ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Mermaid Shark ", " Soul Rope " and " Dragunity Corsesca ". 
 This card can be revived by " Call of the Haunted " even if said card is chained to the effect that would destroy it, due to this card's specific destruction immunity. 
 This card can attack directly even if " Des Counterblow " is on the field, and it can also survive " Ancient Forest ". 
 This card will survive " Levia-Dragon - Daedalus 's" effect as it  destroys , but not " Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus " because it  sends . It easily fits in an " Umi " Deck so long as the difference is noted.
