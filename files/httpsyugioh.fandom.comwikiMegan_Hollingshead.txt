 Megan Hollingshead  is an American voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime before leaving  4Kids Entertainment  in 2004. [ citation needed ] 
 She is also well-known for voice acting work on other 4Kids anime, including  Pok√©mon  and  Sonic X  up until her departure from the company. She has also voiced in various  Viz Media  and  Bandai Entertainment  anime, including  Bleach  and  Eureka Seven . Some of her work is done under the pseudonym  Karen Thompson .

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Hollingshead's major role on the anime was portraying  Mai Valentine  from  Episode 3  to  Episode 144 . This role was taken over by  Bella Hudson  in season 4 onwards following Hollingshead's departure from 4Kids. Other roles include the synthetic voice of  Seto Kaiba 's computer from  Episode 9 ,  10 ,  143 [ citation needed ]  and  Cecelia Pegasus [ citation needed ] .
 
 Trivia 
 Since her departure from the series, she, along with fellow former Yu-Gi-Oh! voice actress  Tara Sands , worked together on  Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World  as Sheena Fujibayashi and Genis Sage respectfully, though neither of them were credited for it.
