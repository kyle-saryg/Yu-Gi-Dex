 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Ritual 
 
 This card can be used to  Ritual Summon  any  Cyberse   Ritual Monsters . Ritual Summon 1 Cyberse Ritual Monster from your  hand , by  Tributing   monsters  from your hand or field whose total  Levels  equal or exceed the Level of that Ritual Monster. During your  Main Phase  if you  control  no monsters, except during the turn this card was sent to the  GY : You can  banish  this card and 1 Ritual Monster from your GY; Special Summon 2 " Cynet Tokens " ( Cyberse / LIGHT / Level  4/ ATK  0/ DEF  0) in  Defense Position .
