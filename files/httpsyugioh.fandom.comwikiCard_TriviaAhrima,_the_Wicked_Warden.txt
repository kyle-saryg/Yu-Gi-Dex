 Names 
 
 This card’s Japanese name translates to " Evil King Ahrima ". The Japanese names of this card, " Lich Lord, King of the Underworld ", " Prometheus, King of the Shadows ", " Duke Shade, the Sinister Shadow Lord ", and " Diabolos, King of the Abyss " each correspond to the Japanese name of one of the five " Virus " Trap cards; in this case, " Grinning Grave Virus " (" Evil Deck Destruction Virus ").
