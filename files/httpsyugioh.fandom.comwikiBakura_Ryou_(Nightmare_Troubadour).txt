 Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour 
 
 
 
 Bakura Ryou  (獏良了  Bakura Ryō ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour . In the Japanese version, "Bakura" is his surname, while "Ryou" is his given name. This is the game's incarnation of  Bakura Ryou , from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime  and  manga .
