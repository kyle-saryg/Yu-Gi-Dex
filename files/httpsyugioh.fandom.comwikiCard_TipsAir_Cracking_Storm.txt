 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Mythical Bestiamorph ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Iron Blacksmith Kotetsu ", " Hidden Armory ", " Power Tool Dragon ", " Vylon Cube ", and " Vylon Delta ". 
 This card is best equipped to  Machine  monsters with not-once-per-turn effects that activate when they destroy a monster by battle, such as " Blue Thunder T-45 ", " Delta Tri ", " FalchionÎ² ", " Helping Robo for Combat ", " KA-2 Des Scissors ", " Lord British Space Fighter ", " Truckroid " or " Victory Viper XX03 ". 
 You can also equip this card to Machine monsters that can inflict  piercing  damage, such as " Ancient Gear Golem ", " Cyber End Dragon ", " Cyberdark Horn ", " Deskbot 007 ", " Drill Driver Vespenato ", " F.A. Dawn Dragster " or " Hi-Speedroid Kendama ". 
 When combined with " Break! Draw! ", you can potentially draw two cards for destroying two monsters by battle in one turn.
