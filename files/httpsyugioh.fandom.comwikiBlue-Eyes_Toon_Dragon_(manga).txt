 This was not a physical card, but " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " affected by " Toon World ".
 
 
 
 When this card is selected as an  attack target , except by another  Toon monster , it can dodge the attack without being  destroyed . At the end of your turn, you can hide this card in "Toon World", where it cannot be selected as an attack target.
