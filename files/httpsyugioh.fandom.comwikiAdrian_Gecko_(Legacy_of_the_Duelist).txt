 Adrian Gecko , known as Amon Garam (アモン・ガラム,  Amon Garamu ) in the Japanese version,  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Adrian Gecko , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
