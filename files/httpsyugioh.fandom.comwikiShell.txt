Monsters with shells  are a group of  monsters  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  which can be powered up with " Steel Shell ". Most of them are  Aqua - Type   WATER  monsters.
