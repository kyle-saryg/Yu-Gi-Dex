 Akira Zaizen , known as ( 財 前   晃 ,  Zaizen Akira ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Akira Zaizen , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
