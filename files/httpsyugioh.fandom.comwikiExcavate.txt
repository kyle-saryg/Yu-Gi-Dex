 Excavate  (めくる  mekuru , lit. "flip" or "turn over") is a game term introduced in  War of the Giants: Round 2  and featured prominently on " Sylvan " monsters in  Legacy of the Valiant .
 When a card says to "excavate" cards from your Deck, you  reveal  those cards to both players. Cards that are being excavated are still treated as being in the Deck until sent elsewhere by the card instructions. "Excavate" and "reveal" are not always the same thing. Some cards will "reveal" cards but not "excavate" them. Unless a card specifically refers to "excavating", then it does not excavate. [1]
