 Mystic Horseman , known as  Kentauros  (ケンタウロス  Kentaurosu ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . It is a  ka  summoned by  Seto . Its corresponding  Duel Monsters  card, " Mystic Horseman " is used by  Seto Kaiba .
 
 Biography 
 In  a battle  against  Bandit King Bakura ,  Seto  Summoned Mystic Horseman, alongside the previously summoned  Battle Ox .  Karim  then used the  Millennium Scales  to fuse them into the  Rabid Horseman . [1]
