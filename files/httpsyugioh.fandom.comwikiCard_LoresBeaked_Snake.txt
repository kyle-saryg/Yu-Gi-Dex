 The  Greek                lore given is not official.  
 
 Greek - Φίδι με Ράμφος 
Αυτό το πλάσμα περιτυλίσσει τον εαυτό του γύρω απο το θύμα του τσιμπώντας δυνατά με το μεγάλο ράμφος του.
 
 
 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was " This monster wraps itself around an opponent and pecks away with its huge beak. " 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Ce monstre s'enroule autour de son adversaire et le picore avec son énorme bec. " 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Dieses Monster wickelt sich selbst um einen Gegner und stochert mit seinem riesigen Rüssel herum. " 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Mostro che si arrotola intorno al corpo del nemico e comincia a colpirlo con il suo enorme becco. " 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Este monstruo se envuelve alrededor de un adversario y lo picotea con su enorme pico. "
