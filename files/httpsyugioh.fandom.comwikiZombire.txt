 Zombire  (ゾンバイア  Zonbaia ), known as  Zompire  in the  Dungeon Dice Monsters video game , is an American comic book hero, existing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! manga . He is based on the super hero  Spawn  and has an  OCG / TCG  card based on him " Zombyra the Dark ".
 
 Biography 
 Zombire was once the God of Death, but he then learned how to love and began fighting evil. This causes his face to rot and shorten his lifespan, regardless, Zombire continues to fight evil. His name probably is a combination of "zombie" and "vampire".
 
 Influence 
 In the manga,  Tomoya Hanasaki  and his family are obsessed with the hero and have a massive amount of Zombire merchandise. Tomoya goes as far as wearing a Zombire costume and attempting to become a hero himself.
