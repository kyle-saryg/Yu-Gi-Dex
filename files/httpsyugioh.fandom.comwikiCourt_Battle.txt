 When  activated , each player gains one scale. When a player  destroys  an opponent's  monster , place that monster as a  counter  on their scale. The player who gains 3 counters first can remove all of their counters, then  attach  those cards to a  face-up   Xyz Monster (s) they  control  as  Xyz Materials .
