 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 Equip  only to a  Cyberse   Link Monster . If it  battles  an opponent's  monster , its  ATK  becomes equal to the current ATK of the opponent's monster it is battling, during  damage calculation  only. If this  face-up  card on the  field  is  destroyed  and  sent  to the  GY : You can  Special Summon  up to 2 " Salamangreat Tokens " (Cyberse/ FIRE / Level  1/ATK 0/ DEF  0) in  Defense Position  to either player's field. You can only use this  effect  of "Salamangreat Sparks"  once per turn .
