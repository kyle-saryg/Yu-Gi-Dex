 Starter Deck 2018  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the thirteenth Deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2017 . [1]

 Breakdown 
 Each  Starter Deck 2018  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 45 cards
 2  Ultra Rares 
 3  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 34  Commons 
 1 Official Rulebook 
 1 Double-sided  Playmat /Starter Guide 
 In addition, the first print of  Starter Deck 2018  contains either 1 Token or 1 rare card from the past. Each rare card in this Set is limited to a number of copies between 10 and 300.
