 "Curtains For Jonouchi?!"  is the twelfth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! R  manga. It was first printed in Japan on  March 20, 2005 in  V Jump  magazine. It was later reprinted in volume 2 of  Yu-Gi-Oh! R .
 Jonouchi 's progress to the upper floors is again blocked, this time by  Mendo Cino .
 
 Featured Duel: Katsuya Jonouchi vs. Mendo Cino 
 Turn 1: Mendo 
 Mendo   Sets  a card and  Normal Summons  " Praying Mantis " (1500/1200) in  Attack Position .
 Turn 2: Jonouchi 
 Jonouchi  Sets a card and Normal Summons " The Legendary Fisherman " (1850/1600 ) in Attack Position. It  attacks  "Praying Mantis", but Mendo  activates  his  face-down  " Forced Withdrawal ",  returning  "Fisherman" to Jonouchi's  hand .
 Turn 3: Mendo 
"Praying Mantis" attacks  directly  (Jonouchi 4000 → 2500). Mendo activates " Labor Pain ". Each time a player Normal Summons a monster, they must  pay  1000  Life Points . He then activates the  Field Spell Card , " Mantis Egg ". It will  Special Summon  a " Baby Mantis Token " during each of Mendo's  Standby Phases . Mendo Sets another "Forced Withdrawal".
 Turn 4: Jonouchi 
Jonouchi Normal Summons "The Legendary Fisherman" (1850/1600) in  Defense Position , paying 1000 Life Points via "Labor Pain" (Jonouchi 2500 → 1500). He then Sets a card.
 Turn 5: Mendo 
"Mantis Egg" Special Summons a "Baby Mantis Token" (500/500). Mendo  Tributes  "Praying Mantis" and the "Baby Mantis Token" to Normal Summon " Dreadscythe Harvester " (2300/1600) in Attack Position, paying 1000 Life Points via "Labor Pain" (Mendo 4000 → 3000). "Dreadscythe Harvester" attacks "The Legendary Fisherman", but Jonouchi activates his face-down " Scapegoat ", Special Summoning four " Sheeps " (0/0) in Defense Position. One of them becomes the new attack  target  and is destroyed.
 Turn 6: Jonouchi 
Jonouchi Sets a card.
 Turn 7: Mendo 
"Mantis Egg" Special Summons a "Baby Mantis Token" (500/500). "Baby Mantis Token" and "Dreadscythe Harvester" each attack and destroy a "Sheep Token".
 Turn 8: Jonouchi 
Jonouchi activates " Cross Shift ", returning his last "Sheep Token" to his hand and Special Summoning " Swordsman of Landstar " (500/1200). He Tributes "Swordsman of Landstar" and "The Legendary Fisherman" to Normal Summon " Jinzo " (2400/1500) in Attack Position, paying 1000 Life Points via "Labor Pain" (Jonouchi 1500 → 500). The effect of "Jinzo" destroys Mendo's face-down "Forced Withdrawal".
 Duel continues in  the next chapter .
