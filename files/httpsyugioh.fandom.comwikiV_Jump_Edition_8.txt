 V Jump Edition 8  is the twenty-fifth installment of the Limited Edition series. This pack contains  FIRE -themed cards: a " Fire Fist " monster, a " Fire King " monster and 3 cards used by  Devon Knox  in the anime. The previous pack was  V Jump Edition 7 .
