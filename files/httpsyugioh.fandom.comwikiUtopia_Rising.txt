 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Utopia "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 希 望 皇 ホープ」 .   As its English name contains "Utopia", it  may  be treated a member of the "Utopia" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Effect 
 Continuous 
 Continuous 
 Special Summon  1 " Number 39: Utopia " from your  Graveyard  and  equip  it with this card. While equipped by this  effect , "Number 39: Utopia" gains the effects of all  Xyz Monsters  you  control  that are  activated  by  detaching  their own  Xyz Material (s). When this card  leaves the field ,  destroy  the equipped  monster .
