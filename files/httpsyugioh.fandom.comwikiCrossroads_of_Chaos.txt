 Crossroads of Chaos   is the second set of the OCG's sixth Series after  The Duelist Genesis . This set is followed by  Crimson Crisis .

 Features 
 Features cards used by  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Leo ,  Trudge ,  Mr. Armstrong  and  Sayer . The TCG version also features cards used by  Seto Kaiba ,  Yami Bakura ,  Keith Howard ,  Yako Tenma ,  Bastion Misawa  and  Jaden Yuki .
 Introduces the " Morphtronic ", " Plant Princess " and " Iron Chain "  archetypes . Many new cards and  Synchro Monsters  from  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  are included, as well as  Plant ,  Psychic  and  Zombie - Type  monsters and support.
 TCG exclusives  include a new Level 4  Tuner monster , as well as  Spellcaster  and  Zombie  support.
 
 Breakdown 
 In the Japanese version there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box.
 The  OCG  set includes 80 cards or 86 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
