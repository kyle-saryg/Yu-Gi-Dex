 Effect type
 
 
 
 
 Trigger-like 
 Continuous 
 During your  End Phase , you can  remove from play  1  Plant - Type   monster  from your  Graveyard  to inflict 300  damage  to your opponent. When you remove from play a monster to  activate  this  effect , if there are no Plant-Type monsters in your Graveyard, inflict 300 damage to your opponent for each Plant-Type monster removed by this card's effect. If there are no Plant-Type monsters in your Graveyard,  destroy  this card.
