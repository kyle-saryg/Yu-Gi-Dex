 Luna , known as  Ruka  ( 龍可 ,  Ruka ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Luna , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Biography 
 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock " Sunlight Unicorn " by defeating Luna for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Treasure Map " by defeating Luna for the second time. 
 The player can unlock " Goblin's Secret Remedy " by defeating Luna for the third time. 
 The player can unlock " Dian Keto the Cure Master " by defeating Luna for the fourth time. 
 The player can unlock " Truesdale's Mischief " by defeating Luna for the fifth time. 
 The player can unlock " Numinous Healer " by defeating Luna for the sixth time. 
 The player can unlock  Luna's Duel Runner  by defeating Luna for the first time. 
 The player can unlock Luna's Decklist by winning the  Gold Cup .
