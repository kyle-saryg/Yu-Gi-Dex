 Deck Build Pack: Spirit Warriors  is a  Booster  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Includes members/support for the " Goblin ", " Shien " and " Six Samurai " archetypes, as well as the  "C"  series. 
 Introduces the " Magical Musket ", " Secret Six Samurai " and " The Weather " archetypes to the OCG. 
 Breakdown 
 Each box has 15 packs, 5 cards per pack (4 Commons and 1 Normal Parallel Rare or Foil card).
 The set contains 45 cards, or 78 cards in the  Master Set :
 
 3  Ultra Rares  /  Secret Rares 
 12  Super Rares 
 30  Commons  /  Normal Parallel Rares
