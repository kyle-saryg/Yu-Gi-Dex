 Breakers of Shadow: Special Edition  is a  Special Edition  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Breakdown 
 Each North American  Breakers of Shadow Special Edition  is comprised of:
 
 3  Breakers of Shadow  booster packs ( Unlimited Edition ) 
 1 of 2  Super Rares  (" Beast-Eyes Pendulum Dragon " or " Number 23: Lancelot, Dark Knight of the Underworld ") 
 1 of 2 Super Rares of preview cards from  Shining Victories 
 Each European and Oceanic  Breakers of Shadow Special Edition  is comprised of:
 
 27 cards equivalent to 3  Breakers of Shadow  Booster Packs 
 1 of 2 Super Rares ("Beast-Eyes Pendulum Dragon" or "Number 23: Lancelot, Dark Knight of the Underworld") 
 1 of 2 Super Rares of preview cards from  Shining Victories
