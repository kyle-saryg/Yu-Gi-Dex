 Yuma Tsukumo 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 English name 
 Yuma Tsukumo 
 
 
 Japanese name  
 九十九   遊馬 九十九 遊馬 つくも ゆうま Rōmaji: ( Tsukumo Yūma ) 
 
 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Yuma Tsukumo , known as ( 九十九   遊馬 ,  Tsukumo Yūma ) in the Japanese version is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yuma Tsukumo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
 
 Decks 
 Yuma's Decks are based on his Duels throughout the anime.
