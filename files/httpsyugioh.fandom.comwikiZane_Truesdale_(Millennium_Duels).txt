 Zane Truesdale , known as  Ryo Marufuji  (丸藤 亮,  Marufuji Ryō ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Zane Truesdale , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
