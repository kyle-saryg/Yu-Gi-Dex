 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 Because this card essentially takes up your Normal Summon/Set, it is best followed up with copies of " Photon Advancer " or " Photon Vanisher " in your hand in order to go into  Rank 4   Xyz Monsters . 
 Special Summon " Galaxy Wizard " via this card, change its Level to 8 and activate " Galaxy Expedition " to summon " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon ", " Galaxy Knight ", " Galaxy Tyranno " or " Photon Caesar " from your Deck, setting up a  Rank 8   Xyz Summon .
 If  three materials  are required, Special Summon " Guardian of Order " from your hand.
