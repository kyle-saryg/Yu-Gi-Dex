 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Single Purchase ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Sangan ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon ", " Supreme King Dragon Odd-Eyes ", " Rescue Hamster ", " Pendulum Rising ", " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Luster Pendulum, the Dracoslayer ", " Magical Abductor ", " Majester Paladin, the Ascending Dracoslayer ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Soul Rope ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Dark General Freed ", " Creeping Darkness ", " Chaos Zone ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ", " Abyss Actor - Sassy Rookie ", " Abyss Actor - Extras ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ", and " Abyss Script - Abysstainment ". 
 Make the most out of this card's effect by using it to attack multiple monsters while affected by the effects of " Abyss Script - Fantasy Magic ".
 Have " Spirit Barrier " face-up to avoid taking damage when it battles. 
 Use this card's  Pendulum Effect  on an " Abyss Actor " monster with high ATK.
 Use " Half Shut " on an opposing Attack Position monster so that your "Abyss Actor" monster targeted by this card can attack that monster three times. For an OTK, your "Abyss Actor" monster should have 2700 more ATK than the halved ATK of the monster targeted by "Half Shut".
