 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 The first time the  equipped   monster  would be  destroyed by battle  or  card effect  each  turn , it is not  destroyed . If the equipped monster  attacks , your opponent cannot  activate  any  Spell / Trap Cards  until the end of the  Damage Step . If the equipped monster attacks a  Defense Position  monster, inflict  piercing   battle damage  to your opponent. If the equipped monster destroys an opponent's monster by  battle : You can make any  battle damage  taken from that attack become doubled.
