Not to be confused with the monster which has the same Japanese name, " Crow Tengu ". 
 The  Chinese, Croatian, French, German, Greek, Italian  and  Portuguese                  names given are not official.  
 The  Chinese                lore given is not official.  ( card lores ) 
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Goblin "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ゴブリン」 .   As its English name contains "Goblin", it  may  be treated a member of the "Goblin" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
