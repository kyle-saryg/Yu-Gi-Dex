 Yusei Fudo  is a video game depiction of  Yusei Fudo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 He appears as a Legend Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner number is P008.
 In the beta, his partner number was P004.

 Description 
 A " Signer ." One of those who bear the  Mark of the Dragon . Can be taciturn, but is actually very passionate about his friends. Riding the  Duel Runner  " Yusei Go ," he fights to protect  New Domino City .
