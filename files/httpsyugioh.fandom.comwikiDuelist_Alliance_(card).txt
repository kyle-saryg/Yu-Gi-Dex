 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If there is a card in the  Pendulum Zone :  Add  1 " Pendulum "  Pendulum Monster  or "Pendulum"  Spell / Trap  from your  Deck  to your  hand . You can only  activate  1 "Duelist Alliance" per  turn .
