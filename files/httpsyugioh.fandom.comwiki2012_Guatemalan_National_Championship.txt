 2011 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  2012 Guatemalan National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Guatemala. It took place in The Hideout in Guatemala City [1]  and was won by Alejandro Cañas, who used an " Inzektor " Deck. [2]
