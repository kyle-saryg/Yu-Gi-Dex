 King's Court 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 King's Court  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto / Yami Yugi ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Bandit Keith ,  Yako Tenma ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Prince Ojin ,  Yusei Fudo , Jack Atlas ( 5D's anime  and  manga ),  Akiza Izinski ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Yuto ,  Yugo  and  Yuri 
 Includes new members and/or support for the " Magna Warrior ", " Magnet Warrior ", " Eyes Restrict ", " Relinquished ", " Prophecy ", " Rose ", " Number ", " Utopia ", " Utopic ", " Utopic Future ", " Galaxy ", " Fusion ", " Xyz ", " Pendulum ", " Tindangle " archetypes, as well as the  Gearfried ,  Signature move  and  Three Musketeers of Face Cards  series. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 65 cards:
 
 3  Ultra Rare (Pharaoh's Rare)s 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 15  Super Rares 
 37  Rares 
 In addition, 15 cards in this set are also available as  Collector's Rare .
