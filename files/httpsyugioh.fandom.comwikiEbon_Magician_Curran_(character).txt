 Ebon Magician Curran  is a character version of the card " Ebon Magician Curran ". She appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .
 In  GX Spirit Caller , she appears as a  Duel Monster Spirit . By beating her a few times, she can be unlocked for use by the player. She can be found at the east side of  Duel Academy .
 In  World Championship 2007 , she appears as a Level Four Limited Duelist and she challenges the player to not battle during the  Duel . By beating her, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Five Limited Duelists and a new Booster Pack.
 
 Ability 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , Ebon Magician Curran has the ability to allow the player to get a lovely present from a  duelist . To improve this ability, the player has to level up the spirit by  Summoning  the corresponding card in a   Duel  and winning.
 
 Deck 
 Ebon Magician Curran uses a  Burn  Deck titled, "Curran's Punishment", "Cold War", "Super Burn", and "It's Going To Sting".
