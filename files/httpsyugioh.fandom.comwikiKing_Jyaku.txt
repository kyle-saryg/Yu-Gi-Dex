 Decks 
 Tag Force series 
 King Jyaku uses  Dragon  Decks , as well as  DARK   Synchro Decks  featuring " Assault Mode Activate ".

 King Jyaku's first Deck is the same as his second. Also, his third and fourth Decks are identical.
