 Reginald Kastle , known as  Ryoga Kamishiro  ( 神 代   凌 牙 ,  Kamishiro Ryōga ) in the Japanese version and nicknamed  Shark , is a character in the  Tag Force  games. This is a video game version of the anime character,  Reginald Kastle .
