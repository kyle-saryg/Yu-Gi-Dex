 Shin Yoshida  is a Japanese screenwriter and series composer.
 
 Work 
 Yoshida is credited as series composer for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  episodes 145-184 and 199-224,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  episodes 157-180,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's   episodes 27-154,  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL [1]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS . He is credited as the screenwriter for the film  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time . [2] 
 Yoshida is also responsible for the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL manga 's story.
