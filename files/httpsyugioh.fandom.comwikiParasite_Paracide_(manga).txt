 If this card is in your  hand , it is forced into play. All your  monsters  become parasitized. 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> When you  draw  this card, it is immediately  Special Summoned  on your side of the  field   face-up  in  Defense Position . From this point, all face-up  Monster Cards  on your side of the field are treated as  Insect - Type  monsters.
