 Volcanic Shell  is a character version of the card " Volcanic Shell ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , as a Tag Duel opponent. 
 This character is unlocked as a Tag Duel opponent with  Flamvell Firedog  as the team mate. The team is called  Explosive Tag.
