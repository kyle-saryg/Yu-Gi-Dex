 Blackwing - Zephyrus the Elite  is a character version of the card " Blackwing - Zephyrus the Elite ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .
 This character can be unlocked as a single opponent after summoning " Blackwing - Silverwind the Ascendant ". This character and  Harpie's Pet Baby Dragon  can be unlocked as Turbo Tag Duel opponents after Summoning " Ultimate Axon Kicker ". Their Tag Team name is  Hurricane Regiment .
