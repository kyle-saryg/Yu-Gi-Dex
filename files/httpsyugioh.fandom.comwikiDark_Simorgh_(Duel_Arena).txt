 Dark Simorgh  is a character version of the card " Dark Simorgh ". 
 It appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P043.
 
 Description 
 A  monster  with the powerful  effect  to prevent your opponent from  Setting  cards.
 
 Deck 
 A  Deck  with mixed  DARK  and  WIND  Monsters in order to fulfill the  Special Summon  conditions for " Dark Simorgh ". Quickly  send  the required monsters to the  Graveyard  to get ready for the  Summon . If you can line up " Anti-Spell Fragrance " then you can seal your opponent's  Spell Cards , too!
