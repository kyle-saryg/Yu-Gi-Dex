 English name 
 IN4-M8 
 
 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels 
 
 
 
 IN4-M8  is a supporting character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Generation ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . In the two Legacy of the Duelist games, IN4-M8 explains the stories of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime series. IN4-M8 also appears on some official  Yu-Gi-Oh!  social media pages to announce news.
