 Chevelle Kayama  (シュベール  Kayama Shubēru ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS , and an antagonist in the  Goha 6th Elementary arc . He is a member of  Goha 6th Elementary ’s  Heavy Cavalry Duel Club , more specifically a Tactical Advisor, he is always together with his pet pigeon  Trapigeon .

 Design 
 Appearance 
       Chevelle Kayama full body view with  Trapigeon     Chevelle is a young boy with curly purple hair and golden eyes. He wears a yellow jacket over a white collared undershirt, white pants with a brown belt, a green bowtie, white gloves and black boots, with a red cape. He also has a brown pad in his left shoulder
 Personality 
 He is arrogant but at the same time he shows great respect for his friends , He cares deeply for  Trapigeon . Together, they played multiple times the role of a trickster in their duel against  Luke  ,seting pranks and acting dramatic every time they activated  Trap Card   effects . Sometimes he use French words when he speaks and ends his sentences with his name. He also acts as the translator of Trapigeon ,when he translate Trapigeon's words, he replace his name at the end of the sentence with a "coo". [1] 
 
 
 Deck 
       Chevelle and Trapigeon together     
 Chevelle does not duel alone, he owns a  Winged Beast   Deck  with a lot of  Trap Cards , but he execute what moves Trapigeon telling to him.
