 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
This card's name becomes " Great Sand Sea - Gold Golgonda " while on the  field  or in the  GY .  Once per turn , if a card(s) you  control  would be  destroyed by battle  or  card effect , you can  send  1  Fusion Monster  that mentions " Fallen of Albaz " as  material  from your  Extra Deck  to the GY instead. During each  End Phase , if you have "Fallen of Albaz" on your field or in your GY: You can  Special Summon  1 "Springans" monster from your  hand  or  Deck . You can only use this effect of "Decisive Battle of Golgonda" once per turn.
