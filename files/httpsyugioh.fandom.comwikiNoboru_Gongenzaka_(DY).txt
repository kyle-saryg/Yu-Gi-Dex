 Noboru Gongenzaka  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Gong Strong , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Gongenzaka is one of  Yuya 's closest friends.
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Gongenzaka runs a  " Superheavy Samurai " Deck .
