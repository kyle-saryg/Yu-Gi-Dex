 2014 Mega-Tin Mega Pack  or  Mega Tin 2014 Mega Pack  is a  Mega Pack  set released with the  2014 Mega-Tins .
 The set contains 247 cards, made up of a year's worth of  Booster Packs  from the sets  Lord of the Tachyon Galaxy ,  Judgment of the Light ,  Shadow Specters , and  Legacy of the Valiant .
