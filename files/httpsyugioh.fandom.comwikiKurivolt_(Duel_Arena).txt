 Kurivolt  is a character version of the card " Kurivolt ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P090. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 5-1 under Quest mode.
