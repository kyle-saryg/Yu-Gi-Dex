 Starter Deck 2007  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the second deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2006 .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Starter Deck 2007  contains 40 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 1  Ultra Parallel Rare
