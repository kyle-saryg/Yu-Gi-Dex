 A  column  ( 縦 列   tateretsu ) is a vertical line of  Monster Zones  and  Spell & Trap Zones  on the  field  stretching from one player's  Spell & Trap Zone  to their opponent's Spell & Trap Zone. There are five columns on the field.
 The  TCG  term "column" was first mentioned in  card text  in  Cyberdark Impact , and there was no  OCG  equivalent for quite some time until the  OCG  equivalent of the  TCG   Problem-Solving Card Text  was implemented, prior to which, instead of being in the "same column", your opponent's cards were said to be "opposite" (正面  shōmen ) to yours.

 Rules 
 Master Rule 3 
 Every column contains exactly 4 Zones: a Monster Zone and a Spell & Trap Zone on each player's side of the field.
 
 New Master Rule 
 As per New  Master Rule , the second column and the fourth column (counting from either direction) have 5 Zones, while all others contain 4 Zones. The second and fourth columns each contain an  Extra Monster Zone , in addition to the  Main Monster Zone  and  Spell & Trap Zone  on each player's side of the field.
