 The official  Greek  name given (" Προηγμένη Ασπίδα ") is an exact translation of the English name of this card. A possible translation of the Japanese name (" Advanced Shield Armour ") in Greek could be " Προηγμένη Ασπίδα Θώρακως " or " Προηγμένος Προστατευτικός Θώρακας ". 
 
 
 The Spanish name of this card in the videogame  Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler  is " Blindaje Avanzado ", whilst its name in the American Spanish dub is " Escudo Avanzado ".
