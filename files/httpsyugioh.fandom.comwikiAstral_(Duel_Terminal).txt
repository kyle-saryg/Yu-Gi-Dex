 Astral  (アストラル,  Asutoraru ) is a character in the  Duel Terminal  arcade games. This is a video game depiction of  Astral , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. Unlike many of the other characters, Astral only narrates the  Duels  and assists the player during their Duels.
