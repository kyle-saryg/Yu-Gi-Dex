 Appearances 
 Anime 
 It appears briefly in one of the  Duel Monsters Spirit World  dimensions, attacking a group of  Garuda the Wind Spirits  that had been harassing weaker monsters. [1] 
 
 Spirit Caller 
       Acrobat Monkey, in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller     
 Acrobat Monkey appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller . By beating it a few times, it can be unlocked for use by the player. It can be found at the north side of  Duel Academy  and at the  Abandoned Dorm . 
 
 Ability 
 In  Spirit Caller , it has the ability to allow the player to get a strange encounter. To improve this ability, the player has to level up the spirit by  Summoning  the corresponding card in a Duel and winning.
 
 Deck 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , Acrobat Monkey uses a  Machine  Deck tilted, "Acrobat Attack!".
