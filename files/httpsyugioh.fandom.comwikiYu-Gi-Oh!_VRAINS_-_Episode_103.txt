 " Reflections ", known as " Journey to the End " in the Japanese version, is the one-hundredth-and-third episode of  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. It aired in Japan on May 22, 2019. It aired in English on Teletoon on January 24, 2021. This episode is a clip show.
 After his life-and-death battle with  Bohman ,  Playmaker  was able to free the consciousness of his allies. However,  Ai , who has used up his power during the battle, as well as the other  Ignises , didn't come back to Playmaker.
