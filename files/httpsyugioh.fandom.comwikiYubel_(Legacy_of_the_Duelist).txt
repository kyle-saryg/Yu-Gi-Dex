 Yubel , known as (ユベル,  Yuberu ) in the Japanese version,  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yubel , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
