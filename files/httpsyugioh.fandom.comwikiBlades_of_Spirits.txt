 Blades of Spirits  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on December 1, 2017, 7:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 For honor they fight! Mighty warriors of the blade unite! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Great Shogun Shien ," The leader of the " Six Samurai " who has 3 powerful effects. Prepare 2 "Six Samurai" monsters to Special Summon this card! 
" Cyber Harpie Lady ," This monster is always treated as " Harpie Lady ". Be aware that you can only have a total of 3 monsters who are treated as "Harpie Lady" in your Deck. 
" Parallel Twister ," By sending a Spell/Trap Card other than this card to your Graveyard, you can destroy 1 card on the field. Include it in your Deck alongside cards such as "Powerful Rebirth" which will remain on the field even after its activation. 
" Powerful Rebirth ," This Trap Card lets you Special Summon a Level 4 or lower monster from your Graveyard. The Special Summoned monster will gain 1 level and 100 ATK/DEF. 
A total of 40 types of Cards: 2  UR , 8  SR , 14  R , and 16  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Blades of Spirits  contains 80 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 5 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 10 copies
