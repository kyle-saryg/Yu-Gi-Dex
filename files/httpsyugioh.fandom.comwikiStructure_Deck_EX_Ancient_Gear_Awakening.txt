 Structure Deck EX: Ancient Gear Awakening  is an original Structure Deck in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It's the third in the new series of Structure Decks EX. It was first released in-game on February 4th, 2019, 08:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Use Ancient Gears with the Skill "Middle Age Mechs"! 
 
 " Ancient Gear Golem - Ultimate Pound " This monster can deal piercing damage, as well as attack again after destroying a monster. These two battle related effects, combined with its high ATK, allows you to inflict massive damage to your opponent! 
" Ancient Gear Wyvern " When this monster is Normal or Special Summoned, you can add an Ancient Gear card from your Deck to your hand. In addition to searching for monsters, you can also add Spell Cards such as " Ancient Gear Fortress " and " Ancient Gear Tank " to your hand. Since you cannot set cards after activating this effect, make sure to Set Quick-Play Spell Cards and Trap Cards before Summoning this monster. 
A total of 18 types of Cards: 2  UR , 4  SR , 8  R  and 4  N .
