 Names 
 
 The given unofficial Arabic name (فنّ الطّقسيّة المتقدّم) translates to  Advanced Art of Ritualism . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Προηγμένη Τελετουργική Τέχνη ") translates to " Advanced Ritualistic Art ". A possible translation of the Japanese name of this card (" High Class Ritual Art ") in Greek could be " Τελετουργική Τέχνη Υψηλής Κλάσης ".
