 Selection 10 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Selection 10  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Includes new members/support for the " Cipher ", " Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes ",  Laval ", " Hieratic ", " Nekroz ", " Time Thief , " Shaddoll " and " Vampire " archetypes, as well as  Fairy Tail  series.
 Introduces the " Starry Knight ", " Sunavalon " and " Sunvine " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Introduces the first support for the " Cipher Dragon " archetype to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards. The set contains 45 cards.
 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 8  Super Rares 
 12  Rares 
 25  Commons 
 All Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards also come in  Secret Rare . 
 10 of 25 Common cards also come in  Normal Parallel Rare .
