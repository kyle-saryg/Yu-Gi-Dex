 [  Rock  /  Plus Minus  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card can only be  Special Summoned  by  sending  1  Plus monster  and 1  Minus monster  from your  hand  or your side of the  field  to the  Graveyard . When this card  attacks , you can  select  1  face-up  Plus monster or Minus monster on the field to have this card gain  ATK  equal to half that monster's ATK, until the  End Phase . If your opponent  controls  a Plus monster and/or Minus monster, this card must attack that monster, if able.
