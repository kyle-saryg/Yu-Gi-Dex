 " Space Operations Duel Squadron " is the forty-second episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on April 4, 2021.
 Hearing that Goha will be hosting a  Rush Duel  tournament,  Luke  proclaims that he will beat everyone and become the Duel King. A three-member team is required for the tournament. However,  Yuga  refuses to take part in it. Luke doesn't fully agree with Yuga's decision. He's planning to take part in the tournament no matter what, so he goes to the underground area to dig up some Maximum cards to prepare for it. There, he runs into three strangers wearing space suits!
 
 Featured Duel: Tatsuhisa "Luke" Kamijo VS Honya Braun 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
