 Marshmallon 
 
 Marshmallon  is a character version of the card " Marshmallon ". It appears briefly in one of the  Duel Monsters Spirit World  dimensions, attacking a group of  Garuda the Wind Spirits  that had been harassing weaker monsters. [1]

 Non-canon appearances 
 It appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .
 
 World Championship 2007 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 , he is a Limited Duel duelist challenging the player not to use non- LIGHT  monsters.
 
 World Championship 2008 
       World Championship 2008     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , this monster appears as a Duelist in Duel World, a tag duel opponent in CPU Tag Duel, and a downloadable Ghost Duelist. As a Duelist in Duel World, by beating it five times, it unlocks  Watapon  as an opponent in Free Duel Mode. As a tag duel opponent, Marshmallon partners with  Spirit Reaper  and their team name is  Dangerous Pair .

 Over the Nexus 
         Marshmallon in Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus      In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , it appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for July 2011.
