 Luna , known as  Luca ( 龍可   Ruka ) in the Japanese version, is an Supporting Character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Luna , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
