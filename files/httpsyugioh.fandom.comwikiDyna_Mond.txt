 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  monsters , including a  Ritual Monster If this card is  Special Summoned : You can  target  1 card on the  field  and 1 Ritual Monster in your  GY ;  return  both to the  Deck . During your opponent's  turn  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Tribute  this card, then target 1 Ritual Monster in your GY; either  add  it to your  hand  or Special Summon it. You can only use each  effect  of "Dyna Mond"  once per turn .
