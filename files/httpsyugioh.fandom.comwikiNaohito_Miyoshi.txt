 Naohito Miyoshi 
 
 English name 
 Naohito Miyoshi 
 
 
 Japanese name 
 三好   直人 
 
 
 Rōmaji name 
 Miyoshi Naohito 
 
 
 Occupation 
 Manga artist 
 
 
 Naohito Miyoshi  is a Japanese manga artist.
 
 Work 
 Naohito is credited as the artist of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V [1]  manga.
 Naohito is credited with character designs for  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . [2]
