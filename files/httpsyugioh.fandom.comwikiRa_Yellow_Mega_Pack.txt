 Ra Yellow Mega Pack 
 Legendary Collection 2: The Duel Academy Years Mega Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 Ra Yellow Mega Packs  are a remix of the  Legendary Collection 2: The Duel Academy Years Mega Pack . According to  Kevin Tewart , the creation of this set resulted from unexpectedly high demand for  Legendary Collection 2: The Duel Academy Years Mega Pack . [1]

 Features 
 Features two main differences from  Legendary Collection 2: The Duel Academy Years Mega Pack :
 
 Each pack contains 2 additional  Commons , for a total of 11 cards per pack (1 Rare, 1 Super Rare, 1 Ultra Rare, 1 Secret Rare, 7 Commons). 
 The total set size has been reduced to 113 cards from 266, containing the most popular cards from  Legendary Collection 2 , some with different rarities than before. 
 As a result, the chances to pull any one particular card are increased.
