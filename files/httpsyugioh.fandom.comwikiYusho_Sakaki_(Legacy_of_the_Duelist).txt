 Yusho Sakaki , known as  Yusyo Sakaki  ( 榊   遊 勝 ,  Sakaki Yūshō ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yusho Sakaki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
