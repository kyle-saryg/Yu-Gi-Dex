 This card can be searched by " Heritage of the Chalice ", " Infernoble Knight - Roland ", " Noble Knight Borz ", " Ignoble Knight of High Laundsallyn ", " Ignoble Knight of Black Laundsallyn ", " Glory of the Noble Knights ", " Armory Call ", " Noble Knight's Spearholder ", " Hidden Armory ", " Gearbreed ", " Power Tool Dragon ", " Vylon Cube ", " Vylon Delta ", " Vylon Sigma ", " Iron Blacksmith Kotetsu " and " Fairy Tail - Rella ". 
 Use  "Infernoble Arms - Durendal"  to search for the monster to be summoned by this card's second effect. Activate this card's second effect by equipping it to the same monster that was equipped with "Durendal", then using that monster for a  Link Summon  or  Synchro Summon .
