 Anime 
 
 In  episode 122 , a  Duel Ghoul  uses this card during his Duel against  Chazz Princeton . He summons this card on the first turn of the duel. Chazz then activates " Tribute to the Doomed " to destroy this card. 
 In episode 138 of Yu-Gi-Oh! GX, this card appeared as one of the three Dark Army Soldiers that were trying to destroy the defenses of a village.  Burgundy  however fought them off and prevented them from completely destroying the defenses around the village.
