 Condition 
 If this card is  Set  by the  effect  of an “ Altergeist ” card, it can be  activated  this  turn . You can only use each of the following effects of “Altergeist Haunted Rock”  once per turn . ● When this card is  activated :  Send  1 “ Altergeist ”  monster  from your  hand  to the  GY . ● When your opponent activates a  Trap Card : You can send 1 “Altergeist” monster from your hand to the GY;  negate  the activation, and if you do,  destroy  that card.
