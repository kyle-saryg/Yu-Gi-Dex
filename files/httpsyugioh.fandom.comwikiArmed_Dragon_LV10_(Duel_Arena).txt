 Armed Dragon LV10  is a character version of the card " Armed Dragon LV10 ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P082.
 In the beta, it had a default of 4 Rental Points instead, and its partner ID was P038.

 Decks 
 Partner 
 A Level Up Deck focused on fighting with a leveled up " Armed Dragon ." Take appropriate steps with " Armed Dragon LV3 ", such as  Special Summoning  during your opponent's  turn  or protecting it with  Trap Cards , to ensure you get the  Level up!
