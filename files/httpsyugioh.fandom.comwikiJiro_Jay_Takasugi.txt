 Jiro Jay Takasugi  ( 高杉 ’ Ｊａｙ ’ 次郎 ,  Takasugi Jē Jirō ), born  Shigeo Shiraishi  ( 白石   重雄 ,  Shiraishi Shigeo ) is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices Pegasus J. Crawford ( Maximillion Pegasus ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ), [1]   Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ), [2]   Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters: Pyramid of Light  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Super Fusion! Bonds That Transcended Time  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time ).
