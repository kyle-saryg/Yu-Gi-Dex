 A  One-Shot Run  ( 一撃疾走 ,  Wan Shotto Ran ) is a type of Duel fought on  Duel Runners  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . This kind of Duel seems to exist since ancient times, being played in horses instead of Duel Runners. [1] 
 
 Rules 
 Each player Summons one monster. The two monsters battle each other, where their success is dictated by the  Sense  of their controller. Each player starts with 2 shields, each divided into 6 crests. The players attempt to break the crests off their opponent's shields. The player who ends with the most crests wins. [2]
