 Extra Pack 2018  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG - exclusive  cards to the  OCG .

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive cards from  Code of the Duelist ,  Circuit Break ,  Extreme Force , and  Flames of Destruction  as well as  Legendary Collection Kaiba  and  Legendary Dragon Decks . 
 Includes members/support for the " F.A. " and " Vendread " archetypes.
