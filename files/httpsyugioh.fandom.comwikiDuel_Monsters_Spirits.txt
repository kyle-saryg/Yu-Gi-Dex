 Harpie Lady 
       Harpie Lady       Harpie Lady  ( ハーピィ・レディ ,  Hāpi Redi ) is a Duel Spirit that  Jesse Anderson  faces. Rather than dueling with cards, the Harpie Lady simply attacks the duelists themselves, forcing them to fight her (and later, her sisters) off with their monsters.
