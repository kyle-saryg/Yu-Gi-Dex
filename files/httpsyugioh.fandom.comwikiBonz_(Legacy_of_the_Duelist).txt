 Bonz , known as  Ghost Kotsuzuka  (ゴースト骨塚,  Gōsuto Kotsuzuka ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Bonz , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
