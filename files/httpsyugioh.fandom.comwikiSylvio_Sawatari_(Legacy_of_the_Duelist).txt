 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Sylvio Sawatari , known as  Shingo Sawatari ( 沢 渡  シンゴ,  Sawatari Shingo ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Sylvio Sawatari , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
