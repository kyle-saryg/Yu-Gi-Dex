 Lightning Overdrive  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fourth set in the  OCG ' s  11th series , following  Blazing Vortex  and followed by  Dawn of Majesty .

 Features 
 The first print runs of Lightning Overdrive boxes include 1  Lightning Overdrive +1 Bonus Pack . 
 Introduces new support/members for the " Guardian ", " sphinx ", " Drytron ", " @Ignister ". " A.I. ", " Evil★Twin ", " Live☆Twin ", " Lil-la ", " Ki-sikil ", " Rose ", " Rose Dragon ", " Scrap ", " S-Force ", " Paleozoic ", " Plunder Patroll ", " Laval ", " Magistus ", " Starry Knight ", " Springans ", " Dogmatika ", " Tri-Brigade ", " Bujin ", " Hole ", " Trap Hole ", " Traptrix ", " Ice Barrier ", " ZW - ", " Utopia ", " Number ", and " Rank-Up-Magic " archetypes, as well as  Sky Scourge ,  Dark counterpart  and  Book of  series. 
 Introduces the " Branded ", " Amazement ", and " Attraction " archetypes, as well as the first support for " ZS - ", " Zexal " and " Rank-Down-Magic " archetypes to the  OCG/TCG . 
 TCG  version also includes new members/support for " War Rock ", " D/D/D ", " Sunvine ", and " Sunavalon " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box. The set contains 80 cards, or 125 cards in the  Master Set :
 
 1  Holographic Rare 
 8  Ultra Rares   (also available as  Ultimate Rare ,  Secret Rare , and  Prismatic Secret Rare ) 
 10  Super Rares   (also available as  Secret Rare  and  Prismatic Secret Rare ) 
 18  Rares 
 44  Commons 
 In the TCG, the set contains 101 cards:
 
 5  Starlight Rares 
 10 Secret Rares 
 14 Ultra Rares 
 26 Super Rares 
 50 Commons
