 Spencer , known as  Taichi  ( 太 一   Taichi ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. He is an alternate universe version of the  Spencer  that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
 
 Biography 
 Spencer was seen in  the new resistance hideout  along with  Devon Knox  waiting in line for food being served by  Saya Sasayama . Saya then ran away, much to Spencer's confusion (in the dub Spencer does not speak)  [1]
