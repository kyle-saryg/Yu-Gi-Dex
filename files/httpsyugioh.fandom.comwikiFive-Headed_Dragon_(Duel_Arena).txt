 Five-Headed Dragon  is a character version of the card " Five-Headed Dragon ". 
 It appears as an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , as the boss of Stage 12 under Quest mode. Defeating it for the first time as an opponent earns the player 50,000 Duel Points.

 Description 
 This is an evil  dragon  with five  elements :  DARK ,  EARTH ,  WATER ,  FIRE , and  WIND . With its mighty power, it is plotting to conquer the world. But its ambition will be crushed by an  ultimate dragon knight .
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Don't be a bad loser. Admit your defeat and surrender! Ha-ha-ha-ha-ha.
 
 Upon defeat for the first time:
 
 Argh...I lost... You sure have the devil's luck.

 Trivia 
 Each opponent in Stage 12 is based on a member of  the Big Five . This character is based on the Big Five united in the  Legendary Heroes  and  Virtual World  arcs. In both arcs, they used this character's  card counterpart , but were defeated by  Yami Yugi  and a partner ( Seto Kaiba  in the Legendary Heroes arc and  Joey Wheeler  in the Virtual World arc).
