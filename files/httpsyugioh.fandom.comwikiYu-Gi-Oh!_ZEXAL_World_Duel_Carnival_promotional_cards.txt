 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival Bundles 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival  promotional cards  are a set of 3 cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival .  The 3 cards, " Snow Plow Hustle Rustle ", " Night Express Knight " and " Special Schedule ", were all used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime by  Anna Kaboom .
