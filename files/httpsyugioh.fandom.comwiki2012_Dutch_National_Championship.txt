 The  2012 Dutch National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of the Netherlands. It took place in Utrecht on June 9−10, 2012 [1]  and was won by Floris van Asch. [2]

 Main event 
 183 people took part in the main event. [3]  There were eight rounds of  Swiss , after which sixteen of the highest scoring players were entered into the top 16  single-elimination  rounds. [4] 
 In the semi finals, Karsten van Eechoud defeated Thomas Grotenhuis 2-0 [5]  and Floris van Asch defeated Yvar Stok 2-1. [6] 
 In the final, Floris van Asch defeated Karsten van Eechoud 2-1. [7]
