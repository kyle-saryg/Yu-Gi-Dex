 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 When an opponent's monster declares an attack, if you control a  face-up  "Ninja" monster: You can flip this card face-up; negate that attack and end the Battle Phase.  Once  this card is face-up on the field, monsters your opponent controls cannot change their  Battle Positions  while you control a face-up "Ninja" monster(s) . 
 When an opponent's monster declares an attack, if you control a "Ninja" monster: You can flip this card face-up; negate that attack and end the Battle Phase.  While  this card is face-up on the field  and you control a "Ninja" monster , monsters your opponent controls cannot change their  battle positions .
