 Match of the Millennium Twisted Nightmares 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Speed Duel Starter Decks: Twisted Nightmares  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Match of the Millennium , it is the 5th set of Decks in the TCG's  Speed Duel Starter Decks  series, following  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Ultimate Predators .
