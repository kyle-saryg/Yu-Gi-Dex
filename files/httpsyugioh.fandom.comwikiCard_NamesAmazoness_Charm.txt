 Names 
 
 The  Greek  name given translates to " Amazon's Charm ". 
 The name given to this card in the Spanish dub was " Hechizo de la Amazona ". Its name in the American Spanish dub was " Encanto de la Amazona ". Both names mean " Amazon's Charm ", but have different undertones.
