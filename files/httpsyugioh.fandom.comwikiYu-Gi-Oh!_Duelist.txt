 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  is the portion of the English  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga , which was not serialized in the  Shonen Jump  magazine. It occurs when the plot shifted to mainly Duel Monsters related storylines. While the Japanese manga continued numbering as normal and kept the name  Yu-Gi-Oh! ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  started numbering volumes and chapters from 1.
 The first chapter,  Duel 1  is Duel 60 in the Japanese manga. Volume 1 of  Duelist  is Volume 8 of the Japanese manga. The plot contains the original script for the  Duelist Kingdom ,  Dungeon Dice Monsters ,  Battle City  and  Tournament Finals  arcs.
