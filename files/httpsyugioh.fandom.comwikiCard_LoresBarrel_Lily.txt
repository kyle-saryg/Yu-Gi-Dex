 Greek - Κρίνος Πολυβόλο ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards  lore) 
 Αυτό το σατανικό λουλούδι συμπεριφέρεται σαν ένα πιστόλι φτιαγμένο απο τη φύση. Επιτίθεται εκτοξεύοντας "σφαίρες" απο γύρη. 
 
 
 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was " This wicked flower attacks enemies with pollen projectiles. " 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Cette fleur malfaisante attaque ses ennemis avec des projectiles de pollen. " 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Diese bösartige Blume attackiert ihre Feinde mit Pollengeschossen. " 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Fiore malefico che attacca i suoi nemici con proiettili di polline. " 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Esta malvada flor ataca a sus enemigos con proyectiles de polen. "
