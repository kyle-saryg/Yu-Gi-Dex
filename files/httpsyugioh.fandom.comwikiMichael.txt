 Michael , known as  Yuhei Matsuda  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Slifer Red  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 - 6 , his name is given to  Kentaro Miyazaki .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Michael gifts the player with a " Neck Hunter " when his  Deck Recipe  is acquired, and is  Giry 's partner during the Tag Force tournament.
