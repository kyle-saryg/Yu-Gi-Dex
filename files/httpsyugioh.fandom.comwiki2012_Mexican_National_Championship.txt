 The  2012 Mexican National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Mexico. It took place in Queretaro from May 19 to 20, 2012 [1]  and was won by Eli Cerdan. [2]
