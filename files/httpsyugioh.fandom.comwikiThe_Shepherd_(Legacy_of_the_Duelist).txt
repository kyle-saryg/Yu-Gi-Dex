 The Shepherd , known as  Blood Shepherd  (ブラッドシェパード,  Buraddo Shepādo ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  The Shepherd , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
