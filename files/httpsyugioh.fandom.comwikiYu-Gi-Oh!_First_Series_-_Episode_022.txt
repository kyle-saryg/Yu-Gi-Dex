 Summary 
 Featured Duel 
 Sugoroku Mutou vs Seto Kaiba 
 The beginning of the  Duel  is skipped.
 Sugoruku's turn 
 Sugoroku  draws and summons " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " (3000  ATK  / 2500  DEF ) in  Attack Position .
 Kaiba's turn 
 Kaiba  summons 2 " Blue-Eyes White Dragons " (3000 ATK / 2500 DEF) in Attack Position.
 Sugoruku's turn 
 
 Sugoruku passes. 
 Kaiba's turn 
Kaiba summons another " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ". He  attacks  with all three " Blue-Eyes White Dragons ".
 Kaiba wins.
