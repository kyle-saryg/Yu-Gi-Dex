 Dark Ceremony Edition 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Dark Ceremony Edition , while not a Premium Pack, was released on tournament occasions, like the others. It expanded upon the pre-existing  Premium Pack 2 . This pack contained six cards per pack; three  Ritual Monsters  and three related  Ritual Spell Cards . It is the final  Series 1  pack.
