 " Yuga Goha " is the seventy-eighth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on December 12, 2021.
 Duelists enjoyed the various new rules born from the Original Duel Development Road. However, there was someone who cast a dark shadow on the world of Dueling and stoked conflict between the different rules. An envoy of destruction who believes that Dueling is all about fighting and crushing others. His name:  Yuga Goha !
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Yuga Goha 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
 
 Duel continues in  the next episode
