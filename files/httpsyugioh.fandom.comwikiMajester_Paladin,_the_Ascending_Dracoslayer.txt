 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  4  Pendulum Monsters When this card is  Xyz Summoned : You can  add  1 Pendulum Monster from your  Deck  to your  hand  during the  End Phase  of this  turn .  Once per turn : You can  detach  1  material  from this card;  Special Summon  1 face-up " Dracoslayer " Pendulum Monster from your  Extra Deck , but it cannot be used as material for an  Xyz Summon .
