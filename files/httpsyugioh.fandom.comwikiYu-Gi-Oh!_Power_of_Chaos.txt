 Yu-Gi-Oh! Power of Chaos  is a 2004 series of three  Yu-Gi-Oh!  video games for PC. Its titles in order of release are the following:
 
 Yugi the Destiny 
 Kaiba the Revenge 
 Joey the Passion 
 The total number of cards in the game is 711. A number of new cards is introduced in each of the games (155 in the first game, 311 in the second game and 245 in the third game). To have all the cards available, players must choose the "Full Deck" option in the last game ( Joey the Passion).
