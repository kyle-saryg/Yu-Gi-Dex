 Legendary Gold Box 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Legendary Gold Box  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Dark Magician ", " Elemental HERO ", " HERO ", " Neos ", " Prophecy ", " Red-Eyes ", " T.G. ", " Number ", " Utopic ", " Utopic Future ", " Odd-Eyes ", " The Phantom Knights ", " Code Talker ", " Blue-Eyes ", " Crystal Beast ", " Ultimate Crystal ", " Galaxy-Eyes ", " D/D/D ", " Dark Contract ", " Borrel ", " Rokket " and " Yubel " archetypes.
 Introduces the first support for the " Rebellion " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Legendary Gold Box  contains:
 
 1 of 3  Premium Gold Rare  promotional cards. 
 6 Special Packs. Each pack contains 1  Ultra Rare  or  20th Secret Rare , and 4  Normal Parallel Rares . The set contains 48 cards:
 12 Ultra Rares / 20th Secret Rares 
 36 Normal Parallel Rares 
 1 of 3  Field Center Cards . 
 1 Card Display Case. 
 1 of 3 Storage Boxes. 
 Each Korean  Legendary Gold Box  contains:
 
 1 of 3  Premium Gold Rare  promotional cards. 
 6 Special Packs. Each pack contains 1  Ultra Rare  or  Prismatic Secret Rare , and 4  Normal Parallel Rares . The set contains 48 cards:
 12 Ultra Rares / Prismatic Secret Rares 
 36 Normal Parallel Rares 
 1 of 3  Field Center Cards . 
 1 Card Display Case. 
 1 of 3 Storage Boxes.
