 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Chaos Zone ", " Damage = Reptile ", " King of the Feral Imps ", " Flying Saucer Muusik'i ", " Crop Circles " and " "A" Cell Recombination Device ". 
 The effect of " Alien Dog " provides the most reliable means of meeting the cost of 2  A-Counter s required to Special Summon this card. You will be able to bring three " Alien " monsters on the field in one turn. 
 This card being able to spread more than one A-Counter among opposing monsters also helps " Alien Hunter ", " Alien Hypno " and " Alien Telepath ".
