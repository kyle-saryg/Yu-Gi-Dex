 Legendary Collection 4: Joey's World  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  collector's set. It is the fourth in the  Legendary Collection  series. The set contains a collapsible hard-backed game board, three promotional cards, six Tokens and five  Legendary Collection 4: Joey's World Mega Packs . [1]

 Features 
 Three originally  TCG exclusive  cards are included per box:  Joey Wheeler 's " Blue Flame Swordsman ",  Mai Valentine 's " Harpie Lady Phoenix Formation " and  Marik Ishtar 's " Card of Last Will ", the last of which is  illegal  in official tournaments due to its "insane power level". [2]  Also included are 6  Ultra Rare   Token  cards: Joey's 4  Sheep Tokens  (previously released only as convention exclusives) and 2  Lamb Tokens  (never before released in the  TCG ). [3]
