 Starter Deck 2013 Enhancement Pack  is a special  Booster Pack  for  Starter Deck 2013 . Each  Starter Deck 2013  contains 2  Enhancement Packs .

 Features 
 Players can use the cards in the  Enhancement Pack  to power up their  Starter Deck 2013 .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Starter Deck 2013  contains 2 packs of  Starter Deck 2013 Enhancement Pack . 1 of the pack contains 1 " Number C39: Utopia Ray V " and 4 other cards, while the other pack contains 1 " Rank-Up-Magic Limited Barian's Force " and 4 other cards. There are 15 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
