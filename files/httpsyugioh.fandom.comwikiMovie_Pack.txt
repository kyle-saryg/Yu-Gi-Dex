 Movie Pack 
 Bonds Beyond Time Movie Pack Movie Pack Vol.2  (Korean) 
 
 
 
 
 
 Movie Pack  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). This pack was given to people who attended the film " Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light ".
 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 4 cards, including:
 
 1  Super Rare 
 3  Commons
