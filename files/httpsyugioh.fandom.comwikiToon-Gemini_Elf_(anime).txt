 Trigger 
 [  Spellcaster  /  Toon  ] This card cannot  attack  during the  turn  that it is  Normal Summoned ,  Flip Summoned , or  Special Summoned . When " Toon World " on the  field  is  destroyed , this card is also destroyed. If "Toon World" is on your side of the field and your opponent does not  control  a  Toon Monster , this card can attack your opponent's  Life Points   directly . When this card inflicts  Battle Damage  to your opponent's Life Points, your opponent  discards  1 card  randomly  from his/her  hand .
