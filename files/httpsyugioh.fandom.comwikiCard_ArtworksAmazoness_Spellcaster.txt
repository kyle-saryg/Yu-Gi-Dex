 Anime Anime Manga 
 
 When this card was released in the  OCG , it was completely redrawn compared to its  anime  artwork. 
 When this card was released internationally, its artwork was edited to change the pentacle into another symbol, to avoid any religious connotations. The new symbol is reminiscent of the emblem of the Freemasons.
