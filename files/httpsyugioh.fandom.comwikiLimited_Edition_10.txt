 Distributed by V Jump Magazine for the July Issue of 2007. Two different packs were released:  Chaos Black , which includes cards used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime, and  Holy White , which has cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga and  Yu-Gi-Oh! R .
 
 Breakdown
