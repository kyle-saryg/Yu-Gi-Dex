 Names 
 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Φράγμα Απόλυτου Μηδέν ") is an exact translation of the TCG name of this card in Greek. A possible translation of the OCG name (" Absolute Ice Wall ") could be " Τοίχος απο Απόλυτο Πάγο ". 
 The Croatian name pays homage to it's Japanese original name, and translates into "Wall of Total Ice"
