 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Masked Atticus   is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of the  Nightshroud -possessed  Atticus Rhodes , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
