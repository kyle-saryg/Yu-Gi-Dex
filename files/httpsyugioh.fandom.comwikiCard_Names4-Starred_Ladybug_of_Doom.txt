 This card's approximated Arabic name (دعسوقة الهلاك ذات النجوم الأربع) roughly translates to " The Doom Ladybug with Four Stars ". 
 The Japanese name of this card ( 4-Star Ladybug of Death ) can be translated in Greek as " Πασχαλίτσα 4 Αστέρων του Θανάτου ".
