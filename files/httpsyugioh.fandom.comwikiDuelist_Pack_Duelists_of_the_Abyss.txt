 Duelist Pack: Duelists of the Abyss  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Mako Tsunami ,  Reginald Kastle/Nash ,  Ryoga Kamishiro ,  Trout  and  Skye Zaizen . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Number ", " Number 10X ", " Rank-Up-Magic ", " Chaos ", " CXyz ", " Xyz ", and " Marincess " archetypes, as well as the  Umi  series.
 Introduces the first members/support for " Barian's ", " Seventh ", " Kairyu-Shin " , and " Sea Stealth " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 46 cards:
 
 1  Holographic Rare  (1  Extra Secret Rare  in the Korean set) 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 7  Super Rares 
 9  Rares 
 24  Commons 
 The Ultra Rare and Super Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rare .
