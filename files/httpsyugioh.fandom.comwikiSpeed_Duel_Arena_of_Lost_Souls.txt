 Speed Duel: Arena of Lost Souls 
 Speed Duel: Attack from the Deep 
 
 
 
 
 
 Speed Duel: Arena of Lost Souls  is the first  Speed Duel   Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes 5 new  Skill Cards  (1 for  Yugi , 1 for  Ishizu  and 3 for  Bonz ). 
 Introduces to the Speed Duel format new members and/or support for the " Gravekeeper's " archetype, as well as the  Three Musketeers of Face Cards  series. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 4 cards. The set contains 45 cards and 5 Skill Cards:
