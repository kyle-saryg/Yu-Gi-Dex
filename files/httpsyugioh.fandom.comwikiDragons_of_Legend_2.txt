 Dragons of Legend 
 Dragons of Legend 2 
 Dragons of Legend: Unleashed 
 
 
 
 
 
 Dragons of Legend 2  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  (TCG) . It includes dozens of famous cards from the original  Yu-Gi-Oh!  TV series,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Ally  and  Aura Sentia . 
 Includes members/support for the " Ancient Gear ", " Legendary Dragon ", " Legendary Knight ", " Red-Eyes " and " Toon " archetypes.
 Introduces the " Aquaactress ", " Aquarium " and " Prediction Princess " archetypes to the TCG.

 Trivia 
 Early promotional material claimed this set would include cards from "all four TV series" [1]   ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  having not been announced in English at the time). However, when it was released, it only contained cards from the original  Yu-Gi-Oh!  TV series,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V .
