 "Opening of the Duel Tournament!!"  is the eighth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  manga. It was first published November 3, 2012, in the  Saikyō Jump  magazine.

 Summary 
 Yuma  and his friends manage to enter the  Team Duel Tournament . After  Gauche  announces the rules,  Team ZEXAL  is set to face  Team Fire Stars . The real challenge starts now!
 
 Featured Duel: Team ZEXAL vs. Team Fire Stars 
 This is a Team Duel which uses the rules of the  Team Duel Tournament ; check the article for rules.
 
 Duel 1: Yuma Tsukumo vs. Shota Geki 
 Turn 1: Yuma 
 Yuma 's starting  hand  contains " Dododo Bot ", " Goblindbergh ", " Blustering Winds ", " Gagagadraw " and " Xyz Reborn ". He  draws  " Amarylease ". He  Sets  a  monster .
 Turn 2: Shota 
 Shota 's starting hand contains " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Gorilla ", " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Snake ", " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Spirit ", " Pyrotech Mech - Shiryu " and " Pyrorex the Elemental Lord ". He draws " Fire Formation - Tensu ". Shota  Normal Summons  "Gorilla" (1600/1000). He then  activates  "Tensu", which allows him to Normal Summon one more Level 4 or lower Beast-Warrior Type monster per turn in addition to his Normal Summon/Set. Shota Normal Summons "Snake" (1800/600) via its  effect . The second effect of "Tensu" raises the  ATKs  of his  Beast-Warrior - Type  monsters by 100 ("Gorilla": 1600 → 1700) ("Snake": 1800 → 1900). Shota  attacks  Yuma's Set monster with "Gorilla"; it is "Amarylease", who is  destroyed . The effect of "Gorilla" allows Shota to Set a " Fire Formation "  Spell Card  directly from his  Deck , as it destroyed a monster by battle and sent it to the Graveyard. "Snake" attacks Yuma  directly  (Yuma 4000 → 2100). Shota Overlays his two  Level  4 monsters to  Xyz Summon  " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Tiger King " (2200/1800 →2300/1800) in  Attack Position ; its effect allows Shota to Set another "Fire Formation" Spell/ Trap Card  from his Deck. Shota activates his Set " Fire Formation - Tenki ",  adding  " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Raven " from his Deck to his hand. "Tenki" also increases the ATK of all Beast-Warrior-Type monsters Shota controls by 100 ("Tiger King": 2300 → 2400).
 Turn 3: Yuma 
Yuma Normal Summons " Goblindbergh " (1400/0) and activates its effect,  Special Summoning  "Dododo Bot" (1800/1900) from his hand while changing itself to  Defense Position . Yuma Overlays his two Level 4 monsters to Xyz Summon " Zubaba General " (2000/1000) in Attack Position.
 Duel continues in  the next chapter .
