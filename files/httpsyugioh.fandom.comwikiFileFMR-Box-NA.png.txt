 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Forbidden Memories  promotional cards  are a set of 3  Prismatic Secret Rare  cards that came with the Premium Edition of the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . [1] 
 Although  Forbidden Memories  and its bundled cards were released on November 25, Konami's official card database lists the cards as having been released on the preceding March 20. [2]
