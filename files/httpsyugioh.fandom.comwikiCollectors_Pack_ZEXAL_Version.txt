 Features 
 A new concept of pack that contains cards from both the animated series and the manga! 
 Existing themes from the animated series will be powered up! It will lead to a further diversification of Decks by strengthening existing themes. 
 Included inside the Japanese set is a completely brand new kind of rare card: " Collector's Rare ".
 The " Super Rare " and " Ultra Rare " cards are also available as " Collector's Rare " in the Japanese set, and as " Secret Rare " in the Korean set. 
 By reprinting popular cards inside, it's a product that will appeal to existing players of the Official Card Game. 
 This pack is the  OCG  equivalent of  Number Hunters . 
 Contains cards used by  Ryoga Kamishiro ,  Bronk Stone ,  Quattro ,  Trey ,  Dextra ,  Nistro ,  Alito ,  Fender ,  Devon Knox ,  Lotus Hanazoe ,  Carlyle Chesterton ,  Art Stanley ,  Brooke Walker ,  Mayday Walker , the  Shadow Giant ,  Mach  and  Cologne . 
 New cards for the " Chronomaly ", " Gimmick Puppet ", " Heroic Challenger ", " Butterspy ", " Number " and " Photon " archetypes.
