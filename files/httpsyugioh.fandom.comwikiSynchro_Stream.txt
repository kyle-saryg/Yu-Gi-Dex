 Effect 
 Trigger-like 
 Activate  only while there are 2 or more  face-up   Synchro Monsters  on the  field .  Equip  this card to one of them. When the equipped monster  attacks , you can have it gain  ATK  equal to the ATK of another face-up Synchro Monster on the  field  until the  End Phase .

 シンクロ・ストリーム
