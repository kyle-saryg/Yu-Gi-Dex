 The Gods 
 The three  Egyptian God  normally card cannot be played without the corresponding item required to summon them.  The item can only be wielded by the "Master of Gods". These items can only be used once per mission. Once used it is gone for the rest of that mission.
 
 Obelisk the Tormentor  (use " Soul of Obelisk ") 
 Slifer the Sky Dragon  (use " Slifer's Orb ") 
 The Winged Dragon of Ra  (use " Eye of Ra ") 
 Fusions 
 Some Monsters takes special means to use them in battle, these monsters would be summoned by either fusing two monsters already on your team together or by using a specific card on corresponding monster. An example of this would be  Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon  by fusing all three " Blue-Eyes White Dragons , and using Black Luster Ritual on Gaia the Fierce Knight to summon Black Luster Soldier.
 
 Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon  (use " Polymerization " on all three " Blue-Eyes White Dragons ") 
 Black Skull Dragon  (use " Polymerization " on " Red-Eyes Black Dragon " and " Summoned Skull ") 
 Meteor Black Dragon  (use " Polymerization " on " Red-Eyes Black Dragon " and " Meteor Dragon ") 
 Gaia the Dragon Champion  (use " Polymerization " on " Gaia The Fierce Knight " and " Curse of Dragon ") 
 Thousand Dragon  (use " Polymerization " on " Baby Dragon " and " Time Wizard ") 
 Twin-Headed Thunder Dragon  (use " Polymerization " on " Thunder Dragon " and " Two-Headed King Rex " 
 Magician of Black Chaos  (use " Dark Magic Ritual " on " Dark Magician ") 
 Cosmo Queen  (use " Polymerization " on " Dark Elf " and " Mystical Elf ") 
 Black Luster Soldier  (use " Black Luster Ritual " on " Gaia the Fierce Knight ") 
 Rabid Horseman  (use " Polymerization " on " Mystic Horseman " and " Battle Ox ") 
 Chimera the Flying Mythical Beast  (use " Polymerization " on " Gazelle the King of Mythical Beasts " and " Berfomet ") 
 Crimson Sunbird  (use " Polymerization " on " Mavelus " and " Wing Eagle ") 
 Perfectly Ultimate Great Moth  (use " Cocoon of Evolution " on " Larvae Moth ") 
 Gate Guardian  (use " Polymerization " on " Sanga of the Thunder ", " Kazejin ", and " Suijin ") 
 Armored Zombie  (use " Call of the Haunted " on " Zanki ") 
 Dragon Zombie  (use " Call of the Haunted " on " Crawling Dragon ") 
 Metalzoa  (use " Metalmorph " on " Zoa ") 
 Red-Eyes Black Metal Dragon  (use " Metalmorph " on " Red-Eyes Black Dragon ") 
 Valkyrion the Magna Warrior  (use " Polymerization " on " Alpha The Magnet Warrior ", " Beta The Magnet Warrior ", and " Gamma The Magnet Warrior ")
