 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (الوحش المسلّح القديم) literally  Old/Ancient Equipped Monster . This is probably because in English "Gear" can mean "Equipment". 
 The given approximated Arabic name (حيوان التّروس الأثريّة) translates to  Animal of Ancient/Antique Gears .
