Main card page: " Clear Wing Synchro Dragon " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Clear "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「クリアー」 .   As its English name contains "Clear", it  may  be treated a member of the "Clear" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
