 King  (キング  Kingu ) is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. He is a higher-up at  SOL Technologies  who communicates via a hologram of a white king chess piece. In the third season, its revealed that the title of King is shared by three individuals who are the stockholders of  SOL Technologies . They communicate via holograms of red, blue and yellow stars.
 
 History 
 Season 1 
 King was present when  Akira  was speaking to  Bishop . [1] 
 
 Season 3 
 After hearing about  Queen 's defeat the Kings worry that SOL Tech's stock will crash if the public find out about it and order Akira to take care of the matter. [2]
