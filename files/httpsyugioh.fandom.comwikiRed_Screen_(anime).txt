 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 While this card is  face-up  on the  field ,  monsters  with 2000 or more  ATK  cannot  declare  an  attack . During each of your  End Phases , take 2000  damage . If you  control  a face-up " Red Dragon Archfiend ",  negate  this card's  effects  and  destroy  it.
