 Battles of Legend: Relentless Revenge  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Duke Devlin ,  Sam ,  Leon von Schroeder ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Jesse Anderson ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Lucien Grimley ,  Z-one ,  Bronk Stone ,  Vector ,  Iggy Arlo , and  Varis . 
 Includes new members and/or support for the " Borrel ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Galaxy ", " Neo-Spacian ", " Number ", " Photon ", " Noble Knight ", and " Timelord " archetypes, as well as the  Fairy Tale ,  Railway , and " Topologic " series.
