 OCG Rulings 
 This card targets 1 " Ancient Gear " monster in your  Graveyard . [1] 
 This card can also target " Ultimate Ancient Gear Golem ". (In this case, it is returned to the  Extra Deck .) [1] 
 When resolving this card, if the targeted monster is no longer in the Graveyard, the effect is not applied. [1]
