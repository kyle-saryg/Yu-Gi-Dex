 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 
 
 
 Aoi Kisaragi  is the name of a  KaibaCorp  employee who was one of  Trueman's victims .
 His name appeared on a list of names of people at  KaibaCorp . Each name disappeared after  Trueman  sent them to the  World of Darkness . Aoi was the second last to disappear from the list, leaving only  Kazuhiko Mashirito  and  Seto Kaiba .
