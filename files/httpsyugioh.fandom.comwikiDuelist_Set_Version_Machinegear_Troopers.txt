 Features 
 This set includes many " Geargia " and " Gadget " reprints and 3 new cards (1 new  Xyz Monster  and 2 new  Effect Monsters ). 
 Breakdown 
 Both the Japanese and Korean version contains:
 
 3 new " Geargia " cards (1  Secret Rare , 2  Ultra Rare ) 
 The Japanese version also contains:
 
 37 reprinted cards (1 for the Extra Deck) 
 1 Rubber Duel Field 
 1 Deck Case 
 70 Card Sleeves 
 1 Power-Up Pack (10 different cards, 5 cards each pack in Ultra Rare) 
 1 special Code card from the "V Jump 20th Anniversary Edition" in full-color for the new 3DS game 
 The Korean version also contains:
 
 41 reprinted cards (1 for the Extra Deck) 
 1  Gold Series 2013  Booster Pack 
 2  Return of the Duelist  Booster Packs
