 Names 
 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Γήϊνη Προστασία ") translates to " Earthly Protection ". A possible translation of the Japanese name (" Divine Protection of the Goddess ") could be " Θεϊκή Προστασία της Θεάς ".
