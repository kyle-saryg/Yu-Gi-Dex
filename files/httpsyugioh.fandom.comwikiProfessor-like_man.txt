 The  professor-like man  (教授風の男) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is one of two characters in the series whose appearance is based on  Professor Yoshimori  from the  manga , the other being the series  actual incarnation of Yoshimori .
 
 Biography 
 The professor-like man was one of the customers at  Burger World , during the search for  Tetsuo Sasaki . [1]
