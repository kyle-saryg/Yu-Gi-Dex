 Kite Tenjo , known as  Kaito Tenjo  ( 天 城  カイト,  Tenjō Kaito  ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Kite Tenjo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
