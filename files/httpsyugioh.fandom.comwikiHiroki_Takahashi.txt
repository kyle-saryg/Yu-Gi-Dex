 Hiroki Takahashi  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He provides the voices of Katsuya Jonouchi ( Joey Wheeler ) and  Legendary Knight Hermos  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) [1] .
