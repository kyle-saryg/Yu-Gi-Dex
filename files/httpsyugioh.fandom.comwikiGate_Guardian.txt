   English 
 
 
Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . Must first be  Special Summoned  (from your  hand ) by  Tributing  1 " Sanga of the Thunder ", " Kazejin ", and " Suijin ".

   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
This card can only be summoned by offering " Sanga of the Thunder ", " Kazejin ", and " Suijin " as a Tribute. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
 A combined being that consists of  Sanga ,  Kazejin , and  Suijin . Its powers place it among the strongest of all beings.
