 Structure Deck: Soulburner  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  (OCG) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  (TCG). It is the 37th Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Master Link . It is the 44th Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Zombie Horde .
