 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  control  a " Pegasus/Spell A ", this card can attack your opponent  directly . When this card inflicts  battle damage  to your opponent: Target 1 card in either  Graveyard ; return that target to the  hand . -I  Summon  " Toon Masked Sorcerer "!-
