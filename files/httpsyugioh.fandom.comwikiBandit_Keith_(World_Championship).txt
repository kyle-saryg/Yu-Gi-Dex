 Bandit Keith  (真崎杏子  Bandetto Kīsu ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  and  World Championship  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Bandit Keith , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  and  anime . Interestingly, this depiction shows him with a  KC mass production Disk , which has never used canonically.

 Appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , Keith is a Level 2 opponent, unlocked by defeating all Level 1 opponents, at least five times each.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , Keith is a Level 3 opponent, unlocked by defeating all Level 2 opponents, at least five times each.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 6 EX 2 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monster International  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Worldwide Edition: Stairway to the Destined Duel , Keith is an unlockable opponent. He becomes available after the player has at least five more wins than losses against every character, with the exceptions of  Mokuba Kaiba  and  Duke Devlin .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 7: The Duelcity Legend  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards , Keith is a member of the  Ghouls . The player can find him at the park, while searching for  Joey Wheeler  and  Tea Gardner , who have been taken captive by  Marik Ishtar . Keith defeats  Weevil Underwood  in a Duel and demands that he leave his Deck. He then faces the player in a Duel. When Yugi and the player rescue  Joey Wheeler  and  Tea Gardner  from the Ghouls, the player Duels Keith again.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 8: Reshef of Destruction  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction , 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters EX 3  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship Tournament 2004 , Keith is a Level 3 opponent. The player can unlock him by defeating all Level 2 opponents, at least five times each and by having the card " Barrel Dragon ", in their  trunk , but not their  Deck . By defeating him at least ten times and having at least twice as much wins as losses against him, the player can unlock the  Dark Ruler Ha Des  Booster Pack.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters World Championship 2008  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , the player can unlock Keith as an opponent, by conducting 1000  Summons .
