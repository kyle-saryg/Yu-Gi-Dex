 Elemental HERO Bubbleman  is a character version of the card " Elemental HERO Bubbleman ". 
 He appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. His partner ID is P077.

 Description 
 A slightly portly " Elemental HERO " who's got your back when you don't have much in your  hand .
 
 Decks 
 A  Deck  designed to cause maximum damage in a single  turn . Use the  Special Summon   effects  of " Elemental HERO Bubbleman " and " Photon Thrasher " to instantly line up  Xyz Materials . Make skillful use of " Elemental HERO Stratos " and " A Hero Lives " and you can win in a single turn!
