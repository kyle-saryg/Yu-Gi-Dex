 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 [Requirement] : If you  control  a  face-up  " Red-Eyes Black Dragon ". [Effect] : Inflict 2400  damage  to your opponent, also you cannot  attack  with "Red-Eyes Black Dragon" this  turn .
