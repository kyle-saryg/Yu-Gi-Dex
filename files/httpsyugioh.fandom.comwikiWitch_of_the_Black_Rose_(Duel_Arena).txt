 Witch of the Black Rose  is a character version of the card " Witch of the Black Rose ".
 She appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P096. She also appears as an opponent at Stage 7-3 under Quest mode.

 Description 
 A  Spellcaster - Type   Tuner  that supports  drawing  cards, though limited to  Monster Cards .
 
 Opponent Quote 
 ...So many monsters.
 
 Decks 
 A  Deck  comprised entirely of Monster Cards that can use their own  effects . A mixture of monsters whose effects can be applied in a variety of places, including your  hand , the  field  and the  Graveyard . You will also be able to perform  Synchro  and  Xyz Summons !
