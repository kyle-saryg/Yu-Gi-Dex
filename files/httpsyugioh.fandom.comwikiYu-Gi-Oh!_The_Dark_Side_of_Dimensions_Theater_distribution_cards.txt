 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Theater distribution card 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Theater distribution cards  are a series of cards that were given to people who attended the film  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions .

 Breakdown 
 In Japan, 4  Kaiba Corporation Common  cards were distributed during the first four weeks:
 
 " Gandora-X the Dragon of Demolition " (April 23 - April 29) 
 " Lord Gaia the Fierce Knight " (April 30 - May 6) 
 " Lemon Magician Girl " (May 7 - May 13) 
 " Palladium Oracle Mahad " (May 14 - May 20) 
 In South Korea, 8  Ultra Rare  cards were distributed in packs of 2 cards. Each pack contains:
 
 1 new card (MVPC-KR001 ~ MVPC-KR004) 
 1 reprinted card (MVPC-KR005 ~ MVPC-KR008) 
 In the  TCG :
 
 1  Gold Secret Rare  card
