 Phantom Darkness  is the seventh set of the  OCG  Series 5 Booster Packs after  Gladiator's Assault . It is followed by  Light of Destruction .
 The North American Sneak Peeks for this set took place from January 26–27, 2008.
 Packs of the  TCG  set were also printed in the  Phantom Darkness: Special Edition .
 This is the first pack that has a  Ghost Rare  that is not the same monster as the  cover card .

 Features 
 This pack features a new  series  of  DARK  monsters, focusing on  Dark counterparts  of other cards. 
 The TCG version features cards by  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Yami Bakura , and  Yami Marik  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime, as well as  Deschutes Lew ,  Yami Yugi ,  Tilla Mook  and  Yako Tenma  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! R . This pack also features cards used by characters in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  like  Yubel ,  Axel Brodie ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Jesse Anderson ,  Zane Truesdale  and  Aster Phoenix .
