Capsule Monsters  may refer to any of the following:
 
 Capsule Monster Chess , a board game from the manga and first anime series. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters , a spin-off anime series. 
 Capsule Monsters , a board game played in the above spin-off series. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game , a real world game based on the  Capsule Monsters  game. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum  a video game, featuring a variation of the  Capsule Monsters  Game.
