 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Take 1 " Photon " or " Galaxy "  Continuous Spell / Trap  from your  Deck , and either  add  it to your  hand  or place it  face-up  on your  field . If this  Set  card you  control  is  destroyed  by an opponent's  card effect  during their  turn  and you  control  " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon " or an  Xyz Monster  that has it as  material : You can make it become the  End Phase .
