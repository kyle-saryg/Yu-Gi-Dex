 " Gagagigo " and " Invader of Darkness " are featured on this card's artwork. 
 The card art depicts a reverse angle shot of the artwork featured in " Spirit Barrier ". 
 This card has a  Continuous Trap Card  counterpart, " Astral Barrier ". 
 This card's artwork is a reversal of " My Body as a Shield " (in which " Marauding Captain " shields "Gagagigo" from " Blazing Inpachi "'s attack), as "Gagagigo" shields one of "Marauding Captain"'s men from "Invader of Darkness"'s attack.
