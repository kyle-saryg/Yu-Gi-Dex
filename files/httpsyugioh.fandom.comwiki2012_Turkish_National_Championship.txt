 The  2012 Turkish National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Turkey. It took place in Istanbul on June 3, 2012. [1] 
 
 Events 
 The entry fee was 45  TL  for the main event and 20 TL for the  Dragon Duel . Registration took place between 11:00−12:55 and the tournament began at 13:00. [1]
