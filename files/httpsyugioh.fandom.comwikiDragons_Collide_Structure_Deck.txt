 Dragons Collide Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the twenty-first Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Gates of the Underworld Structure Deck . This Deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Dragonic Legion  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 This is the first Structure Deck with 2 cover-cards. This Deck features  LIGHT  and  DARK  attributes, most of them are  Dragon -Type monsters. It also includes " Lightsworn " monster reprints, as well as new  Dragon -Type monsters and support cards.
 The "Power Up With Packs!" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Darkstorm Dragon " and " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation " from  Order of Chaos , " Judgment Dragon " from  Ra Yellow Mega Pack , " Alexandrite Dragon " and " Thunder End Dragon " from  Photon Shockwave  (LIGHT-supporting Spell and Trap cards from the set are also recommended) and " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon " and " Ehren, Lightsworn Monk " from the  Collectible Tins 2011  Wave 2 (from the  Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon Tin  and  Number 10: Illumiknight Tin , respectively).
