 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Seto Kaiba ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Crow Hogan ,  Kite Tenjo , and  Sylvio Sawatari . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Abyss Actor ", " Abyss Script ", " Blackwing ", " Blue-Eyes ", " Chaos ", " Cyber ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Fusion ", " Galaxy ", and " Photon " archetypes, as well as the  signature move  and  Signer Dragon  series.
