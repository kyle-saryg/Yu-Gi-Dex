 Astral Pack Two  is a  Booster Pack  that was released to  TCG  tournament locations. This set of 25 cards contains 1 foil card in each pack. There is also a new hard-to-get  Monster Card  in this set. It is the second  Astral Pack , following  Astral Pack One .
