 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Fairy "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「フェアリー」 .   As its English name contains "Fairy", it  may  be treated a member of the "Fairy" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 For Tag Force version of this card, see  Fortune Fairy Chee (Tag Force) .

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When you  draw  this card: You can  reveal  it;  Special Summon  it from your  hand . If this card is Special Summoned from the hand: You can make each player draw 1 card. You can only use each  effect  of "Fortune Fairy Chee"  once per turn .
