 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 This card can be  searched  by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick " and " Lilith, Lady of Lament ". 
 Using this card when there are only  Monster Cards  that can be  Special Summoned  in the player's  hand  ensures something will be Summoned. It is best used when there is only 1 card in your hand, ensuring complete control over what card is  Special Summoned / discarded . 
 This card may be used to Summon high  Level  monsters without Tribute.
