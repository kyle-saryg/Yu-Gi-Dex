 This card's name is treated as " Revival Jam " while  face-up  on the  field . This card's  ATK  and  DEF  become equal to the DEF of the " Metal Reflect Slime " used in its  Fusion Summon . When this card is  destroyed by battle  and  sent  to the  Graveyard , its  controller  can  Special Summon  it to their side of the field in  face-up   Defense Position .
