 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena 
 
 
 
 Des Koala  is a character version of the card " Des Koala ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P066. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 6-4 under Quest mode.

 Decks 
 Partner 
 A  Deck  comprised of  monsters  that can be used as walls which cause  damage  to your opponent, starting with " Des Koala ", and  Spell  &  Trap Cards  with the same  effect . Rather than defending all the time, also  attack  when the time is right, whittling down your opponent's  Life Points !
