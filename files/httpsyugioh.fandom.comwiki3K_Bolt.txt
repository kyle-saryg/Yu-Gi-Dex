 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 [Requirement]  If you  control  3  face-up   monsters  ( Aqua ). [Effect]  Choose 1 face-up monster ( Level  8 or lower) your opponent controls. Its  original ATK  becomes 0 until the end of this  turn .
