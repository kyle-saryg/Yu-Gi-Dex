 OTS Tournament Pack 19  is a booster pack that is available as a Tournament Prize or Participation Pack released to the  TCG  tournament locations. It is the nineteenth  OTS Tournament Pack , following  OTS Tournament Pack 18 .
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 3 cards per pack.The set contains 29 cards:
 
 3  Ultimate Rares 
 10  Super Rares 
 16  Commons 
 The Portuguese version contains 11 more Common cards, bringing the total to 40 cards
