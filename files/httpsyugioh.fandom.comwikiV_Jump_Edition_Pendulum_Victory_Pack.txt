 V Jump Edition Pendulum Victory Pack  is the twenty-eighth installment of the  Limited Edition  series. This pack contains cards used by  Yuya Sakaki  and  Declan Akaba  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  special chapter .
