 These cards were handed out to participants of the 2011 Duelist League, open only to those aged 14 and under. All cards were available in purple, red, green, and blue  Rare  versions. These colors are the same as their  Duelist League 2 participation cards  counterparts. This set is followed by  Duelist League 13 participation cards .
 
 Breakdown 
 All 20 cards are  Rare .
