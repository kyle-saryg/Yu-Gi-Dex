 Biography 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 
 When the  Dark Magician Girl  brought  Yugi  and  Yami Yugi  to the  Duel Monsters Spirit World  to revive the  Timaeus , Fairy Guardian was among the other  spirits  in the background.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Fairy Guardian appeared in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  as a background character observing the  Duel  between  Kaibaman  and  Jaden Yuki . [1]
