The  Space Zone  is the setting for the first stage of  Death-T  theme park.
 It consists of an artificial space station. A guide comes to the players to warn them that the station is being attacked. The players then put on players and proceed to the battlefield to play the  Stardust Shootout  game with the attackers.
 Anzu Mazaki  was hired to play the guide. Unbeknownst to her employer, she was friends with  Yugi Mutou , who was being made play the game.
 The zone consists of tunnels, a rack of life vests used to play the game and the battlefield for the game.
