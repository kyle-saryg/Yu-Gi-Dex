 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Pay  half your  LP ;  send  1  monster  from your  Extra Deck  to the  GY . You can only  activate  1 "Extra-Foolish Burial" per  turn . You cannot  Set   Spells / Traps  during the turn you activate this card.
