This is the Forbidden/Restricted List for  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller , used as the Default List when you first start the game. It is based off the  September 2006 Forbidden/Limited List .
 Note that after you beat the game's story, you'll be able to add 1  Forbidden  card to your Deck.
