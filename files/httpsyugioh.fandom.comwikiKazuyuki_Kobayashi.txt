 Kazuyuki Kobayashi  is a Japanese animation director.
 
Kobayashi is credited as animation director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh!  episodes. #068 " Legendary Fisherman, Part 1 " #075 " Friends 'Til the End, Part 1 " #081 " Let the Finals Begin! " #087 " Awakening of Evil, Part 2 " 
Kobayashi is credited as animation director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episodes. #011 " The Take Back, Part 1 " #042 " The Signs of Time " #130 " Bound to the Future, Bonds to Our Companions " #134 " For Synchro's Sake " #141 " Duel of Despair! Fortissimo the Mobile Fortress! "
