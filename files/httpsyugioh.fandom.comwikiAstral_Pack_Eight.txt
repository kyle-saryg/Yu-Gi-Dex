 Astral Pack Eight 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Astral Pack Eight  is a booster pack that was released to  TCG  tournament locations. It is the eighth  Astral Pack , following  Astral Pack Seven .
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 3 cards per pack. The set contains 27 cards. This consists of:
 
 3  Ultimate Rares 
 10  Super Rares 
 14  Commons
