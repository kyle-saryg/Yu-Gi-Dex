 Tristan Taylor , known as  Honda Hiroto  (本田ヒロト,  Hiroto Honda ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Tristan Taylor , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
