 Anna Kaboom , known as  Anna Kozuki  ( 神 月  アンナ  Kōzuki Anna ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Anna Kaboom , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
