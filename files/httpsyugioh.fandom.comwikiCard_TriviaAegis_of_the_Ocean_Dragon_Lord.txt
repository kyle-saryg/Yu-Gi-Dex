 True to name, " Ocean Dragon Lord - Neo-Daedalus " appears in this card's artwork. 
 In Greek mythology, aegis is the shield used by the Greek god Zeus. In description it is a large collar or cape worn in ancient times to display the protection provided by a high religious authority. 
 This card is the WATER-version of " Gagagaguard ".
