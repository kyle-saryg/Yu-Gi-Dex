 Yami Bakura , also translated as  Dark Bakura  ( 闇 バクラ,  Yami Bakura ), is a character in the  Duel Terminal  arcade games. This is a video game depiction of  Yami Bakura , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga and anime.
