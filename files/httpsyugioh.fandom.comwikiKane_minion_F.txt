 Kane minion F , known as  Hirutani henchman F  (蛭谷の子分Ｆ  Hirutani no kobun Effu ) in the Japanese version is a character in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters . He is the game's incarnation of  yo-yo trooper 8  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Description 
 A  Gammon High School  goon who pledged allegiance to  Diesel Kane . Beyond that, there is nothing else noteworthy about this thug.
 
 Crest Pool 
 Minion F's  Crest Pool  consists of  Warriors ,  Spellcasters  and  Beasts .
