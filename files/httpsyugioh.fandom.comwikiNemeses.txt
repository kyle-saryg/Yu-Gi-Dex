 " Nemeses " (ネメシス  Nemeshisu ) is an  archetype  that debuted in " Eternity Code ".
 
 Playing Style 
 The core style revolves around banishing monsters of different types/attributes for the Archnemesis monsters to activate their effects.
 This archetype works well with " Myutant " as both involving banishing with various Attributes and Types.
 
 Etmology 
 The lower level monsters are called "Nemeses", whilst the Level 11 monsters are called "Archnemeses".
" Archnemeses Protos " is named after πρῶτος (first in Greek) and " Archnemeses Eschatos " is named after ἔσχατος (last in Greek).
