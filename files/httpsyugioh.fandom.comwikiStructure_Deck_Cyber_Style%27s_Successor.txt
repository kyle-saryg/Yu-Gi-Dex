 Structure Deck: Cyber Style's Successor  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the 43rd Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Ice Barrier of the Frozen Prison .

 Features 
 Includes new members/support for the " Cyber ", " Cyber Dragon  and " Cyberdark " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck: Cyber Style's Successor  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 43 cards
 2  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 35  Commons 
 1  Secret Technique Inheritence Pack  of 5 cards (4 Super Rares and 1  Ultra Rare ). All 5 cards from this pack are also available as  Secret Rare . 
 1 Double-sided  Duel Field /Playing Guide
