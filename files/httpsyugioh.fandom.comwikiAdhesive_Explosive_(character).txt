 Appearances 
 Adhesive Explosive appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . 
 
 World Championship 2008 
 In  World Championship 2008 , it appear as a Tag Duel opponent and it Tag partner is  Blast Sphere . 
 
 Unlockables 
 In  World Championship 2008 , they can be unlocked if the player has beaten the Team known as,  Technology Master  five times. Their team name is  Startled Mad .
 
 Ability 
 In  GX Spirit Caller , Adhesive Explosive has the ability to allow the player to connect with other  duelist  easier. To make this ability better, the player has to level up the spirit. In order to do this, the player must  summon  the corresponding card and win the  Duel .
