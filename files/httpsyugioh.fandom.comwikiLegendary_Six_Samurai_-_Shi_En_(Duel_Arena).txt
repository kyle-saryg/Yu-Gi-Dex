 Legendary Six Samurai - Shi En  is a character version of the card " Legendary Six Samurai - Shi En ". 
 He appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. His partner ID is P018.
 In the beta, his partner ID was P013 instead.

 Description 
 A young warrior who leads the " Six Samurai " to conquer all under heaven.
 
 Decks 
 A  Deck  comprised of  Warrior - Type  monsters with the "Six Samurai"  archetype . Many of the "Six Samurai" are most effective when another of their kind is also on the  field . Take the force of a storm to your opponent's door!
