 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During the  Battle Phase , if 5 or more  attacks  were  declared  this  turn  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . You can only use this  effect  of "Meteorush - Blackwhiteroid"  once per turn . If this card  battles , during the  Damage Step  only, it gains 1000  ATK  for each attack declared this turn.
