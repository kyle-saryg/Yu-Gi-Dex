 Warriors' Strike Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fifteenth deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Spellcaster's Command Structure Deck . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Warrior's Strike  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 The deck features  Warrior - Type  monsters. It is designed to bring Warrior Decks up to date and includes many reprinted cards. The Deck puts forward a new Warrior Deck strategy utilizing  Gemini monsters .
 The "Power Up With Packs" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Crusader of Endymion " and " Knight of the Red Lotus " from  Stardust Overdrive  (along with other Gemini monsters from the set), " Grave of the Super Ancient Organism " from  Raging Battle , " Release Restraint Wave " and " Reinforce Truth " from  Ancient Prophecy , " Assault Armor " from  Crimson Crisis  and " United We Stand ", " Don Zaloog " and " Gorz the Emissary of Darkness " from  Retro Pack 2 .
