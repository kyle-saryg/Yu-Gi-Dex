 Members Support 
 
 
 
 
 " Rose " (ローズ  Rōzu ) is an  archetype  used by  Yubel  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime and  Akiza Izinski  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . Originally a manga-exclusive archetype, this archetype got its first  OCG/TCG  support in  LINK VRAINS Pack 3 .  OCG/TCG  support is currently only valid for  Synchro Monsters  of this archetype.
 " Rose Dragon " is a sub- archetype  of this archetype.
