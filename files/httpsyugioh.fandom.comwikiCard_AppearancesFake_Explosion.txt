 In  episode 20 ,  Greiger  uses this card during his Turbo Duel against  Yusei Fudo . When " Speed Warrior " (whose ATK was 1800 due to its own effect) attacks " Spell Reactor・RE ", Greiger activates this card to prevent "Spell Reactor" from being destroyed by battle this turn. The attack fails, but Greiger still takes Battle Damage. Greiger then activates the second effect of this card to Special Summon " Summon Reactor・SK ". 
 In  episode 52 , Greiger uses this card during his Turbo Duel against  Crow Hogan . When " Blackwing - Bora the Spear " attacks " Trap Reactor・Y FI ", Greiger activates this card to prevent "Trap Reactor" from being destroyed by battle this turn, however Greiger still takes Battle Damage. Greiger then activates the second effect of this card to Special Summon " Summon Reactor・SK ".
