 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 Neku  is a character who first appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is one of the  Heishin's  guardians along with  Sebek . Neku is suprised to see Sebek defeated after he loses to  the prince . Neku then duels and loses to the prince, allowing the prince into Heishin's lair.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom , he appears as an enemy Marshal named  Necubetos .
 
 Deck 
 Neku's Deck contains many Fiend-Type and Spellcaster-Type monsters such as " Zoa ", " Skull Knight " and " Kazejin ". He also uses the  Yami  Field Spell to boost the ATK of his monsters.
