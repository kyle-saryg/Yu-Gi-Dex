 This card can be searched by " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Summoner Monk ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Shining Angel ", " Soul Rope ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Chaos Zone ", " Photon Veil ", " Nova Summoner " and " Cyber Angel Benten ". 
 This card's effect is best activated during your opponent's turn using a matchup-reverser such as " Shrink ", " Rising Energy " and especially " Honest ".
 " Prideful Roar " also works, as this card's effect can recoup the Life Points paid.
