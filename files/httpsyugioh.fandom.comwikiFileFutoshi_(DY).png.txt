 Futoshi Harada  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Frederick , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Futoshi is a friend of  Yuya Sakaki 's.
