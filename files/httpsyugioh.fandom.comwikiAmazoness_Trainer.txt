   Quick-Play 
 
 When " Amazoness Tiger " cannot  destroy  an opponent's  monster  by  battle , it gains 400  ATK  and it can  attack  that monster again. Then, if "Amazoness Tiger" has an ATK that is higher than the  DEF  of your opponent's monster, inflict the difference as  damage  to your opponent's  Life Points .
