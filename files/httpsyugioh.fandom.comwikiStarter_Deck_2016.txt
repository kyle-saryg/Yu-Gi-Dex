 Starter Deck 2016  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the eleventh deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Duelist Entry Deck VS . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Starter Deck - Yuya .

 Features 
 This Starter Deck is a preconstructed " Performapal " Deck. 
 It has a lot of Rare or higher foil cards, including the Deck's trump card. 
 It includes lots of new cards, while including a large number of practical cards. 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Starter Deck 2016  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 40 cards
 2  Ultra Rares 
 3  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 29  Commons
