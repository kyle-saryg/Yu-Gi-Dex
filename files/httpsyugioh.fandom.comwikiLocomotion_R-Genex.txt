 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1 " Genex "  Tuner  + 1 or more non-Tuner  DARK   monsters When this card is  Synchro Summoned , take  control  of the  face-up  monster your opponent  controls  with the highest  Level . (If it's a tie, you get to choose.)

   German
