 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Neither player can  Special Summon   monsters  of the same type ( Ritual / Fusion / Synchro / Xyz / Link ) as a monster they  control  for the rest of this  turn , also, if you do not control more than 1 each of Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, and/or Link Monsters, all Ritual, Fusion, Synchro, Xyz, and Link Monsters you currently  control  gain 500  ATK . You can only  activate  1 "Simul Archfiends" per  turn .
