 Villager 1  is one of the villagers who goes to the Dueling Grounds in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories .
 He meets the  prince  at the Dueling Grounds, although he is unaware of his royal status.
 He also appears in The Falsebound Kingdom as a Marshal named Jusell, where he serves as one of the first enemy Marshals faced by Kaiba.
