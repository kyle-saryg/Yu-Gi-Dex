 Quattro , known as  IV  ( Ⅳ ,  Fō ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Quattro , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
