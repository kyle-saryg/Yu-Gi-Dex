 " Ebon Magician Curran " and " White Magician Pikeru " appear in this card's artwork.
 This card was released in  Cybernetic Revolution  at the same time as "Ebon Magician Curran", thus introducing "Curran" as a rival or counterpart of the earlier-released "Pikeru".
