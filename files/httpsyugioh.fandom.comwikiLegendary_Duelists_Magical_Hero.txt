 Legendary Duelists: Magical Hero  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yami Yugi ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Yuya Sakaki  and  Z-ARC . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Elemental HERO ", " Warrior ", " Gagaga ", " Performapal ", " Magician ", " Dark Magician " and " Dark Magician Girl " archetypes.
