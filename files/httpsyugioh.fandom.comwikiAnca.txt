 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In  Tag Force 1-3 , she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , if the player gets her  Deck Recipe , they also get the card " Enchanted Javelin ".
 If the player loses to her, she suggests that he treat her to lunch, although she won't accept his sandwiches.
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 Anca can be found in the  City 's coastal region.
 
 Tag Force 6 
 During  Aporia 's second story event, Anca and  Primo  Tag Duel against the player and Aporia
 During  Luna 's third story event, Anca and  Cali  Tag Duel against the player and Luna.

 Decks 
 Anca uses  Warrior Toolbox  Decks  during the first three  Tag Force  games, which also feature several disruptive measures against her opponent's actions such as " Creature Swap " and " Royal Decree ".
 From  Tag Force 4  onwards, Anca uses  Anti-Meta  Decks . She continues using  Warrior Toolbox Decks  as well, but they now include more  banishing  and negation.
