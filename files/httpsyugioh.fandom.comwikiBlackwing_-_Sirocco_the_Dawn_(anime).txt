 [  Winged Beast  /  Effect  ] If your opponent  controls  a  monster  and you control no monsters, you can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . Once per  turn , you can  select  1  face-up  " Blackwing " monster you control. Until the  End Phase , it gains  ATK  equal to the total ATK of all face-up "Blackwing" monsters currently on the  field , except itself. Monsters other than the selected monster cannot  attack  during the turn you  activate  this  effect .
