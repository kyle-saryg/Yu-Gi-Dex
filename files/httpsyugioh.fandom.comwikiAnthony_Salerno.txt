 Tony Salerno  is an American voice actor, ADR director and scriptwriter who has worked with  4Kids Entertainment . He often works as actor or staff crew on anime dubs. He is also credited as  Agent Zero ,  Anthony Salerno  and  Anthony Haden Salerno .

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Salerno's first role in Yu-Gi-Oh! history was portraying  Rex Raptor  in the  second series anime  starting from  episode 188 , replacing  Sebastian Arcelus , who had himself replaced  Sam Regal  starting from  episode 144 .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Salerno provided the voice of  Chazz Princeton  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  until episode 89, when he was replaced by  Marc Thompson .  Lorenzo , a member of the  Society of Light , was also voiced by Salerno.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Salerno did the voices of  Lenny ,  Aero  and  Haley  within  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
