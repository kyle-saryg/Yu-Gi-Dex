 Tori Meadows , known as  Kotori Mizuki  ( 観月 小鳥 ,  Mizuki Kotori ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  series. This is a video game depiction of  Tori Meadows , a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
