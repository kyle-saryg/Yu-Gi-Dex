 Premium Pack Vol.8  is a Korean pack, containing a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 45 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 6  Secret Rare 
 9  Ultra Rare 
 30  Super Rare
