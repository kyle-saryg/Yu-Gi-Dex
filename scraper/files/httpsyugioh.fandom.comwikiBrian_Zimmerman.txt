 Brian Zimmerman  is a voice actor for the Singapore dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime. 
 
 Roles 
 Zimmerman provided the voices for several important characters in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime, including  Tristan Taylor ,  Yami Marik ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Bandit Keith ,  Para ,  Dox ,  Gansley ,  Kemo ,  Leichter ,  Lumis ,  Nesbitt ,  Odion ,  Roland ,  Shadi  and  Weevil Underwood .
