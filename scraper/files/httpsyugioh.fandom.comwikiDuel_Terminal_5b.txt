 5b  is the second part of  Duel Terminal 5  made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Raid of the Inverz!!  and was released September 29, 2011.

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. Includes further support for " Gishki ", " Gusto " and " Steelswarm " archetypes.
