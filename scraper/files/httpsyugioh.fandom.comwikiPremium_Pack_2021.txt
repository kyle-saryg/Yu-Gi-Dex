 Premium Pack 2021  is the 23rd  Premium Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ), following  Premium Pack 2020 .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yako Tenma ,  Masumi Momono ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Kalin Kessler ,  Cologne  and  Specter . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Charmer ", " Fossil ", " Infernity ", " Sunavalon ", " Sunvine ", " Infernoble Knight ", " Machina ", " Mathmech ", " Tenyi ", " Valkyrie ", " Virtual World " and " Void " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Doll Monster " archetype to the OCG. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 32 cards:
 
 12  Secret Rares 
 3  Ultra Rares 
 3  Super Rares 
 14  Commons 
 The Ultra Rare, Super Rare and Common cards are also available as Secret Rare.
The Common cards are also available as  Normal Parallel Rare .
