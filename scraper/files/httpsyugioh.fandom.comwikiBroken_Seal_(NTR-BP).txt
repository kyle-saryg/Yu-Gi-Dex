 Broken Seal  is the fifth pack in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour , and is accessible when Level 3 is attained. This pack is packed with easy-to-use  Normal / Effect Monsters  and cards useful on the Duel Field. This pack will help you become a champion!
 The 52 cards in  Broken Seal  include 20  Normal Monster Cards , 9  Effect Monster Cards , 1  Fusion Monster Card , 0  Ritual Monster Cards , 16  Spell Cards , and 6  Trap Cards .
