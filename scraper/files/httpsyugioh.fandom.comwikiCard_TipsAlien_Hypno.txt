 Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 This card can be searched by " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Soul Rope ", " Resonator Engine ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Flying Saucer Muusik'i ", " Crop Circles ", " "A" Cell Recombination Device ", " Summoner Monk ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Mysterious Triangle ", " Damage = Reptile ", " Snake Whistle ", " King of the Feral Imps ", " Woodland Archer ", " Gemini Soldier ", " Chemicritter Carbo Crab " and " Shark Cruiser ". 
 This card's effect can be used to take control of up to four opposing monsters with an  A-Counter (s) and use them for a  Link Summon  (potentially of  Link 4  or higher). 
 Special Summon this card with an effect (such as " Herculean Power ") so that you can Normal Summon it on the same turn to use its own effect. At the same time that you Normal Summon this card, Special Summon " Alien Dog " whose effect gives up to two opposing monsters an A-Counter(s) so that this card's effect can take control of them.
 If 2 A-Counters are already on opposing monsters when you Special Summon this card, Special Summon " Alien Overlord " instead by removing those A-Counters and placing them back on all opposing face-up monsters so that you can take control of up to three of them via this card.
