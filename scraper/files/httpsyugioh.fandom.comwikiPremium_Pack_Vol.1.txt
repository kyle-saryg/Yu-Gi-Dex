 Premium Pack Vol.1  is a Korean pack, containing a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean.
 This pack shares its prefix,  PP01 , with the  TCG   Premium Pack , although they contain different cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 30 cards or 36 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 6  Secret Rare 
 30  Ultra Rare
