A  TCG -only  card is a card currently released in the  TCG , but not the  OCG .
 Strike of Neos  was the first booster pack to issue a  TCG -only card at the time of issue. From  Tactical Evolution  through to  Eternity Code , at least ten  TCG -only cards were issued in each main-series booster. 
 TCG -only cards are often later released to the  OCG  in an  Extra Pack .
