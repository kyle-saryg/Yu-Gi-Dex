 Kageyama sister B  (影山姉妹Ⓑ  Kageyama shimai Bī ) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . She is one of the three  Kageyama sisters .

 Biography 
 Kageyama sister B and her sisters Dueled Yugi, after stripping him of the  Millennium Puzzle . Yugi was about to lose, until  Seto Kaiba  appeared and took over. [1]
