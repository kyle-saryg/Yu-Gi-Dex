 Hidden Arsenal: Special Edition , known as  Hidden Arsenal 4: Special Edition  in Europe, is a  Special Edition  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ) and the Korean  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Each North American  Hidden Arsenal: Special Edition  contains:
 
 1  Super Rare  promotional card (" XX-Saber Emmersblade " or " Naturia Pineapple ") 
 1  Hidden Arsenal  Booster Pack 
 1  Hidden Arsenal 2  Booster Pack 
 1  Hidden Arsenal 3  Booster Pack 
 Each European  Hidden Arsenal 4: Special Edition  contains:
 
 1  Super Rare  promotional card (" XX-Saber Emmersblade " or " Naturia Pineapple ") 
 3  Hidden Arsenal 4: Trishula's Triumph  Booster Packs 
 Each  Korean  Hidden Arsenal: Special Edition  contains:
 
 1  Super Rare  promotional card (" Fabled Raven ") 
 1  Hidden Arsenal  Booster Pack 
 1  Hidden Arsenal 2  Booster Pack 
 2  Hidden Arsenal 3  Booster Packs
