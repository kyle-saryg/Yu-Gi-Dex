 Duelist Pack Collection Tin 2011 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This cool black-tinted tin commemorates  Yusei Fudo 's battles against the villainous Dark Signers. Each Tin includes 3 packs of Duelist Pack: Yusei 3 and 1 pack of Duelist Revolution. In these packs fans can easily collect some of Yusei's favorite Monsters, Spells, and Traps to recreate his game-winning strategies .
 To represent the forces of darkness, each Tin also includes 4 Synchro Monsters used by Yusei's adversaries, the  Dark Signers . Three of these are Super Rare reprints of hard-to-find  Synchro Monsters  released previously. The fourth is a brand new,  Secret Rare  Synchro Monster never seen before." 
 (Note the Oceania / Europe release is not a tin but instead a box similar to Special Edition Boxes. Additionally they will not contain a Duelist Revolution Booster Pack which has been replaced with another Duelist Pack 10: Yusei 3).
