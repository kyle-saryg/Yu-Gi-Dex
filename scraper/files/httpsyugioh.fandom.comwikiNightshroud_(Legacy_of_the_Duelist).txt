 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Nightshroud , known as  Darkness  (ダークネス,  Dākunesu ) in the Japanese version,  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Nightshroud , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
