 Gate Key
 
 
 Gate Keys  are a type of item in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links  used to Duel against  Legendary Duelists  at the  Gate .
 Each Legendary Duelist has an associated color of Gate Key. To Duel a Legendary Duelist at the Gate, the player must spend both a number of "plain" Gate Keys and a number of colored Gate Keys. However, it does not cost any Gate Keys to Duel a Legendary Duelist who appears in Duel World outside of the Gate.
 During special events, event Duelists require event items instead of Gate Keys to be Dueled at the Gate.
 
 Costs 
 There are different Gate Key costs depending on the Level the player chooses to challenge the Legendary Duelist at, with a higher cost for higher Levels.
