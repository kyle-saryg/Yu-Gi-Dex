 Reiji Akaba  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Declan Akaba , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Reiji is the president of  Leo Institute of Dueling .
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Reiji runs a  " D/D " Deck .
