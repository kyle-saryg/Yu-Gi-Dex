 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 
 
 Orbital 7  is a character appearing in  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL . He is  Kaito Tenjo 's personal servant.
 
 Biography 
 Haruto  attended to the opening of the  Duel Team Carnival  along with Orbital 7 to watch  Kaito   Duel  and cheer him on. Kaito was against Haruto being there, and yelled at Orbital, with Orbital apologizing.
 When the  Litterbots  go crazy, Orbital detects the radio waves which were manipulating the Litterbots being broadcast from three points around the Tournament Grounds. He then leads  Team ZEXAL  to those points in order to stop the signals and return the Litterbots to normal. [1]
