For the " Chronomaly " monster, see  Chronomaly Crystal Skull .      
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Crystal "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 宝 玉 」 .   As its English name contains "Crystal", it  may  be treated a member of the "Crystal" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The  Croatian  and  Greek                       names given are not official.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  Summoned : Take 1000  damage , then change this card to  Defense Position . During the  End Phase , if you took no effect damage this  turn : You can take 1  Rock   monster  with 0 ATK from your  Deck , and either  add  it to your hand or  Special Summon  it. You can only use this  effect  of "Crystal Skull"  once per turn .
