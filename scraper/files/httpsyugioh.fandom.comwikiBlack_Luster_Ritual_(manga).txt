 Spell 
 
 
 
 Play this card and sacrifice 2  monsters , each with an  ATK  of 1500 or less. The power of chaos dwells in the  Fierce Knight . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> This card is used to  Ritual Summon  " Black Luster Soldier ." You must also offer monsters whose total  ATK  equals 1500 or more as a  Tribute  from the  field  or your  hand .
