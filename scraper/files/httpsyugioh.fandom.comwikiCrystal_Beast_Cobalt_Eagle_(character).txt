 Crystal Beast Cobalt Eagle , known as  Gem Beast Cobalt Eagle  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Crystal Beast Cobalt Eagle ".
 
 Appearances 
 Anime 
 He appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  as one of  Jesse Anderson 's Duel Monster Spirits. While  Jaden  was Dueling Yubel-possessed Jesse, Topaz Tiger and the other Crystal Beasts contacted him with the help of  Winged Kuriboh . He watches as  Sapphire Pegasus  explains how to free Jesse.
