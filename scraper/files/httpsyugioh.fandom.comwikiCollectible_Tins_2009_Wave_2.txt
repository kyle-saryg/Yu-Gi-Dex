 Celebrate the holiday season with a bang! The final Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 2009 Collectible Tins feature the ultimate monsters from the epic Dark Signers' story arc of Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's. The first tin features Rex Goodwin's “Earthbound Immortal Wiraqocha Rasca,” the final and mightiest of the Earthbound Immortals. The second tin features Yusei's “Majestic Star Dragon,” his latest, upgraded version of “Stardust Dragon.” Each tin contains 5 booster packs, including 2 advance packs of Stardust Overdrive – before they're available in stores!. 
 —  Konami
