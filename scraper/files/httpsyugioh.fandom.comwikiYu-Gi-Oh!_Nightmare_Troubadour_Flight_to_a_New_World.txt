Flight to a New World  is the fourth pack in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour , and is accessible from the beginning of the game. This pack contains gorgeous monsters and cards that will match your  Deck 's battling style. This series increases the variety of  Decks  you can construct.
 The 52 cards in  Flight to a New World  include 18  Normal Monster Cards , 14  Effect Monster Cards , 0  Fusion Monster Cards , 0  Ritual Monster Cards , 17  Spell Cards , and 3  Trap Cards .
