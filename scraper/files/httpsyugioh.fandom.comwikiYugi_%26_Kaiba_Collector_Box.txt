 Yugi & Kaiba Collector Box 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Yugi & Kaiba Collector Box  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the European equivalent of the North American  Yugi's Collector Box  and  Kaiba's Collector Box .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Yugi & Kaiba Collector Box  contains:
 
 1  Starter Deck Yugi Reloaded 
 1  Starter Deck Kaiba Reloaded 
 3  Duelist Pack: Yugi   Booster Packs 
 3  Duelist Pack: Kaiba  Booster Packs 
 2  Ultra Rare  promotional cards (" Dark Magician " and " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ")
