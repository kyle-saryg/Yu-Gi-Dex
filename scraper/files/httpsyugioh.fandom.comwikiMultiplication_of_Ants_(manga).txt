 Multiplication of Ants 
 
 Japanese:   アリの増殖 
 Romaji:   Ari no Zōshoku 
 
 
 
 
 en 
 ja 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell 
 
 
 
 A " Soldier Ari " in  Attack Position  becomes 10  Army Ants . The Army Ants may not attack. When your turn is over, the Ants go to the  Graveyard . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> This card can only be played if you have " Soldier Ari " on the field in face-up Attack Position. 9 additional Soldier Ants appear on the field. The Ants may not attack. When your turn is over, the Ants go to the Graveyard.
