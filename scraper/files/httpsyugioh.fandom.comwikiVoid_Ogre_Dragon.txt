   English 
 
 
1  DARK   Tuner  + 1+ non-Tuner  monsters Once per turn , when your opponent  activates  a  Spell / Trap Card  while you have no cards in your  hand  ( Quick Effect ): You can  negate  the activation, and if you do,  destroy  it.
