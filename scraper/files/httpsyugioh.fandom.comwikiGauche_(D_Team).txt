 Gauche  (ゴーシュ,  Gōshu ) is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL . This is an alternate incarnation of  Nistro , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime .

 Biography 
 Gauche is part of the  Team Duel Tournament 's committee. In order to enter the tournament, a member of the  Dueling Team  should face Gauche in a  Duel , and if he judged the team was worthy, then it would enter.  Yuma  faces Gauche representing the  Team ZEXAL , and by defeating him, makes his team able to enter the tournament. [1] 
 Gauche later shows up again to announce the beginning of the tournament and explain the rules. [2]  He also announces news about the tournament, like when  Team Shark  became the first team to advance to the finals. [3] 
 
 Deck 
 Gauche uses a  " Heroic " Deck , primarily focused on  Xyz Summoning  " Heroic Champion - Excalibur (D Team) ". He also utilizes cards such as " Heroic Challenger - Night Watchman " to punish his opponent's  defensive  tactics.
