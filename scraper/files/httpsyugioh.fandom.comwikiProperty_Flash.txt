 Property Flash 
 
 Japanese:   プロパティ・フラッシュ 
 Romaji:   Puropati Furashu 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Activate  this card when you take  battle damage : Place 1  Property Counter  on this card for every 1000 damage you took, then end the  Battle Phase .  Once per turn : You can  Tribute  1 " Hydradrive "  Link Monster ; place 1 Property Counter on this card. You can  send  this card with a Property Counter to the  GY ;  Special Summon  1 "Hydradrive" Link Monster from your  Extra Deck  with the same  Link Rating  as the number of Property Counters that were on this card. You can only activate 1 "Property Flash" per  turn .
