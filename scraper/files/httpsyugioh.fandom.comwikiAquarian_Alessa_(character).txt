 Aquarian Alessa  is a character version of the card, " Aquarian Alessa ".
 Aquarian Alessa appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . She can be unlocked if the player has beaten  Maiden of the Aqua  five times. If the player has unlocked this character, the player can unlock her as a tag partner by beating her ten times.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2008 , Aquarian Alessa uses a  WATER  Deck titled, "Dripping Water".
