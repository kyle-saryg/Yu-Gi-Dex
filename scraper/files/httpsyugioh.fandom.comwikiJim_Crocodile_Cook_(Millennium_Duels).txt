 Jim Crocodile Cook  (ジム・クロコダイル・クック,  Jimu Kurokodairu Kukku ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Jim Crocodile Cook , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
