 Bolt Tanner , known as  Jin Himuro  (氷室仁,  Jin Himuro ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Bolt Tanner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
