 TCG Rulings 
 You cannot use  Eccentric Boy  as a  Tuner  to  Summon  this monster because the other material monster must be 1 monster in your hand, and this requires 2 non-Tuner monsters. [1] 
 Your opponent only needs to have a  face-up   LIGHT  monster when you activate this effect, not when it resolves. [1] 
 Destroying 1  Set  card targets that card. [1] 
 Sending cards from your hand to the  Graveyard  for second or third effects is a  cost . [1] 
 The effect to send  LIGHT  monsters to the  Graveyard  and inflict damage counts  LIGHT  monsters sent from your opponent’s hand to your  Graveyard . [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The text, " If your opponent controls a face-up LIGHT monster, you can select and activate 1 of the following effects once per turn: " of " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " is not treated as an effect. [2] 
 The effect of " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " which destroys one Set card your opponent controls  targets . [2] 
 Discarding your entire hand is a  cost  to activate the effect of " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " which discards from your opponent's hand and inflicts damage. [2] 
 If one of the monsters sent to the Graveyard by the effect of your " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " was sent to your Graveyard because it was owned by you and added to your opponent's hand by " Exchange ", then the sent monster will be included in the damage inflicted. [3] 
 If an effect is  chained  to the effect of " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " so that your opponent controls no LIGHT monsters when resolving the effect of " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor ", then the effect of " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " will still resolve properly. [4] 
 If " Eccentric Boy " is used as a  Synchro Material Monster , then the other  Synchro Material Monsters  must be one monster from the hand. Thus, you cannot  Synchro Summon  a monster like " Mist Wurm " or " Ally of Justice Decisive Armor " which require two or more non-Tuner monsters. [5]
