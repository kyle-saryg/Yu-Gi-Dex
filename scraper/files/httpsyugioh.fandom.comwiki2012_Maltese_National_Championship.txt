 The  2012 Maltese National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Malta. It took place on May 20, 2012 in Msida, Malta. [1] 
 
 Events 
 The entry fee for the main event was €25. The entry fee for the  Dragon Duel  was €5.
