 Effect 
 Both players  draw  1 card and  reveal  it, then if either of those cards was a  monster , each player  Special Summons  their monster to their opponent's side of the  field  in  face-up   Defense Position , and if either player does, the  Summoning  player(s) gains  Life Points  equal to the  ATK  of the monster they Summoned.
