 Vennominaga the Deity of Poisonous Snakes , known as  Poisonous Snake God Venominaga  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card " Vennominaga the Deity of Poisonous Snakes ".
 She appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator  where she and  Cosmic Fortress Gol'gar  are a team in Tag Duels. To unlock the team, the player must summon her card in a Duel, and win by using her effect.
