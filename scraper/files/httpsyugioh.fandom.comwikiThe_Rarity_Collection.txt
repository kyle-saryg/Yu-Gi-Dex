 The Rarity Collection  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack (3 Super Rares and 1 Ultra Rare or higher rarity; in the Korean version it can also be 4 Super Rares per pack). The set contains 51 cards:
 
 1  Holographic Rare 
 5  Extra Secret Rares 
 4  Ultimate Rares  (also available as  Collector's Rare ) 
 10  Ultra Rares  (also available as  Secret Rare  and Collector's Rare) 
 31  Super Rares  (also available as Secret Rare and Collector's Rare) 
 The  Master Sets  contain:
 
 Japanese, 137 cards. 
 Korean, 92 cards (doesn't have the  Collector's Rare  variants).
