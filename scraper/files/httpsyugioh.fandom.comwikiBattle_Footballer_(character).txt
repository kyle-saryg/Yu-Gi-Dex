 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  Duel Monsters Spirit World , two Battle Footballers that were part of  The Supreme King's Army  hunted  Duelists . They attempted to  Duel  an  old man  and his grandson  Nero , but were defeated by  Axel Brodie  and  Jim Crocodile Cook  and got sent to the stars. [1] 
 
 Non-canon appearances 
       Battle Footballer in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia .     
 Battle Footballer appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia . This character and  Gene-Warped Warwolf  are unlocked as Tag Duel opponents; their Tag Team name is named  Skill Drain Love .
