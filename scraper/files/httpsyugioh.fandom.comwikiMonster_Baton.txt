 Quick-like 
 When you  activate  this card,  send  1  Effect Monster  from your  hand  to the  Graveyard . Once, you can activate that monster's  effect  as this card's effect. Then, while this card is  face-up  on the field, you can activate that monster's effect as this card's effect by sending 1 Effect Monster from your hand to the Graveyard whose  Level  is equal to the Level of the  monster  sent to the Graveyard by this card's effect.
