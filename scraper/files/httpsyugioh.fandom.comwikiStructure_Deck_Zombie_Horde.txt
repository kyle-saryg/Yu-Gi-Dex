 Zombie Horde Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the 43rd Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following the  Structure Deck: Powercode Link . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck R: Undead World  in the  OCG .
