 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  activate  this card from your  hand .  Once per turn , during your  Main Phase : You can  Special Summon  1 " Z-ONE "  monster  from your hand, except " Z-ONE/Monster E ",  ignoring its Summoning conditions . During each of your  Standby Phases :  Destroy  all "Z-ONE" and " Timelord " monsters you  control . This card's activation and  effect  cannot be  negated . -I activate " Nonexistence "!-
