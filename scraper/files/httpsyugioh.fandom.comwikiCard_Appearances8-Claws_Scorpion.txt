 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler 
 
 In  episode 121 ,  Titan, the Rock Spirit  "summons" this card to attack  Jaden Yuki  and company while they were trying to get necessaries from an abandoned submarine. Jaden Normal Summons " Elemental HERO Clayman " to defend against this card and Titan's " Fiend Scorpions ", but Titan later activates " Book of Moon " to flip " Elemental HERO Clayman " into face-down Defense Position. This card then attacks "Clayman". Due to this card's second effect, its ATK becomes 2400 during damage calculation only. This card then destroys "Clayman".  Jesse Anderson  then Normal Summons " Crystal Beast Amethyst Cat " and " Crystal Beast Topaz Tiger " and uses them to attack and destroy this card and the " Fiend Scorpions ". 
 This card appeared in  episode 35 .
