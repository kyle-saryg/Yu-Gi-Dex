 Tara Sands , also credited as  Tara Jayne , is an English-language voice actress.
 
 Roles 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! , Sands voiced  Mokuba Kaiba  in the first four seasons before moving to Los Angeles, where she currently resides. This role was succeeded by  Carrie Keranen .
 Sands reprised the role of Mokuba in the movie  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions   and in the game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . 
 
 Trivia 
 She, along with fellow former Yu-Gi-Oh! voice actress  Megan Hollingshead , worked together on "Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World." as Genis Sage and Shihna Fujibayashi respectfully, though neither of them were credited for it.
 She also voiced Seles Wilder, a Female Noble and Verius, the Summon Spirit of Heart. 
 For a time, she was the co-hostess of Cartoon Network's Fridays block.
