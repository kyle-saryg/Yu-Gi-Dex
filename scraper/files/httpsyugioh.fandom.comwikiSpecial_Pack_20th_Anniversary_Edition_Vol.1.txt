 Special Pack 20th Anniversary Edition Vol.1  is a supplementary  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  given to buyers, who spent ¥1000 or more on Yu-Gi-Oh! products between March 24 and April 13, 2018
