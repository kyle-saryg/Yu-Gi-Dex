      Queens Duel Academy     
 Queen's Duel Academy , known as  Duel Academy Queen's  or  Duel Academia Queen's  in the Japanese version, is an all-girl  Duel Academy  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga.
 Akiza Izinski  attends this school and is very popular, such that she has earned the title "Queen of Queens".
 
 Students 
 Known students include:
 
 Akiza Izinski 
 Ran Kobayakawa 
 Sakura 
 Mei
