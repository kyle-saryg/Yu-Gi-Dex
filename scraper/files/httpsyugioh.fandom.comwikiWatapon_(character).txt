 Watapon  is a character version of the card, " Watapon ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus  as a Tag Duel opponent partnered with  Spore .
 This character and Spore can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents by clearing chapter 5. Their Tag Team name is  Downy Duo .
