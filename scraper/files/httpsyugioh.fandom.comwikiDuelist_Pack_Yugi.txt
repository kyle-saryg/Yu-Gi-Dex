 Duelist Pack: Yugi  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the ninth set in the  TCG ' s Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Yusei .
 It was reprinted in  Duelist Pack: Yugi & Kaiba Special Edition . It will also be reprinted in  Yugi's Collector Box , and  Kaiba's Collector Box .

 Features 
 The set features cards used by  Yugi Muto . It contains new artwork for many cards, including " Dark Magician Girl ", " Summoned Skull ", " Dark Paladin " and " Polymerization ". 
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack of  Duelist Pack: Yugi  contains 5 cards and each box contains 30 packs. There are 30 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
