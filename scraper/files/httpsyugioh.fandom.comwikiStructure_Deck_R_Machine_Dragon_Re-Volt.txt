 Structure Deck R: Machine Dragon Re-Volt  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the third Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck R series, following  Structure Deck R: Revival of the Great Divine Dragon . [1] [2]

 Features 
 The "Power Up with Packs" supplement on the back of the included play mat suggests combining this Deck with the following cards from these sets.
 
 " Scapeghost ", " Cosmic Cyclone ", and " Floodgate Trap Hole " from  The Dark Illusion 
 " Green Gadget ", " Tribute Burial ", and " Metal Reflect Slime " from  Duelist Pack: Rivals of the Pharaoh 
 " Torque Tune Gear ", " Performapal Seal Eel ", and " Dimensional Barrier " from  Invasion: Vengeance 
 " Destiny HERO - Dynatag ", " Destiny HERO - Plasma ", and " Dark Hole " from  Destiny Soldiers
