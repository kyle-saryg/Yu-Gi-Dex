 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist   Arma Knight  was drawn with a different artwork. Its  Rush Duel  variant is drawn in the style of this card's first  OCG  artwork, but with more details and a different background.
