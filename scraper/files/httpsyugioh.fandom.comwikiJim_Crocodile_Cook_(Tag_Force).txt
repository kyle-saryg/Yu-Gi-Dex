 Jim Crocodile Cook  (ジム・クロコダイル・クック,  Jimu Kurokodairu Kukku ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Jim Crocodile Cook , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2 
 Jim and  Adrian Gecko  Tag Duel against  Tyranno Hassleberry  and the player in the last event of Tyranno's story.
 The player and  Jesse Anderson  duel against Jim and Tyranno as part of Jesse's sixth story event.
 In an optional random event, the player and their partner can have a Tag Duel against Jim and  Axel Brodie .
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Bastion Misawa  and the player Tag Duel against Jim and Tyranno during Normal Bastion's first story event.
