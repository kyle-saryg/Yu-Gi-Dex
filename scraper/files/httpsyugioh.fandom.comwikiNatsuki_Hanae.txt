 Natsuki Hanae  is a Japanese voice actor, affiliated with Across Entertainment.
 He provides the voice of Hokuto Shijima ( Dipper O'rion ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . [2] 
 He is also the voice actor of Gakuto Sōgetsu ( Gavin Sogetsu ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS . [3]
