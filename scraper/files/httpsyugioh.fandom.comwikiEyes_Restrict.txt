 " Eyes Restrict ", known as " Eyes Sacrifice " (アイズ・サクリファイス) in Japanese, is an  archetype  of  Level  1  DARK   Spellcaster   Fusion Monsters  with 0  ATK  and  DEF , used by the  Maximillion Pegasus  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime and  manga . All of its members list " Relinquished " as one of its  Fusion Materials .
 In the French version and the  OCG , the names of this archetype derives from the names of "Relinquished".
