 Names 
 
 The unofficial Arabic name given (نصير العدالة: الحفّارة الحراريّة  Naṣīru l-ʿAdālah: al-Ḥaffāratu l-Ḥarāriyyah ) translates to  Ally of Justice: Thermal Drilling Rig . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Σύμμαχος της Δικαιοσύνης Θερμικός Διατρητής ) is a translation of the official French name of this card ( Foreuse Thermique, Allié de la Justice ).
