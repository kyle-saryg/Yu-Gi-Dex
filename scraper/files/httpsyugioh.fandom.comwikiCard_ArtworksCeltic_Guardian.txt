 When this card was released it was drawn in a different position, compared to its  manga  artwork. As with all cards released in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Bandai's Official Card Game  this card is drawn with a different background and with a slightly different color scheme. 
 This card has three different artworks printed by  Konami , all featuring the monster in different positions and with different backgrounds. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , this card was drawn with a different artwork.
