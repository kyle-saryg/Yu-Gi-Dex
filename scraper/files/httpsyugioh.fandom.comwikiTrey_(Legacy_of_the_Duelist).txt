 Trey , known as  III  ( Ⅲ ,  Surī ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Trey , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
