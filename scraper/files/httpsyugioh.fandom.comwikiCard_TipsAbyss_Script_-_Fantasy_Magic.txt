 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " and " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ". 
 This card is best used on " Abyss Actor - Twinkle Little Star " (which can attack multiple monsters without being destroyed by battle), " Abyss Actor - Comic Relief " (which prevents damage to you when it attacks) or " Abyss Actor - Sassy Rookie " (which protects itself from battle destruction once per turn).
