Cosplay , short for costume roleplay, as an act common to anime, manga and video game fandoms, where fans dress themselves as characters.
 The  V Jump  magazine features a parody of  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , called  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5C's , which features group of people cosplaying as  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  characters. It has also featured cosplay of characters from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , some of which include  Reginald Kastle  and  Bronk Stone .
 Throughout the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime series, characters have dressed as  Duel Monsters ' characters.
 
 In the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime :
 Although not strictly cosplay,  Yami Bakura  trapped  Yugi  and his friends in their Duel Monsters cards. 
 During a dream,  Téa  was called on stage by the  Dark Magician Girl  and dressed as her. 
 In  Kaiba Land  U.S.A., several employees dressed as monsters 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX :
 on  Duel Monsters Spirit Day , people dressed as Duel Monsters for a festival. 
 during episode 161's party several students dressed up as monsters. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , characters dressed as  Dark Magician  sell the player  Duel Runner  parts, while characters dressed as  Dark Magician Girl  sell them  Booster Packs . 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , during the event in Yugi's time, several characters were dressed as  Harpie Ladies  and  Magician's Valkyrias . 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , students of  Heartland Academy  dress up as various monsters during a fun fair.
