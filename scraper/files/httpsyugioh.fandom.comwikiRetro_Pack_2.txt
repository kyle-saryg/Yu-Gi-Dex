 Retro Pack 2 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Retro Pack 2  is a  Booster Pack  in  TCG . It was preceded by the first  Retro Pack .

 Features 
         Blister pack     
 Packed with all-powerful cards, the  Retro Pack  series is designed to help Duelists collect hard to get and out of print cards. Perfect for duelists looking for a chance to enter the world of Yu-Gi-Oh! and unleash forbidden powers. A great value with 3  Booster Packs  and 1 " Gorz the Emissary of Darkness ". Return to greatness with the  Retro Pack 2 .
 Each  Retro Pack 2  box or blister-pack contains 3 packs of  Retro Pack 2  plus 1  Limited Edition   variant card . The North American version includes a variant " Green Baboon, Defender of the Forest " while the European, Latin American, and Oceanic versions contain " Gorz the Emissary of Darkness ". "Gorz the Emissary of Darkness" is available inside the American packs while "Green Baboon, Defender of the Forest" is available inside the other packs.
