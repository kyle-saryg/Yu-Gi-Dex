 Story 2: Domino City Battles  ( Ｓｔｏｒｙ ２  童実野 町 での 戦 い  Sutōrī Ni: Domino-chō de no Tatakai ) is an arc in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . It's more or less part of Duelist Kingdom arc.
 It consists of three sub-stories.
 
 The Girl From America  (アメリカからきた 少女   Amerika Kara Kita Shōjo ):  Rebecca Hawkins  faces  Yugi Muto  in a Duel for the ownership of  Solomon Muto 's " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " card. 
 Legendary Heroes , known as  Duel Monsters Quest  ( ＤＭ クエスト  Dyueru Monsutāzu Kuesuto ) in the Japanese version: Yugi and his friends participate in the  Legendary Heroes  virtual-reality game to save  Seto Kaiba . 
 Dungeon Dice Monsters  (ダンジョン・ダイス・モンスターズ  Danjon Daisu Monsutāzu )  Duke Devlin  blames  Yami Yugi  for the disappearance of  Maximillion Pegasus  and tries to exact revenge through the game,  Dungeon Dice Monsters .
