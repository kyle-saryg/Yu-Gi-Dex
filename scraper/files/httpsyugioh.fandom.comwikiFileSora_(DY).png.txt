 Sora Shiun'in  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate version of  Sora Perse , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime . 
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Sora runs an  " Edge Imp / Fluffal " Deck .
