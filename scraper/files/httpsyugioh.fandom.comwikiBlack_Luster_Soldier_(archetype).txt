 Members Support 
 
 
 
 
 " Black Luster Soldier ", known as " Chaos Soldier " (カオス・ソルジャー  Kaosu Sorujā ) in the  OCG , is an  archetype  of  Warrior   monsters  with 3000  ATK  used by  Yugi Muto  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime  and  manga , as well as  Dimitri  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime, that includes " Black Luster Soldier " and monsters based on it. It is also a sub-archetype of the " Chaos " archetype.
 Members of this archetype have existed since the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters National Tournament , but did not receive  support  until  Dimension of Chaos .
 This archetype is related to the " Gaia The Fierce Knight " archetype, as cards from both archetypes support each other.

 Playing style 
 Of the many search effects of this archetype, the most prominent are those of the  Field Spell Card  " Gateway to Chaos ", which can add either a " Black Luster Soldier " Ritual Monster or a "Gaia the Fierce Knight" monster upon activation, and with enough  Spell Counters  can also search for any  Ritual Spell Card . Leftover counters can be used to activate the effect of " Super Soldier Shield " in the Graveyard. " Beginning of Heaven and Earth " can set up the Graveyard or " Super Soldier Synthesis " to  Ritual Summon  him from the Hand or Graveyard.
 "Super Soldier Synthesis", " Envoy of Chaos " and " Manju of the Ten Thousand Hands " tend to make Decklists of this archetype Level 4 based because of needing Tribute of exactly Level 8.
 " Super Soldier Soul " can banish itself from the Graveyard to search either Level 4 " Evening Twilight Knight " and " Beginning Knight " Monsters, which can also search when banished but cannot do this themselves and need effects of "Chaos" Monsters like " Envoy of the Beginning ", " Lightpulsar Dragon ", " Black Dragon Collapserpent " and " White Dragon Wyverburster " or the  Ritual Spell Card  " Super Soldier Ritual " to activate them.
 " Black Luster Soldier - Sacred Soldier " can be searched or Special Summoned by the effects of both the "Gaia The Fierce Knight" monsters, is able to recycle banished cards to the Graveyard and when destroyed by an opposing effect can return a card from the Graveyard to the Hand.
 "Black Luster" cards are sometimes run alongside " Trade-In " and " The Melody of Awakening Dragon  to search cards from the " Blue-Eyes " archetype.  Using either " Chaos Form " or " Advanced Ritual Art " to their fullest.

 Banishing effect
 
 
 Envoy of the Beginning 
 Banish 1 monster on the field face-up at the cost of not being allowed to attack with this card during that turn.
 
 
 Envoy of the Evening Twilight 
 One of two variations based on the Attribute of the monsters that were banished from your GY to Special Summon this card at the cost of not being allowed to conduct your Battle Phase during that turn.
 LIGHT: Banish 1 monster on the field. 
 DARK: Banish 1 random card from your opponent's hand face-down until their End Phase. 
 
 
 Sacred Soldier 
 Banish 1 card your opponent controls and return 1 of your banished LIGHT or DARK monsters to your GY when this card is Normal or Special Summoned.
 
 
 Soldier of Chaos 
 Banish 1 card on the field when this card destroys an opponent's monster by battle.
 
 
 Super Soldier 
 N/A
 
 
 Toon 
 Banish 1 card on the field, provided you control "Toon World", at the cost of not being allowed to attack with this card during that turn.
