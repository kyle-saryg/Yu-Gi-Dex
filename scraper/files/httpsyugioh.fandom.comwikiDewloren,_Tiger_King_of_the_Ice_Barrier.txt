   English 
 
 
1  Tuner  + 1+ non-Tuner  WATER   monsters You can  target  any number of other  face-up  cards you  control ;  return  those targets to the  hand , then, this card gains 500  ATK  for each card returned to the hand by this  effect , until the end of this  turn . You can only use this effect of "Dewloren, Tiger King of the Ice Barrier"  once per turn .

   Japanese (translated) 
 
 
1 Tuner + 1 or more non-Tuner WATER monsters You can return any number of face-up cards you control to the owner's hand. For each card returned to the owner's hand by this effect, this card gains 500 ATK until the End Phase. You can only use this effect of "Dewloren, Tiger King of the Ice Barrier" once per turn.
