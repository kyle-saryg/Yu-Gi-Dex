 The  2012 Belgian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Belgium. It took place in Outpost Brussels in Brussels, Belgium [1]  and was won by Kim van Campenhoudt. [2] 
 The entry fee was 20€ for the main event and 5€ for the side events. [1]
