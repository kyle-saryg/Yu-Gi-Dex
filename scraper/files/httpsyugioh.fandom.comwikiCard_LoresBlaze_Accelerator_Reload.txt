 The Portuguese lore differs from the English one. While the English lore says  "During either player's Main Phase: You can send 1 "Volcanic" card from your hand to the Graveyard" , the Portuguese version says  "Durante o turno de qualquer duelista: você pode enviar 1 card "Vulcânico(a)" da sua mão para o Cemitério" , which means  "During either player's  turn ..." .
