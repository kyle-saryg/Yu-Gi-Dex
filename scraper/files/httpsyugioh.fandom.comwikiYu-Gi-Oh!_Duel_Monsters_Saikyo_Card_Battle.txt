 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Saikyo Card Battle  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh!  video game for the Nintendo 3DS. It was announced in 2015 and details were revealed in April 2016. It was released in Japan on July 6, 2016. [2]
