 Notes 
 
 ↑  1.0   1.1   Although the anime card was printed as a  Normal Trap Card , matching its card frame with its  Trap Card   its  OCG / TCG  counterpart , in  episode 27  "Dragon Capture Jar" functioned as an Effect Monster Card with 100  ATK  and 200  DEF . However, it functioned as a Trap Card in  episode 2  and  Legendary Heroes . This card is listed as a  Continuous Trap Card  on  yugioh.com . 
 
 ↑   "Dragon Capture Jar" functioned as a  Continuous Trap Card  with the following effective effect: "After activation, this card is treated as an Effect Monster Card (ATK 100/DEF 200). (This card is also still treated as a  Trap Card .) While you  control  this card, all  Dragon - Type  monsters that your opponent  controls  are  removed from play . This card gains  DEF  equal to the total combined  DEF  of all monsters that are  removed from play  by this card's effect."
