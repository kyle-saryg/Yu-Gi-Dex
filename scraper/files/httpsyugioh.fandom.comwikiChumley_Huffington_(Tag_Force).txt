 Chumley Huffington , known as  Hayato Maeda  (前田隼人,  Maeda Hayato ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Chumley Huffington , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Chumley is a Tier 1 character, and can be selected as a partner for the Tag Force Tournament.
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Chumley is a Tier 2 character, and can partner with the player if he is defeated 10 times.
 
 
 Decks 
 Chumley uses several  Beast  Decks  based on his and  his father 's Decks in the anime.

 Chumley uses this Deck during the first part of Part 1.
