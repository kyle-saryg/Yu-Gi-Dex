 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 49 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  plays this card in his duel against three  Obelisk Force  members with  Celina ,  Shay Obsidian  and  Moon Shadow . He adds this card from his Deck to his hand using " Abyss Actor - Superstar 's" effect, then activates it, targeting "Superstar". "Superstar" attacks the Yellow Obelisk Force member's " Triple Ancient Gear Hunting Hound " and returns it to his Extra Deck as per this card's effect. 
 In  episode 72 , during Sylvio's  Friendship Cup   Turbo Duel  with  Yugo , Sylvio activates his "Abyss Actor - Superstar's" effect, expecting to add this card from his Deck to his hand, but Yugo chains his " Clear Wing Synchro Dragon 's" effect to destroy "Superstar" and absorb its ATK. 
 In  episode 96 , when Sylvio confronts  Sector Security  outside the  Duel Palace , his "Abyss Actor - Superstar" is already affected by this card's effect when it attacks. 
 In  episode 100 , during Sylvio's duel with  Gong Strong  and  Yuya Sakaki  against three  Duel Academy  soldiers, Sylvio's "Abyss Actor - Superstar" is already affected by this card's effect when it attacks. 
 In  episode 118 , during Sylvio's duel with the other  Lancers  against Slifer Red Duel Academy soldiers, Sylvio's "Abyss Actor - Superstar" is seen, already affected by this card's effect.
