 Green Baboon, Defender of the Forest , known as  Forest Guard Green Baboon  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Green Baboon, Defender of the Forest ". He appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia .
