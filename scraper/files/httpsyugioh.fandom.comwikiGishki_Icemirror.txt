 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
This card can be used to  Ritual Summon  any " Gishki "  Ritual Monster . Ritual Summon 1 "Gishki" Ritual Monster from your  hand  by  Tributing  either 1  face-up   monster  your opponent  controls , or monsters from your hand or  field , whose total  Levels  exactly equal its Level, and if you do, you lose  LP  equal to its  original ATK . If this card is in your  GY : You can  target  1 "Gishki" monster in your GY; place it on the top of the  Deck , and if you do, place this card on the bottom of the Deck.
