 Kit Blade , known as  Yaiba Todo ( 刀 堂   刃 ,  Tōdō Yaiba ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Kit Blade , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
