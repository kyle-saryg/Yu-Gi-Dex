 Disconnect Linker 
 
 Japanese:   ディスコネクト・リンカー 
 Romaji:   Disukonekuto Rinkā

 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Target  1  Link -2 or lower  Link Monster  in your  GY ;  Special Summon  it, but it has its  effects   negated  (if any), and if you do, immediately after this effect  resolves ,  Link Summon  using that  monster  as  material . If you cannot Link Summon,  banish  that monster. The Link Summoned Link Monster is  unaffected  by your opponent's card effects, also monsters in the  Extra Monster Zones  cannot  attack  while it is  face-up  on the  field .
