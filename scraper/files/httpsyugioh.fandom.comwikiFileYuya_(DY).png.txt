 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 
 
 
 Yuya Sakaki  is the protagonist of the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Yuya Sakaki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Yuya is a 14 year old boy who aims to be an  Entertainment Duelist .
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Yuya runs a  " Performapal " Deck .
