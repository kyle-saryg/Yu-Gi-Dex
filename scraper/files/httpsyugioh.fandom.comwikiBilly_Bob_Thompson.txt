 Billy Bob Thompson  is a voice actor for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Thompson's first and most notable roles were as  Vector  and  Mizar .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Thompson provides the voice of  Gong Strong ,  Declan Akaba ,  Leo Akaba , and  Skip Boyle .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 Thompson provides the voice of the  pigeon reporter Saito  in Link VRAINS. He also provides the voice for Varis' assistant,  Specter . [1] 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 Thompson takes over the role of  Weevil Underwood  from his original VA, Jimmy Zoppi.
