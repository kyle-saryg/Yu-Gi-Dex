 This card can be searched by " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King " and " Endymion, the Mighty Master of Magic ". 
 This one card can set up a  Pendulum Summon  by searching " Abyss Actor - Mellow Madonna " and " Abyss Script - Dramatic Story ". Activate "Mellow Madonna" to search "Abyss Actor - Wild Hope". Normal Summon "Wild Hope", then activate "Dramatic Story" to destroy "Wild Hope" and Special Summon another " Abyss Actor " monster from the Deck. This also activates "Wild Hope's" effect to search another "Abyss Actor" monster to place in the other  Pendulum Zone  and Pendulum Summon "Wild Hope" from the  Extra Deck .
