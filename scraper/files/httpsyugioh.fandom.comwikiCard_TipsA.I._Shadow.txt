 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " The Despair Uranus " and " Pikari @Ignister ". 
 Use this card with " Summon Limit " to not only disrupt your opponent's access to their Extra Deck, but force their monsters to attack if they're in  Attack Position .
