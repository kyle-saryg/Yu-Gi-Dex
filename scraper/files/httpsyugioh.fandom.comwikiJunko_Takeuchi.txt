 Junko Takeuchi  is a Japanese actress and voice actress.
 She voices  Mokuba Kaiba  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters . [2] 
 She is also best known for voicing the titular character in  Naruto , as well as the  Digimon  characters Gomamon and Takuya Kanbara.
