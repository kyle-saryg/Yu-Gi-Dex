 Seto Kaiba  ( 海馬 瀬人 ,  Kaiba Seto ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Seto Kaiba , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
