 Structure Deck: Overlay Universe  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the 44th Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Cyber Style's Successor .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck: Overlay Universe  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 43 cards
 4  Normal Parallel Rares 
 39  Commons 
 3  Ultra Rares  bonus cards 
 1  Emperor's Door Pack  of 5 cards (3  Super Rares  and 2 Ultra Rares). All 5 cards from this pack are also available as  Secret Rare . 
 1 Double-sided  Duel Field /Playing Guide
