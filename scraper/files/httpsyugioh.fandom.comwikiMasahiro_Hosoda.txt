 Masahiro Hosoda  is a Japanese director and storyboard artist.
 Hosoda was the series director for episodes 1-13 of  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS .
 
Hosoda is credited as director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh!  episodes. #051 " The Mystery Duelist, Part 2 " #056 " Yugi vs. The Rare Hunter, Part 1 " #062 " The Master of Magicians, Part 3 " #068 " Legendary Fisherman, Part 1 " #075 " Friends 'Til the End, Part 1 " #081 " Let the Finals Begin! " #087 " Awakening of Evil, Part 2 " #093 " A Duel with Destiny: Kaiba Vs. Ishizu, Part 1 " #099 " Isolated in Cyber Space, Part 1 " 
Hosoda is credited as director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  episodes. #013 " Playback " 
Hosoda is credited as storyboard artist in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  episodes. #001 " Link into the VRAINS " #013 " Playback "
