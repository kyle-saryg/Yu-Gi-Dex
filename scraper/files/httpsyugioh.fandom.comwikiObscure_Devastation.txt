 Obscure Devastation  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from June 27, 2013. Each pack costs   15.
 This collection includes Gishki, Ancient Gear, Elemental HERO, Dark World Monsters and Spells, as well as Zombies with graveyard effects
