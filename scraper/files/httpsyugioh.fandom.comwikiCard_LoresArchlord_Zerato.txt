 English PSCT 
 Cannot be Normal Summoned/Set. Must be Special Summoned by Tributing 1 face-up "Warrior of Zera" while "The Sanctuary in the Sky" is on the field. You can discard 1 LIGHT monster; destroy all monsters your opponent controls. You must control "The Sanctuary in the Sky" to activate and to resolve this effect.
