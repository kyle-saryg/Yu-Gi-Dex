 Effect 
 Printed lore : If your opponent  activates  a  Spell / Trap Card  that would  destroy  a  monster (s) on the  field :  Send  1  Level  3 or lower  DARK  monster from your  Deck  to the  Graveyard , and if you do,  negate  that  effect . Millennium Memory lore :  Send  1  Level  3 or lower  DARK   monster  from your  Deck  to the  Graveyard , and  negate  a monster  destruction  by your opponent’s  Spell / Trap   effect . [1] [2]
