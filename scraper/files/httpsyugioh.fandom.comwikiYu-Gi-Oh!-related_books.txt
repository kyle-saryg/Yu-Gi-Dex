 Marik's Monster Challenges  - A Marik-themed activity book. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh: Monster Mazes  - By Jeff O'Hare. A maze book. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Handbook  - By Tracey West. A handbook releasing in 2016 detailing the characters and monsters of the Yu-Gi-Oh anime, compiling information from all the previous guide books.
