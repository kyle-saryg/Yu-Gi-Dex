 " Live☆Twin " is an  archetype  of  Level  2  monsters  with 500  ATK  and 0  DEF  that debuted in " Deck Build Pack: Genesis Impactors ".
 The archetype is strongly linked by the " Evil★Twin " archetype that supports it. Also, it is loosely linked by  Chaos , due to  LIGHT  and  DARK  monster theme.
 
 Design 
 Appearance 
 While the younger "Live☆Twin" appear as internet influencers, the mature "Evil★Twin" are luxurious thieves.
 
 Etymology 
 While the related series' name is " Evil ★Twin", this archetype's name is inverted as " Live ☆Twin".
