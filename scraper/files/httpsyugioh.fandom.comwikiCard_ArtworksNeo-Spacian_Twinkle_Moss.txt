 Anime 
 
 When this card was released in Japan, its artwork was completely redrawn, as compared to its  anime  artwork. 
 When this card was released internationally, the monster's bust reduced, and has lines drawn near her wrists and neck to give the impression she's wearing clothes (the lines indicating sleeves).
