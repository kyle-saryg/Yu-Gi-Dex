 Beginner's Edition 2 (2011) 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Beginner's Edition 2 (2011)  is the second reprint set of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  era. This follows  Beginner's Edition 1 (2011) .

 Features 
 This set is based on  Beginner's Edition 2 . 
 A variety of cards have been specially selected, all of which have been renewed with a new card and text format. 
 Additionally, 5  Secret Rare  cards have also been included. 
 Breakdown 
 12 cards per pack, 12 packs per box. Includes 210 cards, comprised of:
 
 5  Secret Rares 
 14  Ultra Rares 
 20  Super Rares
