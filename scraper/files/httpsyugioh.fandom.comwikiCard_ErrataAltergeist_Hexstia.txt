 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 2 "Altergeist" monsters Gains ATK equal to the original ATK of each "Altergeist" monster it points to. When a Spell/Trap Card is activated (Quick Effect): You can Tribute 1 "Altergeist" monster this card points to; negate the activation, and if you do, destroy that card. If this card is sent from the field to the GY: You can add 1 "Altergeist" card from your Deck to your hand. You can only use this effect of "Altergeist Hexstia" once per turn.
