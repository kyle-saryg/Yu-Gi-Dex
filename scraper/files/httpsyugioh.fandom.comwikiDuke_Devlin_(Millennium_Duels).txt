 Duke Devlin , known as  Ryuji Otogi  (御伽龍児,  Otogi Ryūji ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Duke Devlin , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
