The  Regional Duel Monsters Championship  are mentioned as being a lower stage than the  National Duel Monsters Championship , and are usually the considered qualifiers to the National Tournaments.
 In the dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime , a Regional Championship was won by  Weevil Underwood  who defeated  Rex Raptor . (In manga and Japanese anime, Weevil won the  Japanese National Duel Monsters Championship .)
