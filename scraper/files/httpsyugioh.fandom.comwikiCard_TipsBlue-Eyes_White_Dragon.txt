 This card can be Tribute Summoned with one Tribute by using " Totem Dragon ", " Keeper of the Shrine ", " Kaiser Sea Horse ", " Light Effigy " and " The First Monarch ". 
 With the highest  ATK  among all  Normal Monsters , this card is ideal to  Special Summon  with " Ancient Rules ", " Swing of Memories ", " Birthright ", or " Silver's Cry ".
