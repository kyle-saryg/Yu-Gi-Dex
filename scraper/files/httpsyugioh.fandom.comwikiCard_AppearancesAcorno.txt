 Anime 
 
 In  episode 9 ,  Yuma Tsukumo  uses this card during his Duel against  Cathy Katherine . He  Special Summons  this card via its own effect by  discarding  " Pinecono ". After Yuma activates the effect of "Pinecono" to revive itself, he  overlays  this card, with "Pinecono" and " Joe the Pixie " in order to  Xyz Summon  " Baby Tiragon ". 
 In  episode 10 , this card is seen when  Caswell Francis  checks Yuma's Deck for his "Number" monsters (which were not present at the time due to Astral not being around at the time). 
 In  episode 14 , during Yuma's Duel against  Kite Tenjo , this card appears in Yuma's Deck as part of the effect of " Monster Slots ". 
 In  episode 20 ,  Number 96  uses this card during his Duel against  Bronk Stone  while the former was using Yuma's Deck. He Special Summons this card via its own effect by discarding "Pinecono". After Number 96 activates the effect of "Pinecono" to revive itself, he Normal Summons " Darklon " and activates its effect to treat this card and "Pinecono" as  DARK  monsters and increase the Level of every monster he controls by one. Number 96 then overlays this card, "Pinecono", and "Darklon" in order to Xyz Summon " Number 96: Dark Mist ". 
 In  episode 24 , Astral uses this card during his Duel against Kite. He Special Summons this card via its own effect by discarding " Decrease ". Astral then activates " Drop Exchange " to send this card and " Zubaba Knight " to the Graveyard and Special Summon " Kagetokage " (as the latter's Level was equal to the total Levels of this card and "Zubaba Knight").
