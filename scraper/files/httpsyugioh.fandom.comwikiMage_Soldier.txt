 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 The  Mage Soldier  is a Mage in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories .
 One of them duels  the prince , when he finds him at the remains of the palace, while the Mages are in control of the metropolis. After losing, he admits to underestimating the prince.
 Another Mage Soldier guards the Vast Shrine, preventing the prince to Heishin's temple.
 Officers in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom  bears a striking resemblance to the Mage Soldier.
