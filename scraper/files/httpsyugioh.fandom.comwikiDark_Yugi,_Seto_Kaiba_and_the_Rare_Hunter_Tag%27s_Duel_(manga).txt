 Dark Yugi and Seto Kaiba  faced the  Rare Hunter Tag  in a Duel in the  Battle City  tournament, during the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Events 
 While searching for his friends, who had been taken captive by  Marik Ishtar ,  Dark Yugi  was stopped by two  Rare Hunters . They challenged him to Duels, threatening to have his friend  Katsuya Jonouchi  killed, if he refused to accept. As the Rare Hunters tried to decide which of them should Duel first,  Seto Kaiba  arrived and insisted that they make it a  Tag Duel . He told Dark Yugi not to get the wrong idea, he was not there to help him, but rather to wipe out the Rare Hunters.
 The Duel quickly ended when Kaiba defeated the two Rare Hunters with " The God of the Obelisk "
