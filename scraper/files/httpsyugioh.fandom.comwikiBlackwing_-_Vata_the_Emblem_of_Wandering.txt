 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  control  a " Blackwing "  monster  other than "Blackwing - Vata the Emblem of Wandering", you can  Special Summon  this card (from your  hand ). You can only Special Summon with this  effect  of "Blackwing - Vata the Emblem of Wandering"  once per turn . During your  Main Phase : You can  send  this card on the  field  and 1 or more "Blackwing" non- Tuners  from your  Deck  to the  GY , whose total  Levels  exactly equal 8, and if you do, Special Summon 1 " Black-Winged Dragon " from your  Extra Deck , also you cannot Special Summon monsters from the Extra Deck for the rest of this  turn , except  DARK  monsters. You can only use this effect of "Blackwing - Vata the Emblem of Wandering" once per turn.
