" Yusei's Theme " ( 遊星 のテーマ,  Yūsei no Tēma ) is an instrumental composition from the Japanese  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 It is 2:51 long and has been released on  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Sound Duel Vol II .
