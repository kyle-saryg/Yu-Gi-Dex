 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 When this card on the  field  is  destroyed  and  sent  to the  Graveyard ,  select  1 card from your  hand ,  Deck , or Graveyard, and  remove it from play . Then, you can  activate  this card in the Graveyard as the removed card on the field. If this card is on the field with this  effect , it cannot be destroyed.
