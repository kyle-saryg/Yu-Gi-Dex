 VWXYZ-Dragon Catapult Cannon  is a character version of the card " VWXYZ-Dragon Catapult Cannon ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P081.
 In the beta, its partner number was P037 instead.
