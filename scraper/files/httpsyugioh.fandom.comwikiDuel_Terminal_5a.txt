 5a  is the first part of  Duel Terminal 5  made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Vylon Descends!!  and was released May 29, 2011.

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. Includes further support for " Gem-Knight ", " Laval " and " Vylon " archetypes.
