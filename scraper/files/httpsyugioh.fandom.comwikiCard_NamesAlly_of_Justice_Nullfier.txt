 The unofficial Arabic name given (نصير العدالة: المبيد  Naṣīru l-ʿAdālah: al-Mubīd ) translates to  Ally of Justice: Annihilator . 
 The Japanese name of this card ( Ally of Justice Blind Sucker ) can be translated in Greek as " Σύμμαχος της Δικαιοσύνης Τυφλός Ρουφηχτής ".
