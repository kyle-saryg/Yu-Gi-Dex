 Structure Deck: Lost Sanctuary  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the twentieth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-eighth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Dragunity Drive . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Lost Sanctuary Structure Deck .

 Features 
 The deck features the  Fairy -Type Theme. It is a  Fairy -Type Deck orientated on utilizing the " Agents ". It includes 5 new cards and many reprints.
