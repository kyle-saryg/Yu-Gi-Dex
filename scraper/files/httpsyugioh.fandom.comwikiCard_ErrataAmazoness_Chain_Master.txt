 Second erratum 
 
 
 When this monster is destroyed  as a result of  battle and sent to the Graveyard,  by   paying  1500 Life Points ,  look at your opponent's hand  and  select 1 Monster Card  from  it .   Add   the selected  card to your hand , and you can use it in this Duel .  (When the card is sent to the Graveyard, it is sent to the Graveyard of the owner.) 
 When this  monster  is destroyed  by  battle and sent to the Graveyard,  you can   pay  1500 Life Points  to  look at your opponent's hand ,  select 1 Monster Card  in  it , and   add   that  card to your hand. 
 When this  card  is destroyed by battle and sent to the Graveyard ,   you  can pay 1500 Life Points  to  look at your opponent's hand,  select 1 Monster Card in it, and  add  that card  to your hand.
