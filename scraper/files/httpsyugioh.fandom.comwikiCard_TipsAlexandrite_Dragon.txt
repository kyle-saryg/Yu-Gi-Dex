 This card can be  searched  by " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Transmodify ", " Summoner Monk ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Rabbit ", " Princess Cologne ", " Unexpected Dai ", " Painful Decision ", " Painful Escape ", " Soul Rope ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Carboneddon ", " Blue Dragon Summoner ", " Dragoons of Draconia ", " Photon Veil ", " Chaos Zone ", " Dragunity Knight - Gae Dearg ", " Dragonic Guard ", " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation ", " Draconnection ", " Tempest, Dragon Ruler of Storms ", " Minerva, Lightsworn Maiden ", " Guardragon Corewakening " and the  Hieratic  monsters " Atum ", " Eset ", " Gebeb ", " Nebthet ", " Nuit ", " Su " and " Tefnuit ". 
 This card also benefits from support for  Dragon   Normal Monsters  such as " Dragon Revival Rhapsody ", " Dragon Shrine ", " Keeper of the Shrine ", " Neo Kaiser Glider ", " Rider of the Storm Winds ", " Silver's Cry ", " Vanguard of the Dragon " and " Ventdra, the Empowered Warrior ". 
 Use this card with "Rescue Rabbit":
 To  Special Summon  " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon " 
 To  Xyz Summon  any Rank 4 monsters 
 Alternately, you can use the two Special Summoned monsters to pay for " Dragonic Tactics " and then recover them by using " Dark Factory of Mass Production ". 
 This card can also be used to work with " Lightpulsar Dragon " and " Darkflare Dragon ".
