 Light and Darkness Dragon  is a character version of the card " Light and Darkness Dragon ".
 In the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga , Light and Darkness Dragon is  Chazz Princeton's  Spirit partner.
 
 Biography 
 Light and Darkness Dragon appeared to Chazz through his " Light and Darkness Dragon " card.
 After Chazz started attending  Duel Academy , people began spreading rumors of him only gaining access to the Academy because of his wealth. In order to prove them wrong, Chazz buried the Dragon, which was his best card, and proceeded to Duel  Jaden Yuki  without it. Despite that Chazz abandoned it, Light and Darkness Dragon watched over the Duel. Having realized Jaden also had a spirit partner,  Winged Kuriboh , which he used to beat Chazz, he recovers the "Light and Darkness Dragon" card and vows to defeat Jaden with Light and Darkness Dragon as his partner.
 Chazz fulfilled his vow, as he and Light and Darkness Dragon defeated Jaden in the tournament. The card is referred to as the " Feather of Ma'at " by  Tragoedia  and its allies, who fear it as a severe threat to their plans.
 
 Non-canon appearances 
       Light and Darkness Dragon in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , Light and Darkness Dragon can be unlocked in Free Duel Mode, after 100 hours of playing time.
 
 Deck 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator  Light and Darkness Dragon uses a Deck titled, "You Me Dark Light".
