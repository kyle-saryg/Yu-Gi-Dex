 Normal 
 
 Activate  only if you  control  a  face-up  " Amazoness "  monster .  Select  1  face-up   Fusion Monster  on the  field , and  return  it to the  Fusion Deck . Then, if all of the  Fusion Material Monsters  that were used to  Fusion Summon  that monster are in the  Graveyard (s),  Special Summon  them to their owners' sides of the field.
