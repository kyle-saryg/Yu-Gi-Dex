 V Jump Edition  is the eighteenth installment of the newly renamed Limited Edition series and could be pre-ordered with the July Issue of V Jump Magazine in 2010. It consist of cards that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  and  5D's mangas  shortly before the set's release. It introduces the " Vision HERO "  archetype  and cards with red name, morover supports the " Sphere "  archetype .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards: 5  Ultra Rares .
