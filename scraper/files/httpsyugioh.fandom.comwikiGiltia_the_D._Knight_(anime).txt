 Main card page: " Giltia the D. Knight " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Knight "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ナイト」 .   As its English name contains "Knight", it  may  be treated a member of the "Knight" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
