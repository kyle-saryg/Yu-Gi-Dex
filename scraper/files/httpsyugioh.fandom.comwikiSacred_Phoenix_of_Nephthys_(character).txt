 Sacred Phoenix of Nephthys  is a character version of the card, " Sacred Phoenix of Nephthys ."
 Sacred Phoenix of Nephthys appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . In  World Championship 2008 , she can be unlocked if the player has beaten  Horus the Black Flame Dragon LV8  five times. If the player has unlocked this character, the player can unlock her as a tag partner by beating her ten times.

 In  World Championship 2006 , Sacred Phoenix of Nephthys uses a  Zombie  Deck titled, "Flight of the Phoenix". In  World Championship 2007  and  2008 , she uses an  EARTH  Deck titled, "Reset Syndrome" and "Eternal Phoenix".
