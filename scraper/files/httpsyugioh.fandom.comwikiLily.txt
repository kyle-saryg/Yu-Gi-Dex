 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Lily uses a  Dinosaur  Deck  focused on " Tyranno Infinity ". Another character,  Andrea , would use similar Decks for the next two games.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Lily uses Decks based around the effect of " Destiny Board ".
 From  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4  onwards, Lily's Decks use a variety of  alternate win conditions .
