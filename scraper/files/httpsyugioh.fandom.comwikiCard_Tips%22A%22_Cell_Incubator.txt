 This card can be  searched  by " Watch Cat ", " Watch Dog ", " Rogue of Endymion ", " Cosmic Slicer Zer'oll " and " The Despair Uranus ". 
 The following cards specifically remove  A-Counters  from the field: " Alien Hypno ", " Alien Telepath ", " Alien Overlord ", " Brainwashing Beam ", " Code A Ancient Ruins ", "Cosmic Slicer Zer'oll" and " Cosmic Fortress Gol'gar ". 
 If you control 3 copies of this card, each A-Counter removed from the field will place 3 more to use.
