 This card has a light counterpart  Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus . 
 This card has the highest  ATK  points out of all  Advanced Crystal Beast  monsters. 
 Just like its light counterpart  Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ,  Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus  is called a Pegasus on the card but is actually an Alicorn (Which is a Unicorn with wings, or a Pegasus with a horn).
