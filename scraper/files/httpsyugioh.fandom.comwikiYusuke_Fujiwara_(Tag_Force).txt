 Yusuke Fujiwara  is a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Yusuke Fujiwara , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 3 
 In the last event of Season 4  Jaden 's and Excited  Bastion 's stories, Yusuke and  Nightshroud  Tag Duel against the player and their partner.
 
 
 Decks 
 Yusuke uses  Clear  Decks  similar to his Deck in the anime - he uses " Clear World " to inflict various effects on his opponents depending on the  Attributes  of their monsters while he remains unaffected due to "Clear" monsters not possessing any Attribute while on the field.
