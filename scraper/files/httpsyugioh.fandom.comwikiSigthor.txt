 Sigthor , known as  Ikuo Kiyokuni  in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Slifer Red  student.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 When the player receives Sigthor's  Deck Recipe  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , they also receive the card " The Graveyard in the Fourth Dimension ".
 Unless partnered with the player, he partners with  Joshua  during the Tag Force tournament.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 In the fourth-to-last event of  Chazz Princeton 's story, Sigthor and  KENYoU  Tag Duel against the player and Chazz.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Sigthor uses a Deck largely composed of Normal Monsters. Its goal is to use " Heart of the Underdog " to have large amounts of them in hand and  fuse  them into more powerful monsters with " Fusion Gate ".
 After the first game, Sigthor uses Decks focused on  Union Monsters .
