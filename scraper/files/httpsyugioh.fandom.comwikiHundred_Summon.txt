 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 If you  control  3 or more " Number 10X "  Xyz Monsters :  Special Summon  1 "Number 10X"  monster  from your  Extra Deck . (X is any number from 1 to 7.) Its  effects  are  negated , it cannot  attack , also it is  shuffled  into the  Extra Deck  during the  End Phase .
