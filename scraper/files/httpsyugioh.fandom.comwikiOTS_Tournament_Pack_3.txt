 OTS Tournament Pack 3  is a booster pack that is available as a Tournament Prize or Participation Pack released to the  TCG  tournament locations. It is the third  OTS Tournament Pack , following  OTS Tournament Pack 2 .
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 3 cards per pack. The set contains 27 cards. This consists of:
 
 3  Ultimate Rares 
 10  Super Rares 
 14  Commons
