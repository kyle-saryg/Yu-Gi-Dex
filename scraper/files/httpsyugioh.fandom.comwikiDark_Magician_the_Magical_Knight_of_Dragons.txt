 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
" Dark Magician " + 1  Level  7 or higher  Dragon  or  Warrior   monster If your monster  attacks  a  Defense Position  monster, inflict  piercing   battle damage  to your opponent.  Once per turn , when your monster  destroys  an opponent's monster  by battle : You can inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the destroyed monster's  original ATK . If this card is destroyed: You can  Special Summon  1 "Dark Magician" and 1 " Gaia the Dragon Champion " from your  hand ,  Deck ,  Extra Deck , and/or  GY .
