 Extra Pack 2016  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG - exclusive  cards to the  OCG , and introduces previously  OCG  region-exclusive  Booster Pack  cards to the other region. [1]

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive cards from the Booster Packs  Clash of Rebellions ,  Dimension of Chaos ,  Breakers of Shadow  and  Shining Victories , as well as reprints of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2015 prize cards .
 The Japanese version also includes the Korean  OCG -exclusive  cards from  Clash of Rebellions  and  Dimension of Chaos . 
 The Korean version also includes the Japanese  OCG -exclusive cards from  Clash of Rebellions  and  Dimension of Chaos . 
 Introduces the " Kozmo " and " Kaiju " archetypes to the  OCG , as well as the " Paleozoic " archetype to the Japanese  OCG .
