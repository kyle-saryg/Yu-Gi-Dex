      The disciplinary group     
 The  disciplinary group  is a group of hall monitors at  Domino High School  led by  Ushio  in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime .  [1] 
 The group is set on enforcing school rules and regulations, although Ushio is shown to have done this through unsavory means and for personal gain. [1]
