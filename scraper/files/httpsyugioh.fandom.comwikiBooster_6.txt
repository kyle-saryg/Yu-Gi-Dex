 Booster 6  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  that was released on December 1, 1999. This set was sold only in  Carddass  vending machines. 
 Like other cards printed early on in the game, the cards have no printed set numbers, and lack the distinctive  Eye of Anubis Hologram  in the lower right-hand corner of each card.
 Of the 40 cards in  Booster 6 , all but 7—" Boulder Tortoise ", " Dragon Statue ", " Garvas ", " Ill Witch ", " Mechaleon ", " Rock Spirit ", and " Spirit of the Winds "—have since been printed in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game .
 
 Breakdown 
 Booster 6  contains 40 cards.  Rarities  are distributed as follows:
 
 5  Rare  cards 
 35  Common  cards 
 The set also contains six  non-playable   FAQ Cards .
