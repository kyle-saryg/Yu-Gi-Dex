 Jesse Anderson , known as  Johan Andersen  (ヨハン・アンデルセン,  Yohan Anderusen ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Jesse Anderson , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2 
 Jesse is one of the Tier 1 characters the player can have as a partner right from the start of the game.
 During the story for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters, Jesse is dueled twice.
 The player can Tag Duel against Jesse and  Tyranno Hassleberry  as part of an optional random event.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 In the first event of Slifer Red  Blair Flannigan 's story,  Sartorius  and Jesse duel the player and Blair.
 Jesse teams up with  Aster Phoenix  during the last story event for Young  Blair Flannigan  and Normal  Chazz Princeton 's third event.
 During the first event for Slifer Red and Ra Yellow  Syrus Truesdale 's stories, Syrus and the player duel Jesse and Chazz.
 Jesse and  Camula  Tag Duel against the player and their partner during the story for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters. If Jesse is the player's partner, they duel against Camula and  Atticus Rhodes  instead.
