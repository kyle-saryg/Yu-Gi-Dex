 " Writing Life " ( Ｗｒｉｔｉｎｇ   Ｌｉｆｅ ,  Raitingu Raifu ) is the second Japanese ending theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime, performed by  Goodbye holiday . It replaces  Believe In Magic  in  episode 25 . It was replaced by  BOY  starting  episode 47 .
