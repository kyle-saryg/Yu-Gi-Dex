 Activation requirement 
 Continuous 
 Continuous 
 If the only  monster  you  control  is 1  face-up   Xyz Monster :  Activate  this card by  targeting  that monster. While that monster is face-up on the  field ,  negate  the  effects  of all other face-up monsters on the field whose  Rank  or  Level  is higher than that monster's Rank, also those monsters cannot target the targeted monster for an  attack . When that monster  leaves the field ,  destroy  this card.
