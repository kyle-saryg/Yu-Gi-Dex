 Thestalos the Firestorm Monarch  is a character version of the card, " Thestalos the Firestorm Monarch ". 
 Thestalos the Firestorm Monarch appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus . In  World Championship 2007 , by beating him five times, the player gets closer to a new Booster Pack.
