 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . When a  monster   declares  an  attack : You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand .  Once per turn : You can choose 1 player; make that player's  LP  4000. Once per turn: You can  return  all  Spell / Trap Cards  on the  field  to the hand. -I  Summon  " Darkness Neosphere "!-
