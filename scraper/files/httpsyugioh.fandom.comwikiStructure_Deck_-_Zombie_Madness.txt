 Structure Deck: Zombie Madness  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the second deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (tenth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Dragon's Roar . In the  TCG , it is the second deck.
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Structure Deck: Zombie Madness  was also released on December 15, 2005 bundled with  Structure Deck: Dragon's Roar  as the  Structure Deck: Special Set .
 
 Ferocious  Dragons  and ravaging  Zombies  clash in these two new  Structure Decks . The  Structure Decks  are tournament tough, ready to play with out of the box with powerful hard to find cards. Each  Deck  contains 40 cards, including one brand new  Ultra Rare  monster, rulebook, and Dueling guide which assists a Duelist in taking his game to the next level. 
 —  Upper Deck Entertainment

 Features 
 The deck features  Zombie - Type  monsters, with feature cards including " Vampire Genesis ", " Dark Dust Spirit ", and " Call of the Mummy ".
 The "Let's Power Up This Deck! (With Booster Packs)" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Hallowed Life Barrier " from  Soul of the Duelist ; " Serial Spell " from  Rise of Destiny ; " Red-Moon Baby ", " Tribute to the Doomed ", " Painful Choice " and " Premature Burial " from  Dark Beginning 1 ; and " Fear from the Dark ", " Dark Core ", " Rope of Life " and " Judgement of Anubis " from  Dark Revelation Volume 1  ("Rope of Life" is also available as a promotional card in  Structure Deck: Deluxe Edition ).
