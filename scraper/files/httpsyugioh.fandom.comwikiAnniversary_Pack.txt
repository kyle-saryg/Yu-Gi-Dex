 Anniversary Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 

This set features reprints of past cards each with new artworks drawn by  Kazuki Takahashi . " Dark Magician Girl " was excluded from the International print of the set, but was finally officially released in 2020 as part of the  The Lost Art Promotion 2020 . Kazuki Takahashi has his signature in the lower right hand corner on the artworks. This set is unique in that the card descriptions for  Normal Monsters  are not italicized and there is no border around the artworks.
