 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 The Meadow Mage  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is located at the Meadow Shrine.  The Prince  must defeat him before proceeding to  High Mage Kepura  to duel for the  Millennium Eye  and he's Kepura's top minion in The Falsebound Kingdom under the name Granus.
 
 Powerful Cards 
 The Meadow Mage is particular popular among players of Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories, due to the powerful cards that can be won off him. Good cards that can be obtained by defeating the Meadow Mage include:
