 Joey's red jacket opponent  is an unnamed tournament  Duelist  that  Joey Wheeler  Dueled in a dream while he was in a coma, after his Duel with  Yami Marik . He ultimately defeated Joey and moved on to the finals of the tournament. [1] 
 
 Deck 
 This Duelist plays a Deck composed of weak  Normal Monsters , much like what Joey himself was playing at the time.
