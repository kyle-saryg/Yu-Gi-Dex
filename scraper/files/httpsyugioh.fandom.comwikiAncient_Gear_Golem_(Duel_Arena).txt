 Ancient Gear Golem  is a character version of the card " Ancient Gear Golem ". 
 It appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P034. It is also available as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 3 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, it had a default of 5 Rental Points as a partner, and its partner ID was P017.
