 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When this card  resolves , you can  add  1 " Kashtira "  monster  from your  Deck  to your  hand . Monsters you  control  gain 100  ATK / DEF  for each different  Attribute  among monsters on the  field . If " Kashtira Shangri-Ira " you control  activates  its  effect : You can  target  1 card on the field;  destroy  it. You can only use this effect of "Prime Planet Paraisos"  once per turn . You can only activate 1 "Prime Planet Paraisos" per  turn .
