 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (Toei) 
 
 
 
 Sumomo-chan  is  Anzu Mazaki 's  Digital Pet  in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . She is based on  Peachy , from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . "Sumomo" means "peach" or "plum".
 
 Biography 
 Kujirada  grabbed Sumomo from Anzu and linked it with his own Digital Pet,  Devil Master , allowing Devil Master to eat Sumomo.
