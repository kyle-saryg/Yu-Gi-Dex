 Duelist Legacy Volume.1  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in the  Duelist Legacy  series. It contains re-prints of cards from  Magic Ruler ,  Pharaoh's Servant  and  Curse of Anubis  using the "World Standard" card design format introduced by the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  (TCG).
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack, and 30 packs per box.
