 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In Tag Force 1-3, he is a  Ra Yellow  student at  Duel Academy .
 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , when the player gets Enzo's  Deck Recipe , they also receive the card " Big Core ".
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 Enzo is a resident of  Tops .
 
 Tag Force 4 
       Enzo, in  Tag Force 4 .     In his own story, she competes with the player against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the Player will receive a photo of himself, Enzo and  Mathew  called "Tops Guy".
 Tag Force 5 
 In  Crow 's third story event, Enzo and  KENYoU  Tag Duel against the player and Crow.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Enzo uses a Deck with a large number of high DEF monsters and  Life Point  recovery cards.
 In  Tag Force 2  and  Tag Force 3 , he switches to  Stall / Burn  Decks  focused on " Fire Princess ".
 In the  Tag Force 5D's  games, Enzo uses  EARTH   Rock  Decks  with monsters that flip themselves face-down and return his opponent's cards to their hand.
