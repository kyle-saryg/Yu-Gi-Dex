 V Jump promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  V Jump  promotional cards  are a series of  OCG  promotional card releases available through  V Jump , a Japanese manga anthology which is published monthly on the 21st. Most of the cards are included individually in issues of the magazine, sometimes alongside promotional  Tokens , but four each year are available in biannual subscription bonuses, two cards each in Spring and Fall bonus packs, for those who subscribe or renew their subscription to the magazine within a certain time frame.
 Many of these and the  Weekly Shōnen Jump  promotional cards  are introduced to the  TCG  as  Shonen Jump  promotional cards , mirroring the status of  Shonen Jump  (and its digital successors  Weekly Shonen Jump Alpha  and  Weekly Shonen Jump ) as an English version of  V Jump  and  Weekly Shōnen Jump . The magazine's promotional cards are usually exclusive, but sometimes get reprints in premade Decks and Booster Packs.
 
 Features 
 Features cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ,  GX ,  5D's ,  ZEXAL ,  ARC-V  and  VRAINS  anime series and   Pyramid of Light ,  Bonds Beyond Time  and  The Dark Side of Dimensions  anime films, and the  Duelist ,  R ,  GX ,  5D's ,  ZEXAL  and  ARC-V  manga series, as well as the  ARC-V  manga one-shot.
