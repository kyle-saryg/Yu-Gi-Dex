 The  Mythyrian Numbers War  is the fifth arc of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . The arc focuses on the upcoming battle of Yuma, Astral and their allies efforts to obtain the 7  Mythyrian "Number"  cards before the  Seven Barian Emperors  can use them to find the  Numeron Code . However, thanks to Vector, the  Barian  deity,  Don Thousand , has been released and begins to set his own secret  grand   design  for the Code and  the   three   worlds  in motion.
