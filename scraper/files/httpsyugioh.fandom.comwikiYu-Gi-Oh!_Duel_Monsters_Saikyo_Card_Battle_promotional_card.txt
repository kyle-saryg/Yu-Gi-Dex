 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Saikyo Card Battle promotional card 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Saikyo Card Battle  promotional card  is a " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " card that was given to elementary and junior high school students who completed the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Saikyo Card Battle .
