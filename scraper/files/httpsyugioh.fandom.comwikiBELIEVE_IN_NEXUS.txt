" BELIEVE IN NEXUS " ( ＢＥＬＩＥＶＥ   ＩＮ   ＮＥＸＵＳ ,  Birību in Nekusasu ) is the fourth opening theme to  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  as of  episode 104 . It was preceded by " FREEDOM ". The  third  " Meklord Emperor Granel ", like its brethren originally were in the previous opening until this one, had continued to be hidden from view in the opening and was not truly revealed until  episode 110 . " Shooting Star Dragon " also stayed hidden until   episode 109  where it was somewhat revealed, then in 110 onwards, it is fully revealed.
 It is performed by  Masaaki Endoh . The full version of this was officially released on May 12, 2010.
 It is replaced by " Road to Tomorrow ~Going My Way!!~ " in  episode 130 .

 Changes 
 From  episode 109  onwards,  Shooting Star Dragon , while still covered in a blue glow is more visible. 
 From  episode 110  onwards,  Shooting Star Dragon  is no longer covered in a blue glow while  Meklord Emperor Granel  is no longer covered in shadow.
