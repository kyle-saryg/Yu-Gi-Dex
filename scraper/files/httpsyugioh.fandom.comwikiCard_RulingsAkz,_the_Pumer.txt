 OCG Rulings 
 This monster is a  Special Summon monster . [1] 
 " This card cannot be Normal Summoned or Set. " is not treated as an Effect Monster's effect. [1] 
 When Special Summoning this card from your hand, the  Beast-Warrior - Type  monsters are  banished  as part of the Special Summoning procedure. [1] 
 Special Summoning this card does not start a Chain Link. [1] 
 You select the effect when  activating  the effect.  The  Beast-Warrior - Type  monster is discarded as an  activation   cost . [1] 
 The effects do not  target . [1]
