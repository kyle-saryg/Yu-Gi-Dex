 Legend of Duelist Kingdom  is a tournament which took place in the timeline  Z-one  was from in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime. [1]  Its name is derived from the original  Duelist Kingdom  tournament.
 The tournament was won by  Yusei Fudo  and his victory was listed on a profile Z-one had of Yusei. [1]
