 " Attraction " (Japanese:  Ɐ ) is an  archetype  consisting of  Normal Traps  that debuted in  Lightning Overdrive . It is closely related to the " Amazement " archetype.
 
 Design 
 In the OCG, the name of the archetype uses a  turned capital letter A , Ɐ, in its base text. A similar symbol, ∀, represents the "for all" logical operator used in mathematics. 
 
 Appearance 
 The "Attraction" cards are carnival rides that are inspired by various  archetypes .
