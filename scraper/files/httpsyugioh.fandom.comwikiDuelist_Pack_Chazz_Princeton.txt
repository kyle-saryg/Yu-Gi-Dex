 Duelist Pack: Chazz Princeton  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the second set in the Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Jaden Yuki .
 In the  TCG , the set was also released in  Duelist Pack: Special Edition  alongside its companion booster pack  Duelist Pack: Jaden Yuki . These Special Edition boxes contained one of 3  Limited Edition  cards (" Treasure Map ", " Hero Spirit ", and " Blockman ").

 Features 
 The set features cards used by  Chazz Princeton  (Manjoume Jun) from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  series. The cards in the set are mostly reprints with a few new cards.
 New cards are  Armed Dragon LV10 ,  Magical Mallet ,  Inferno Reckless Summon , Ring of Defense and  The Grave of Enkindling .
 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Chazz Princeton  contains 5 cards and each box contains 15 packs. In the  TCG , each pack of  Duelist Pack: Chazz Princeton  contains 6 cards and each box contains 30 packs. There are 30 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
