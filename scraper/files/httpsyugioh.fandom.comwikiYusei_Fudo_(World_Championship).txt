 Unlockables 
 Stardust Accelerator 
 
 The player can unlock the  leather top and bottom , by defeating Yusei in ten  Turbo Duels . 
 The player can unlock  Yusei's hybrid Disk , by defeating Yusei ten times in standing Duels. 
 Reverse of Arcadia 
 
 The player can unlock the leather top and bottom, by defeating Yusei in ten  Turbo Duels . 
 The player can unlock Yusei's hybrid Disk, by defeating Yusei ten times in standing Duels. 
 The player can unlock the pack,  Ruler of Chaos!! , by defeating Yusei,  Crow ,  Jack  and  Kalin  three times each. 
 Over the Nexus 
 
 The player can unlock the  Team 5D's crew  outfit, by defeating Yusei and Jack ten times each in Turbo Duels. 
 The player can unlock Yusei's hybrid Disk, by defeating Yusei ten times in standing Duels. 
 The player can unlock the pack,  Starstrike Blast , by defeating Yusei and Jack three times each in single, Tag and Turbo Duels and Crow three times in single and Turbo Duels. 
 The frame of Yusei's Duel Runner,  Yusei Go , can be unlocked by achieving an S rank in all the time trials.
