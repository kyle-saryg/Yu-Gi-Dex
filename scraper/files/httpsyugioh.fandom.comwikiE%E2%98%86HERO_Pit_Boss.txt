 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
3  Level  6  monsters Must be  Xyz Summoned  and cannot be  Special Summoned  by other ways. If this card  attacks  your opponent  directly  and reduces their  Life Points  to 0, while it has an  Xyz Material   attached  that was originally  Spellcaster - Type , you win the  Match .
