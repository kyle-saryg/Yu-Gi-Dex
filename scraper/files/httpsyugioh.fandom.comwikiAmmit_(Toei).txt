 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (Toei) 
 
 
 
 Ammit  is a monster summoned by  Shadi  during his  Shadow Games , in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . This is the series incarnation of  Ammit , from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Biography 
 Shadi transformed  Curator Kanekura 's chair into an illusion of Ammit, during a  Weighing of the Heart   Game of Darkness . After Kanekura failed the game's trial, he was rendered unconscious by Ammit. [1]
