 Starter Deck: Xyz Symphony  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixth deck in the  TCG ' s annual Starter Deck series (fourteenth overall), following  Starter Deck: Dawn of the Xyz . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Starter Deck 2012  in the  OCG .
 
 Our largest Starter Deck in 8 years, this deck comes with 5 Xyz Monsters, including Yuma's signature monster Number 39: Utopia!
 Chock full of easy-to-use but plenty-powerful monsters, Spells, and Traps, this Starter Deck is designed to give first-time Duelists an action-packed crash course in the world of the Yu-Gi-Oh! TRADING CARD GAME. Combine the cards in the Starter Deck with cards from Galactic Overlord for exciting combos!

 This starter deck features "Number 39: Utopia" and the Xyz " Djinn " monsters that can be easily made with your many level 4 monsters included in the deck.
