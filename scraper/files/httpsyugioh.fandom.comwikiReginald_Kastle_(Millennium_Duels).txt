 Reginald Kastle , known as  Ryoga Kamishiro  ( 神 代   凌 牙 ,  Kamishiro Ryōga ) in the Japanese version and nicknamed  Shark , is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Reginald Kastle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
