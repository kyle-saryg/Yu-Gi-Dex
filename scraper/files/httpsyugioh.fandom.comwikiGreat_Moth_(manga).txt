 This was not a physical card, but " Larvae Moth " after prematurely hatching from " Cocoon of Evolution " after four turns.
 
 Description
 
 
 Before  Dark Yugi  attacks this card with " Gaia the Dragon Champion ",  Haga  explains that this monster scatters "poison pollen", infecting all monsters on the field. This causes the affected monsters' ATK and DEF to decay in multiples of 100.
