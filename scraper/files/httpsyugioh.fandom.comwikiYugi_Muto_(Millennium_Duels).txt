 Yugi Muto , known as  Yugi Mutou  (武藤遊戯,  Mutō Yūgi ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Yugi Muto , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
