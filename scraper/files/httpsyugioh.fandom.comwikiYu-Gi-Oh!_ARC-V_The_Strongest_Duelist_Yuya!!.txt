 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!!  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  spin-off manga series published in the  Saikyō Jump  magazine. The series features characters from the anime but follows an original manga only story, and is written and drawn by  Akihiro Tomonaga , who was also responsible for the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  spin-off manga,  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL .

 Summary 
 Like  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL , this series focuses on humor and all characters are drawn in a   chibi  style. Different from the canon series, which aim at a juvenile public, this manga series aims at younger children, and teaches them how to play the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  OCG  with a light and humored plot. 
 The series follows  Yuya  as he aims to become the strongest  Entertainment Duelist  out there.
 
 Rules 
 The rules used in the manga are the  OCG  ones. However, in order to make the gameplay simpler for the children, the manga has the  Monster Cards  have no  effects  whatsoever, and the effects of the  Spell  and  Trap Cards  are greatly simplified.
