 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto / Yami Yugi ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Rex Raptor ,  Duke Devlin ,  Nezbitt ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Jesse Anderson  (possessed by  Yubel ), Leo ( anime  and  manga ),  Luna ,  Tenzen Yanagi ,  Reginald Kastle/Nash ,  Nistro ,  Erazor ,  Scritch , Yuya Sakaki ( anime  and  manga ),  Frederick ,  Nagi ,  Gloria Tyler ,  Grace Tyler ,  George Gore ,  Earth , Crow Hogan ( 5D's anime  &  ARC-V anime  and  5D's manga ),  Sora Perse ,  Varis ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Yusaku Fujiki  and  Noah Kaiba . 
 Includes new members/support for for the " Amazoness ", " Crystal ", " Crystal Beast ", " Elemental HERO ", " Gadget ", " Heroic ", " Morphtronic ", " Neo-Spacian ", " Ninja ", " Ninjitsu Art ", " Number ", " Performapal ", and " Odd-Eyes " archetypes, as well as the  Three Musketeers of Face Cards  series to the TCG. 
 Introduces the " Advanced Crystal Beast ", " Doodle Beast ", " Doodlebook " and " G Golem " archetypes, as well as the  Ancient Treasure  series to the  TCG .
