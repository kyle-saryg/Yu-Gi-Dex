 Alien Overlord , known as  Alien Revenger  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Alien Overlord ".

 Appearances 
 Alien Overlord appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator  as an unlockable opponent. 
 
 Unlockables 
 To unlock it, the player must buy all centerpiece parts  Duel Runner  parts.
