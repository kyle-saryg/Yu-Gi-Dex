 " Rush Duel Sealed Away " is the seventy-ninth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on December 19, 2021.
 Luke ,  Gavin ,  Romin ,  Roa ,  Nail , and  Asana  rushed to the rooftop of the  Goha  building in order to take down  Yuga Goha . Luke faces off against Yuga in a Duel. However, the light radiating from Yuga's  forbidden card  engulfs him...
 
 Featured Duel: Lucidien "Luke" Kallister VS. Yuga Goha 
 Duel continues from the previous episode .
