Main card page: " Blackfeather Darkrage Dragon " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Blackwing "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 ＢＦ 」 .   As its English name contains "Blackwing", it  may  be treated a member of the "Blackwing" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
