 When this card was shown in the international  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime, its artwork was completely redrawn, as compared to its anime artwork.
 This artwork was also used as an alternate artwork in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Online . 
 When this card was released in the  OCG / TCG , its artwork was completely redrawn, as compared to either of its anime artworks. This artwork was used in all versions of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
