 Lore
 
 
 The cards of  Thunder , Water and  Wind  unite into the  Gate Guardian . 
 Rule 
 
 
 
 ~Special Rule~ 
 When the 3 Guardians are arranged together, they are played as the "Gate Guardian Spirit" ( ATK  2800/ DEF  2800) and can continue to battle for 3 turns.
