 Features 
 Introduces the " P.U.N.K. " and " Exosister "  archetypes , and the  Adventurer Token   series . 
 Includes new members/support for the " Noble Arms " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 7 cards. The set contains 60 cards.
 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 15  Super Rares 
 35  Rares 
 In addition, 15 cards in this set are also available as  Collector's Rare .
