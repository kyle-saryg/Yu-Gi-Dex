   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
Toss a coin 3 times. If 2 out of 3 results are Heads, destroy 1 opponent's monster. This card can only be used during your own turn, once per turn. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
A mechanical dragon fitted with cannons. It can wipe out up to 3  monsters  on the opponent's  field  with a 2-to-1 success rate. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
Toss a coin 3 times. If 2 out of 3 results are Heads, destroy 1 monster on your opponent's side of the field. This card's effect can only be used during your own turn, once per turn.
