 Anime 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 In  episode 108 , this card is seen in  Seto Kaiba 's hand during his duel against  Leichter . 
 In  episode 149 , Kaiba uses this card during his Duel against  Alister  while the latter was disguised as  Maximillion Pegasus . He uses this card to return " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " to his hand and destroy both " Toon World " and " Charm of Lamentation ". This causes Alister's " Toon Dark Magician Girl " to be destroyed by its own effect since "Toon World" was destroyed. 
 In  episode 200 , this card appears within Kaiba's briefcase when he opens it up and takes his Duel Disk with him. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  episode 22 ,  Bastion Misawa  uses this card during his duel against  Jaden Yuki . He uses this card to return " Hyozanryu " to his  hand  and destroy Jaden's " Skyscraper ". 
 In  episode 66 , this card appears in a flashback Jaden has when he sees  Wheeler  as Bastion which makes Jaden remember his Duel against Bastion. 
 In  episode 173 , during  Atticus Rhodes ' duel against  Yusuke Fujiwara , Atticus is forced to send this card from the top of his Deck to the Graveyard via the effect of " Clear Phantom ". 
 Scripted Duels 
 In  North American WCQ 2012 Special: Joey vs. Kaiba , Kaiba activates this card by returning " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " to his hand to destroy Joey's face-down " Skull Dice ". 
 In  World Championship 2016 Special: Yugi vs. Kaiba , Kaiba activates this card by returning "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" to his hand to destroy Yugi's " Dark Magical Circle " and Set " Mystic Box ".
