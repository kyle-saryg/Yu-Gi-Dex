 When Helpoemer dies, it sleeps in the opponent's  Graveyard  and sings poems of Hell. [note 2] 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> When this card has been  sent  to the  Graveyard  as a  result of battle , this card's effect is activated. As long as this card exists in the Graveyard, your opponent  discards  1 card  randomly  from his/her hand at the end of his/her  Battle Phase .
