 Booster 7 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Booster 7  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  that was released on March 1, 2000. This set was sold only in  Carddass  vending machines. It is the last set in the  Booster  series. 
 Like other cards printed early on in the game, the cards have no printed set numbers, and lack the distinctive  Eye of Anubis Hologram  in the lower right-hand corner of each card.
 Of the 40 cards in  Booster 7 , all but 5—" Ancient Sorcerer ", " Beautiful Beast Trainer ", " Grappler ", " Job-Change Mirror " and " Night Lizard "—have since been printed in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game .
