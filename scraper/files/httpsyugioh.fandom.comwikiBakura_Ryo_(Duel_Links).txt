 Bakura Ryo  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . This is a video game depiction of  Bakura Ryo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions  film. He appears at the end of " Téa Gardner 's  Téa Gardner's Reminiscence of the Future " event where he offers to lend his deck to " Joey Wheeler " with the latter refusing to "even touch that creepy deck of yours", suggesting that he still runs an "Occult" deck. He became unlockable on March 27th, 2022, during the event "The Shadow Memories: Bakura".

 Description 
 Bakura was once possessed by the evil of the Millennium Ring, but he broke free when the Pharaoh's soul left this world and the Millennium Items were buried under the earth. Though excited to find out what the future would hold for him and his friends, Bakura would learn that evil was never far away....

 White Magician's Defensive Spell
 
 Can be used if you control a Spellcaster-Type monster. Roll a die two times and apply the appropriate effect based on the result.
 2: Decrease DEF of all monsters your opponent controls by 1000 
 3-7: Decrease DEF of all monsters your opponent controls by 700 
 8-11: Decrease DEF of all monsters your opponent controls by 500 
 12: Decrease DEF of all monsters your opponent controls by 100 
 This Skill can only be used once per turn and twice per Duel.
 
 
 Level 1
 
 
 Master of Rites
 
 Can be used each time your Life Points decrease by 1000. Return 1 card from your hand to your Deck and choose 1 of the following:
 Add 1 random Ritual Monster from your Deck to your hand. 
 Add 1 random Ritual Spell Card from your Deck to your hand. 
 This Skill can only be used twice per Duel.
 
 
 Level 13
 
 
 Building a Diorama
 
 Can be used during a Duel where you have Ritual Summoned a Ritual Monster whose original Level is 8 or higher. Add 1 Field Spell Card from your Deck to your hand. Then, return 1 card from your hand to the bottom of your Deck. This Skill can only be used if you begin the Duel with a Deck that contains at least 4 Ritual Monsters and 4 Ritual Spell Cards. This Skill can only be used once per Duel.
 
 Level 20
 
 
 Double Normal Summon
 
 Can be used after your Life Points decrease by 1500. You can Summon 1 Normal Monster in addition to your Normal Summon/Set. This Skill can only be used once per turn.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Master of Advanced Rites
 
 Each of the following effects can be used once per Duel. If you use this Skill, you cannot Normal or Special Summon any monsters except Ritual Monsters until the end of your next turn.
 Return 1 Normal Monster in your hand to the Deck. Then, add 1 " Advanced Ritual Art " from your Deck to your hand. 
 Select 1 Normal Monster and 1 Ritual Spell Card in your Graveyard and return them to your Deck.

 Monster Encounter
 
 Send 1 Fiend-Type or Zombie-Type non-Effect Monster from your hand to the Graveyard. Roll a die tow times and play 1 Fiend-Type or Zombie-Type non-Effect Monster with the Level/Rank below from your Graveyard based on the result.
 2: Level/Rank 8 or lower 
 3-11: Level/Rank 4 or lower 
 12: Level/Rank 2 or lower 
 This Skill can only be used if you begin the Duel with a Deck (excluding Extra Deck) that includes at least 1 monster and contains no monsters other than Fiend-Type or Zombie-Type monsters. This Skill can only be used once per Duel.

 Occult Maniac

 Dialogue 
 Character-specific 
 Yami Yugi 
 When starting a Duel against  Yami Yugi , he says "You're... Yugi!"
 When he wins the Duel, he says "See you later, Yugi!" 
 When he loses the Duel, he says "No matter when we Duel, you prove you're the strongest." 
 Yami Bakura 
 When starting a Duel against  Yami Bakura , he says "My friends and i won't let you take control of me again!"
 When he wins the Duel, he says "I won't let you possess me anymore!" 
 When he loses the Duel, he says "No... I can't let you control me again..." 
 Maximillion Pegasus 
 When starting a Duel against  Maximillion Pegasus , he announces "It's Maximillion Pegasus! The creator of this game!"
 When he loses the Duel, he says "It's not fair! You created the best cards for yourself!" 
 Yugi Muto (DM), Yugi Muto (DSOD) 
 When starting a Duel against  Yugi Muto (DM)  or  Yugi Muto(DSOD) , he announces "What!? I have to face Yugi? Please be gentle."
 When he loses the Duel, he says "Yugi, you truly are the best Duelist!" 
 Seto Kaiba (DM),  Seto Kaiba (DSOD) 
 When he loses a Duel against  Seto Kaiba (DM)  or  Seto Kaiba (DSOD) , he says "How can anyone defeat a deck that has three Blue-Eyes!?" 
 Aigami 
 When starting a Duel against  Aigami , he announces "Aigami..."
 When he wins the Duel, he says "The Ring used to control me, but it no longer does." followed by "Still, I am so sorry. I know it's not easy to lose someone." 
 When he loses the Duel, he says "Aigami... Please forgive me..." 
 Sera 
 When starting a Duel against  Sera , he announces "You're..."
 When he loses the Duel, he says "I cannot take back what I've done. And for that, I'm sorry."
