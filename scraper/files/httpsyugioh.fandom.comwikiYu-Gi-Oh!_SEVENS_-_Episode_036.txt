 " Open♡ Ranze Eyes " is the thirty-sixth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on February 13, 2021.
 Yuga  and co. have found the underground Machine Cavalry Arena.  Galient  then stands in their way once again. He challenges them to a Duel, but he's also in bad shape… Seeing this,  Ranze Nanahoshi  simply can't stand by and do nothing. In an unexpected turn of events, Ranze starts to have fantasies about Galient, and decides to Duel in his stead!
 
 Featured Duel: Ranze Nanahoshi vs. Rinnosuke Nanahoshi 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
