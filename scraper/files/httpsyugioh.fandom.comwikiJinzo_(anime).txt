 Notes 
 
 ↑   Despite having the Japanese lore of its  OCG  counterpart , prior to its errata, "Jinzo" did not function that way in the anime. Instead, it had the following effective effect: 
"When this card is  Summoned ,  destroy  all  Trap Cards  on the  field , including  face-down  Trap Cards. Any Trap Cards  Set  or  activated  are  negated  and destroyed."
