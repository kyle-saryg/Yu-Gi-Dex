 In  episode 57 ,  Shark  uses this card during his Duel against  Quattro . When Quattro activates the effect of " Magma Field " to destroy " Hammer Shark " and " Tripod Fish ", Shark activates this card to prevent his  WATER  monsters from being destroyed by Spells and Traps when they are summoned. In  the next episode , this card remained on the field until the Duel was over.
