 Piercing
 
 
 
 " Piercing Battle Damage "  Tip Card 
 
 
 
 
 Japanese (base text)
 
 
 貫通ダメージ
 
 
 
 English
 
 
 piercing battle damage
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Other names 
 
 
 
 French : dommages de combat perçants 
 German : durchschlagender Kampfschaden 
 Italian : danno da combattimento perforante 
 Portuguese : dano de batalha perfurante 
 Spanish : daño de batalla de penetración 
 
 
 Piercing battle damage  is  battle damage  dealt by a  card effect  to the controller of a  Defense Position  monster by an  attacking  monster. If the defending monster's  DEF  is lower than the attacking monster's  ATK , the difference is inflicted as battle damage. It is occasionally referred to unofficially as "trample" damage, due to that term having a similar meaning in  Magic: The Gathering .
 Interestingly, because some  Equip Spell Cards  that allow monsters to inflict piercing damage deal damage to the opponent of the  controller  of the Equip Spell Card rather than the opponent of the controller of the monster, if a player  equips  a card like " Fairy Meteor Crush " to an opponent's monster and the opponent attacks the player's Defense Position monster, the opponent will take battle damage instead of the player.

 Example 
 Player A's " Dark Driceratops " (ATK 2400) attacks Player B's Defense Position " Mystical Elf " (DEF 2000). Regardless of whether "Mystical Elf" is  destroyed  or not, Player B takes the difference (400 points in this case) as  damage .
 
 Text 
 Effects that give monsters the ability to inflict piercing battle damage have the following text:
 
 If (???*) attacks a Defense Position monster, inflict piercing battle damage to your opponent. 
 In Japanese, it is written as:
 
 「 守 備 表 示 モンスターを 攻 撃 した 場 合 、その 守 備 力 を 攻 撃 力 が 超 えた 分 だけ 戦 闘 ダメージを 与 える。」( Shubi Hyōji Monsutā o kōgeki shita baai, sono Shubiryoku ga koeta-bun dake Sentō Damēji wo Ataeru )
