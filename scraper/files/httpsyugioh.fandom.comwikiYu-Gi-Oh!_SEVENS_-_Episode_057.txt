 " The Fourth Assailant Cometh " is the fifty-seventh episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on July 18, 2021.
 Gavin  was asked to help out with a kendo tournament, so he went to the kendo hall with  Yuga  and the others. However, there was no one there except  Yuran Goha , the third eldest brother of the Goha Siblings. In this silent, empty hall, a Kendo  Rush Duel  between Yuran and Gavin will now begin!
 
 Featured Duel: Gavin Sogetsu VS. Yuran Goha 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
