 Yami Marik , also known as Dark Marik ( 闇 マリク,  Yami Mariku ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yami Marik , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
