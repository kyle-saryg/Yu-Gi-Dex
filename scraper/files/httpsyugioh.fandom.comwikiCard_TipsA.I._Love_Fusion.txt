 This card can be  searched  by " Doshin @Ignister ", " Pikari @Ignister ", " Keeper of Dragon Magic ", " Predaplant Darlingtonia Cobra ", " Predaplant Chimerafflesia ", " Synchro Fusionist ", " Evil Mind ", " Supreme King Gate Zero ", " Cyberdark Inferno ", " Sabatiel - The Philosopher's Stone " and " Endymion, the Mighty Master of Magic ". 
 Of the eligible monsters that can be Fusion Summoned by this card using an opponent's  Link Monster  as material, " Cyberse Clock Dragon " and " Earth Golem @Ignister " are the only ones that can use an " @Ignister " monster(s) as material, "Cyberse Clock Dragon" specifically requiring the "@Ignister" monster to also be a Link Monster.
