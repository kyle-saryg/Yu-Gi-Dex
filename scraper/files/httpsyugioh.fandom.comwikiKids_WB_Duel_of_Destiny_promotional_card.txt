 Kids WB Duel of Destiny promotional card 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 This article is a stub. You can help the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  Wikia by  expanding it . 
 This promo card was given as a prize by Kids WB Television for the  Return to Battle City "Duel of Destiny" Contest  on May 15, 2004. The tournament was organized to celebrate the release of the  Battle City  arc on Kids WB. 8 promos were given to the first 3 classified at the "Duel of Destiny" tournament. Moreover the winners received 4 passes for the World Championship 2004. The cards were not distributed at the Kids WB event itself, but were delivered to their owners only at the World Championship. 100 copies were distributed together with 100 " Exclusive Packs " to the participants of a sweepstake publicized on Kids WB.
