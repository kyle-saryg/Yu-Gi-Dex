 TCG Rulings 
 You can still activate this card even if you control no face-up  Level  3 or lower  WATER  monsters at the time. [1] 
 This effect is applied until you choose to end it during the  End Phase . [1] 
 This effect applies to all  Level  3 or lower  WATER  monsters you control, even ones that you  Summon  after this card's resolution. [1] 
 Mentions in Other Rulings 
 Armed Sea Hunter :  You cannot choose to destroy monsters that cannot be destroyed due to the effect of “ Aegis of the Ocean Dragon Lord ” instead of destroying “ Armed Sea Hunter .” 
 OCG Rulings 
 You choose the time during the End Phase when the effect of " Aegis of the Ocean Dragon Lord " disappears.  After it disappears, players can destroy Level 3 or lower  WATER  monsters during the same turn. [2] 
 You can activate " Aegis of the Ocean Dragon Lord " even if you do not control any Level 3 or lower  WATER  monsters.  If you Summon such a monster after " Aegis of the Ocean Dragon Lord " resolves, then that monster cannot be destroyed. [3] 
 If a Level 3 or lower  WATER  monster is Summoned after " Aegis of the Ocean Dragon Lord " resolves, then that monster also cannot be destroyed. [4] 
 While the effect of " Aegis of the Ocean Dragon Lord " is active, Level 3 or lower  WATER  monsters cannot be destroyed.  Thus, when " Armed Sea Hunter " is destroyed, you cannot choose to apply its effect which destroys a Level 3 or lower  WATER  monster instead. [5]
