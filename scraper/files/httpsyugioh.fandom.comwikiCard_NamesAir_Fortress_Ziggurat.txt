 Names 
 
 The official  Greek  name given (" Εναέριο Φρούριο Ziggurat ") is an exact translation of the English name of this card.
 A possible translation of the Japanese name (" Sky Devil Fort Ziggurat ") could be " Εναέριο/Ιπτάμενο Διαβολικό Φρούριο Ziggurat " or " Εναέριος/Ιπτάμενος Διάβολος Φρούριο Ziggurat ".
 A transliteration of the word " Ziggurat " in Greek could be " Ζιγκουράτ ".
