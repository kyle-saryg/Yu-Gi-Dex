 Duelist Pack: Legend Duelist 2  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . [1] [2]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Bandit Keith ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Weevil Underwood ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Vellian Crowler  and  Jesse Anderson . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Ancient Gear ", " Armed Dragon ", " Crystal ", " Crystal Beast ", " Eyes Restrict ", " Fusion ", " LV ", " Ojama ", " Ultimate Crystal God " and " Worm " archetypes; as well as members of the  A-to-Z  and  Gun Dragon  series. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Armed Dragon " and " Eyes Restrict " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 50+1 cards:
