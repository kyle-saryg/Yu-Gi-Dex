 [  Machine  /  Fusion  /  Effect  ] 
 " Truckroid " + " Expressroid " + " Drillroid " + " Stealthroid " 
 During  battle  between this  attacking  card and a  Defense Position   monster  whose  DEF  is lower than the  ATK  of this card, inflict the difference as  battle damage  to your opponent.  Once per turn , during your  Main Phase , you can  select  1  face-up  monster on the  field , except a  Machine - Type  monster, and  equip  it to this card. While equipped by this  effect , this card can attack twice during each  Battle Phase , and it gains the effects of that equipped monster. Once per Battle Phase, if this card attacks while equipped by this effect, its  original ATK  is halved until the end of the Battle Phase.
