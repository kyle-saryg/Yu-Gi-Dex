 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 In the French version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Utilisez cette carte pour désactiver l'effet de "Monster Reborn" joué par votre adversaire." 
 In the German version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Deaktiviere die Wirkung von "Wiedergeburt", wenn dein Gegner sie spielt." 
 In the Italian version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Disattiva gli effetti della carta "Mostro Resuscitato" quando il tuo avversario la gioca." 
 In the Spanish version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Desactiva el efecto de "Monstruo Renacido" cuando tu adversario lo juega."
