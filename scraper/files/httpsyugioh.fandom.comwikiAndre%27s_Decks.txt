 Andre's plays a  Beast  Deck , which consists of many flexible strategies, one of which is referred to as  Fast Power . He includes an array of  monsters  with  effects  that  activate  upon being  removed from play . His Deck is unique in the sense that he can change strategies very quickly to adapt to his opponent's moves in order to counter them.
