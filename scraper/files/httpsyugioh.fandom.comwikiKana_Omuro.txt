 Kana Omuro  is a Japanese voice actress.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , she voices  Max [1] , who had previously been voiced by  Mika Ishibashi . [2] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , she voices a classmate. [3] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS , she voices a younger version of  Yusaku Fujiki . [ citation needed ]
