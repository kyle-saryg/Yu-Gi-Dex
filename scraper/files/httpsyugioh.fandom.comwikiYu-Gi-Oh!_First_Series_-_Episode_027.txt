 Summary 
 Even when seemingly defeated,  Dark Master Zorc  rises again and threatens to overwhelm the gang. It is up to  Bakura  to defeat  his evil counterpart  with help from  Dark Yugi .
 Eventually, Bakura is able to destroy Dark Bakura's possessed dice, with Yugi and his friends saving him from death, allowing the gang to win the  Shadow Game . Afterwards, their souls are freed from the  Monster World  figurines.
