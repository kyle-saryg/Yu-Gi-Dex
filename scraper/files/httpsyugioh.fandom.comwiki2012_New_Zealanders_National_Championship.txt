 The  2012 New Zealanders National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of New Zealand. It took place in Ellerslie Events Centre in Auckland on June 3, 2012  [1]  and was won by Chris Dalton [2]

 Main event 
 The main event consisted of eight rounds of Swiss, after which eight of the highest scoring players advanced to the top cut. [3] 
 In the quarter finals, Timmy Chiew defeated Newsun Zeng, Sezan Hossain defeated Aaron Dennis, Hyunwoo Jun defeated Carne de Vreede and Chris Dalton defeated Gavin Wu. [5] 
 In the semi finals, Timmy Chiew defeated Sezan Hossain and Chris Dalton defeated Hyunwoo Jun. [5] 
 In the final, Chris Dalton defeated Timmy Chiew. [5]
