 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation ", " Chaos Zone ", " Dragonic Guard ", " Draconnection ", " Minerva, Lightsworn Maiden ",  " Tempest, Dragon Ruler of Storms ", " Hieratic Dragon King of Atum " and " Empowerment ". 
 Pendulum Summon  this card and " Aether, the Evil Empowering Dragon " at the same time. Resolve "Evil" first before using this card's effect to bounce "Evil". That way, you can Tribute this card to Tribute Summon "Evil", banishing two opposing monsters in one turn.
