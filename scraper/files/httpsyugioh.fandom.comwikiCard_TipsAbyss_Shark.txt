 Names 
 
 This card can be searched by " Mermaid Shark ", " Ice Barrier ", " Buzzsaw Shark ", " Crystal Girl ", " Whitefish Salvage ", " Flying Red Carp ", " Eria the Water Charmer, Gentle ", " Mermail Abyssalacia ", " Mother Grizzly ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Chaos-End Master ", " Umbramirage the Elemental Lord ", " Sangan " and " Witch of the Black Forest ". 
 This card's self-summon from the hand can search " Crystal Shark ", which also Special Summons itself from the hand, enabling an  Xyz Summon  of any  WATER   Xyz Monster  between Ranks 3 and 5 inclusive. If you Normal Summoned a Level 3 or 4 WATER monster in order to Special Summon this card, you enable the Xyz Summon of " Mermail Abysstrite ", " Shark Caesar ", " Tri-Edge Levia ", " Number 32: Shark Drake " or " Gagagigo the Risen ".
 If you summon this card and subsequently "Number 32: Shark Drake", the latter must have at least 2350 more ATK than an opposing Attack Position monster with 1000 or more ATK, in order to OTK successfully.
