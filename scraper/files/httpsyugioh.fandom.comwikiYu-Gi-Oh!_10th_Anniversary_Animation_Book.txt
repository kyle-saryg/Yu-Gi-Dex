 Yu-Gi-Oh! 10th Anniversary Animation Book  (遊☆戯☆王 テンス　アニバーサリー　アニメーション　ブック  Yūgō! Tensu Anivāsarī Animēshon Bukku ) is a book released to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the NAS adaption of the anime.
 The book features scenes from  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , a quick review of the three  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series, character profiles, Duels and interviews with the staff of the movie. [2] 
 A fold-out double-sided poster is included with the book. [2]
