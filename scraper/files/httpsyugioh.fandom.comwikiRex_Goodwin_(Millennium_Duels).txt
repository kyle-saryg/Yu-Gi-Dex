 Rex Goodwin  (レクス・ゴドウィン, Rekusu Godowin), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Rex Goodwin , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
