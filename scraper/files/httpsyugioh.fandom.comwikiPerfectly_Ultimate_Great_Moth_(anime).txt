 Notes 
 
 ↑   Although the anime card was printed with the effect of  its  OCG / TCG  counterpart , "Perfectly Ultimate Great Moth" did not function that way in the anime. It could be Special Summoned by Tributing a " Larvae Moth " that had been equipped with " Cocoon of Evolution " for 5 of your turns.
