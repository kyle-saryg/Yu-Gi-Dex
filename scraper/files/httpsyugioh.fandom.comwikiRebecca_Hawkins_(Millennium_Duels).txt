 Rebecca Hawkins , known as  Rebecca Hopkins  (レベッカ・ホプキンス,  Rebekka Hopukinsu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Rebecca Hawkins , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
