 Ishiki Erishi , known as  Ishiki Eriito  ( 江里糸   石輝 ,  Eriito Ishiki ) in the Japanese version, is a  Ra Yellow  student. He is seen in the anime, with  America Uchimura , as a spectator to  Jaden Yuki  and  Dimitri's  Duel.
He is also seen being beaten by  Chazz Princeton  along with  America Uchimura  and another Ra Yellow member when Chazz is in the Society of Light.
In the 3rd season, he was one of many students who were transported in second alternate dimension and became a  Duel Ghoul .
 
       Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller .     
 Ishiki appears in the game  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller , as a duelable opponent.
