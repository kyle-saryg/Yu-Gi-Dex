 Battles of Legend: Light's Revenge  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Rex Raptor ,  Rebecca Hawkins ,  Johnny Steps ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Aster Phoenix  ( ARC-V anime  and  GX Manga ),  Tyranno Hassleberry ,  Z-one ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Kite Tenjo ,  Fortuno ,  Mizar ,  Yuya Sakaki ,  Gong Strong ,  Sylvio Sawatari ,  Yuto ,  Declan Akaba ,  Moon Shadow ,  Yugo ,  Battle Beast ,  Sanders  and  Ren . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Abyss Actor ", " Abyss Script ", " Abyss- ", " Archfiend ", " Artifact ", " Bujin ", " Change ", " D/D ", " D/D/D ", " Destiny HERO ", " Dragunity ", " Elemental HERO ", " Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes ", " Gladiator Beast ", " HERO ", " Ice Barrier ", " Infernoid ", " Lightsworn ", " lswarm ". " Magician ", " Mermail ", " Ninja ", " Number ", " Number 10X ", " Odd-Eyes ", " PSY-Frame ", " Performapal ", " Phantom Knights ", " Photon ", " Prophecy ", " Raidraptor ", " Saber ", " Spellbook ", " Superheavy Samurai ", " Synchro Dragon ", " Synchron ", " Tachyon Dragon ", " The Phantom Knights ", " Venom ", " Vision HERO ", " Void " and " X-Saber " archetypes, as well as the  Evolution Pill ,  Gaia Knight ,  Synchro ,  with Eyes of Blue  and  Zombie counterpart  series. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Timelord " archetype to the TCG.
