 The Sanctuary in the Sky  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is named after the card " The Sanctuary in the Sky ".
 In other languages, this set is amalgamated with  Pharaoh's Inheritance  to create  Ancient Sanctuary .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards and each box contains 30 packs.
 There are 57 cards in total, or 61 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 1  Secret Rare 
 3  Ultra Parallel Rare 
 2  Ultimate Rare 
 3  Ultra Rare 
 4  Super Rare 
 6  Rare 
 42  Common
