 Aerial Assault   is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on November 12, 2019.
 
 Description 
 Blackwings have returned, their forces now more powerful than ever! 
 " Blackwing - Zephyros the Elite " 
 If this monster is in your Graveyard, you can Special Summon it by returning 1 face-up card you control to your hand!
Use it with Skills that let you begin the Duel with a Field Spell Card on the field!
 
 " Brionac, Dragon of the Ice Barrier " 
 A Synchro Monster that lets you discard any number of cards from your hand and return the same number of cards your opponent controls to their hand!
When using its effect, discard cards with Graveyard effects such as " Blackwing - Zephyros the Elite " and " Jet Synchron "!
 
 " Dark End Dragon " 
 A Synchro Monster that can decrease its ATK and DEF by 500 to send one of your opponent's monsters to the Graveyard!
Use it to get rid of monsters that cannot be destroyed by card effects or monsters that have effects that activate when destroyed!
 
 " Swallow's Nest " 
 A Quick-Play Spell Card that lets you Tribute a Winged Beast-Type monster to Special Summon a Winged Beast-Type monster with the same level from your Deck!
Activate it during the Battle Phase and Tribute a monster you already attacked with to Special Summon a new monster and attack again!
 
 Total of 90 types: 9  Ultra Rares , 11  Super Rares , 28  Rares , and 42  Normals 
 
 Each box of  Aerial Assault  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Inzektor Ladybug ", " Inzektor Giga-Cricket ", " Raptor Wing Strike ", " Black-Winged Strafe ", and " Variable Form ".
