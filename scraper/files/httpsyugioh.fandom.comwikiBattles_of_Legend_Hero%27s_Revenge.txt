 Battles of Legend: Hero's Revenge  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Aster Phoenix ,  Astral ,  Carly Carmine ,  Gansley ,  George Gore , Jaden Yuki ( anime  and  manga ),  Leon von Schroeder ,  Shinji Weber ,  Skeleton Knight ,  Theodore Hamilton ,  Varis ,  Vizor ,  Yusei Fudo  and  Yuya Sakaki . 
 Includes new members and/or support for the " Black Luster Soldier ", " Gimmick Puppet ", " Harpie ", " HERO ", " Magical Musket ", " Number ", " PSY-Frame ", " Salamangreat ", " Speedroid ", " T.G. ", " Vendread " and " Vision HERO " archetypes, as well as the  Fairy Tale  and  Railway  series. 
 Introduces the " Battlewasp " and " Fortune Fairy " archetypes, and the first support for the " Vision HERO " archetype to the TCG.
