 Breakdown 
 Each Tin contains:
 
 1  Photon Shockwave  Booster Pack 
 2  Order of Chaos  Booster Pack 
 2  Galactic Overlord  Booster Pack 
 1  Super Rare  " Rescue Rabbit " 
 In Ninja Grandmaster Hanzo Tin:
 
 1  Secret Rare  " Ninja Grandmaster Hanzo " 
 3  Super Rare  monsters: " Maxx "C" ", " Tour Guide From the Underworld " and " Number 16: Shock Master " 
 In Hieratic Sun Dragon Overlord of Heliopolis Tin:
 
 1  Secret Rare  " Hieratic Sun Dragon Overlord of Heliopolis " 
 3  Super Rare  monsters: " Malefic Truth Dragon ", " X-Saber Souza " and " Leviair the Sea Dragon "
