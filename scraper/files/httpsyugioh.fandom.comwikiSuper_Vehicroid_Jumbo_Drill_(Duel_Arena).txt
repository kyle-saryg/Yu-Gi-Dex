 Super Vehicroid Jumbo Drill  is a character version of the card " Super Vehicroid Jumbo Drill ". 
 It appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P038. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 3-5 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, it had a default of 5 Rental Points, and its partner ID was P019.

 Description 
 A super jumbo drill  Vehicroid  fused of three " Roids " -  Steam ,  Drill  and  Submarine .
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Jumbo! I'm a Vehicroid  Fusion Monster . I fuse with my own  special support card ! Drills ROCK!
 
 Decks 
 A  Fusion   Deck  centered on  Machine - Type   monsters  with the "Roid"  archetype . " Chain Material " can be used to bring together  Fusion Materials  regardless of time. Furthermore, combine it with "Vehicroid Connection Zone" and you'll avoid the downside entirely!

 Trivia 
 " No Entry!! " is most likely included in the 2nd version of this character's partner Deck as a reference to  Syrus Truesdale , a Duelist who used a " roid " Deck and also used that card. 
 " Dark Jeroid ", " Elemental HERO Necroid Shaman " and " Magical Android " are included in this character's partner Side Decks as a joke, alluding to the fact those monsters are technically "roid" monsters.
