 Yuga Goha  also known by his earlier alias  Swirly  is the main antagonist of  Goha Employee  arc in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS .

 Design 
 Appearance 
 Yuga Goha's appearance strongly resembles that of  Yuga Ohdo  but with white hair and red accents.
 As Swirly, he appears as a short stature boy wearing an aqua lizard costume with a red polka-dot bowtie.
 
 Personality 
 Yuga Goha's nature has been shown to be sadistic and cruel. He seeks to use dueling to bring devastation and despair to all duelists.
