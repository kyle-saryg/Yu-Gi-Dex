 " Celestial ", known as " Heavenly Halo " ( 天 輪   Tenrin ) in the Japanese version, is an  archetype  of cards used by  Rex Goodwin  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga.
 
 Design 
 All members of the archetype have a large halo in their backs, and their bodies have a robotic-looking angelic appearace. Their names are composed of "Celestial (occupation)".
 
 Playing style 
 The members of this archetype focus on  swarm  and  lockdown  tactics. Cards like " Celestial Undertaker " and " Celestial Bowman " swarm low  Level   monsters  in order to  Synchro Summon  low-Level  Synchro Monsters , ultimately swarming up the field with " Celestial Double Star Shaman " for Synchro Summons up to Level 10. Goodwin uses this archetype with his " Celestial Conquering Dragon - Drago Ascension ", using the latter's last effect and " Celestial Bell Tower " in order to create a loop, protecting "Ascension" from destruction and Summoning it back while drawing more cards.
