 All " Stormrider "  monsters  on the  field  gain 400  ATK , and 300 ATK for each "Stormrider"  Field Spell  in your  GY . While you  control  exactly 1 "Stormrider"  Link Monster ,  negate  the  effects  of Link Monsters your opponent controls with an equal or lower  Link Rating . While you control a "Stormrider" Link Monster in the  Extra Monster Zone , your opponent's monsters must  attack  it, if able.
