 Croquet , known as  Mr. Crocketts  in Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . This is the original incarnation of  Croquet . He is one of the few human characters in the original manga to not retain his Japanese name in the English printing.
 Croquet is the right-hand man of  Maximillion J. Pegasus . He helped Pegasus in his plan to acquire  Seto Kaiba's  technology. In  Yu-Gi-Oh! R , after Pegasus' death, he served  Yako Tenma , and was a spectator of the possessed  Gekko  and  Dark Yugi's  Duel. He stopped the Duel when Yugi was about to make his winning move, and declared Yugi as the winner.
