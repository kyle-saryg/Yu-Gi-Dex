 Varis , known as  Revolver  (リボルバー  Riborubā ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Varis , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
