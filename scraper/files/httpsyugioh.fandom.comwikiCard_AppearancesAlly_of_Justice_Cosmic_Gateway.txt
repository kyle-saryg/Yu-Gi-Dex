 Anime 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 In  episode 106 , one of the  Diablo  Duelbots uses three copies of this card during the Battle Royale that was around  Neo Domino City . Two copies of this card were added from the Duelbot's Deck to the hand via the effect of " Ally of Justice Tractor ". The Duelbot then Special Summons three copies of this card via their own effects (as Yusei controls a  LIGHT  monster). One copy then attacks " Driven Daredevil ". Both monsters are then destroyed in a  Double KO . The other two copies then attack Elsworth directly, defeating him. All three copies left the field in between this episode and  the next episode  through unknown means. 
 Video Games 
 Globe  uses this in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .
