 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you have " Fallen of Albaz " on your  field  or it is in your  GY : You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . During the  Main Phase  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Fusion Summon  1  Level  8 or higher  Fusion Monster  from your  Extra Deck , using  monsters  from your hand or  field  as  material . During the  End Phase , if a Fusion Monster(s) was  sent  to your GY this  turn : You can  add  this card from your GY to your hand. You can only use each  effect  of "Blazing Cartesia, the Virtuous"  once per turn .
