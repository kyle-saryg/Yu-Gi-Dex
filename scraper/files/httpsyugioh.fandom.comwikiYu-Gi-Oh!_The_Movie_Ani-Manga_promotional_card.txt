 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie Ani-Manga 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie Ani-Manga  promotional card  is a promotional card, included with  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie Ani-Manga . The English version included  YMA-EN001  " Slifer the Sky Dragon ", the French version included  YMM-FR001  " Theinen the Great Sphinx " and the Italian version included  MOV-IT001  " Blue-Eyes Shining Dragon ".
