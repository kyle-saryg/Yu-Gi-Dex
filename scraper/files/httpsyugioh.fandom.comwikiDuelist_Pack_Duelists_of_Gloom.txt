 Duelist Pack: Duelists of Gloom  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes new cards that can enhance the Deck of 3 Duelists that use DARK monsters. 
 Includes cards used by  Yami Marik ,  Primo ,  Jakob ,  Lester ,  Aporia ,  Espa Roba  and  Makoto Inotsume . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Meklord ", " Meklord Army ", " Meklord Emperor " and " Jinzo " archetypes, as well as " Egyptian God " series.
 Introduces first support for " Meklord Astro " archetype in  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 46 cards:
 
 1  Holographic Rare  (Japanese) 
 1  Extra Secret Rare  (Korean) 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 7  Super Rares 
 9  Rares 
 24  Commons 
 The Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rare .
