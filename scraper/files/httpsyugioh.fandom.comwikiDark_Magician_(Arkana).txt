This article is about the alternate artwork card used by  Arkana . For the primary version, see " Dark Magician ". For the character, see  Dark Magician (character) . For the  archetype , see  "Dark Magician" (archetype) .    
 The  Croatian  and  Serbian                       names given are not official.  ( card names ) 
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Magician "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 魔 術 師 」 .   As its English name contains "Magician", it  may  be treated a member of the "Magician" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
