 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 The feather in this card's artwork appears in the headdress of " Hand of Nephthys ". 
 A phoenix is said to rise from the ashes of its grave, good as new. This card lets you choose a card in your Graveyard for you to draw next turn.
