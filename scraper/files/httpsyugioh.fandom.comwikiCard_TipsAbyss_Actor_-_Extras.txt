 If you go second, you can play this card in the  Pendulum Zone  first before summoning it in order to use its Scale-swapping effect without using up your Normal Summon.
 Place the Scale 0 " Abyss Actor - Mellow Madonna " so that you can  Pendulum Summon  this card from your Extra Deck.
