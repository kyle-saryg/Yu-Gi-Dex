This article is about the card. For the  archetype , see  Skyscraper (archetype) .      
 The  Arabic, Croatian, Greek  and  Hungarian                     names given are not official.  ( card names ) 
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Scrap "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「スクラップ」 .   As its English name contains "Scrap", it  may  be treated a member of the "Scrap" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If an " Elemental HERO "  monster   attacks  a monster that has a higher  ATK , the attacking monster gains 1000 ATK during  damage calculation  only. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Si un monstre "HÉROS Élémentaire" attaque un monstre qui a une ATK supérieure, le monstre attaquant gagne 1000 ATK durant le calcul des dommages uniquement.

 Falls ein „Elementar-HELD“-Monster ein Monster mit höheren ATK angreift, erhält das angreifende Monster nur während der Schadensberechnung 1000 ATK.
