 Structure Deck: Order of the Spellcasters  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the 45th Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following the  Structure Deck: Soulburner . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck R: Lord of Magician  in the  OCG .
