 " Astral " (アストラル,  Asutoraru ) is an  archetype  used by  Astral  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga .
 "Astral"  monsters  are "Astral" counterparts of  Yuma 's monsters. Astral uses them alongside cards like " Ritual Refinement " and " Parallel Material " in order to massively  swarm   Xyz Monsters  on his  field .
