 Normal 
 
 Activate  only during the  Battle Phase . Each player  selects  a  Defense Position  monster they  control . The  effect (s) of the selected monsters are  negated  until the  End Phase  of this  turn . Then switch the selected monsters to  Attack Position  and conduct  battle .
