 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 The Ocean Mage  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is located at the Ocean Shrine.  The Prince  must defeat him before proceeding to  High Mage Secmeton  to duel for the  Millennium Necklace .
