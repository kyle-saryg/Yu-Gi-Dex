 Vellian Crowler , known as  Cronos de Medici  (クロノス・デ・メディチ  Kuronosu de Medichi ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Vellian Crowler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Vellian can be found throughout Part 1 at the Classroom before class, and at the Chancellor's Office after class. If the player wants to partner with Vellian, they must beat Part 3 and then win a duel against Vellian while having at least 8000 more Life Points than he did at the time the duel ended.
 During the Tag Force tournament, for Part 2, Vellian partners with  Chumley Huffington . In Part 3, Vellian is brainwashed by  Titan  and teams up with him against the player and their partner.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 The player duels against Vellian in the second story event for every Tier 1 character.
 The player and  Chazz Princeton  Tag Duel against Vellian and  Vice-Chancellor Bonaparte  during the seventh event of Chazz's story.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 In Child Blair's third event, the player and Blair duel against Crowler and  Georg .
 The player and Hat Blair Tag Duel against Crowler and  Wade  during her story's fourth event.
 Vellian and  Alexis Rhodes  duel against the player and Obelisk Blue  Chazz Princeton  during the latter's third story event.
 In the first story event for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters, the player and their partner Tag Duel Vellian and  Tyranno Hassleberry . If the player is partnered with either Vellian or Tyranno, they duel against  Lily  and  Andrea  instead.
