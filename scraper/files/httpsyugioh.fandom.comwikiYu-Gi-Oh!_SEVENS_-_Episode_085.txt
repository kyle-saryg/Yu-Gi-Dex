 " The Return of the King " is the eighty-fifth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on February 6, 2022.
 Yuga  and  Roa 's intense  Duel  unfolded on the stage for  RoaRomin 's secret concert. After Yuga figured out his intention during the Duel, Roa proceeded to laugh at himself. However,  Mimi  and other RoaRomin fans then cheered him on, allowing Roa to regain his "Roa-ness". Roa then declared that he'll give his fans a lot of love today!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Roa Kirishima 
 Duel continues from the previous episode .
