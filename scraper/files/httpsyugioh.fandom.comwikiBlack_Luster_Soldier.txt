   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
This monster is summoned with the Ritual Magic Card, " Black Luster Ritual ". You must also offer monsters whose total Level Stars equal 8 or more as a Tribute from the field or your hand. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
 The strongest of all  warriors , it suitably wears black. It equals the  Blue-Eyes White Dragon  in ability. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
This monster can only be Ritual Summoned with the Ritual Spell Card, " Black Luster Ritual ". You must also offer monsters whose total Level Stars equal 8 or more as a Tribute from the field or your hand.
