 " Adamancipator Seeker ", " Adamancipator Analyzer ", and " Adamancipator Researcher " appear in this card's artwork. 
 This card's name is a reference to Laputa, the floating island from Studio Ghibli's 1986 animated film  Castle In The Sky .
