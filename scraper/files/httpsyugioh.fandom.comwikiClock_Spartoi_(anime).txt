 If this card is  Link Summoned : You can add 1 " Cynet Fusion " from your  Deck  to your  hand . If a  monster  is  Special Summoned  to a  zone  this card  points to : You can target 1 Level 4 or lower Cyberse monster in your  GY ; Special Summon it to your zone this card points to, but  negate  its  effects , also you cannot Special Summon monsters from the  Extra Deck  for the rest of this  turn , except  Fusion Monsters . You can only use each effect of "Clock Spartoi"  once per turn .
