 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM , outside of the cards,  Power-Ups  can also be used to help you overcome difficult situations and change the outcome of the game.
 The Power-Ups are first introduced in the tutorial in Chapter 3 of the game. 
 Power-Ups can be used by placing them on appropriate power-up slots before the player hits BAM (There are 6 power-up slots on the top right corner of 6 slots on the field and 2 slots correspond to you and your opponent's Life Points). A slot can be affected by multiple Power-Ups. There can be maximum used of 3 Power-Ups per turn. Slots that affected by Power-Ups will have the correspond Power-Ups icon over it.
 Each account can own an unlimited number of Power-Ups. The Power-Ups do not stack in your inventory.
