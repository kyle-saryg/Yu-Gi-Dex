 OCG Rulings 
 The  Flip effect  of " Alien Grey "  targets  1 monster the opponent controls, also the effect can be activated during the  Damage Step . [1] 
 The effect to draw a card when " Alien Grey " has been flipped face-up is a  Trigger Effect . It does not target. [1] 
 The effect to draw a card when " Alien Grey " has been flipped face-up is activated even if it had been flipped face-up earlier in the Duel (including  Flip Summoned  or being changed face-up by a card effect). It does not have to be flipped face-up as a result of the battle that destroyed it. [1] 
 The effect to reduce the  ATK  and  DEF  of a monster attacking an " Alien " monster is a  Continuous Effect . [1] 
 Previously Official Rulings 
   These TCG rulings were issued by  Upper Deck Entertainment  and have since been deemed unofficial by Konami. [2]  They were previously considered official and can still be correct, as long as they follow the official gameplay rules, or unless Konami has issued a ruling that says otherwise. References:  [3] [4] [5] [6] [7] [8] [9] [10] [11] 
 
 The effect to reduce the ATK of monsters with  A-Counters  is an effect of this card. If this card (or another with the same effect) is not on the field, then a monster with an A-Counter will not lose ATK while battling an "Alien". 
 If 2 " Alien Grey " (or other monsters with the A-Counter text) are on the field, then a monster with 1 A-Counter will lose 600 ATK while battling an " Alien ".
