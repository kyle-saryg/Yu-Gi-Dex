 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
" Dark Blade " + " Pitch-Dark Dragon " Each time this card inflicts  Battle Damage  to your opponent, you can  select  up to 3  Monster Cards  from your opponent's  Graveyard  and  remove them from play .
