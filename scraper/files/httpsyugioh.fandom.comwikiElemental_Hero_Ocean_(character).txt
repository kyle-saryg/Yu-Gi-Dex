 Elemental Hero Ocean  is a character version of the card, " Elemental Hero Ocean ". He appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .
 
 Deck 
 Elemental Hero Ocean uses a Deck titled "There and Back" and "Rhythm of the Sea".
