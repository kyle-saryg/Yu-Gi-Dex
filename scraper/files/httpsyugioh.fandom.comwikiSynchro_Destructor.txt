 Effect 
 Activate  only when a  Synchro Monster  you  control   destroys  an opponent's  monster   by battle . Inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to half the  original ATK  of the destroyed monster. If the destroyed monster is a Synchro Monster, this effect is  activated  twice.
