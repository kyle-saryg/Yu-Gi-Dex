 Yuamu Ohdo  is one of the main characters in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GO RUSH!! . She is the younger twin sister of  Yuhi Ohdo  and the CEO of UTS.
 
 Personality 
 Yuamu stays calm in all sorts of situations and eager to explain the rules of  Rush Duel .
