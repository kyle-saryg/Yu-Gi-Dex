 Stone Statue of the Aztecs , known as  Aztec Stone Statue  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card " Stone Statue of the Aztecs ".
 It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator  as a Single Duel opponent. In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia  it is partnered with  Stealth Bird . Their Tag Team name is " Protect & Burn ".
 
 Decks 
 In  World Championship 2006 , Stone Statue of the Aztecs uses a Deck titled "Fight or Flight", which focuses on deflecting massive damages from the opponent, when they go against  Attack Position  monsters.
 In  World Championship 2009 , Stone Statue of the Aztecs uses a Deck titled "Clang-a-Damage", which focuses on inflicting massive damages to the opponent, when they attack  Defense Position  monsters. Strangely enough, this Deck contains three copies of " Bark of Dark Ruler " without a single Fiend-Type monster in the Deck.
 In  World Championship 2010 , Stone Statue of the Aztecs uses a Deck titled "Reflection", which also focuses on inflicting massive damages to the opponent, when they attack Defense Position monsters.
