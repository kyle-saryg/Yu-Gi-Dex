 Andrew Rannells 
 
 
 Birth 
 August 23 1978 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 Occupation 
 Voice actor/Broadway Actor 
 
 
 Andrew Rannells  is an American actor who previously worked for  4Kids Entertainment . He has provided voices for a number of characters in the English language dub of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series. In 2006 he retired from voice acting to pursue a career on Broadway.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Rannells voiced  Mako Tsunami ,  Noah Kaiba , and  Leon von Schroeder  in the  second series anime 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Rannells provides the voice of  Zane Truesdale 's younger self in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . He also voiced  Wheeler  and  Belowski  in the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Rannells provides the voice of  Toby Tredwell  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
