 Dark Crisis  is a  Booster Pack , which combines the Japanese sets,  Power of the Guardian  and  Threat of the Dark Demon World . It is the ninth worldwide Booster Pack.
 It has been released in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ) in English and in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in  Asian-English  and Korean. The English  TCG  has two different prints, one with set numbers DCR-### and one with DCR-EN###, the second of which were available through  Gladiator's Assault: Special Edition  and  Master Collection Volume 2 .
 In 2010, an  Unlimited Edition  North American version of the set (DCR-###) was reprinted again and released in  Legendary Collection .

 Features 
 Dark Crisis  introduces the  archetypes  " Guardian " and " Archfiend " to the  TCG .
 Highly sought-after cards include " Vampire Lord ", " D.D. Warrior Lady ", " Butterfly Dagger - Elma ", " Terrorking Archfiend ", and " Skill Drain ".
 
 Breakdown 
 In the English and Asian-English versions of this set, each pack contains 9 cards and each box contains 24 packs. In the Korean version, there are 5 cards per pack.
