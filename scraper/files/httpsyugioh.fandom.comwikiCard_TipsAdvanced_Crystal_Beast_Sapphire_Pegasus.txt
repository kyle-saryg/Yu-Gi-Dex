 This card can be searched by " Crystal Bond ", " Noble Knight's Shield-Bearer ", " Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ", " Crystal Beast Rainbow Dragon ", " Crystal Beacon ", " Rainbow Bridge of Salvation ", " Awakening of the Crystal Ultimates ", " Rainbow Refraction ", " Crystal Tree ", " Crystal Conclave ", " Crystal Master ", " Crystal Miracle ", " Crystal Release ", " Crystal Pair ", " Wynn the Wind Channeler ", " Melffy Catty ", " Tri-Brigade Airborne Assault ", " Tri-Brigade Shuraig the Ominous Omen ", " Armored White Bear ", " Desmanian Devil ", " Ninjitsu Art of Transformation ", " Wynn the Wind Charmer, Verdant ", " Crystal Clear Wing Synchro Dragon ", " Divine Wind of Mist Valley ", " Kujakujaku ", " Summoner Monk ", " Primathmech Alembertian ", " Battle Royal Mode - Joining ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Resonator Engine ", " Soul Rope ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Witch of the Black Forest " and another copy of "Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus". 
 This card's effect to search another " Advanced Crystal Beast " to place in your  Spell & Trap Zone  activates even if " Advanced Dark " is not on the field, allowing you to trade one-for-one with that "Advanced Crystal Beast" that you do place. Note that the common  negative effect  of "Advanced Crystal Beast" monsters only applies to them while they are in a  Monster Zone .
