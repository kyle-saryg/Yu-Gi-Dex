 Reptilianne Vaskii , known as  Reptiless Vaskii  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card " Reptilianne Vaskii ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia  and in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , as a Single Duel opponent.
 In  Reverse of Arcadia , this opponent can be unlocked during story mode.
 In  Over the Nexus , this opponent can be unlocked during story mode by clearing  Chapter 3 .
