 Number 12: Crimson Shadow Armor Ninja  is a character version of the card " Number 12: Crimson Shadow Armor Ninja ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P103. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 5-5 under Quest mode.

 Description 
 One of the  monsters  with the " Number "  archetype . A crimson ninja that bears the number 12.
 
 Opponent Quote 
 I am no run-of-the-mill  ninja ! I am the  Armor Ninja ! And one of the chosen Numbers! These pretenders who call themselves "ninja" are nothing compared to me!
 
 Decks 
 Partner 
 An  Xyz  Ninja Deck composed of monsters with the "Ninja" archetype. Use " Senior Silver Ninja " and " Upstart Golden Ninja " to  Special Summon  "Ninja" cards and link that into an Xyz Summon. Also features some  Dinosaur - Type  monsters for " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation ". Rawr!
