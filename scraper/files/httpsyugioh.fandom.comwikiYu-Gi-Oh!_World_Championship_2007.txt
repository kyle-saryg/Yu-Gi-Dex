For the first time, the Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship Tournament series makes its way to the Nintendo DSâ„¢. The official software of  World Championship 2007  makes worldwide wireless dueling possible utilizing the Nintendo Wi-Fi connection. The game will include 1,600 of the latest cards and more cards will be available for download. Create your best Deck and prepare for the World Tournament in the Summer of 2007. World Championship 2007 uses the same card packs from  Spirit Caller  as well, as additional standard packs,  TYPE  and  ATTRIBUTE  collections.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 Features:
 Head to head worldwide dueling via the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection 
 Contains 1640 cards, up to  Strike of Neos . Includes  Egyptian Gods  and the  Wicked Gods 
 Voice chat available before you start a Wi-Fi Duel. 
 Create and edit the look of your character. 
 Each game includes three exclusive trading cards.
