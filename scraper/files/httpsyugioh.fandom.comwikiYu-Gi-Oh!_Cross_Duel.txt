 Yu-Gi-Oh! Cross Duel  is a game releases on September 5, 2022 for mobile platforms. It was announced on July 20th, 2021 in the first Yu-Gi-Oh! Digital NeXt presentation. 
 
 Gameplay 
 NOTE: The information below is from the closed beta of the game, details may change upon general release.  
The game features dueling 3 opponents at once. Monsters have their individual skill trees where they can awaken skills exclusive to Cross Duel. You can pick tag partners and opponents from all the 7 anime series. There has Raid Boss which you can co-op against boss. There has Solo Mode, which basically campaign mode, and you play against the character from various Yu-Gi-Oh series.  
 There is a Gacha system where you draw for rare cards.
