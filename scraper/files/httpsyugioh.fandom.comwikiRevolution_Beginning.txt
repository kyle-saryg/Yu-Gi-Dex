 Revolution Beginning  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on January 1, 2019.
 Revolution Beginning  is the first box to introduce reprinted cards from the previous boxes.
 
 Description 
 Witness the beginning of a new age... Join the revolution with " Junk Destroyer "! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Junk Destroyer ", When this monster is Synchro Summoned you can destroy as many cards on the field up to the number of non-Tuner monsters you used to Synchro Summon it. Use " Synchro Material " to use your opponent's monsters as Synchro Materials to destroy even more cards! 
" Doomcaliber Knight ", When a monster's effect activates, this monster Tributes itself to negate the effect. It boasts a high ATK as well, but be careful, because this monster will negates your own monsters' effect as well. 
" Miracle Fusion ", A powerful Spell Card that lets you Fusion Summon an " Elemental HERO " monster by banishing monsters from your field or Graveyard. First, use " Polymerization " to Fusion Summon with monsters in your hand. Then, you can Fusion Summon the same monster again by using "Miracle Fusion"! 
" Karakuri Shogun mdl 00 "Burei" ", When this monster is Synchro Summoned, you can Special Summon a " Karakuri " monster from your Deck. In addition, once per turn, you can change the battle position of a monster on the field. Use this effect to destroy monsters that have a high ATK but low DEF. 
 
A total of 90 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 42  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Revolution Beginning  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Non-Fusion Area ", " Reanimation Wave ", " Fabled Dyf ", " Karakuri Ninja mdl 7749 "Nanashick" ", and " Karakuri Anatomy ". 
 Reprinted cards from previous boxes include: " Fabled Urustos ", " Fabled Dyf ", " Koa'ki Meiru Boulder ", " Karakuri Anatomy ", and " Karakuri Ninja mdl 7749 "Nanashick" ".
