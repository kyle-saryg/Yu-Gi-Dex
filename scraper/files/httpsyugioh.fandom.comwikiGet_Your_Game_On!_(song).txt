Get Your Game On!  is the English opening theme for  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . It is written by Matthew Drdek, Jake Siegler, and Alex Walker. A shortened form is also played during the end credits.

 Appearances 
 In the English dub of  episode 35 ,  Jaden  sings the theme song as he walks through the woods with  Chazz .
 Also when  Atticus Rhodes  floats by on the boat playing guitar in  episode 60  he sings something similar to the theme song, yet the lyrics are changed from "Get your game on" to "rock on" and "Chillin out with the crew" to "Chillin' out on my boat with the guitar".
