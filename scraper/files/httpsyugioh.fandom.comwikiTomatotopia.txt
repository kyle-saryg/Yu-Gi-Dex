        Tomatotopia     
 Tomatotopia , known as  Tomato Paradise  in the Japanese version, is a tomato-themed amusement park envisioned by  Tombo Tillbitty [1] . Tombo joins the  World Duel Carnival  in the hopes of winning so that he can finance his dream. Upon meeting  Tori , he desires to have her as the Tomato Paradise "Image Girl".  Yuma Tsukumo  challenges him to a  Duel  over this, due to his hate of tomatoes.
