 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 77 ,  Ghost Gal  uses this card during her  Speed Duel  against  The Shepherd . She  Synchro Summons  this card by tuning " Altergeist Fifinellag " with " Altergeist Marionetter ". Ghost Gal then Tributes this card to activate " Altergeist Hexstia 's" effect to negate and destroy Shepherd's " Capture Drone "; this card then revives itself to one of "Hexstia's"  Link Points . This card then attacks and destroys Shepherd's " Drone Corporal ". On Shepherd's next turn, Ghost Gal Tributes this card again with "Hexstia's" effect, allowing this card to revive itself again. Shepherd then uses his "Drone Craft Force" Skill to weaken this card by 1000 ATK before attacking it with " Commandrone Double Sniper "; the attack is negated by Ghost Gal's " Altergeist Kunquery ".
