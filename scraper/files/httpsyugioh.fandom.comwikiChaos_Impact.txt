 Chaos Impact  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) [1]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the tenth set in the  OCG ' s  10th series , following  Rising Rampage  and followed by  Ignition Assault .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Skye Zaizen ,  George Gore ,  Varis  and  Theodore Hamilton . 
 Includes members/support for the “ Aroma ”, " Evoltile ", " Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon ", " Gladiator Beast ", " Salamangreat ", " Marincess ", " Evil Eye ", " Dinowrestler ", " Watt ", " World Legacy ", " Infinitrack " and " Tenyi "  archetypes , as well as the  Attribute Spirit ,  D.D. ,  Firewall ,  Starliege , and  Test   series . 
 Introduces the " Unchained " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box. The set contains 80 cards or 119 in the  Master Set :
 
 1  Holographic Rare 
 6  Ultra Rares  (also available as  Ultimate Rare ,  Secret Rare  and  20th Secret Rare ) 
 10  Super Rares  (also available as  Secret Rare  and  20th Secret Rare ) 
 18  Rares 
 46  Commons 
 In the  TCG , there are 9 cards per pack and 24 packs per box. The set contains 100 cards:
 
 4  Starlight Rares  (alternate rarities, only present in the 1st Edition print) 
 8  Secret Rares 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 14  Super Rares 
 20  Rares 
 48  Commons
