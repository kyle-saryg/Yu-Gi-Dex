 Ghosts From the Past: The 2nd Haunting 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Ghosts From the Past: The 2nd Haunting  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Bandit Keith ,  Bonz ,  Reiko Kitamori ,  Yako Tenma ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Zane Truesdale , Aster Phoenix ( anime  and  manga ), Jim Crocodile Cook ( anime  and  manga ),  Kaibaman ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Misty Tredwell ,  Paradox ,  Kalin Kessler , Kite Tenjo ( ZEXAL manga  and  ARC-V anime ),  Cologne ,  Syndy Chross , Yuya Sakaki ( anime  and  manga ), Declan Akaba ( anime  and  manga ),  Yusaku Fujiki  and  Theodore Hamilton . 
 Includes new/existing members/support for the " Armed Dragon ", " Blue-Eyes ", " Red-Eyes ", " Dark Magician ", " Puppet ", " Crystal Beast ", " Cyber ", " Kuriboh ", " Fossil ", " Archfiend ",  Red Dragon Archfiend , " Infernity ", " Void ", " Performapal ", " Code Talker ", " Salamangreat ", " Borrel ", " Magician Girl ", " Hyperion ", " Time Thief  and " The Agent " archetypes, as well the  Chimeratech  and  Grepher  series.
