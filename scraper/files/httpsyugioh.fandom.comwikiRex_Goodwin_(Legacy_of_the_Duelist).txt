 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Rex Goodwin , known as  Rex Godwin  (レクス・ゴドウィン ,  Rekusu Godowin ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of the Dark Signer incarnation of  Rex Goodwin , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
