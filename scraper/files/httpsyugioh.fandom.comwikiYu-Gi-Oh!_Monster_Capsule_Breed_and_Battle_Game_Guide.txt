 Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle Game Guide  (遊・戯・王　モンスターカプセルブリード＆バトル　PS版  Yū-Gi-Ō Monsutā Kapuseru Burīdo ando Batoru PS-ban ) is the game guide for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle . It was authored under the collective name STUDIO HARD [1]  and published in Japan by  Shueisha  in August  1998 . [2]
