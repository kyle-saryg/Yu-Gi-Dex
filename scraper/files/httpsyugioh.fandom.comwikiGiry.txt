 Giry , known as  Yoshinori Kawamata  in the Japanese version, is a character from the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Slifer Red  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 - 6 , his name is given to  Tsukasa Naritasan .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 Giry is timid and jumpy. As referenced in  Fonda Fontaine 's bio, he is one of the many male students who has a crush on  Duel Academy 's head nurse.
 
 Tag Force 1 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , when the player receives his  Deck Recipe , they also receive a " Giant Mech-Soldier ".
 He partners with  Michael  for the Tag Force tournament.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Giry uses a  Female  Warrior  Deck  oriented towards summoning the  Fusion Monsters  " Empress Judge " and " Warrior of Tradition ". Nearly all his cards are  Normal Monsters  allowing him to use " Heart of the Underdog " to quickly get the Fusion Material into his  hand .
 In later installments, Giry uses Decks which revolve around the use of the  " Allure Queen " " LV " monsters , and complements them with effects that summon low-Level  Tokens  to the opponent's field and with  Warrior -Type monsters that are in turn powered up by " The A. Forces ".
