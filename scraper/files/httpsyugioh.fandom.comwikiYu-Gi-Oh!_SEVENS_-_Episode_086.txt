 " The Kirishima Affair " is the eighty-sixth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on February 13, 2022.
 Yuga  and co. gather at the Road Laboratory to celebrate  Romin 's solo debut. However,  Luke  can't accept that Romin is smiling while she still doesn't have the memories with her friends. During the evening, as Luke mopes around by himself in the clubroom,  Bakuro , who also has his memories stolen by  Yuga Goha , shows up and challenges Luke to a  Duel .
 
 Featured Duel: Lucidien "Luke" Kallister VS. Bakuro Shinjitsu 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
