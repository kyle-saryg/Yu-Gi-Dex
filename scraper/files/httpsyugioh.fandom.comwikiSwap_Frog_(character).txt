 Swap Frog , known as  Oni Frog  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card " Swap Frog ".
 It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , as an unlockable Free Duel opponent. It is unlocked by defeating  The Dark Creator  three times.
 
 Deck 
 Swap Frog  uses a  Frog  Deck entitled  Frog Panic .
