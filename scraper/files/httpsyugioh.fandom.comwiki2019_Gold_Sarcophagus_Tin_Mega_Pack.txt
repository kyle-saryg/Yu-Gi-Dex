 2019 Gold Sarcophagus Tin Mega Pack  is a  Mega Pack  set released with the  2019 Gold Sarcophagus Tin .
 The set contains cards, made up of a year's worth of  Booster Packs  from the sets  Flames of Destruction ,  Dark Saviors ,  Cybernetic Horizon , and  Soul Fusion . 
 Unlike previous Mega-Tin Mega Packs this one has two main differences, it contains cards from a non-Main Set in the form of  Dark Saviors  and changed the rarities for foil cards to make them a different rarity than their original release.
