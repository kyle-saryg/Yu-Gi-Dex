      The Upper Deck logo     The Upper Deck Company, LLC  is a California-based trading card producing company. Upper Deck Entertainment, was formerly licensed to manufacture the worldwide version of the game, commonly known as the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game , under  Konami 's direction, until losing it in a  lawsuit .
