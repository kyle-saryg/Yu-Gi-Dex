 Summary 
 Dark Yugi  aids  Yugi  and his friends, who have had their souls trapped inside  Bakura 's figurines, against  Dark Bakura  and his  Monster World  avatar,  Dark Master Zorc . Dark Yugi and Yugi see each other for the first time. Yugi's friends are surprised to see that there are two Yugis, but they don't mind. They continue the game and after many trials, they finally confront Zorc...

 Errors 
 The last dice roll in this episode shows the red die with an 8 and the white die with a 0. Since the red die is the "tens", this would mean the number rolled is 80, but the characters announce that it is a critical, meaning the number is mistakenly seen as 08.
