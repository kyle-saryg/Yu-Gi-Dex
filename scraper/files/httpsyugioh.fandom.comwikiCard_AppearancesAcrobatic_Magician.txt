 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 97 ,  Yuya Sakaki  uses this card during the  Friendship Cup  Final against  Jack Atlas . When the effect of Jack's " Tyrant Red Dragon Archfiend " negates that of " Evasion " which Yuya had found and activated, Yuya Special Summons this card from his hand. "Tyrant" then attacks and destroys this card; Yuya activates this card's other effect to place itself in one of his vacant  Pendulum Zones . It remains on the field until the duel ends in the  following episode . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 In  chapter 8 ,  Yuya Sakaki  uses this card during his duel against  Mieru Houchun . With the  Pendulum Scales  set at 3 and 8, he  Pendulum Summons  this card from his hand, as well as " Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon " from his Extra Deck.
