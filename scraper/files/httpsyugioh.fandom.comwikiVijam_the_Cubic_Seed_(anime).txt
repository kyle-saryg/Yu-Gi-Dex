 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  destroyed by battle . If this card is  attacked , place this card in your  Spell & Trap Zone  as a  Spell Card , and place 1  Cubic Counter  on the  monster  that attacked. A monster with Cubic Counter has its  effects   negated , and it cannot attack. When this card is in the Spell & Trap Zone, you can move it to a  Monster Zone . [1] [2]
