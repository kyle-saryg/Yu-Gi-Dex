 [  Beast  /  Effect  ] Select  3 " Cubic Seed "  monsters  you  control , stack them on top of each other, and stack this card on top of them to  Special Summon  this card. This  effect  can be  activated  during the  Battle Phase . This card gains 1000  original ATK  for each monster stacked underneath it. This card can  attack  three times during each Battle Phase. If this card  destroys  a monster  by battle , you can  return  this card to the  hand  to Special Summon the "Cubic Seed" monsters underneath it to your  field . You can  banish  this card from your  Graveyard  to  add  1 “ Vijam the Cubic Seed " from your Graveyard to your hand. [1] [2]
