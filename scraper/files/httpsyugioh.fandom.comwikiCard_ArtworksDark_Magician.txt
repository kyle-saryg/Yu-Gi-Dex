Dungeon Dice Monsters (video game) 
 
 This card has had many different artworks in all media it has been released in. 
 The first  OCG / TCG  artwork comes from the position "Dark Magician" appeared in when  Yugi  Summoned him against  Pegasus  in their first Duel in the manga. 
 The second  OCG / TCG   artwork comes from the position "Dark Magician" appeared in when Yugi Summoned him in the  Labyrinth Duel  in the manga. 
 The fourth  OCG / TCG  artwork comes from the position "Dark Magician" appeared in when Yugi first Summoned him against  Pandora  in the manga. 
 The sixth OCG artwork comes from the position "Dark Magician" appeared in when  Atem  Summoned him in the  Ceremonial Duel  in the manga. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , this card was drawn with a different artwork.
