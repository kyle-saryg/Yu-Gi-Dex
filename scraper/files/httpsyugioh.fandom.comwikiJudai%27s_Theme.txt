 " Judai's Theme " (十代のテーマ,  Jūdai no Tēma ) is an instrumental composition from the Japanese version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . It is the 38 th  listed track on  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX Sound Duel Vol I  and is 1:08 long.
 
 Appearances 
 "Judai's Theme" is played in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time  during Jaden's turn. 
 The theme plays in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  when the player is capable of winning the Duel on the current turn.
