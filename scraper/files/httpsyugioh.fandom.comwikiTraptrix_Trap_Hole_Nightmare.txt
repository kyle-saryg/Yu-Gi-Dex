 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the following  archetypes :
 " Traptrix ", because its Japanese name does not contain  「 蟲 惑 魔 」 
 " Hole ", because its Japanese name does not contain  「ホール」 
As its English name contains the aforementioned strings, it  may  be treated a member of the aforementioned archetypes at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The  Arabic, Chinese  and  Croatian                      names given are not official.  
 The  Chinese                lore given is not official.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When a  monster  that was  Special Summoned  this  turn   activates  its  effect  on your opponent's  field :  Negate  that effect, and if you do,  destroy  that card.
