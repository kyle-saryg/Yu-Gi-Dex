 Box Set
 
 
 A  Duelist Set , known as a  Box Set  in the TCG, is a product that contains 1 Preconstructed Deck, 1 Power-Up Pack, 1 Game Mat, and 70 Card Sleeves. Older OCG Duelist Sets also contained 1 Stock Case, 1 Storage Case, 1 Playing guide, and 1 Rulebook. All cards in the TCG Box Sets are  Platinum Rare .
