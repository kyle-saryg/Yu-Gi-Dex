A  manga promotional card  is a card included with a volume of the manga. Promotional cards have been included with volumes from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium World ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! R ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!!  and with the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie Ani-Manga .
