 The Japanese version of this card has its artwork redrawn with more detail, as compared to the Japanese anime version. 
 The  TCG  and Korean version of this card has the crosses edited out, to avoid any religious connotation as well as to give the tombstones more detail. 
 The dubbed anime version has the tombstones further reshaped, and also erases the text on the tombstones.
