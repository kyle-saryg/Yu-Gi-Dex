 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  7  monsters You can also  Xyz Summon  this card by using a  Rank  2 monster you control with 5 or more  materials . ( Transfer  its materials to this card.) During your  Main Phase : You can  detach  1 Xyz Material from this card, and if you do,  negate  the  effects  of all  face-up  monsters your opponent currently  controls , until the end of this  turn . Your opponent cannot  activate  cards or effects in  response  to this effect's activation if this card has a Level 1 " Purrely " monster as material. When an  attack  is  declared  involving this card tha has 5 or more materials: Inflict 1500  damage  to your opponent.
