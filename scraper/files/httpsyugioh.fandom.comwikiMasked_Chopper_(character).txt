 Masked Chopper  is a character version of the card, " Masked Chopper ". 
 
       Masked Chopper in  Over the Nexus .     
 Masked Chopper appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus . In  World Championship 2007 , he appears as a Level Three Limited Duelist and he challenges the player to include only  Fiend - Type  monsters in the Deck. By beating him, the player gets closer to unlocking Level Four Limited Duelists and a new Booster Pack.
 
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2007 , Masked Chopper play a Fiend Deck titled, "Fiend's Nest". In  5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , he plays an  EARTH  Deck titled, "Go, Chopper".
