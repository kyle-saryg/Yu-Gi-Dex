 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During your  Main Phase 2 , if no  monster   battled  another monster this  turn : You can  discard  1 card;  Set  1 "Battlefield Tragedy" directly from your  Deck  to your  Spell & Trap Zone . You can only use this  effect  of "Battlefield Tragedy"  once per turn . Once per turn, during the  End Phase , if a monster battled another monster this turn: The  turn player   sends  the top 5 cards of their Deck to the  GY .
