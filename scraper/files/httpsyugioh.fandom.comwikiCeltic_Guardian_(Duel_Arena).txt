 Celtic Guardian  is a character version of the card " Celtic Guardian ". 
 He appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. His partner ID is P061. He is only obtainable as a reward after clearing Stage 1-2 in Quest mode.
 In the beta, he had a default of 2 Rental Points, and his partner ID was P027.
 
 Description 
 An Elf that baffles his enemies with his sword and his quickness, rather than power.
 
 Deck 
 This  deck  is designed as an introduction to  Ritual summons  and  Tribute summons . To  summon  a  level  5 or higher  monster , you need to  Tribute  a monster card from the  field .  Ritual monsters  can't be  normal summoned  instead they can be summoned using a  Ritual spell card . You can Tribute monsters from your field and from your  hand .
