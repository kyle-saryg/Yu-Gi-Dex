 " Construction Chaos " also know as " Goha 6th Elementary School " is the thirty-third episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on January 23, 2021.
 Yuga  and co. have been transferred to  Goha 6th .  Gavin , who lost the position of president of  Goha 7th 's  student council , displays the symptoms of "Student Council Presidency Withdrawal Sundrome"... In order to return to Goha 7th's student council, Gavin decides to Duel the  Heavy Cavalry Duel Club , but  Galient Tazaki , who took away  Ranye  and  Rinnosuke  to Goha 6th, stands before him.
 
 Featured Duel: Gavin Sogetsu vs. Galient Tazaki 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
