 Yami Bakura , known as  Dark Bakura  ( 闇 獏良},  Yami Bakura ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yami Bakura , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
