 Trey , known as  III  ( Ⅲ   Surī ) in the Japanese and Italian versions, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival . This is a video game depiction of  Trey , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
