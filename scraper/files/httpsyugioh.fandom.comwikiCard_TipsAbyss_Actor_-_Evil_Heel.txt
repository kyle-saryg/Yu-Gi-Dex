 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Pendulum Rising ", " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Luster Pendulum, the Dracoslayer ", " Magical Abductor ", " Majester Paladin, the Ascending Dracoslayer ", " Performapal Handsamuraiger ", " Chaos Zone ", " Magical Contract Door ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Doomdog Octhros ", " Abyss Actor - Extras ", " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ", and " Abyss Script - Abysstainment ".
 The  Pendulum Effect  of "Abyss Actor - Wild Hope" allows this card to be  Pendulum Summoned  with just " Abyss Actor " monsters in your  Pendulum Zones . Similarly, you can use " Abyss Actor - Twinkle Little Star ", which has the natural  Pendulum Scale  of 9. 
 Use " Evil HERO Infernal Gainer " to allow this card to attack and destroy two monsters in one turn, and thus Set two " Abyss Script "  Spell Cards  in your Graveyard.
