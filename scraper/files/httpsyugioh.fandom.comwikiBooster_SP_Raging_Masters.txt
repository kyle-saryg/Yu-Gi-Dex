 Booster SP: Raging Masters  is a Special  Booster  ("SP" for "special") in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). The pack contains mostly cards used by characters in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime series. 
 In romaji, the name of this booster is read as  Reiji ngu Masutāzu (レイジング・マスターズ), which is a pun on  Reiji Akaba , the Japanese name of  Declan Akaba , a main character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  and the cover character of this pack.

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Declan Akaba ,  Dipper O'rion ,  Julia Krystal ,  Kit Blade , and  Jaden Yuki . 
 Contains members/support for the " D/D ", " Constellar ", " Gem-Knight ", " X-Saber / XX-Saber " and " Elemental HERO " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 Each Korean box comes with 20 packs, 7 cards each; 2 Parallel rares each pack (even when there is a Super rare in the pack, but only 1 when there is a Secret rare).
 The set contains 60 cards, consisting of:
 
 3  Secret Rares 
 15  Super Rares 
 42  Commons  /  Normal Parallel Rares
