 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  monsters  with different  Types You can  target  1 monster " Fur Hire " you  control  and 1 monster your opponent controls;  destroy  them. You can  Tribute  1 monster;  Special Summon  1 monster "Fur Hire" with a different original name from your  hand  or  GY . If you Tributed a  Link Monster , you can Special Summon 2 such monsters, instead. You can only use 1 of each  effect  of "Donner, Dagger Fur Hire" per  turn , and only  once that turn .
