 Anime 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (المدرّج الأمازونيّ) literally  Amazonian Coliseum . 
 The given unofficial Arabic name (حلبة الأمازونيّات) translates to  Arena of the Amazons . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Αρένα Αμαζόνων ) translates to " Amazons' Arena ".
 The Japanese name of this card ( Amazoness Death Ring ) can be roughly translated in Greek as " Κλουβί Θανάτου των Αμαζόνων ". 
 În the American Spanish dub, this card was named " Arena de las Amazonas " (" Amazons' Arena "), which its Spanish dub name was " Arena Amazona " (" Amazon Arena ").
