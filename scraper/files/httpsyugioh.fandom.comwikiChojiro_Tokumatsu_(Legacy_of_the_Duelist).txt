 Chojiro Tokumatsu , known as ( 徳 松   長 次 郎   Tokumatsu Chōjirō ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Chojiro Tokumatsu , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
