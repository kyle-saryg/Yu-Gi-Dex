 LINK VRAINS Duelist Set 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LINK VRAINS Duelist Set  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . [1]

 Features 
 "LINK VRAINS Special Pack" contains 10 Brand New cards. 
 "Deck Enhancement Pack" contains 10 Reprinted cards, which work with the Brand New cards from the 'LINK VRAINS Special Pack'. 
 Includes members/support for the " Tindangle ", " Code Talker ", " Salamangreat ", " Borrel ", " Marincess ", " Dinowrestler ", " Trickstar " and " Altergeist " archetypes 
 Breakdown 
 Each  LINK VRAINS Duelist Set  contains:
 
 1 "LINK VRAINS Special Pack". Each pack contains 9 Ultra Rares and 1 Secret Rare, with every card in the pack having a chance of being a Secret Rare.
 10  Secret Rares / Ultra Rares 
 1 "Deck Enhancement Pack". Each pack contains 5 out of 10 Super Rare cards.
 10  Super Rares 
 1 out of 3 original designs of 70  card sleeves . 
 1 out of 6 original designs of  Field Center Cards .
