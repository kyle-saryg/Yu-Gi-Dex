 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 
 
 
 Syuzo Hiiragi  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Skip Boyle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Shuzo is the father of  Yuzu  and the principal of  You Show Duel School .
