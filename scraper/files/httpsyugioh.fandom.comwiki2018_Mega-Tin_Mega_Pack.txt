 2018 Mega-Tin Mega Pack  or  Mega Tin 2018 Mega Pack  is a  Mega Pack  set released with the  2018 Mega-Tins .
 The set contains cards, made up of a year's worth of  Booster Packs  from the sets " Maximum Crisis ", " Code of the Duelist ", " Circuit Break ", and " Extreme Force ".
 Cards reprinted from "Maximum Crisis" have been updated to match the new card layout.
