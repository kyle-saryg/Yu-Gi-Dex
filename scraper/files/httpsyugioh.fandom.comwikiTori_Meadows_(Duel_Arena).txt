 Tori Meadows  is a video game depiction of  Tori Meadows , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. She appears as a Legend Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 6 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P014.

 Description 
 A female classmate and childhood friend of  Yuma . She worries about Yuma after he is drawn into the battle for the " Number ," and forms the " Super Secret Number Card Investigation Club " in order to help him out, even just a little.
 
 Decks 
 A  Synchro  &  Xyz   Deck  focused around  Fairy - Types  with " The Agent "  archetype . Use the  effect  of " The Agent of Mystery - Earth " to search for other The Agent cards and get your  Synchro  &  Xyz Materials  together!

 Trivia 
 This partner's Deck is " The Agent "-themed, due to the fact that her  anime counterpart's  voice actress,  Mikako Komatsu , is very skilled with using a "The Agent" Deck.
 However, this Deck also incorporates some of this partner's anime counterpart's cards, such as " Little Fairy " and " Fairy Cheer Girl ".
