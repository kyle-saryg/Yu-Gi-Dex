 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] This card gains 500  ATK  for each  face-up   FIRE   monster  on the  field . When this card  destroys  an opponent's monster  by battle  and  sends  it to the  Graveyard ,  Special Summon  that monster to your side of the field in face-up  Attack Position  at the end of the  Battle Phase . That monster is treated as a FIRE monster, and its  effect (s) is  negated . When this card is  removed from the field , destroy all monsters Special Summoned by this card's effect.
