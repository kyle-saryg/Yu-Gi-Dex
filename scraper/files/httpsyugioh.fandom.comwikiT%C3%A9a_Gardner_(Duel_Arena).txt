 Téa Gardner  is a video game depiction of  Téa Gardner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime. She appears as a Legend Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 6 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P135.
 She was only obtainable by winning at least 30 times in Arena mode (Single Round only), during the time period of January 12, 2015, 00:00 (UTC) through January 18, 2015, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received her in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on January 21, 2015.

 Description 
 Yugi's  childhood friend. Inspired by the musical she saw as a child, she has a dream to be a dancer. She works hard to save money to attend a dancing school in New York.
 
 Deck 
 A  Deck  themed on " Dark Magician Girl: Sage's Stone ", which is the musical Téa saw as a child.  Dark Magician  and  Dark Magician Girl  are Spell cards that can  Special Summon . This Deck also includes a variety of specialized support cards.

 Trivia 
 This is the first Legend Rare Partner in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena  that is not available through Partner Recruiting. 
 This partner's Deck name, "Stand By for Duel!", is a translation of the Japanese phrase declared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  anime at the end of the "next episode preview" that was displayed at the end of each Japanese episode. This was voiced by Téa Gardner each time. 
 " Musician King " in this character's Extra Deck is possibly a reference to the card being used by  Johnny Steps , a minor antagonist who faced off against Téa in a dance battle arcade game.
