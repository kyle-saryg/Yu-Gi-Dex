The  Dark Master  is a role played by one player in the tabletop game,  Monster World .
 One player plays the Dark Master, while the other players play a team of adventurers. The Dark Master tries to defeat the adventurers to win the game. The adventurers face the Dark Master as a final boss and must defeat it to win. [1] 
 The only Dark Master seen in the series was  Dark Master Zorc , who was played by  Dark Bakura .
