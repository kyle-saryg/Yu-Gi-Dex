 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Cannot be  destroyed in battle  with a  monster  with the same  Type  and/or  Attribute . You can  declare  1 Type and 1 Attribute; this card's Type and Attribute become the declared Type and Attribute until the end of the opponent's  turn . You can only use this  effect  of "Dimensional Isoallotope Varis"  once per turn .
