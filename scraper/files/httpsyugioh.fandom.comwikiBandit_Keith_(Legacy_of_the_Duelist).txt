 Bandit Keith  (バンデット・キース  Bandetto Kīsu ) is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Bandit Keith , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
