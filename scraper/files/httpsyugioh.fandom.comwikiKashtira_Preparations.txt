 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During either player's  turn : You can  Special Summon  1 of your " Kashtira "  monsters  that is  banished  or in your  hand . If your opponent  activates  a  Trap Card  or  effect  while this card is  face-up  in the  Spell & Trap Zone , and you  control  a "Kashtira" monster: You can  look  at your opponent's hand and banish 1 card from it  face-down . You can only use each  effect  of "Kashtira Preparations"  once per turn .
