 Raging Battle  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).  is the fourth set of the OCG's 6th series following  Crimson Crisis . It is followed by  Ancient Prophecy .
 This was the final  Booster Pack  to use  AE  in its Asian-English print set numbers.

 Features 
 Raging Battle  contains new cards used by  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Leo ,  Crow Hogan  and others. It introduces the " Koa'ki Meiru " and " Earthbound Immortal "  archetypes , as well as further support for the " Blackwing ", " Ojama " and " Morphtronic " archetypes. This pack also includes new support cards for  Beast - Type  monsters.
