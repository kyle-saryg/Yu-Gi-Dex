 Vampire Genesis  is a character version of the card, " Vampire Genesis ".
He is an opponent in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 .
 In  World Championship 2006 , Vampire Genesis is a Tier 3 opponent in Campaign Mode, unlocked (along with  Infernal Flame Emperor ) by beating all Tier 2 opponents at least three times. His Deck is based on the  Zombie Madness  Structure Deck, but with several different cards to make it even more effective.
 In  World Championship 2007 , Vampire Genesis appears as a Level Six Limited Duelist, who challenges the player to only include  Zombie - Type  monsters in their Deck. By beating him, the player gets closer to unlocking a new Booster Pack.
