 Baxia, Brightness of the Yang Zing  is a character version of the card " Baxia, Brightness of the Yang Zing ". 
 It appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P132.
 It was only obtainable through 2 ways. One way was by having 15 or more of the following " Yang Zing "-related cards in one's trunk (including multiple copies of 1 or more cards) before maintenance occurred on January 7, 2015: " Suanni, Fire of the Yang Zing ", " Bi'an, Earth of the Yang Zing ", " Bixi, Water of the Yang Zing ", " Pulao, Wind of the Yang Zing ", " Chiwen, Light of the Yang Zing ", "Baxia, Brightness of the Yang Zing", " Yang Zing Path ", " Yang Zing Prana ", " Yang Zing Creation ", and/or " Yang Zing Unleashed ". The reason for this limited section of cards was because  The New Challengers  and newer packs had not yet been released in-game. Applicable players received this partner in their Gift Boxes on January 9, 2015.
 The second way was by achieving any Rank from 1 to 100 on the Arena Weekly Win Ratings (Single) for the time period of January 12, 2015, 00:00 (UTC) through January 18, 2015, 23:59 (UTC). This was compensation for the fact that this character's silhouette was accidentally used in promotions advertising the January 2015 "1-week Arena Challenge!" event. Applicable players received it in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on January 21, 2015, along with the original planned partner,  Saffira, Queen of Dragons . Players who already had this partner at this point had its Rental Point maximum increased by 1 and simultaneously refreshed.

 Trivia 
 This character's partner description refers to the fact that " Bi'an, Earth of the Yang Zing " constitutes most of this monster (" Chiwen, Light of the Yang Zing " and " Suanni, Fire of the Yang Zing " forming the rest). 
 When this partner was first released, its Deck name was "Queen's Blessings" instead. This was a mistake, one that was likely related to the fact that this character's silhouette was accidentally used in promotions for the "1-week Arena Challenge!" event of January 2015, instead of that of  Saffira, Queen of Dragons .  Stellarknight Delteros  had this partner's Deck name instead, "Threads of Stars". These mix-ups were fixed during the same maintenance that released Saffira as a partner.
