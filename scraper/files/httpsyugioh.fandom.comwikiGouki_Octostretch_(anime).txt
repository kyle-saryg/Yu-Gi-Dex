 [  Warrior  /  Effect  ] During  damage calculation , if an opponent's  monster   attacks : You can  discard  this card; any  battle damage  you take from that  battle  is halved. When your opponent  activates  a card or  effect  that would inflict  damage  to you (including during the  Damage Step ) ( Quick Effect ): You can discard this card; any damage you take from that effect is halved. If this card is  sent  from the  field  to the  GY : You can  add  1 " Gouki " card from your  Deck  to your  hand , except "Gouki Octostretch". You can only use this effect of "Gouki Octostretch"  once per turn .
