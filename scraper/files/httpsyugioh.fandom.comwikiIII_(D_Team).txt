 III  ( Ⅲ ,  Surī ) is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL . This is an alternate incarnation of  Trey , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime .
 
 Biography 
 III appeared at the opening ceremonies of the  Duel Team Carnival , having formed a team with  IV  and  V . [1]
