 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 If you control a monster that is not a "Burning Abyss" monster, destroy this card. You can only use 1 of  the following  effects of "Alich, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss" per turn, and only once that turn. ● If you control no Spell/Trap Cards: You can Special Summon this card from your hand. ● If this card is sent to the Graveyard: You can target 1 face-up monster on the field; negate its effects until the end of this turn.
