 Edmund "Ted" Lewis , sometimes credited as  Ed Paul  or  Eddie Paulson , is an American voice actor based in New York City. He has provided voice-overs for several properties of  4Kids Entertainment .
 
 Roles 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! , Lewis' most notable roles were  Bakura Ryou ,  Yami Bakura ,  Thief King Bakura ,  Bandit Keith [1] ,  Alister  and  Croquet . He also voiced  Odion Ishtar 's younger self,  Richard Goat  and  Gozaburo Kaiba 's season five dialogue.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , Lewis' most notable role was voicing  Chumley Huffington . He also voiced  Admiral ,  Howard X Miller  and  Neo-Spacian Grand Mole .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , his most notable role was voicing  Jack Atlas [1] . He also voiced  Mitch ,  Rex Goodwin 's younger self and the  fake Jack Atlas .
 In    Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  he voices  Dempsey Crossit  and  Cameron Clix .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , he reprises his role as  Jack Atlas [1] .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links , Lewis reprises his roles as  Bandit Keith ,  Yami Bakura  and  Jack Atlas .
