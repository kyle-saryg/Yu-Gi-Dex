 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Synchro Fusionist ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Predaplant Chimerafflesia ", " Sabatiel - The Philosopher's Stone ", " Predaplant Darlingtonia Cobra ", " Supreme King Gate Zero ", and " Cyberdark Inferno ". 
 Use this card to activate the effects of " Metaphys " or " Thunder Dragon " monsters already in your hand, by using them as  Fusion Material  for " Gem-Knight Seraphinite " or " Gem-Knight Prismaura " respectively.
