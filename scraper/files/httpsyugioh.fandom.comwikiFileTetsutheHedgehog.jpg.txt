 Tetsuo Sasaki  (ササキテツオ  Sasaki Tetsuo ), also known as  Tetsu the Hedgehog  (針ねずみのテツ  Harinezumi no Tetsu ), is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is person who was framed by  Jiro the Jorogumo . Jiro disguised himself as Tetsu and broke out of prison. While Tetsu also broke out and attempted to commit some robberies, he was also framed for a variety of crimes that were committed by Jiro. He was in the restaurant,  Burger World , and everyone thought that he was the escaped convict after an investigation. However, he accidentally, but fortunately, made Jiro discovered.
