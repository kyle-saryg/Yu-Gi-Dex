 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 
 
 
 Illusion Magician  ( 幻 想 の 魔 術 師   Gensō no Majutsushi ) was an  Egyptian Spirit Monster , Summoned by  Mahad  to help stop  Thief Bakura . After Mahad was killed, he fused himself with "Illusion Magician", creating " Dark Magician ".
 Illusion Magician could make an attack similar to " Spellbinding Circle ", which he used against "Diabound".
