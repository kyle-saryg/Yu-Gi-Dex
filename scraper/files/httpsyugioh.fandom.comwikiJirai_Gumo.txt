 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When this card  declares  an  attack : Toss a coin and call it. If you call it wrong, lose half your  Life Points . 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Lorsque cette carte déclare une attaque : jouez à pile ou face. Si vous perdez, perdez la moitié de vos Life Points. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Wenn du mit dieser Karte angreifst, musst du eine Münze werfen und dich für Kopf oder Zahl entscheiden. Wenn du richtig vorausgesagt hast, greifst du normal an. Wenn du falsch vorausgesagt hast, verlierst du die Hälfte deiner Life Points, und greifst dann an. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Quando questa carta dichiara un attacco: lancia una moneta e scegli testa o croce. Se sbagli, perdi la metà dei tuoi Life Point. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Portuguese 
 
 
 Quando você declara um ataque com esta carta, jogue uma moeda e selecione Cara ou Coroa. Se você errar, reduza seus LPs pela metade antes de atacar. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Spanish 
 
 
 Cuando esta carta declara un ataque: lanza una moneda y elige Cara o Cruz. Si fallas, pierde la mitad de tus Life Points. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Japanese 
 
 
 このカードの攻撃宣言時、コイントスで裏表を当てる。当たりの場合はそのまま攻撃する。ハズレの場合は自分のライフポイントを半分失い攻撃する。 
 
 
 
 
 
   Chinese 
 
 
 這張卡攻擊宣言時，猜硬幣的正反。猜中的話就繼續攻擊。猜不中的話自己的基本分減半再攻擊。 
 
 
 
 
 
   Korean 
 
 
 이 카드의 공격 선언시, 코인 토스로 앞뒷면을 맞춘다. 맞추면 그대로 공격한다. 못 맞추면 자신의 라이프 포인트를 절반 상실하고 공격한다. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
When you attack with this card, toss a coin and call it. If you call it right, attack normally. If you call it wrong, reduce your Life Points by half before attacking. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
 A stealthy spider that lurks out of sight under the ground. It makes prey out of anything that passes over its lair. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
When you attack with this card, toss a coin and call it. If you call it right, attack normally. If you call it wrong, reduce your Life Points by half before attacking.
