   English 
 
 
1  Tuner  + 1 or more non-Tuner " Gusto "  monsters Once per turn : You can  shuffle  2 "Gusto" monsters from your  Graveyard  into the  Main Deck  to  target  1  face-up  monster your opponent  controls ;  destroy  that target.
