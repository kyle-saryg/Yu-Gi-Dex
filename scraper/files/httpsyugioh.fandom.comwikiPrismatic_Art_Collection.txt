 Prismatic Art Collection 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Prismatic Art Collection  is a   Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes 32 monsters with  variant artworks . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 50 cards.
 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 8  Ultra Rares 
 12  Super Rares 
 27  Normal Parallel Rares 
 The Normal Parellel Rare, Super Rare, and Ultra Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rares  and Prismatic Secret Rares.
