 This was not a physical card, but " Relinquished " fused with " Thousand-Eyes Idol ".
 
 
 
 While this card's eyes are open, enemy monsters are paralyzed. Once per turn, this card can absorb one  monster  your opponent  controls . This card's  ATK  and  DEF  are equal to the combined ATK and combined DEF, respectively, of all monsters currently absorbed. This card cannot be  destroyed  until the monsters it has absorbed are first destroyed. When an absorbed monster is destroyed by battle, its owner takes the  Battle Damage .
