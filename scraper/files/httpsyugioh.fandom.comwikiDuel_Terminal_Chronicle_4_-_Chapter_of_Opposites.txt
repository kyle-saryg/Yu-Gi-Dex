 Duel Terminal Chronicle 4 - Chapter of Opposites 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duel Terminal Chronicle 4 - Chapter of Opposites  (デュエルターミナル「クロニクルⅣ　対極の章」  Dyueru Tāminaru "Kuronikuru IV Taikyoku no Shō" ) is the fourth and final chronicle in the  Duel Terminal  series.

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 This set contains cards from many of the previous  Duel Terminal  sets:  Duel Terminal - Raid of the Inverz!!  and  Duel Terminal - Judgment of Omega!! .
 
 Breakdown 
 This set contains 60 cards, which are from the  Duel Terminals : 10 and 11.
