 Trigger 
 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] If you have no cards in your  hand , when your opponent  activates  an  effect  that would inflict  damage , you can  remove from play  this card from your  Graveyard  to  negate  the effect damage you take. Your opponent then chooses whether or not to apply the following effect.  Reveal  the top card of your  Deck . If it is a  Monster Card , inflict damage to your opponent equal to the amount of effect damage you took this  turn  plus the amount of negated effect damage. If it is a  Spell  or  Trap Card , you take damage equal to the amount of effect damage you took this turn. If your opponent does not choose to apply this effect, you take no effect damage this turn.
