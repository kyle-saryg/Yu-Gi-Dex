 Structure Deck: Lord of the Magician  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the sixteenth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-fourth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Undead World . The  TCG  equivalent of this Deck is  Spellcaster's Command Structure Deck .
 In the Japanese  OCG ,  Structure Deck: Lord of the Magician Limited Edition  was released, containing a promotional card that depended on the retailer.
 This was the final  Structure Deck  to use  AE  in its Asian-English print set numbers.

 Features 
 The deck features the  Spellcaster - Type   monsters . It includes reprints of hard-to-get Spellcaster-Type monsters and focuses on the use of  Spell Counters .
