 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
(This card is always treated as a " Galaxy-Eyes " card.) If there are 3 or more  LIGHT   Dragon   monsters  on the  field  with 3000 or more  original ATK :  Special Summon  1 Dragon " Number "  Xyz Monster  from your  Extra Deck , then  attach  this card on the  field  to it as  material . You can only apply the  effect  of "Numeron Creation"  once per turn .
