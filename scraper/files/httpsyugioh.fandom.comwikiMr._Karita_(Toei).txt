 Mr. Karita , known as is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is the anime's incarnation of  Mr. Karita  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh  manga . Karita is a P.E. teacher, who harassed  Ryo Bakura  on his first day at  Domino High School .
 After suffering a  Penalty Game , he became a villager in a  Monster World  role-playing game.
