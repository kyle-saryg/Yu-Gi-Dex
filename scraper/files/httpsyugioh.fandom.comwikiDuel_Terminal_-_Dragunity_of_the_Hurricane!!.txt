 Duel Terminal - Dragunity of the Hurricane  is sixth installment of the  OCG 's  Duel Terminal  series, following  Duel Terminal - Champion of Chaos!! , and followed by  Duel Terminal - Charge of the Genex!! .

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 Includes members/support for the " Fabled ", " Jurrac ", " Naturia ", " R-Genex ", " Ice Barrier ", " Ally of Justice ", and " Worm "  archetypes . 
 Introduces the " Dragunity " and " Dragunity Knight " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Introduces  Aster Phoenix  and  Luna  as duelable opponents. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards (consisting of 30 New Cards and 20 Reprint Cards), or 54 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares  (all available as Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares) 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 10  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 30  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
