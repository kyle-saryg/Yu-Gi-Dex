 Damon , known in Japan as  Taira Taizan , is a character in the  Tag Force  games. This is a video game version of  Damon , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Damon can become the player's partner in Part 1 after beating the game by clearing a minigame while he is at the Store.
 Beating Damon 10 times gets the player his  Deck Recipe  and a copy of " Doron ".
 
 
 Tag Force 2 
 During  Tyranno 's sixth story event, Damon and  Jesse  Tag Duel against the player and Tyranno.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Damon duels against the player in the third story event of Normal  Jaden 's story.
 Damon partners with  Don Zaloog  to Tag Duel against the player and  Chazz Princeton  as part of Normal Chazz's last story event.
 
 Decks 
 Damon's Decks are composed almost entirely of cards with effects that let him draw more cards.
