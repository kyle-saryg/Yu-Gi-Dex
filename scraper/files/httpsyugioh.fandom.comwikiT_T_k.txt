 T T k  appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for November 2010.
 He is a character created and used by the third player of the Reverse of Arcadia tournament, Naoki Shibata. [ citation needed ]   Peke-Hime  and  Kami  were first and second place respectively in the tournament.
