 " Are you ready? " ( Ａｒｅ   ｙｏｕ   ｒｅａｄｙ ？,  Ā Yū Redi? ) is the fifth Japanese ending theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime, performed by  BiS . It replaces " glory " in  episode 096 .
