 " Butterfly"  ( 蝶   Chō ) is an  archetype  of  monsters  used by  Dextra  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. Members had previously existed in the  TCG  and  OCG , but had no  support  until  ZEXAL . The " Butterspy " archetype is also a part of this group, and not all cards with "Butterfly" in their English names are necessarily members.
