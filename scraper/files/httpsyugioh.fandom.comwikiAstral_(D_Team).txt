 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 
 
 Astral  (アストラル  Asutoraru ) is a mysterious entity in  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL . He is a Dueling Ghost who helps  Yuma  in his  Duels . However, he can only be seen by him.
