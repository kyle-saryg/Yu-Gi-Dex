 Light of Destruction  is the eighth and last set of the  OCG   Series 5   Booster Packs  after  Phantom Darkness . It is followed by the first set of  OCG   Series 6  packs,  The Duelist Genesis .
 The North American release of this set took place on May 13, 2008. [1] 
 Packs of the  TCG  set were also printed in the  Light of Destruction: Special Edition .

 Features 
 This set focuses on  LIGHT   Attribute   monsters , featuring  Sartorius ' " Arcana Force " cards and the " Lightsworn ". This pack also features cards used by  Jaden Yuki ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Yusuke Fujiwara ,  Makoto Inotsume ,  Trueman ,  Sartorius ,  Jesse Anderson  and  Vellian Crowler . The  TCG  version also includes cards used by  Gekko Tenma ,  Masumi Momono ,  Yami Yugi ,  Prince Ojin ,   Amnael ,  Jim Crocodile Cook ,  Adrian Gecko  and  Joey Wheeler . The  cover card  is " Honest ".
