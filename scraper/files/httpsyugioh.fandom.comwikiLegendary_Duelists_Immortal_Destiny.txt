 Legendary Duelists: Immortal Destiny  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by various male antagonists over the course of the Yu-Gi-Oh! series, including  Yami Bakura ,  The Supreme King ,  Rex Goodwin ,  Quattro , and  Yuri . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Earthbound Immortal ", " Evil HERO ", " Gimmick Puppet ", " HERO ", " Predap ", " Predaplant " and " Spirit Message " archetypes, as well as the " Inca " series.
