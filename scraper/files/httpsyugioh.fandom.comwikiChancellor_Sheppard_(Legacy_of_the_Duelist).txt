 Chancellor Sheppard , known as  Samejima  (鮫島) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Chancellor Sheppard , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
