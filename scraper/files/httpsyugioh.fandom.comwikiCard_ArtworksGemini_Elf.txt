 "Gemini Elf" has two different artworks, though only the first one has been released in the  TCG . The international version of "Gemini Elf" is given more clothing to cover the elves' cleavage. Their busts are also reduced. In the international versions of video games that feature the second artwork, the elves yet again have their busts reduced and cleavage covered. In the international version of " Non-Spellcasting Area " the elves remain unedited. Also, in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories , "Gemini Elf" was drawn with a different artwork.
