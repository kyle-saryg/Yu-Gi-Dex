 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Field 
 
 When you  activate  this card,  select  1  face-up   monster  you  control . It gains 3000  ATK  and  DEF . If that monster  attacks , its ATK and DEF becomes their values before that monster was selected during the  Damage Step  only. If that monster is removed from the  field , select 1 face-up monster you control and apply this card's  effects  to it.
