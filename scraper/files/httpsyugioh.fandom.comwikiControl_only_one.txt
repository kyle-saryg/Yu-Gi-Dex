This Property entry details every card with a  Condition  that prohibits you from having another card with its name on your side of the  field . It is also known as side-unique. This is a side-specific variation of  Field-Unique .
 
 This means that if a "side-unique" card is  face-up  on any side of the field, and a new copy of that card would enter/become face-up on that side of the field, then all new copies are sent to the  Graveyard  as a state-based action instead. Also, a  face-up   Summon  may not be attempted if there is already one of those monsters face-up on the field.
 Additional copies can exist  face-down  (you can  Set / Special Summon  face-down additional copies); however, the moment they become face-up, they also become subject to this rule and are then sent to the Graveyard. Also, they may not be  Flip Summoned  if another copy is already face-up on the field. 
 If the attempted Summon is from the Graveyard or the so-called  Banished  Zone, such as with " Return from the Different Dimension ", then all illegal Summons will simply fail at resolution.
