 Remove from play  1 card in your  hand .  Select  1  Fusion Monster  that was  destroyed  this turn while on your side of the  field , then  Special Summon  it during your next  Standby Phase ,  ignoring the Summoning conditions . That  monster  cannot  declare  an  attack  during the  turn  it is  Summoned .
