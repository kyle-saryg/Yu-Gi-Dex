 Speed Duel Starter Decks: Destiny Masters 
 Speed Duel Starter Decks: Duelists of Tomorrow 
 
 
 
 
 
 Speed Duel Starter Decks: Destiny Masters  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Duelists of Tomorrow , they introduce the  Speed Duels  and  Skill Cards  to the  TCG .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Destiny Masters  contains:
 
 1 20-Card  Yami Yugi  Deck 
 1 20-Card  Ishizu Ishtar  Deck 
 1 20-Card  Maximillion Pegasus  Deck 
 9  Skill Cards  (3 for Yugi, 3 for Ishizu and 3 for Pegasus) 
 3  Ultra Rare   variant cards
