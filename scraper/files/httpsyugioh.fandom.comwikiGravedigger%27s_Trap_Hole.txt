 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Trap Hole "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 落 とし 穴 」 .   As its English name contains "Trap Hole", it  may  be treated a member of the "Trap Hole" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When your opponent  activates  a  monster effect  in their  hand  or  GY , or when your opponent's  banished  monster effect is activated:  Negate  its  effect , and if you do, inflict 2000  damage  to your opponent. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Lorsque votre adversaire active un effet de monstre dans sa main ou Cimetière, ou lorsque l'effet du monstre banni de votre adversaire est activé : annulez son effet, et si vous le faites, infligez 2000 points de dommages à votre adversaire. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Wenn dein Gegner einen Monstereffekt in seiner Hand oder seinem Friedhof aktiviert oder wenn ein Effekt eines verbannten Monsters deines Gegners aktiviert wird: Annulliere seinen Effekt und falls du dies tust, füge deinem Gegner 2000 Schaden zu. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian

   Portuguese 
 
 
 Quando seu oponente ativar um efeito de monstro na mão ou no Cemitério dele, ou quando um efeito de monstro banido do seu oponente for ativado: negue seus efeitos e, se isso acontecer, cause 2000 de dano ao seu oponente.
