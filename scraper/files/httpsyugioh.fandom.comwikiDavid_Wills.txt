 David Wills  is a voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment . He has done a number of characters in the English dubs of  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime series.
 
 Roles 
 In the  NAS  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series , Wills made his  Yu-Gi-Oh!  debut, by voicing  Nezbitt . [ citation needed ]  He also voiced  Roland [ citation needed ]  (with  Wayne Grayson  standing in for episodes 128 to 148). [ citation needed ] 
 Wills has his most major Yu-Gi-Oh! role in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , where he voices  Tyranno Hassleberry . [ citation needed ]  Here, he also voices  Chancellor Sheppard , [ citation needed ]   Kagemaru [ citation needed ]  and  Lucien Grimley . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , Wills voices  Tank , [ citation needed ]   Mr. Armstrong , [ citation needed ]  and  Dragan . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , Wills voices  Roscoe , [ citation needed ]   Wolfsbane [ citation needed ]  and  Don Thousand [ citation needed ] .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links , Wills reprises his role as  Tyranno Hassleberry .
