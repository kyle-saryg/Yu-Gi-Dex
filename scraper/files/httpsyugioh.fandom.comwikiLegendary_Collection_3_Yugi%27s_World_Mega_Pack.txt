 Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World Mega Pack  is a  Mega-Pack  released with  Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World  boxes. Each box came with five Mega-Packs. It is the largest set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  and has been released in English, French, German, Italian and Spanish.

 Features 
 The set features many cards linked with  Yugi Muto  and his opponents.
 
 Cards 1 to 102 were used by Yugi Muto and  Yami Yugi  or related to cards that they used. 
 Cards 103 to 118 were used by  Maximillion Pegasus . 
 Cards 119 to 128 were used by  Yami Bakura  or related to him. 
 Cards 129 to 142 were used by  Rafael  or related to cards he used. 
 Cards 143 to 148 were used by  Yami Marik . 
 Cards 149 and 150 were used by  Mai Valentine . 
 Cards 151 to 153 were used by  Keith Howard , under  Marik Ishtar 's control. 
 Cards 154 to 156 were used by  Solomon Muto . 
 This set also debuted 1 card in the  TCG : Prior to the release of this set, " Kanan the Swordmistress " was  OCG -exclusive.
 " Bowganian " was specifically released as a  Super Rare  in this pack to fulfill the request of a person asking for a foil release of "Bowganian" for his little brother. [1]
