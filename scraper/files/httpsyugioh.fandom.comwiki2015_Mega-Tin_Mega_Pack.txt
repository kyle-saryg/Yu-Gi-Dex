 2015 Mega-Tin Mega Pack  or  Mega Tin 2015 Mega Pack  is a  Mega Pack  set released with the  2015 Mega-Tins .
 The set contains 247 cards, made up of a year's worth of  Booster Packs  from the sets  Primal Origin ,  Duelist Alliance ,  The New Challengers , and  Secrets of Eternity .
