 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 Bakura  is the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  incarnation of  Yami Bakura . He is a member of the Red Roses and located at  Rennes . He claims to also answer to  Jack Cade (or Mortimer) , the leader of the 1450 Kent revolt against Henry VI.
 
 Deck 
 Bakura plays a  Beast / Plant  Deck . Among his strategies, he uses the Crush terrain to protect his  Plant  monsters.
