 Sector Security on the Move! Officer Tetsu Trudge
 
 
 Sector Security on the Move! Officer Tetsu Trudge  is an  event  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . When in effect,  Tetsu Trudge  appears as a temporary opponent at the Gate, who can be Dueled in order to claim multiple rewards, including the ability to unlock him as a playable character.

 Event details 
 The player must be at Stage 4 or higher. 
 Collect Security Badges by defeating Duelist in Duel World and Ranked Duels. 
 Use Security Badges at the Gate to Duel Tetsu Trudge.

 Special Orders 
 After Dueling in Duel World, Duelists may drop a “Special Order” in addition to Security Badges. 
 These are event missions that offer additional rewards such as EX Jewels and Gold. 
 Completing 100 Special Orders throughout the event will unlock an exclusive icon.
