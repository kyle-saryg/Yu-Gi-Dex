 Condition 
 Continuous 
 At the end of your opponent's  turns , place 1 " Spirit Message " card from your  hand  or your  Deck   face-up  to the  field . If it is from your Deck, then  shuffle  your Deck. If "Spirit Message" cards " I ", " N ", " A ", and " L " are all on the field in their proper order, you are declared the winner. If any of the cards are  destroyed  or  removed from the field  while constructing the message, they are all  sent  to your  Graveyard  immediately.
