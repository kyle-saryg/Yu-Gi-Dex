 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If your  LP  are lower than your opponent's: You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . During your  Main Phase : You can  Fusion Summon  1  Fusion Monster  from your  Extra Deck , using  monsters  from your  hand  or  field  as  Fusion Material , including this card on the field. You can only use each  effect  of "Dyna Base"  once per turn .
