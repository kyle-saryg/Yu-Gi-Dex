 20th Anniversary Legend Collection 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 20th Anniversary Legend Collection  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack (2  Super Parallel Rares , 1  Ultra Parallel Rare , and 1  Secret Rare  or higher rarity) and 15 packs per box. The set contains 100 cards:
 
 20  20th Secret Rares  (Japanese) 
 20  Extra Secret Rares  (Korean) 
 30  Ultra Parallel Rares 
 50  Super Parallel Rares 
 All cards are also available as  Secret Rare .
