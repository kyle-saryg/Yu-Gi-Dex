 Quinton , known as  V  ( Ⅴ   Bui ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Quinton , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
