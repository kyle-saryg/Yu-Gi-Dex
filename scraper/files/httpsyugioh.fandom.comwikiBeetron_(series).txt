 "Beetron"  (ビートロン  Bītoron ) is a  series  of  EARTH   Machine   monsters  that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime. They were used by  Chazz Princeton  during his second Duel against  Bastion Misawa  and his Duel against  Blair Flannigan . Similiar to the " A-to-Z " cards, two of them are  Union monsters  and all three monsters can be  fused together  without " Polymerization ".
