   English 
 
 
3  Level  7  monsters You can  detach  1  Xyz Material  from this card; roll a six-sided die twice and this card's  ATK  becomes the larger number rolled × 700 until your opponent's next  End Phase , then if the total roll was exactly 7, apply 1 of these  effects . ●  Send  all other cards on the  field  to the  Graveyard . ●  Special Summon  1 monster from your  hand  or from either Graveyard. ●  Draw  3 cards, then  discard  2 cards.
