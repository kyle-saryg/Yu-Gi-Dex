 Mythological Age  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the penultimate set of the  OCG Series 2 .
 In other languages, this set is amalgamated with  Struggle of Chaos  to create  Legacy of Darkness .

 Features 
 Mythological Age  introduces  Spirit monsters  and features the first release of " Bottomless Trap Hole " and " Fiber Jar " in the  OCG . " Black Skull Dragon " was reprinted as an  Ultimate Rare .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contained 5 cards and each box contained 30 packs.
 The set contains 52 cards in total, or 55 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 3  Ultra Parallel Rare 
 1  Ultimate Rare 
 3  Ultra Rare 
 4  Super Rare 
 6  Rare 
 38  Common

 Trivia 
 Yami Yugi 's appearance on the cover of this booster pack is taken from the omake page for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 85: " The Room of Revenge " . 
 Despite the English name written on the pack, this set's Japanese name translates to "Revived Soul".
