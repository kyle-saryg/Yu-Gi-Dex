 Starter Deck 2011  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the sixth deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2010 . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Starter Deck: Dawn of the Xyz .
