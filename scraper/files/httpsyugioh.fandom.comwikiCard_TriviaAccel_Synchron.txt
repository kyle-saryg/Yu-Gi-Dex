 Both this card's name and  Quick Effect  are references to  Accel Synchro Summoning . 
 This monster's body is based on  Yusei Go . 
 This card has many similarities to " Formula Synchron ".
 Both are " Synchron " monsters. 
 Both are  Tuner Synchro Monsters . 
 Their ATK is equal to their  Level  x100. 
 Both have the effect of Synchro Summoning during the opponent's Main Phase by using it as a material. 
 This monster has the highest  DEF  of all "Synchron" monsters. 
 The Synchro Summon rings that are depicted within the background of this card are red instead of the usual green.
 There also three stars in between the two rings, meaning it's likely depicting this monster's Summoning.
