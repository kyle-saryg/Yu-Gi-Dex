 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When a  monster   declares  an  attack :  Activate  1 of these  effects . ●  Destroy  the attacking monster. ●  Target  1 monster on the  field , except the attacking monster; change the  attack target  to it and perform  damage calculation . You can only activate 1 "Attack Guidance Armor" per  turn .
