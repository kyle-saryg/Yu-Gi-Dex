 Dr. Faker  ( Ｄｒ． フェイカー  Dokutā Feikā ), is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Dr. Faker , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
