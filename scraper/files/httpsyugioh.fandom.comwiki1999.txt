 20  - Release date for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Bandai Sealdass . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Perfect Master BOOK Volume 2 , the second game guide for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , is published in Japan. [1] 
 February 
 4  - The  OCG  is first introduced with the release of  Vol.1 . [2]  This set introduces the rarities  Common ,  Rare ,  Super Rare , and  Ultra Rare , and the card types  Normal Monster ,  Normal Spell ,  Equip Spell , and  Normal Trap . Monster  attributes  introduced are  DARK ,  LIGHT ,  EARTH ,  WATER , and  WIND , and monster  types  introduced are  Aqua ,  Beast ,  Beast-Warrior ,  Dinosaur ,  Dragon ,  Fairy ,  Fiend ,  Insect ,  Plant ,  Rock ,  Spellcaster ,  Thunder ,  Warrior ,  Winged Beast , and  Zombie .
