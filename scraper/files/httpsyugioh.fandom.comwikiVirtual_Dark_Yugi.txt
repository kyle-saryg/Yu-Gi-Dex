 Virtual Dark Yugi  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions . He is a virtual simulation created by  Seto Kaiba , using his memories of  Dark Yugi .

 Biography 
 The real Dark Yugi's consciousness left for the underworld, following the  Ceremonial Battle . Dark Yugi's rival, Seto Kaiba, was still eager to face him in another Duel and made attempts to communicate with him, including building a virtual reconstruction of him, using his own memories.
 At the  KaibaCorp space station , Virtual Dark Yugi and Kaiba  Dueled  in a church-like virtual-reality setting. Kaiba eventually won, but was disappointed, since he believed he had not accurately recreated Dark Yugi.
 
 Deck 
 Virtual Dark Yugi's Deck is based on the Deck used by the real  Dark Yugi .
