 World Premiere Pack 2021  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -only  cards to the  OCG .

 Features 
 Introduces to the OCG previously TCG-only cards from  Phantom Rage ,  Blazing Vortex ,  Lightning Overdrive ,  King's Court  and other products. 
 Introduces the " Myutant " and " War Rock " archetypes to the OCG. 
 Breakdown 
 5 Cards per Pack, 15 Packs per Box 
 Includes 70 cards:
 4  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 6  Ultra Rares 
 8  Super Rares 
 15  Rares 
 37  Commons 
 All Ultra Rare and Super Rarecards are also available as  Secret Rares .
