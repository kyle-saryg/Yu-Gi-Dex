      A Genex Medallion.     
 A  Genex Medallion , known as a  Genex Medal  in the Japanese version, is a token issued to each participants in the  Genex Tournament . Whenever a player wins a Duel, they take their opponent's medallions. The player left with all the medallions in the end is the winner.
