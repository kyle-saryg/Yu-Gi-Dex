 Duel Terminal 2  is made up of the OCG sets  Duel Terminal - Justice Strikes Back!!  and  Duel Terminal - Demon Roar God Revival!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 100 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 60 of the cards are new, while 40 are reprints. Includes further support for the " Ice Barrier ", " Genex ", " Mist Valley ", " Ally of Justice ", " Worm ", and " Flamvell " archetypes.
 
 Breakdown
