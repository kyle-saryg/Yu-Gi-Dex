 Dark Necrofear  is a character version of the card  Dark Necrofear . She appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .
 
 Deck 
 Dark Necrofear plays a  Destiny Board  Deck, named "Message of Fear" and "Destiny Board".
