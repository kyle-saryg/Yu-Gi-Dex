 "Monster World"  is the seventh volume of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . It is the last manga titled  Yu-Gi-Oh!  in the English series, as the next volume part of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  series.
 In the Japanese version, this volume went up to  chapter 60 . This was the first chapter in the  Duel Monsters  centered universe, so in the English manga it was excluded and added as chapter 1 of the next volume, which is the  first volume of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist .
