Both players  reveal  their  hands  and  add  1 card from each other's hand to their hand.

   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
Both players show their  hands  to each other. You both select 1 card from each other's hand and add it to your own. When sent to the Graveyard, the cards are placed in the Graveyard of the original owner.
