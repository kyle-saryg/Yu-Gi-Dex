 Once per  turn , while this card is  face-up  on the  field , you can  negate  1  attack  from a  monster  your opponent  controls . If you would take  damage  from a monster  effect   activated  on your opponent's side of the field: You can activate this card from your  hand ; that damage becomes 0, also change all monsters you currently  control  to  face-up   Defense Position .
