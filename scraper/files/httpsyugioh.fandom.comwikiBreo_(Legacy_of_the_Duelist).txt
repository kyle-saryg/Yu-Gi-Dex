 Breo , known as (ブレオ,  Bureo ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Breo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
