The  Arabic, Croatian  and  Greek                      names given are not official.  ( card names ) 
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Trap Hole "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 落 とし 穴 」 .   As its English name contains "Trap Hole", it  may  be treated a member of the "Trap Hole" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
