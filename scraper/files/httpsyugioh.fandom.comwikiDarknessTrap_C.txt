 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Activate  only by the  effect  of " Darkness/Trap B ". When you activate this card: You can  banish  1 card your opponent  controls . Each time another " Darkness "  Continuous Trap Card  is activated, you can banish 1 card your opponent controls. -I activate " Darkness 1 "!-
