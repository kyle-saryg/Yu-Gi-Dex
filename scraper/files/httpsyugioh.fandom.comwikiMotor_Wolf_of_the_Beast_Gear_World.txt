 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 A beast who repeatedly plunders from the people in search of food and gasoline. It's easy to think of him as a lone wolf, but he believes his cruelty is justice, and he's merely doing the best for his gang. Why is howling so heavily ingrained in his heart, even though he's one of the bad guys?
