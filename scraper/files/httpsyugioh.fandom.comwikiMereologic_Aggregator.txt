 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2+  Level  9  monsters If this card is  Xyz Summoned : You can  send  1 monster from your  Extra Deck  to the  GY . You can  target  monsters in your GY up to the number of  materials   attached  to this card;  detach  materials from this card equal to the number targeted, and if you do, attach the targeted monsters to this card as material. If this card is  sent  to the GY: You can target 1  face-up  card on the  field ;  negate  its  effects  until the end of this  turn . You can only use each  effect  of "Mereologic Aggregator"  once per turn .
