 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Activate  this card by  targeting  1  Link Monster  you  control ; place  Satellite Counters  on this card equal to that  monster 's  Link Rating . When that  monster   leaves the field ,  destroy  this card. Neither player can  declare  an  attack .  Once per turn , during your  End Phase , remove 1 Satellite Counter from this card. When the last Satellite Counter(s) is removed from this card, destroy it.
