 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When you  Synchro Summon  " Stardust Dragon ": You can  Special Summon  this card from your  GY  in  Attack Position . Each  turn , the first time this card would be  destroyed by battle , it is not  destroyed .
