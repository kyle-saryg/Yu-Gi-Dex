 3  Level  9  monsters 
 Cannot be  destroyed by battle . When this card is  Special Summoned : Change all  Attack Position  monsters your opponent  controls  to  Defense Position . The  battle positions  of those monsters cannot be changed.  Negate  any  battle damage  you would take from battles involving this card, then inflict an equal amount of  damage  to your opponent, and if you do inflict damage this way, gain that many  Life Points . If this card was Special Summoned, all cards your opponent places on the  field  are  banished  the next  Standby Phase . When this card with no  Xyz Materials  is  destroyed  and sent to the  Graveyard : You can Special Summon this card from your Graveyard only once. If Summoned this way, this card's ATK is equal to the number of banished cards x 1000.
