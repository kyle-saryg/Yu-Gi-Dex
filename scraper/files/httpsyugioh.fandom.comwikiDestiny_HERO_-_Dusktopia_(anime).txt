 " Destiny HERO - Dystopia "  
 1 " Destiny HERO "  monster 
 During your  Main Phase , if this card was  Fusion Summoned  this  turn : You can Fusion Summon 1  Fusion Monster  from your  Extra Deck , using monsters from your  hand  or  field  as  Fusion Materials .  Once per turn , during either player's  turn : You can  target  1 monster on the field; this turn, it cannot be  destroyed by battle , also its  controller  takes no  battle damage  from  attacks  involving it. Once per turn, during either player's turn, if a card or  effect  that would  destroy  a monster(s) on the field is  activated : You can target 1 of those monsters; it is not destroyed by that effect.
