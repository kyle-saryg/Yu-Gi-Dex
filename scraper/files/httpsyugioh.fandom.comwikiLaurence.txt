 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Laurence uses a  Goblin Deck .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Laurence instead uses Decks based on " Warrior Dai Grepher ".
