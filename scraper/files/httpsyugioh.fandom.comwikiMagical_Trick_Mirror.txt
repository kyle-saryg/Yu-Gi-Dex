 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When an opponent's  monster   declares  an  attack :  Target  1  Spell  in your opponent's  GY ;  Set  it to your  field . You can  banish  this card from your GY and  send  1 " Monster Reborn " from your  hand  or Set on your field to the GY;  Special Summon  1 " Obelisk the Tormentor " from your GY in  Defense Position , and if you activated this  effect  during your opponent's  turn , for the rest of this turn while you  control  the Special Summoned monster, all monsters your opponent controls must attack that monster, if able.
