 Crow Hogan  (クロウ・ホーガン,  Kurou Hōgan ) is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Crow Hogan (ARC-V) , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
