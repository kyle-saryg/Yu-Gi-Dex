 Lists 
 
 
 Members 
 
 
 
 
 " with Eyes of Blue " ( 青 き 眼 の  Aokime no ) is a  series  of  Level  1  LIGHT   Spellcaster   Tuner monsters  that  support  " Blue-Eyes " monsters or " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ". They have  effects  that either  target  an  Effect Monster  and send it to the  Graveyard  to allow for a quick  Special Summon  of the high Level "Blue-Eyes" monsters, or effects that activate when targeted by card effects.
 
 Members 
 
 
 Monster with Eyes of Blue 
 Unique effect
 
 
 Maiden 
 Special Summon 1 "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" from your hand, Deck, or Graveyard.
 
 
 Master 
 Shuffle this card from the Graveyard into the Deck, then target 1 Effect monster you control: send the target to the Graveyard and Special Summon 1 "Blue-Eyes" monster from your Graveyard, except the target.
 
 
 Priestess 
 Send 1 Effect Monster you control to the Graveyard, and if you do, add up to 2 "Blue-Eyes" monsters with different names from your Deck to your hand.
 
 
 Protector 
 Target 1 Effect Monster you control; send it to the Graveyard, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Blue-Eyes" monster from your hand.
 
 
 Sage 
 Discard this card, then target 1 Effect Monster you control; send it to the Graveyard, and if you do, Special Summon 1 "Blue-Eyes" monster from your Deck.
 
 Trivia 
 All female monsters' effects activate when targeted, while male monsters target other Effect Monsters
 The male and female's effects can be used effectively with each other to get out more Blue-Eyes.
