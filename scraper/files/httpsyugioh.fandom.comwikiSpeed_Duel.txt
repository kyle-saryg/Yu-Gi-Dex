Speed Duel  may refer to either of the following.
 
 Speed Duel (format) : A format used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! OCG . And a type of Duel introduced in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime . 
 Speed Duel (Duel Terminal) : A mode of play in the  Duel Terminal  machines. 
 Speed Duel (ZEXAL) : A type of Duel introduced in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga .
