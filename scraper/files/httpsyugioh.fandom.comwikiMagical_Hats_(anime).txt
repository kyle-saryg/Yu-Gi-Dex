 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Quick-Play 
 
 Activate  when you  control  a " Magician "  monster .  Set  up to 4 monsters you control (there are still treated as  face-up  and their previous  battle position ). Then, for each monster not Set by this effect, Set 1 "Hat Token" (???-Type/???/Level ???/ATK ???/DEF ???). During your Main Phase, you can shuffle your  Monster Zone . During your Main Phase, you Set 1  Spell / Trap Card  in your hand in place of a "Hat Token" as a Normal Monster (ATK 0/DEF 0). If a "Hat Token" or Spell/Trap Card in your Monster Zone is  targeted  for an attack or  card effect ; the  damage  is treated as 0 and the target is destroyed. If a Spell/Trap Card in your Monster Zone is targeted by your opponent, you can activate its effect(s). If you flip up a monster Set by this effect, send all  "Hat Tokens" to the Graveyard. [note 1]
