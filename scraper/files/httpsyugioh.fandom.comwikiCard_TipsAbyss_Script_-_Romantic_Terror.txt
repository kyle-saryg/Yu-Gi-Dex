 This card can be searched by " Abyss Playhouse - Fantastic Theater ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ", " Quick Booster ", " Mecha Phantom Beast Jaculuslan ", " Spell Calling " and another copy of "Abyss Script - Romantic Terror". 
 This is the only " Abyss Script "  Spell Card  that reliably permits the activation of " Abyss Actors' Curtain Call " on the same turn. However, this requires you to have two " Abyss Actor " monsters face-up in your  Extra Deck  (one to be summoned by this card, the other to be added to your hand by "Curtain Call") as well as another "Abyss Actor"  Pendulum Monster  face-up on your field with a name that differs from the ones in your Extra Deck.
