 Inoso  is a character who appears in the  Tag Force  series . He is a parody of  Roland  (who is known as Isono in Japan) and works for  UmiumaCorp  as the right-hand man of  Emperor.Umiuma .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , he arrives on the island alongside Umiuma and  Mokuma  after the player has completed the game with three of the Tier 1 characters as partners. He requests the player's assistance in UmiumaCorp's feud with  Sanzyudai Yuki . Should the player agree, after winning a Tag Duel with Inoso as a partner, he and his companions can be selected as partners for the Tag Force tournament.
 
 Tag Force 2 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2 , Inoso can appear in a special story event at the  Ra Yellow  dorm, partnered with  Senjome , if either  Syrus  or  Tyranno  has been defeated in duels at least 15 times. After defeating them, both Inoso and Senjome can be selected as tag partners.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Inoso uses a  Skull Servant Deck  featuring a variety of card effects which send them to the Graveyard in order to power up " King of the Skull Servants " and " Chaos Necromancer ".
 In later installments, Inoso uses  Rock  Decks , with many of his monsters being included in the  The Lost Millennium  set and the  Structure Deck: Invincible Fortress .
