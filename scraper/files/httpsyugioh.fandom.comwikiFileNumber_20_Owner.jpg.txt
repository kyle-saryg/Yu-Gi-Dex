 Number 20's holder  is an unnamed criminal in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga, and was the original owner of " No. 20: Giga-Brilliant " in the manga.

 Biography 
 He threatened people in a shopping mall. As his demands for money weren't fulfilled, he kicked an  obot . At that moment the time stopped, and  Kaito Tenjo , a  Numbers Hunter , came gliding with his robot,  Orbital 7 . Kaito defeated him in a Duel with " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon ". As result, he claimed both the criminal's "Number" card and soul with  Photon Hand , causing him to age incredibly fast. [1] 
 
 Deck 
 The only shown card in this Duelist's Deck was " No. 20: Giga-Brilliant ".
