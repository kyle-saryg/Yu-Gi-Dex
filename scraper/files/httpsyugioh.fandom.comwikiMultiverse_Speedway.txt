      Multiverse Speedway.     
 The  Multiverse Speedway , known as the  Dimension Highway  in the Japanese version, is a  Turbo Dueling  course made of  Solid Vision .
 
 Overview 
 When activated, the Multiverse Speedway materializes in mid-air and spreads out through the  dimensional corridor gates  and expands throughout the  Four Dimensions . It was first used by  Declan Akaba  for  Yuya  and  Jack Atlas ' Duel in order help  Riley  smile and show the people of every dimension the Ultimate  Turbo Duel . [1]
