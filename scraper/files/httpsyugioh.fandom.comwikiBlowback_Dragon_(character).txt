 Blowback Dragon  is a character version of the card, " Blowback Dragon ". It appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008  in both the adventure mode (Duel World) and the World Championship mode.
 
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2008 , Blowback Dragon uses a  coin toss  Deck called  Gambling Addiction , featuring cards such as " Sasuke Samurai #4 ", " Snipe Hunter " and " Second Coin Toss ".
