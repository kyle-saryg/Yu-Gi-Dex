 Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants 
 War of the Giants: Round 2 
 Battle Pack 3: Monster League 
 
 
 
 
 
 War of the Giants: Round 2  is an expansion pack, designed specifically to be used in combination with  Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants  to adapt the latter for the  Draft environment . It was only released in North America; its European equivalent is  War of the Giants Reinforcements , which includes 1 extra card (" Zubaba General ").

 Features 
 Supplements the original  Battle Pack 2  with  monster  removal  effects ,  Spell / Trap  removal effects, and single- Tribute  monsters, all of which were previously scarce. 
 Includes a "bonus"  Ultra Rare  card per pack excluded from drafting. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 16 cards:
 
 1  Ultra Rare  card 
 6  Super Rare  cards 
 9  Common  cards 
 The set contains 103 cards:
 
 5  Ultra Rare  cards 
 43  Super Rare  cards 
 55  Common  cards 
 The cards from  Battle Pack 2  can also be found in the box, but are not part of this named set.
