 First erratum 
 
 
 Effects of  LIGHT  monsters  that battled this card  are  negated. 
 Any  LIGHT  monster  that  has  battled this card  has its effects  negated  (starting after damage calculation) .

 Third erratum 
 
 
 Effects of  LIGHT  monsters  that battled this card  are  negated. 
 If this card battles a LIGHT monster, after damage calculation: Negate the effects of that monster (including in the GY).
