 This card can be  searched  by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " The Despair Uranus " and " Mermail Abyssmegalo ". 
 You can recycle this card with " Mermail Abyssleed ". 
 Use this card to bait Spell/Trap removal by Special Summoning " Mermail Abysshilde " or " Mermail Abysslinde ", so that when they are destroyed, you can summon another " Mermail " monster from your hand or Deck respectively.
