 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament " and " Mermail Abyssmegalo ". 
 " Mermail Abyssleed " can return this card to your hand from your Graveyard. 
 " Mermail Abysshilde ", " Mermail Abysslinde ", " Mermail Abysstrite " and " Mermail Abyssnerei " all have effects that activate when destroyed and/or sent to the Graveyard, which this card cannot negate. 
 Use " Wonder Xyz " to set up an  Xyz Summon  on the same turn that you activate this card. Or, you can forego "Wonder Xyz" by having " Dust Tornado " ready to Set this card during your opponent's turn so that you can use it on your turn.
 You can also set up a  Tribute Summon  on the same turn by activating " Arrivalrivals ", " Escalation of the Monarchs " or " Free-Range Monsters ".
