 In  episode 41 ,  Alexis Rhodes  uses this card during her Duel against  Titan . After Alexis summons " Cyber Tutu ", she activates this card to destroy  Titan 's  face-down  " Des Counterblow ", which would have  destroyed  "Cyber Tutu" if it had  attacked   directly . 
 In the original anime, Alexis specified this card could be used when a "Ballerina-type" monster was summoned.
