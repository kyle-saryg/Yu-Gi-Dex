 Sealed Play Battle Kit 2  is a  Battle Kit  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the second Battle Kit in the  TCG ' s Battle Kit series, following  Sealed Play Battle Kit . The kit includes 10 booster packs of " Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants ", and 1 of 3 "Legendary  God Card   playmats .
