 Premium Collection 
 Zexal Collection Tin 
 
 
 
 
 
 Three 4-card promo packs (all foil cards) + 2 variant cards - total of 14 foils in each tin.
The 12 random foil cards will make up a 23-card set, which includes new cards from each of the 4  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series. The 2 variant cards will be the same in each tin.
 " This will be the only chance for gamers to get their hands on these Premium collector cards as they will be sold exclusively in this tin release! " - Konami [ citation needed ]
