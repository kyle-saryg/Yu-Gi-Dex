Main card page: " Number 39: Utopia Beyond " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Utopia "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 希 望 皇 ホープ」 .   As its English name contains "Utopia", it  may  be treated a member of the "Utopia" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Number 39: Utopia "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 Ｎｏ. ３９  希 望 皇 ホープ」 .   As its English name contains "Number 39: Utopia", it  may  be treated a member of the "Number 39: Utopia" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Top "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 Ｔ 」 .   As its English name contains "Top", it  may  be treated a member of the "Top" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
