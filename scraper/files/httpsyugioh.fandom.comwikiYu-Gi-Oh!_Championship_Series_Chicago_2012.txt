 Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series Chicago 2012  was a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series  tournament held at Donald E. Stephens Convention Center in Chicago, Illinois, United States from April 14 to 15, 2012. [1]  It was won by Aaron Noel. [2]

 Events 
 Main event 
 After the sixth round, seventeen people tied first undefeated on 18 points. [6]  After the seventh round, eight tied first undefeated on 21 points. [7]  After the ninth round, 128 of the top scoring players got to continue into the second day to participate in two more rounds of Swiss. [8]  After the eleventh round, thirty two of the top scoring players got to continue to the single-elimination stage. [5] 
 The main event was won by Aaron Noel, who used a " Dino Rabbit " Deck. [2]  He defeated Daniel Nunnally, who also used a "Dino Rabbit" Deck, 2-1 in the finals. [3] 
 
 Side events 
 Side events included  Dragon Duel , 3 vs. 3 Team Dueling,  Attack of the Giant Card!! , Classic Drafts,  Dawn of the Xyz  Starter tournaments, Retro Drafts,  Win-A-Mats  and an eight-player single-elimination  Dragons Collide   Sealed  Deck tournament. [1] 
 On the first day, the Dragon Duel was won by Michael Sakowicz. On the second day, it was won by Brian D. Ahle. [2] 
 The first  Attack of the Giant Card!!  was won by Matthew White, who used a " Wind-Up " Deck and won the giant " Tour Bus From the Underworld " card. [9]  The second was won by Steffon Bizzell, who used a " Chaos Dragon " Deck and won the giant " Inzektor Giga-Mantis " card. [10] 
 The public events had two playoffs; one between the players who had accumulated the most points in the public events and one between two randomly chosen players. The winner of the random playoff was Brian Small, who used a " HERO " Deck. He defeated Andrew Henderson, who used a " Dino Rabbit " Deck. [11]
