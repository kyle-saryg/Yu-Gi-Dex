 Vampire Lord  is a character version of the card, " Vampire Lord ".
 Vampire Lord appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , he appears as a Tag Duel opponent and his Tag partner is  Vampire Lady . Their team name is  Ladies and Gents . By beating them in World four Darkness and they can be unlocked in Free Tag Duel.
