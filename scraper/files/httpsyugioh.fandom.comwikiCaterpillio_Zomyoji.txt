 Caterpillio Zomyoji  (キャタピリオ  Zōmyōji Kyatapirio ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS . He is a member of  Goha 6th Elementary 's  Heavy Cavalry Duel Club , the mechanic of the group.
