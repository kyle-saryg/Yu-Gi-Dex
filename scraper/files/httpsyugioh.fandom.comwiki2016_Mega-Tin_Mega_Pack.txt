 2016 Mega-Tin Mega Pack  or  Mega Tin 2016 Mega Pack  is a  Mega Pack  set released with the  2016 Mega-Tins .
 The set contains cards, made up of a year's worth of  Booster Packs  from the sets  Crossed Souls ,  Clash of Rebellions ,  Dimension of Chaos , and  Breakers of Shadow .
