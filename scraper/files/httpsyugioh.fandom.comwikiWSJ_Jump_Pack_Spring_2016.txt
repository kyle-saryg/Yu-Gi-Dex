 The  Weekly Shonen Jump  Jump Pack Spring 2016  is an issue of WSJ Jump Pack that contains the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga. This issue of  Jump Pack 2016  can be found at the Scholastic Book Fairs.
 This issue of  Jump Pack 2016  also included a code for a 1-month weekly digital subscription to Weekly Shonen Jump, 1  Ultra Rare  " Blue-Eyes White Dragon "  promotional card , 1 " Demo Deck ", and guide/mat. [1]
