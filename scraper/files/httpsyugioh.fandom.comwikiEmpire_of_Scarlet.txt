 Empire of Scarlet  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on August 08,2018, 8:00 AM (EST). 
 
 Description 
 Cries of anguish echo through the night. A symphony of terror under the scarlet moon. 
 
 Featuring: 
" Vampire Scarlet Scourge ," New " Vampire " monsters are here! They allow you to Special Summon monsters they destroyed by battle at the end of the Battle Phase! 
" Inzektor Centipede ," " Inzektor " monsters fight by equipping each other! When a card equipped to this monster is sent to the Graveyard, you can add 1 "Inzektor" monster from your Deck to your hand! 
" Mithra the Thunder Vassal ," A monster that Special Summons itself from your hand! If it is Tributed for a Tribute Summon, you can perform another Tribute Summon that turn! 
" Flash of the Forbidden Spell ," This Spell Card can destroy all of your opponent's monsters if all of your opponent's Monster Zones are filled up. If your opponent controls many powerful monsters, this card to turn the tides of the Duel! 
A total of 40 types of Cards: 3  UR , 10  SR , 17  R , and 20  N .
 
 
 Each box of  Empire of Scarlet  contains 100 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 9-10 copies
