 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Target  1 opponent’s  direct   attacking   monster :  Special Summon  1 " Cubic " monster from your  hand . Special Summon 2 monsters with the same name as the targeted monster from your opponent’s hand,  Graveyard , and  Deck  to your opponent’s  field . The targeted monster and all monsters with the same as it your opponent controls has their  ATK  become 0, and place 1  Cubic Counter  on each of them. A monster with Cubic Counter has its  effects   negated , and it cannot attack. [1] [2] 
 
 4K Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English card uses the same lore as the OCG/TCG card. The Japanese card and reference sources state the effect differently.):</mobile> When an opponent's  monster   declares  a  direct attack :  Target  the  attacking  monster, your opponent  Special Summons  in  Attack Position  as many monsters as possible from their  hand ,  Deck  and/or  Graveyard  with the same name, also the  ATK  of the targeted monster and those Special Summoned monster(s) becomes 0 and place 1  Cubic Counter  on each (Monsters with Cubic Counters cannot attack, also  negate  their  effects ). Also, after that, if any Cubic Counters were placed by this effect, Special Summon 1  Level  4 or lower " Cubic " monster from your hand,  ignoring its Summoning conditions .

 Lore 
 
 
 Japanese 
 方界輪廻 
 ①：相手モンスターの直接攻撃宣言時に、その攻撃モンスター１体を対象として発動できる。相手はその同名モンスターを可能な限り自身の手札・デッキ・墓地から特殊召喚する。対象のモンスター及びこの効果で特殊召喚したモンスターの攻撃力は０になり、それらのモンスターに方界カウンターを１つずつ置く。方界カウンターが置かれたモンスターは攻撃できず、効果は無効化される。その後、自分の手札から「方界」モンスター１体を特殊召喚する。
