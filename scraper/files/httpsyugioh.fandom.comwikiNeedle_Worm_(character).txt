 Needle Worm  is a character version of the card " Needle Worm ". It appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , this character and  Morphing Jar  are unlocked as Tag Duel opponents. Their Tag Team name is  Deck Crushers .
