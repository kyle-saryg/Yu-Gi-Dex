 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  Normal  or  Special Summoned , or  flipped   face-up : You can  target  up to 2 face-up  monsters  on the  field ; change them to  face-down   Defense Position . Opponent's monsters changed to face-down Defense Position by this  effect  cannot change their  battle positions . If another monster(s) on the  field  is flipped face-up while this monster is on the  field : You can target 1 card your opponent  controls ;  destroy  it. You can only use each effect of "Jio the Stacked Ninja"  once per turn .
