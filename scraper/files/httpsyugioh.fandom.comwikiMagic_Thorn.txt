 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When your opponent's card(s) in their  hand  are  discarded  to the  Graveyard , inflict 500 points of  damage  to their  Life Points  for each card that was discarded. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Lorsque la ou les cartes de la main de votre adversaire sont défaussées au Cimetière, infligez 500 points de dommages à ses Life Points pour chaque carte défaussée. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Wenn Karten aus der  Hand  deines Gegners auf den  Friedhof   abgeworfen  werden, füge den  Life Points  deines Gegners für jede abgeworfene Karte 500 Punkte  Schaden  zu. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Quando 1 o più carte in mano al tuo avversario vengono scartate al Cimitero, infliggi 500 danni ai suoi Life Points per ogni carta scartata. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Portuguese 
 
 
 Quando uma ou mais cartas na mão do seu oponente são descartadas ao Cemitério, inflija 500 de dano ao seu oponente para cada carta descartada. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Spanish 
 
 
 Cuando la mano de tu adversario es descartada al Cementerio, inflige 500 puntos de daño a los Life Points de tu adversario por cada carta descartada.

 相手の手札が墓地へ捨てられた時、捨てたカードの数×５００ポイントダメージを相手ライフに与える。

 對方的手卡丟棄去墓地時，每丟棄1張卡就給予對方基本分500分的傷害。

 상대의 패가 묘지에 버려졌을 때,  버린 카드 1장당 500 포인트 데미지를 상대 라이프에 준다. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
You can inflict 500 points of  damage  per card to your opponent's  Life Points  when your opponent's cards are  discarded  to the  Graveyard  by the  effects  of a  Magic ,  Trap  or  Effect   Monster Cards . 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
You can inflict 500 points of damage per card to your opponent's Life Points when your opponent's cards are discarded to the Graveyard by the effects of Spell, Trap or Effect Monster Cards.
