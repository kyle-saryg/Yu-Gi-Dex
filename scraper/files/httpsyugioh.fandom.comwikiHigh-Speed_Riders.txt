 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Akiza Izinski ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Sayer , and  Officer 227 . 
 Introduces the first support for the " Red Dragon Archfiend ", " PSY-Frame ", and " Speedroid " archetypes to the  TCG . 
 Breakdown
