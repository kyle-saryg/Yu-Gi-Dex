 Premium Pack Vol.3  is a Korean pack, containing a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean. The set contains 30  Ultra Rare  cards, 6 of which are also avalible as  Secret Rares .
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 30 cards or 36 in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 6  Secret Rare 
 30  Ultra Rare
