 Dragons of Legend  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It includes dozens of famous cards from the first four  Yu-Gi-Oh!  TV series. All cards in this Booster Pack, except " Guardian Eatos ", had not been previously released in the  TCG .

 Features 
 Brand new cards from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . 
 Introduces cards used by  Yami Yugi ,  Rafael ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Chumley Huffington ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Franz ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Tetsu Trudge ,   Crow Hogan ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Anna Kaboom ,  Nash ,  Vector , and  Girag . 
 New members and/or support for the " Blackwing ", " Galaxy ", " Guardian ", " Noble Arms ", " Number ", " Number C ", " Photon ", " Polymerization ", " Rank-Up-Magic ", " roid " and " Utopia "  archetypes , as well as the " Hand "  series . 
 Also includes the first support for the " Dark Magician ", " Stardust " and " Winged Kuriboh ", archetypes. There is also new support for  DIVINE - Attribute  monsters. 
 Introduces the " Legendary Dragon " and " Legendary Knight " archetypes.
