 Notes 
 
 ↑   Despite having no icon, this card was a  Continuous Spell Card  in the anime. 
 
 ↑   Despite having the Japanese lore of its  OCG  counterpart , "Shine Palace" did not function that way in the anime. Instead, it had the following effective effect: 
"Activate only if you control " Toon World ". Increases the  ATK  of all  LIGHT   monsters  you  control  by 500."
