 Ryou Bakura  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  and  World Championship  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Bakura Ryou , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  and  anime .
 Bakura is a friend of  Yugi Muto , who possess the  Millennium Ring , causing his body to house the soul of  Yami Bakura .
 
 Appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , Bakura appears as a Level 1 opponent, available from the start.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories , Bakura appears a Level 1 opponent, available from the start. His other self,  Dark Bakura , also appears.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters III: Tri-Holy God Advent  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories , Bakura only appears possessed by Yami Bakura.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , Bakura only appears possessed by Yami Bakura.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 5 EX 1  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Eternal Duelist Soul , Bakura appears as a Level 1 opponent, available from the start. His other self, Yami Bakura, also appears.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 6 EX 2 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monster International  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Worldwide Edition: Stairway to the Destined Duel , Bakura is available from the start, but gets possessed by Yami Bakura, as soon as the player challenges him to a Duel.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 7: The Duelcity Legend  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards , the player can Duel Bakura outside the art gallery.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 8: Reshef of Destruction  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction , Bakura only appears possessed by Yami Bakura.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters EX 3  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship Tournament 2004 , Bakura is a Level 1 opponent, available from the start. His other self, Yami Bakura, also appears.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters International 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 7 Trials to Glory: World Championship Tournament 2005 , the player Duels Bakura at the coliseum. He also faces the player in the Limitation Tournament, where the player cannot use  DARK  monsters, and is a potential round one opponent in the Sugoroku Tournament. His other self, Yami Bakura, also appears.
