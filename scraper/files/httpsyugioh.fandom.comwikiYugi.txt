Yugi  can refer to any of the following characters:
 
 Yugi Muto : The  series protagonist. 
 Yami Yugi : The spirit of the Pharaoh, who has been sealed in the Millennium Puzzle and resides in Yugi's body. 
 Henry "Yugi" Tudor : the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  incarnation of Yugi.
