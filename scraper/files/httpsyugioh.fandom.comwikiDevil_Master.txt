 Devil Master  is  Kujirada 's  Digital Pet  in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . In is based on  Kujirada's Digital Pet  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Biography 
 Devil Master was a Gold Pet, which was a more expensive Digital Pet with superior properties to a regular Digital Pet. Kujirada stole it from a shop. [1] 
 Kujirada tried making Devil Master stronger in order to win a competition. He did this by swiping other people's pets and feeding them to Devil Master. Among Devil Master's meals were  Sumomo-chan  and  Joe . Kujirada tried to feed  U2  to it, but  Hiroto Honda  arrived, after spending three sleepless nights taking care of  Super Strawberry . Honda connected Super Strawberry to Devil Master and Super Strawberry swept Devil Master away. [1]
