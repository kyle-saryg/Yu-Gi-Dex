 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 In  chapter 2 ,  Yuma Tsukumo  plays this card against  Tokunosuke Omoteura . When Normal Summoned, it is changed to Defense Position, due to Tokunosuke's " Level Limit - Area B ". This card and " Gagaga Magician " are then used to  Xyz Summon  " Number 39: Utopia ". 
 Scripted Duels 
 In  North American WCQ 2012 Special: Joey vs. Seto Kaiba . Kaiba discards this card via " Graceful Charity " and then  Special Summons  it with " Monster Reborn ". It attacks and destroys Joey's " Rocket Warrior ". It would later be destroyed by battle against Joey's " Panther Warrior ". Kaiba then banishes this card with " Soul Demolition " and Special Summons it again with " Dimension Fusion ". It played no further role in the duel. 
 In  World Championship 2016 Special: Yugi vs. Kaiba , Kaiba Normal Summons this card on the fourth turn of the duel. This card attacks and destroys " Dark Magician " (who was affected by " Kaiser Vorse Raider " and " Shadow Spell "), and is later destroyed by Yugi's " Black Luster Soldier - Envoy of the Beginning ".
