 Anime Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was given two names:
 العملاق المدرّع القديم =  Old/Ancient Armored Giant 
 العملاق المسلّح القديم =  Old/Ancient Equipped Giant 
 The given approximated Arabic name (عملاق التّروس الأثريّة) translates to  Giant of Ancient/Antique Gears . 
 In the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  first season American Spanish dub, this card is named " Golem de Engranajes Ancestrales ", meaning " Ancestral Gear Golem ". 
 The official  Greek  name given translates to " Ancient Mechanical Golem ".
