 These cards were handed out to participants of the 2013 Duelist League. The Duelist League is independent of age and open for all duelists. Once a Duelist has won three events, he or she will not participate in future Duelist League events in the same business. Not affected by this rule are duelists who were born in 2000 or later. All cards were available in purple, red, green, and blue  Rare  versions.
