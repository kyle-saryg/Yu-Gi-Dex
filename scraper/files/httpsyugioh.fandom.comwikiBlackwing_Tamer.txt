 " Blackwing Tamer ", known as " Black Feather Tamer " ( ＢＦ Ｔ   Burakkufezā Teimā  ) in Japan, is a sub- archetype  of the standard " Blackwing " archetype. It is used by  Crow Hogan  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . It only has one member - " Blackwing Tamer - Obsidian Hawk Joe " - and one support card - " Black Cyclone ".
