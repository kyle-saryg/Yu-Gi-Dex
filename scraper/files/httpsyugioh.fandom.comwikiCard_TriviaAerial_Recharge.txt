 " Mecha Phantom Beast Hamstrat " and " Mecha Phantom Beast Megaraptor " appear in this card's artwork.
 The former can be seen refueling the latter, in reference to this card's name. 
 This card's artwork resembles the anime only card, " Double Type Rescue ".
