 Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  promotional cards , are a set of  Prismatic Secret Rare  cards that came with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories .
 In North America two different sets were released. The first contained " Exodia the Forbidden One ", " Dark Magician " and " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ". The second set contained " Acid Trap Hole ", " Seiyaryu ", and " Salamandra ".
 The manual for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories  shows a print of " Magician of Black Chaos " with the  Set Number   DDS-000 .
 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 6  Prismatic Secret Rare  cards, consisting of:
 
 3  Normal Monsters 
 1  Effect Monster 
 1  Spell Card 
 1  Trap Card
