 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 
 
 
 Chie Ookawachi  is the name of a  Domino City  resident who was one of  Trueman's victims . Her name appeared on a list of names. Each name disappeared after  Trueman  sent them to the  World of Darkness .
