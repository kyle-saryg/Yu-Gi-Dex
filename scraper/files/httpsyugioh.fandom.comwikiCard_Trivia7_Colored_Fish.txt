 This was one of the first 1800  ATK   Level  4 or lower  monsters . 
 This monster appears in the artworks of " Forgotten Temple of the Deep ", " Fish and Kicks ", " Fish and Swaps ", " Single Purchase " and " Catastrophe Draw ". 
 Ironically, the artwork of "Fish and Swaps" contradicts this monster's flavor text.
