 The Agent of Mystery - Earth  is a character version of the card " The Agent of Mystery - Earth ". 
 She appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Her partner ID is P049. She also appears as an opponent at Stage 10-4 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, her partner ID was P022 instead.

 Decks 
 A Fairy- Type   Deck  that adds " Herald of Perfection " to "The Agent". Proceed along  Synchro Summons  from "The Agent of Mystery - Earth," eventually aiming for the successful  Ritual Summon  of "Herald of Perfection"!
