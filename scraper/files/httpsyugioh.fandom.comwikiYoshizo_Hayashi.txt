 Biography 
 Yoshizo and his friends lived in a rural area in Japan, when  Taro Yamashita , sees a newspaper in a story about the  WRGP  and has the idea of entering the tournament with Yoshizo and  Jinbei Tanigawa . Although Jinbei does not think that something is possible. 
 They buy a  Duel Runner , but it was not only in poor condition but with parts missing. Seeing Taro so dedicated to fix it, his father shows another Duel Runner, which the boy can fix. 
 Jinbei arranges some cards, but they were all weak and without  effects . Taro tries to create a strategy using the cards, but can't. 
 Until seeing a card in the middle of the others and has an idea. They go to the WRGP and reach the finals, but the  Diablo  damaged their Duel Runner. 
 In one test, they find  Leo , offering the help of  Yusei , despite the insistence of Jinbei not to receive his help, they agree and Yusei arranges the Duel Runner. 
 But in a twist of fate, they discover that they will face  Team 5D's  in the next round. Being the First Wheeler in the team, he faces  Jack Atlas .
 
 Deck 
 Yoshizo uses a  Stall / Normal Monster  Deck  focused around stalling through cards such as " Hand-Holding Genie " and " Scrum Force " to lockdown the opponent's moves. He also utilizes the  burn  effect of " Speed World 2 " to deal damage without disrupting his defensive strategy.
