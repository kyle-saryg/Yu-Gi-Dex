 Premium Pack 
 Premium Pack 2 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Premium Pack 2  is a pack in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game . It has been released in English, French, German, Italian and Spanish.
 Premium Pack 2  contains cards that had not previously been released in the  TCG , including many cards original to the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga . This pack featured all the cards from  OCG   Premium Pack 10  and  Premium Pack 11  (except " Shuttleroid "), plus 3 additional cards which had previously been released in Japanese in other  Premium Packs .
 As per the first  Premium Pack , all cards could be obtained as  Secret Rares , as  misprints , although they were not widely released as such.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contained 5 cards, 2  Secret Rares  and 3  Super Rares .
