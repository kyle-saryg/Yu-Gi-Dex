 Duelist Legacy Volume.5 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duelist Legacy Volume.5  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in the  Duelist Legacy  series. It contains reprints of cards from  Struggle of Chaos ,  Mythological Age  and  Pharaonic Guardian .

 Trivia 
 Yami Yugi 's appearance on the cover of this booster pack is taken from the title page for  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 148: " One-Turn Kill " . This was also used earlier for  Struggle of Chaos  and later for  Ancient Sanctuary . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack, and 30 packs per box.
