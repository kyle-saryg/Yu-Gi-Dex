 Kaito  (天城カイト,  Tenjō Kaito )  is a alternate incarnation of  Kaito Tenjo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga  and  anime , appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL spin-off manga . [1]

 Design 
 Appearance 
       Full body view of Kaito.     
 Kaito appears similar to anime and manga appearance with light grey eyes, pale skin and blonde hair that is curled up in a point with a dark green spiked front, but in a chibi style form.
 
 
 Biography 
 Kaito challenged  Shark , wishing to test him. Shark was able to defeat "Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon", Kaito calls the "test" off, and the Duel ends with no result. [1]  Shark is approved in Kaito's test, and now, Yuma is the next target.
 
 Deck 
 Kaito plays a  " Photon " Deck , primarily focused on  Summoning  his " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon " as quickly as possible through cards such as " Paladin of Photon Dragon " and " Starliege Lord Galaxion ". He also uses cards such as " Dimension Wanderer " to further support it. Later on, Kaito uses cards like " Galaxy Knight " and " Galaxy Wizard " to easily  Xyz Summon  his " Neo Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon ".
