 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 Activate only   when  a Reptile-Type monster you control is destroyed by battle with an opponent's attacking monster and sent to the Graveyard .  Take control of the  attacking  monster, and  treat  it  as a  Reptile-Type  monster . 
 When  a Reptile-Type monster you control is destroyed by battle with an opponent's attacking monster and sent to the Graveyard :  Take control of the  opponent's  monster, and  if you do,  it  becomes  Reptile-Type.
