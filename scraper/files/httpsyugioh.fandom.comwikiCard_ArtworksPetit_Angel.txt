 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , "Petit Angel" was drawn with a different artwork. 
 In international video games, and the  Legendary Collection   Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon  reprint, "Petit Angel" had its halo removed to avoid religious connotations.
