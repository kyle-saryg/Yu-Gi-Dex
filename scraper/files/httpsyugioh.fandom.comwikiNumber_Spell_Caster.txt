 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Number "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 Ｎｏ. 」 .   As its English name contains "Number", it  may  be treated a member of the "Number" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
