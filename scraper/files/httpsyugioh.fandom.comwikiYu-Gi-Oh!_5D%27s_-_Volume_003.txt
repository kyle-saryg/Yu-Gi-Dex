 Blurb 
 This section, or a part of it, has been transcluded from  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's - Volume 001 ; any changes involving it should be made  there  instead.  /  
 In New Domino City, the hottest game in town is the Turbo Duel, fought from blazingly fast motorcycles called Duel Runners. On the outskirts of New Domino City, in a district known as Satellite, a new Turbo Duel hero emerges—Yusei Fudo! On his custom-built Duel Runner, Yusei takes on all challengers, fighting for his friends and the future of Satellite! [1] 
 Duel Dragons!! 
 Yusei has made it into the second round of the D1 Grand Prix, but his friend Sect has made a dark pact with the ominous Skeleton Knight in exchange for the powerful Shadow Card. Yusei learns that he'll need a special card to free Sect, and the only way to get it is to win the tournament! [2] 
 
 Story 
 New Domino City, in the year 20XX, Turbo Duels, fought from the seats of motorcycled-shaped duel disks called "Duel Runners," are the hottest game in town. 
 Satellite is the birthplace of the Duel Runner, and Yusei Fudo is the toughest Duelist in it. Abruptly challenged to a duel by Jack Atlas, Yusei crashed out and lost, and swore to duel Jack again. Now Yusei has entered the D1 Grand Prix for a chance to confront Jack again!! As the strongmen of the Dueling world slug it out, Yusei wins his second duel in style! But something strange is happening to Sect! [2] 
 
 In the Next Volume… 
 The first-round finalists head for a deserted island that will be the site of the next round of the D1 Grand Prix, Yusei wonders about the fate of his friend Sect and how he can save him, but first he will have to face a field of tough competitors, starting with his rival from the first round, Crow Hogan! 
 
 Messages 
 Message from Masahiro Hikokubo 
 So, about the Chinese character for "dragon"… I'm using both variations, the complicated one "龍" for Chinese (long-bodied) dragons, and the simpler one "竜" for Western (short-bodied) dragons, but I don't know if that's how it should be. It anybody knows, tell me! [2] 
 
 Message from Masashi Sato 
 I turned around, and volume 3's on sale!! The series has been running for more than two years already!! I want to keep on racing through it with Yusei and company! [2] 
 
 Character 
 Info below is retrieved from Viz Media. [2] 
 
 Yusei Fudo  - A Turbo Duelist who rides a Duel Runner. He's the toughest duelist in the satellite district. 
 Akiza Izinki  - A Turbo Duelist who holds the title "Queen of Queens". 
 Jack Atlas  - A Turbo Duelist known as "the King," and feared by all around him. 
 Crow Hogan  - A super high-speed synchro-user, nicknamed "The Black Whirlwind". 
 Sect  - He's like a kid brother to Yusei. His goal is to be Yusei in a Turbo Duel.
