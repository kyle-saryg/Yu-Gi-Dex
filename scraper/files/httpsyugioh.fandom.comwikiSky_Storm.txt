 Sky Storm  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from January 10, 2013. Each pack costs   10. But, as of November 18, 2013, the cost of the pack becomes   5.
 This collection includes Spellcaster, Winged Beast and Thunder Monsters as well as some Continuous Spells.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 9 cards.
 The set contains 85 cards in total, including:
 Guaranteed:
 
 5  Commons 
 3  Rares 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 Bonus Chance:
 
 1:10  BAM Legend 
 As of November 18, 2013, the new ratios of the pack is as follows:
 Guaranteed:
 
 5  Commons 
 3  Rares 
 1  Ultra Rare 
 Bonus Chance:
 
 1:4  BAM Legend
