 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During the  Main Phase , if you  control  a " Traptrix "  monster  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand , also you cannot Special Summon monsters from the  Extra Deck  for the rest of this  turn , except  Insect  and  Plant  monsters. You can only use this  effect  of "Traptrix Arachnocampa"  once per turn .  Unaffected  by the effects of " Hole "  Normal Traps . The first time each  Set  card in your  Spell & Trap Zone  would be  destroyed  by card effect each turn, it is not destroyed.
