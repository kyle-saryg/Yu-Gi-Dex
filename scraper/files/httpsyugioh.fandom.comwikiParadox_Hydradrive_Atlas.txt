 If this card is  Special Summoned : You can roll a six-sided die, and this card becomes the  Attribute  that corresponds to the result. ● 1:  EARTH  ● 2:  WATER  ● 3:  FIRE  ● 4:  WIND  ● 5:  LIGHT  ● 6:  DARK During your Main Phase: You can roll a six-sided die, and Special Summon 1 "Hydradrive" Link Monster from your Extra Deck with the same Link Rating as the result to your zone this card points to. You can only use this effect of "Paradox Hydradrive Atlas" once per turn. Monsters this card points to become the same Attribute as this card. While another monster with the same Atribute as this card is on the field, this card is unaffected by your opponent's card effects, also it cannot be targeted for attacks.
