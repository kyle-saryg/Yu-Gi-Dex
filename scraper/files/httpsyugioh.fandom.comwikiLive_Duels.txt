 Live Duels  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from April 18, 2014. Unlike the other Booster Packs, you cannot buy this pack directly from the shop. All cards from this set are available as a reward from the  Coliseum .
