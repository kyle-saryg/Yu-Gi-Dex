 The  Duel Academy  is the sixth and final arc of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . It covers the  Lancers  and their allies invading  Duel Academy  to rescue  Zuzu ,  Celina ,  Lulu , and  Rin  from  the Professor 's clutches while also learning the truth behind the  Four Dimensions , as well as the Duel against  Z-ARC  and  Declan Akaba  to restore  Zuzu Boyle  and  Yuya Sakaki  from their original selves.
