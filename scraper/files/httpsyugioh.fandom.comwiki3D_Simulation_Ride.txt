      The 3D Simulation Ride     
 The  3D Simulation Ride , known as the  3D Motion Ride  ( ３ Ｄ モーションライド  Surī Dī Mōshon Raido ) in the Japanese version, is an attraction at  Kaiba Land  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . [1] [2] 
 It features lifelike  Solid Vision  projections.  Yugi Mutou  and  Katsuya Jonouchi  went on this ride at the  Kaiba Land grand opening  and were frightened by the realistic monsters it generated. [1]
