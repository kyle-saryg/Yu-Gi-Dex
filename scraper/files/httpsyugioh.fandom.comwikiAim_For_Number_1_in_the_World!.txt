 " Aim For Number 1 in the World!  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  Special Featuring Kouhei Uchimura " is a special hour-long, non-canon episode of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . It first aired in Japan on September 17, 2012. 
 Such a special is a through documentation of the " Yu-Gi-Oh! " Card Game World Tournament! What is the fate of the Japanese boys aiming for the top of the world? Furthermore, there's a message and special animation featuring  Kohei Uchimura , a recent Gold Medalist.

 Featured Duels 
 Yuma Tsukumo vs. Astral 
 Turn 1: Yuma 
 Yuma 's  hand  contains " Stinging Swordsman ", " Flelf ", " Gagaga Girl ", " Wonder Wand " and " Mirror Force ". He  draws  " Monster Reborn ". He  Normal Summons  " Gagaga Girl " (1000/800).
 Turn 2: Astral 
 Astral 's hand contains " Gogogo Golem ", " Ganbara Knight ", " Goblindbergh ", " Monster  Slots " and " Kurivolt ". He draws " Cross Attack ". He Normal Summons "Gogogo Golem" (1800/1500). "Gogogo Golem"  attacks  and  destroys  "Gagaga Girl" (Yuma 4000 → 3200)
 Turn 3: Yuma 
Yuma draws and Normal Summons " Gagaga Magician " (1500/1000). He  equips  "Gagaga Magician" with "Wonder Wand", increasing its  ATK  by 500 (1500 → 2000). "Gagaga Magician" attacks and destroys "Gogogo Golem" (Astral 4000 → 3800).
 The rest of the Duel is skipped. Yuma wins.
 
 Yuma Tsukumo vs. Taiki 
 Turn 1: Taiki 
 Taiki   Sets  a  monster  and three cards.
 Turn 2: Yuma 
 Yuma   Normal Summons  " Gagaga Magician " (1500/1000). "Gagaga Magician"  attacks  the Set monster, which is " Muscle Gardna " (1800/2000) (Yuma 4000 → 3500). Taiki  activates  his  face-down  " Secret Barrel ", inflicting 200  damage  to Yuma for each card in his  hand  and on his  field  (Yuma 3500 → 2300). Yuma Sets a card. 
 Turn 3: Taiki 
Taiki  draws  " Mist Valley Falcon " and Sets a card.
 Turn 4: Yuma 
Yuma Normal Summons " Ganbara Knight " (0/1800). He  overlays  his two  Level  4 Monsters to  Xyz Summon  " Number 39: Utopia " (2500/2000) in Attack Position. Yuma attacks "Muscle Gardna" but Taiki activates his face-down " Magic Cylinder ",  negating  the attack and inflicting damage equal to the attacking monster's  ATK  to Yuma damage. (Yuma 2300 → 0)
