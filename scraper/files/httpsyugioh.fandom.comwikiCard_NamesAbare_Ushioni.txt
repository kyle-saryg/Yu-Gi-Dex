 Names 
 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Μαινόμενος Ταύρος Δαίμονας ") responds to the original translation of this card's Japanese name (" Raging Bull Demon "). A transliteration of the TCG name in Greek could be " Αμπάρε Γιουσϊόνι ".
