Cursed Darkness  is the eighth pack in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour , and is accessible when the Beginneer's Cup is obtained. This series focuses on  Trap Cards  with effects that will drive your opponent crazy. Your opponent definitely does not want you using cards in this pack, so use these cards to your advantage.
 The 52 cards in  Cursed Darkness  include 8  Normal Monster Cards , 10  Effect Monster Cards , 2  Fusion Monster Cards , 1  Ritual Monster Card , 7  Spell Cards , and 24  Trap Cards .
