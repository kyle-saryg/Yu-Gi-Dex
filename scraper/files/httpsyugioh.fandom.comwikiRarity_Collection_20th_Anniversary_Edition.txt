 Rarity Collection 20th Anniversary Edition  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack (3 Super Rares and 1 Ultra Rare or higher rarity) and 15 packs per box. The set contains 51 cards, or 136 cards in the  Master Set :
 
 1  Holographic Rare 
 5  Extra Secret Rares 
 5  Ultimate Rares  (also available as  Collector's Rare ) 
 10  Ultra Rares  (also available as  Secret Rare  and  Collector's Rare ) 
 30  Super Rares  (also available as  Secret Rare  and  Collector's Rare ) 
 The  Master Sets  contain:
 
 Japanese, 141 cards. 
 Korean, 96 cards (doesn't have the Collector's Rare variants).
