 A  Duelist  (デュエリスト [Note 1]   Dyuerisuto ), or  player  (プレイヤー  Pureiyā ), is anyone participating in a  Duel .
 By extension, it is also used to describe anyone who frequently plays the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game .
