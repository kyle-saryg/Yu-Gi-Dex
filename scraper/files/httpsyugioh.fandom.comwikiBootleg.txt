      The outside of the bar.     
 Bootleg  is a bar in  New Domino City . In contrast to the well built-up areas of New Domino. Bootleg is a situated in a  back alley  in a poorer rural part of the city.
 
       The inside of the bar.     
 The inside of the bar is lit by lamps. Many of the customers and staff bear a  criminal mark .  Bolt Tanner  often came here before being sent to  The Facility . Under Tanner's instructions  Yusei Fudo  came here in search of  Blister  and was told to show  the owner  Tanner's " Giant Ushi Oni " card.
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , Bootleg is a place where the player can change their Tag partner and get  Duel Puzzles .
