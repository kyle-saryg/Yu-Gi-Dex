 Pegasus and Keith's Duel  was a game of  Duel Monsters  between  Bandit Keith  and  Maximillion Pegasus  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime  and  anime . It is based on  a similar Duel  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 The Duel was the final of the  United States The King of Duelists . In the English dub, it was the final of the Intercontinental Championship.
