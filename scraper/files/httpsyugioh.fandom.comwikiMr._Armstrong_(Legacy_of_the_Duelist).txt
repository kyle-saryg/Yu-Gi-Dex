 Mr. Armstrong , known as ( 鷹栖   Takasu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Mr. Armstrong , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
