 [  Warrior  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except by  Tributing  1  Level  1  Normal Monster  that has remained  face-up  on your side of the  field  for 10 or more of your  turns . If this card  battles  with an opponent's  monster , until the end of the  Damage Step , its  ATK  becomes the ATK of the opponent's monster + 1000 and that monster's  effect (s) is  negated . This card cannot be  targeted  by the effects of  Spells ,  Traps , or  Effect Monsters .
