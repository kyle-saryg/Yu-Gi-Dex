 Ra shall take power from three  Sacrifices . But even if the offering is to Ra's liking, the God shall only answer to the one who speaks the sacred words. When the means of resurrection are granted to it, Ra shall come forth from the earth and those who face the God in war shall be incinerated in flames. In an instant, Ra shall become  a phoenix , and the enemies of Ra shall return to the earth. [note 1]
