 Reginald Kastle  is a video game depiction of  Reginald Kastle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. He is available as a Legend Rare Partner, with a default of 6 Rental Points. His partner ID is P012.

 Description 
 A student ahead of  Yuma , also known as the "Shark." A talented Duelist, he nevertheless ended up quitting after  a certain incident . He started out opposing Yuma, but eventually became his friend.
 
 Decks 
 An Xyz Deck centered on  WATER  and  Fish-Types  monsters. Characterized by having many cards with effects linked to the " Graveyard " and " Banish ", Keep a close eye on the number of your Graveyard and Banish cards and use your card effects well!

 Trivia 
 " Number 30: Acid Golem of Destruction " in the second version of this partner's Extra Deck is a reference to when his  anime counterpart  temporarily took control of that monster in his Duel with  Kite Tenjo , by using " Splash Capture ". 
 The "certain incident" in this partner's description is a reference to when his anime counterpart "cheated" in the  National Duel Circuit  finals by looking at his opponent's Deck before their Duel, even though it was a set-up.
