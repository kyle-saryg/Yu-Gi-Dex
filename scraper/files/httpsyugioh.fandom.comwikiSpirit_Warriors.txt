 Features 
 Includes members/support for the " Battleguard ", " Constellar ", " Photon ", " Shien ", " Six Samurai " archetypes; as well as member of the  Scrap-Iron  series. 
 Introduces the " Magical Musket ", " Secret Six Samurai " and " The Weather " archetypes to the TCG.
