 " A Family of Gears " is the sixty-fourth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on September 5, 2021.
 Yuga  and co. visited the Kamijo family's mansion, known as "Kamijo Gear Company".  Luke  came home at a bad time, however, and was caught by  Tiger . And so, a  Duel  between brother and sister began! After weathering a fierce attack from Tiger, Luke starts to change...
 
 Featured Duel: Tatsuhisa "Luke" Kamijo VS. Haruka "Tiger" Kamijo 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
