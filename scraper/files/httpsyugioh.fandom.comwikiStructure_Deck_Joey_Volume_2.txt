 Structure Deck: Joey Volume 2  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the sixth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Yugi Volume 2 .

 Features 
 The deck features many luck based cards and other cards used by  Katsuya Jonouchi  towards the end of the  Battle City  arc. It contains one previously-unreleased card, " Foolish Burial ".
