 Gong Strong , known as  Noboru Gongenzaka ( 権 現 坂   昇 ,  Gongenzaka Noboru ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Gong Strong , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
