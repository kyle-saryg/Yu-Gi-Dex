 The  Duel Academy examiner  is the  Duel Academy   faculty member  who faced  Bastion Misawa  during the latter's entrance exam. 
 The examiner appeared again during the meeting to discuss who would represent  Duel Academy  during the in the inter-school match against  North Academy , though he does not speak. [1]  Subsequently, he appears in  Professor Satyr's  flashback of the meeting where no one noticed him. [2] 
 The examiner attended  Jaden Yuki 's graduation ceremony, where he stands among other members of the faculty. His final appearance is at the following party, where he is seen standing with Satyr. [3] 
 In the   GX Tag Force  series, this character's design is used for multiple characters as a "generic teacher design". The characters  Wade ,  Georg ,  Lioside ,  Dean  and  Mimicry  all use this design. When the series moves to be based on  5D's , the character design is maintained, but they are retooled to be members of  Sector Security . 
 
 Deck 
 During his only shown  Duel , the examiner used a Deck specifically designed for testing applicants. The only shown cards were " Big Shield Gardna " and " Gear Golem the Moving Fortress ".
