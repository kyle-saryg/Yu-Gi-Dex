 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In Tag Force 1-3, he is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 
 Tag Force 
 Upon acquiring his  Deck Recipe , he also gifts the player with a " Marie the Fallen One " card.
 Simon teams up with  Anca  for the Tag Force tournament.
 
 Tag Force 4 
       Simon, in  Tag Force 4 .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 , Simon is located in the city areas.
 In the first event of  Mina Simington 's story, the  Player  and Mina challenge Simon and  Rio  to a duel.
 In his own story, he competes with the Player against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the  Player  will receive a photo of himself,  Terence , Simon,  Rio  and  Bivin  called "City Guy".
 
 Tag Force 5 
 In  Tag Force 5 , Simon is located in the coastal region.
 During the first story events of Poncho  Yusei Fudo 's and  Sherry LeBlanc 's stories, Simon and  KENYoU  Tag Duel against the player and their partner.
 During Academy  Akiza 's second story event, Simon and  Bright  Tag Duel against the player and Akiza.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Simon uses a  Chaos  Deck .
 When " Chaos Sorcerer " was  Forbidden  from the  Advanced Format , Simon began using  Highlander Decks  - a Deck concept by which any card used is featured at only one copy. Once "Chaos Sorcerer" became legal once again many years later, Simon began using  Chaos Decks  again, now leaning more towards  Cy-Caliber  Decks . He continues using  Highlander Decks  as well until  Tag Force 6 .
