 TCG Rulings 
 You can still  Tribute  2 monsters to  Tribute Summon  a  Level  7 or higher monster if you want to. [1] 
 Only the controller of " Advance Force " gets the effect. [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Advance Force " can only be used by the player who  activated  it. [2] 
 When  Tribute Summoning  a Level 7+ monster, applying the effect of " Advance Force " is optional.  If " Advance Force " is active, then you can choose to not apply its effect and  Tribute Summon  a Level 7+ monster by  Tributing  two monsters, including a Level 5+ monster(s). [3]
