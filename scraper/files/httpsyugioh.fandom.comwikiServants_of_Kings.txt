 Servants of Kings  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on October 17, 2017, 7:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 The master of spells emerges from the darkness! Chaos shall reign! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Dark Magician of Chaos ," a Monster Card with the effect to retrieve Spell Cards from the Graveyard as well as banish monsters it destroys in battle. You can Special Summon it with " Dedication through Light and Darkness ", so use it to strengthen your " Dark Magician " Deck. 
" Elemental HERO Flare Neos ," a Fusion Monster whose ATK increases by 400 for each Trap/Spell Card on the field. Include many Continuous and Equip Spells in your Deck to increase its ATK. It will return to the Extra Deck during the End Phase, so try to finish off your opponent in one devastating blow! 
" Forbidden Chalice ," a Quick-Play Spell Card that can negate a monster's effect. It also raises the target monster's ATK, so be careful when using it against your opponent. 
" Wall of Disruption ," a Trap Card that can greatly reduce the ATK of monsters on your opponent's side of the field. The more monsters your opponent has, the stronger this effect becomes! 
A total of 40 types of Cards: 2  UR , 8  SR , 14  R , and 16  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Servants of Kings  contains 80 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 5 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 10 copies
