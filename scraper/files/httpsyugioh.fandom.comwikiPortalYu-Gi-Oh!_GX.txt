 Welcome to the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  portal. This page contains information regarding  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  is set in the school,  Duel Academy , which trains students to become  Duel Monsters  professionals.
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  has an  anime series , a  manga , a number of video games and various  Trading Card Game  sets based on it.
