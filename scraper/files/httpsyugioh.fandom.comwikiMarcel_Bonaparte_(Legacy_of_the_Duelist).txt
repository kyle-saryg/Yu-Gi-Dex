 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Marcel Bonaparte , known as  Martin Kanou  (加納マルタン,  Kanō Marutan ) in the Japanese version,  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of the  Yubel -possessed  Marcel Bonaparte , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
