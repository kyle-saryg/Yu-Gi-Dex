 Legendary Collection Kaiba Mega Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Legendary Collection Kaiba Mega Pack  is a  Mega-Pack  released with  Legendary Collection Kaiba  boxes.

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Seto Kaiba ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Kaibaman ,  Yugi Muto ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Yuma Tsukumo ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas ,  Luna , and  Sect Ijuin . 
 Includes members/support for the " Blue-Eyes ", " Dragunity , " Fusion ", " Number ", " Number S ", " Utopia ", and " Vampire " archetypes, as well as the  A-to-Z ,  Dark counterpart ,  Duel Dragon ,  Yo-kai Girl ,  Mirror Trap ,  Signer Dragon ,  Treasure Cards ,  Virus , and  with Eyes of Blue  series.
