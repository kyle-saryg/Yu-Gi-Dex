 The  boss-like man  (親分風の男) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is based on the  ZTV producer  from the  manga , in terms of appearance.
 
 Biography 
 The man was a customer at  Burger World  while  Jiro "The Jorogumo"  and  Tetsuo "The Hedgehog" Sasaki  were  on the loose . When the staff were searching for Tetsuo among the customers, they checked the customers legs, believing Tetsuo had a bruise on his.  Anzu Mazaki  tried checking the man's leg, using the excuse that he should not have his feet on the table. However the man kicked-up a fuss over this, leading the  detective  to explain what was really happening. [1]
