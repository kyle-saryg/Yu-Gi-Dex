 Chazz-A-Thon
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 English
 
 
 Serious Chazz
 
 
 Chazz-A-Thon is an  event   in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links  from February 5th, 2017 to February 13h, 2017. The event has the mechanics of both a Duel-A-Thon and a roaming event

 Event details 
 Serious Chazz does not appear at the Gate. 
 Serious Chazz has different Decks/Rewards from the regular Chazz Princeton. 
 Chazz Princeton will not appear in Duel World if you are using him as your character. 
 Only 1 Serious Chazz will appear in Duel World at once. 
 The chance of Serious Chazz appearing in Duel World is not affected by the appearance of other Standard/Legendary Duelists or by the use of Duel Orbs/Boosted Duel Orbs.
