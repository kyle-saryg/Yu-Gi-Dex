 Marc Diraison  is an American actor and voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment . 
 Diraison voices a variety of characters in the English dubbed version of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series.
 
 Roles 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! , Diraison voiced  Sergei Ivanoff , [ citation needed ]  young  Solomon Muto [ citation needed ]  and  Karim . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , he voiced  Chancellor Foster [ citation needed ]  and one of the  Goblin Elite Attack Force members . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , he voiced  Commander Koda [ citation needed ]  and  Kalin Kessler . [ citation needed ] 
 
 Other 
 Diraison relocated to California, where he resides as a California-based voice actor. [ citation needed ]
