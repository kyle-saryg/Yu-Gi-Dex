 This card can be searched by " Abomination's Prison ", " Unchained Twins - Sarama ", " Unchained Twins - Aruha ", " Unchained Twins - Rakea ", " Dark Spirit's Mastery ", " Doomdog Octhros ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Magical Contract Door ", " Chaos Zone ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Link Party " and any " Unchained " Spell/Trap. 
 This card is best summoned on your opponent's turn via any appropriate  Spell Speed 2  effect, including those that destroy a Set "Unchained" Spell/Trap you control in order to summon this card from the Deck. Discard " The Fabled Catsith ", " Danger! Bigfoot! " or " Danger! Thunderbird! " to destroy an additional card and gain advantage.
