   English 
 
 
 Target  1 " Malefic "  monster  in your  GY ;  Special Summon  it,  ignoring its Summoning conditions , but its  effects  are  negated , also  banish  it during the  End Phase .

   5D's Tag Force 6 
 
 
 Target  1 " Malefic "  monster  in your  Graveyard ;  Special Summon  it, ignoring the  Summoning conditions . It cannot  attack   directly  and its  effect (s) are  negated . During the  End Phase :  Destroy  it.
