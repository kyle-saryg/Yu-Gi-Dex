 Scarr, Scout of Dark World  is a soldier in the  Dark World Army  that is headed by  Brron, Mad King of Dark World .  Jaden  Duels him in order to defend a young boy named  Kyle  in the second alternate dimension. Despite his " Fusion Devourer " reducing the  ATK  of  Fusion Monsters  it battles to 0 and his  Spell  and  Trap Cards  focusing on locking down other means of Summoning, Scarr is easily defeated by Jaden.
 In the dub, his speech resembles that of  Damon  who is based on Tarzan in that he speaks in mostly broken English and refers to himself in the third person, replacing "I" with "me".
