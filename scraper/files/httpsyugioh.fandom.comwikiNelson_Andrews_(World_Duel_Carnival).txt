 Nelson Andrews , known as  Fuya Okudaira  ( 奥 平   風 也 ,  Okudaira Fūya ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival . This is a video game depiction of  Nelson Andrews , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
