Main card page: " Parasite Paracide " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Parasite "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「パラサイト」 .   As its English name contains "Parasite", it  may  be treated a member of the "Parasite" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
