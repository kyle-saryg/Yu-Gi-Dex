 Section 8: King's Memories arc  ( 第 八 章   王 の 記 憶 編   Daihasshō Ō no Kioku-hen ) is a story arc in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . In the absence of furigana on  王 , it has also been read as  Pharaoh's Memories arc  ( 王 の 記 憶 編   Farao no Kioku-hen ).
 Dark Yugi  replays his life as Pharaoh Atem, through the  Shadow RPG . His friends from the modern world participate in the RPG as non-player characters.
