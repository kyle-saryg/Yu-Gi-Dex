 Effect 
 Condition 
 Trigger-like 
 If you  control  no  monsters :  Special Summon  as many  face-up   Level  1 " Performapal "  Pendulum Monsters  from your  Extra Deck  as possible, and if you do, they can  attack  your opponent  directly , but their original  effects  are  negated . You cannot Special Summon other monsters the  turn  you  activate  this card. At the end of the  Battle Phase , if this card is in the  Graveyard  because it was activated this turn: You can  shuffle  1 "Performapal" monster on the  field  into the  Deck ;  destroy  all monsters that were Special Summoned this turn.
