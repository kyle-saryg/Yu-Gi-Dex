 The  2012 South African National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of South Africa. It took place in Johannesburg on June 16, and was won by Krishern Govender. The tournament was attended by 43 people, 8 of whom got to qualify for the World Championship Qualifier continentals that year. [2] 
 The entry fee was  R  150 for the main event and R 100 for the  Dragon Duel . [1]
