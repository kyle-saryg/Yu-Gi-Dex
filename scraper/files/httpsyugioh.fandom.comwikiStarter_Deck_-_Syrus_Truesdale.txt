 Starter Deck - Syrus Truesdale  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Starter Deck - Jaden Yuki , they are the eighth and ninth deck in the  TCG ' s Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2006 .
 
 The  Syrus Truesdale  Starter , "The first Duel Academy's Starter Deck", features popular cards from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  series.
 Also included is an updated graphical Beginner's Guide with a "How to power-up your Deck" section, along with a deluxe game mat and a  Super  and  Ultra Rare  card.
