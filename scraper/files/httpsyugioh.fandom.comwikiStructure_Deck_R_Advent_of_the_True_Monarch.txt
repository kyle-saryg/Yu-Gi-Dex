 Structure Deck R: Advent of the True Monarch  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is part of a new Structure Deck series that takes the concept of an old Structure Deck's tactics, in this case  Structure Deck: Advent of the Emperor , and revises them for the modern tournament environment.

 The Deck is focused on Tribute Summons. 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Monarch " archetype.
