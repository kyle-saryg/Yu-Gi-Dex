 Structure Deck R: Revival of the Great Divine Dragon  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the second Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck R series, following  Structure Deck R: Advent of the True Monarch .

 Features 
 The old structure deck " Revival of the Great Dragon " with its central concept of summoning giant dragons and battling with them is born anew as "Revival of the Great Divine Dragon" with new cards to power-up its strategies as well as other new support cards which synergize greatly with the deck! 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck R: Revival of the Great Divine Dragon   contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 40 cards
 1  Ultra Rare 
 3  Super Rares 
 6  Normal Parallel Rares 
 30  Commons 
 1 Ultra Rare promotional card ("Arkbrave Dragon") 
 1 Common  Token 
 1 Rulebook 
 1 Double-sided  Playmat /Playing Guide
