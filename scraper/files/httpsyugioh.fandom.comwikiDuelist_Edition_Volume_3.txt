 Features 
 A variety of cards have been specially selected, all of which have been renewed with a new card and text format. 
 Reprint cards from 2008 Booster Packs series:  The Duelist Genesis ,  Crossroads of Chaos ,  Crimson Crisis , and  Raging Battle . 
 Includes one new card, " Mono Synchron ".
