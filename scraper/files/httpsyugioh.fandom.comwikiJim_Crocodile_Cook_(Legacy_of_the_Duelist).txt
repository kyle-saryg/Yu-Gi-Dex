 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Jim Crocodile Cook , known as (ジム・クロコダイル・クック,  Jimu Kurokodairu Kukku ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Jim Crocodile Cook , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
