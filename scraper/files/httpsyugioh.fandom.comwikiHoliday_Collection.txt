 Holiday Collection  is a Time-Limited  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from December 12, 2013 to January 15, 2014. Each pack can be bought using either   75000 or   249.
 This Special Holiday Collection includes the best Rare, Ultra Rare and BAM Legends from Series 1 and 2. It also includes Arena cards and unique, time-limited Holiday cards.
