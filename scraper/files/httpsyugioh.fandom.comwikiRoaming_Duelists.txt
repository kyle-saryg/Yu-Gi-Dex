Link: https://yugioh.fandom.com/wiki/Roaming_Duelists

These events feature a  Legendary Duelist  appearing in Duel World. They cannot be unlocked, but they can be Dueled repeatedly in order to obtain event-exclusive cards. These can feature either new characters, or "Super" versions of previously available characters.
