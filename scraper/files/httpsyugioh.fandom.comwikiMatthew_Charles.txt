 Matthew Charles Labyorteaux [1]  (credited as  Matthew Charles ), is an American voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment . He has provided voices for a number of characters in the English language dub of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series. In 2017, he reprised his role as  Jaden Yuki  in the mobile app,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .
 A former live-action star from Los Angeles, Labyorteaux is best known for portraying Albert Quinn Ingalls and Young Charles Ingalls on  Little House on the Prairie from 1976 to 1983 .  Since 1994, however, he has pursued a career in voiceover, appearing in cartoons such as  Aaahh!!! Real Monsters , animated films such as  Mulan , and video games such as the  .hack//  series. He later moved to Manhattan's Upper West Side, eventually finding work at 4Kids Productions.

 Charles' most notable role was  Jaden Yuki  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . He also portrays Jaden's dark counterpart,  The Supreme King .
