 Equip  only to a  face-up   Xyz Monster . Once per  turn : You can change the equipped  monster  from  Defense Position  to  Attack Position , and if you do, it gains  ATK  equal to the total ATK of all face-up Defense Position Xyz Monsters you currently  control , until the  End Phase . During your  Battle Phase : You can  send  1 other face-up Xyz Monster you control to the  Graveyard ; during this Battle Phase, the equipped monster can attack once again for each monster sent to the Graveyard this way, also it can attack your opponent  directly .
