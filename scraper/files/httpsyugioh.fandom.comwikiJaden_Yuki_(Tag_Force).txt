 Jaden Yuki , known as  Judai Yuki  ( 遊 城   十 代 ,  Yūki Jūdai ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Jaden Yuki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Jaden is a Tier 1 character in every game.
 
 
 Tag Force 
 If the player does not partner with Jaden for the Tag Force tournament, he will team up with  Chazz Princeton  in Part 2 and with  Amnael  in Part 3.
 
 
 Tag Force 2 
 The player duels against Jaden four times during the story for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 There are three versions of Jaden in this game: himself with his Season 4 design, Jaden as he appears in Tag Force 2, and  The Supreme King . The latter can technically be chosen as a partner for Story Mode, but the game will lock up due to his story never been actually implemented into the game.
