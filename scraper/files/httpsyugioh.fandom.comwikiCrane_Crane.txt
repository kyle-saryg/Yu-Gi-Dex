 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When this card is  Normal Summoned : You can  target  1  Level  3  monster  in your  GY ;  Special Summon  that target, but it has its  effects   negated . You can only use this effect of "Crane Crane" once per  turn .
