 The  Greek                lore given is not official.  
 
 Greek - Ηλεκτρικός Πιγκουίνος 
Και με τα δύο του χέρια να σχηματίζουν ηλεκτρικά μαστίγια αυτό το τέρας παραλύει τους εχθρούς του.
 
 
 In the English version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "With both arms forming an electric whip, this monster paralyzes its enemies with electric shocks." 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Avec ses deux bras formant un fouet électrique, ce monstre paralyse ses ennemis à coups de chocs électriques." 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Dieses Monster lähmt seine Gegner mit elektrischen Schocks, indem es mit seinen beiden Armen eine elektrische Peitsche formt." 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Le braccia di quest'essere formano una frusta elettrificata che egli utilizza per paralizzare i nemici con colpi ad altissimo voltaggio." 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Con ambos brazos formando un látigo eléctrico, este monstruo paraliza a sus enemigos mediante descargas eléctricas."
