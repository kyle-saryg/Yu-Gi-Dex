 " Dissonance " is the fourty-fourth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on April 18, 2021.
 Roa  and his team interrupt the tournament's opening ceremony! In order to achieve his goal, he challenges  Mimi  to a Duel.  Getta  and  Ushiro  were expecting a team Duel, but much to their shock, Roa tells them to stand behind him and watch...
 
 Featured Duel: Roa Kirishima VS Mimi Atachi 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
