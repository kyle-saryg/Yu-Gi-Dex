 Rathie , known as  Kurumizawa  in the Japanese version is a  shop  owner in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's . His name is not mentioned in the English anime, but has been used in various video games.
 An  alternate universe version  of Rathie appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . 
 
 Biography 
 Rathie helped  Carly  investigate the  Crimson Dragon  appearance at the  Fortune Cup , by researching the  Signers . Knowing Carly would want to contact  Akiza Izinski , he also dug up information on the  Arcadia Movement , which he wasn't willing to give her for free. To his delight, Carly gave him a rare " Dark Magician Girl " figure in exchange for the information.
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 6 , Rathie sells  Booster Packs  and starter packs to the player in his shop.
 By giving him items, the player gets discounts on Booster Packs. The player can unlock the  Lovey Dovey  pack in  Tag Force 4 ;  Picking Flowers  in  Tag Force 5 ;  My Wife Plus Plus  in  Tag Force 6  by giving him each of the following figures; " White Magician Pikeru ", " Ebon Magician Curran ", " Dark Magician Girl ", " Card Ejector " and " Cyber Tutu ". These are the figures seen in his shop when he appears in the anime.
