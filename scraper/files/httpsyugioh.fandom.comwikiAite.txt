 Aite , known as  Nagare Kandagawa  in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force  series. He is a  Slifer Red  student.

 Decks 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Aite uses a  Zombie  Deck .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Aite switches to Decks that feature a large number of " Goblin " cards.
