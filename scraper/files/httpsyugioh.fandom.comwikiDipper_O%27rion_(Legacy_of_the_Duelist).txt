 Dipper O'rion , known as  Hokuto Shijima ( 志 島   北 斗 ,  Shijima Hokuto ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Dipper O'rion , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
