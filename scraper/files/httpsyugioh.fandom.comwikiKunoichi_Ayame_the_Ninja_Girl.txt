 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Ninja "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 忍 者 」 .   As its English name contains "Ninja", it  may  be treated a member of the "Ninja" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
