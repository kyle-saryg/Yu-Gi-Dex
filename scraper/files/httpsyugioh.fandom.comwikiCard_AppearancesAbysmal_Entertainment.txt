 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 148 ,  Yuya Sakaki used  this card during his second rematch against  Declan Akaba . On the third turn of the duel, he sets this card. On the next turn, after Declan activated the effect of " D/D/D Super Doom King Purple Armageddon " to destroy Yuya's " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon " and then inflict damage to Yuya equal to the destroyed "Rebellion Dragon"'s  ATK . Yuya activates this card to send the destroyed "Rebellion Dragon" to his  Pendulum Zone  and prevent himself from taking any damage for the rest of the turn. (This included the 3000 damage he would have received from the effect of "Purple Armageddon".)
