 [  Fairy  /  Effect  ] Select  3 " Cubic Seed "  monsters  you  control , stack them on top of each other, and stack this card on top of them to  Special Summon  this card. This  effect  can be  activated  during the  Battle Phase . When this card is Special Summoned, inflict 800  damage  to your opponent. This card gains 800  original ATK  for each monster stacked underneath it. When this card is  destroyed , you can  return  this card to your  hand  to Special Summon the "Cubic Seed" monsters stacked underneath it to your  field . [1] [2]
