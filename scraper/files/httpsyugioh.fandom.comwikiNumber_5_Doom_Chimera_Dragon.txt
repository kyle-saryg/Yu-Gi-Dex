 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2+  Level  5  monsters Gains 1000  ATK  for each  material  attached to it. While this card has material, it can  attack  all your opponent's monsters, once each. At the end of the  Battle Phase , if this card  battled : You can  activate  1 of these  effects . ●  Target  1 monster in your  GY ; attach it to this card as material. ● Target 1 card in your opponent's GY; place it on top of their  Deck .
