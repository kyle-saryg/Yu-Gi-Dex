 " Rock the Rush Duel ", known in Japan as " Romin's Secret " is the third episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It aired in Japan on April 18, 2020 and in the US on June 7, 2022.
 Goha Corp has started taking action in order to eliminate  Rush Duels . However, they don't directly target  Yuga  and his friends for some reason. Feeling that Goha might be leaving them at large on purpose in order to observe them,  Rook  decides to do some investigation on  Romin , who's been acting suspicious. Yuga suggests using a Rush Duel to get a read on Romin, but...
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo vs. Romin Kassidy 
 The Duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
 Turn 1: Romin Kassidy 
Romin  Normal Summons  " Spell Archer " (Level 3/1000/400) and Tributes it to  Tribute Summon  " Answerer the Demonic Swordsman " (Level 6/2000/500). Roman Sets 2 cards.
 Turn 2: Yuga Ohdo 
Yuga Normal Summons " Mystic Dealer " (Level 3/1000/0), " Luminous Shaman " (Level 2/500/0) and " Child of Wind, Cyc " (Level 2/400/500). Yuga Tributes "Luminous Shaman" and "Child of Wind, Cyc" to Tribute Summon " Sevens Road Magician " (Level 7/2100/1500). Yuga Normal Summons "Spell Archer" (Level 3/1000/400). Yuga activates the effect of "Sevens Road Magician", letting him send the top card of his Deck to the GY to increase the ATK of "Sevens Road Magician" by 300 for each different Attribute in his GY until the End Phase ("Sevens Road Magician": 2100 → 2700/1500). "Sevens Road Magician" attacks and destroys "Answerer" (Roman: 4000 → 3300 LP). "Mystic Dealer" and "Spell Archer" attack Romin directly (Romin: 3300 → 2300 → 1300 LP).
 Turn 3: Romin Kassidy 
As Romin has one card in her hand, she draws four cards. Romin Normal Summons " Stray Familiar " (Level 1/0/1400), "Luminous Shaman" (Level 2/500/0) and " Magical Beast Wolfram " (Level 3/1100/100). Romin Tributes "Stray Familiar" and "Luminous Shaman" to Tribute Summon " Prima Guitarna of the Colorful Light " (Level 7/2200/0). Romin Normal Summons " Dark Sorcerer " (Level 4/1500/0). Romin activates the effect of "Prima Guitarna", paying 1000 LP (Romin: 1300 → 300 LP) to increase the ATK of her monsters by 300 for each monster Yuga controls ("Prima Guitarna": 2200 → 3100/0; "Wolfram": 1100 → 2000/100; "Dark Sorcerer": 1500 → 2400/0). "Prima Guitarna" attacks and destroys "Sevens Road Magician" (Yuga: 4000 → 3000 LP). "Dark Sorcerer" attacks and destroys "Mystic Dealer" (Yuga: 3000 → 1600 LP). "Wolfram" attacks and destroys "Spell Archer" (Yuga: 1600 → 600 LP).
 Turn 4: Yuga Ohdo 
As Yuga has one card in his hand, he draws four cards. Yuga activates " Recovery Force " to shuffle 3 Spellcaster-Type monsters in his GY into his Deck and draw 1 card. He shuffles "Luminous Shaman", "Spell Archer" and "Sevens Road Magician" into his Deck and draws "Sevens Road Magician". Yuga Normal Summons "Magical Beast Wolfram" and "Stray Familiar" and Tributes both monsters to Tribute Summon "Sevens Road Magician" (Level 7/2100/1500). Yuga activates the effect of "Sevens Road Magician", sending the top card of his Deck ("Sevens Road Magician": 2100 → 3600/1500). "Sevens Road Magician" attacks and destroys "Prima Guitarna" (Romin: 300 → 0 LP).
