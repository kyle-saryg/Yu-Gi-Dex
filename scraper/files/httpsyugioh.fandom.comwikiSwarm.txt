Not to be confused with " lswarm ". 
 Swarming  is the the act of quickly Summoning many monsters on the same turn.  Archetypes  with swarming capabilities include " Blackwing ", " Raccoon ", " Six Samurai " and " Synchron ". Decks utilizing the  Pendulum Summon  mechanic are also capable of swarming.
 Anti-swarm  refers to cards that help to counteract swarming. Cards such as  Mirror Traps , " Torrential Tribute " and " Dark Hole " are considered anti-swarm cards.
