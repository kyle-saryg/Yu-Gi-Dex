 Structure Deck: Blitzkrieg of the Mechlight Dragons  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the twenty-sixth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (thirty-fourth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: The Blue-Eyed Dragon's Thundering Descent . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Cyber Dragon Revolution Structure Deck .

 Features 
 The Deck is centered around the popular " Cyber Dragon ". 
 Includes a new Xyz Monster, along with a wide variety of new and old "Cyber Dragon" related cards. 
 Includes a number of reprints in order to meet the needs of players both new and old.
