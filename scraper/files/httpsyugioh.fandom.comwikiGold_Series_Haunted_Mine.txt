 Gold Series 4: Pyramids Edition 
 Gold Series: Haunted Mine 
 Premium Gold 
 
 
 
 
 
 Gold Series: Haunted Mine  is the fifth  TCG  set in the  Gold Series , following  Gold Series 4: Pyramids Edition . Befitting the "Haunted Mine" theme, it introduces the set-exclusive  Ghost/Gold Rare  rarity, and also contains support for  Zombie  and  Fiend - Type  monsters, as well as several  Trap Monsters  and members of the " Dark Scorpion " archetype.
