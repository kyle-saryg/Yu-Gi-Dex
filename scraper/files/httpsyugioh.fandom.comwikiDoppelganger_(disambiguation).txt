" Doppelganger " may refer to any of the following:
 
 " Doppelganger ": a Trap Card 
 " Mimicat ": a Spell Card, known as " Doppleganger " in the anime and " Doppelganger " in the manga 
 " Doppel-ganger ": a monster hologram, used by  Seto Kaiba  in artificial  Penalty Games  in the manga 
 " Greenkappa ": a Monster Card, known as "Doppelganger" in the Japanese version 
 " Doppelganger Token ": a  Token Monster  in the anime
