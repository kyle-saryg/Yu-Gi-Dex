 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Stack all " Vijam the Cubic Seed " from your  hand  underneath a " Cubic "  monster  on your  field . When a " Cubic Seed " monster is  Special Summoned  during your opponent’s  turn  by the  effect  of a "Cubic" monster: You can  send  this card to the  Graveyard ; halve your opponent’s  Life Points . [1] [2] 
 
 4K Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English card uses the same lore as the OCG/TCG card. The Japanese card and reference sources state the effect differently.):</mobile> When this card is  activated : You can  target  1 " Cubic "  monster  you  control , except " Vijam the Cubic Seed ";  send  any number of "Vijam the Cubic Seed" from your  hand  and/or  Deck  to the  Graveyard , then that monster gains 800  ATK  for each (even if this card  leaves the field ). During your opponent's  turn , if "Vijam the Cubic Seed" is  Special Summoned  by the  effect  of a "Cubic" monster: Send this card to the Graveyard, and if you do, halve your opponent's  LP .
