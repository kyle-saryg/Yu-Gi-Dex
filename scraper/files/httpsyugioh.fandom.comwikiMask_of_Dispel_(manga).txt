 Mask of Dispel 
 
 Japanese:   魔力無力化の仮面 
 Romaji:   Maryoku Muryokuka no Kamen 
 Translated:  Mask of Magic-Power Dispelment 
 
 
 
 
 en 
 ja 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell 
 
 
 
 A cursed  mask  that attaches to a  Spell Card  on the field. The owner of the Spell Card takes 300  damage  each turn. 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile>  Select  1  face-up   Spell Card  on the  field . The Spell Card has no  effect . The  controller  of the Spell Card must take  damage  of 300  Life Points  during each of his/her  Standby Phases .
