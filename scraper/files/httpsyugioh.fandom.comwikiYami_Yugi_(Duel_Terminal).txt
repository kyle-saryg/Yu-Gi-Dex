 Yami Yugi , also translated as  Dark Yugi  ( 闇 遊戯 ,  Yami Yugi ), is a character in the  Duel Terminal  arcade games. This is a video game depiction of  Yami Yugi , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  and   anime .
