In the game of  Dungeon Dice Monsters ,  Magic Crests  (also known as " Spell Crests ") are specifically used to pay the activation cost required to use the effect(s) of certain monsters.
 If the effect of a monster requires you to spend Magic Crests for its activation, you  cannot  activate that effect during your opponent's turn.
