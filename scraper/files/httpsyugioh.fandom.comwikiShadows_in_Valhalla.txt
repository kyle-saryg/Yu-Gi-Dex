 Shadows in Valhalla  is a  Booster  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Zigfried von Schroeder ,  Duke Devlin ,  Moon Shadow ,  Kaze ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Aster Phoenix ,  Rin ,  Yugo ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Akiza Izinski , and  Jesse Anderson . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Invoked ", " Ninja ", and " Ninjitsu Art " archetypes, as well as the  Entity  series. 
 Introduces the "Valkyrie" archetype to the TCG. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Elder Entity ", " Old Entity ", and " Outer Entity " archetypes to the TCG.
