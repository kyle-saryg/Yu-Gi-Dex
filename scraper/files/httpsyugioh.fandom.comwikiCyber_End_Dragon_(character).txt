 Cyber End Dragon , known as  Final Space Dragon  in the Arabic anime, is a character version of the card, " Cyber End Dragon ". It is a combination of 3  Cyber Dragons .
 
 Biography 
 Cyber End Dragon is part of the  Cyber Legacy  and considered the opposite to the Underworld  Cyberdark  series. Its spirit could communuicate with the human in  Chancellor Sheppard 's dojo, where a banner of it hung.
 After  Zane Truesdale  proved to be a powerful duelist, Sheppard awarded him the "Cyber End Dragon" card.
 While looking at the banner of the dragon, Zane asked Cyber End Dragon, how he was to Summon it. Cyber End Dragon signalled that he was to look at his Deck, where Zane noticed the cards: 3 " Cyber Dragons " and 1 " Polymerization ".
 Zane betrayed Cyber End Dragon in order to get the " Cyberdark " cards, by allowing it to get destroyed in his Duel with Sheppard in order to win.
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , as an unlockable opponent. its Tag Partner  Chimeratech Overdragon  are unlocked by reaching 20,000  Attack  point or more in a Duel. Their team name is  Not Fusion Yet .
 
 Deck 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006 , Cyber End Dragon uses a Cyber Dragon Deck titled Cyberdragons.
In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 , Cyber End Dragon uses a Machine Deck titled, "Machines for Food".
In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 , Cyber End Dragon uses a Cyber Dragon Deck titled, "Cyber Diver".
In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , Cyber End Dragon uses a  Machine  Deck titled, "Yes Polymerization".
