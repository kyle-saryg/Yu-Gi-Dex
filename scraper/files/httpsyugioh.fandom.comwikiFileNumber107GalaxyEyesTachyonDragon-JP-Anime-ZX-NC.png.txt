 " Tachyon Dragon " ( 時空竜   Takion Doragon ) is an  archetype  of high- Rank   LIGHT   Dragon   monsters , which are used by  Mizar  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . It is a sub-archetype of the " Tachyon " archetype.
 A  tachyon  or tachyonic particle is a hypothetical particle that always moves faster than light, and most physicists believe they cannot exist because they are not consistent with the natural laws of physics. The word comes from the Greek word  tachys , meaning "swift, quick, fast, rapid". This is a reference to the rivalry between Mizar's " Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon " and  Kite Tenjo 's " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon ".
