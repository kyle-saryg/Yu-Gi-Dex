 Duel Terminal - Demon Roar God Revival!!  is fourth installment of the  OCG 's  Duel Terminal  series, following  Duel Terminal - Justice Strikes Back!! , and followed by  Duel Terminal - Champion of Chaos!! .
 
 Price: 100 Yen per game. 
 Type: Arcade (Card Based). 
 Includes more " Fabled " monsters as well as the new " Jurrac " monsters, the latter with  Dinosaurs  as motive. 
 It also includes further support for " Naturia ", " Genex " and " Mist Valley " archetypes. 
 Introduces  Yami Bakura ,  Hell Kaiser ,  Tyranno Hassleberry  and  Akiza Izinski  as duelable opponents. 
 Action Button Add-On, changing the Action Duel Game into something new. 
 Cards for Duel Terminal will only be available in the arcade. 
 ＤＵＥＬ ＴＥＲＭＩＮＡＬ is written on the left opposite to the Set Number. 
 Very faint (near invisible)  QR code  is printed in the center of the card. 
 Features: " Action Duel ", " Speed Duel ", " Tutorial ", & " Card Only Get " 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards (consisting of 30 New Cards and 20 Reprint Cards), or 54 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares  (all available as Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares) 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 10  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 30  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
