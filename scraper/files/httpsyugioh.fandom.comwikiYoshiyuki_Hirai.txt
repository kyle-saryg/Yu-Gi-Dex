 Yoshiyuki Hirai  is a star from the tv show  Sakiyomi JumBANG! . He and his co-stars  Akina Minami  and  Tetsuya Yanagihara  make a brief appearance in the film  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time .
 
 Character Biography 
 Yoshiyuki Hirai and his co-stars Akina Minami and Tetsuya Yanagihara work as MCs at an event held by Maximillion Pegasus in Bonds Beyond Time.
 Later, three people resembling the trio appear in Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL to promote the World Duel Carnival.
