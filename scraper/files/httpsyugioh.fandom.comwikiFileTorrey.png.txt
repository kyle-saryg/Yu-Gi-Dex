 Torrey , known in Japan as  Takadera  (高寺), is an  Obelisk Blue  student who used to attend  Banner's  Para-Dueling class.

 Biography 
       Takadera Occult Brothers     
 After being told that duel spirits may come to life under certain conditions, he and some friends ( Isaka  and  Mukouda  in the Japanese version; unnamed in the English version) tried to create those conditions and eventually successfully summoned the Duel Spirit  Jinzo . Jinzo, however, eventually went after Torrey and his friends in order to be free. Torrey escaped and Jinzo ended up Dueling  Jaden Yuki . When Jinzo's own  card  was destroyed during the Duel, its spirit possessed Torrey and attempted to finish the Duel. Despite this, Jinzo still lost. Torrey returns to normal afterwards.
 He and his friends made a cameo in  episode 179  watching Jaden's Graduation ceremony.
