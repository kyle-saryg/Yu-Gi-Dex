      The USS  Courage .     
 The  USS  Courage  is an aircraft carrier appearing in the anime, during the  Waking the Dragons  arc.
 The crew observed the unusual phenomena carried out by  Paradius . Since the country's central rule fell under the control of Paradius, they were unauthorized to fight against them. Instead they asked  Yugi Muto  and  Seto Kaiba  for help, as they understood  Duel Monsters  had a huge role in Paradius' plans and Yugi and Kaiba were among the world's best Duelists.
 In the real world, the USS  Courage  (PG-70) was the name given to the ship the  HMS  Heartsease  (K15) , while serving the United States Navy during World War II.
