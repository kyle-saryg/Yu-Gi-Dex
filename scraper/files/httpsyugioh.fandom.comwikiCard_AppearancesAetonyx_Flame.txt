 In  episode 150 ,  Alister  uses this card during his Duel against  Seto Kaiba . He uses this card to destroy Kaiba's two " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " and revive his " Gorlag " (whose ATK becomes 2000 due to its own effect and " The Seal of Orichalcos ") at the cost of forbidding himself from declaring an attack this turn.
