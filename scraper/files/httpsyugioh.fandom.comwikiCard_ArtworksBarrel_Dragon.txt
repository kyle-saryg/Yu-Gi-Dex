 The guns on the Japanese artwork have been edited in the international artwork to seem less realistic. The background has also been changed. 
 In the international anime, an alternate version artwork with less detail was used in episode 32 of Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters. 
 Like all  Dungeon Dice Monsters  cards "Barrel Dragon" has different artwork in that game. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , " Barrel Dragon " was drawn with a different artwork.
