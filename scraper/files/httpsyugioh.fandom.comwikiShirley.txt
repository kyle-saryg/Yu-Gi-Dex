 Shirley , known in Japan and  Tag Force  as  Karen , is the crocodile that  Jim Cook  carries around on his back. She is considered by Jim to be family and he carries her around in a special backpack. He often uses her as a judge of character when meeting someone new, which led to his friendship with  Jaden Yuki . Jim met Shirley while walking through a forest as a kid, when he spotted her unknowingly heading towards a trap. He jumped towards her and pushed her out of the way, saving her from injury and lost his right eye as a result. They have been together ever since. 
 
       Shirley in Jim's backpack.     
 During the school's opening ceremony, when Jim shakes hands with  Jesse Anderson , Shirley growls at Jesse and he freaks out. When Jim first meets  Hassleberry , Shirley attacks him and Jim puts it down to the electro-magnetic waves emitting from  Viper's  lab. When Jim is defeated by  The Supreme King , Shirley attempts to block the blow but is sent to the stars with Jim. She later comes back with everyone else.
