 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . Must first be  Special Summoned  (from your  hand ) by  banishing  1  LIGHT   monster  from your  GY . Once per turn, during your opponent's  End Phase : Gain 1000  LP .
