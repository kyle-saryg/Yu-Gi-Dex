 "The Adam Factors!"  is the fortieth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga .
 This chapter was first printed in September in the January 2019 issue of  V Jump , released on November 21, 2018.

 Summary 
 Out of scientific curiosity, bearing the two parts of the Adam Factor,  Reiji Akaba  challenges his arch-rival,  Yuya Sakaki , to obtain the  Genesis Omega Dragon  card and declare himself as a god.
 
 Featured Duel: Yuya Sakaki vs. Reiji Akaba 
 This Duel is conducted as an  Action Duel . Both players can obtain and use  Action Cards .
 Turn 1: Reiji 
Yuya uses  Poppo  to obtain an Action Card. However, Reiji outmaneuvers him and obtains the Action Card.
 Reiji places " D/D Ark " ( PS : 1) and " D/D Evil " (PS: 8) in his  Pendulum Zones , and Pendulum Summons " D/D Dog " (2000/0). Reiji  sets  two cards.
 Turn 2: Yuya 
Yuya tries to obtain an action card, but Reiji distracted Yuya and obtained an Action Card.
 Yuya  Normal Summons  " Speedroid Block-n-Roll " (1000/800). Since he controls a " Speedroid " monster, Yuya  Special Summons  " Speedroid CarTurbo " (800/1200) from his hand. Yuya tunes "Speedroid Block-n-Roll" with "CarTurbo" to  Synchro Summon  " Clearwing Fast Dragon " (2500/2000) in Attack Position.
 Duel continues in the next Scale .

 Errors 
 The names of " D/D Arch " and " D/D Evil " are switched in this chapter. This mistake is corrected in the next chapter. 
 The name of "D/D Arch" is changed to "D/D Ark" in the next chapter.
