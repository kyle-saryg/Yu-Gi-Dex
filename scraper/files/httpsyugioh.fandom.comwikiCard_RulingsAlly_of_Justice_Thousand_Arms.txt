 TCG Rulings 
 This card’s self-destruction ability starts a  Chain  after an opposing monster would be flipped  face-up  but before  damage calculation . [1] 
 If this card attacks a  face-down   LIGHT  monster you cannot continue to attack other  LIGHT  monsters. [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 This card's effect which destroys itself is a  Trigger Effect  and starts a chain.  It activates at the beginning of the  Damage Step , after flipping the monsters face-up, before  Damage Calculation . [2] 
 When " Ally of Justice Thousand Arms " attacks a face-down  LIGHT  monster, you cannot use his effect to continue to attack all other face-up  LIGHT  monsters. [3]
