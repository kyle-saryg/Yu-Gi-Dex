 Valiant Souls  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on April 3, 2018, 1:00 AM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Strike at the speed of light! No enemy can stand in your way! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Elemental HERO Plasma Vice ," an Elemental HERO monster that destroys 1 Attack Position monster on your opponent's side of the field by discarding a card. Its fusion materials, " Elemental HERO Sparkman ", can be ontained as a Duel Reward from  Jaden Yuki , and " Elemental HERO Bladedge " can be obtained from this BOX! 
" Vision HERO Witch Raider ," a monster that can be Tribute Summoned in Attack Position by Tributing Trap Cards on your side of the field. When it is successfully Summoned, it will destroy all Spell/Trap Cards on your opponent's side of the field. 
" Dark World Dealings ," This card, which was pre-released in the Selection BOX, is now available! This BOX also has Fabled and Paleozoic cards, which work great with this card. 
" Needle Ceiling ," a powerful Trap Card that destroys all face-up monsters on the fiels, if there are 4 or more monsters on the field. Keep your monsters face-down, so only opponent's monsters will be destroyed. 
A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Valiant Souls  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
