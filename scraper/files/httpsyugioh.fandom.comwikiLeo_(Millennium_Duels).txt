 Leo , known as  Rua  ( 龍亞 ,  Rua ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Leo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
