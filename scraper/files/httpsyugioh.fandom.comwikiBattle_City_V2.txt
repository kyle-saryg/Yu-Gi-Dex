 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 
 
 
 Battle City V2  was a tournament mentioned in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . As its name suggests, it is the second  Battle City  tournament.
 It was won by  Yugi Muto . When  Jaden Yuki  travelled back in time to Duel Yugi, he found a flyer featuring a photo of  Yugi Muto , holding the  Egyptian Gods  and accompanied by  Téa Gardner ,  Joey Wheeler  and  Tristan Taylor  after the tournament. [1]
