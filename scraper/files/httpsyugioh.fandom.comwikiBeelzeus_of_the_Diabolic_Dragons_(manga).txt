Main card page: " Beelzeus of the Diabolic Dragons " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Archfiend "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「デーモン」 .   As its English name contains "Archfiend", it  may  be treated a member of the "Archfiend" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
