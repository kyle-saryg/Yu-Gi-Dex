 Red-Eyes B. Dragon  is a character version of the card, " Red-Eyes Black Dragon ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , as a Single Duel opponent.  In  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 , he is a Limited Duel duelist who challenges the player not to use  Effect monsters .
 This character is unlocked as a Single Duel opponent.
