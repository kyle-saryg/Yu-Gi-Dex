 If you  Synchro Summon  this card using a Level 2  Tuner monster  and a Level 6 non-Tuner monster, you can immediately set up a Level 10  Synchro Monster , especially " Satellite Warrior ".
 Level 6 non-Tuner monsters that are easily Special Summoned include " Hieratic Dragon of Tefnuit ", " Gillagillancer " and any Level 6  Fusion Monster  that can be summoned by " Ready Fusion ". 
 This card's  Quick Effect  is meant to set up the Synchro Summon of " Shooting Star Dragon " using any Level 2  Tuner Synchro Monster , ideally one that was used as  Synchro Material  for this card and revived by its effect.
