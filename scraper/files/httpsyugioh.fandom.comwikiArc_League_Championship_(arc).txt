 The  Arc League Championship  is the second arc of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime, encompassing the preliminary rounds of the  Arc League Championship  tournament. 
 A  Duel Monsters  tournament hosted by  Declan  and  Henrietta Akaba  for the purpose of recruiting  Lancers  that are required to battle against  Duel Academy  and protect the  Standard Dimension .
