 Names 
 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Προσκολλητική Τρύπα Παγίδα ") translates to " Adhesive Trap Hole ". A possible translation of the OCG name of this card (" Adhesive Pitfall ") in Greek could be " Προσκολλητικός Λάκκος ".
