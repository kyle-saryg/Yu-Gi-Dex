 Activate  this card by  targeting   monsters  your opponent  controls , up to the number of  co-linked  monsters you control; those monsters cannot activate their  effects  while you control co-linked monsters, also  destroy  this card during the  End Phase  of this  turn . After  damage calculation , if your  Cyberse   Link Monster   battled : Place 1  Battle Buffer Counter  on this card. The  ATK  of all Cyberse Link Monsters you control become their  original ATK  + the number of Battle Buffer Counters on this card x 700. [Note 1]
