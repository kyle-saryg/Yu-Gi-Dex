 Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction Bundles 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction  promotional cards  are a set of 3 cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction . The 3 cards in the English, German and Spanish promotions were all used in the  anime  by  Yugi Muto .
 
 Breakdown 
 In English.
 
 1  Effect Monster 
 2  Spell Cards 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 In French, German, Italian, and Spanish.
 
 1  Effect Monster 
 1  Spell Card 
 1  Trap Card 
 3  Prismatic Secret Rares
