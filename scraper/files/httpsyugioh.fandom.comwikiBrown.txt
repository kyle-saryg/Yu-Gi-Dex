 Brown , known as  Yuiitsu Kurokawa  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is an  Obelisk Blue  student.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Brown is found at the  Obelisk Blue  dorm or the Stadium, and is grouped with  Truly ,  Celia ,  Seydina ,  Dante  and  Rock  as each wield a Deck of one of the six  Attributes . Brown is the group's  DARK  user.
 Upon acquiring his  Deck Recipe , Brown also gifts the player with a " Two-Mouth Darkruler " card.
 
 Tag Force 3 
 In Obelisk Blue  Syrus ' third event, Brown and  Dante  Tag Duel against the player and Syrus.

 Decks 
 Brown uses  DARK  Decks  in every game. He mostly uses  Fiend - Type  monsters with disruptive battle-based effects in the first installment, while his Decks in the later games are more  Beatdown -oriented and also feature " Virus " cards.
