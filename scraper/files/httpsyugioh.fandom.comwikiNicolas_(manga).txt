 Nicolas  (ニコラス,  Nikorasu ) is a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . 
 
 Biography 
 Nicolas placed a wager on one of  Leo 's  Duels , and when the latter lost and could not afford his side of the bet, Nicolas attacked him. [1]
