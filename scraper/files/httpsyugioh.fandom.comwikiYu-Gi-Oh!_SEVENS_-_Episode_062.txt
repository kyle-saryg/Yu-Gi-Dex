 " The☆Lukeman! The☆Lukeman!! " is the sixty-second episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on August 22, 2021.
 Tiger  tells  Yuga  and co. that  Luke  hasn't come home, so they go look for him. They arrive at the Goha Sixth Quarry, but standing in their way is  Yuo ! They then hear someone yell, "hold it right there!" Just who is it...?!
 
 Featured Duel: "The☆Lukeman" VS. Yuo Goha 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
