 Members 
 
 
 
 
 Summoned Skull , known as " Daemon’s Summoning " (デーモンの 召 喚   Dēmon no Shōkan ) in Japanese, is a  series  of  Level / Rank  6  DARK   Fiend   monsters  with 2500  ATK  and 1200  DEF . It is a sub-series of the " Archfiend "  archetype , comprised of " Summoned Skull " and its  retrains .
