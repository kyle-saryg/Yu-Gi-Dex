 Gillian , known as  Mamoru Hatsugokoro  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is a  Slifer Red  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 , his name is given to  Yasunobu Hamada .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 - 6 , his name is given to  Shuji Maruyama .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , when the player receives his  Deck Recipe , they also receive a " Skull Servant ".  He is also your opponent in the practical section of the first test.
 Whenever you talk to him he always reminds you of the basics of dueling, and he berates you for forgetting them if you lose to him.

 Decks 
 Gillian uses  WATER  Decks  in the first two  Tag Force  games. His Deck in the first game is identical to the player's Starter Deck in that game.
 In  Tag Force 3 , Gillian uses  Warrior  Decks . His first Deck is once again the same as the player's Starter Deck, but his later Decks are more oriented towards the  X-Saber  archetype.
