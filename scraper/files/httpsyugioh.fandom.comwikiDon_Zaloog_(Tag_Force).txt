 Don  Zaloog  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Don Zaloog , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , Don Zaloog appears during the game's third part, partnered with  Chazz Princeton . After completing the game, he can be found in the game's first part at the  Slifer Red  dorm after 10:00 PM. Upon acquiring his  Deck Recipe , Don Zaloog also gifts the player with three copies of the " Dark Scorpion - Tragedy of Love " card.
