      The  Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game .     
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game  is a game released by Mattel, based on the  Capsule Monsters  game from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters  anime series, which was partly based on  Capsule Monster Chess  from early issues of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
       The  Capsule Monsters  game     
 The objective of the game is to defeat your opponent's  King . The first player to reduce their opponent's King's Hit Points to 0 is the winner. The Game has two different levels, the  Basic Game  for beginners and the  Advanced Game  for more experienced players.
 
       Capsule Monsters     
 The game was little known and production was apparently discontinued after the release of just a two-player Starter Set and one Booster Pack.
