 Features 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Secret Shiny Box  contains:
 
 1 of 3 specific archetypes (" Sky Striker ", " Mayakashi ", or " Witchcrafter ")
 2  Secret Rare  cards (2 per archetype, 6 total) 
 1 Special acrylic stand: (2 per archetype, 6 total) 
 1 Special Duelist Card Protector (70 cards): (2 per archetype, 6 total) 
 Special Storage Box: 1 (1 per archetype, 3 total)
 Made of paper / H 76 x W 175 x D 110 (millimeters) 
 Special Duelist Card Protector Large Clear (70 cards) 
 5 Secret Shiny Box Special Pack (5 Cards per Pack, 40 Different Cards)
 6  Super Rare 
 34  Normal Parallel Rare 
 Some Normal Parallel Rare also come in Secret Rare.
