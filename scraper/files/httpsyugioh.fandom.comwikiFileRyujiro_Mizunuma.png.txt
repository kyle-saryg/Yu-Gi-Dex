 Ridley , known as  Ryujiro Mizunuma  in the original Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. He calls himself  Mad Marsh .

 Etymology 
 Ridley, in the English dub, may have been named for a character of the same name in the  Metroid  series responsible for the  main character 's trauma in specific adaptations of the storyline.
 
 Abilities 
 Biography 
 Deck 
 Ridley uses a  " Zombie " Deck . His strategy is to summon multiple Zombies with 1000 or less ATK so that he can use them for defense and getting card advantage, then use Edge of Darkness to easily search out and summon "Despair from the Dark".
