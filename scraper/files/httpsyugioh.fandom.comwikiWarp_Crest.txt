 Warp Crest 
 
 Japanese:   ワープの紋章 
 Kana:   ワープのもんしょう 
 Romaji:   Wāpu no Monshō 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Type
 
 
 Item 
 
 
 This item's effect can only resolve when there are one or more other "Warp Crest" itmes active in the dungeon. Whenever a monster passes through this item, it can be moved to another space on the field containing another active "Warp Crest".
