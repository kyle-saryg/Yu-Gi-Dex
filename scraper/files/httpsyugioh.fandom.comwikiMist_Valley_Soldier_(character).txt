 Mist Valley Soldier  is a character version of the card " Mist Valley Soldier ".
 He appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . He and  Red Dragon Archfiend/Assault Mode  can be unlocked as a Tag Team, if the player Summons the card, " Red Dragon Archfiend/Assault Mode " in a Duel. 
He also appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia  where he plays a deck titled  Hidden Mist Refuge  and is unlocked as a Tag Team with  Doomcaliber Knight .
 
 Deck 
 Mist Valley Soldier uses a Deck titled "Easy Power Up".
