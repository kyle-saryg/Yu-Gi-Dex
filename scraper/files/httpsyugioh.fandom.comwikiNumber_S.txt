 " Number S ", short for " Shining Number " ( ＳＮｏ.   Shainingu Nanbāzu ), is a sub- archetype  of both the " Number " and " Shining " archetypes. They are evolved forms of the base "Number", which are Summoned by  overlaying  their base counterpart through  Shining Xyz Evolution . They are used by  Yuma Tsukumo  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  manga .
 They are the manga counterparts of the " Number C " archetype, as they use  Shining  Xyz Evolution as opposed to the  Chaos  Xyz Evolution.
 
 Members 
 So far, 3 "Number S" are known to exist. See  List of "Number S" cards .
