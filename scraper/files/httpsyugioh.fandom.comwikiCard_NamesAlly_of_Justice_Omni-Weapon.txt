 Names 
 
 The unofficial Arabic name given (نصير العدالة: السلاح الفتّاك  Naṣīru l-ʿAdālah: as-Silāḥu l-Fattāk ) translates to  Ally of Justice: Lethal Weapon . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Σύμμαχος της Δικαιοσύνης Θανάσιμο Όπλο ) is a translation of the Japanese name of this card ( Ally of Justice - Lethal Weapon ).
