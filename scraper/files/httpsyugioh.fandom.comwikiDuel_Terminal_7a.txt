 7a  is the first part of  Duel Terminal 7  made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Sacred Star Knights!!  and was released September 28, 2012.

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. It also includes further support for known archetypes: " Gem-Knight ", " Laval ", " Gusto ", and " Gishki ". Introduces the " Constellar " and " lswarm " archetypes. Introduces  Aporia ,  Slifer Red Blair Flannigan  and  Kite Tenjo  as duelable opponents.
