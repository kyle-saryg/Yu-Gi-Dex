 " Ultimate Crystal " (Japanese:  究 極 宝 玉 神   Kyūkyoku Hōgyokushin  lit. "Ultimate Gem God") is a small  archetype  of  Dragon   monsters  with 4000  ATK  and 0  DEF  used by  Jesse Anderson  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime that  support  the " Crystal Beast " archetype. It is also a sub-archetype of the " Crystal " archetype. All members of this archetype are  LIGHT  monsters, except for the  DARK  " Rainbow Dark Dragon ".
 Members of this archetype in languages outside Japanese do not have the words "Ultimate Crystal" in their names, but instead include  archetype conditions  specifying that they are members. Prior to the release of  Legendary Duelists: Ancient Millennium , all support cards for this archetype instead named " Rainbow Dragon " and "Rainbow Dark Dragon" specifically in their text. With the release of the Fusion Monster "Rainbow Overdragon", all members and support for this archetype received errata to make this archetype clear (except for " Rainbow Path ", but since it only affects monsters in the Main Deck, it cannot search "Rainbow Overdragon").
