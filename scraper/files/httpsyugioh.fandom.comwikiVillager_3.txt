 Villager 3  is one of the villagers who goes to the Dueling Grounds in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories .
 He meets the  prince  at the Dueling Grounds, although he is unaware of his royal status.
 The prince can only Duel him when he first visits the Dueling Grounds, before meeting  Jono . After the fall of the prince, he says he that he does not Duel anymore and only visits the Dueling Grounds for card trading.
 
 Trivia 
 He is the only Forbbiden Memories villager to not appear in The Falsebound Kingdom.
