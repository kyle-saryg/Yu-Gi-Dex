 Each player gains the following  effects  based on the  Attributes  of the  monsters  they  control : ●  LIGHT : Play with your  hand   revealed . ●  DARK : Monsters you control cannot  declare  an  attack . ●  EARTH : Once per  turn ,  destroy  1 monster you control. ●  WATER : During your  End Phase ,  discard  1 card. ●  FIRE : During your End Phase, take 1000  damage . ●  WIND : You cannot  activate   Spell Cards .
