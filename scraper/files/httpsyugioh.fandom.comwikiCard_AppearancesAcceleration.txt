 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 This  Action Card  appears to be exclusive to the " Crossover " and " Crossover Acceleration " Action Fields. 
 In  episode 78 , during  Yuya Sakaki 's  Turbo Duel  against  Shinji Weber , Yuya finds and activates this card to avoid taking damage from the effect of Shinji's " Battlewasp - Dart the Hunter ". Four turns later in the  following episode , Yuya finds and activates the Action Card " Encore " to copy the effect of this card in the Graveyard and avoid taking damage from the effect of Shinji's " Battlewasp - Hama the Conquering Bow ". 
 In  episode 97 , during the  Friendship Cup  Final between  Jack Atlas  and Yuya, the latter finds and activates this card to avoid taking damage from the effect of Jack's " Scarlight Red Dragon Archfiend ". 
 In  episode 104 , during Yuya's Action Duel against  Aster Phoenix , Yuya finds and activates this card to avoid taking damage from the effect of Aster's " D-Faceoff ". 
 In  episode 119 , during  Shay Obsidian 's Action Duel against  Lulu Obsidian , Shay finds and activates this card to avoid taking damage from the effect of Lulu's " Lyrilusc - Independent Nightingale ". 
 In  episode 122 , during Yuya's duel with Jack against  Battle Beast  and  Sanders , Yuya finds and activates this card to avoid taking damage from the effect of Battle Beast's " Gladiator Beast Fort ". 
 In  episode 145 , during Yuya's Action Duel against Shay,  Yuya finds and activates this card to avoid taking damage from the effect of Shay's " Raidraptor - Rudder Strix ", but Shay finds and activates " No Action " to negate this card; thus, Yuya takes 300 damage from "Rudder Strix's" effect.
