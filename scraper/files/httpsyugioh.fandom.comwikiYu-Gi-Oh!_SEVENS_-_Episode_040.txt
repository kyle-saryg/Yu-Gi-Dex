 " Give Me Jam♪ " is the fourtieth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on March 13, 2021.
 Princess G  will be retiring! Apparently, her record label, Goha Music, has dropped her since she acknowledged  Rush Duels  as legit. After learning this news,  Romin  decides to quit Rush Dueling, choosing  Yuga  as her last opponent and declaring, “this is gonna be my last gig!”
 
 Featured Duel: 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
