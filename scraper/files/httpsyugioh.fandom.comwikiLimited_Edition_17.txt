 Limited Edition 17 
 V Jump Edition 
 
 
 
 
 
 Limited Edition 17  was distributed by  V Jump  for the December Issue of 2009. It contains cards that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  and  5D's  manga series shortly before the set's release. It includes further support for the " Warrior " and " Junk "  archetypes , and members of the  Legendary Planet   series . It is followed by  V Jump Edition .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards: 5  Ultra Rares .
