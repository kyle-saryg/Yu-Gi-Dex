 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom Bundles 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom  promotional cards  are a set of cards included with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom .
