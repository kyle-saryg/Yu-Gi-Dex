 This card can be searched by " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Transmodify ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Soul Rope ", " Resonator Engine ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Summoner Monk ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Giant Rat ", " BOXer ", " Frontline Observer ", " Warrior Lady of the Wasteland ", " Field-Commander Rahz ", " Reinforcement of the Army ", " Red Sparrow Summoner ", " Dragodies, the Empowered Warrior " and " Freed the Matchless General ". 
 Increase this card's ATK to at least 1900 so that it cannot be destroyed by battle at all while in  Attack Position . " Gaia Power " provides the most straightforward boost. Many  Equip Spell Cards  can also raise this card's ATK.
 Similarly, increasing this card's DEF to at least 1900 prevents it from being destroyed by battle while in  Defense Position . The best way to do this is through " D2 Shield " (which can be activated when this card is flipped up in battle). However,  Defense Position  monsters are more prone to destruction by effect. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by  " M-X-Saber Invoker " and " Last Will ".
