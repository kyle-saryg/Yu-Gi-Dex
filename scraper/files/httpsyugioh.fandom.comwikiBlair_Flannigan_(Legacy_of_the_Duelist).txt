 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Blair Flannigan , known as  Rei Saotome  ( 早乙女  レイ,  Saotome Rei ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Blair Flannigan , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
