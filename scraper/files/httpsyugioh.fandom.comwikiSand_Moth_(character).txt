 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 A Sand Moth attacks  Jaden Yuki ,  Jesse Anderson ,  Adrian Gecko ,  Axel Brodie  and  Jim Crocodile Cook  when they near  Duel Academy  after it was transported to  Duel Monsters Spirit World . Axel used " Blaze Accelerator " to fire a " Volcanic Shell " at it, destroying it. Two more Sand Moths attack and Axel holds them off as the others flee, again using "Blaze Accelerator". [1] 
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 Sand Moth appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . It first appears bullying  Pyramid Turtle  and then challenges the player to a Duel. After its defeat, Sand Moth leaves Pyramid Turtle alone. By beating it the first time, it can be unlocked as a single Duel opponent and by beating it ten times, it can be unlocked as a tag partner.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2008 , Sand Moth uses a Deck titled "Rock Star" and "My Kingdom".
