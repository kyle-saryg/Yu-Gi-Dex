 Masami Iwasaki  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices various minor characters.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) he voices Kajiki's father ( Mako's father ) and Isono ( Roland ). [2] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ) he voices Principal Samejima ( Chancellor Sheppard ) and Isono (Roland). [3] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , he voices Lotten ( Lawton ). [4]
