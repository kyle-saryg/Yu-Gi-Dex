 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 FLIP : Increase your  Life Points  by 3000 points. When this card is  sent  from the  field  to the  Graveyard , you lose 5000 Life Points.

 A kindly-looking being that actually is very quick to anger. It has no qualms about  hitting  anyone with a blot of lightning.
