 " Maximum Unearthed! " is the thirty-eighth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on February 27, 2021.
 Yuga  and co. have found the prototype  Duel Disks . As they've discovered the  Machine Cavalry Duel Club 's secret,  Asana  shows up and challenges Yuga to a  Duel . Thus begins a decisive showdown, with  Rush Dueling  and the pride of the Machine Cavalry Duel Club at stake!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo vs. Asana Mutsuba 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
 Duel continues in the next episode .
