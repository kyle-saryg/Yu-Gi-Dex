 OCG Rulings 
 The effect is an  Ignition Effect . [1] 
 Tributing  this card is a  cost  to activate the effect.  The effect  targets  one face-up " Ally of Justice " monster you control. [1] 
 When " Rush Recklessly " and " Ally of Justice Unlimiter " both affect " Ally of Justice Catastor ", [2] 
 If " Rush Recklessly " is activated first and then the effect of " Ally of Justice Unlimiter " is activated, then the ATK of " Ally of Justice Catastor " will be 4400 - its  Original ATK , doubled. [2] 
 If the effect of " Ally of Justice Unlimiter " is activated first and then " Rush Recklessly " is activated, then the ATK of " Ally of Justice Catastor " will be 5100. [2] 
 If " Ally of Justice Catastor " is equipped with " Megamorph " and has its ATK halved to 1100, when the effect of " Ally of Justice Unlimiter " is applied, then " Ally of Justice Catastor " will have 4400 ATK.  If " Ally of Justice Catastor " has its ATK doubled to 4400, then the effect of " Ally of Justice Unlimiter " will not change the ATK of " Ally of Justice Catastor ". [3] 
 If " Ally of Justice Catastor " is affected by " Ally of Justice Unlimiter " and then is equipped with " Megamorph ", then " Ally of Justice Catastor " will have 1100 ATK or 4400 ATK, as determined by " Megamorph ". [3]
