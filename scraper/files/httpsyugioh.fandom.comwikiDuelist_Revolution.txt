 Duelist Revolution  is the first set of the  OCG 's seventh Series after  The Shining Darkness . It is followed by  Starstrike Blast .

 Features 
 Duelist Revolution  introduces  Fusion Monsters  that requires a  Synchro Monster  as their  Fusion Material Monster . It also introduces the " Scrap "  archetype  and provides further support for the " Watt ", " Naturia ", " Amazoness ", " Flamvell ", " Fabled ",  "D.D." , " Genex " and " Ally of Justice " archetypes and series, as well as a great number of  Beast -Type monsters from  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 Includes cards used by  Yusei ,  Team Unicorn ,  Tetsu Trudge  and  Yugi Muto .
 
 Breakdown
