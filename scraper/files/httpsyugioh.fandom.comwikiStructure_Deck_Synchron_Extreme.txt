 Structure Deck: Synchron Extreme  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the twenty-eighth Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: HERO's Strike .

 Features 
 The Deck is focused on Synchro Summons. 
 Includes a record-setting 10 cards that will become the new power of Synchro Summoning! 
 The reprinted cards inside the Deck meet the needs of players, both new and old.
