 If this card was  Fusion Summoned  this  turn  using only  monsters  on the  field  as  Materials : You can make this card gain  ATK  equal to the combined ATK of all monsters your opponent currently  controls , until the end of this turn.  Once per turn : You can  target  1  face-up  monster your opponent controls; until the end of this  turn , that monster has its  effects   negated , and replace this effect with that monster's original effects. If this card is  destroyed : You can target 1 card in your  Pendulum Zone ;  Special Summon  it, and if you do, place this card in your Pendulum Zone, then destroy as many monsters your opponent controls as possible, and if you do that, inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the total ATK those destroyed monsters had on the field.
