 A Newcomer in Alba Litora  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from December 12, 2012. Each pack costs   40000.
 This collection includes Beast, Insect and Machine monsters as well as simple spells.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 9 cards.
 The set contains 112 cards in total
