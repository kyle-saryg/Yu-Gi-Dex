 Structure Deck: Wave of Light  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the 40th Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Cyberse Link , and followed by  Structure Deck: Lair of Darkness . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck R: Surge of Divine Light  in the  OCG .
