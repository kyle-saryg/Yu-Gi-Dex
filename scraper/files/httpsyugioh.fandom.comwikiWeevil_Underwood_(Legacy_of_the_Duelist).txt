 Weevil Underwood  known as  Insector Haga  (インセクター 羽蛾 ,  Insekutā Haga ) in the Japanese and Spanish version,  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Weevil Underwood , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
