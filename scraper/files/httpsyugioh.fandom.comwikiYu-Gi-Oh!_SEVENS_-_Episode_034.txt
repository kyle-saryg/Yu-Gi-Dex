 " The Acupressure Undercountry Strikes Back " is the thirty-fourth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on January 30, 2021.
 In order to discover the secret of  Asana  and co.,  Yuga  sends  Kaizo  and  Sebastian  to  Nail . Before the two of them, who are taking shelter from the rain, there's an assassin of the  Heavy Cavalry Duel Club  who's a denizen of the underground!?  Finger Chikako  appears, and challenges Sebastian and Kaizo to a Duel!
 
 Featured Duel: Sebastian vs. Finger Chikako 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
