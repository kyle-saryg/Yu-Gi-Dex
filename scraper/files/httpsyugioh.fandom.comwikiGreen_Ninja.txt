 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If a  monster(s)  is  Special Summoned  to your  field   face-up : You can  target  1 of them; Special Summon this card from your  hand , and if you do, change that monster to  face-down   Defense Position . If this card is  sent  from the hand or field to the  GY : You can target 1 monster on the field; change it to face-up  Attack Position  or face-down Defense Position. You can only use each  effect  of "Green Ninja"  once per turn .
