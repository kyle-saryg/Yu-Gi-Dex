 Notes 
 
 ↑  1.0   1.1   Despite being displayed as a  Normal Monster  like its  OCG / TCG  counterpart , in the anime "Monster Tamer" functioned as an  Effect Monster  with the following effect: This card can  control  one of your  teammates '  monsters , until the end of the  turn . While under this card's control, the selected monster gains 600  ATK .
