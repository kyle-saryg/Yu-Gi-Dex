 Skye , known as  Sunny  in in the Japanese version, is the pet wolf of  Ironheart  and  Chris . Like her owners, Skye conceals her true identity from the world. 
 
 Biography 
 The Atlantis Battle 
 In the original battle of  Atlantis , Ironheart, Chris and Skye lead a group of refugees from the city to the nearby mountains. Together, they fought in a battle against  Dartz . However neither side was victorious. Chris' spirit along with Ironheart and Skye's continued to live on. 
 
 Present Day 
 Like Chris and Ironheart, Skye was absorbed by " The Seal of Orichalcos " when she tries to help to  Yami Yugi  Duel against one of the  Orichalcos Soldier , but is later released, along with the rest of the Seal's victims. Skye was able to transform into  Silver Fang  when trying to assist the Pharaoh.
