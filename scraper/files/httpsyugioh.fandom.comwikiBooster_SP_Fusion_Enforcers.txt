 Booster SP: Fusion Enforcers  is a Special  Booster  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). [1]

 Features 
 Contains 3 different theme Decks based around  Fusion Summon . 
 Contains cards used by Yuri ( ARC-V  manga  and  ARC-V  anime  versions) and  Sora Perse . 
 Contains members/support for the " Edge Imp ", " Fluffal ", " Frightfur ", " Fusion ", " Penguin ", and " Polymerization " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Invoked " archetype to the OCG, as well as the first support for the " Predaplant " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 45 cards, consisting of:
 
 3  Secret Rares 
 12  Super Rares 
 30  Commons  /  Normal Parallel Rares
