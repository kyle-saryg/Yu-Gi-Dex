 Duel Terminal 5  is going to be released in two parts.  Duel Terminal 5a  is the first part made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Vylon Descends!!  and was released May 29, 2011, and  Duel Terminal 5b  will be made up of the OCG set  Duel Terminal - Raid of the Inverz!!  and was released September 29, 2011.

 Features 
 The set includes 100 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 60 of the cards are new, while 40 are reprints. Includes further support for " Gem-Knight ", " Gishki ", " Gusto ", " Laval ", " Steelswarm " and " Vylon " archetypes.
