 The  2012 Swiss National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Switzerland. It took place in Aarau, Switzerland on May 26, 2012. [1]

 Main event 
 The main event was won by Reto Canova, who defeated Marc Berger in the finals. [3] 
 
 Side events 
 Twenty two people took part in the  Dragon Duel  tournament, which lasted eight rounds. It was won by Fabian Zweifel, who defeated Tim Bauer in the final round. [3] 
 Sixty one people took part in the  Battle Pack: Epic Dawn   Sealed  tournament, which lasted seven rounds. It was won by Michael Mösli and the runner-up was Alexander Schwestka. [4] 
 Fifteen people took part in the  World Championship Qualifier : Regional, which lasted six rounds. It was won by Noah Eigenheer and the runner-up was Alexander Eigenmann. [5]
