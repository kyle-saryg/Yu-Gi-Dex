 Duel Terminal - Vylon Descends!!  is ninth installment of the  Duel Terminal  series which follows  Duel Terminal - Pulse of the Trishula!! . It is followed by  Duel Terminal - Raid of the Inverz!! .

 Features 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints. 
 It also includes further support for  Normal Monsters , the Fusion Series based  Gem-Knight  group, and the Gem series also is accompanied by the Special Appearance of Spell Cards! 
 New " Gem-Knight ", " Vylon ", and " Laval "  Archetype .
