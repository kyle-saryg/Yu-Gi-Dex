 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 108 ,  Ai  uses this card during his  Speed Duel  against  The Gore . He adds this card from his Deck to his hand, then activates it, fusing his " Doshin @Ignister " with Gore's " Gouki The Master Ogre " (because Ai controls an " @Ignister " monster and Gore controls a  Link Monster ) to  Fusion Summon  " Earth Golem @Ignister ".
