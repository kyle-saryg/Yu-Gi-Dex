 Machine Reactor Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the thirty-seventh Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Pendulum Domination Structure Deck . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck R: Machine Dragon Re-Volt  in the  OCG .

 " Destiny HERO - Dynatag ", " Destiny HERO - Plasma ", and " Dark Hole " from  Destiny Soldiers
