 The  Pair Duel Tournament  was a Duel Tournament hosted by  Tyranno Hassleberry  and  Blair Flannigan  for the third-years during the fourth season of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . [1] 
 The Duels only consists of  Tag-Team Duels , with each team consisting of one male and one female, and two seniors or juniors, respectively.
