 Card type
 
 
 Trap 
 
 
 
 When you have no cards in your  hand  or on your  field  and your  LP  hit 0,  activate  this card from the  Graveyard .  Special Summon   Void Ogre Dragon . When this  monster  is  destroyed , you  lose  the  game . All other defeats are  negated .
