 Premium Pack Vol.14 1st Wave  is a Korean pack, containing the cards that were released in the Japanese  Premium Pack 2019  and a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean.

 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 40 cards.
 
 2  Extra Secret Rares 
 9  Ultra Rares  also available as  Secret Rares 
 29  Super Rares 
 All the cards are also available as  Normal Parallel Rares
