 Sherry LeBlanc  (シェリー・ルブラン,  Sherī Ruburan ) is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Sherry LeBlanc , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
