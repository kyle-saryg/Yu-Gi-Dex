 Terence , known as  Yuji Kubo  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  Tag Force  series .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 - 6 , his name is given to  Shuji Kubota .

 Appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , when the player recieves his  Deck Recipe , they also recieve the card " Neo the Magic Swordsman ".
 Terence usually is with  Ivan , and teams up with him for the Tag Force tournament.

 Decks 
 Terence uses  Normal Monster  Decks , with a high number of  Beast -Type and  Beast-Warrior -Type monsters. In  Tag Force 2  and  Tag Force 3 , he also uses " Lycanthrope " and " Cú Chulainn the Awakened ".
