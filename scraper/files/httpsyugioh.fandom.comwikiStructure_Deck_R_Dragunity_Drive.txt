 Structure Deck R: Dragunity Drive  is a  Structure Deck R  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the eleventh Deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck R series, following  Structure Deck R: Machiners Command .
 As with other products in the Structure Deck R series, it is based on an older Structure Deck, namely  Structure Deck: Dragunity Drive .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Structure Deck R: Dragunity Drive  contains:
 
 1 Preconstructed Deck of 43 cards 
 1  Legend of the Dragon Ravine Pack  that contains 5 cards. The set contains 1 Ultra Rare and 4 Super Rares. These 5 cards are also available as Secret Rare. 
 1 Double-sided  Playmat /Playing Guide
