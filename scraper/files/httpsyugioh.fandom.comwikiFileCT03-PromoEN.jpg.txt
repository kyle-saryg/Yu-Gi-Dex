 Collectible Tins 2006 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Collectible Tins 2006  are a set of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game   collectible tins . They were released in 2 waves:
 
 Collectible Tins 2006 Wave 1 
 Collectible Tins 2006 Wave 2 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Collectible Tin 2006  contains 5  Booster Packs  and 1  Secret Rare  promotional card.
