 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 
 
 
 Rare Hunters  are generic characters in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . They cannot be unlocked, only appearing as opponents. This is a video game depiction of a  Rare Hunter , a member of a group from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 They appear as opponents during the  special events   Attack of the Rare Hunters! Yami Marik Returns!  and  Unleash the Egyptian God Card! Yami Marik Returns II . They also appear as an opponent during the special event  Duelist Chronicles: Battle City Begins!
