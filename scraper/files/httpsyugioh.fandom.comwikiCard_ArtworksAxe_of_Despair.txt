 The International version of this card has the head removed, to make the card look less horrific. Some episodes of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime (see  here  and  here ) use the International artwork instead. Also in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , this card was drawn with a different artwork.
