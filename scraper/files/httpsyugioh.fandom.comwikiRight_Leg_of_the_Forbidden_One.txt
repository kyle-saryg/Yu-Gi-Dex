 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Forbidden "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「禁じられた」 .   As its English name contains "Forbidden", it  may  be treated a member of the "Forbidden" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
