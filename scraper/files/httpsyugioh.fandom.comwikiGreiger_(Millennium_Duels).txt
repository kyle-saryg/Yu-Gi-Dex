 Greiger , known as  Bommer  (ボマー,  Bommer ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Greiger , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
