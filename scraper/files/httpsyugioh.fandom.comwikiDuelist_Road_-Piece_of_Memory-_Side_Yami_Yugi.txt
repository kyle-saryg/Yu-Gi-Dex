 Duelist Road -Piece of Memory- Side: Yami Yugi 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duelist Road -Piece of Memory- Side: Yami Yugi  is one of two special packs released for the fifteenth anniversary of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  Official Card Game , the other being  Duelist Road -Piece of Memory- Side: Yugi Muto .

 Features 
 All Common and  Secret Rare  cards also come in a new type of Parallel Rare:  Millennium Rare . 
 Shares its  Holographic Rare  card, " Blue-Eyes Ultimate Dragon ", with  Side: Yugi Muto . 
 Shares one other card, " Harpie's Feather Duster ", though this side has it as a  Secret Rare . 
 Contains  errata  for two previously  Forbidden  cards. 
 Contains two brand new cards. 
 Contains  reprints  of cards that have never been reprinted before. 
 Contains reprints of the three  Egyptian Gods . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 7 cards per pack (2 Secret Rares, 2  Millennium Rares  ( Normal Parallel Rares  in Korean version), and 3 Commons) and 15 packs per box. There are 60 cards in total. This comprises:
 
 1  Holographic Rare 
 20  Secret Rares 
 39  Commons
