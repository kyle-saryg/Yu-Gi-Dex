 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " and " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ". 
 Target " Abyss Actor - Evil Heel " using this card, in order to apply the effects of both "Evil Heel" and this card when "Evil Heel" destroys a monster. By rulings, the banishing of the first effect does not start a Chain, so will work even if this card returns to the field. 
 On a turn after you use this card, " Scanner " can copy the stats of one of the banished monsters (as long as it has a Level). 
 This card also adds up to 900 more damage to the effect of " D.D. Dynamite ".
