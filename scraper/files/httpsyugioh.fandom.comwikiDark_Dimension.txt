 Dark Dimension   is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on September 26, 2019.
 
 Description 
 The gateway to darkness has opened. The next stage of Dueling has arrived! 
 " Palladium Oracle Mahad " 
 When you draw this card, you can show it to your opponent to Special Summon it!
Its ATK is doubled when battling a DARK monster!
 
 " Chaos Sorcerer " 
 This monster can be Special Summoned by banishing 1 LIGHT and 1 DARK monster from your Graveyard!
Once per turn, you can banish 1 face-up monster on the field!
You won't be able to attack with this monster when you activate this effect, so use it as a Tribute or Synchro Material afterward!
 
 " Aleister the Invoker " 
 When this card is Normal Summoned or flipped face-up, you can add 1 " Invocation " from your Deck to your hand.
Use the "Invocation" you added to Fusion Summon a powerful " Invoked " monster!
In addition, you can send this card from your hand to the Graveyard to increase the ATK/DEF of a Fusion Monster on the field by 1000!
 
 " Quintet Magician " 
 A Fusion Monster that requires 5 Spellcaster-Type monsters as Fusion Material!
If you use 5 Spellcaster-Monsters with different names, you can destroy all cards on your opponent's field!
 
 Total of 90 types: 9  Ultra Rares , 11  Super Rares , 28  Rares , and 42  Normals 
 
 Each box of  Dark Dimension  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Karakuri Gold Dust ", " Dimension Explosion ", " Paradox Fusion ", " Karakuri Cash Shed ", and " Fuh-Rin-Ka-Zan ".
