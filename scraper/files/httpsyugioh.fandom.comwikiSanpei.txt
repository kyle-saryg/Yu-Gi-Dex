 Sanpei  ( 三 平 じーさん  Sanpei ji-san ) [2]  is a grocer in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions .
 In the English dub, his line of dialogue, "Sorry, Joey" was given to the male Minneapolis winner of the  4K Media Inc. voiceover contest , Ted Kong Yang. [1] 
 
 Biography 
 He accidentally wets  Katsuya Jonouchi , when he empties a bucket of water onto the street, while Jonouchi is speeding past on his bicycle. He apologizes afterwards and Jonouchi is okay with it. When Jonouchi is sent to an alternate dimension constructed from his memories, Sanpei similarly wets him, but this time is oblivious to Jonouchi's existence. [3]
