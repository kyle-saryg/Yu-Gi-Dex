 Yuma Tsukumo  is a character in the  Duel Terminal  arcade games. This is a video game depiction of  Yuma Tsukumo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime. Yuma is energetic and loves to Duel, but is also clumsy.
 Yuma is available as an opponent. He also appears with  Astral  in a tutorial. In the tutorial, he disregards Astral's advice, in favor of his gut feeling, resulting in a poor move.
