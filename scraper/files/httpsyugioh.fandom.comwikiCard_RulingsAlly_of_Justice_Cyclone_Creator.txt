 TCG Rulings 
 This effect does not  target . You choose the cards to return when the effect resolves. [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 This effect does not  target .  You select which cards to return when resolving the effect. [2] 
 You cannot activate this effect if there are more Tuner monsters than Spell/Trap Cards on the field.  When resolving the effect, if there are more Tuner Monsters than Spell/Trap Cards, it resolves without effect and you do not return any cards. [3]
