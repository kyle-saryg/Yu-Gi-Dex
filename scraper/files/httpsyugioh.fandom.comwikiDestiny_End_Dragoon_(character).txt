 Destiny End Dragoon , known as  Dragoon D-END  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Destiny End Dragoon ".
 He appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , as an unlockable opponent. To unlock him, the player must Summon the card " Destiny Hero - Plasma ".
 To unlock him as an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , the player must defeat  Underground Arachnid  in 3 separate duels.
 
 Deck 
 Destiny End Dragoon uses a Deck titled "Final D".
