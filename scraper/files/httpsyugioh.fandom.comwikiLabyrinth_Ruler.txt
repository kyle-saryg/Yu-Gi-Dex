 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 Labyrinth Ruler  is the Duelists of the Roses version of  Labyrinth Mage , who is part of the Rose Crusaders. He is battled in the city of Newcastle.
 
 Deck 
 His Deck mostly focuses on cutting off the right half of the field with " Labyrinth Wall " or " Magical Labyrinth " to prevent strong monsters from reaching his Deck Leader in conjunction with the Crush terrain, and Summoning monsters advantageous to the Labyrinth terrain.
