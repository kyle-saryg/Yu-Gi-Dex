 Dragunity Legion Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the eighteenth deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Marik . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Dragunity Drive  in the  OCG .
