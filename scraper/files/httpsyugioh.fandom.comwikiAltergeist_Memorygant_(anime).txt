 [  Spellcaster  /  Link  /  Effect  ] 
 2+ " Altergeist "  monsters 
 ( Quick Effect ): You can  Tribute  1 other monster; this card gains  ATK  equal to the ATK the Tributed monster had on the  field , until the end of this  turn . If this card would be  destroyed  by  battle  or card  effect , you can  banish  1  Link Monster  from your  GY  instead. When this card destroys a monster by battle: You can  target  1 monster your opponent  controls ; destroy it, and if you do, this card can make a second  attack  during this  Battle Phase . You can only use each effect of "Altergeist Memorygant"  once per turn .
