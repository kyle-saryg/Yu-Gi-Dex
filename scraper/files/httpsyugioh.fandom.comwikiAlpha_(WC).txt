 Alpha  is a character appearing in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . Alpha is one of the clones of  the player  and as such takes on their appearance. The four clones are named after the first four letters of the Greek alphabet.
 The player must face all four clones, Alpha,  Beta ,  Gamma  and  Delta  in order, without recovering  Life Points  between Duels.
 Alpha and the other clones are not supposed to be available in Free Duel mode, but players have been able to access them there through devices which affect the behavior of video games, in which case the clones have commonly appeared as a red crudely designed person.
