 Absolute Inferno  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on February 20, 2019.
 
 Description 
 New  Fire Fists  and  Nephthys  Ritual Monsters! The soul of the one and only King! " Red Dragon Archfiend "! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Red Dragon Archfiend ",  Jack Atlas ' ace monster "Red Dragon Archfiend" is here! When this card attacks a monster in Defense Position, it will destroy all of your opponent's monsters in Defense Position! 
" Lavalval Dragon ", A Synchro Monster that lets you return 1 card on your opponent's side of the field to your opponent's hand by returning 2 " Laval " monsters from your Graveyard to your Deck. This effect can be used multiple time per turn, so if you have enough "Laval" monsters in your Graveyard, you'll be able to clear your opponent's entire field! 
" Cerulean Sacred Phoenix of Nephthys ", This Ritual Monster lets your destroy any number of " Nephthys " cards on your field and in your hand. Then, you can destroy the same number of monsters your opponent controls! In addition, even if this monster is destroyed by battle or card effect, you can Special Summon it from the Graveyard at the beginning of your next turn! 
" Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Horse Prince ", When this monster is Synchro Summoned, you can Special Summon a Level 3 FIRE monster from your Deck! Special Summon " Brotherhood of the Fire Fist - Rooster " and use its effect to add a "Fire Fist" monster from your Deck to your hand! 
 
A total of 90 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 42  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Absolute Inferno  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies 
 Reprinted cards from previous boxes include: " Laval Cannon ", " Laval Miller ", " Flamvell Dragnov ", " Neo Flamvell Shaman ",  " Molten Whirlwind Wall ", and " Fire Formation - Kaiyo ".
