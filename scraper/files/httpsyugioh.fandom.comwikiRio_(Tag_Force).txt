 Rio , known as  Ryo Kato  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  series. He is a  Slifer Red  student.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 , his name is given to  Masaya Nakamura .

 Decks 
 Rio's Deck in the first game uses a large number of monsters that can  flip  themselves  face-down , several of which have additional effects activated upon being  Flip Summoned . He supports them with effects that increase their  DEF  or punish  face-up  monsters.
 In  Tag Force 2  and  Tag Force 3 , Rio's Decks revolve around summoning, protecting, and triggering the effect of " Criosphinx ".
