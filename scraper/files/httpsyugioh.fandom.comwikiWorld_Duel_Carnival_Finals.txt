 World Duel Carnival Finals  is the third arc of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime, encompassing the final rounds of the  World Duel Carnival , beginning with the  Duel Coaster . Here, the plans of the  Vetrix Family  and  Dr. Faker 's faction collide, with  Yuma  and  Astral  caught in the middle.
