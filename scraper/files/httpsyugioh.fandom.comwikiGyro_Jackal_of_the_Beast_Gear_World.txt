 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 A beast that regularly runs wild in search of pleasure. He's the stingy kind of guy who's only willing to pick fights he knows he'll win, but he's weak against unexpected counterattacks. He tends to get full of himself when he bands together with others, which gives him a wrong impression of his real worth, as he hasn't realized yet that he's useless on his own.
