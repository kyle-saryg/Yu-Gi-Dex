 Gene-Warped Warwolf , known as  Genetic Werewolf  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , is a character version of the card, " Gene-Warped Warwolf ". It appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 ,   Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia .
 
 Deck 
 Gene-Warped Warwolf uses a Deck titled, "Attack and Defend" in  2007 , "Beast Warriors" in  Stardust Accelerator  and another called "Fight to Live" in  Reverse of Arcadia .
