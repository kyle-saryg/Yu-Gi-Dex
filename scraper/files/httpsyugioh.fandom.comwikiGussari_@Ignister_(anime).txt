 [  Cyberse  /  Effect  ] When a  Link Monster  is  destroyed  by  battle : You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand  in  Defense Position . When your Link Monster  linked  to this card destroys an opponent's  monster  by battle: You can inflict  damage  to your opponent equal to the  ATK  of that monster you  control . During  damage calculation , if your " @Ignister " Link Monster battles an opponent's monster ( Quick Effect ): You can  banish  this card from your  GY ; each battling monster's ATK becomes 3000 during that damage calculation only, also you can Special Summon 1 "@Ignister" Link Monster from your GY with a lower  Link Rating  than your Link Monster at the end of the  Damage Step . You can only use each  effect  of "Gussari @Ignister"  once per turn .
