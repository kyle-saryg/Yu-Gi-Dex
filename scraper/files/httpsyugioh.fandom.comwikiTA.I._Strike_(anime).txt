 Effect 
 Condition 
 During  damage calculation , if your " @Ignister "  monster   battles  an opponent's monster: Your battling monster's  ATK  become equal to the ATK of the opponent's monster it is battling, during that damage calculation only, also the  controller  of a monster  destroyed by that battle  takes  damage  equal to their  destroyed  monster's  original ATK  at the end of the  Damage Step . You can only  activate  1 "TA.I. Strike" per  turn .
