 These are the  Worldwide Forbidden and Limited Lists  for the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  World Championship 2015 , effective August 14, 2015. [1] [2] [3] 
 The list is constructed by giving each card the maximum restriction it has between the  July 2015  OCG  and the  July 2015  TCG  Forbidden/Limited Lists. Cards that were not available in either the Japanese  OCG  before July 16, 2015 or the North American  TCG  before July 18, 2015 are  Forbidden ; " Wavering Eyes " and " Return of the Red-Eyes "—which were included in  Crossed Souls Advance Edition  as preview cards—are considered to be  Clash of Rebellions  cards (and thus  not yet legal  in the  TCG ), so they are also Forbidden. Cards that had only been released in the  TCG  as  Shonen Jump  or  manga promotional cards  or as tournament prize cards (except the previous year's  Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series Prize Card ) are also Forbidden; " Ascension Sky Dragon ", the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Championship Series 2014 Prize Card , is instead  Limited . Finally, the  Special Rule  cards " Emergency Provisions ", " Mystik Wok ", " Rainbow Life ", and " Tri-and-Guess " are also Forbidden. [3]
