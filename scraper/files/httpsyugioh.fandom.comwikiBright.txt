 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2-3 
 In  Tag Force 2  and  3 , she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy . She is quite studious, and reminds the player to do his chores if he loses to her.
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 In  Tag Force 4-6 , she is a student at  Duel Academy  in  New Domino City .
 
 Tag Force 4 
       Bright, in  Tag Force 4 .     
 In the second event of Duel Academy  Akiza 's storyline, the  player  and Akiza Tag Duel against Bright and  Trudge .
 In her own story, she competes with the  Player  against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the  Player  will receive a photo of himself,  Cali ,  Naomi ,  Wisteria ,  Kami  and Bright called "Tops Student".
 
 Tag Force 5 
 Bright teams up with  Hana  to duel  Luna  and the player during Academy Luna's third event.
 During Academy  Akiza 's second event, Bright reluctantly pairs with Simon in a Tag Duel against the player and Akiza to drive Simon away as he had been following Bright constantly and would not stop. Akiza's success leads Bright to admire her.
 
 Tag Force 6 
       Bright, in Tag Force 6     
 In Academy Akiza's last story event, Bright and  Naomi  Tag Duel against the player and Akiza.

 Decks 
 Bright uses  Burn  Decks  in all of her appearances. Using a minimal monster count, she can use a larger amount of  Spell  and  Trap Cards  to quickly deal massive amounts of damage and draw more cards for the next turn.
