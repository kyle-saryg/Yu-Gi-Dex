 Jay Snyder , [2]  also known as  Dan Green , is an American voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment . He has provided voices for a number of characters in the English language dub of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series.

 Roles 
 Anime 
 Green's first and most notable roles in Yu-Gi-Oh! history are the voices of  Yugi Muto  and  Yami Yugi / Pharaoh Atem  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . [3]  He also portrays  Timaeus [1]  and  Trudge [1]  in the same series.
 Green reprises his role as  Yugi Muto  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . [1]  He also voices  Beauregard [ citation needed ]  and  Guardian of the Labyrinth . [ citation needed ] 
 Green's most notable role in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  was as  Tetsu Trudge . [4] [1]  He also portrays the  Guard Robot . [ citation needed ] 
 Green portrays the voice of  Tombo Tillbitty  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . [ citation needed ] 
 
 Movies 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light , Green reprises his roles as  Yugi Muto  and  Yami Yugi .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , Green reprises his roles as  Yugi Muto  and  Yami Yugi . [3] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions , Green reprises his roles as  Yugi Muto  and also voices  Virtual Yami Yugi .
 
 Video Games 
 Green reprises his roles as Yugi Muto and Yami Yugi in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum . 
 Green reprises his roles as Yugi Muto, Yami Yugi and Officer Tetsu Trudge in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . 
 Other 
 Green has been a special guest and presenter at several official Konami Tournaments, where he often participates in a  Scripted Duel  and sometimes participates in Q&As. 
 Green has advertised  www.yugioh.com . [5]
