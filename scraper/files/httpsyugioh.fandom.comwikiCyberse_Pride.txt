   Quick-Play 
 
 During  damage calculation , if your  Cyberse   monster   battles  an opponent's monster:  Target  that Cyberse monster you  control ; it cannot be  destroyed by battle  this  turn , also if it  battles  an opponent's monster with higher  ATK ,  pay   LP  equal to the difference in ATK, and if you do, it gains ATK equal the LP you paid, until the  End Phase .
