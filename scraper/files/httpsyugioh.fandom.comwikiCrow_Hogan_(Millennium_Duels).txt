 Crow Hogan  (クロウ・ホーガン,  Kurou Hōgan ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Crow Hogan , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
