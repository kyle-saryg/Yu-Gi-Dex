 Breakdown 
 Each  Soul Fusion Special Edition  contains:
 
 3  Soul Fusion  Booster Packs 
 1 of 2  Super Rares , either " Isolde, Two Tales of the Noble Knights " or " Summon Sorceress " 
 1 of 2 Super Rare preview versions of  Common  cards from  Savage Strike
