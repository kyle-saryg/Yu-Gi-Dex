 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 
 
 
 Mind-Controlled Joey  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . He cannot be unlocked, only appearing as an opponent. This is a video game depiction of  Joey Wheeler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime, while under the control of  Marik Ishtar .
 He exclusively appears as an opponent during the  special event   Duelist Chronicles: Battle City Begins! 
 He has no dialogue while dueling (not even ellipses like other silent duelists), except upon taking damage or losing the duel.
