 Star Boy  is a character version of the card " Star Boy ". This  Duel Monster Spirit  appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .

 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller 
       Star Boy in  Spirit Caller     
 Star Boy appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spirit Caller . By beating him a few times, he can be unlocked for use by the player. He can be found at the north side of  Duel Academy  and at the  Dried Well .
 
 Ability 
 In  Spirit Caller , Star Boy has the ability to allow the player to get better packs than usual when it is available. To improve this ability, the player has to level up the spirit by  Summoning  the corresponding card in a   Duel  and winning.
 
 Deck 
 In  Spirit Caller , Star Boy uses a  Beatdown  Deck tilted "Star Land!"
