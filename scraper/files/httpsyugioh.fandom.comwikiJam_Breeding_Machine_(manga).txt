 Once per turn "Jam Breeding Machine" produces 1 " Slime " with 500  ATK , but you cannot Summon other monsters while they are on the  field . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> During each of your Standby Phases,  Special Summon  1 " Slime " ( ATK  500/ DEF  500) in  Attack Position  to your side of the  field . As long as this card remains face-up on the field, you cannot summon any other monster (except Slimes).
