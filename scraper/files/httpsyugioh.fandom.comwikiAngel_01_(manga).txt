Main card page: " Angel O1 " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Angel "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 天 使 」 .   As its English name contains "Angel", it  may  be treated a member of the "Angel" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
