 OCG Rulings 
 The effect that negates the effects of  Trap Cards  does not start a chain. When the opponent activates a  Trap Card , at the time that effect will resolve, the effect of this card is applied immediately. [1] 
 After this card has been equipped, if the opponent activates a  Trap Card , this effect must be applied mandatorily when that effect resolves. [1] 
 The effect that negates the effects of  Trap Cards  is applied even during the  Damage Step  or  Damage Calculation . [1] 
 This effect can negate the effects of  Counter Trap Cards  activated by the opponent. [1]
