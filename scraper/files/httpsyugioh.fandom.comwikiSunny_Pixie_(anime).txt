Main card page: " Sunny Pixie " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Pixie "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「妖精」 .   As its English name contains "Pixie", it  may  be treated a member of the "Pixie" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
