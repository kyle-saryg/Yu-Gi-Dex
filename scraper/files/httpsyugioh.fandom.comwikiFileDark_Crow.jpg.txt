 Dark Crow Hogan  (闇クロウ・ホーガン,  Yami Kurō Hōgan ) is a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . He is a dark counterpart of  Crow Hogan .
 
 Biography 
 During the second stage of the  D1GP  tournament,  Crow  passes by a  blank   Duel Dragon  card. At that moment, the card creates a dark aura, which takes Crow's form. Crow then had to  Duel  him, and if he won, he would obtain the Duel Dragon.
 
 Deck 
 Main article:  Crow Hogan's Decks § Manga 
 Dark Crow uses the same  " Blackwing " Deck  as Crow, using even the exact same strategies, with the only addition of the  Duel Dragon  card, " Blackwing Dragon Black Feather ".
