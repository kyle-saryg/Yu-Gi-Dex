 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Scrap "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「スクラップ」 .   As its English name contains "Scrap", it  may  be treated a member of the "Scrap" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Once per turn , during your  Main Phase , if you  control  this card: You can  target  1 " Elemental HERO "  monster  in your  Graveyard  that was  destroyed by battle ;  Special Summon  that target. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Une fois par tour, durant votre Main Phase, vous pouvez Invoquer Spécialement depuis votre Cimetière 1 monstre "HÉROS Élémentaire" qui a été détruit au combat (seul le contrôleur de cette carte peut utiliser cet effet).
