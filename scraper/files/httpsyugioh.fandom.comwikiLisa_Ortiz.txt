 Lisa Ortiz  is a voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Ortiz's first major role was as  Serenity Wheeler  in the  second series anime . Other roles include  Cinderella  and  Injection Fairy Lily  and  Toon Gemini Elf  in  Pyramid of Light .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Ortiz voices  Mindy ,  Yasmin ,  Linda ,  Elemental Hero Burstinatrix ,  Claret ,  Ms. Dorothy  and  Maiden in Love .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Ortiz voices various characters including  Barbara ,  Patty , and  Claire .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Ortiz voices  Lotus Hanazoe  in the Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL series.
