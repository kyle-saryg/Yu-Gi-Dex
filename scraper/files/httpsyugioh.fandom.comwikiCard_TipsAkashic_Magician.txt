 Names 
 
 This card can be Link Summoned after Special Summoning " Lava Golem " or a " Kaiju " monster to the opponent's field, returning it to the hand, so that it can be Special Summoned again, Tributing another monster(s). 
 This card's effect upon  Link Summon  is mandatory - to use this card's other effect to excavate cards, Link Summon this card first before summoning the  Link Monster  that will form its  co-link . 
 If you declare a card name of a card that is not in your Deck, you can ensure that you mill as many cards as possible, usually four from your own setup, but 14 is possible if both players control a " The Arrival Cyberse @Ignister " in their  Main Monster Zones  below the same  Extra Monster Zone  this card is summoned to. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card is a key card in any combo revolving around Special Summoning " Grinder Golem " several times in one turn and using its Tokens as Link Materials. For more details, see  Card Tips:Saryuja Skull Dread .
