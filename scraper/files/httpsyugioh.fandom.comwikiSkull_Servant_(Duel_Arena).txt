 Skull Servant  is a character version of the card " Skull Servant ". 
 He appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. His partner ID is P060. He also appears as an opponent at Stage 11-1 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, his partner number was P026 instead.

 Description 
 A skeleton  monster  that can turn up anywhere. Each individual  attack  is weak but a horde of them can hurt!
 
 Opponent Quote 
 Kah kah kah kah kah... (I want to drink tea with  The Lady in Wight  too. Although even if I do... it'll just fall through me.)
 
 Decks 
 A " Wight " and "Skull Servant"  Deck  packed with trouble-causing "Wight" and "Skull Servant". The very first thing you need to be doing is sending each "Wight" and "Skull Servant" to the  graveyard  and increasing the  attack  of " King of the Skull Servants ". Line up alongside " The Lady in Wight " for a great boost to defense, too!
