 Can't See a Thing 
 
 King Dragun  is a character version of the card, " King Dragun ". 
 King Dragun appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . He appears as a Level Six Limited Duelist and he challenges the player to only included  DARK  monsters in the Deck. By beating him, the player gets closer to unlocking a new Booster Pack.
 
 Deck 
 In  World Championship 2007 , King Dragun plays a DARK Deck titled, "Can't See a Thing".
