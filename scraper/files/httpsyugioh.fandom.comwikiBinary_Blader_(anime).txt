 This card gains these  effects  based on the number of  monsters   co-linked  to this card. ● 1+:  Once per turn , when this card declares an  attack  on an opponent's monster: You can  activate  this effect; this  turn , this card can make a second attack during each  Battle Phase , but opponent's monsters cannot be  destroyed by battle  with this card. ● 2: After  damage calculation , if this card battled an opponent's monster: You can  banish  that opponent's monster.
