 Axel Brodie , known as  Austin O'Brien  (オースチン・オブライエン,  Ōsuchin Oburaien ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Axel Brodie , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2 
 In  Syrus Truesdale 's fifth story event, Axel and  Vice-Chancellor Bonaparte  Tag Duel against the player and Syrus.
 Axel teams up with  Professor Viper  to duel against the player and  Jesse Anderson  during the last event of Jesse's story.
 Axel can duel against the player and their partner in optional random events, with either  Jim Crocodile Cook  or  Bastion Misawa  being his partner.
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Axel and Professor Viper Tag Duel against the player and Excited Bastion during his second story event.
