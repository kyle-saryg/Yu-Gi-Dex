In the anime  Mr. Armstrong  uses an  " Iron Chain " Deck  focused on milling the opponents  Deck  and inflicting  Effect damage . Although he is only featured once in the anime, he is featured in multiple video games, with his Decks being a mix of a  Mill  and  Lockdown  strategies.
