      The aces from a deck of standard playing cards     
 Standard playing cards  or the  French deck  is a deck of 52 cards or more if jokers are included.
 A deck contains four suits; two red; hearts ( ♥ ) and diamonds ( ♦ ) and two black; clubs ( ♣ ) and spades ( ♠ ). Each suit has thirteen cards; an ace, the numbers 2 to 10 and the face cards; jack, queen and king.
 The deck can be used for a variety of different games. In  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series, the following games have been played:
 
 Clock solitaire 
 Four Aces 
 Dark Yugi and Ushio's Shadow Game (Toei) 
 Yugi Muto  once made a card tower using these cards. [1] 
 A number of  Duel Monsters / Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  cards have been based on standard playing cards or games involving standard playing cards, including:
 
 Three Musketeers of Face Cards 
 Jack's Knight 
 Queen's Knight 
 King's Knight 
 Arcana Knight Joker 
 Royal Straight 
 Royal Straight Slasher 
 Straight Flush
