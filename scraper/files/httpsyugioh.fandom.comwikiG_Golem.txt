 " G Golem " ( Ｇ ゴーレム  Jī Gōremu ) is an  archetype  ( series  in anime) of  Cyberse   monsters  used by  Earth  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS . All of its members are  EARTH  monsters, except for the  WATER  " G Golem Crystal Heart ".
