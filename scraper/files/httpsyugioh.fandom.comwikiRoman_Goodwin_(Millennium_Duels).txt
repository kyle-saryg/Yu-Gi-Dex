 Roman Goodwin , known as  Rudger Goodwin  (ルドガー・ゴドウィン,  Rudogā Godowin ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Roman Goodwin , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
