 " Under VRAINS" , known as " Doorway to the Abyss " in the Japanese version, is the thirtieth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. It first aired in Japan on December 6, 2017. It became available with official subtitles via Crunchyroll on the same day. The English dub aired on Teletoon on December 9, 2018 and on Pluto TV February 26, 2021.
 Yusaku  questions why  Varis  did not show up at all during  the Deleted  Incident. In order to clear things up, he enters  LINK VRAINS  to investigate. There, he runs into  Ghost Gal , who is also investigating something. The two then discover a place where something abnormal is happening...

 Featured Duel: Ghost Gal vs. Varis 
         
 The Duel is conducted as a Speed Duel.
 Turn 1: Ghost Gal 
Ghost Gal Normal Summons " Altergeist Marionetter " (1600/1700). As "Marionetter" was Normal Summoned, Ghost Gal activates its effect to Set an " Altergeist " Trap directly from her Deck. She Sets " Altergeist Manifestation ". Ghost Gal Sets a card.
 Duel continues in the next episode .

 Trivia 
 In the dub, Ai says, "Heart of the cards, guide me!" when he's about to draw from Ghost Gal's deck. This is a common phrase used by  Yugi Muto  and  Yami Yugi   from  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  during duels.
