 TCG Rulings 
 This effect  targets  the monster in your  Graveyard  and continues to  target  that monster after its  Special Summoned . [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Amazoness Willpower "  targets  the one " Amazoness " monster that it Special Summons, and afterwards continuously  targets  that monster. [2]
