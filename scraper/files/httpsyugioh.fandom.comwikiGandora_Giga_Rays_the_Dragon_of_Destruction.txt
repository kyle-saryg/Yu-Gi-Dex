 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . Must first be  Special Summoned  (from your  hand ) by  sending  2 other  monsters  from your hand and/or  field  to the  GY . Gains 300  ATK  for each  banished  card.  Once per turn : You can  pay  half your  LP ; apply this  effect , depending on the number of " Gandora " monsters with different names in your GY. ● 1:  Destroy  all other cards on the field. ● 2: Banish all other cards on the field. ● 3 or more: Banish all other cards on the field and in the GYs.
