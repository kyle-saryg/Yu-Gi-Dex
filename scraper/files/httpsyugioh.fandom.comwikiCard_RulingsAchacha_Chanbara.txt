 OCG Rulings 
 This is a  Quick Effect  that is activated in a chain to the effect of an  Effect Monster ,  Spell  or  Trap Card  that inflicts damage to  Life Points . [1] 
 This effect does not  target . [1] 
 The effect of the  Effect Monster ,  Spell  or  Trap Card  that inflicts damage to  Life Points  is not negated. [1] 
 This effect cannot be activated during the  Damage Step . [1] 
 Special Summoning  this card and inflicting damage to the opponent are considered to happen simultaneously. [1]
