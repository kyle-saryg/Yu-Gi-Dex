 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Odion , known as  Rishid  (リシド,  Rishido ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Odion , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
