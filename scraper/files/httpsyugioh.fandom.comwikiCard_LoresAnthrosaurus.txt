 Greek - Ανθρώπινος Δεινόσαυρος ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards  lore) 
 Ένας δεινόσαυρος με ανθρώπινη σωματική δομή που στέκεται στα δύο πόδια. Αν και εξαιρετικά ευφυής, δεν είναι καθόλου δυνατός. 
 
 
 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Man-like dinosaur with a high I.Q. that is lacking in strength." 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Dinosaure au Q.I. élevé et à la force physique faible." 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Ein Menschen ähnlicher Dinosaurier mit einem hohen Intelligenzquotienten, dem es an Stärke fehlt." 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Dinosauro dalle sembianze umane la cui scarsa forza è bilanciata da un elevatissimo quoziente intellettivo." 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Dinosaurio como hombre con un alto coeficiente intelectual pero al que le falta fortaleza."
