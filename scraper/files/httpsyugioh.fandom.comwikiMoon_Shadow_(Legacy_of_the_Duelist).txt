 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Moon Shadow , known as  Tsukikage Fuma  ( 風 魔   月 影   Fūma Tsukikage ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Moon Shadow , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
