 Megumi Han  is a Japanese voice actress employed by Atomic Monkey. She is the daughter of famous Western astrologer and voice actress Keiko Han. 
 She voices Rio Kamishiro ( Rio Kastle ) [2]  and  Iris  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL .
