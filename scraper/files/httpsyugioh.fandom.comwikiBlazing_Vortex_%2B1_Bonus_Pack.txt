 Blazing Vortex +1 Bonus Pack  is a supplementary  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  that is included in the first print runs of  Blazing Vortex  boxes.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 1 card. The set contains 19 cards:
 
 1  Prismatic Secret Rare  (2 Prismatic Secret Rares in Korean version) 
 8  Ultra Rares 
 10  Super Rares 
 The Ultra Rare and Super Rare cards are also available as Prismatic Secret Rare, and they are the same as the ones included in  Blazing Vortex
