 Star Pack ARC-V  is the third booster set in the  TCG  exclusive  Star Pack  series. This is contains Deck themes from the first season of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime, like " Raidraptors ", " Performapals " and " Superheavy Samurai ". It also has popular cards that were available from  Legend of Blue Eyes White Dragon  to  Hidden Arsenal . Designed to appeal to  Yu-Gi-Oh!  fans of all ages, with a cost of $0.99 per pack, Duelists can collect at an incredible value.

 Features 
 3 cards per Booster Pack, 50 packs per display. 
 50 different cards (all reprints): all the cards are from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime. 
 Includes cards used by  Yuya Sakaki ,  Zuzu Boyle ,  Gong Strong ,  Sora Perse ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Yuto ,  Dipper O'rion ,  Julia Krystal ,  Sylvio Sawatari ,  Kit Blade , and  The Sledgehammer .
