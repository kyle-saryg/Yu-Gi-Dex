   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
Increase the  ATK  and  DEF  of all  Fish ,  Sea Serpent ,  Thunder  and  Aqua - type   monsters  by 200 points. Also decreases the ATK and DEF of all  Machine  and  Pyro -type monsters by 200 points. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
Transforms the  field  into a sea. It benefits  aqua ,  thunder  and  sea dragon   types  and disadvantages  machines  and  pyro   monsters . 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
Increases the ATK and DEF of all Fish, Sea Serpent, Thunder, and Aqua-Type monsters by 200 points. Also decreases the ATK and DEF of all Machine and Pyro-Type monsters by 200 points.
