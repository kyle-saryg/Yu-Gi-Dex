 White Rose 
 
 
 
 
 
 Description
 
 
 8 members of the Yorkists organization, the White Roses, each had a White Rose card. The objective of the game was to collect all 16 Rose Cards White and  Red . If the player sided with the Yorkists, they automatically had all 8 White Rose cards, if they sided with the Lancasterians, the objective was to win each of the White Rose cards off the Yorkists.
