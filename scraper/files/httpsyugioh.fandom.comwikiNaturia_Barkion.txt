   English 
 
 
1  EARTH   Tuner  + 1+ non-Tuner EARTH  monsters When a  Trap Card  is  activated  ( Quick Effect ): You can  banish  2 cards from your  GY ;  negate  the activation, and if you do,  destroy  that card. This card must be  face-up  on the  field  to activate and to  resolve  this  effect .
