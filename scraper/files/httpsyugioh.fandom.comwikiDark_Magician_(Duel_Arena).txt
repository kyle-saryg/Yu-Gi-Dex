 Dark Magician  is a character version of the card " Dark Magician ".
 He is available as a Rare Partner, with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P027. He is also available as an opponent at Stage 7-2 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, he had a default of 6 Rental Points instead, and his partner ID was P016.
