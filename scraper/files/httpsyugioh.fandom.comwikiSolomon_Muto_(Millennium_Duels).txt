 Solomon Muto , known as  Sugoroku Mutou  (武藤双六,  Mutō Sugoroku ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Solomon Muto , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
