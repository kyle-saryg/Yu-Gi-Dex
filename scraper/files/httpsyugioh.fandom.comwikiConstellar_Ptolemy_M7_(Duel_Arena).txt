 Constellar Ptolemy M7  is a character version of the card " Constellar Ptolemy M7 ". 
 It appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P021. It also appears as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 10 under Quest mode.

 Description 
 A Divine Star Dragon from the group of star knights known as " Constellar ".
 
 Opponent Quote 
 We Constellar have descended to destroy all evil. Through our  Duel  you shall experience our power, bequeathed to us in order to protect the stars themselves.
 
 Upon defeat:
 
 Impressive. Continue to hone your skills for the coming battle. May you be blessed by the devine  [ sic ]  protection of the stars.
 
 Decks 
 A  Deck  comprised of  LIGHT   monsters  with the "Constellar"  archetype . Many of the "Constellar" cards have  effects  that are  Activated  when they are  Summoned . Use these well to link into a rapid  Xyz Summon !
