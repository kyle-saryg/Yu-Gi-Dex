 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When your opponent  activates  a  monster effect  ( Quick Effect ): You can  send  this card and 1 other  Fairy   monster  from your  hand  to the  GY ;  negate  the activation, and if you do,  destroy  it.
