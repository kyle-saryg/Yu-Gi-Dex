 Shunsuke Kazama  is a Japanese voice actor, singer and actor.
 He provides the voices of  Yugi Muto , Dark Yugi ( Yami Yugi )/ Pharaoh Atem  and  Legendary Knight Timaeus  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ) [2] .
 He continues to voice Yugi and Dark Yugi in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters GX  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ) [3]  and the films  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters: Pyramid of Light  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Super Fusion! Bonds That Transcend Time  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time ). He reprised the role of Yugi in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions , and also voiced  Virtual Dark Yugi  in the same film.
 He also reprises his role as Yami Yugi and Yugi Muto in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .
 He also reprised his role of Yami Yugi in  JUMP FORCE .
