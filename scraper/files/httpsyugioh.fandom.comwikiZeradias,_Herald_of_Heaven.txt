 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  discard  this card to the  GY ;  add  1 " The Sanctuary in the Sky " from your  Deck  to your  hand . If "The Sanctuary in the Sky" is not on the  field ,  destroy  this card.
