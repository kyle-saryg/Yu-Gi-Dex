 Sneak Peek Participation Cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Every three months, a new  Yu-Gi-Oh!   TCG  booster pack set is released with brand-new cards to collect and add to your Deck, making it different, better, and more fun! To encourage attendance a special promotional card is given away to all in attendance.
 All the Promo Cards that were originally released in Japan have been Ultra Rare, while all the initial  TCG  exclusives and set specific promos are Super Rare. Starting with  Ancient Prophecy , all of the promos have been changed to Ultra Rare.
