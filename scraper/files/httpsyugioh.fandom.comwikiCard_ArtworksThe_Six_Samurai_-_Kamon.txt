 Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 In the international version, the thin wooden stick jutting out of "Kamon"'s mouth is removed.  It is possible that this was done to avoid references to smoking due to the full extent of the stick's length being right next to the lit fuse on the TNT stick, giving the illusion of a lit cigarette.
