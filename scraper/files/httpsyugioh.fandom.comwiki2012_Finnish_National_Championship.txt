 The  2012 Finnish National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of Finland. It took place in Helsinki on June 16–17 and was won by by Alexander Trivedi. The tournament was attended by 86 people, 15 of whom got to qualify for the World Championship Qualifier continentals that year. [2] 
 The entry fee was €10 for the main event and €5 for the  Dragon Duel . [1]
