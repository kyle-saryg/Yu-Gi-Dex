 The  turn  that Zoma is  defeated  by an opponent's  monster , Zoma turns into ectoplasm and inflicts  damage  to the opposing player equal to twice the  ATK  of that  monster . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> When Zoma is  sent  to the  Graveyard  as a  result of combat , it turns into ectoplasm and inflicts  damage  to the opposing player equal to twice the  ATK  of the  monster  that killed it.
