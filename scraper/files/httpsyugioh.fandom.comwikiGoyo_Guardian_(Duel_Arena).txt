 Goyo Guardian  is a character version of the card " Goyo Guardian ". 
 He appears as a Super Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. His partner ID is P113.
 He was only obtainable by achieving any Rank from 1 to 50 on the Arena Weekly Win Ratings (Single), by the end of the time period of July 14, 2014, 0:00 (UTC) through July 20, 2014, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received him in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on July 23, 2014.

 Description 
 Guardian of justice is finally back. He roughs up ruffians with his signature move,  Goyo Lariat .
 
 Decks 
 This  deck  consists of powerful  Trap Cards  as well as a wide variety of  Monster Cards . It is a great Deck to restrain your opponents and put them into a corner. Use "Goyo Guardian" to snatch Monster Cards away from your opponents.

 Trivia 
 Most of this character's Main Deck cards consists of the cards  Tetsu Trudge  used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's   anime . This is because Trudge also used this card.
 As a result, this character's Side Deck consists of cards that Trudge used while under the control of one of  Roman Goodwin 's spiders. 
 Despite not being used by Tetsu Trudge, " Deep Dark Trap Hole " is likely included in this Deck since " Goyo Guardian " appears in its artwork.
 This is further reinforced by the fact that " Extra Net " was added to this character's partner Deck after maintenance on January 21, which has "Goyo Guardian" in its artwork, yet was not used by Tetsu Trudge.
