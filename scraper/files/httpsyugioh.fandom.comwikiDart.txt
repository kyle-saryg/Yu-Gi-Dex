 Dart , pluralized  Darts  (ダーツ,  Dātsu ) in the Japanese version, is an  archetype  of  EARTH   Machine   monsters  used by  Sylvio Sawatari  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
 
 Playing style 
 So far, all revealed "Dart" cards are  Level  5 or higher monsters. They seem to focus on quick  beatdown  tactics,  Tributing  themselves in order to power-up another "Dart", preferably " Ultimate Dart Striker ", who will  Special Summon  the Tributed "Dart" monsters back from the  Graveyard  afterwards.
