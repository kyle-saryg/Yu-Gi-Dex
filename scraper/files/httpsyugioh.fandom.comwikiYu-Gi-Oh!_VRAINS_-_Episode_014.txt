 "An Invitation" , known as  "Ghost Girl's Invitation"  in the Japanese version, is the fourteenth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime. It first aired in Japan on August 16, 2017 and aired on Teletoon on October 14, 2018. It became available with official subtitles via Crunchyroll on the same day. Pluto TV aired it January 1, 2021.
 Interested in the incident that happened ten years ago,  Ghost Gal  challenged  Playmaker  to a Duel, with  Ai  as a wager. Learning that he will be able to obtain the program that allows him to infiltrate  SOL Technologies  Data Bank if he wins, Yusaku heads to  LINK VRAINS . Fully aware of the risks, Yusaku still accepted Ghost Gal’s challenge in order to discover the truth about the incident that happened ten years ago. However, he faces a tough battle against Ghost Gal’s elusive " Altergeist "  Deck !

 Featured Duel: Playmaker vs. Ghost Gal 
         
 The Duel is conducted as a  Speed Duel .
 Turn 1: Ghost Gal 
 Ghost Gal   Normal Summons  " Altergeist Marionetter " (1600/1700). As "Marionetter" was Normal Summoned, Ghost Gal  activates  its  effect , which lets her  Set  an " Altergeist "  Trap Card  from her  Deck . She Sets " Altergeist Protocol ". Ghost Gal Sets another card.
 Turn 2: Playmaker 
 Playmaker  Normal Summons " Cyberse Wizard " (1800/800). "Wizard" attacks "Marionetter", but Ghost Gal activates the effect of " Altergeist Kunquery " in her hand, Special Summoning it in  Defense Position  and negating the attack (0/2400). As "Kunquery" was Special Summoned, Ghost Gal activates its effect, allowing her to  negate  the effects of a monster Playmaker controls. She negates the effects of "Cyberse Wizard".
 Turn 3: Ghost Gal 
Ghost Gal Normal Summons a second "Marionetter" (1600/1700), activating its effect to Set " Altergeist Camouflage " from her Deck. Ghost Gal uses the two copies of "Marionetter" and "Kunquery" to  Link Summon  " Altergeist Primebanshee " (2100/LINK-3/↓↘→) to the  Extra Monster Zone . Ghost Gal activates her face-down " Altergeist Manifestation ", letting her Special Summon an " Altergeist " monster from her  GY ; she Special Summons "Marionetter" (1600/1700). Ghost Gal activates her face-down "Altergeist Protocol". She then activates the second effect of "Marionetter", allowing her to send a  face-up  "Altergeist" card she  controls  to the GY to Special Summon an "Altergeist" monster from her GY. She sends "Altergeist Protocol" to the GY and Special Summons the other "Marionetter" (1600/1700). Ghost Gal activates the effect of "Primebanshee", which lets her  Tribute  an "Altergeist" monster to Special Summon another "Altergeist" monster from her  Deck  to a  zone  that "Primebanshee"  points to . She Tributes a "Marionetter" to Special Summon " Altergeist Meluseek " (500/300) to the bottom Link Point of "Primebanshee". Ghost Gal Sets a card.
 The effect of "Meluseek" lets it  attack  Playmaker  directly  (Playmaker: 4000 → 3500 LP). As "Meluseek" inflicted  battle damage  to Playmaker, Ghost Gal activates its effect, allowing her to send a card Playmaker controls to the GY. She sends "Cyberse Wizard" to the GY. "Marionetter" attacks Playmaker directly (3500 → 1900 LP).
 Duel continues in the next episode .
