 Features 
 This set contains cards from many of the previous  Duel Terminal  sets: " Duel Terminal - Charge of the Genex!! ", " Duel Terminal - Pulse of the Trishula!! ", " Duel Terminal - Vylon Descends!! ", and " Duel Terminal - Ouroboros, Wicked Dragon of Destruction!! ".
 
 Breakdown 
 This set contains 120 cards, which are from the Duel Terminals: 7, 8, 9, and 14.
