 Strike of Neos  is the third set of the  OCG  Series 5 Booster Packs after  Cyberdark Impact . It is followed by  Force of the Breaker .
 This set also hails the return of  Secret Rare  cards in the  TCG , as well as being the debut for  TCG  exclusive  cards.
 This set is also the point in where the Korean sets remained in line with the Japanese ones. Before the merge in where the  TCG  and  OCG  60-card sets were the same, the Korean sets were the same as the  TCG  ones.

 Features 
 This set features a new series of cards centered around the " Neo-Spacians " and " Dark World ", as well as the " Attribute Reptile "  series , and a new  archetype  of cards known as " Six Samurai ", which power up when there's more than one of them on the field. 
 The  TCG  version of this set also includes the eight new cards first printed in the  OCG -only  Structure Deck: Surge of Radiance .  
 
 Breakdown 
 In the Japanese version there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box.
 The  OCG  set includes 60 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 11  Ultimate Rares 
 4  Ultra Rares 
 7  Super Rares 
 14  Rares 
 35  Commons
