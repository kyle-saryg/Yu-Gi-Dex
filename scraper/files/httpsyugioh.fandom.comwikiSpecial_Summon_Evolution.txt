 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yuya Sakaki ,  Declan Akaba ,  Sora Perse ,  Gong Strong , and  Shay Obsidian . 
 Contains members/support for the " D/D ", " Frightfur ", " Performapal ", " Raidraptor , and " Superheavy Samurai " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5  Secret Rare  cards.
