 Psychic Time Warp  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from January 30, 2013. Each pack costs   10.
 This collection includes Psychic, Sea Serpent, Dinosaur, Rock, Fish monsters and Continuous Spell Cards as well as the Bam-O-Ween 2012 cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 9 cards.
