 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Butterfly "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 蝶 」 .   As its English name contains "Butterfly", it  may  be treated a member of the "Butterfly" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
