 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Fossil "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 化 石 」 .   As its English name contains "Fossil", it  may  be treated a member of the "Fossil" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  flipped   face-up :  Destroy  all  Special Summoned   monsters  on the  field . Neither player can Special Summon monsters.
