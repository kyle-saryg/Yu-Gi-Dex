 Ghost Gal , known as  Ghost Girl  (ゴーストガール,  Gōsuto Gāru ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Ghost Gal , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
