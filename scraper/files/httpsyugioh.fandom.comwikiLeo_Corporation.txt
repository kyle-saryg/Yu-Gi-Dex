 The  Leo Corporation  (レオ・コーポレーション  Reo Kōporēshon ) is a gaming company in  Paradise City . The company created " Solid Vision  with mass", which led to the birth of  Action Duels . Same as the company  Industrial Illusions , this company makes cards. Its CEO is  Declan Akaba [2] , who succeeded  his father  after his mysterious resignation and disappearance. [1] 
 
 Technology 
 Leo Corporation's technology allows Declan and his subordinates to monitor every scene in each part of Paradise City through security cameras. Also, it is able to detect whether someone comes from a different dimension if the summoning signatures of monsters from their  Extra Deck  reaches higher levels than the summonings of the students of  LID .
