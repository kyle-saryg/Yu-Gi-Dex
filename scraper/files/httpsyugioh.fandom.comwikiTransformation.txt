 Property 
 
 
 Equip 
 
 
 
 Equip  this card to a player.  Destroy  any  monster  that  attacks  the equipped player  directly , and the equipped player takes no  battle damage  from direct attacks. When this card is  sent  to the  Graveyard , the equipped player takes  damage  equal to the combined  attack points  of all monsters destroyed by this  effect .
