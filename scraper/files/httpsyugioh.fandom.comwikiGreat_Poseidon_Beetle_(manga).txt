 When this card  attacks  a  face-up   monster  in your opponent's  field , if that monster is not  destroyed  by that  battle , this card can attack that monster again in a row. You can only use this  effect  twice per  turn . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> When  attacking   monsters  in  Attack Mode , and only then, this card can attack up to 3 times.
