 Effect 
 Send  1  random   Xyz Material  from a  monster  your opponent  controls  to the  Graveyard , then, if that Xyz Material was a  DARK  monster,  Special Summon  that monster, and if you do,  draw  1 card. Otherwise, halve your  Life Points .
