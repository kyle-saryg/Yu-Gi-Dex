 Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters 
 
 
 
 Joey Wheeler , known as  Katsuya Jonouchi  in the Japanese version, is a character in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dungeon Dice Monsters . He is based on  Katsuya Jonouchi  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Description 
 An Enigma with untold potential. His constantly evolving skills and recklessly unpredictable strategy defy all logic and convention.
