 Hobson , known as  Daimon  (大門  Daimon ) in the Japanese version is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He serves as butler to the Kaiba family.
 Hobson is the present-day incarnation of  Gebelk .
 
 Biography 
 He was once the butler of  Gozaburo Kaiba . After his death, he became the butler of Gozaburo's successor,  Seto Kaiba , and aided in the kidnapping of  Solomon Muto . [1]
