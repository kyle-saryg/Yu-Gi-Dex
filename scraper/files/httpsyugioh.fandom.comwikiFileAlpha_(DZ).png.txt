 Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL 
 
 
 
 Alpha  is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  manga. He is a member of the  Team Air Force , who enters the  Team Duel Tournament .

 Biography 
 Alpha enters the  Team Duel Tournament  as part of the  Team Air Force . The team is able to earn a total of 7  Digital Stones . With only 1 Digital Stone remaining for them to advance in the tournament, Alpha and his team challenge  Team ZEXAL , who is the only team besides them who has only 1 Digital Stone remaining. Team ZEXAL accepts the challenge, and the match begin with Alpha vs.  Yuma Tsukumo . [1]
