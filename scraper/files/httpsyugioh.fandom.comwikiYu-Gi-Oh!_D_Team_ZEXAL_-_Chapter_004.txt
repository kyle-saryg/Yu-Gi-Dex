 "Conclusion of the Fierce Fight!!"  is the fourth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL  manga. It was first published July 3, 2012, in the  Saikyō Jump  magazine.
 
 Summary 
   This summary is currently incomplete. 
 Yuma  continues his  Duel  against  Shark , with their  Teams ' badges and  Tokunosuke  on the line. Yuma apparently has taken advantage of Shark after the use of a  Trap Card , but Shark manages to make a comeback by  Summoning  his own " Number ", " Shark Drake "!
 
 
 Featured Duel: Yuma Tsukumo VS Shark 
 Duel continues from  the previous chapter . 
 Turn 7: Shark 
 Shark   activates  " Monster Reborn ",  Special Summoning  " Hammer Shark " from his  Graveyard . He then Special Summons " Shark Stickers " (200/1000) from his  hand  via its own  effect . Shark then activates " Star Changer " increasing the  Level  of "Shark Stickers" by 1 ("Shark Stickers": Level 3 → 4). He then  Overlays  his three Level 4 monsters to Xyz Summon " Number 32: Shark Drake " (2800/2100) in  Attack Position . Shark  attacks  " Gagaga Gardna " with "Shark Drake", but  Yuma  activates the effect of "Gagaga Gardna",  discarding  a card to prevent its  destruction by battle .
 Turn 8: Yuma 
Yuma  Sets  three cards.
 Turn 9: Shark 
Shark activates " Forbidden Chalice ",  targeting  Yuma's "Gagaga Gardna". Until the  End Phase , its  ATK  is increased by 400, but its effects are  negated . Shark attacks "Gagaga Gardna" with "Shark Drake",  destroying  it. He activates the effect of "Shark Drake",  detaching  an  Xyz Material  in order to Special Summon the destroyed "Gagaga Gardna" (1500/2000) in Attack Position, though its ATK is decreased by 1000 (ATK 1500 → 500), and to allow "Shark Drake" to attack it once again. Yuma activates his  face-down  " Impenetrable Attack ", negating all  Battle Damage  to Yuma's  Life Points . Yuma also activates the effect of "Gagaga Gardna", discarding a card to prevent its destruction by battle.
 Turn 10: Yuma 
Yuma  Normal Summons  " Achacha Archer " (1200/600) and activates its effect, inflicting 500 points of damage to Shark's Life Points (Shark: 1500 → 1000). He activates his face-down " Call of the Haunted ", Special Summoning " Number 39: Utopia " (2500/2000) from his Graveyard in Attack Position.
 Turn 11: Shark 
Shark attacks "Achacha Archer" with "Shark Drake", destroying it (Yuma: 1900 → 300). He activates the effect of "Shark Drake", detaching an Xyz Material to Special Summon the destroyed "Achacha Archer" in Attack Position, decreasing its ATK by 1000 (ATK: 1200 → 200), and attacking once again. "Achacha Archer" is destroyed (Yuma: 300 → 0).
