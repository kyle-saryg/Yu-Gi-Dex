 The Dark Creator , known as  Dark Creator  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card " The Dark Creator ".
 It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , as an unlockable Free Duel opponent.
 
 Deck 
 The Dark Creator  uses a Deck made up entirely of Monsters, appropriately entitled  Monsters Only .
