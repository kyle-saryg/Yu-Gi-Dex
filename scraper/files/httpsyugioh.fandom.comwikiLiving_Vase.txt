 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 A living vase that bites enemies and blinds them with a blast of its flower. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Questo vaso morde i suoi nemici dopo averli accecati con le nuvole di polline emesse dai fiori in esso contenuti.

   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
 An animated floral vase that scatters pollen from its flowers. While it is scattering pollen, it lunges and bites.
