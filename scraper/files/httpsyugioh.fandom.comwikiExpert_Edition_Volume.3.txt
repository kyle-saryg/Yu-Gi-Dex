 Expert Edition Volume 3  is a  Reprint   Booster Pack  in the  OCG  that is a combination of  Soul of the Duelist ,  Rise of Destiny ,  Flaming Eternity , and  The Lost Millennium 
 The  TCG  equivalent of this Booster Pack is  Dark Revelation Volume 3 .
 The rarities exactly duplicate the parent sets, but do not include  Ultimate Rares .
