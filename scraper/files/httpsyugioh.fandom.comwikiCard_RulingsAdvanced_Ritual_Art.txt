 TCG Rulings 
 Mentions in Other Rulings 
 Djinn of Rituals : You cannot use this card for a  Ritual Summon  conducted with “ Advanced Ritual Art .” 
 OCG Rulings 
 The Normal Monsters are sent from the Deck to the Graveyard when this effect resolves. [1] 
 The monster  Special Summoned  by this card is treated as properly  Ritual Summoned . [1] 
 This effect does not  target . [1] 
 " Nekroz of Valkyrus " is a  Ritual Monster  with the text " Must be Ritual Summoned without using any Level 8 monsters, and cannot be Special Summoned by other ways ". It can be Ritual Summoned by the effect of " Advanced Ritual Art ", but you cannot send a Level 8  Normal Monster  from the Deck to the Graveyard. (You can send Normal Monsters of other Levels, as long as their total Levels add up to 8.) [2] 
 When  Ritual Summoning  with " Advanced Ritual Art ", you can only send  Normal Monsters  to the Graveyard. You cannot send monsters like " Ritual Raven " or " Djinn Releaser of Rituals " to the Graveyard. Furthermore, you may not banish a monster like " Djinn Releaser of Rituals " from your Graveyard when  Ritual Summoning  with " Advanced Ritual Art ". [3] 
 You can  Ritual Summon  " Evigishki Levianima ", etc. with the effect of "Advanced Ritual Art". Also, you can Ritual Summon " Demise, King of Armageddon " and " Black Luster Soldier " with " Contract with the Abyss " and " Earth Chant ". [4] 
 If " Advanced Ritual Art " is activated while " Macro Cosmos " is applying, the chosen  Normal Monsters  are  banished  without being sent the Graveyard, but the Ritual Monster can still be Ritual Summoned. [5] 
 If " Divine Grace - Northwemko " is  Ritual Summoned  by " Advanced Ritual Art ", then the Normal Monsters sent from the Deck to the Graveyard will count towards " the number of monsters used to Ritual Summon this card ". [6] 
 Mentions in Other Rulings 
 Amorphactor Pain : You can Ritual Summon " Amorphactor Pain, the Imagination Dracoverlord " with " Advanced Ritual Art " or " Earth Chant ". [7]
