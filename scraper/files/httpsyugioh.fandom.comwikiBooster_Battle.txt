A  Booster Battle  is a new type of  Duel  introduced in  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM  on October 18, 2013.
 
 Rules 
 Buy 2 Booster Packs and get a chance to win another Booster Pack for free 
 Duel an opponent. Your deck is made of the cards found in your 2 Booster Packs. Your opponent uses the cards for his 2 Booster Packs from the same collection. The size of the deck is 18, instead of the regular 15. 
 You cannot use  Power-Ups , Shuffle your deck or ask for friend's help. 
 If you win: Get a 3rd Booster Pack for free. 
 If you lose: Keep the cards. 
 If you lose: Retry using the same cards and extra 5000 Life Points at the cost of   7. 
 Currently, only 3 Booster Packs have this feature. The Booster Packs with this feature are:
 Alba Litora's Elite  (added on November 27, 2013) 
 Root of Evil  (added on October 18, 2013) 
 Wings of Destiny  (added on November 13, 2013)
