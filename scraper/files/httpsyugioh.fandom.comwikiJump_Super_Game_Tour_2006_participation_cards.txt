 Jump Super Game Tour 2006 participation cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Jump Super Game Tour  2006 participation cards  are 3 " Token " cards that were distributed at the Jump Super Game Tour event that was held from July 26 to August 26, 2006. 
 The  Ojama Green  "Token" was given to people who played the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force  video game, the  Ojama Black  "Token" was given to people who played the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Online  video game, and the  Ojama Yellow  "Token" was given to the participants of the OCG tournament. The players who won 3 consecutive Duels got the 3 cards.
