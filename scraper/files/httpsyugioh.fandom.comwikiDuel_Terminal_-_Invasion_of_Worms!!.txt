 Duel Terminal - Invasion of Worms!!  is second installment of the  OCG 's  Duel Terminal  series, following  Duel Terminal - Synchro Awakening!! , and followed by  Duel Terminal - Justice Strikes Back!! .

 Features 
 Introduces the " Genex "  Archetype . Includes further support for the " Ice Barrier ", " Mist Valley ", " X-Saber ", " Ally of Justice ", " Worm ", and " Flamvell " cards. Introduces  Mai Valentine ,  Jesse Anderson ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Jack Atlas  and  Tetsu Trudge  as duelable opponents.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards. This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 10  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 30  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
