 Soul Fusion  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) [1]  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixth set in the  OCG ' s  10th series , following  Cybernetic Horizon  and followed by  Savage Strike .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Theodore Hamilton  and  George Gore . 
 Includes new members and/or support for the " Cyber ", " Fur Hire ", " Galaxy ", " Galaxy-Eyes ", " Gravekeeper's ", " Impcantation ", " Krawler ", " Crusadia ", " Sky Striker ", " Traptrix " and " World Legacy "  archetypes ; as well as  Cynet  and  Attribute Spirit  series.
 The  TCG  version includes members and/or support for the " Predaplant ", " Danger! ", " Noble Knight , " Frightfur ", and " D/D/D " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Dinowrestler ", " Orcust " and " Salamangreat " archetypes to the  OCG / TCG;  as well as the first support for the " Thunder Dragon " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 In the  OCG , there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box. The set contains 80 cards, or 119 cards in the  Master Set :
 
 1  Holographic Rare 
 6  Ultimate Rares 
 16  20th Secret Rares 
 16  Secret Rares 
 6  Ultra Rares   (also available as  Ultimate Rare ,  Secret Rare  and  20th Secret Rare ) 
 10  Super Rares   (also available as  Secret Rare  and  20th Secret Rare ) 
 18  Rares 
 46  Commons 
 In the TCG, there are 9 cards per pack. The set contains 100 cards:
 
 8  Secret Rares 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 14  Super Rares 
 20  Rares 
 48  Commons
