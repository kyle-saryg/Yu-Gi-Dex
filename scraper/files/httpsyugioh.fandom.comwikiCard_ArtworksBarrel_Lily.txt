 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , "Barrel Lily" was drawn with a different artwork. In the English video games, "Barrel Lily" has its front sight removed, and later has its barrel recolored.
