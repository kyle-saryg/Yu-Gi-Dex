      Sugoroku's  dogs and jackals board.     
 Dogs and jackals  is an ancient Egyptian game where players race pieces around a board.
 The pieces are large pegs with a dog or a jackal head on top. The gameboard is a rectangular wooden box shaped like a four legged animal. The board is made of ivory carved with a palm tree and has fifty eight holes.
 In the manga,  Sugoroku Mutou  introduces this game to  Yugi Mutou ,  Katsuya Jonouchi  and  Anzu Mazaki . He didn't mention its name, but said that it is an ancient Egyptian game where players race their pieces around a board shaped like a frog. [1]  Sugoroku's board was shaped like a frog and the game is referred to as  frog  ( 蛙 ,  Kaeru ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Character Guidebook: The Gospel of Truth .
