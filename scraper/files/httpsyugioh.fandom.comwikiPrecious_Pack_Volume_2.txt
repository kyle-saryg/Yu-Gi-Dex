 Precious Pack Volume 2 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Precious Pack Volume 2  is a Korean pack in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the second Precious Pack to be released.
 This Booster Pack and  Precious Pack Volume 1  are the Korean equivalents of  Duelist Edition Volume 1 . This pack contains 80 cards from  Duelist Edition Volume 1  plus 5 cards that were previously unreleased in Korean.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 85 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 47  Common 
 20  Rare 
 3  Secret Rare 
 5  Ultra Rare 
 10  Super Rare
