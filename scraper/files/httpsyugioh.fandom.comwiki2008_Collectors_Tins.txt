 The  Collectible Tins 2008  are a set of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game   collectible tins . They introduced the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  series to the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game . They were released in 2 waves:
 
 Collectible Tins 2008 Wave 1 
 Collectible Tins 2008 Wave 2 
 Additionally, the  Collectible Tins Exclusive 2008  were also released, which all feature  Yusei Fudo  on their tins and include different  Secret Rare  promotional cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Collectible Tin 2008  contains 5  Booster Packs , 1  Token Pack , and 1  Secret Rare  promotional card.
