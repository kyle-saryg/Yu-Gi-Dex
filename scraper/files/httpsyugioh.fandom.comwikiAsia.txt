Asia  is the principle distribution  region  for the  Official Card Game , including the countries of  Japan ,  South Korea ,  Hong Kong ,  Malaysia , the  Philippines ,  Singapore ,  Taiwan , and  Thailand , with  Japanese ,  Japanese-Asian ,  Korean ,  Traditional Chinese , and  Asian-English  prints being officially distributed and  legal  for tournament play (though not all prints are legal in all countries). Japanese prints are distributed in Japan, Korean prints are distributed in Korea, Traditional Chinese prints are distributed in Hong Kong and Taiwan, and Japanese-Asian and Asian-English prints are distributed in Hong Kong, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan, and Thailand.
