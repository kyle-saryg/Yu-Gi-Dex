 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 Target  1 " Rokket "  monster  in your  GY ;  Special Summon  it in  Defense Position , and if you do,  equip  this card to it. If the equipped monster is  destroyed  and this card is  sent  to the GY: You can  draw 1 card . You can only use each effect of "Borrel Regenerator" once per turn. When this card  leaves the field ,  banish  the equipped monster.
