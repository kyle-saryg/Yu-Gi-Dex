 Normal 
 
 
 
 Ties of the Brethren can  Special Summon  up to 2 four- stars   monsters  from the  Deck  with the same  Type  as a monster on the  field  by  paying  1000  Life Points  of the player. However they can't  attack  or be  sacrificed . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile>  Pay  1000  Life Points .  Special Summon  2  Level  4  monsters  from your  Deck  that have the same  Attribute  as a monster you  control . They cannot  attack  or be  tributed .
