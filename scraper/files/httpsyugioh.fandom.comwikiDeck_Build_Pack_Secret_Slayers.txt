 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards, and each box contains 15 packs.
 
 3  Ultra Rares  /  Secret Rares 
 12  Super Rares 
 30  Commons  /  Normal Parallel Rares 
 Features 
 Introduces the " Adamancipator ", " Rikka ", " Eldlich ", " Eldlixir " and " Golden Land " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Includes new members/support for the " Guardian " archetype.
