 Buster Blader  is a character version of the card " Buster Blader ". 
 He appears as a Super Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. His partner ID is P120.
 He was only obtainable by winning 30 times in Arena mode (Single Round only), during the time period of October 13, 2014, 00:00 (UTC) through October 19, 2014, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received him in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on October 22, 2014.

 Trivia 
 This partner, along with  Gaia The Fierce Knight , were likely released in commemoration of  Duel Monsters Arena  mode (released on the same day).
 This is also further supported by the fact that the first versions of their Decks comply with the card restrictions of Duel Monsters Arena mode. 
 Notes 
 
 ↑   This update was made in response to  a user's observation on the Duel Arena Forums  of being unable to use the previous Deck due to having 2 copies of the recently  Limited  " Super Polymerization " in that partner Deck, as well as the  Fusion Monster -oriented  Side Deck  being useless, since the partner had no Extra Deck and couldn't have cards swapped into it as a result.
