 TCG Rulings 
 The effects of “ Alien Dog ” do not target. You select the monster(s) when the effect resolves. [1] 
 Even if you have more than 1 “ Alien Dog ” in your hand, you can only  Special Summon  1 when you  Normal Summon  an “ Alien ” monster. [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Alien Dog " which places  A-Counters  does not  target . [2] [3]   You choose which the monster(s) on which you place the  A-Counters  when resolving the effect. [3] 
 The effect of " Alien Dog " can place one  A-Counter  on two different monsters. [3] 
 If you have multiple copies of " Alien Dog " in your hand when an " Alien " monster is  Normal Summoned , then you can only activate the effect of and Special Summon one copy of " Alien Dog " [4]
