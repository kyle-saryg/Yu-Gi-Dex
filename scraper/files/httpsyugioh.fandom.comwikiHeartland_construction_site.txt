      The construction site.     
 The  Heartland construction site  is a construction site located in  Heartland City  at  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
 
 History 
 While  Yuma  and  Tori  are cutting through the construction site, a pair of support beams nearly fall on Tori. A young boy named  Cody Callus  uses a crane to prevent the beams from crushing her. Yuma and Cody  Duel  there, and are then chased out by a  construction worker . [1]
