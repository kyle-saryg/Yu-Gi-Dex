 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  target  1  Fish   monster  you  control  and 1 monster your opponent controls;  banish  both monsters until your next  Standby Phase . If a Fish monster(s) is Special Summoned to your  field , while any card is banished: You can banish this card from your  GY  or your  face-up   Spell & Trap Zone ; all Fish monsters you currently control gain 100  ATK  for each card currently banished, until the end of this  turn . You can only use each  effect  of "Ghoti Fury"  once per turn .
