 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If a  LIGHT  or  DARK   monster(s)  is  banished  (except during the  Damage Step ): You can  target  1 of those monsters; place that monster on the bottom of the  Deck , and if you do,  draw  1 card. You can only use this  effect  of "Branded Regained"  once per turn . Once per turn, when your opponent  Normal  or  Special Summons  a monster (except during the  Damage Step ): You can target 1 " Bystial " monster in your  GY ; Special Summon it. You can only use this effect of "Branded Regained"  once per Chain .
