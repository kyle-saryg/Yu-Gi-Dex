 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  promotional cards  is a set of  Konami's  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  promotional cards given away with the Game Boy game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , published in Japan on December 16,  1998 . Each copy of the game featured three cards randomly selected from the ten-card set. [1] 
 These differ from  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  cards due to the fact that they were released before the  Official Card Game  game was created; this set and the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle  promotional cards  are the only sets to contain such cards.
