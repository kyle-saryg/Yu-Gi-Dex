 " Trump Card " ( 切 り 札 ,  Kirifuda ) is the fourth Japanese opening theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime, performed by  cinema staff . It debuted on October 4, 2015, in  episode 76 . It was replaced by " LIGHT OF HOPE " in  episode 99 .
