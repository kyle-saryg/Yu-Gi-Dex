 Spell 
 
 
 
 Put 3 cards  face-down  on the  field . Perform a lottery to select 1 of them. If the card is a  Monster Card ,  Summon  it. The remaining cards go to the  Graveyard . 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> Select 1  Monster Card  and 2 non-Monster Cards from your  hand .  Randomly  select 1 card among them. If it is a Monster Card, it is  Special Summoned  and  send  the remaining 2 cards to the  Graveyard . If not, send all the cards to the Graveyard.
