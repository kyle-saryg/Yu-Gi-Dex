 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When this card inflicts  Battle Damage  to your opponent: Your opponent must  discard  2  random  cards. Your opponent must have at least 5 cards in their  hand  for you to  activate  and to  resolve  this  effect .
