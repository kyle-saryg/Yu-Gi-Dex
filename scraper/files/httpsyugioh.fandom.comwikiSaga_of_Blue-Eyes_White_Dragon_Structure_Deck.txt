 Saga of Blue-Eyes White Dragon Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the twenty-fifth Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Onslaught of the Fire Kings . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: The Blue-Eyed Dragon's Thundering Descent  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 The "Power Up With Packs!" supplement suggests combining this Deck with "March of the Monarchs" and "Quick Booster" from Cosmo Blazer, "Number 107: Galaxy-Eyes Tachyon Dragon" and "Dragoncarnation" from Lord of the Tachyon Galaxy, "Armades, Keeper of Boundaries" and "Shapesister" from Judgment of the Light, and "Gagaga Cowboy" and "Forbidden Dress" from Abyss Rising.
