 Story 3: Battle City (part 1)  ( Ｓｔｏｒｙ ３ バトルシティ（ 前 編 ）  Sutōrī San: Batoru Shiti (zenpen) ) is an arc in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . In the English dub, it has been referred to as  Battle City Duels  and  Rulers of the Duel .
 It features the preliminary and quarterfinal rounds of the  Battle City  tournament.
