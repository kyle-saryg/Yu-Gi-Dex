 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  episode 82 ,  Chazz Princeton  uses this card during his Duel against  Bastion Misawa . He uses this card to take " Beetron-2 Beetleturbo " from his Graveyard and place it on top of his deck by discarding " Ojamagic ". The effect of "Ojamagic" then activates, allowing Chazz to add " Ojama Yellow ", " Ojama Green ", and " Ojama Black " from his Deck to his hand. 
 In  episode 96 ,  Dr. Eisenstein  uses this card during his Duel against  Jaden Yuki . He sends both this card and " Singularity Fiend " from his hand to the Graveyard in order to activate the effect of "Singularity Fiend". 
 In  episode 150 ,  Adrian Gecko  uses this card during his Duel against  Jesse Anderson  while the latter was possessed by  Yubel . He uses this card to take " Exodia the Forbidden One " from his Graveyard and place it on top of his Deck. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 27 , during his duel against  Brave Battler , a  Knight of Hanoi  draws this card via the effect of " Hand Destruction ". He then activates this card, discarding " Steelswarm Mantis " to return "Hand Destruction" to the top of his Deck. 
 Video Games 
 Nova  uses this in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force .
