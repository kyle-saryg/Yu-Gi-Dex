 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Προσκολλητικό Εκρηκτικό ") is an exact translation of the  English  name of this card. A possible translation of the  Japanese  name (" Instantaneous Bomber ") could be " Στιγμιαίος Εκρυγνητήρας ".
