 Structure Deck: Warrior's Triumph  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fifth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (thirteenth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Fury from the Deep . In the  TCG , it is the fifth deck.
 Although this Structure Deck was not released in Spanish, it is listed on the Spanish version of the official card database. [1] 
 
 Mighty  Warriors  await your commands in the newest  Structure Deck .  Summon  your army and  equip  them with magical swords to wage epic battles in your Duels. "Warrior's Triumph" is ready to play with out of the box, and is perfect for new Duelists, and is packed with powerful cards. Each  Deck  contains 40 cards, including 3 brand new cards and an  Ultra Rare  " Gilford the Legend ". Also included is a rulebook, game mat, and a Dueling Guide which assists Duelists in improving and perfecting the  Deck 's strategies. 
 —  Upper Deck Entertainment

 Features 
 The deck features  Warrior - Type  monsters, and features cards such as " Gilford the Legend ", " Command Knight ", and " Divine Sword - Phoenix Blade ".
 The "Let's Power Up This Deck! (With Booster Packs)" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Elemental Hero Bubbleman " from  Cybernetic Revolution , " Mystic Swordsman LV6 " from  Rise of Destiny , " Elemental Hero Avian ", " Elemental Hero Flame Wingman ", " Hero Signal " and " Elemental Hero Burstinatrix " from  The Lost Millennium , and " Zombyra the Dark ", " Blade Knight ", " Freed the Matchless General " and " Fusion Gate " from  Dark Beginning 2 .
