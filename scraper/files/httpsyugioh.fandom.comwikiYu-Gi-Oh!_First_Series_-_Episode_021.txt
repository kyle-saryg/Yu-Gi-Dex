 Completed!! The Ultimate Game Land  is the twenty first episode of the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime .
 Seto Kaiba  kidnaps  Yugi Mutou's grandfather  and dares  Yugi  to come to  Death-T , an amusement park full of deathtrap games. But Yugi is hesitant to put his friends in danger.
