Main card page: " Submersible Carrier Aero Shark " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Carrier "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 Ｃ 」 .   As its English name contains "Carrier", it  may  be treated a member of the "Carrier" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
