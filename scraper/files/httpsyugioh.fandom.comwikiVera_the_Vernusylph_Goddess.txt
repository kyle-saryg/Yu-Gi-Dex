 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  target  1  face-up   monster  your opponent  controls ; take control of it, and if you do, it becomes  EARTH . During your opponent's  turn  ( Quick Effect ): You can target 1 EARTH monster in your  GY ;  Special Summon  it. You can only use each  effects  of "Vera the Vernusylph Goddess"  once per turn . Once per turn, while you control 5 or more EARTH monsters, you can  negate  any  monster effect  activated by your opponent, and if you do,  destroy  that monster.
