 Jason Adam Griffith , sometimes credited as  J. Griff  or  Adam Caroleson , is a voice actor for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime. He was best known for voicing Sonic and Shadow (and he also voiced Jet from the same series) from the Sonic the Hedgehog series in  Sonic X , another  4Kids  property.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Griffith's first major role in Yu-Gi-Oh! history was portraying  Atticus Rhodes  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . He also portrays  Nightshroud ,  Osamu , and  Harrington Rosewood  in the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Griffith has a number of notable roles in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , providing the voices for  Primo  and  Aporia . He also provides the voices for  Taka ,  Shira  and  Ghost .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Griffith most notable role in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  was portraying  Caswell Francis . He also voiced  Nistro .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Griffith provides the voice of  Reed Pepper  and  Iggy Arlo  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V .
