 Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM Pocket  was a free-to-play mobile version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM , playable on iOS and Android devices. Like the original, Frima Studio were contracted by  Konami  to develop the game; Konami, however, maintains publishing rights. [1] [2] [3] . While the browser version closed on September 30, 2014,  BAM Pocket  continued to operate until September 9, 2016. [4]

 Features 
 While  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM Pocket  is largely similar to its browser counterpart, it does contain a few functions the original did not:
 
 Pro Mode 
 A $4.99 in-app purchase that grants the player access to the arena, the complete story mode, 13 side chapters with new challenges and rewards, the ability to buy BAM Legend cards, and 25 DP.
 
 Save Modes 
 ONLINE (FACEBOOK ACCOUNT)
 Progress will be saved when connected to the App with your Facebook account. If you play from a different device, but connect using the same Facebook account, your progress will be available to you.
 OFFLINE
 Any progress made in the offline mode will only be available on the same device. If the app is reloaded or deleted, progress will be lost. Progress that has been lost in the offline mode is unable to be restored.
