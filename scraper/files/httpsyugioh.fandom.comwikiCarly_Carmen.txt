 V Jump 
 
 
 
 Carly Carmen , known as  Carly Nanosa  in the Japanese version and simply  Nanosa  in the English  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 , is a  cosplayer  who appears in  V Jump . She is a parody of  Carly Carmine , whose Japanese name is  Carly Nagisa .

 Tag Force series 
 Carly uses Decks focused on the  Fabled  archetype, and they are based on the Deck she used in  The Third Charisma Duelist Determining Fight Tournament .
 
 Tag Force 5 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 5 , Carly uses Decks titled  "Duel Terminal is the Best!"  (Japanese: 「決闘端末最高！」)
 All 4 of her Decks have the exact same contents in both the  Main Deck  and  Extra Deck .
