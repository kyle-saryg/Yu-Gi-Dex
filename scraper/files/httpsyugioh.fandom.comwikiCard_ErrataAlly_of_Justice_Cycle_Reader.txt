 [Machine/Tuner/Effect] 
 
 
 You can discard this card to the Graveyard  to select  up to 2 LIGHT monsters in your opponent's Graveyard , and remove them from play .  This effect can be activated during either player's turn. 
 During either player's turn :  You can discard this card to the  Graveyard , then target  up to 2 LIGHT monsters in your opponent's  Graveyard ; banish those targets . 
 (Quick Effect) : You can discard this card to the  GY , then target up to 2 LIGHT monsters in your opponent's  GY ; banish those targets.
