 When this card is  Special Summoned ,  destroy  all  monsters  on your opponent's  field . If this card  attacks  a  Defense Position  monster, inflict  piercing   battle damage  to your opponent. If this card destroys a monster  in battle , it can  attack  once again. When this card is destroyed and  sent  to the  Graveyard , you can  banish  1 " White " monster from your Graveyard to Special Summon this card as a  Tuner monster .
