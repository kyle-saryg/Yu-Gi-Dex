 World Premiere Pack 2022 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 World Premiere Pack 2022  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -only  cards to the  OCG .

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 Introduces to the OCG previously TCG-only cards from  Dawn of Majesty ,  Burst of Destiny ,  Battle of Chaos ,  Dimension Force  and other products. 
 Introduces the " Beetrooper " and " Libromancer " archetypes to the OCG. 
 Breakdown 
 5 Cards per Pack, 15 Packs per Box 
 Includes 57 cards:
 5  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 6  Super Rares 
 12  Rares 
 29  Commons 
 The Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rares .
