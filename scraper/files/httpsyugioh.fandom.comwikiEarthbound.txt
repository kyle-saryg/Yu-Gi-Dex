 " Earthbound " ( 地 縛   Jibaku ), is an  archetype  of  DARK   monsters  used by the  Dark Signers  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  and  Sergey Volkov  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . The archetype consists of two sub-archetypes,  " Earthbound Immortal " and " Earthbound Servant ".
 
 Design 
 The monsters are based on either the  Nazca Lines  (in the case of the " Earthbound Immortals "), or simply other figures using the same design. Each monster has a black body with lines through their bodies of a predetermined color.
