 The Different Dimension Warrior - ESPer Robin  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! D Team ZEXAL . He is the protagonist of a TV Show named after his character.

 Biography 
 One day,  Astral  was watching Robin's TV Show, amazed at it, when  Yuma  received a  Duel  challenge in the  D-Network . When Yuma and  his team  arrived, they found Robin waiting for them, surprising Yuma. Robin then said he would show them the power of his  D.D.   Deck . Astral said that a Different Dimension Deck sounds quite interesting, acceptig his challenge. Yuma and Robin then Duel.
 After a tough battle, Yuma defeats Robin, with Astral asking him for an autograph. Robin and Yuma then praise each other, with Robin suggesting Yuma and his team to enter the  Team Duel Tournament , saying that a lot of top Duelists would be there. [1] 
 
 Deck 
 Robin plays a  Sparrow Family  Deck , focused on quickly gathering all of its members in the  field . He can then fuse them into " D.D. Jet Iron " or use cards like " Galaxy Queen's Light " to perform high- Rank   Xyz Summons  with them.
