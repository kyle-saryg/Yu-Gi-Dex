 Luna , known as  Ruka  ( 龍可 ,  Ruka ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Luna , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
