 Leo Akaba  is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga . He is the father of  Reiji Akaba .
 
 Personality 
 Unlike his anime counterpart, Leo appears to be a caring and a loving father to his son. He also likes to playfully tease him for fun.
 
 Biography 
 Leo was mentioned by  Ren  during his  Duel  against  Yugo . When Yugo was surprised about Ren knowing him, the latter explained that he knew lots of things, such as about how  Yuya Sakaki 's father,  Yusho Sakaki , and  Reiji Akaba 's father, Leo, brought about "World Illusion", further shocking Yugo.
