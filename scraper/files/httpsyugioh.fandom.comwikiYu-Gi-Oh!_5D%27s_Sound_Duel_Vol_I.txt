 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Sound Duel I  is the original anime soundtrack to " Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's ".
 It features music by Yutaka Minobe and Wall5 Project and TV-sized versions of opening theme,  Kizuna  and ending theme  START .
 The soundtrack had minor duplicates (Tracks 9, 21, 7 and 26). Marvelous Entertainment will replace the duplicate tracks.
