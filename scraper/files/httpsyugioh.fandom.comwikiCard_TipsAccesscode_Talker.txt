 This card is best  Link Summoned  using a  Link 3  monster and a  Link 1  monster with different Attributes - it will have 5300 ATK and be able to destroy two cards without being negated.
 If the Link 3 monster was itself summoned with  Link Monsters  of different Attributes as material, this makes for up to five unanswerable pops a turn.
