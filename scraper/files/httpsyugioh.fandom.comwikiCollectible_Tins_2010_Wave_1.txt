 The Collectible Tins 2010 come in two waves: This first two tins feature “Majestic Red Dragon” and “Black-winged Dragon”, known from the popular Absolute Powerforce and The Shining Darkness  booster series. The Majestic Red Dragon Tin burns like a small star, with a bright golden paint job to show off Jack’s latest and most dangerous Synchro Monster, and the Black-Winged Dragon Tin beckons you like the night sky, with a deep black paint job to show off Crow’s glowing Synchro Monster, Black-Winged Dragon. 
 —  Konami 
 
 The Dragon Knight Draco-Equiste 2010 Collectible Tin is one of five new Collectible Tins for the 2010 holiday season – and exclusively available only in select retail stores. Each Tin comes with 5 Booster Packs and 5 different Variant Cards! The Dragon Knight Draco-Equiste Tin comes adorned with proud royal blue heraldry, befitting a chivalrous Dragon Knight like Draco-Equiste, with purple highlights to show off this Fusion Monster. This Tin is a great way to store – with pride and honor – your Decks, your favorite cards, or anything else you want to keep safe! 
 —  Konami
