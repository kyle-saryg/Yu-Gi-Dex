 Blazing Wind 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Blazing Wind  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from May 14, 2014. Each pack costs   25.
 This collection includes Mecha Phantom Beast, Flamvell, Brotherhood of the Fire Fist, Mist Valley, Elemental HERO, Six Samurai, Skull, Dark World Monsters and Spells, as well as Fusion Monsters.
