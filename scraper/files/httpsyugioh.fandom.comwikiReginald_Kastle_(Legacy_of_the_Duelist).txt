 Reginald Kastle , known as  Ryoga Kamishiro ( 神 代   凌 牙 ,  Kamishiro Ryōga ) in the Japanese version, and nicknamed "Shark", is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Reginald Kastle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
