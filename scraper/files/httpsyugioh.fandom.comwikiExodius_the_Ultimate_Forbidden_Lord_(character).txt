 Exodius the Ultimate Forbidden Lord , known as  Ultimate Sealed God Exodios  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Exodius the Ultimate Forbidden Lord ".
 He appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , as an unlockable character. The player may unlock him by winning a Duel using the effect of the card " Exodius the Ultimate Forbidden Lord ".
 
 Deck 
 Exodius the Ultimate Forbidden Lord uses a Deck titled, "Forbidden Grave" and "Dear Exodius".
