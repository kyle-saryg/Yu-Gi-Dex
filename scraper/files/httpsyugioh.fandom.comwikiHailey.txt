 Mobile appearances 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 
 
 
 Hailey  is a  Standard Duelist  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . She appears in Duel World (DM) and Duel World (DSOD). Additionally, she cannot be unlocked, only appearing as an opponent. She can also be dueled in  Duel Island: -Gladiator- .

 (Voice lines found in game files but not currently not in game yet) 
 
 Whenever Hailey Summons " Black Luster Soldier - Envoy of the Beginning ", she announces "I Special Summon Black Luster Soldier - Envoy of the Beginning!"

 When Hailey activates the effect of that monster, she announces "Black Luster Soldier - Envoy of the Beginning's effect activates! It banishes monsters on the field!" 
 Whenever Hailey Summons " Cyber-Tech Alligator ", she announces "I summon Cyber-Tech Alligator!"
 Most of the time when declaring an attack with that monster, she announces "Cyber-Tech Alligator, take a bite out of 'em!"

 Most of the time when declaring an attack with that monster, she announces "I attack with D.D. Warrior Lady!"

 Whenever Hailey Summons " Light and Darkness Dragon ", she announces "I summon Light and Darkness Dragon!"
 Most of the time when declaring an attack with that monster, she announces "Light and Darkness Dragon, attack!"

 When Hailey activates the effect of " Tin Goldfish ", she announces "Tin Goldfish's effect activates! I Special Summon a Level 4 monster from my hand!"

 Whenever Hailey activates " Pot of Avarice " she announces "From my hand, I activate Pot of Avarice! I return 5 monsters from the Graveyard to my deck, and then I draw 2 cards!"

 Whenever Hailey activates " Stop Defense " she announces "From my hand, I activate a Spell! Stop Defense! I change one monster from defense position to attack position!"
