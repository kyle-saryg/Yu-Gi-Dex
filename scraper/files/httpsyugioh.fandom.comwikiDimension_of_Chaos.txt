 Dimension of Chaos  (ディメンション・オブ・カオス  Dimenshon obu Kaosu ) is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixth set in the  OCG ' s  9th series , following  Clash of Rebellions , and followed by  Breakers of Shadow .

 Features 
 Introduces cards used by  Yuya Sakaki ,  Gong Strong ,  Sora Perse ,  Declan Akaba ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Dennis McField ,  Trout ,  Crow Hogan , and  Jack Atlas 
 Includes members/support for the " Blackwing ", " Chaos ", " D/D ", " Deskbot ", " Frightfur ", " Gaia The Fierce Knight ", " Igknight ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Performage ", " Performapal ", " Raidraptor ", " Rank-Up-Magic ", " Superheavy Samurai " and " Toon " archetypes.
 The  OCG  version contains members/support for the " Artifact ", " Bujin ", " Sylvan ", " Symphonic Warrior " and " Traptrix " archetypes. 
 The  TCG  version contains members/support for the " Kaiju " and " Kozmo " archetypes. 
 The Korean  OCG  version contains members/support for the " Paleozoic " archetype. 
 Introduces the " Graydle " and " Majespecter " archetypes to the  OCG / TCG  as well as the first support for the " Black Luster Soldier " archetype. 
 Breakdown 
 In the Japanese  OCG , there are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per box. The set contains 85 cards, or 108 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 1  Holographic Rare  (also available as Ultra Rare, Secret Rare, and Ultimate Rare) 
 6  Ultimate Rares 
 16  Secret Rares 
 6  Ultra Rares  (all available as Secret Rares and Ultimate Rares) 
 10  Super Rares  (all available as Secret Rares) 
 20  Rares 
 49  Commons 
 In the  TCG , there are 9 cards per pack, 24 booster packs per box, and 12 display boxes per case. The set contains 100 cards, or 106 in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 1  Ghost Rare 
 5  Ultimate Rares 
 8  Secret Rares 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 14  Super Rares 
 20  Rares 
 48  Commons
