 Names 
 
 This card can be searched by " Terraforming ", " Madolche Butlerusk ", " Favorite Hero ", " Planet Pathfinder ", " Metaverse ", " Set Rotation ", " Demise of the Land ", " Pop-Up ", " Ghostrick Renovation " and " An Owl of Luck ". 
 This card allows your Set " Abyss Script "  Spell Cards , " Silver Sentinel " and " Toy Magician " to be destroyed as if they were destroyed by your opponent. This is also a  Continuous-like Effect , therefore, if you control a  Pendulum Summoned  " Abyss Actor " monster, you can chain "Metaverse" or "Pop-Up" to your opponent's monster effect in order to activate this card, overwrite said effect and destroy one of those aforementioned Set cards you control. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Ancient Fairy Dragon ".
