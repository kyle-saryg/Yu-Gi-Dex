 Team Air Force  is a  Dueling Team  in Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . As its name hints, it is themed after military aircrafts.

 Biography 
 Team Duel Tournament 
 Team A Force enters the  Team Duel Tournament  and is able to earn a total of 7  Digital Stones . With only 1 Digital Stone remaining so that they could advance in the tournament, they challenge  Team ZEXAL , who is the only team besides them that also has only 1 Digital Stone remaining. They being the match with  Alpha  vs.  Yuma .
