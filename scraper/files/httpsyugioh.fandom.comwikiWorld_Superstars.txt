 World Superstars 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 World Superstars  is a Booster Pack in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Battlin' Boxer ", " Chronomaly ", " Dododo ", " Gagaga ", " Galaxy ", " Ghostrick ", " Gogogo ", " HERO ", " Heraldic ", " Heroic Challenger ", " Mecha Phantom Beast ", " Number " and " Star Seraph "  archetypes . 
 Features previously  OCG -exclusive  cards from the Booster Packs  Legacy of the Valiant ,  Primal Origin ,  Duelist Alliance ,  The New Challengers ; as well as reprints of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2012 prize cards . 
 Features the  Prismatic Secret Rare  rarity for the first time in years. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 24 packs per box and 5 cards per pack (4 Super Rares and 1 Prismatic Secret Rare). The set contains 52 cards, including:
 
 18  Prismatic Secret Rares 
 34  Super Rares
