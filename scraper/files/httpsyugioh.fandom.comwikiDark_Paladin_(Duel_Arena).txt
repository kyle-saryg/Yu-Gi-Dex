 Dark Paladin  is a character version of the card " Dark Paladin ".
 He appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P033. He also appears as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 7 under Quest mode.
 Defeating him as an opponent for the first time earns the player 7000 DP.

 Description 
 A super-magical swordsman fused from " Dark Magician " and " Buster Blader ".
 
 Opponent Quote 
 I have fused the powers of  dark magic  and  dragon destroyer . Do you think you stand a chance?
 
 Upon defeat:
 
 Impressive. You could become a Duelist equal to  my own master . I look forward to seeing you progress.
 
 Decks 
 Using cards like " Summoner's Art " and " Emblem of Dragon Destroyer " makes it easy to bring  Fusion Materials  into your  hand . Stack up  Dragon - Type   monsters  in the  Graveyard  until you are ready for the  Fusion !
