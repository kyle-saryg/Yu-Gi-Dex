 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
During the  Main Phase  ( Quick Effect ): You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand , and if you do,  banish  1 " Kashtira " or " Tearlaments " card from your hand or  GY . If this card is  Normal  or Special Summoned: You can  send  the top 3 cards of either  Deck  to the GY. If this card is sent to the GY by  card effect : You can send the top 2 cards of your Deck to the GY. You can only use each effect of "Tearlaments Kashtira"  once per turn .
