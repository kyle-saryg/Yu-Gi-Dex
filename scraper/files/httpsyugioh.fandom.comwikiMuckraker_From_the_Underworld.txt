 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Effect Monsters Cannot be used as  Link Material  the turn it was  Link Summoned . If a  monster(s)  you  control  would be  destroyed by battle  or  card effect , you can  Tribute  1  Fiend  monster instead. You can  target  1 Fiend monster in your  GY , except "News Reporter from the Underworld"; you cannot  Special Summon  monsters for the rest of this  turn , except Fiend monsters, also  discard  1 card, and if you do, Special Summon that monster. You can only use this effect of "News Reporter from the Underworld"  once per turn .
