 Rarity Collection Premium Gold Edition  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Breakdown 
 There are 15 packs per box and 4 cards per pack. 
 Each Japanese pack contains 2  Super Rares , 1  Ultra Rare , and 1  Secret Rare ,  Collector's Rare , or  Premium Gold Rare . 
 Each Korean pack contains 2  Super Rares , 1  Ultra Rare , and 1  Secret Rare ,  Extra Secret Rare , or  Prismatic Secret Rare . 
 The set contains 50 cards:
 15 Ultra Rares 
 35 Super Rares 
 In the Japanese set, the Ultra Rare cards are also available as Premium Gold Rare, and all cards are also available as Secret Rare and Collector's Rare. 
 In the Korean set, the Ultra Rare cards are also available as Prismatic Secret Rare, and all cards are also available as Secret Rare and Extra Secret Rare.
