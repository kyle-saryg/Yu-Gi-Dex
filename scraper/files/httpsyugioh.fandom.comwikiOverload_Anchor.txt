 Effect type

 Quick-like 
 When an opponent's  monster  declares an  attack  while you control 1  Link Monster  (and no other monsters): You can make that monster you control unable to be  destroyed  by that  battle , and if you do, halve any battle damage you take from that battle, then end the  Battle Phase . If this effect was applied this  turn : You can  banish  this card you  control ; the ATK of all Link Monsters your opponent controls become half their current  ATK  until your opponent's next  Standby Phase .
