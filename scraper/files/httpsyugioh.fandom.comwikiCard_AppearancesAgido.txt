 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 In  episode 94 ,  Ishizu Ishtar  uses this card during her Duel against  Seto Kaiba . After Ishizu Normal Summons this card, Kaiba activates " Soul Exchange " to let him Ishizu's monsters as if he were their controller. Kaiba then Tributes Ishizu's three monsters and  Tribute Summons  " Obelisk the Tormentor ".
 In this Duel, this card was a  Normal Monster  with diferent artwork. 
 This card also appears in a flashback Ishizu has when she notes to herself what happened when Kaiba used "Soul Exchange" to tribute her monsters and summon "Obelisk the Tormentor". 
 Video games 
 Ishizu Ishtar  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour   and  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V Tag Force Special .
