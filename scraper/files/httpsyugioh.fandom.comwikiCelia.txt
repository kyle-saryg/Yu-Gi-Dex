 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In Tag Force 1-3, she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 In  Tag Force , she is usually seen with  Seydina ,  Dante ,  Rock ,  Brown , and  Truly , as each of them have a Deck focusing on one of the six  Attributes  - in her case,  WIND .
 
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 Celia can be found in Satellite.
 
 Tag Force 4 
 In  Crow Hogan 's third story event, the player and Crow Tag Duel against Celia and  Carly Carmine .
 
 Tag Force 5 
 Celia mentions her school days with her friends as part of the  Six Attribute Deck Masters  to the player character in passing during one of their possible conversations.

 Decks 
 Celia uses  WIND  Decks  during the first three  Tag Force  games, emphasizing on backrow removal.
 From  Tag Force 4  onwards, Celia's Decks shift their focus towards the summon of " Dark Simorgh ".
