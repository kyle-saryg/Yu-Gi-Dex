 Number 39: Utopia  is a character version of the card " Number 39: Utopia ".
 He appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. His partner ID is P051. He also appears as an opponent, as the boss of Stage 8 under Quest mode.
 In the beta, he had a default of 5 Rental Points, and his partner number was P023.
