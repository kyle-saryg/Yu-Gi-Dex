 Red  (レッド  Reddo ) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is the series incarnation of  Charlie Gale  from the  manga . Red, along with  Black  and  Blue  were hired by  Seto Kaiba  to face  Yugi Mutou  in a shootout game.
