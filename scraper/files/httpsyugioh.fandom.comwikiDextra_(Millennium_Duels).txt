 Dextra , known as  Droite  (ドロワ,  Dorowa ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Dextra , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
