 Features 
 Contains Link Monsters for 20 different Themes. And, each Theme Deck will have vital cards needed to construct those Decks reprinted. 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Bujin ", " Mayakashi ", " Simorgh ", " Artifact ", " Tenyi ", " Abyss Actor ", " Sky Striker Ace ", " Mecha Phantom Beast ", " Ancient Gear ", " HERO ", " Reptilianne ", " Melodious ", " Endymion ", " Fusion ", " Polymerization ", " Predaplant ", " Destruction Sword ", " Buster Blader ", " Rose Dragon ", and " World Legacy " archetypes, as well as the " Herald ", " Link " and " Xtra HERO " series.
 Introduces support for the " Rose " archetype in OCG/TCG. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 4 cards per pack and 15 packs per box.
