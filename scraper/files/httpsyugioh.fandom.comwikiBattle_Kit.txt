 A Battle Kit is a special pack of trading cards that includes 1  playmat , and comes with 10  Booster Packs . So far, there are 3 Battle Kits:
 1.  Sealed Play Battle Kit : This set includes a playmat featuring (going left to right)  Dark Magician of Chaos ,  Gem-Knight Pearl ,  Gagaga Magician ,  Skull Archfiend of Lightning , and  Ancient Gear Knight . This set also includes 10  Battle Pack: Epic Dawn  booster packs that also feature the monsters on the playmat.
 2.  Sealed Play Battle Kit 2 : This set includes a playmat featuring 1 of the 3  Egyptian God Cards . This set also includes 10  Battle Pack 2: War of the Giants  booster packs that also feature one of the God Cards.
 3.  Sealed Play Battle Kit 3 : This set includes a playmat from 5 playmats. This set also includes 10  Battle Pack 3: Monster League  booster packs that also feature the monsters on the playmat.
