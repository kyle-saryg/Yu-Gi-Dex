 Design 
       Misaki in her android form.     
 She is similar to  Bruno , in that they both change into an android. While in this form, she rides the  Omega Hawk   Duel Runner , which is similar to  Delta Eagle . They are also similar in appearance; While in human form, they have long hair that slicks down, whilst in their alter egos, their hair spikes up wildly. In android form, they both wear a mysterious opaque visor and similar outfits. Also like Bruno, Misaki is a skilled mechanic. 
 While in human form, Misaki does not like talking much. Most of her dialogues begin or end with an ellipsis, and she does not smile often. When she talks, she is sarcastic and intimidating. 
 
 Biography 
       Misaki at the end of the Final Chapter.     
 The  player  meets Misaki after they move from  Satisfaction Town  to  Daimon Area . Misaki becomes part of the duel team the player makes upon entering the WRGP. Misaki is also available as CPU  Tag-Team Duel  and CPU Turbo Tag-Team Duel partner.
