 Trigger 
 
 
 If you have 2 or more  monsters  in your  Graveyard , you can  Normal Summon  this card without  Tributing . When this card is Normal Summoned: You can  Special Summon  as many " Gagaga " monsters as possible from your Graveyard. During your  Main Phase : You can  activate  this effect;  Xyz Summon  1 " Number "  Xyz Monster  from your Graveyard using all "Gagaga" monsters you  control . If this card is used as an  Xyz Material  for an Xyz Summon:  Draw  cards equal to the number of "Gagaga" monsters used as Xyz Materials for that Xyz Summon.
