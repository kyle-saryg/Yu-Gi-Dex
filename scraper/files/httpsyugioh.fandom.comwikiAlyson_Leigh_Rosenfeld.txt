 Alyson Leigh Rosenfeld  is a voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  franchise.
 
 Roles 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , Rosenfeld voices  Rio Kastle/Marin [2]  and  Iris . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , Rosenfeld voices  Allie [2] ,  Riley Akaba [2] ,  Grace Tyler  &  Gloria Tyler . [3] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS , Rosenfeld voices  Queen .
