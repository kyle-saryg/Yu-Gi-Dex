 Effect 
 
 
 When a  monster  you  control   attacks , if the  ATK  of the  attack target  is greater than or equal to the ATK of the attacking monster's: Double the ATK of your attacking monster. Then, if the attack target is  destroyed by that battle :  Destroy  1  Spell Card  your opponent controls.
