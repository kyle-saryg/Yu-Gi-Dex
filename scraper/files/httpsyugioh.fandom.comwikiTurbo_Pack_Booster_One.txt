 Turbo Pack  is a booster pack that was released to TCG tournament locations, featuring powerful and expensive cards, including " Judgment Dragon " and " Doomcaliber Knight ". It is followed by  Turbo Pack: Booster Two .
 Even though this Pack apparently was not released in Spanish, it is listed on the Spanish version of the official card database. [1]
