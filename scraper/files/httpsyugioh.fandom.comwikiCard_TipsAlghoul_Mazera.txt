 This card can be searched by " Transmodify ", " Chaos Zone ", " Magical Contract Door ", " Enma's Judgment " and " Glow-Up Bloom ". 
 Use " Elemental Absorber ", " Fusion Gate " or " Unicorn Beacon " to banish this card from your hand. 
 Discard this card to Special Summon " Jack-o-Bolan "; if your opponent activates an effect that would destroy it, banish this card in response to prevent its destruction. This card's other effect then activates to Special Summon itself as a Level 7 monster, setting up a  Rank 7   Xyz Monster  with just two cards in hand, although relying on your opponent to play a disruption. 
 You can send this card from the Deck to the Graveyard first via " Samurai Skull ", " Gozuki " or " Uni-Zombie ", then banish this card to summon " Gigantes " or " The Rock Spirit ", thus easily setting up a  Link 3  monster with two cards.
 You can tune "Uni-Zombie" with "Gigantes" or "The Rock Spirit" to  Synchro Summon  a Level 8  Synchro Monster  first, then Xyz Summon a  Rank 8   Xyz Monster .
