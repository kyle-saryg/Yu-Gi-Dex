 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival 
 
 
 
 Zexal , known as  Zeal  (ゼアル  Zearu ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival . This is  Yuma Tsukumo , combined with  Astral , through the power of  ZEXAL .
