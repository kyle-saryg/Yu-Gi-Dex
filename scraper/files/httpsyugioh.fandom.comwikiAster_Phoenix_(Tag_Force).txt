 Aster Phoenix , known as  Edo Phoenix  (エド・フェニックス,  Edo Fenikkusu ) in the Japanese version, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Aster Phoenix , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2 
 Aster is one of the Tier 1 characters you can partner with right from the start of the game.
 During the story for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters, Aster is dueled twice.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Dark  Zane Truesdale  and Aster duel against the player and Obelisk Blue  Chazz Princeton  in his last story event.
 In Child  Blair Flannigan 's last story event and in Normal Chazz's third, the player and their partner duel against Aster and  Jesse Anderson .
 During Dark Zane's last story event and in Normal  Alexis Rhodes ' third, the player and their partner Tag Duel against Aster and Chazz.
