 The  Friendship Cup Finals  is the fourth arc of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V . It covers the placing rounds of the  Friendship Cup  as  Jean-Michel Roget  initiates his "King's Gambit", with both the  Lancers  and  Sergey Volkov  (following Roget's orders) attempting to defeat  Jack Atlas  in order to establish their strength and reputation to the general populace of   New Domino City . New Domino must also prepare for the invasion by  Duel Academy  when  Leo Akaba  sends  Barrett ,  Yuri  and the  Obelisk Force  to capture  Zuzu  and  Celina .
