 Bronk Stone , known as  Tetsuo Takeda  (武田鉄男,  Takeda Tetsuo ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Bronk Stone , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
