 Dimension World  is the third arc of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime, encompassing the series' third season. [1]  The dubbed version splits into three arcs, with  Survival Duels  encompassing the first third,  Quest for the Rainbow Dragon  the second third and  Into the Shadows  the final third.
 This is the "final" arc of the dubbed version because the dubbed series wasn't finished.

 Summary 
 Into the Shadows 
 Jaden Yuki ,  Syrus Truesdale  and the others get pulled into an alternate dimension to find  Jesse Anderson . While in there, Jaden sends his friends to the stars and allows an extreme evil to take over his mind, thus he becomes  the Supreme King . The Supreme King's deck uses " Elemental HEROs " but uses " Dark Fusion " to bring out a new breed of heroes, the " Evil HEROs ". Using this new power, he sends Jim to the stars. The Supreme King is then challenged to a duel by Axel. The Duel ends in a DRAW but the Supreme King's spirit comes out of Jaden and both Axel and the spirit are sent to the stars. Now Jaden becomes afraid of using one of his key cards, " Polymerization ". Later, in the duel against the Yubel-possessed Jesse, he overcomes his fear and uses " Super Polymerization " to save Jesse.
