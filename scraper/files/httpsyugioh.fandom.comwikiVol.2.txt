 Vol.2  or  Volume 2  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .
 Like other cards printed early on in the game, the cards have no printed set numbers, and lack the distinctive  Eye of Anubis Hologram  in the lower right-hand corner of each card.
 Of the 40 cards in  Vol.2 , all but 6— " Dorover ", " Holograh ", " Solitude ", " Supporter in the Shadows ", " The Bewitching Phantom Thief ", and " Wood Remains "—have since been printed in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game .
