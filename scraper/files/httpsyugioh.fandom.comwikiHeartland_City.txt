        Heartland     
 For the location in the  Xyz Dimension , see  Heartland City (ARC-V) .      
 Heartland City  is a city in a near future, where the main events of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  take place. The mayor of Heartland was  Mr. Heartland , while its founder is  Dr. Faker . [1]  It has the defining characteristic of almost every building being brightly colored.
 In several parts of it, it resembles  New Domino City , especially the pier as several highways tower over it. By the river, there is a place that looks like the same site where  the Duel  between  Yami Yugi  and  Strings  took place. 
 Heartland City was the setting for the  World Duel Carnival , which set up to find all the " Number " cards and their  holders . [2] 
 In the center of Heartland City is  Heartland , which is surrounded by a wall and contains  Heartland Tower . [3]
