Jinzo Art , or  Jinzo Legacy , known as  Psycho Art  (サイコ流  Saiko Ryū ) in the Japanese version, is a term used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime. It denotes the  "Jinzo" archetype  of cards. It was wiped out as a  Dueling  strategy after a coming into conflict with the  Cyber Art  and as a result, the only known practitioner was  Makoto Inotsume . [1]
