 Advanced Tournament Pack 2014 Vol.2  is the twenty-ninth installment of the Japanese  Tournament Pack  series. The advanced packs come in two styles  Gold  and  Silver . The  Gold  pack is awarded to the tournament winners and is guaranteed to have a Parallel Rare inside. The  Silver  packs are awarded as participation packs and have a chance of having a Parallel Rare card.
