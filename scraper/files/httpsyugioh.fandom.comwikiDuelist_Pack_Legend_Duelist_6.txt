 Duelist Pack: Legend Duelist 6  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by the first five protagonists over the course of the Yu-Gi-Oh! series, including  Yami Yugi ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Yuma Tsukumo , and Yuya Sakaki ( anime  and  spin-off manga ). 
 Includes members and/or support for the " HERO ", " Elemental HERO ", " Kuriboh ", " Neo-Spacian ", " Warrior ", " Stardust ", " Junk ", " Synchron ", " Gagaga ", " Zubaba ", " Dododo ", " Gogogo ", " Performapal ", " Magician ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Smile ", " Pendulum ", " Dark Magician " and " Dark Magician Girl " archetypes, as well as  " Synchro " and " Scrap-Iron " series.
 Introduces first support for " Palladium ", " Utopic Future " and " Onomat " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 55+1 cards.
