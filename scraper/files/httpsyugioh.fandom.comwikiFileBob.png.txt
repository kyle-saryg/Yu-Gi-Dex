 Biography 
 He appears in the second season as one of the many students of  Domino City 's Duel Academy. He hangs around with the twins, Leo and  Luna , as well as with  Patty , and Dexter, who also are in his class.   
 Bob and the rest of his class were going to be expelled from the Duel Academy by  Rudolph Heitmann  until  Yusei Fudo  challenged Heitmann to a Duel to prevent this. [1] 
 When  Lester  enrolled at Duel Academy as a normal student, Bob was impressed with Lester's skills. Later on, after Lester defeats Leo and Luna, it is revealed Bob no longer knows who Lester is. [2] 
 He is often seen in the stands with his friends, watching  Team 5D's ' Duels in the  WRGP .   
 During Yusei's Duel with Z-one, he is seen cheering Yusei on. [3] 
 
 Deck
