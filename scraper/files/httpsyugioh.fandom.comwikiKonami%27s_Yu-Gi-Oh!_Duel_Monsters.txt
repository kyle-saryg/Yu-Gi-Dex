Konami's  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  is a collection of 21  Holofoil Rare  promotional cards released by  Konami  in two sets of 10 cards and one set of one card: the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule: Breed and Battle  promotional cards , the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  promotional cards , and the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  meeting experience card . As these cards all predate the introduction of the  Official Card Game  and use a different design, they are  illegal  for tournament play, even though they were printed by Konami.
 The collection consists of 18  Monster Cards  and three  Spell Cards . The monsters only have a  Level ,  ATK , and  DEF , and the Spells feature only an  effect .
