 Spellcaster's Command Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fourteenth Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Zombie World Structure Deck . This Deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Lord of the Magician  in the  OCG .
 Spellcaster's Command Structure Deck  was reprinted as  Unlimited Edition  in North America on August 1, 2014. [1]

 Features 
 This deck features  Spellcasters  and the use of  Spell Counters ; it also contains 6 new cards and reprints of previously hard-to-find rares such as " Dark Red Enchanter " and " Magical Exemplar ".
