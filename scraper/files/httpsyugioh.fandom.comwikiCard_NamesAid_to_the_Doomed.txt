 Names 
 
 The official  Greek  name given (" Βοήθεια στους Χαμένους ") is an exact translation of the English name of this card. A possible translation of the Japanese name (" Divine Protection of the Deceased ") could be " Θεϊκή Προστασία των Απωβιώντων ".
