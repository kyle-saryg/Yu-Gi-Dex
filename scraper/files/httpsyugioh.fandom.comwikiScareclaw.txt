 " Scareclaw " (スケアクロー  Sukeakurō ) is an  archetype  introduced in  Dimension Force . They are composed predominantly of  Level  3  EARTH   Beast - Type   monsters , with the exceptions of " Scareclaw Reichheart " and " Scareclaw Tri-Heart ", and have  effects  that mostly support  Defense Position  monsters.
