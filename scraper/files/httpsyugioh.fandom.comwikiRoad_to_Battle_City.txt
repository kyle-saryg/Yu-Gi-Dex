 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Battle City Edition: Special Edition Road to Battle City 
 
 
 Air date ( ja ) 
 April 7, 2015 
 
 
 
 " Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters Battle City Edition: Special Edition Road to Battle City " is a half-hour, special episode of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters . It first aired in Japan on April 7, 2015, at 2:05 AM.
 It is a recap of  Duelist Kingdom  and the events leading up to  Battle City , narrated by  Seto Kaiba .
