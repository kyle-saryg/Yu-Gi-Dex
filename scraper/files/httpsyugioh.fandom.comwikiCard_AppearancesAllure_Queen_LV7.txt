 Anime 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 104 ,  Queen  uses this card during her  Master Duel  against  Ai . She controls two copies of this card face-up when the duel is first shown. Queen links one copy with " Allure Queen LV5 " and " Allure Queen LV3 " to  Link Summon  " Golden Allure Queen ", which  points to  the remaining copy of this card and increases its ATK by 500. The remaining copy is destroyed by the effect of Ai's " Light Dragon @Ignister ". 
 Video games 
 Allure Queen LV5  and  Allure Queen LV7  use this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 . 
 Giry  uses this card in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 .
