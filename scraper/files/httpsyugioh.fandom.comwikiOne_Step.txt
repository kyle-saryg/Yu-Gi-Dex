 " One Step " ( Ｏｎｅ   Ｓｔｅｐ ,  Wan Suteppu ) is the first Japanese ending theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime, performed by  Pendulum☆Cute . It debuted on April 6, 2014, and aired during episodes  1  to  30 . It was replaced in  episode 31  by " Future fighter! "
