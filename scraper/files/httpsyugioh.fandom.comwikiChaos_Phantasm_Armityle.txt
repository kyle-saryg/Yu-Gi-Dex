 This card cannot be  Special Summoned , except with " Dimension Fusion Destruction ". This card cannot be  destroyed by battle . This card can inflict 10,000  Battle Damage  to a  monster  your opponent  controls . During your  Main Phase , you can switch  control  of this card to your opponent until the end of this  turn . If you do,  remove from play  all other monsters your opponent controls during the  End Phase  of this turn.
