 Structure Deck: Dragunity Drive  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the nineteenth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-seventh including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Machiners Command . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Dragunity Legion Structure Deck .
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Structure Deck: Dragunity Drive  was released on September 7, 2010 as  Structure Deck: Dragunity Drive Special Edition .

 Features 
 The deck features the popular " Dragunity " theme from the  Duel Terminal  series. It includes many reprints of older cards and 5 brand new cards.
