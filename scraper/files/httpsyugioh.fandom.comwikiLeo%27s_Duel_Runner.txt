       Leo's Duel Runner.     
 As a young adult,  Leo  used a  Duel Runner .
 Leo used the  Duel Runner  as a professional  Turbo Duelist . He also gave  Luna  a ride to  Somerset House  on it, during which she screamed in terror and afterwards told him off for driving too fast.
