 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 This  card cannot be attacked  if there is a Plant-Type monster on your side of the field. Offer  1 Plant-Type monster on your side of the field  as a Tribute to  destroy 1 Spell or Trap Card on the field. 
 While there is a Plant-Type monster on your side of the field, this  card cannot be attacked.  By Tributing  1 Plant-Type monster on your side of the field ,  destroy 1 Spell or Trap Card on the field.
