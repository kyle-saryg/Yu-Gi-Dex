 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Once per turn , during your  Main Phase : You can  send  1 card from your  hand  to the  GY ; add 1 " Mirror Force " from your  Deck  or  GY  to your  hand . If this  Set  card is  destroyed  by an opponent's  card effect  and  sent  to the GY: You can take this card from the GY and 1  "Mirror Force" from your hand, Deck, or GY, and Set them to your  field . The cards Set by this effect can be activated during the turn they are Set.
