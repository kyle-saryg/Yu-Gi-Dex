 Jakob uses a  " Granel " Deck  focused around the latter form of " Meklord Emperor ". His strategy revolves around using " Infinite Aura " along with " Speed Spell - Takeover " to increase his  Life Points , which in turn increases his Granel's  ATK . After fusing into Aporia, his Deck included   " Meklord Astro Mekanikle ", in addition to Lester's " Skiel " and Primo's " Wisel " cards in support of it.
