 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 
 
 
 Yuzu Hiiragi  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Zuzu Boyle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Yuzu is a childhood friend of  Yuya 's.
