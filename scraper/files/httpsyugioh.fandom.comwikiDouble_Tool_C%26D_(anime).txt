 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 Equip  only to " Power Tool Dragon " or a  Level  4 or higher  Machine - Type  " Morphtronic "  monster . While equipped: ● During your  turn : It gains 1000  ATK . If it  attacks , the  effect (s) of the  attack target  is  negated  during the  Battle Phase . ● During your opponent's turn: Your opponent cannot  select  a monster other than the equipped monster as an attack target. An opponent's monster that  battles  the equipped monster is  destroyed  at the end of the  Damage Step .
