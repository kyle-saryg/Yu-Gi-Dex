 Marc Thompson  is an American voice actor for the English dubs of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Thompson's first major role in  Yu-Gi-Oh!  history was portraying  Duke Devlin  in the second series anime. He also did the voices of  Valon ,  Rafael ,  Gansley ,  Zigfried von Schroeder 's father and  Aknamkanon  in the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Thompson took over the role of  Chazz Princeton  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  starting with episode 90, replacing  Anthony Salerno . He also did the voices of  Sartyr ,  Burgundy ,  Dimitri ,  Prince Ojin ,  Franz ,  Orlando ,  Frost ,  Kagemaru 's younger self,  Mr. Huffington ,  Skilled White Magician ,  Mr. Stein ,  Yubel 's human self, and  The D  within the same series.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Thompson did the voices of  Z-one ,  Hunter Pace ,  Tenzen Yanagi ,  Sayer ,  Hideo  (Akiza's father),  Roman Goodwin ,  Dr. Schmidt ,  Officer Kaz ,  Nicolas ,  Hanson  and  Broder  within  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Thompson's most notable role in  Yu-Gi-Oh!  history is portraying  Astral  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . He also does the voices of  Number 96 ,  Mr. Heartland ,  Girag , and  Alito .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Thompson voices  Frederick ,  Barrett  and  Sanders . He also reprises his role as  Hunter Pace .
 
 Trivia 
 Marc Thompson was also the voice actor for several characters in the 2003 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles television series.
