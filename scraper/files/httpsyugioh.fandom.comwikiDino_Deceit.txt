 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Activate  only if your opponent  controls  a  Link Monster  with more  Link Markers  than the amount of Link Markers of a " Dinowrestler " Link Monster you control.  Negate  the  effects  of all  monsters  your opponent controls. After this effect  resolves , you can Set a  Spell / Trap Card  from your  hand , also, your opponent must conduct their  Battle Phase  the  turn  you activate this effect.
