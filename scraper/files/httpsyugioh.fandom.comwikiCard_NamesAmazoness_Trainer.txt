 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (المدرّبة الأمازونيّة) literally  Amazonian Trainer  or  Trainer of the Amazon . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (الأمازونيّة المدرّبة) translates to  Trainer Amazon . 
 The  Greek  name given translates to " Amazon Trainer ".
