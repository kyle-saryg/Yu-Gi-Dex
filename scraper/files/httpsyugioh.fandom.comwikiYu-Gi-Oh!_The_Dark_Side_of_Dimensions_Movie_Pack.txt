 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Movie Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions Movie Pack  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ) and the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ).

 Features 
 Introduces cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba  and  Aigami . 
 Includes members/support for the " Blue-Eyes " and " Gadget " archetypes.
 The  TCG  version also includes members/support for the " Celtic Guard ", " Dark Magician ", " Dark Magician Girl ", " Gaia The Fierce Knight ", " Gandora ", " Kuriboh ", and " Noble Arms " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Cubic " archetype to the  OCG / TCG  as well as the first support for the " Chaos " and " Magician Girl " archetypes.
