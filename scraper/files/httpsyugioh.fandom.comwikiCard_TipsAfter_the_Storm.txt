 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Magical Burst ", and " Left Arm Offering ". 
 Use this card on the first turn to retrieve a  WATER  or  WIND  monster whose effect searches another card by discarding itself, such as " Harpie Queen ",  " Warrior of Atlantis ", " Gishki Shadow ", " Gishki Vision ", " Glife the Phantom Bird ", " King of the Swamp ", " Nekroz of Brionac ", " Nekroz of Clausolas " or " Nekroz of Unicore ".
 " Wynn the Wind Channeler ", " Deep Sea Minstrel " and " Tempest, Dragon Ruler of Storms " discard themselves and another monster of the same Attribute for their effects; you can use this card to retrieve either them or the other monster discarded with them. 
 This card can be used after using the effect of board-nuke cards like " Black Rose Dragon ", " Earthbound Immortal Ccarayhua ", " El Dorado Adelantado ", " Jurrac Impact ", " Rainbow Dragon ", " Rainbow Overdragon ", " The Wicked Eraser ", " Topologic Zeroboros " or " Yang Zing Prana ", as long as a WATER or WIND monster is in your Graveyard.
