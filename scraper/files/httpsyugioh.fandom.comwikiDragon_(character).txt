 Dragon  is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is the series incarnation of  Street Fighter  from the  manga .
 
 Biography 
 Dragon and  Katsuya Jonouchi  fought over a pipe. Although Dragon was armed with a nunchaku, Jonouchi managed to defeat him. [1]
