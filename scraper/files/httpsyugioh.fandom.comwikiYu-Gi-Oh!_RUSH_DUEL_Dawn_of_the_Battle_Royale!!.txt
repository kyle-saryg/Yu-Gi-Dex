 Yu-Gi-Oh! RUSH DUEL: Dawn of the Battle Royale!!  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh!  video game for the Nintendo Switch. It was released in Japan on August 12, 2021, and in both North America and Europe on December 7, 2021. The game was first announced at Jump Festa 2021, and a playable demo was released on July 12, 2021. The game was first revealed in the west in the first Yu-Gi-Oh! Digital neXt presentation. It features characters and locations from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime.
 
 Gameplay 
 The game is played using the  Rush Duel  format. 
 
 Cards 
 Includes 359 cards (counting alt. arts). Goes up to  Destined Power Destruction ,  Character Pack , and  Maximum Ultra Enhancement Pack .
