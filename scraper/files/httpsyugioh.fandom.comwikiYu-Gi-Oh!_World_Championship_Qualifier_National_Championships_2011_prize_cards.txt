 Yu-Gi-Oh! WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP 2011 Qualifier 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship Qualifier National Championships 2011 prize cards  are cards which were given to the winners of the  National Championships and Continental Championships  in 2011. They were not available at the North American World Championship Qualifiers. [1]  The cards became tournament legal on July 5, 2011. [2]
