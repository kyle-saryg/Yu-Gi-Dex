 Aqua Spirit  is a character version of the card " Aqua Spirit ". 
 
 Biography 
 In  episode 147 , this character is among the group of  Duel Monster Spirits  that plead with  Yami  and  Yugi  to free  Timaeus  and defeat  Dartz .
