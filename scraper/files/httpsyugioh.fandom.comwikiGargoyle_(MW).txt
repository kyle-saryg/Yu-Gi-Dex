 The  Gargoyle  is a minature in  Monster World . It appears at the back of  volume 6  of the  manga , as a cut-out piece to use in a real life game of  Monster World .
 Gargoyle is also a difficult to obtain Monster Capsule, from the Monster Capsule Machines, on the first floor in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Monster Capsule GB .
