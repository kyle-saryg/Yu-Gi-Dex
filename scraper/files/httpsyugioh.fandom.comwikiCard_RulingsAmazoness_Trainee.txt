 TCG Rulings 
 The  ATK  gains are cumulative and last as long as this card is  face-up  on the  field . [1] 
 If you destroy your own monster that your opponent controls,  Amazoness Trainee  gains 200  ATK . [1] 
 If you control both  Grave Protector  and  Amazoness Trainee  and you destroy a monster in battle with  Amazoness Trainee , you choose which effect to apply. [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 If " Amazoness Trainee " and " D.D. Warrior Lady " battle, then first, the effect of " D.D. Warrior Lady " is applied and both monsters are removed from play.  Afterwards, the effect of " Amazoness Trainee " cannot be applied, and you cannot return the monster to the bottom of the Deck. [2] 
 The ATK gains last as long as " Amazoness Trainee " remains face-up on the field.  In addition, each time " Amazoness Trainee " destroys a monster by battle it gains an additional +200 ATK. [3]
