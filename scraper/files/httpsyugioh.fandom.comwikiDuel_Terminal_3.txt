 Duel Terminal 3  is made up of the OCG sets  Duel Terminal - Champion of Chaos!!  and  Duel Terminal - Dragunity of the Hurricane!! .

 2   Breakdown 
 3   Gallery

 Features 
 The set includes 100 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 60 of the cards are new, while 40 are reprints. Introduces the " R-Genex ", " Dragunity ", and " Dragunity Knight " Archetypes to the TCG. Includes further support for the " Fabled ", " Jurrac ", " Ice Barrier ", " Worm ", " Naturia ", and " Ally of Justice " archetypes.
