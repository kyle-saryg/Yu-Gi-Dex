A  ka battle  is a form of combat used in ancient Egypt, in the  manga  and  anime .
 Practitioners summon  ka  from either their soul or stone tablets and use them to fight. The lifeforce of the ka is linked to its user's  ba . Such battles were recreated in the modern world through the card game,  Duel Monsters .
