 Spell of Mask  is a Japanese set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the fifth  Booster Pack  in the  OCG Series 2 .
 In other languages, the set  Labyrinth of Nightmare  is a combination of this set and the Japanese set  Labyrinth of Nightmare .

 Features 
 The first portion of the set contains many cards from the beginning of  Battle City , including cards from  Yami Yugi 's Duel with  Marik Ishtar  possessing  Strings ,  Joey Wheeler 's Duel with  Mako Tsunami  and Yami Yugi and  Seto Kaiba 's Duel with  Umbra  and  Lumis . The next portion contains  OCG  original cards, including a number of  FIRE - Attribute  and  Fairy - Type  monsters, many of which inflict  effect damage  to the opponent or cause the player to gain  Life Points . The set re-releases " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " as an  Ultimate Rare .
 Highly sought after cards include " United We Stand ", " Mage Power ", " Card of Safe Return " and " Torrential Tribute ".
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contained 5 cards and each box contained 30 packs.
