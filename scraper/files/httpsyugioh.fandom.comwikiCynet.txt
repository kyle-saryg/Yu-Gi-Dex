 " Cynet ", known as " Cybenet " (サイバネット  Saibanetto ) in the  OCG , is an  archetype  of  Spell  and  Trap Cards  centered around supporting both  Cyberse  and  Link Monsters . They are used by  Yusaku Fujiki (Playmaker)  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS .
 Starting with  Structure Deck: Master Link , the series focuses on more support for the " Code Talker " archetype.
