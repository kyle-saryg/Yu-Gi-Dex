   Quick-Play 
 
 When your opponent  activates  a  monster effect  that  targets  an " Appliancer " monster(s) you  control :  Negate  that effect, and if you do,  draw  1 card. If an "Appliancer"  Link Monster (s) you control is  destroyed  by a  card effect  and  sent  to the  GY : You can target 1 of your destroyed Link Monsters and 1  Link -1 Link Monster in your opponent's GY;  Special Summon  your monster to your  field  so it is  co-linked , and if you do,  banish  that opponent's monster, then banish this card.
