 The  card shop owner  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories  appearing in the card shop in Ancient Egypt.
 When the player enters the card shop, the owner gives the player the option to:
 
 Build Deck 
 Save Game 
 Return to title Screen . 
 When  the prince  returns to Ancient Egypt after  the Mages  have taken over, the Dueling Grounds and Card Shop relocate at the Hiding Card Shop. Here he gives the player the option to go to the card shop or Dueling Grounds.
