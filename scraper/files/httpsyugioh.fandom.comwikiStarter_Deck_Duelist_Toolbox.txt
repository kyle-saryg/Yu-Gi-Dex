 Starter Deck: Duelist Toolbox  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fourth deck in the  TCG ' s annual Starter Deck series (twelfth overall), following  Starter Deck: Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 2009 . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Starter Deck 2010  in the  OCG .

 The deck features cards used by  Yusei Fudo  in the anime.

 Each  Starter Deck: Duelist Toolbox  contains 44 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
