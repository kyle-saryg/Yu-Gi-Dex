 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Sartorius Kumar , known as  Takuma Saio  (斎王琢磨,  Saiō Takuma ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Sartorius Kumar , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
