 D.D. Tower: Water Dimension
 
 
 D.D. Tower: Water Dimension  is an  event  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It is the second D.D. Tower event.

 Event details 
 The D.D. Tower: Water Dimension has 30 floors. 
 The player must defeat all monsters on a floor to move on to the next floor. Also, they will receive a reward each time they complete a floor. 
 Each Duel has Duel Missions that the player can complete to earn Mission Points. 5 Mission Points can be exchanged for a  D.D. Box . 
 The player's LP carry over between Duels, but if the player's LP exceeds 4000 at the end of a Duel, they will start with 4000 LP during the next Duel. The player gains 100 LP every 5 minutes and cannot Duel while they have 0 LP. 
 This is the first D.D. Tower event to include D.D. Invaders, characters that appear at random and offer large Mission Point rewards for defeating them. In this event, the only D.D. Invader is  D.D. Trainer .
