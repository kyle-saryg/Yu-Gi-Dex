 Mimi Atachi  ( 安 立 ミミ  Atachi Mimi ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS . She is one of the six  Tops of Hexagon  which are Goha's executives, infiltrating  Yuga 's school as a transfer student to get information about  Rush Duels . [3]

 Mannerisms 
 When talking about herself, she uses the pronoun "atachi" (あたち), which is homophonous to her surname, and is a childish version of "atashi", a generally feminine first person pronoun.
