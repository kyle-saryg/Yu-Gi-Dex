The  Chinese, Croatian  and  Greek                      names given are not official.  ( card names ) 
 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Mythical Beast "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 魔導獣 」 .   As its English name contains "Mythical Beast", it  may  be treated a member of the "Mythical Beast" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
" Gazelle the King of Mythical Beasts " + " Berfomet " (This card is always treated as a " Phantom Beast " card.) When this card is  destroyed : You can  target  1 "Berfomet" or 1 "Gazelle the King of Mythical Beasts" in your  Graveyard ;  Special Summon  that target.

   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
 A  beast  that obtained its power by achieving  fusion . However, its life lasts a mere five minutes. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
" Gazelle the King of Mythical Beasts " + " Berfomet " When this card is destroyed, you can Special Summon either "Gazelle the King of Mythical Beasts" or "Berfomet" from your Graveyard, and place it on the field face-up in either Attack or Defense position.
