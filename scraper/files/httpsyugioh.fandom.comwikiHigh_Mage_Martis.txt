 High Mage Martis  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories . He is located at the Desert Shrine.  The Prince  faces him after defeating the  Desert Mage . The Prince duels and defeats him, taking the  Millennium Scale .
 He appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom , named  Marthis , as an assistant to  Emperor Haysheen  and one of the starting Marshals on  Kaiba 's team. After Kaiba's betrayal of the Empire, Marthis deserts Kaiba and alerts Haysheen to his defection. Marthis later appears as an enemy under Haysheen's command, intending to end Kaiba's rebellion but instead is defeated.
 He also appears as an enemy in Joey's mode.
