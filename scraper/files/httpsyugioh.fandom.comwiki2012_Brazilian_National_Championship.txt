 The  2012 Brazilian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Brazil. It took place in Sao Paulo and from May 19 to 20 [1]  and was won by Vinicius de Souza. [2]
