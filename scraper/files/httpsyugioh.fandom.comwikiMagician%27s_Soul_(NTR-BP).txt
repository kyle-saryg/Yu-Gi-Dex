Magician's Soul  is the tenth pack in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Nightmare Troubadour , and is accessible when Level 10 is attained. This series contains cards that power up and support specific monsters. There are also powerful monsters and numerious  Spells , so use these special cards to win!
 The 52 cards in  Magician's Soul  include 4  Normal Monster Cards , 13  Effect Monster Cards , 1  Fusion Monster Card , 0  Ritual Monster Cards , 23  Spell Cards , and 11  Trap Cards .
