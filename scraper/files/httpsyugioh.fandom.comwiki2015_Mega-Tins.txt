 The  2015 Mega-Tins  are a set of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game   collectible tins .  Mega-Tins  are about 15% larger than  Collectible Tins  from previous years and only come in one wave.

 Breakdown 
 Every tin contains three 16-card  Mega Packs  as well as three promotional cards:
 The " Odd-Eyes Pendulum Dragon " Tin contains:
 
 1  Platinum Secret Rare  promotional card 
 2  Super Rare  promotional cards 
 The " Dark Rebellion Xyz Dragon " Tin contains:
 
 1  Platinum Secret Rare  promotional card 
 2  Super Rare  promotional cards
