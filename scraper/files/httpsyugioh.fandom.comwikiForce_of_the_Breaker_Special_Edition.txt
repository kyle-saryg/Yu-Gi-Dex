 Force of the Breaker: Special Edition  is a  Special Edition  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).
 Two different variants were available: a blister pack variant and a box variant.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Force of the Breaker: Special Edition  contains:
 
 2  Force of the Breaker   Booster Packs 
 1   Invasion of Chaos  Booster Pack 
 1 of 2  Super Rare  cards (" Phantom Beast Rock-Lizard " or " Winged Rhynos ")
