 Kazuhiko Mashirito  is the name of a  KaibaCorp  employee who was one of  Trueman's  victims.
 His name appeared on a list of names of people at  KaibaCorp . Each name disappeared after Trueman sent them to the  World of Darkness . Kazuhiko was the last victim on this list to be sent, leaving only  Seto Kaiba .
 The origin of his name is a nickname of Kazuhiko Torishima, who is a former editor in chief of the Weekly Shounen Jump.
