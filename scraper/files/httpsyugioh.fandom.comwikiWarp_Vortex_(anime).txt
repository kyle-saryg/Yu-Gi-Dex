 Main card page: " Warp Vortex " 
 Warp Vortex ワープホール 
 
 Japanese:   ワープホール 
 Romaji:   Wāpuhōru 
 Translated:  Warp Hole 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Type
 
 
 Item 
 
 
 This item's effect can only resolve when there are one or more other "Warp Vortex"s active in the dungeon. Whenever a monster passes through this item, it can be moved to another space on the field containing another active "Warp Vortex".
