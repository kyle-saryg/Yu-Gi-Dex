Main card page: " Earth Armor Ninja " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Earth "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 地 」 .   As its English name contains "Earth", it  may  be treated a member of the "Earth" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
