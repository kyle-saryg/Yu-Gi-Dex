 Invader of Darkness  is a Japanese  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It is the seventh pack in  Series 3 .
 In other languages, it is amalgamated with  Controller of Chaos  to create  Invasion of Chaos .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards and each box contains 30 packs.
 The set contains 57 cards, or 61 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 1  Secret Rare 
 3  Ultra Parallel Rare 
 2  Ultimate Rare 
 3  Ultra Rare 
 4  Super Rare 
 6  Rare 
 42  Common
