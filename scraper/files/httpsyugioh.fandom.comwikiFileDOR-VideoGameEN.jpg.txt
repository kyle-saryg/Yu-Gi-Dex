 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  promotional cards  are a set of  Prismatic Secret Rare  cards bundled with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses . Two different sets of three cards were bundled with the game in different regions: the English, French, German, and Italian versions featured the three " Magnet Warrior " cards, whereas the Spanish version featured " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ", " Dark Magician ", and " Exodia the Forbidden One ".
 In Japan, the game was bundled with  five different cards  and also had a  guidebook  with its own  promotional card .
