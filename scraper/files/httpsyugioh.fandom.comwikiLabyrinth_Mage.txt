 Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories 
 
 
 
 The Labyrinth Mage  is one of the Mages in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories .
 He confronts  the Prince  and  Jono , when they go to the Vast Shrine, to rescue  Teana , who had been kidnapped by  Seto .
 The player may have to face the Labyrinth Mage multiple times, depending on how they navigate through the Labyrinth. To only have to face him once the player should go, right, right, left, right.
 He also appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Falsebound Kingdom , named  Labyrinth Ruler . He is an assistant to  Pegasus , and joins Kaiba's team after Marthis discovers Kaiba making a secret deal with Pegasus.
 
 Deck 
 The Labyrinth Mage plays a  Labyrinth Deck  including 3 copies of " Gate Guardian ".
 
 
 Trivia 
 Labyrinth Mage is the ancient Egyptian counterpart to the  Paradox Brothers , as he uses a Deck similar to theirs.
