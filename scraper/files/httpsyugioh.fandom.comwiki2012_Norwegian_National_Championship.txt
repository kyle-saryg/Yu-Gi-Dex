 The  2012 Norwegian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Norway. It took place in Oslo, Norway on May 27, 2012. [1] 
 
 Events 
 The entry fee was 200  kr  for the main event and 50 kr for the  Dragon Duel . The timetable of events was as follows: [1]
