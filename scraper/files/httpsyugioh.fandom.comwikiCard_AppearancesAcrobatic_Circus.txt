 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 This is the Action Field played for  Yuya Sakaki 's first duel against  Declan Akaba , in  episodes 12  and  13 . It also appears in flashbacks in:
 Episode 14 , when Yuya recalls Declan saying he knows who  Yusho Sakaki  is 
 Episodes 15,   16  and  21 , when Yuya recalls Declan saying that the  Pendulum Summon  can evolve further 
 Episode 27 , when Yuya gives his speech to the crowd at the Paradise Stadium 
 Episode 51 , during Yuya's first rematch against Declan 
 Episode 97 , during the  Friendship Cup  Final between Yuya and  Jack Atlas 
 Episode 147 , prior to Yuya's second rematch against Declan
