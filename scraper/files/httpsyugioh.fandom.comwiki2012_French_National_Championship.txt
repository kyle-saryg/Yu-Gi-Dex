 The  2012 French National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . It determined the 2012 national champion of France. It took place in Espace Charenton in Paris. [1]  It was attended by 504 players [2]  and was won by Sebastien Ma. [3] 
 The entry fee for the event was 20€ and 5€ for the  Dragon Duel  event. [1]

 Decks 
 Overall the tournament consisted of the following Deck types; 96 " Inzektor ", 81 " HERO ", 52 " Dino Rabbit ", 47 " Dark World ", 34 " Twilight   Dragon ", 23 " Six Samurai ", 20 " Wind-Up " and 151 others. [4] 
 The top 64 Decks consisted of 17 "Inzektor", 14 "Dino Rabbit", 11 "HERO", 9 "Dark World", 5 "Wind-Up", 3 "Wind-Up  Beast ", 2 "Twilight Dragon", 1 " Chain Burn ", 1 " Frognarch " and 1 " Gravekeeper's ". [4]
