 Continuous 
 [  Warrior  /  Effect  ] If this card was  Summoned , you can  destroy  all  monsters  you  control  (except for " Destiny Hero " monsters), and then  Special Summon  up to 2 "Destiny Hero" monsters from your  Graveyard . During the  turn  this card is Summoned, "Destiny Hero" monsters you control cannot be  destroyed , and you take no  Battle Damage  when they  battle . The  ATK  and  DEF  of this card are equal to the total ATK of all other "Destiny Hero" monsters you control.
