   Field 
 
 When this card is  activated , increase the  Life Points  of both players by 600 points. Each time a player  declares  an  attack  with a  monster , they can  pay  100 Life Points to inflict 100 points of  damage  to the opponent's Life Points at the end of the  Damage Step .
