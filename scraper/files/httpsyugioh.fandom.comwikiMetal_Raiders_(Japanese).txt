 Revival of Black Demons Dragon 
 Metal Raiders 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Metal Raiders  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game   Booster Pack , released only in Japanese. In other languages another  set of the same name  is a combination of this set and  Revival of Black Demons Dragon .
 The set is named after the manga chapter  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  Duel 061: " Heavy Metal Raiders "  and contains many cards used during  Duelist Kingdom .
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains five cards and each box contains 30 packs.
 The set includes 84 cards consisting of:
 
 2  Secret Rare 
 3  Ultra Rare 
 7  Super Rare 
 12  Rare 
 60  Common
