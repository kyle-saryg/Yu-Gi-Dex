 Twilight Edition 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Twilight Edition: Light & Dark , also known as simply  Twilight Edition , is a set which combines the best  LIGHT  and  DARK  monsters from 2008 and 2009.
 It contains one  Ultra Rare  " Honest " promotional card, and one of each of the following  Booster Packs :  Light of Destruction ,  Phantom Darkness  and  Tactical Evolution .
