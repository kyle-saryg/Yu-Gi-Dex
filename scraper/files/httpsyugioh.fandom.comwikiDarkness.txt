 Continuous 
 When you  activate  this card,  destroy  all cards in your  Spell & Trap Card Zones , then  Set  1 " Zero ", " Infinity ", " Darkness 1 ", " Darkness 2 ", and " Darkness 3 " at  random  from your  hand  or  Deck . You cannot look at these  face-down  cards. During each  End Phase , Set all  face-up  Trap Cards you  control , and randomly rearrange all cards in your Spell & Trap Card Zone. If a Spell or Trap Card you control is  removed from the field , destroy all Spell and Trap Cards you control.
