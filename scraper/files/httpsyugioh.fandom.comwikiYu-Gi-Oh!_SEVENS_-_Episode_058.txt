 " Intertwining Fates " is the fifty-eighth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on July 25, 2021.
 It is evening at Goha Seventh. Hearing  Luke 's scream,  Yuga  and the others rush to the hallway, only to find it filled with puppets. The one who is controlling these puppets is  Yuo Goha , the fourth brother of the Goha Siblings! Despite being at a disadvantage during his  Duel  with Yuga, he sports an enigmatic smile on his face...
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Yuo Goha 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
