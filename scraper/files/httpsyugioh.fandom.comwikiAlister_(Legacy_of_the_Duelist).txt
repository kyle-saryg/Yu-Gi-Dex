 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Alister , known as  Amelda  (トロン  Ameruda ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Alister , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
