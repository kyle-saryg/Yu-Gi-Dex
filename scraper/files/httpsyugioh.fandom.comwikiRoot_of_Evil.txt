 Root of Evil  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from September 18, 2013. Each pack costs   15.
 This collection includes Evilswarm, Reptilianne, Laval, Vampire, Constellar and Hieratic Monsters and Spells.
