 TCG Rulings 
 This effect  targets  the monster in your opponent’s  Graveyard . [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Anarchist Monk Ranshin " is a  Trigger Effect  that activates in the  Graveyard . [2] 
 The effect always activates when it is sent from the field to the Graveyard by battle with an opponent's monster or by an opponent's card effect while under its  owner 's control. [2] 
 It activates even during the  Damage Step . [2] 
 It  targets  1 monster in the opponent's  Graveyard . [2]
