 " Road vs Daor " is the fifty-first episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It aired in Japan on June 6, 2021.
 Both  Luke  and  Yuga 's teams currently have the same amount of points. They both pledge to have a good Duel with each other. Yuga will push forward on his road with  Hyper Engine Vast Vulcan , while Luke will sprint down his door with Dragias the Striking Dragon! The final match, with the King of Duels title at stake, will now begin!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Tatsuhisa "Luke" Kamijo 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
