 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Ally of Justice Cycle Reader " is a  Quick Effect  which  activates  in the hand. [1] 
 Discarding " Ally of Justice Cycle Reader " from your hand is a  cost  to activate its effect. [1] 
 The effect of " Ally of Justice Cycle Reader "  targets  one to two  LIGHT  monsters in your opponent's Graveyard. [1] 
 If an effect like " The Transmigration Prophecy " or " Disappear " is  Chained  to the effect of " Ally of Justice Cycle Reader " so that one of the  targeted   LIGHT  monsters is not in the Graveyard when resolving the effect of " Ally of Justice Cycle Reader ", then the remaining monster in the Graveyard is still removed from play. [2]
