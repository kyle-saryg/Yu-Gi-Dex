 D.D. Trainer  is a character version of the card " D.D. Trainer ". This  Duel Monster Spirit  appears in the video games  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .
