 Jack Atlas  (ジャック・アトラス ,  Jakku Atorasu ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Jack Atlas , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Biography 
 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock Jack by defeating him for the first time. 
 The player can unlock Jack's  Duel Runner ,  Phoenix Whirlwind  by defeating him for the first time. 
 The player can unlock Jack's Type-A Decklist by winning the  Platinum Cup . 
 The player can unlock Jack's Type-B Decklist by winning the  Gold Cup .
