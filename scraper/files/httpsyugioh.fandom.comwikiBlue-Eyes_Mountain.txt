 Blue-Eyes Mountain  is a brand of coffee in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , named after " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ".
 The coffee is expensive, being sold at ¥3000 ($30.00 USD in the dub), which matches the  ATK  of "Blue-Eyes White Dragon".  Jack Atlas  has drank this coffee on occasion at  Cafe la Geen , despite trying to save money.
 This coffee is likely based on  Jamaican Blue Mountain Coffee , which is one of the most expensive coffee brands.
 Blue-Eyes Mountain coffee will be a featured item in the  Anime Plaza Collaboration Cafe . It comes with a free sandwich and is the aforementioned Jamaican Blue Mountain Coffee. It can be purchased for 3000 yen and is the most expensive item on the menu. [1]
