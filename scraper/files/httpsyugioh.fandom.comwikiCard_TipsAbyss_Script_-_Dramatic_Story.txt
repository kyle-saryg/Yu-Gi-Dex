 This card can be searched by " Abyss Actors' Dress Rehearsal ", " Abyss Actor - Liberty Dramatist ", " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King " and " Endymion, the Mighty Master of Magic ". 
 This card's strongest summon target is " Abyss Actor - Evil Heel ". 
 Use this card to target " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope " for destruction and get another free search, or target " Abyss Actor - Sassy Rookie " to summon a monster without losing "Sassy Rookie" due to its protection.
 If targeting "Sassy Rookie", you can summon another Level 4 " Abyss Actor " monster with a different name and go into a  Rank 4   Xyz Monster , provided that you do not use effects that block out-of-archetype Special Summons.
