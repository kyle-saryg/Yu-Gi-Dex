 King of the Skull Servants  is a character version of the card, " King of the Skull Servants ".
 King of the Skull Servants appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . He can be unlocked if the player has beaten  Skull Servant  five times. If the player has unlocked this character, the player can unlock him as a tag partner by beating him ten times.
