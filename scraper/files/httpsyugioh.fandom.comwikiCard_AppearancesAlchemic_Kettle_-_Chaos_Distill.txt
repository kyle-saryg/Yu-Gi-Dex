 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 In  episode 45 ,  Amnael  uses this card during his Duel against  Jaden Yuki . After this card is activated, Amnael explains that all of his cards will be banished if they would be sent to the Graveyard. This in turn sends " Steel Lamp ", " Bronze Scale ", " Lead Compass ", " Black Process - Negledo ", " Alchemy Beast - Salamandra the Steel ", " Alchemy Beast - Ouroboros the Bronze ", " Alchemy Beast - Leon the Lead ", " Tin Spell Circle ", " Mercury Hourglass ", " Silver Key ", " White Process - Albedo ", " Golden Homunculus ", " Alchemy Beast - Aretos the Tin ", " Alchemy Beast - Ekenas the Mercury " and " Alchemy Beast - Moonface the Silver "  to the  Banished  Zone after they left the field. In  the next episode , this card banishes " Chaos Greed " and " Elemental Absorber " after they left the field. Amnael later banishes this card in order to  activate  " Macro Cosmos ". 
 Video games 
 Amnael uses this in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force .
