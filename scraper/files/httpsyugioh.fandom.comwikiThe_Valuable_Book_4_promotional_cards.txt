 The  The Valuable Book 4  promotional cards  are two promotional cards, " Viser Des " and " Legendary Fiend ", included with copies of  The Valuable Book 4 .
 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains two  Ultra Rare   Effect Monsters , both of which were used by  Yami Marik .
