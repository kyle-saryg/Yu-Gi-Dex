 Yudias Velgear  is the main protagonist of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GO RUSH!! . He is from the Velgear Cluster and came to Earth to learn about  Rush Duels  to lead his 8.88 million fellow inhabitants to a new future, and is currently learning Rush Duels under  Yuhi Ohdo  and  Yuamu Ohdo 's tutelage as an employee of UTS.

 Personality 
 Yudias is a very serious individual. He respects Rush Duels deeply, but did understand the gameplay before meeting Yuhi Ohdo and Yuamu Ohdo. Due to his new life on another planet, he is considered clueless when it comes to understanding basic things and trusts people easily, like Chupataro.
 Yudias is considered a pacifist for his idealism to stop war in a peaceful way for his countrymen and his home, defying Zwijo's ideas and refusing to join him to use Duel Monsters as weapons including.
 
 Abilities 
 Yudias has the ability to fire laser beams from his eyes, that can cut "and" repair anything that was previously cut by those same beams. Given his military background, he is a talented swordsman, extremely athletic and also possesses superhuman strength, able to shatter ice surrounding his body with ease.
 
 Appearaance 
 Design 
 Yudias has fair skin, blue spiky hair in two different shades of light blue separated by a black headset. The front layer has two "horns" or "antenna" resembling rabbit ears, while the back layer spikes up and is a lighter blue and aqua color eyes with yellow crescent shaped highlights.
 His alien form has red markings under the eyes. He wears a closed white trench coat with a red inner-lining and red trim running down each side of the torso, black leggings, and blue and gold boots covered in segmented armor. He wears a golden brooch resembling outstretched wings, set with a red stone, a single blue and gold pauldron over his right shoulder strapped over his coat, the strap also holds a blue Deck box, blue hip guards with red trim connected by crossed belts, and carries a short sword at his left hip with a golden guarded hilt and a blue blade that can increase in length.
 
       Yudias first human form     
 He has two human forms. The first one has a long blue coat with red collar and red sleeves. He wears red gloves and a pink with black trim shirt, blue jeans/pants and red boots with black sole and blue ribbons. He also wears a blue gakuran with a gold star.
 
       Yudias second human form     
 In his second human form, Yudias wears the UTS employee uniform, an orange long coat with red inner-lining and the sleeves rolled up to the elbow, brown belts with gold buckles hanging from the shoulders and waist and thick pouches on each side, a larger one and a smaller one above it. He also wears a pink shirt with a dark collar and zip, denim blue pants, dark red gloves, red shoes with the laces wrapped around the ankle. He also wears an orange gakuran with a brown band and a gold star that resembles the UTS badge.
