 Wingman (ウィングマン Winguman) is an  archetype  of  Warrior   Fusion Monsters  that are counterparts or upgraded forms of " Elemental HERO Flame Wingman ", the first  signature card  of  Jaden Yuki  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime. All Members are part of the " Elemental HERO " archetype that received its first support in  Power of the Elements .
