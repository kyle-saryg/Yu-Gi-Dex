 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Crystal "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 宝 玉 」 .   As its English name contains "Crystal", it  may  be treated a member of the "Crystal" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
