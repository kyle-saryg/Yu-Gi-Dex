 Industrial Illusions Tournament  was a tournament mentioned in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
 The tournament was sponsored by  Industrial Illusions  and won by  Aster Phoenix . After the tournament,  Maximillion Pegasus  threw a party at his company HQ where he told Aster about his top five favorite  Duelists .
