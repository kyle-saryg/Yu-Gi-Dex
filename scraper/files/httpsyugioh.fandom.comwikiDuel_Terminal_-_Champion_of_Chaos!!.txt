 Duel Terminal - Champion of Chaos!!  is fifth installment of the  OCG 's  Duel Terminal  series, following  Duel Terminal - Demon Roar God Revival!! , and followed by  Duel Terminal - Dragunity of the Hurricane!! .

 Features 
 Price: 100 Yen per game 
 Type: Arcade (Card Based) 
 DUEL TERMINAL is written on the left opposite to the Set Number. 
 Very faint (near invisible)  QR code  is printed in the center of the card. 
 It also includes further support for " Fabled ", " Jurrac ", " Ice Barrier ", " Worm " and " Naturia "  archetypes . 
 Introduces the new  R-Genex   Archetype . 
 Introduces  Yami Marik ,  Dr. Vellian Crowler  and  Crow Hogan  as duelable opponents. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 50 cards (consisting of 30 New Cards and 20 Reprint Cards), or 54 cards in the  Master Set . This comprises:
 
 4  Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares 
 4  Duel Terminal Ultra Parallel Rares  (all available as Duel Terminal Secret Parallel Rares) 
 6  Duel Terminal Super Parallel Rares 
 10  Duel Terminal Rare Parallel Rares 
 30  Duel Terminal Normal Parallel Rares
