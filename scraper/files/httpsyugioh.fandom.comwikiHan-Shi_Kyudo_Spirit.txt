 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Pendulum Effect : If a  monster(s)  is  Pendulum Summoned :  Return  this card to the  hand . Monster Effect : When this card is  Normal Summoned : You can  return  as many of your cards in the same  columns  as your cards in the  Pendulum Zone  as possible to the  hand , then you can  add  1 monster with 2400  ATK /1000  DEF  from your  Deck  to your hand, except "Han-Shi Kyudo Spirit".  Once per turn , during the  End Phase , if this card was Normal Summoned or  flipped   face-up  this  turn : Return it to the hand.
