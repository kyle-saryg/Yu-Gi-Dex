 Anime Anime Manga Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (فتاة الأمازون السيّافة) literally  Swordswoman Girl of the Amazon . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (الأمازونيّة السيّافة) translates to  Swordswoman Amazon . 
 The  Greek  name given translates to " Amazon Swordswoman ". 
 In the Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Spanish dub, her name was " Mujer de Armas Amazona " (" Amazon Weapons Woman "), which its name in the American Spanish dub was " Espadachina Amazónica " (" Amazonic Swordswoman ").
