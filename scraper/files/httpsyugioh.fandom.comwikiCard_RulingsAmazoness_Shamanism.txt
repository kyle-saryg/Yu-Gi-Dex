 OCG Rulings 
 The effect does not  target . [1] 
 You cannot activate this card during the  Damage Step . [1] 
 The player chooses how many Level 4 or lower " Amazoness " monsters to Special Summon after destroying all " Amazoness " monsters. [1] 
 Destroying all " Amazoness " monsters and Special Summoning the " Amazoness " monsters do not occur simultaneously.  The effect finishes resolving with you Special Summoning " Amazoness " monsters.  If you do not Special Summon any monsters, then the effect finishes resolving with you destroying all " Amazoness " cards. [2]
