 [  Divine-Beast  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except by  sending  3  Spell Cards  you  control  to the  Graveyard . When this card  destroys  an opponent's  monster   by battle  and sends it to the Graveyard, inflict 1000  damage  to your opponent. While you control this  face-up   Defense Position  card, other monsters you control cannot be  selected  as  attack targets . If this Defense Position card is destroyed, you take no damage for the rest of this  turn .
