 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  Normal  or  Special Summoned : You can  target  1 of your  banished   Level  4 or lower  Fish   monsters ; Special Summon it in  Defense Position , but  negate  its  effects . You can banish 1 Fish monster from your  hand  or  face-up   field ;  add  1 " Ghoti "  Trap  from your  Deck  to your hand. You can only use each effect of "Eanoc, Sentry of the Ghotri"  once per turn .
