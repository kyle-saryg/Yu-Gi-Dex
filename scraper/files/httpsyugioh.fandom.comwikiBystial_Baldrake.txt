 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can target 1  LIGHT  or  DARK   monster  in either  GY ;  banish  it, and if you do,  Special Summon  this card from your  hand . This is a  Quick Effect  if your opponent  controls  a monster. If your opponent Special Summons a  Ritual ,  Fusion ,  Synchro ,  Xyz , and/or  Link Monster (s): You can  Tribute  1 other LIGHT or DARK monster, then target 1 of those  Summoned  monsters;  banish  it. You can only use each effect of "Bystial Baldrake" once per turn.
