 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5C's 
 
 
 
 Jumbo Ushio  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5C's  character. He is a parody of  Tetsu Trudge , who is known as Tetsu Ushio in the manga and Japanese version.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 4 
 Jumbo along with other 5C's characters appear in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 . In his own story, he competes with the  Player  against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the Player will receive a photo of Jumbo called "5C's Jumbo".
 
 Tag Force 5 
 Jumbo and the other 5C's characters appear once again in  Tag Force 5  after clearing the stories of a certain number of characters.
 
 Over the Nexus 
       Jumbo in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , he appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for July 2011.
