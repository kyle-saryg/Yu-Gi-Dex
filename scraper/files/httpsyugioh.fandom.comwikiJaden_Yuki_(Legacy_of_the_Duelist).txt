 English name

 Japanese name  
 遊城 十代 遊城十代 ゆうきじゅうだい Rōmaji: ( Yūki Jūdai ) 
 
 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Jaden Yuki , known as  Judai Yuki  ( 遊城 十代 ,  Yūki Jūdai ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Jaden Yuki , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
 
 Decks 
 Jaden's Decks are based on his Duels throughout the anime.
 
 "Flame Wing HERO" -  Jaden Yuki and Alexis Rhodes First Duel
