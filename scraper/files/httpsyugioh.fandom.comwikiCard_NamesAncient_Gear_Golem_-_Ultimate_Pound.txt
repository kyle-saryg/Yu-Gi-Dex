 Names 
 
 In this card's official German TCG name, "pound" was translated as the weight or currency unit. Also, the name doesn't correspond to Ancient Gear Golem's attack name in the German dub of Yu-Gi-Oh! GX ("Mechanischer Faustschlag", " Mechanized Punch ").
