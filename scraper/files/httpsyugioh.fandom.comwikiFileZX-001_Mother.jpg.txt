 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 
 
 
 Mirai Tsukumo  is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL manga . She is the wife of  Kazuma Tsukumo  and the mother of  Yuma Tsukumo  and  Akari Tsukumo .
 
 Biography 
 Mirai is an adventurer together with her husband, Kazuma Tsukumo. They gave Yuma  his pendant , and taught him the " kattobingu " spirit, before disappearing. Yuma claimed he could always "kattobingu", no matter how much people laughed, because he always had the pendant with him, and he felt like his parents were always cheering him up.
