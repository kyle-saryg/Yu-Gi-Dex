 Crusaders' Battlegrounds  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on May 22,2018, 1:00 AM (EST).
 
 Description 
 A silent strike cuts through the darkness. 
 
 Featuring: 
" Silent Swordsman LV7 ,"  Yugi Muto 's " Silent Swordsman " monsters are here! "Silent Swordsman LV7" can completely negate the effect of Spell Cards on the field. If your opponent uses a Deck that uses many Spell Cards, this monster will turn the tide of the Duel. 
" Nobleman of Crossout ," This is a powerful Spell Card that destroys and banishes 1 face-down monster. Not only that, but if the destroyed monster is a Flip monster, you can banish all Cards with the same name from both yours and your opponent's Deck! Flip monsters are often the key card of a Deck, so get rid of them with this card and gain the upper hand! 
" D.D.R. - Different Dimension Reincarnation ," An Equip Spell Card that lets you send 1 Card from your hand to the Graveyard to Special Summon 1 banished monster in Attack Position. This BOX contains many cards that let you banish monsters, so use them alongside this card! 
" Granmarg the Rock Monarch ," This monster destroys 1 Set card on the field when it is Tribute Summoned. It can destroy not only Spell/Trap Cards but monster cards as well, making it highly versatile! 
A total of 100 types of Cards: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Crusaders' Battlegrounds  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
