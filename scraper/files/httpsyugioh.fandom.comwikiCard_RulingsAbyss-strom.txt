 OCG Rulings 
 This card does not  target . [1] 
 This card cannot be activated during the  Damage Step . [1] 
 Sending 1  face-up  " Umi " you control to the  Graveyard  is a  cost  to activate this card. [1] 
 If the only Spell/Trap Cards on the field are " Umi " and a Set " Abyss-strom ", you cannot activate " Abyss-strom ". [1] 
 You can send " A Legendary Ocean " or " Lemuria, the Forgotten City " to the Graveyard to activate " Abyss-strom ". You cannot send " Maiden of the Aqua " to the Graveyard to activate " Abyss-strom ". (While the effect of " Maiden of the Aqua " is being applied, the field is treated as " Umi ", but the name of " Maiden of the Aqua " is not treated as " Umi ".) [2]
