 " go forward " ( ｇｏ   ｆｏｒｗａｒｄ ,  Gō Fōwādo ) is the Second Japanese opening theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime, performed by  KIMERU . It replaced  With the Wind  starting  episode 47  and was replaced by  Calling  starting  episode 103 .

 Changes 
 Episode 48  onward -  Bohman 's " Judgment Arrows " is no longer obscured. 
 Episode 54  onward - All scenes featuring  Blue Angel  are replaced with Blue Girl. 
 Episode 55  onward -  Cyberse Clock Dragon  is no longer covered in shadow.
