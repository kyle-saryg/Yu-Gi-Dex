 Blue-Eyes White Dragon  is a character version of the card " Blue-Eyes White Dragon ".
 It appears as a Rare Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 4 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P025. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 9-2 under Quest mode.

 This  Deck  is also centered around "Blue-Eyes White Dragon". Aim for a " Special Summon " of "Blue-Eyes White Dragon" rather than a  Tribute Summon . It isn't hard to get three of them together, so aiming for a further  Fusion Summon  is also an option.
