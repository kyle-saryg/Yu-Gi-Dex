 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Red "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「レッド」 .   As its English name contains "Red", it  may  be treated a member of the "Red" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Condition 
 When a  monster  you  control   equipped  with an  Equip Spell Card  is  destroyed :  Special Summon  that monster from the  Graveyard , and if you do, equip it with the  Equip Card  it was equipped with. It cannot be  destroyed by battle  or by  card effects  this  turn .
