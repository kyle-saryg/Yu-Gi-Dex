 " RoaRomin " is the fourty-ninth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on May 23, 2021.
 The first match of the finals is between  Roa  and  Romin . Romin drove Roa into a corner with her new "Jam" style, and told him that he can't stand on the stage by himself. Roa was shaken up by her words. As they observed the match,  Getta  and  Ushiro  just couldn't keep still...
 
 Featured Duel: Romin Kirishima VS. Roa Kirishima 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
