 The  helicopter pilot  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's . He is a member of  Sector Security .
 The pilot flew to  Yusei's hideout  to inform  Rally Dawson  that he was to be arrested. However Yusei managed to distract the securities and convince them to drop Rally's conviction. [2]
