 Duelist Entry Deck VS: Saber Force   is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It was released alongside  Duelist Entry Deck VS: Dark Legion , following  Starter Deck 2014 . This Deck is the Korean equivalent of one half of  Duelist Entry Deck VS  in Japan.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Duelist Entry Deck VS: Saber Force  contains 41 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows: 
 
 1  Extra Secret Rare 
 7  Normal Parallel Rares 
 33  Commons 
 In addition, it also contains:
 
 1 Rulebook 
 1  Game Mat
