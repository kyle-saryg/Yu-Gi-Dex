   English 
 
 
Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . When this card is  Special Summoned : Special Summon 1 " Hailon, the Timelord Token " ( ATK  0/ DEF  0) and 1 " Kamion, the Timelord Token " (ATK 0/DEF 0). These  Tokens  and this card gain these effects. ● Cannot be  destroyed by battle  or by an opponent's  card effect , also you take no  battle damage  from  attacks  involving this card. ● At the end of the  Battle Phase , if this card attacked or was attacked: Halve your opponent's  Life Points . -I  Summon  " Michion, the Timelord "!-
