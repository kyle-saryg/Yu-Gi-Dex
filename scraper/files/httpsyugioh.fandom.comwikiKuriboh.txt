   English 
 
 
During  damage calculation , if your opponent's  monster   attacks  ( Quick Effect ): You can  discard  this card; you take no  battle damage  from that  battle .

   Chinese

   Korean 
 
 
 ①: 상대 몬스터의 공격으로 자신이 전투 데미지를 받는 데미지 계산시에 이 카드를 패에서 버리고 발동할 수 있다. 그 전투로 발생하는 자신에게로의 전투 데미지를 0 으로 한다. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
Discard this card from your hand to the Graveyard to negate the damage inflicted by an opponent's monster. This effect can only be used once, and must be activated during your opponent's Battle Phase. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
Discard this card from your hand to make the Battle Damage inflicted to your Life Points by 1 of your opponent's monsters 0. This effect can only be activated during your opponent's Battle Phase.
