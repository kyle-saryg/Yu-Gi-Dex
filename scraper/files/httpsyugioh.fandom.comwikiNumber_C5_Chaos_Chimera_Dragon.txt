 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
3+  Level  6  monsters Gains 1000  ATK  for each  material   attached  to it. If this card  attacks , at the end of the  Damage Step : You can  detach  1 material from this card; this card can attack an opponent's monster again in a row. At the end of the  Battle Phase : You can  pay  half your  LP , then  target  2 cards in any  GY (s); place 1 of them on the top of the  Deck , and if you do, attach the other to this card as material. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Min. 3 monstres de Niveau 6 Gagne 1000 ATK pour chaque Matériel qui lui est attaché.
