 Toon Chaos  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes new members and/or support for the " Black Luster Soldier ", " Chaos ", " Code Talker ", " Edge Imp ", " Fluffal ", " Frightfur ", " Harpie ", " Magician ", " Noble Knight ", " Toon " and " Valkyrie " archetypes as well as the  Creator ,  Daedalus  and  Gearfried  series in the TCG. 
 Introduces the  Infernoble Knight  series to the  TCG . 
 Introduces the  Collector's Rare  rarity to the  TCG . 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 7 cards. The set contains 60 cards:
 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 15  Super Rares 
 35  Rares 
 In addition, 15 cards in this set are also available as  Collector's Rare .
