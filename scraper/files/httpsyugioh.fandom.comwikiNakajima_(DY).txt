 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 
 
 
 Nakajima  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate incarnation of  Claude , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 Nakajima works under  Reiji Akaba .
