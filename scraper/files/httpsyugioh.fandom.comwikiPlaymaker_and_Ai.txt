 Playmaker and Ai  are a pair of characters who function as a single playable  Legendary Duelist  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . This is a video game depiction of  Yusaku Fujiki  and  Ai , characters from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime.
 He only appears in-game after the player reaches Stage 10 of Duel World (VRAINS) and can then be unlocked as a playable character.
 Black Gate Keys are required to Duel Playmaker and Ai at the Gate.

 Description 
 Ten years after surviving the Lost Incident, Playmaker enters LINK VRAINS to seek revenge against the Knights of Hanoi. Aiding him on his mission is Ai - a mysterious and mischevious AI who's hunted by the Hanoi. While they bicker while they battle, this mismatched duo will decide the fate of both humans and AIs. Playmaker's true identity is Yusaku Fujiki - a skilled hacker who attends Den City High School.

 Skills 
 
 
 Skill
 
 Effect
 
 Unlock
 
 
 Storm Access
 
 At the beginning of the Duel, add 1 "Decode Talker" and 1 "Firewall Dragon" to your Extra Deck. The following effect can be used if your Life Points are at 1000 or below: Add 1 random "Code Talker" monster to your Extra Deck via a Data Storm. This Skill can only be used once per Duel.
 
 Level 1
 
 
 Draw Sense: Cyberse
 
 Can be used before your normal draw each time your Life Points decrease by 1000. One of the cards from your normal draw this turn will be a random Cyberse monster from your Deck.
 
 Level 13
 
 
 Code Extended
 
 Select 1 Link 3 "Code Talker" Link Monster you control and change it to "Decode Talker Extended". If you select "Decode Talker" in your Extra Monster Zone, you can also select 1 opponent monster and move that target to their Main Monster Zone in the same column as the monster you have selected. This Skill can only be used once per Duel.
 
 Level 20
 
 
 Code Talker Alive
 
 Can be used if you have "Decode Talker" in your Extra Monster Zone. Set one "Recoded Alive" to your field from outside of your Deck. This Skill can only be used once per Duel if you begin the Duel with a Deck that follows the conditions below: 
 Your Deck contains at least 12 Cyberse monsters or "Cynet" Spell/Trap cards. 
 All monsters in your Extra Deck have different card names. 
 
 Drop
 
 
 Cynet Conversion
 
 Can be used if your Life Points are at 2500 or below. Send 1 Cyberse monster from your hand to the Graveyard, and add 1 "Cynet" Spell/Trap card from your Deck to your hand. This Skill can only be used once per Duel.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Three Effects!
 
 Select 1 Link Monster you control, and apply one of the following effects of your choice: 
 For one time only until the end of your opponent's next turn, that monster will not be destroyed by battle. 
 Until the end of your opponent's next turn, you take no battle damage from attacks involving that monster. 
 Increase your Life Points by an amount equal to the attack of that monster. 
 This Skill can only be used once per Duel.
 
 
 Drop
 
 
 Link Arrow Gain
 
 Select 1 Link Monster you control. Until the end of the turn, monsters this card points to gain ATK equal to the Link Rating of the selected Link Monster x 100. This Skill can only be used once per turn.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Link Booster
 
 Each Link Monster you control gains 100 ATK for each monster it currently points to.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Monster Move
 
 Select 1 monster you control, and move it to another of your Main Monster Zones. If you select a monster in the Extra Monster Zone, move it to your Main Monster Zone in the same column. This Skill can only be used once per turn.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Level Tuning
 
 Can be used if your Life Points are at 2000 or below. Select 2 monsters on your side of the field and declare a monster Level between 1 and 4. The selected monster's Levels will be equal to the number you declared. This Skill can only be used once per Duel.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Balance
 
 Your starting hand will reflect the card balance of your deck. This Skill will not activate unless you have at least 6 of each type of card (Monster, Spell, and Trap) in your Deck. You cannot Special Summon or activate monster effects until the end of the Main Phase of your first turn.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Draw Sense: DARK
 
 Can be used before your normal draw each time your Life Points decrease by 1500. One of the cards from your normal draw this turn will be a random DARK monster in your Deck.
 
 Drop
 
 
 Restart
 
 Can be used only once after starting hands are drawn. Reduce your starting hand by 1, and then redraw your hand. You cannot Special Summon or activate monster effects until the end of the Main Phase of your first turn.

 Dialogue 
 Skill-Specific 
 When Playmaker and Ai activate the Skill "Storm Access", they will announce one of the following dialogues:
 Playmaker announces "I activate my Skill!" followed by Ai announcing "Seize the wind, Playmaker!" followed by Playmaker announcing "Storm Access!" 
 Playmaker announces "Ai, are you ready?" followed by Ai announcing "Sure am, my dude! I'm whipping up a Data Storm!" followed by Playmaker aannouncing "Storm Access!" 
 Character-specific 
 Soulburner 
 When starting a Duel against  Soulburner , Ai announces "Just cuz that meatbag's your friend, don't go easy on him!" followed by Playmaker announcing "I never take Soulburner lightly - not if I want to win." 
 When passing the turn to Soulburner in a Tag Duel, Playmaker says "You're up, Soulburner!" 
 The Gore 
 When starting a Duel against  The Gore , Ai announces "It's the Gore! What deck ya think he's playing this time?" followed by Playmaker announcing "It doesn't matter. As long as I can outstrategize him!" 
 Blue Angel 
 When starting a Duel against  Blue Angel , Ai announces "Be careful! I heard she's a superstar in LINK VRAINS!" followed by Playmaker announcing "I'm always careful, Ai." 
 Knight of Hanoi 
 When starting a Duel against Knight of Hanoi, Playmaker announces "Knight of Hanoi! You'll be seeing a lot more of me." followed by "So you ought to remember my name. It's Playmaker!"
