 World Duel Carnival Preliminaries  is the second arc of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime, encompassing the preliminary rounds of the  World Duel Carnival  tournament. 
 It is a  Duel Monsters  tournament hosted by  Mr. Heartland  and made created by  Dr. Faker  for purpose of gathering all  Number Holders  in one place, as they need all hundred " Number " for their plans. In the same vein,  Astral  and the  Vetrix Family  also see the tournament as the perfect opportunity to gather more "Numbers".
