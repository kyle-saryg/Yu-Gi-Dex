 Tetsu Trudge , known as  Tetsu Ushio  (牛尾哲,  Ushio Tetsu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Tetsu Trudge , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
