 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 When  this card is  face-up on the field  and control  shifts to your opponent, inflict 2000 points of  Direct   Damage  to your opponent's Life Points. This effect can only be used once as long as this card remains face-up on the field. 
 When  the control of this  face-up  card  on the field shifts to your opponent, inflict 2000 points of  damage  to your opponent's Life Points. This effect can only be used once as long as this card remains face-up on the field.
