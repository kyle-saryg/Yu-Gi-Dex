 Millennium Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Millennium Pack  is a pack in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  that introduced new cards from the original  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yami Marik ,  Yami Yugi ,  Yugi Muto ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Bandit Keith ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  the Big Five ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Atticus Rhodes  and  Adrian Gecko .
