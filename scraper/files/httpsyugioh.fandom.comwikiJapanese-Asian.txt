 Japanese-Asian  cards are  Japanese  cards produced and intended to be sold in Asian countries outside Japan. They are commony abbreviated  JA , but listed as  JP  in card set number.
 JA and JP cards differ in quality. On higher rarty ( Secret Rare ), the foil pattern also differs: JA's would be horizontal and JP would be vertical.
 In OCG tournaments outside JP. The player must use a deck composed of only JA cards if those cards are Japanese. The only exception is if a card is a JP product exclusive.
