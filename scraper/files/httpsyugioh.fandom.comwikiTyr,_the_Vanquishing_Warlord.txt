 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
This card cannot be  Special Summoned . To  Tribute Summon  this card, you must  Tribute  3  Fairy - Type   monsters . If this card  attacks  your opponent  directly  and reduces their  Life Points  to 0, you win the  Match .
