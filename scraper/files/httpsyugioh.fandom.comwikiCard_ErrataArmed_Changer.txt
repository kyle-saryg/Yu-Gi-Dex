 First erratum 
 
 
 Send 1 Equip Spell Card from your hand to the Graveyard to activate this card. If the equipped monster destroys a monster as  a result of  battle, the controller of this Equip Card can add 1 monster with  an  ATK equal to or less than the equipped monster from their Graveyard to their hand.
