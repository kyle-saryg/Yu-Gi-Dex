 Amazoness Village 
 
 Japanese:   アマゾネスの里 
 Romaji:   Amazonesu no Sato 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Field 
 
 All " Amazoness "  monsters  gain 200  ATK . Once per  turn , when an "Amazoness" monster is  destroyed by battle  or by a  card effect  and  sent  to the  Graveyard : You can  Special Summon  1 "Amazoness" monster from your  Deck  with a  Level  less than or equal to that "Amazoness" monster in the Graveyard. You can use  Action Cards , but you can only have 1 Action Card in your  hand .
