 Breakdown 
 Each  Chaos Impact Special Edition  contains:
 
 3  Chaos Impact  Booster Packs 
 1 of 2  Super Rare   variant cards , either " Scrap Wyvern " or " Battle Shogun of the Six Samurai " 
 1 of 2 Super Rare preview cards from  Ignition Assault , either " Dragonmaid Send-Off " or " Armory Call "
