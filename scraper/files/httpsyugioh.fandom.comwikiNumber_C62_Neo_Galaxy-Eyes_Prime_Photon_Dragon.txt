 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
3  Level  8  LIGHT   monsters You can also  Xyz Summon  this card by using " Number 62: Galaxy-Eyes Prime Photon Dragon " you  control . ( Transfer  its  materials  to this card.) At the start of your  Battle Phase : You can  detach  1 material from this card; this card can make up to 3  attacks  on monsters during this  Battle Phase . If this card has " Galaxy-Eyes Photon Dragon " as material, it gains these  effects . ●  Unaffected  by your opponent's  monster effects . ● Gains  ATK  equal to the combined Levels and  Ranks  of all monsters  attached  to it as material x 100.
