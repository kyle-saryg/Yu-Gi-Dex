 Mei 's  father  is an unseen character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . He serves as a member of  Sector Security .
 Mei learned from her father that  Jack Atlas  was in  Satellite  and relayed this information to  Akiza Izinski . [1]
