 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Seto Kaiba ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Crow Hogan ,  Kite Tenjo , and  Sylvio Sawatari . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Blue-Eyes ", " Cyber Dragon ", " Blackwing ", " Galaxy ", and " Abyss Actor " archetypes.
