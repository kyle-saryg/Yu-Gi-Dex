 You can  target  a  Pendulum Monster  you control:  equip  this monster to that target. If the equipped monster  battles , that  monster  is not  destroyed by battle , also you receive no  battle damage  from that battle. If a " D/D " monster you control battles ( Quick Effect ): you can banish this card from your  Graveyard ; you receive no damage from that battle, also  destroy  the monster it  attacked  and inflict damage to your opponent, equal to half the destroyed monster's  ATK  it had on the  field .
