 Premium Pack Vol.2  is a Korean pack, containing a number of cards that were released as promos in the Japanese  OCG , that were previously unreleased in Korean. The shares its set id,  PP02  with the  TCG  version of  Premium Pack 2 , although contains different cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 30 cards or 36 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 6  Secret Rare 
 30  Ultra Rare
