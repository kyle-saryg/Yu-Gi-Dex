 Altar of Mist

 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 After  activation , this card remains on the  field  for 3  turns . This  face-up  card cannot be  destroyed  by  card effects . During your 3rd  Standby Phase  after activation,  send  this card to the  Graveyard  and  add  1  Ritual Spell Card  from your  Deck  to your  hand .
