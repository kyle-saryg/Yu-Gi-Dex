 Starter Deck: Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 2009  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the third deck in the  TCG ' s annual Starter Deck series (eleventh overall), following  Starter Deck: Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Starter Deck 2009  in the  OCG .

 Features 
 The set features 9 new cards, including a new  Synchro Monster  played by  Yusei Fudo . 
 This deck features the Duel Terminal " X-Sabers " archetype for the first time in the  TCG . 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Starter Deck: Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 2009  contains 43 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
