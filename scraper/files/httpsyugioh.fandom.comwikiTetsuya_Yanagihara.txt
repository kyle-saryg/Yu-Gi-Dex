 Tetsuya Yanagihara  is a star from the TV show  Sakiyomi JumBANG!  and a Japanese voice actor in real life. He provides the voice of Yeager ( Lazar ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's [2] , and reprises the role in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  by voicing an  alternate universe version  of the character [3] .
 He has also appeared as a character within the film  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time .
 
 Character biography 
 Tetsuya Yanagihara and his co-stars  Akina Minami  and  Yoshiyuki Hirai  work as MCs at an event held by  Maximillion Pegasus  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time .
 Later, three people resembling the trio appear in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  to promote the  World Duel Carnival .
