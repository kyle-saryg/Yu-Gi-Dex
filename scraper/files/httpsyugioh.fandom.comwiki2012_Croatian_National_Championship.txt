 The  2012 Croatian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of the Croatia. It took place in Zagreb on June 16, 2012. Public events also took place on June 17 [1]  and was won by Martin Rodin. [2]

 Events 
 The entry fee was 110  kn  for the main event and 35 kn for the  Dragon Duel . The public events consisted of four  Win-A-Mat  tournaments, four  Battle Pack: Epic Dawn   Sealed  tournaments and two 8-player  single-elimination  tournaments. [1]
