 Blair Flannigan , known as  Rei Saotome  (早乙女レイ,  Saotome Rei ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Blair Flannigan , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
