 Advanced Tournament Pack 2015 Vol.3  is the thirty-fourth installment of the Japanese  Tournament Pack  series. The advanced packs come in two styles  Gold  and  Silver . The  Gold  pack is awarded to the tournament winners and is guaranteed to have a Parallel Rare inside. The  Silver  packs are awarded as participation packs and have a chance of having a Parallel Rare card.
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 2 cards per pack.
 The set includes 10 cards. This is comprised of:
 
 3  Normal Parallel Rare
