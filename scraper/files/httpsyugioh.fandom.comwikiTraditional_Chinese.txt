Traditional Chinese  cards are  Chinese  cards printed with the  Traditional  set of Han characters. They are manufactured by Konami of Hong Kong, and have been officially distributed in Asia since 2014, introduced in the  Duel Starter Deck . [1]  This is the only printed language in which there is no form of text about the  Attributes . Their ID is  TC . There hasn't been any new release since the last set  Lord of the Tachyon Galaxy  in 2016.
