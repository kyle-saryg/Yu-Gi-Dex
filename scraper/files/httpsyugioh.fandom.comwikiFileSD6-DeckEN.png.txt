 Structure Deck: Spellcaster's Judgment  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the sixth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (fourteenth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Warrior's Triumph . In the  TCG , it is the sixth deck.
 
 Magicians ,  Warlocks , and  Sorcerers  are yours to command with the "Spellcaster’s Judgment"  Structure Deck . Use your  Spell Cards  to strengthen your  monsters  and  damage  your opponents. "Spellcaster’s Judgment" is ready to play with out of the box, and is perfect for new Duelists, and is packed with powerful cards. Each  Deck  contains 40 cards, including 6 brand new cards including the  Ultra Rare  " Dark Eradicator Warlock " and 2 of  Yugi Muto ’s most famous  Spell Cards . Also included is a rulebook, game mat, and a Dueling Guide which assists Duelists in improving and perfecting the  Deck 's strategies. 
 —  Upper Deck Entertainment 
 
 Although this Structure Deck was not released in Spanish, it is listed on the Spanish version of the official card database. [1]

 Features 
 The deck features  Spellcaster - Type  monsters and  Direct Damage  through card  effects . Featured cards include " Dark Eradicator Warlock ", " Magical Dimension ", and " Chaos Sorcerer ".
