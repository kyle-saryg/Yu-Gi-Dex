 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 
 
 Keith  is the  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses  incarnation of  Bandit Keith . He is a member of the White Roses of York.
 If the player chooses to side with the Red Roses, they face Keith at  Towton  after defeating  Weevil Underwood . After defeating him, the player can go to Newcastle to face  Labyrinth Ruler 
 Keith has changed his signature USA doorag for a Union Flag doorag. However, the game is set in the 1400s, nearly 300 years before the Union of the Crowns.
 
 Deck 
 He usually plays a strategy, where he uses three monsters with high ATK to try and box the enemy Deck Leader.
