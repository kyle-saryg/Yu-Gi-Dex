 Extra Pack 2017  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG - exclusive  cards to the  OCG . [1] 
 The Korean first print run of this set had a text error in both the boxes and packs; it stated that "All  Ultra Rare  cards are also available as  Secret Rares " (※ 울트라 레어 카드는 시크릿 레어 카드의 사양으로도 존재합니다). This error has been removed from following printings, making it a 1st print-only mistake. The error was only in the text and no actual rarity-missprinted cards were made. [2]

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive cards from the Booster Packs  The Dark Illusion ,  Invasion: Vengeance ,  Raging Tempest ,  Maximum Crisis  and  Code of the Duelist , as well as reprints of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2016 prize cards . 
 Includes members/support for the " Ninjitsu Art ", " Fusion ", " Pendulum " and " Trap Monster " archetypes, as well as " Match winner " series. 
 Introduces the " F.A. ", " SPYRAL ", " Subterror " and " Vendread " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 First  Extra Pack  to not include  OCG  Region-exclusive imports since  2013 .
