 Names 
 
 This card can be  searched  by " Branded in High Spirits ", " Starliege Seyfert ", " Draconnection ", " Tempest, Dragon Ruler of Storms ", " Hieratic Seal of the Heavenly Spheres ", " Hieratic Dragon King of Atum ", " Dragonic Tactics ", " Blue-Eyes Abyss Dragon ", " Krystal Dragon ", " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation ", " Dragonic Guard ", " Ahrima, the Wicked Warden ", " Magical Contract Door ", " Chaos Zone " and " Link Party ". 
 Use this card to enable the effect of a " Branded " Spell/Trap that activates during the End Phase, by either:
 Sending that "Branded" Spell/Trap from your Deck to the Graveyard to return this card from the Graveyard to the Deck 
 Discarding that "Branded" Spell/Trap to Special Summon this card from your hand. 
 Using this card's effect from the hand to mill a "Branded" Spell/Trap from your Deck will not activate its End Phase effect because this card would return from the hand to the Deck. Only on the field or in the Graveyard is its name treated as " Fallen of Albaz ". 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Guardragon Elpy " and " Eclipse Wyvern ". 
 Support 
 As this card shares the same name as " Fallen of Albaz ", it, too, has the same  card support .
