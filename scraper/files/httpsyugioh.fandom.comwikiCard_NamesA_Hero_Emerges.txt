 This card's Arabic name (انبثاق البطل) could be translated as " Hero's Emergence ". 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Ένας Ήρωας Αναδύεται ") is an exact translation of the  TCG  name of this card.
 A possible translation of the  OCG  name (" Hero Meeting ") in Greek could be " Συνάντηση με τον Ήρωα ".
