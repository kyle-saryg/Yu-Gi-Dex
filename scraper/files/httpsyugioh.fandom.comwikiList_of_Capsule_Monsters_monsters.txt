This is a  list of  Capsule Monsters  monsters .  Capsule Monsters  is a board game featured in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters , partly based on the earlier game  Capsule Monster Chess , and was featured in the real-life game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game .
 For a list of monsters featured in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game , see  List of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monsters Collectible Figure Game  monsters .
