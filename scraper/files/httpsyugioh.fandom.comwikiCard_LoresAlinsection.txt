 Greek - Τομή 
Ένα αρσενικό σκαθάρι με κεφάλι και χέρια σαν πριόνι.
 
 
 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was " A stag beetle with saw-like head and arms. " 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Cerf-volant avec une tête et des bras servant de scie. " 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Ein Hirschkäfer mit einem sägenartigen Kopf und Armen. " 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Cervo volante con braccia e testa affilate come seghe. " 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Un ciervo volante con cabeza y patas como sierras. "
