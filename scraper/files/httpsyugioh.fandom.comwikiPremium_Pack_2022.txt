 Premium Pack 2022  is the 24th  Premium Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ), following  Premium Pack 2021 .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Reiko Kitamori ,  Seika Kohinata ,  Skeleton Knight ,  Astral ,  Nash ,  Cologne  and  Yuya Sakaki . 
 Includes members/support for the " Chaos ", " Dragunity ", " Gaia The Fierce Knight ", " Fusion ", " Ice Barrier ", " Kuriboh ", " Magician ", " Number ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Performapal ", " Seventh " and " Xyz " archetypes, as well as  Grepher  series.
 Introduces the first support for " Puppet " archetype to the OCG. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 32 cards:
 
 12  Secret Rares 
 3  Ultra Rares 
 5  Super Rares 
 12  Commons 
 The Ultra Rare, Super Rare and Common cards are also available as Secret Rare.
The Common cards are also available as  Normal Parallel Rare .
