 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! Destiny Board Traveler 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (منفخ الأمازونيّة) meaning somewhat  Blowpipe of the Amazon . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (الأمازونيّة النافخة) translates to  Blower Amazon . 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given ( Αμαζόνα Αυλητής ) translates to "' Amazon Blowpiper ".
