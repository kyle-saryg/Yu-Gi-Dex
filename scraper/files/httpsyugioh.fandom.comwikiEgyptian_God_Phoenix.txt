 Trigger 
 
 
 
 This was not a physical card; rather, it was the form " The Winged Dragon of Ra " took when it activated its effect to destroy all of the opponent's monsters when it was  Special Summoned .
 
 [  Divine-Beast  ] When this card  battles , you can  pay  1000  Life Points  to  destroy  the  monster  this card is battling. [Note 1]
