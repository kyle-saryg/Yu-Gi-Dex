 Extra Pack Volume 3  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -exclusive cards to the  OCG .

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive  cards from the  Booster Packs   Raging Battle ,  Ancient Prophecy ,  Stardust Overdrive  and  Absolute Powerforce . 
 Includes further support for " Lightsworn ", " Earthbound Immortal ", " Koa'ki Meiru ", and " XX-Saber "  archetypes . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 10 cards per pack and 10 packs per Japanese box. There are 5 cards per pack and 30 packs per Korean box.
 The set includes 40 cards, comprising:
 
 2  Ultra Rare 
 4  Super Rare 
 6  Rare 
 2  Normal Rare 
 26  Common
