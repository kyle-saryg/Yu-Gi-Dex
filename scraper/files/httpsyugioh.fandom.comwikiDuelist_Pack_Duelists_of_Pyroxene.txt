 Duelist Pack: Duelists of Pyroxene 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Duelist Pack: Duelists of Pyroxene  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Leo ,  Ishizu Ishtar ,  Grace Tyler  and  Gloria Tyler . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Amazoness ", " Gadget ", " Gravekeeper's ", " Morphtronic " and " Power Tool " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 The set contains 46 cards:
 
 1  Holographic Rare  (1  Extra Secret Rare  in Korean version) 
 5  Ultra Rares 
 7  Super Rares 
 9  Rares 
 24  Commons 
 The Ultra Rare and Super Rare cards are also available as  Secret Rare .
