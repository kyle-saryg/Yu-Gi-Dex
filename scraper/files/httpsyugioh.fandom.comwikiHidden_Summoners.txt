 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards and each box contains 24 Booster Packs. The set contains 60 cards:
 
 20  Secret Rares 
 40  Super Rares 
 Features 
 Includes members/support for the " Nephthys " archetype. 
 Introduces the " Mayakashi " and " Prank-Kids " archetypes to the TCG.
