 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5C's 
 
 
 
 Ryusei  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5C's  character, who is a parody of  Yusei Fudo .
 He appears in  V Jump  and some video games.

 Appearances 
 V Jump 
 Ryusei frequently appears in  V Jump  alongside other  cosplay  characters.
 He took part in  The Third Charisma Duelist Determining Fight Tournament . [1]  He lost to  Duelua  in the first round. [2] 
 
 Manga 
 Ryusei appears on a page of the final volume of the  Yu-Gi-Oh GX  manga. 
 
 Video games 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 Ryusei appears in the  Tag Force  games after the player clears 5 different stories.
 
 World Championship 2010 
 Ryusei appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for June 2010.
 
 World Championship 2011 
 Ryusei appears as a  downloadable  Ghost Duelist for November 2011.
