 Darkwing Blast  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the tenth set in the  OCG ' s  11th series , following  Power of the Elements .

 Features 
 In the first print runs of Darkwing Blast boxes, includes an  Darkwing Blast +1 Bonus Pack 
 Includes new members/support for the " Blackwing ", " Branded ", " Ninja ", " Ninjitsu Art ", " Prediction Princess ", " Naturia ", " Dracoslayer ", " Igknight ", " Majespecter ", " Dinomist ", " Vernusylph ", " Fur Hire ", " Labrynth ", " Tearlaments ", " Runick ", " Spright ", " Springans ", " Tri-Brigade ", " Scareclaw ", " Vaylantz ", " Bamboo Sword ", " Archfiend ", and " Pendulum "  archetypes , as well as the  Adventurer Token  and  Martial Arts Spirit  series.
 TCG version includes new members/support for the " Amazoness " and " Ghoti " archetypes. 
 Introduces the " Bystial " and " Kashtira " archetypes to the  OCG/TCG . 
 Breakdown
