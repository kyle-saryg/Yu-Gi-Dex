 When "Stop Defense" was released, it was drawn with a different artwork compared to its original  Yu-Gi-Oh! manga  artwork. 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters II: Dark duel Stories ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Dark Duel Stories  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , "Stop Defense" was drawn with a different artwork.
