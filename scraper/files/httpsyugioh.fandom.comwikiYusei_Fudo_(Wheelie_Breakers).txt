 Yusei Fudo  (不動遊星,  Fudō Yūsei ) is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Yusei Fudo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.

 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock Yusei by defeating him for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Stardust Dragon " by defeating Yusei for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Magic Cylinder " by defeating Yusei for the second time. 
 The player can unlock " Scrap-Iron Scarecrow " by defeating Yusei for the third time. 
 The player can unlock " Barrel Behind the Door " by defeating Yusei for the fourth time. 
 The player can unlock " Magic Jammer " by defeating Yusei for the fifth time. 
 The player can unlock " Shift Down " by defeating Yusei for the sixth time. 
 The player can unlock " Slip Stream " by defeating Yusei for the seventh time. 
 The player can unlock " Speed Warrior " by defeating Yusei for the eighth time. 
 The player can unlock " Graceful Charity " by defeating Yusei for the ninth time. 
 The player can unlock " Super Rejuvenation ", " Curse of Aging  and " Synchro Strike " by defeating Yusei for the tenth time. 
 The player can unlock Yusei's  Duel Runner ,  Yusei Go  by defeating Yusei for the first time.
