 Trigger-like 
 When a  monster  you  control  is  targeted  for an  attack  by an opponent's monster: Target the  attack target ; that target cannot be  destroyed by that battle . An opponent's monster that  battles  with that target can make another attack once. Then, if that target is targeted for an attack: Its  ATK  becomes equal to the ATK of the opponent's monster it is battling + 100.
