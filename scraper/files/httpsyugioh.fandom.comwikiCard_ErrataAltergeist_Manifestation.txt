 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 Target 1 "Altergeist" monster in your GY; Special Summon it in Attack Position, and equip it with this card. When this card leaves the field, destroy that monster. You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Altergeist" Trap in your GY; add it to your hand. You can only use this effect of "Altergeist Manifestation" once per turn. 
 Target 1 "Altergeist" monster in your GY; Special Summon it in Attack Position, and  if you do,  equip it with this card. When this card leaves the field, destroy that monster. You can banish this card from your GY, then target 1 "Altergeist" Trap in your GY; add it to your hand. You can only use this effect of "Altergeist Manifestation" once per turn.
