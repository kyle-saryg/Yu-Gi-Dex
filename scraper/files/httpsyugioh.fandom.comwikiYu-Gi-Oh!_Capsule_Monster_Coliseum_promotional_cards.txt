 Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum  promotional cards  is a set of three promotional cards, " Abyss Soldier ", " Inferno Hammer ", and " Teva ", released with the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum ; the cards are  Ultra Rare  in the Japanese release and  Super Rare  in the international releases. "Abyss Soldier" was also released as a  Secret Rare   promotional card  with the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Capsule Monster Coliseum Guide Book  in Japan.
