 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 109 ,  Ai  uses this card during his  Master Duel  against  Pandor . When the effect of Pandor's " Topologic Trisbaena " would banish this card, Ai activates it, increasing the ATK of his " Fire Phoenix @Ignister " by 800 and forcing Pandor's monsters to attack it. However, "Fire Phoenix" is destroyed by the effect of Pandor's " Topologic Bomber Dragon " before she enters her  Battle Phase .
