 Super Starter Power-Up Pack , referred here as  Space-Time Showdown Power-Up Pack  is a  Power-Up Pack  for  Super Starter: Space-Time Showdown . Each  Super Starter: Space-Time Showdown  contains 2  Power-Up Packs .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Super Starter: Space-Time Showdown  contains 2 packs of  Space-Time Showdown Power-Up Pack . 1 of the pack contains 1 " Dark Hole " and 4 other cards, while the other pack contains 1 " Odd-Eyes Dragon " and 4 other cards. There are 15 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
 
 2  Ultra Rares 
 13  Commons
