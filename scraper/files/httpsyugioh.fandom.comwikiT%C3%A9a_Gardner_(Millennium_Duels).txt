 Téa Gardner , known as  Anzu Mazaki  (真崎杏子,  Mazaki Anzu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Téa Gardner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
