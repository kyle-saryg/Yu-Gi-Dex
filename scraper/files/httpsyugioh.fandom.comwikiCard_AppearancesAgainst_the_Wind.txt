 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 66 ,  Crow Hogan  used this card in his  Friendship Cup   Turbo Duel  against  Gong Strong .  He  drew  this card on the final turn of the Duel and subsequently  activated  it,  targeting  " Blackwing - Blizzard the Far North " in his  Graveyard  and  adding  it to his  hand  at the cost of taking  damage  equal to its  ATK . 
 
 
 Video Games 
 Crow Hogan  uses this card in his Deck in the video game  Tag Force 4 .
