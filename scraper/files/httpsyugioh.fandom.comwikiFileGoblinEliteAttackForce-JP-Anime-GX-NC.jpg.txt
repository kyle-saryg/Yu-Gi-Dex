 Biography 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , the Goblin Elite Attack Force served as foot soldiers to  the Supreme King's Army . Two members were lured into Dueling  Zane Truesdale  and  Aster Phoenix  and were defeated. Just before disappearing, one member confirmed that  The Supreme King  is  Jaden Yuki , while the other revealed the Supreme King's next move.
 Other members of the Attack Force hunted down  Maiden of the Aqua  and  Dancing Fairy , sending a trio of  flaming foxes  after them in their efforts. The foxes were defeated by  Axel Brodie  with the Maiden's help.
 
 Non-canon appearances 
       Goblin Elite Attack Force in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator .     
 Goblin Elite Attack Force is an unlockable opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . They are tag partners with  Gagagigo 
 
 Decks 
 Anime 
       The monsters of Goblin Elite Attack Force are  destroyed  by  Zane Truesdale 's " Cyber Dragons ".     
 In the anime, the member of the Attack Force that Dueled  Zane Truesdale  was shown to have the cards " Luster Dragon ", " Spear Dragon " and " Return Zombie ".
