 Wonders of the Sky  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on March 16, 2017, 8:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Soar through the heavens on the wings of The Atmosphere! 
 
 Featuring: 
-" The Atmosphere ," who can equip an enemy  monster  and gains its  ATK / DEF . 
-" Summoned Skull ," a  Level  6 monster with an exceptional 2500 ATK. 
-" Different Dimension Gate ," a  Spell Card  that temporarily  removes  a monster from each player's  field . 
-" Rising Energy ," a  Trap Card  that can greatly increase a monster's ATK. 
-A total of 40 types of Cards: 2  UR , 8  SR , 14  R , and 16  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Wonders of the Sky  contains 80 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 5 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 10 copies
