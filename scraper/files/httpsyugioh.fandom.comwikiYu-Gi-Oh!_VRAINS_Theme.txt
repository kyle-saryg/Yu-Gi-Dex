" Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS Theme " is the English opening theme to the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime series. It is written by Elik Alvarez and Freddy Sheinfeld and debuted on March 11, 2018 as a preview alongside the theatrical re-release of  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie: Pyramid of Light . It is the first opening theme since " Yu-Gi-Oh! Theme " from  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  not to have lyrics in it.
 
 Visual 
 The opening uses clips both from  With The Wind  and  Believe In Magic  for season 1. For season 2 it uses clips from  With the Wind  and  Go forward . For season 3 it uses clips from  calling .
