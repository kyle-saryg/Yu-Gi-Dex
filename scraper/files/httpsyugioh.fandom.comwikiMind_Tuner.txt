 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  activate  1 of these  effects . ●  Banish  1  LIGHT  and/or 1  DARK   monster  from your  hand  and/or  GY ; increase or decrease this card's  Level  by the number banished, until the end of this  turn . ●  Target  up to 2 of your banished monsters (1 LIGHT and 1 DARK);  return  them to the GY, and if you do, increase or decrease this card's Level by the number returned, until the end of this turn. You can only use this effect of "Mind Tuner"  once per turn .
