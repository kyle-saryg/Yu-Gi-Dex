 Professor Yoshimori  ( 吉森 教授   Yoshimori Kyōju ) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . He is the anime's incarnation of  Professor Yoshimori  from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga . Another character in the series, credited as " professor-like man ", is also based on Yoshimori from the manga, in terms of appearance.
 Yoshimori is a  Domino University  professor who is into archaeology. He is friends with  Sugoroku Mutou .
