 Nistro , known as  Gauche  (ゴーシュ  Gōshu ) in the Japanese version,is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Nistro , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
