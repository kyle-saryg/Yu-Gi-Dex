 This set includes cards from the  OCG   Duel Terminal - Judgment of Omega!!  and the  OCG  Duel Terminal - Xyz Startup!! . It is the sixth series of Hidden Arsenal, following  Hidden Arsenal 5: Steelswarm Invasion . It is followed by  Hidden Arsenal 7: Knight of Stars .
 
 Contents 
 
 1   Features 
 2   Breakdown 
 3   Galleries 
 4   Lists 
 
 
 Features 
 Introduces the new Xyz Monsters and their game mechanic as well as further support for " Vylon ", " Laval ", " Gem-Knight ", " Steelswarm ", " Gishki " and " Gusto " cards.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  TCG  pack contains 5 foil cards: 1  Secret Rare  and 4  Super Rares  and each box contained 24 packs.
 This set contains 60 cards, including:
