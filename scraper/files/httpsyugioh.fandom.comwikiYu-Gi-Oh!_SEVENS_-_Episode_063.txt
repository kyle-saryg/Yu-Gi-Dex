 " The Sixth Man " is the sixty-third episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on August 29, 2021.
 Roa , while trying to figure out what happened with  Luke , goes to  Goha  with  Mimi  and  Swirly , based on some information from Mimi. Managing to slip inside the corporate headquarters somehow, Roa plans to get inside the president's office, but standing before him is the "The Last of the Six Goha Siblings" who serve as the company's presidents!
 
 Featured Duel: Tatsuhisa Kamijo VS. Roa Kirishima 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
