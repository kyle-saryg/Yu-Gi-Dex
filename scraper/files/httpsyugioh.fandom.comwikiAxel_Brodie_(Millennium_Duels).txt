 Axel Brodie , known as  Austin O'Brien  (オースチン・オブライエン,  Ōsuchin Oburaien ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Axel Brodie , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
