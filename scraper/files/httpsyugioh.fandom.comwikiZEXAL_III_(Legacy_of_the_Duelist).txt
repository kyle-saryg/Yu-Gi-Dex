 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 ZEXAL 3 , known as  ZEXAL III , (ゼアル  Ⅲ   Zearu Sādo ) in the Japanese version, is the third and strongest version of  ZEXAL (power)  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  ZEXAL III , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
