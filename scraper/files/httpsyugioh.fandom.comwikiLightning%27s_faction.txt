 Lighting's faction  is a group in  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  who aim to achieve complete dominion over Humanity, putting them under the  Ignis’  rule. It is led by the Light Ignis,  Lightning  and is opposed by both  Playmaker's   group  and the  Knights of Hanoi . They serve as the main antagonists of the second half of Season 2.
 All members of this faction ended up being erased or absorbed, leaving only  Bohman . Bohman is then erased after his losing his final duel against Playmaker which ended the faction.
