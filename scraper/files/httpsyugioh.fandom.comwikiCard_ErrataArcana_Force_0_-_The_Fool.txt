 First erratum 
 
 
 This card cannot be destroyed by battle. This card cannot be changed to Defense Position, except with a card effect. When this card is Summoned , toss  a coin :  ● Heads: Negate  the effects of  your  Spells, Traps and Effect Monsters'  effects that target this card, and destroy them. ● Tails: Negate  the effects of  your opponent's  Spells, Traps and Effect Monsters'  effects that target this card, and destroy them. 
 This card cannot be destroyed by battle. This card cannot be changed to Defense Position, except with a card effect.
