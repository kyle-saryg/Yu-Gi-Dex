 Yu-Gi-Oh! Character Guidebook: Millennium Book  is a guidebook written by  Kazuki Takahashi , profiling characters from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 It is preceded by  Yu-Gi-Oh! Character Guidebook: The Gospel of Truth .
