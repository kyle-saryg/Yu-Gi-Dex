 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Red "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「レッド」 .   As its English name contains "Red", it  may  be treated a member of the "Red" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Condition 
 When a  Trap   effect  that would inflict  damage  to a player(s) is  activated : That damage becomes 0, and if it does, 1  Fairy - Type   monster  on the  field  gains  ATK  equal to the total amount of damage that player(s) would have taken. During the  End Phase , you take damage equal to the total amount of damage that player(s) would have taken at the  activation  of this card.
