 Biography 
 Honda (Tristan) first mentions he has a dog, while his friends are playing with their  Digital Pets . [1] 
 Blankey makes her first appearance when Honda is taking her for a walk outside the  Black Clown . She begins barking just before Honda spots  Bakura  entering the shop. Honda then brings Blankey with him to go meet his friends, instructing her not to chew on  Jonouchi's  shoes. [2] 
 
 Non-canon appearances 
 The Sacred Cards 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards , Blankey can be seen with Tristan outside the player's house.
 
 Reshef of Destruction 
       Blankey in  Reshef of Destruction .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction , Lucky can be seen with Tristan,  Duke , and  Serenity  outside the  Kame Game  shop. After the player has done a certain side quest involving Tristan and Duke asking who Serenity likes, a new chapter appears. Lucky arrives after the player receives the  Millennium Scale  and shows it to  Ishizu . Lucky will start barking, prompting Joey to think something is wrong and Ishizu will ask them to check it out. When the player arrives at Kame Game, Serenity and Duke will explain that a  Monkey Robot  came out and that Lucky was talking to it. Duke didn't believe that there was a another person close to Lucky and this causes Duke to realize that the only person who was close to Lucky was Tristan. They explain that Tristan has been turn into a Monkey Robot possibly due to  Reshef's  resurrection.
