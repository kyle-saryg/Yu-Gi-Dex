 Appears in (anime)
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 
 
 Stardust Road  is the name of an oceanside locale located beyond  Den City  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS . It is noted to be a famous spot. Very rarely, luminescent plankton gather and cause the ocean to glow. [1] 
 Varis' house  is located at the top of the cliff, overseeing the ocean.

 Trivia 
 The name of this ocean may be a reference to the " Starlight Road " card from  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
