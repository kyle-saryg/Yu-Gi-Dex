 Yu-Gi-Oh! Double Pack promotional cards 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  Yu-Gi-Oh! Double Pack  promotional cards  are a set of 2 cards included with the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Double Pack  video game bundle, which includes the games  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Sacred Cards  on a single Game Boy Advance Game Pak.
 In North American English, the cards come from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Reshef of Destruction  promotional cards  (even sharing the same set ID), but with different rarities; they are  Super Parallel Rare  whereas the  Reshef of Destruction  cards are  Secret Rare . The cards were used in the  anime  by  Yugi Muto .
