 The Dark Emperor Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the twelfth deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Rise of the Dragon Lords Structure Deck . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Advent of the Emperor  in the  OCG .
