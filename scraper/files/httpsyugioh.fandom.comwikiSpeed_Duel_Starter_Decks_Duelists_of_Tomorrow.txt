 Speed Duel Starter Decks: Duelists of Tomorrow  is a set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). Released alongside  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Destiny Masters , they introduce the  Speed Duels  and  Skill Cards  to the  TCG .

 Breakdown 
 Each  Speed Duel Starter Decks: Duelists of Tomorrow  contains:
 
 1 20-Card  Seto Kaiba  Deck 
 1 20-Card  Mai Valentine  Deck 
 1 23-Card  Joey Wheeler  Deck 
 9  Skill Cards  (3 for Kaiba, 3 for Mai and 3 for Joey) 
 3  Ultra Rare   variant cards
