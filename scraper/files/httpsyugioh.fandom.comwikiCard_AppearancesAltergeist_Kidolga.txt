 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 31 ,  Ghost Gal  uses this card during her  Speed Duel  against  Varis . She  Link Summons  this card by linking " Altergeist Kunquery " with " Altergeist Silquitous ". She then links this card with " Altergeist Marionetter " to Link Summon " Altergeist Primebanshee ", before activating " Altergeist Manifestation " to revive this card. When "Primebanshee" attacks Varis directly, Ghost Gal activates this card's effect, Special Summoning Varis' " Twin Triangle Dragon " to her side of the field, but it cannot attack unless this card has already attacked. This card then attacks Varis directly. But when "Twin Triangle Dragon" attacks Varis, he activates his (then unseen) " Mirror Force " to destroy it, this card and "Primebanshee".
