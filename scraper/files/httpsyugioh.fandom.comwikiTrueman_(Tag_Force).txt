 Trueman , also known as  Mr. T , is a character in the  Tag Force  games. This is a video game version of the anime character,  Trueman .
 
 Appearances 
 In the third events of Season 4  Jaden 's and Normal  Bastion 's stories, two copies of Trueman Tag Duel against the player and their partner.
 
 
 Decks 
 Trueman uses  DARK  Decks  featuring a wide variety of monsters, as well as dedicated  " Dark World " Decks .
