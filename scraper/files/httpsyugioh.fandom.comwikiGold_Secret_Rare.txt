 Gold Secret Rare  (ゴールドシークレットレア  Gōrudo  Shīkuretto Rea), or simply  Gold Secret.  is a variant of  Gold Rare  that debuted in the  OCG  in  Gold Series 2013  and in the  TCG  in  Premium Gold . As its name implies, it features the same gold foil patterns as Gold Rare over the card border, card name, artwork, artwork frame,  Level / Rank  stars (for  monsters ), and text box border, but also features the  Secret Rare  texture pattern wherever this foil appears.
 
 Example 
 
 Trivia 
 Ghost/Gold Rare  is another example of a variant of Gold Rare that combines elements from Gold Rare with another high rarity.
