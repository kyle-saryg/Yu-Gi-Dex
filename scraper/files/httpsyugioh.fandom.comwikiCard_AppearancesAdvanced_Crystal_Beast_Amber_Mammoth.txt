 Anime 
 
 In  episode 147 ,  Jesse Anderson  uses this card during his Duel against  Zane Truesdale  while the former was possessed by  Yubel . After " Cyber Twin Dragon " attacks and destroys " Advanced Crystal Beast Amethyst Cat ", Jesse activates the second effect of " Advanced Dark " to nullify the Battle Damage he would take by sending this card from his Deck to the Graveyard. In  the next episode , this monster is shown when Jesse counts up the number of Crystal Beasts on his field and in his Graveyard. 
 In  episode 151 , Jesse uses this card during his duel against  Jaden Yuki  while the former was possessed by Yubel. After " Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus " is returned to Jesse's hand via the effect of " Neo-Spacian Grand Mole ", Jesse activates the second effect of " Crystal Release " to take this card from his Deck and place it in his Spell & Trap Card Zone as a Continuous Spell Card. In  the next episode , Jaden activates the effect of " Elemental HERO Storm Neos " to destroy all Spell and Trap Cards on the field. This causes this card, "Advanced Crystal Beast Amethyst Cat", "Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus", and " Advanced Crystal Beast Topaz Tiger " to be destroyed since " Advanced Dark " was no longer on the field.
