 In  episode 145 ,  Astral  uses this card during his Duel against  Yuma Tsukumo . When " Number 39: Utopia " attacks " Gagaga Cowboy ", Yuma activates " Half Unbreak " to prevent his monster's destruction and halve the damage he takes, but Astral activates " Absolute Buster " to negate the effect of "Half Unbreak" that prevents a monster's destruction. "Utopia" then destroys "Gagaga Cowboy".
