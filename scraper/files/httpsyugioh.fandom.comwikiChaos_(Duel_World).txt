Chaos  is the sixth and last of the Duel Worlds in  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 .
 The player will see two parts of the world, above and beneath.
 
 Above 
       Chaos (above)     
 Several Duel spirits await the player, as usual.
 
 Evil Hero Malicious Edge  (unlocks  Evil Hero Infernal Gainer ) 
 Destiny Hero - Plasma  (unlocks  Destiny Hero - Disk Commander ) 
 Elemental Hero Neos Alius  (unlocks  Neo Space Pathfinder ) 
 
 Beneath 
       Chaos (beneath)     
 Several Duel spirits await the player, as usual.
 
 Cyber End Dragon  (unlocks  Cyberdark Dragon ) 
 Green Gadget  (unlocks  Ancient Gear Gadjiltron Dragon ) 
 Horus the Black Flame Dragon LV8  (unlocks  Sacred Phoenix of Nephthys ) 
 
 Stone Monument 
       Above Stone Monument     
 If the player beats all Duel spirits in the above area at least five times each, a stone monument will appear. The stone monument has a carving of " Elemental Hero Chaos Neos ". In order to unseal it, the player need to Summon "Elemental Hero Chaos Neos" and win the Duel. If they do,  Jaden Yuki  will be unlocked in World Championship Mode. 
 
         Beneath Stone Monument     
 If the player beats all Duel spirits in the beneath area at least five times each, a stone monument will appear. The stone monument has a carving of " Armityle the Chaos Phantom ". In order to unseal it, the player need to Summon "Armityle the Chaos Phantom" and win the Duel. If they do,  Yubel  will be unlocked in World Championship Mode. 
 If the player clears six stone monuments (across all Duel Worlds),  Joey Wheeler  will be unlocked in World Championship Mode. If the player clears all seven stone monuments (across all Duel Worlds),  Yami Yugi  will be unlocked in World Championship Mode.
