 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Printed Effect Cannot be  Special Summoned . Requires 1  Tribute  to  Normal Summon . Up to twice per turn: You can  target  1  monster  on the  field  or in either  Graveyard ;  equip  that target to this card. This card gains  ATK  and  DEF  equal to the combined ATK and DEF of the monsters equipped to it by this effect. -I  Summon  " Relinquished "!- Hidden Effect This card cannot be  destroyed by battle , also when you take  battle damage  from attacks involving this card, inflict damage to your opponent, equal to the damage you took.
