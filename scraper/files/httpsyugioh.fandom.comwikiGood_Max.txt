 Good Max  (グッドマックス,  Guddomakkusu ) is a web series in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime.
 
 Overview 
       The hero behind the mask     
 In a post-apocalyptic world, the hero decided to transform himself to protect his sick mother.
 Yoshio Atachi  is a great fan of the show.
 
 Trivia 
 The show itself is a reference to  Fist of the North Star .
