 Previously Official Rulings 
   These TCG rulings were issued by  Upper Deck Entertainment  and have since been deemed unofficial by Konami. [1]  They were previously considered official and can still be correct, as long as they follow the official gameplay rules, or unless Konami has issued a ruling that says otherwise. References:  [2] [3] [4] [5] [6] [7] [8] [9] [10] 
 
 The effect of “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ]  that places an A-Counter onto itself whenever an A-Counter is removed by a card effect does not use the  Chain . 
 The effect of “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ]  that distributes the A-Counters on “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ]  and places them onto monsters when it is destroyed begins a  Chain . 
 If an A-Counter on “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ]  is removed by an effect, an A-Counter is still placed upon “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ] . 
 The effect of “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ]  is not applied when A-Counters are removed because the card with the A-Counters was removed from the field or  flipped  face-down, because these A-Counters are not removed by an effect. 
 If an A-Counter is removed to pay a  cost , an A-Counter is placed upon “ ’A’ Cell Incubator ”  [ sic ] .
