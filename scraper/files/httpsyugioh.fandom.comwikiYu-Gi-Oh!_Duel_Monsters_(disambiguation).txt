Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  may refer to:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! , the NAS anime series, called  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  in Asia 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , a series of video games
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , the first video game in the  Duel Monsters  series 
 Konami's Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters , a series of promotional cards not associated with the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  or  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game 
 Duel Monsters , a card game played in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga and anime series. 
 Monster Card , a card type in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game
