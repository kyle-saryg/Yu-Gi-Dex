 Greek - Μεγάλο Έντομο 
'Ενα γιγάντιο μυρμήγκι που κατοικεί στη ζούγκλα και είναι δυνατό είτε επιτίθεται είτε αμύνεται.
 
 
 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was  "A giant ant that dwells in the jungle, it is powerful whether attacking or defending." 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Cette fourmi géante qui vit dans la jungle est puissante tant en position offensive qu'en position défensive." 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Eine gigantische Ameise, die sowohol in der Verteidigung als auch im Angriff sehr gefährlich ist." 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Una formica gigante che vive nella giungla e che dimostra eccellenti capacità di attacco e di difesa." 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Una hormiga gigante que vive en la jungla, es poderosa atacando y defendiendo."
