 Koki Uchiyama  is a Japanese actor and voice actor, affiliated with Gekidan Himawari.
 He provides the voice of Kaito Tenjo ( Kite Tenjo ) in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . He reprises the role of Kaito in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , voicing an  alternate universe version  of the character.
