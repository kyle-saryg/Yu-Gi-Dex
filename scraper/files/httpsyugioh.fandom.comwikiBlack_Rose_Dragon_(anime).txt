 Notes 
 
 ↑   Despite having the lore of its  OCG / TCG  counterpart , this card was used with a different effect in its first appearances. It could activate its effect when Special Summoned rather than when Synchro Summoned, and it could target monsters in either position to reduce their ATK to 0.
