 [  Dragon  /  Toon  ] This card cannot be  summoned  unless " Toon World " is on the  field . This card cannot  attack  in the same  turn  that it is summoned.  Pay  500  Life Points  each time this  monster  attacks. When "Toon World" is  destroyed , this card is also destroyed. If your opponent doesn't  control  a  Toon monster  on the field, this card may inflict  Direct Damage  to your opponent's Life Points. If a Toon monster is on your opponent's side of the field, your attacks must  target  the Toon monster.
