 Original 
 First erratum 
 
 
 If you have an "Aquaactress" monster in your  Graveyard : Target 1 monster on the field; until the end of this turn, its effects are negated, its ATK  and  DEF become 100, also it is unaffected by your opponent's card effects. 
 If you have an "Aquaactress" monster in your  GY : Target 1 monster on the field; until the end of this turn, its effects are negated, its ATK / DEF become 100, also it is unaffected by your opponent's card effects.
