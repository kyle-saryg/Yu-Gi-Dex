Main card page: " Shining Friendship " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Shining "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「シャイニング」 .   As its English name contains "Shining", it  may  be treated a member of the "Shining" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
