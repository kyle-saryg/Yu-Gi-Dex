 World Premiere Pack 2020  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -only  cards to the  OCG .
 
 Contents 
 
 1   Features 
 2   Breakdown 
 3   Galleries 
 4   Lists 
 5   External links 
 
 
 Features 
 Introduces to the  OCG  previously  TCG -only cards from  Rising Rampage ,  Chaos Impact ,  Ignition Assault ,  Eternity Code  and other products. 
 Introduces the " Dream Mirror " and " Plunder Patroll " archetypes to the  OCG . 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack and 15 packs per box. The set contains 79 cards:
 
 2  Extra Secret Rares 
 1  Prismatic Secret Rare 
 7  Ultra Rares 
 9  Super Rares 
 15  Rares 
 45  Commons 
 The Super Rare and Ultra Rare cards, and 1 of the Rare cards, are also available as  Secret Rare .
