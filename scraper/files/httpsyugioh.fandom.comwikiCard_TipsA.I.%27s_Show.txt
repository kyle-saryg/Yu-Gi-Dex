 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament " and " Pikari @Ignister ". 
 Considering the monster you are required to control in order to activate this card, you will be able to summon at least one monster via this card unless the attacking monster manipulates stats during  damage calculation , or its effect activates before damage calculation.
