 Synchron Extreme Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the thirtieth Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  HERO Strike Structure Deck . This deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Synchron Extreme  in the  OCG . Synchro Summoning is at the forefront of the new  Synchron Extreme Structure Deck ! Inspired by Yusei Fudo’s Deck from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D’s  anime and manga series, the  Synchron Extreme Structure Deck  contains a whopping 10 brand-new cards, including 3 brand-new Synchro Monsters! 
 Synchro Summoning depends on swarming the playing field with lower level Monsters like  Speed Warrior  and  Junk Synchron , letting you easily Synchro Summon a more powerful Monster to the field. In the  Synchron Extreme Structure Deck , brand new Monsters like  Jet Synchron  and  Rush Warrior  are introduced, letting you more easily Summon Synchro Monsters like  Stardust Warrior , a behemoth with 3000 ATK and the power to prevent opposing Special Summons or Special Summon your other Synchro Monsters! The  Synchron Extreme Structure Deck  also includes 2 Ultra Rare Cards and 3 new Super Rare cards!
 This 44-card Deck includes cards that meet the needs of players new and old, including a record-setting 10 new cards for a Structure Deck! This set will also have excellent synergy with  Legendary Collection 5D's .
