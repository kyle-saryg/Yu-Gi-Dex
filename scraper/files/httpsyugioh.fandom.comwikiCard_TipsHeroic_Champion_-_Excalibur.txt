 Summoning 
 Normal Summon  " Goblindbergh " and use its effect to  Special Summon  a  Level  4  Warrior  monster from your  hand . 
 While there are no monsters on your field, Special Summon " Photon Thrasher ", then Normal Summon a Level 4 Warrior monster from your hand. 
 Normal Summon a Level 4 Warrior monster, then Special Summon " Doggy Diver " or " Blue Mountain Butterspy " from your hand. 
 In a " Heroic " deck, " Heroic Challenger - Double Lance " can Special Summon another copy of itself, but only from the hand or Graveyard. Otherwise, Special Summon " Heroic Challenger - Assault Halberd " while only your opponent controls a monster. 
 The majority of Warrior archetypes have members capable of Special Summoning themselves, or Special Summoning monsters upon Normal Summon. These include " HERO ", " Six Samurai ", " Noble Knight ", " tellarknight ", " Ninja " and " Battlin' Boxer ". 
 Using this card 
 You can use " Rank-Up-Magic Astral Force " on this card to  Xyz Summon  " Number 39: Utopia Beyond " or " Photon Strike Bounzer ". 
 Using " Heroic Chance " after using this card's effect allows this card's ATK to reach 8000 until your next turn. Add an effect to inflict  piercing  if necessary. 
 Equip " Twin Swords of Flashing Light - Tryce " and " Shooting Star Bow - Ceal " to this card, then use its effect for a 4000 ATK monster that can attack directly twice. (This card's effect would ignore any existing stat modifiers until your next turn.) 
 " Magnum Shield " can allow this card to retain 4000 ATK even after its effect has been applied. 
 " Xyz Override " can allow this card to apply its effect a second time.
