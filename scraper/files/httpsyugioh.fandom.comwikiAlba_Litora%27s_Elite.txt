 Alba Litora's Elite  is a  Booster Pack  in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM .
 It is available from February 13, 2013. Each pack costs   20.
 This collection includes Reptile, Aqua and Pyro Monsters, as well as the Christmas 2012 and end-of-series Boss cards.
