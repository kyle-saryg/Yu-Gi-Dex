 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards. The set contains 45 cards:
 
 3  Ultra Rares  /  Secret Rares 
 12  Super Rares 
 30  Commons  /  Normal Parallel Rares 
 Features 
 Includes members/support for the " Vampire " archetype. 
 Introduces the " Fur Hire ", " Sky Striker " archetypes to the OCG.
