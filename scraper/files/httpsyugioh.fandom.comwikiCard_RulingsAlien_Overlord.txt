 TCG Rulings 
 This card can be  Normal Summoned . [1] 
 OCG Rulings 
 This monster can be  Normal Summoned  and  Set . [2] 
 When  Special Summoning  " Alien Overlord ", if you remove all the  A-Counters  from a monster under the control of " Alien Hypno ", then the monster is destroyed as soon as the  A-Counters  are removed.  After the monster is destroyed, " Alien Overlord " is Special Summoned. [3]
