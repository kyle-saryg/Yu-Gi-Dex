 Blazing Rose  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on May 20, 2019.
 
 Description 
 A cold flame envelops the world! Black flower bloom! Come forth, " Black Rose Dragon "! 
 
 Featuring: 
 Black Rose Dragon 
 Akiza Izinski 's ace monster is finally here! When this monster is Synchro Summoned, it will destroy every card on the field! No matter how many monsters and Spell/Trap Cards your opponent, you can wipe it all clean with this monster! 
 Black Metal Dragon 
This monster can be equipped from your hand or field to a " Red-Eyes " monster you control. In addition, if this card is sent from the field to the Graveyard as a monster or a Equip Card, you can add 1 "Red-Eyes" card from your Deck to your hand. Add cards such as " Red-Eyes Baby Dragon " and " Red-Eyes Spirit ." The former is available in this BOX, while the latter can be obtained with  Card Tickets . 
 Keeper of the Shrine 
This monster can count as 2 Tributes when Tribute Summoning a  Dragon -Type monster. When a monster other than "Keeper of the Shrine" is destroyed by battle or card effect and sent to the Graveyard, you can Special Summon this monster from your Graveyard or hand! In addition, if the monster that was sent to the Graveyard was a Normal Monster, you can add 1 Normal Dragon-Type monster from your Graveyard to your Deck. Put this monster in Decks that utilize monsters such as " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " and " Red-Eyes B. Dragon "! 
 Metaphys Daedalus 
When this monster is Special Summoned by the effect of a " Metaphys " monster, you can banish all other Special Summoned monsters on the field! Special Summon this monster using the effect of " Metaphys Tyrant Dragon ," which is also available in this BOX! 
A total of 90 types of Cards: 9  UR , 11  SR , 28  R , and 42  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Blazing Rose  contains 180 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards: 2 of them are individually guaranteed to be Normal (aka Common) or Rare, while the third card is guaranteed to be Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Krawler Ranvier ", " Subterror Behemoth Phospheroglacier ", " Subterror Behemoth Speleogeist ", " Guardragon Promineses ", and " Aloof Lupine ". 
 The following cards are reprints from previous packs: " Soul Absorption ", " Different Dimension Ground ", " Premature Return ", " 8-Claws Scorpion ", " Arcane Archer of the Forest ", " Great Spirit ", " Swarm of Crows ", and " Sinister Seeds ".
