 Clash of Wings  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on June 22,2018, 7:00 PM (EST). 
 
 Description 
 Glide above the sea of clouds and reach for the stars. 
 
 Featuring: 
" Alector, Sovereign of Birds ," A monster card with a convenient effect that allows you to negate the effect of a face-up card for a single turn. It can also be Special Summoned when there are 2 or more monsters with the same Attribute on your opponent's side of the field. 
" Widespread Ruin ," This Trap Card destroys the monster with the highest ATK on your opponent's side of the field. Combine this with a card that can change battle positions so you can choose which monster to destroy. 
" Beat, Bladesman Fur Hire ," This monster lets you Special Summon a monster " Fur Hire " from your hand, and lets you add a monster "Fur Hire" from your Deck to your hand when a monster "Fur Hire" is Special Summoned. It is a must have monster for "Fur Hire" Decks, which center around Special Summoning monster "Fur Hire"s from the hand. 
" Gatling Dragon ," This monster allows you to to flip 3 coins and destroy a monster for every time it lands on head, once per turn. However, if your opponent only has 1 or 2 monsters on the field, you run the risk of destroying your own monsters as well. 
A total of 40 types of Cards: 2  UR , 8  SR , 14  R , and 16  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Clash of Wings  contains 80 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 5 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 10 copies
