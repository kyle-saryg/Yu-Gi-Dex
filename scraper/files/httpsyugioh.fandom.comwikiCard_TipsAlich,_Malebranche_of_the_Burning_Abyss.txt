 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Sphere of Chaos ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Sangan ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Chaos Zone ", " Scarm, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss ", " Tour Guide From the Underworld ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Graff, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss ", " Draghig, Malebranche of the Burning Abyss " and " Good & Evil in the Burning Abyss ". 
 This card's effect to negate the effect of a monster on the field is  Spell Speed 1  - its most effective use during your opponent's turn is to disrupt the  Ignition Effect  of a monster your opponent just summoned. You can also use this effect during your turn to negate an opposing  Continuous Effect  (such as  battle immunity  or summon/activation floodgates such as a " Barrier Statue " monster). 
 This card can be recycled with " Recurring Nightmare " and " Dark Eruption ".
