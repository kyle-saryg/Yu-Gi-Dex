   English 
 
 
1  Tuner  + 1 or more non-Tuner  monsters Once per turn , during your  Main Phase 1 : You can  destroy  all other  face-up   Attack Position  monsters on the  field . Other monsters cannot  attack  the  turn  you  activate  this  effect .
