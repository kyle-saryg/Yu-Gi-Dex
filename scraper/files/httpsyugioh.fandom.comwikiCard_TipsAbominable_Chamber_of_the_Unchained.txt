 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " Trap Trick ", " Lady Labrynth of the Silver Castle ", " Lilith, Lady of Lament ", " Angel with Blue Tears ", " Farewelcome Labrynth ", " Labrynth Set-Up " and " Abomination's Prison ". 
 This card's activated effect is best used to revive " Unchained Soul of Rage " during your opponent's  Main Phase  so that its effect can be used to  Link Summon  using your opponent's Special Summoned monster.
