 Alien Shocktrooper , known as  Alien Soldier  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card, " Alien Shocktrooper ".

 Appearances 
 Alien Shocktrooper appears in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . It appears as a Tag Duel opponent and it Tag partner is  Alien Infiltrator . 
 
 Unlockables 
 They can be unlocked if the player has beaten them in World four Darkness. Their team name is  A-Type Gene .
