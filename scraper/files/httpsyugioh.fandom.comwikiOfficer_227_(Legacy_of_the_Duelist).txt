 Officer 227 , known as  Duel Chaser 227  (デュエルチェイサー２２７  Deyuerucheisā 227 ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Officer 227 , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
