 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Watch Dog " and " The Despair Uranus ". 
 This card can help ease the summoning of Level 7 or higher monsters that  cannot be Special Summoned , like  Spirit monsters , " Infernal Flame Emperor ", " Gilford the Legend ", " Simorgh, Bird of Divinity " and " Diabolos, King of the Abyss ". 
 Use this card with Level 5 or higher monsters that can Special Summon themselves, like " Cyber Dragon ", " Oracle of the Sun ", " Vice Dragon ", " Quickdraw Synchron ", " The Tricky ", " Apprentice Illusion Magician " or " Interplanetarypurplythorny Dragon ". 
 You can tribute a  Level 5 Fusion monster  summoned with " Instant Fusion ", or a Level 5 or higher Ritual Monster summoned with " Ritual Foregone ". 
 You can tribute " Metal Reflect Slime ", whose effect summons itself to the field as a Level 10 monster. 
 Increase a Level 4 monster to Level 5 with " Noisy Gnat ", " Delta Flyer ", " Star Changer " or " Synchro Boost ".
 Psychic - Type  monsters can also increase their level with " Psi-Station ". 
 " Fortune Lady Water ", " Risebell the Star Psycher ", " Gash the Dust Lord ", " Galaxy Wizard ", " Cupid Dunk " and " Hundred Dragon " are all Level 4 monsters that can raise their own Level to 5 or higher.
