 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If this card is  Normal  or  Flip Summoned , its  original ATK and DEF  are halved until the  End Phase  of your next  turn . After that, the original ATK and DEF are doubled. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Si cette carte est Invoquée Normalement ou par Flip, son ATK et DEF d'origine sont divisées par deux jusqu'à la End Phase de votre prochain tour. Après ça, son ATK et sa DEF d'origine sont doublées. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Falls diese Karte als Normal- oder Flippbeschwörung beschworen wird, werden seine Grund-ATK und -DEF bis zur End Phase deines nächsten Spielzugs halbiert. Danach werden seine Grund-ATK und -DEF verdoppelt. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Se questa carta viene Evocata Normalmente o per Scoperta, i suoi ATK e DEF originali vengono dimezzati fino alla End Phase del tuo prossimo turno. Dopo di che, i suoi ATK e DEF originali vengono raddoppiati.

   Spanish

   Japanese 
 
 
 このカードが召喚・反転召喚に成功した時、このカードの元々の攻撃力・守備力は次の自分のターンのエンドフェイズ時まで半分になる。その後、このカードの元々の攻撃力・守備力は倍になる。 
 
 
 
 
 
   Chinese 
 
 
 召喚·反轉召喚的場合，直到下次自己的回合的結束階段這張卡的原來的攻擊力·守備力減半，之後這張卡的原來的攻擊力·守備力變成2倍。 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
For 3 turns (including your opponent's) following the Summon of this card (including Flip Summon), the ATK and DEF of this monster is halved. After that, both ATK and DEF are doubled for the remainder of the Duel. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
A kindly fairy that is in the form of an hourglass with wings. It gives one courage when one is feeling down.
