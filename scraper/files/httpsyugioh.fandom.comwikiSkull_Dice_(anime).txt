Main card page: " Skull Dice " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Dice "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ダイス」 .   As its English name contains "Dice", it  may  be treated a member of the "Dice" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
