 Marauding Captain , known as  Troop Commander  in the Japanese version, is a character version of the card " Marauding Captain ".
 In the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime, he appears as one of  Duel Monster Spirits  who protected a village in  Duel Monsters Spirit World  from  The Supreme King's Army . However, like the rest of his comrades he was defeated by the enemy leader in a Duel and died as a result. [1] 
 He appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . He and  Breaker the Magical Warrior  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents, by listening to the Tag Duel explanation in Story Mode.

 Marauding Captain uses a Deck titled, "Backup Wanted".
