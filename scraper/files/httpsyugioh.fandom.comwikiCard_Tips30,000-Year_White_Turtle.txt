 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Broken Blocker ", " Sangan ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Summoner's Art ", " Dragoons of Draconia ", " Chaos-End Master ", " Mother Grizzly " and " Princess Cologne ". 
 " Ancient Rules " can Special Summon this card from your hand.
 Due to its stats, you can follow up the Special Summon of this card with " Inferno Reckless Summon " to bring out two more copies of this card and go into a  Rank 5   Xyz Monster . If " Lemuria, the Forgotten City " is on the field, you can go into a  Rank 8  instead. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Last Will ". 
 Video games 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Forbidden Memories , this can be  Fusion Summoned  by combining " Time Wizard " and a turtle monster.
