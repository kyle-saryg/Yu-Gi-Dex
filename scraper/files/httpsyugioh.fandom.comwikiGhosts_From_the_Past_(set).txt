 Ghosts From the Past  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Akiza Izinski ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Breo ,  Chazz Princeton ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Jack Atlas , Kite Tenjo ( anime  and  manga ),  Sayer ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Shay Obsidian ,  Specter ,  Theodore Hamilton ,  Yugi Muto ,  Yusaku Fujiki ,  Yusei Fudo ,  Yugo  and  Yuto . 
 Includes new/existing members/support for the " Armed Dragon ", " Black Luster Soldier ", " Blue-Eyes ", " Dark Magician ", " Dragunity ", " Phantom Knights ", " Raidraptor ", " Shaddoll ", and " Xyz Dragon " archetypes. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Sunseed ", " Sunvine ", and " Sunavalon " archetypes to the  TCG . 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Ghosts From the Past  box contains 3 packs with 5 cards each. The set contains 132 cards:
 
 5  Ghost Rares 
 127  Ultra Rares
