 Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Spirit of the Fall Wind ", " Subterror Behemoth Apparition ", " Transmodify ", " Sangan ", “ Witch of the Black Forest ”, " Mimiclay ", " Obedience Schooled ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Dragunity Corsesca ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Chaos Zone ", " Ninjitsu Art of Transformation ", " Baby Raccoon Ponpoko ", " Baby Raccoon Tantan ", " Super-Nimble Mega Hamster ", " Rescue Cat " and " Key Mouse ". 
 If this card's effect is activated on your turn without " Necrovalley " on the field, " Archfiend's Oath " can add the searched  Trap Card  to your hand. 
 Note that this card  can  put a face-up " Pharaoh's Treasure " on top of your Deck. By doing so on your opponent's turn via this card, you draw "Pharaoh's Treasure" on your next turn and can use its effect. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Last Will ".
