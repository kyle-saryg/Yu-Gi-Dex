 Afro  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . He was a contestant in the  D1 Grand Prix .

 Biography 
 Afro was among the other  D1 Grand Prix  contestants during the opening of the event. He was surprised when  Jack  patronizes the contestants, saying the Grand Prix seems to just be a consolation tournament. [1] 
 Afro's first Duel is against  Kalin Kessler , which he loses with a  direct attack  from Kalin's " Infernity General ". He was unable to inflict any damage to Kalin during the Duel. [2]

 No cards in Afro's Deck were shown.
