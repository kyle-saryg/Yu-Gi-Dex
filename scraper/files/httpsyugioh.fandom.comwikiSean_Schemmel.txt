 Sean Schemmel  is an American voice actor, ADR director, and script writer who has worked for NYAV Post,  Bandai Entertainment ,  Funimation Entertainment , OkraTron 5000, DuArt Film and Video, and  4Kids Entertainment . He has provided voices for a number of English language versions of Japanese anime films, television series, as well as video games. He is best known for voicing the adult version of  Goku , the main protagonist of the  Dragon Ball series  anime. In addition to his voice acting career, Schemmel has a full time position as an ADR director and script writer for NYAV Post, where he has directed and adapted for English several Japanese anime series.
 Schemmel also voices a variety of characters in the English dubbed version of the various  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 Schemmel's first major role in Yu-Gi-Oh! was portraying  Bobasa  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Schemmel's most notable roles in Yugioh GX are   Dr. Crowler ,  Ojama Yellow ,  and  Elemental Hero Neos . He also potrays   Neo-Spacian Dark Panther ,  Mr. Phoenix ,  Mathmatica ,  Mad Dog ,  Doctor Collector ,  Thelonious Viper , Crystal Beast Cobalt Eagle,  Trapper ,  Elroy Prescot ,  Sky Scout ,  Guardian Baou  and  Chaos Sorcerer  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Schemmel portrays  Greiger ,  the MC ,  Zigzix ,  Rudolph Heitmann  and  Hans  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time 
 Schemmel portrays  Paradox  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Schemmel's most notable role in Yu-Gi-Oh! history is portraying  Bronk Stone  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . He also portrays  Orbital 7 ,  Kaze ,  Tetsumi Stone  (Bronk's mother), and  Kazuma Tsukumo  in the same series.
