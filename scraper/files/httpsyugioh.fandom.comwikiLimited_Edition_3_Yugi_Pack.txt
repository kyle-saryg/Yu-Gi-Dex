 Limited Edition 3: Yugi Pack  is one of the three packs that make up  Limited Edition 3 . It was released alongside  Limited Edition 3: Joey Pack  and  Limited Edition 3: Kaiba Pack , all three of which were available by mail order through the  Weekly Shonen Jump  magazine.
 The set features  Yami Yugi  on the packet and contains three  Ultra Rare  cards, which were used by him in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
