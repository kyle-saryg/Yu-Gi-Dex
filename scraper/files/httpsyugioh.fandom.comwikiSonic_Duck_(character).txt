 Sonic Duck  is a character version of the card " Sonic Duck ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .
 This character and  Sonic Chick  can be unlocked as  Tag   Turbo Duel  opponents. Their Tag Team name is  Sonic Sprint .
