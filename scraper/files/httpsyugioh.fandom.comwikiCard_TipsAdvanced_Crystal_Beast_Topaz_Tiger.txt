 If you control three copies of this card, all your " Advanced Crystal Beast " monsters gain a 2400 ATK/DEF swing against your opponent's monsters. In other words, any one copy of this card would be able to destroy a monster by battle if said monster's original stat (depending on position) is below 4000 and it did not gain ATK/DEF through any other effect.
