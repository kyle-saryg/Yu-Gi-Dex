 Condition 
 Send  1  Level  4 or lower  monster  from your  hand  to the  Graveyard ;  Special Summon  1  Rank  4 or lower  Xyz Monster  from your Graveyard, and if you do,  detach   Xyz Materials   attached  to a monster your opponent  controls , equal to the Rank of the monster Special Summoned by this  effect . If you did not send any Xyz Material to the Graveyard by this effect, the  ATK  and  DEF  of the monster Special Summoned by this effect become 0, also its effects are  negated .
