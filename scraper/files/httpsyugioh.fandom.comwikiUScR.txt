An  Ultra Secret Rare  (ウルトラシークレットレア  Urutora Shīkuretto Rea ) card has the  Ultra Rare  foil over its image but has the sparkly silver foil name of a  Secret Rare  card.
 In the  OCG , this rarity is used by all cards in the  V Jump  August 1999 Special Present , the original  Limited Edition , the  Official Guide Starter Book  promotional card , and the  Duel Monsters II  tournament meeting experience promotional card .
 In the  TCG , the only card to be printed in this rarity is the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Special Edition  version of " Elemental Hero Wildheart ". A run of " Collectible Tins 2004 " were also misprinted as Ultra Secret Rare.
 On the Official Card Database, this rarity is labeled as Secret Rare.
