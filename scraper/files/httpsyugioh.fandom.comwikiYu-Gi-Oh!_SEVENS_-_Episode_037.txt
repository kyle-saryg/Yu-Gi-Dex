 " Coo-Cluke-Coo " is the thirty-seventh episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on February 20, 2021.
 Feeling responsible for leading his friends into  Chevelle 's "trumpet duet competition" trap,  Luke  challenges him to a Duel. However, Chevelle tells him that he'll be facing  Dove-rap  the pigeon instead.
 
 Featured Duel: Lucidien "Luke" Kallister vs. Dove-rap 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
