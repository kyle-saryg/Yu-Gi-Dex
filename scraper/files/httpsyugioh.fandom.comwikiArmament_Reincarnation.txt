 Armament Reincarnation 
 
 Japanese:   武装転生 
 Kana:   ぶそうてんせい 
 Romaji:   Busō Tensei 
 
 
 
 
 en 
 ja 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell 
 
 
 
 All  Equip Cards  in the  Graveyard  appear on the  field  as  monsters  with 500  ATK  and 500  DEF . The  effects  of those cards become the special abilities of the monsters. 
 
 Viz Media  lore<nomobile> ? :</nomobile> <mobile>(The English manga by Viz Media sometimes uses lores which are inconsistent with the Japanese lore or how the card worked.):</mobile> Take  Equip Spell Cards  and  Equip Trap Cards  from your  Graveyard , and  Special Summon  as many of them as possible as  Monster Cards  ( ATK  500/ DEF  500). Each of these cards will apply its equipment  effect  on itself if possible.
