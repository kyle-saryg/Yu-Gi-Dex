 Anime 
 
 This card can be searched by " Hieratic Dragon King of Atum ", " Hieratic Seal of the Heavenly Spheres ", " Starliege Seyfert ", " Ninjitsu Art of Super-Transformation ", " Draconnection ", " Masked Dragon ", " Dragonic Guard ", " Ahrima, the Wicked Warden ", " Dwarf Star Dragon Planeter ", " Magical Contract Door ", " Mystic Tomato ", " Chaos Zone ", " Chaos-End Master ", " Umbramirage the Elemental Lord " and " Sangan ". 
 Summon this card when you control " Rokket Synchron " or " Rokket Tracer " in order to go into a Level 8 or higher  Synchro Monster , and search another " Rokket " monster to add to your hand. 
 Summon this card when you control " Exploderokket Dragon " (ideally summoned by " Quick Launch ") in order to go into a  Rank 7   Xyz Monster . Detach this card to activate said monster's effect as well as search a "Rokket" monster. 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Guardragon Elpy " and " Eclipse Wyvern ".
