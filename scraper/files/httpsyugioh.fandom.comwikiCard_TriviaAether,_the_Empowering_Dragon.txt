 This monster is based on the  Yellow Dragon  which is sometimes counted as the fifth Guardian Beast of the  Four Symbols  of the  Chinese constellations , and it's named after the  Aether . 
 This monster appears in the artwork of " Golden Dragon Summoner ". 
 This is the very first monster to have an effect that  activates  when it is  Pendulum Summoned . 
 This monster has a DARK counterpart, " Aether, the Evil Empowering Dragon ".
