 Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 The given unofficial Arabic name ( مانعة الصواعق   māni‘at aṣ-ṣawā‘iq ) is the Arabic term for  Lightning Rod . 
 The unofficial Greek name given ( Αλεξικέραυνο ) translates to " Lightining Rod ".
