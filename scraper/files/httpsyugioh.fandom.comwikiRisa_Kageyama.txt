 Risa Kageyama  ( 影山  リサ  Kageyama Risa ) is a character in the  Toei  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime . She is the only one of the three  Kageyama sisters  whose name is mentioned on screen.

 Biography 
 Risa and her sisters are three witches who want to have the three " Hecate " cards. Risa Kageyama tries to take  Yugi Mutou 's " Violet Hecate " card, by pretending to be Yugi's friend. Yugi, with the  Millennium Puzzle  taken away from him, is forced into a  Duel Monsters  game with Risa and her sisters. Yugi is about to lose until  Kaiba  appears and takes over, defeating them.  Deck 
 Risa collectively uses a " Hecate " Deck, with her sisters.
