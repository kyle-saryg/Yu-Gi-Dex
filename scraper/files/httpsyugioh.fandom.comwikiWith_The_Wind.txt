 " With The Wind " ( ﻿Ｗｉｔｈ   Ｔｈｅ   Ｗｉｎｄ ,  Wizu Za Windo ) is the first Japanese opening theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS  anime, performed by  Hiroaki "Tommy" Tominaga . It was replaced by  Go forward  starting  episode 47 .

 Changes 
 Episode 13  -  Firewall Dragon's  animation is now done in a CG model instead of being hand drawn, while  Borreload Dragon  is no longer covered in shadow, and also uses a CG model instead of being hand drawn.
