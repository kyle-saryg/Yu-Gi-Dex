 Names 
 
 The unofficial  Greek  name given (" Μετά την Πάλη ") is an exact translation of the English name of this card. A possible translation of the Japanese name (" Genocide War ") could be " Πόλεμος Γενοκτονίας ". The alternate name of this card (" After Genocide ") can be translated in Greek as " Μετά την Γενοκτονία ".
