 Saruwatari  is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! the Movie . He is the  Toei series  incarnation of  Saruwatari , from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Biography 
       Saruwatari, attempting to kidnap  Shougo Aoyama     
 Saruwatari kidnapped unwilling invitees to  Seto Kaiba 's tournament.  Katsuya Jonouchi  stopped him from forcing  Shougo Aoyama  to enter. [1]
