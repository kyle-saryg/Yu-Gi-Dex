 Limited Edition 16 
 
 
 
 
 
 Limited Edition 15  was distributed by  V Jump  for the July issue of 2009. June 19, 2009 was the subscription deadline and May 21, 2009 was the release date. Its cost was 540 Yen (including tax). It contains cards that appeared in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  animes.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack contains 5 cards: 5  Ultra Rares .
