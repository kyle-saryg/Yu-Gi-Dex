The  Tin of Ancient Battles  is a  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game   collectible tin .

 Features 
 Includes cards from  Ignition Assault ,  Eternity Code ,  Rise of the Duelist ,  Phantom Rage ,  Secret Slayers ,  Genesis Impact ,  Structure Deck: Shaddoll Showdown , and  Structure Deck: Sacred Beasts , as well as 4 new cards: " Crossout Designator ", " True Light ", " Magician's Salvation ", and " Piercing the Darkness ".
 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Tin of Ancient Battles  contains three 18-card Mega Packs:
 
 1  Prismatic Secret Rare 
 2  Ultra Rares 
 2  Super Rares 
 1  Rare 
 12  Commons 
 The set contains 258 cards:
 
 30 Prismatic Secret Rares 
 50 Ultra Rares 
 36 Super Rares 
 20 Rares 
 122 Commons
