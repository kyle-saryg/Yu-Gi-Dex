 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Misty Tredwell , known as  Misty Lola  (ミスティ・ローラ,  Misuti Rora ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution .. This is a video game depiction of  Misty Tredwell , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
