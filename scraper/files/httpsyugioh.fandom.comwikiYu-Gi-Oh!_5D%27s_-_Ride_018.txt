 "The Handless Combo!!"  is the eighteenth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga .
 This chapter was first printed on January 21, 2011 in the 03/2011 issue of the  V Jump  magazine. It was later reprinted in  volume 3  of the  tankōbon .
 
 Summary 
 Akiza  keeps fighting hard against  Kalin 's "Handless Combo". However, she is put into a corner after Kalin defeat her ace, " Queen Angel of Roses ".
 
 Featured Duel: Akiza Izinski vs. Kalin Kassler 
 Duel continues from  the previous Ride . 
 Turn 4: Akiza 
Akiza  Normal Summons  " Rose Shaman " (1700/200) in  Attack Position . She  attacks  " Infernity Archer " with " Queen Angel of Roses ", but Kalin  activates  his  face-down  " Void Shield ",  negating  the attack. Akiza activates the effect of "Rose Shaman",  sending  it to the  Graveyard  to  draw  1 card. She  Sets  a card.
 Turn 5: Kalin 
Akiza activates her face-down " Briar Pin-Seal " to prevent Kalin from using or  discarding  the only card in his  hand .
 Turn 6: Akiza 
The  effect  of "Queen Angel of Roses"  destroys  "Infernity Archer". She Normal Summons " Rose Princess " (1200/800) in Attack Position. Then, she attacks Kalin  directly  with "Queen Angel of Roses" (Kalin: 3600 → 1200). Kalin activates his face-down " Void Chain ", destroy "Briar Pin-Seal" and  Special Summoning  " Infernity General " (2700/1500) in Attack Position from his hand.
 Turn 7: Kalin 
Kalin attacks and destroys Akiza's "Rose Princess" with "Infernity General" (Akiza: 2000 → 500).
 Duel continues in  the next Ride .
