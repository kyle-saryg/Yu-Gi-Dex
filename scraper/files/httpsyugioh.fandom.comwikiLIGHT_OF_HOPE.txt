 " LIGHT OF HOPE " (キボウノヒカリ,  KIBOU NO HIKARI ) is the fifth Japanese opening theme of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime, performed by  Unknown Number!!!  ( Shuto Miyazaki ,  Ryo Matsuda  and  Ryo Kitamura ). It debuted on April 3, 2016, in  episode 99  to  episode 124 . It was replaced by " Pendulum Beat! " in  episode 125 .
