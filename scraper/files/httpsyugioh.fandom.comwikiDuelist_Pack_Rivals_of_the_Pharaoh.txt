 Duelist Pack: Rivals of the Pharaoh  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the fifteenth set in the  TCG ' s Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Battle City .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Yami Marik ,  Maximillion Pegasus  and  Yami Bakura . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Blue-Eyes ", " LV ", and " Spirit Message " archetypes. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Silent Magician " and " Silent Swordsman " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack of  Duelist Pack: Rivals of the Pharaoh  contains 5 cards and each box contains 36 packs. The set contains 46 cards: 
 
 6  Ultra Rares 
 6  Super Rares 
 10  Rares 
 24  Commons
