 Decks 
 Colin's Decks are composed almost entirely of typical card crushes, and revolve around using them.
 In  Tag Force 1 , Colin is the only Tier 3 character to have more than one Deck. His first Deck is a  Charmer  Deck  that also features " Elemental Mistress Doriado ", while his second Deck is a  Burn  Deck  focusing on " White Magician Pikeru " and " Ebon Magician Curran ".  Deron  would use a Deck similar to the latter in the following  Tag Force  games.
 In  Tag Force 2  and  Tag Force 3 , Colin's Decks focus on " Elemental Mistress Doriado ". As it treats itself as  FIRE ,  WATER ,  EARTH  and  WIND  at the same time, Colin uses it alongside the " Element " monster series and to activate " Fuh-Rin-Ka-Zan ".
