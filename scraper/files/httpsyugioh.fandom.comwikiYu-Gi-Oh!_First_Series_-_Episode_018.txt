 Yugi Mutou  befriends the bullied youth  Imori , and the two discover the forbidden game  Dragon Block . One day, Imori asks Yugi to play the game. The evil Imori overcomes Yugi. Yugi has no clue how to play the game and Imori challenges  Dark Yugi  to a game with his soul itself on the line.  
 
 Mistakes 
 When Dark Yugi and Imori put the game away, it looks like Yugi is in control even though Dark Yugi is in control.
