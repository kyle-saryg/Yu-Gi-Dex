 Ramon Kagura  (神楽 羅門, Kagura Ramon) is a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  manga . He is a finalist in the  D1 Grand Prix .

 Biography 
 History 
 Not much is known about Ramon's past, other than he apparently suffered an embarrassing loss to  Jack Atlas  on his path to become King. [2] 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 Ramon was present at the D1 Grand Prix opening ceremonies where he watched  Rex Goodwin 's introduction to the tournament.  [2]  He then observed Yusei's  Duel  against  Greiger [2] [3]  and later,  Akiza 's Duel with  Sherry . [4] 
 Although Ramon's  Duels  weren't shown, he made it to the tournament finals and was shown on the cruise traveling to  Saiko Island  for the finals. [1]  but so far still didn't obtain any  Star Ticket [5] . An omake from the tankōbon  volume 5  revealed that Ramon also Dueled against  Andre  and lost, with Andre obtaining the Level 11 Star Ticket.
 Lazar revealed to Andre that he defeated Ramon and sealed his soul within a " Soul Prison " card. [6] 
 
 Deck 
 No cards in Ramon's Deck were shown.
