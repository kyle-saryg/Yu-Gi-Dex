 The  2012 Bulgarian National Championship  was a  World Championship Qualifier  for the  2012 World Championship . The tournament determined the 2012 national champion of Bulgaria. It took place in the Pliska Hotel in Sofia, Bulgaria on June 2, 2012. [1]
