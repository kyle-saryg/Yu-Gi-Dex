 Satoshi Hino  is a Japanese voice actor.
 He voices Kagurazaka ( Dimitri ) in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime [2] ,  Vector  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime [2]  and  Mani  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions [3] .
