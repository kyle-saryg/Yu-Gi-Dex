 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2+  monsters , including a  Plant  or  Insect  monster This  Link Summoned  card is  unaffected  by  Trap   effects . " Traptrix " monsters you  control  gain 1000  ATK  while you have a  Normal Trap  in your  GY .  Once per turn : You can  target   face-up  cards your opponent controls up to the number of Insect and Plant monsters you control;  negate  their effects (until the end of this  turn ), then you can  banish  1 Normal Trap from your GY, and if you do,  destroy  1 of the targeted  face-up  cards.
