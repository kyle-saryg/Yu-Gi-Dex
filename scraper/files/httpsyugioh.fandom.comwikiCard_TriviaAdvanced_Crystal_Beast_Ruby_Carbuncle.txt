 This card is the " Advanced Crystal Beast " counterpart of " Crystal Beast Ruby Carbuncle ". 
 This card's "Advanced" effect essentially works the same as its regular " Crystal Beast " counterpart's effect was in the anime though it's now limited to those conditions. 
 This is the also the only "Advanced Crystal Beast" that lacks visible armor. 
 This card has the lowest  ATK / DEF  out of all the "Advanced Crystal Beast" monsters.
