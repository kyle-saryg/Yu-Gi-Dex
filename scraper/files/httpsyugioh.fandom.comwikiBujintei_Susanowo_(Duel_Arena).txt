 Bujintei Susanowo  is a character version of the card " Bujintei Susanowo ". 
 He appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. His partner ID is P116.
 He was only obtainable by achieving any Rank from 1 to 100 on the Arena Weekly Win Ratings (Single), by the end of the time period of August 11, 2014, 00:00 (UTC) through August 17, 2014 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received him in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on August 20, 2014.

 Description 
 A  Bujin  who was  exiled  from the heavenly realm of Takama-ga-hara for false accusation.  He is traveling around to find Bujingis in order to defeat deities who devastate the earth .
 
 Deck 
 A  Deck  designed to fight with the  Beast-Warrior - Type   monsters  named  Bujin  while having Bujingi to support them. Bujingi can be activated from your  hand  or  Graveyard  depends on the card.
