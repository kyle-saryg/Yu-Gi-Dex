 The structure of the location in this card's artwork is similar to that of  Stonehenge . 
 This card's artwork is part of a series of events in the order of " Preparation of Rites " → " Pre-Preparation of Rites " → " Urgent Ritual Art " → "Advanced Ritual Art". 
 This card has an inverted counterpart, " High Ritual Art ".
