Main card page: " Hand-Holding Genie " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Hand "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ハンド」 .   As its English name contains "Hand", it  may  be treated a member of the "Hand" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 手をつなぐ魔人
