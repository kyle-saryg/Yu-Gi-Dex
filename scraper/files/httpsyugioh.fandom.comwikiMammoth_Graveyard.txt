 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 A mammoth that protects the graves of its pack and is absolutely merciless when facing grave-robbers. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Mammouth qui protège les tombes des siens et qui est absolument sans pitié face aux déterreurs de cadavres. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Ein Mammut, das die Gräber seiner Verwandten schützt und absolut gnadenlos vorgeht, wenn es auf Grabräuber trifft. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Un mammuth che protegge il cimitero del suo branco e non ha alcuna pietà quando si trova di fronte a ladri di tombe. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Portuguese 
 
 
 Um mamute que proteje os túmulos de seu bando e que enfrenta ladrões de túmulos impiedosamente. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Spanish 
 
 
 Un mamut que protege las tumbas de su manada y que es absolutamente despiadado cuando se enfrenta a saqueadores de tumbas. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Japanese 
 
 
 仲間のお墓を守るマンモス。墓荒らしを容赦なく攻撃。 
 
 
 
 
 
   Chinese 
 
 
 守護著夥伴墓地的猛獁，對於任何膽敢踐踏墓地的盜墓者進行毫不客氣的攻擊。 
 
 
 
 
 
   Korean 
 
 
 동료들의 무덤을 지키는 맘모스. 도굴꾼은 절대 용서치 않고 공격한다. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
 A mammoth that protects the graves of its pack and is absolutely merciless when facing grave-robbers. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Sacred Cards 
 
 
An animated skeleton of a mammoth with debilitating power. It can power down every  monster  on the enemy's  field .
