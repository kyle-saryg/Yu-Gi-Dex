 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If a  monster (s) " Fur Hire " you  control  is  destroyed   by battle  or an opponent's  card effect : You can  target  1 of those monsters;  Special Summon  1 monster "Fur Hire" from your  Deck . whose  Level  is lower than  target 's original Level. You can only use this effect of "Training Fur Hire, Fur All Your Training Needs"  once per turn .
