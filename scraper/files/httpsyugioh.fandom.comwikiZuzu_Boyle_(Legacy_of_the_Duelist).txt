 Zuzu Boyle , known as  Yuzu Hiiragi  ( 柊   柚 子 ,  Hīragi Yuzu ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Zuzu Boyle , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
