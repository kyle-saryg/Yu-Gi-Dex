 Starter Deck 2019 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Starter Deck 2019  is a  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the fourteenth Deck in the  OCG ' s annual Starter Deck series, following  Starter Deck 2018 . [1]
