 Structure Deck: Machine Re-Volt  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the tenth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (eighteenth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Dinosaur's Rage . In the  TCG , it is the tenth deck.
 In the Korean  OCG ,  Structure Deck: Machine Re-Volt  was released on April 30, 2008 as  Structure Deck: Machine Re-Volt Special Edition .
 
 This 40 card  Structure Deck  focuses on rampaging  machines  from the age of steam and gears. This  Deck  combines the " Ancient Gear " monsters from  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  with  Yugi's  wildly popular " Gadgets ", from the final  Duel  of classic  Yu-Gi-Oh! .
 12 brand new Cards! 
 Yugi 's highly anticipated " Gadget " monsters available for the first time outside Japan. 
 The "Gadget" monsters are very strong in tournaments. Many tournament players will purchase multiple copies of this  Deck  to get all the "Gadget" monsters they need. 
 New cards from Season 2 of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , including " Ancient Gear Engineer ". 
 Includes popular (and very rare)  Spell Cards  " Enemy Controller " and " Pot of Avarice ", making them easily available.

 Features 
 The Deck features card effects that can  Summon   monsters  one after another, overwhelming your opponent's defenses.
 The "Let's Power Up This Deck! (With Booster Packs)" supplement suggests combining this Deck with " Heavy Mech Support Platform " and " Machine Duplication " from  Dark Revelation Volume 3 , " Searchlightman " and " Victory Viper XX03 " from  Enemy of Justice , " Cyber Dragon ", " Gyroid " and " B.E.S. Crystal Core " from  Cybernetic Revolution , " Submarineroid ", " Decoyroid " and " Cyber Summon Blaster " from  Power of the Duelist  and three copies of " Cyber Ogre ", " Cyber Ogre 2 ", " Cyber Esper " and " Linear Accelerator Cannon " from  Cyberdark Impact .
