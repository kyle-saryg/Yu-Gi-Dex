 Structure Deck: Rokket Revolt  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game . It is the 46th Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Order of the Spellcasters .

 Features 
 This deck features monsters that include and support the " Rokket " archetype along with monsters from " Borrel " and " Topologic " archetypes that are featured in  Varis ' Deck from  Yu-Gi-Oh VRAINS  anime. 
 The "Power Up with Packs Supplement suggests combining this deck with " Borreload Savage Dragon " & " Speedburst Dragon " from Savage Strike, " Borreload eXcharge Dragon " from Rising Rampage, " Iron Dragon Tiamaton " from Flames of Destruction, and " Overflow Dragon " & " Crackdown " from Dark Neostorm.
