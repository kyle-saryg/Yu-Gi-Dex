 This card can be searched by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Alchemic Magician ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Mythical Bestiamorph ", " Cyberse Clock Dragon ", " Endymion, the Mighty Master of Magic " and " Left Arm Offering ". 
 Use "A Wingbeat of Giant Dragon" with monsters that can Special Summon themselves more than once per turn, such as " Dark Armed Dragon ", " Rainbow Dragon ", " Hieratic Dragon of Tefnuit ", " Vice Dragon " and " Schwarzschild Limit Dragon ".
 Beware of cards that negate or respond to a Summon, such as " Solemn Strike " and " Torrential Tribute ", however. 
 " King Dragun " and " Red-Eyes Darkness Metal Dragon " can Special Summon the monster returned to the hand again.
