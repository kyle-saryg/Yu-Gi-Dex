 Evilswarm Ouroboros  is a character version of the card " Evilswarm Ouroboros ". 
 It appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P022. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 11-5 under Quest mode.

 Trivia 
 As with the flavor text of " Evilswarm Heliotrope ", this opponent's quote and its Deck's name are meant to be read backwards.
 Unreversed, this character's quote is, "There is no beginning and no end; all will be destroyed and transformed into Evilswarm".
