 Greek - Σερνάμενος Δράκος #2 
 Ένας πανίσχυρος δράκος με δόντια που κάνουν τα πάντα στάχτη. 
 
 
 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Ultimate Masters: World Championship 2006  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was  "A powerful dragon with teeth that can grind almost anything to dust." 
 In the French version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Puissant dragon dont les dents peuvent réduire presque tout en poussière." 
 In the German version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Ein mächtiger Drache mit Zähnen, die fast alles zu Staub zermalmen können." 
 In the Italian version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "I poderosi i denti di questo drago possono sbriciolare qualunque cosa abbia di fronte." 
 In the Spanish version of  World Championship 2006  and  Card Almanac , this card lore was  "Un poderoso dragón con dientes que pueden reducir casi cualquier cosa a polvo."
