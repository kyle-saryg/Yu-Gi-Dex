 Mieru Houchun  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate version of  Aura Sentia , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime .
 
 Deck 
 Like her anime counterpart, Mieru runs a  " Prediction Princess " Deck .
