 Neo-Impact  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on December 27, 2016, 7:00 PM (EST).
 
 Description 
 Fusion Summon Meteor B. Dragon! Recast the world with Neo-Impact! 
 
 -Powerful  monster cards  such as " Meteor B. Dragon " and " Kaiser Sea Horse ." 
-" Paladin of White Dragon ," which is suited for " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " Decks, and " Senju of the Thousand Hands ," which works well with  Ritual Summon   Decks . 
- Spell / Trap Cards  that can fortify your Deck like the Spell Card, " Stamping Destruction ," that can destroy a Spell/Trap Card. Also, the Trap Card, " Mirror Wall ," halves the  ATK  of opponent monster(s) that conducted an attack while "Mirror Wall" was face-up on the field! 
-Total of 100 types: 10  UR , 12  SR , 32  R , and 46  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Neo-Impact  contains 200 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, with these consisting of either 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card; OR 1 Common card, 1 Rare card, and 1 Super Rare, Ultra Rare or (additional) Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 2 copies 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 8 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 9 copies of each instead: " Destruction Cyclone ", " Patrol Robo ", " Scanner ", " Milus Radiant ", " Meteor Dragon ", and " Treaty on Uniform Nomenclature ".
