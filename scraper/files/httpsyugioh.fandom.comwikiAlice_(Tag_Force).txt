 Alice , known as  Uria Yasuda  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's   Tag Force  games.
 Her Japanese name, "Uria" comes from the card " Uria, Lord of Searing Flames ", which she uses in her Decks. Her English name Alice seems to come from the title character from  Alice's Adventures in Wonderland . Her Decks' Japanese names also pay tribute to  Alice's Adventures in Wonderland .

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 4 
       Alice, in  Tag Force 4 .     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4 , Alice has tan skin and light brown hair and wears a lavender elementary school girls uniform.
 During the first stage of the "Angry  Mina " storyline, the  Player  and Mina Tag Duel against  Leo  and Alice in  Tops .
 In her own story, she competes with the Player against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and Leo in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the Player will receive a photo of himself,  Daisy  and Alice called "Tops Girl".
