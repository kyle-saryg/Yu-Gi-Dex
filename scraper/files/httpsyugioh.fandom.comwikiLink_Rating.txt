 A  Link Rating  (Japanese:  ＬＩＮＫ の 数 値 ,  Rinku no Sūchi  "LINK Number") is a property exclusive to  Link Monsters , in place of a  Level  or a  Rank . The original Link Rating of a Link Monster Card is located next to its  ATK , in the space normally taken up by the  DEF  on other  Monster Card  types.
 A Link Monster's Link Rating is equal to the number of  Link Arrows  it has, and also the number of  Link Materials  that must be used for its  Link Summon . If a Link Monster is used as Link Material, it can either be treated as either a single Link Material or multiple Link Materials equal to its Link Rating.
 
 Expression in card texts 
 In card texts, there are two different expression for "Link Ratings", depending on the specification of the card involved.
 
 " Link Rating " (Japanese: リンクマーカーの 数   Rinku Mākā no Kazu , " Link Marker  count "), for when the Link Rating value of the involved monster(s) are  unspecified . 
 " Link- " (Japanese: リンク  Rinku ,  Link ), for when the Link Rating value of the involved monster(s) are  specified . This expression is followed by a number to indicate the specified Link Rating of the involved monster(s).
