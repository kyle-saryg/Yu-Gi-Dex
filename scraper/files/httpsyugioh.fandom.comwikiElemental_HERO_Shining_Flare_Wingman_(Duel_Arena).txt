 Elemental HERO Shining Flare Wingman  is a character version of the card " Elemental HERO Shining Flare Wingman ". 
 He appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. His partner ID is P080. He also appears as an opponent at Stage 8-3 under Quest mode.
