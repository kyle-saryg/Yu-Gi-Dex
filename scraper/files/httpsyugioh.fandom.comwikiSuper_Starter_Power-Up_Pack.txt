 Super Starter Power-Up Pack 
 Space-Time Showdown Power-Up Pack 
 
 
 
 
 
 Super Starter Power-Up Pack  is a  Power-Up Pack  for  Super Starter: V for Victory . Each  Super Starter: V for Victory  contains 2  Power-Up Packs . In the European versions, each  Super Starter: V for Victory  contains 1  Power-Up Pack , but it contains 10 cards instead of 5.

 Breakdown 
 Each  Super Starter: V for Victory  contains 2 packs of  Super Starter Power-Up Pack . 1 of the pack contains 1 " Number C39: Utopia Ray V " and 4 other cards, while the other pack contains 1 " Rank-Up-Magic Limited Barian's Force " and 4 other cards. In the European version, each  Super Starter: V for Victory  contains 1 pack of  Super Starter Power-Up Pack . It contains 1 "Number C39: Utopia Ray V", 1 "Rank-Up-Magic Limited Barian's Force" and 8 other cards. There are 15 cards in total. The number of card of each rarity are as follows:
 
 2  Ultra Rares
