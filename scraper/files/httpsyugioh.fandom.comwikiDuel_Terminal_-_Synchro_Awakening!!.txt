 Duel Terminal - Synchro Awakening!!  is first installment of the  Duel Terminal  series. It is followed by  Duel Terminal - Invasion of Worms!! .

 Features 
 Introduces the " Ice Barrier ", " Mist Valley ", " X-Saber ", " Ally of Justice ", " Worm ", and " Flamvell "  Archetypes . Introduces  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Joey Wheeler ,  Jaden Yuki ,  Chazz Princeton  and  Zane Truesdale  as duelable opponents.
 
 Breakdown 
 The set includes 50 cards, all of which are  Parallel Rare . 30 of the cards are new, while 20 are reprints.
