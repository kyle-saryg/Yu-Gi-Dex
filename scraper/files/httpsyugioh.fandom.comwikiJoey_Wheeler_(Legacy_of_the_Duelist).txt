 Joey Wheeler , known as  Katsuya Jonouchi  ( 城之内 克也 ,  Jōnouchi Katsuya ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Joey Wheeler , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
