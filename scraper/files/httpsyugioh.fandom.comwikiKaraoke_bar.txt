      The karaoke bar.     
 The  karaoke bar  is a location in  Domino City  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 
 Events 
       the interior of the bar.     
 Sozoji  holds his  All Night Solo Live Shows  here. When  Yugi  and  Hanasaki  failed to sell any tickets to one of his shows, he beat up Hanasaki and forced Yugi to listen to his music at a deafening level. When he showed Yugi what he had done to Hanasaki, Yugi switched to  Dark Yugi , who defeated Sozoji in a  silence game   Shadow Game  at the bar.
