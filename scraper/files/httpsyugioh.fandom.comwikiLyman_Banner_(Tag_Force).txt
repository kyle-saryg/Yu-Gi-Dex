 Lyman Banner , known as  Daitokuji  (大徳寺,  Daitokuji ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Lyman Banner , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 
 Lyman can be found throughout Part 1 at the Slifer Red dorm, but will refuse to duel or team up with the player unless Pharaoh is following them around.
 During Part 2, Lyman partners with  Fonda Fontaine . He mentions that they joined the Tag Force tournament as the faculty team, but laments being unable to partner with Pharaoh.
 Weirdly, due to Lyman Banner and  Amnael  not being treated as the same character in  Tag Force , partnering with Lyman throughout Part 3 will eventually lead to a Tag Duel against Amnael and  Jaden Yuki . A similar oddity happens with  Atticus Rhodes  during the encounter with Nightshroud. However, if Amnael is the player's partner, Lyman is nowhere to be found during Part 2.
 Lyman, as Amnael, appears in the Abandoned Dorm after 10:00 P.M during Part 1 if he was defeated during Part 3. Defeating him 10 times will result in Amnael awarding them with both of his  Deck Recipes  as well as 3 copies of " Alchemic Kettle - Chaos Distill "
 If the player wants to partner with both Lyman and Amnael, they have to be asked separately during Part 1.
 
 Tag Force 3 
 Lyman Banner and Amnael are now properly treated as the same character at all times.
 Lyman cameos during the last story event for Normal  Chazz Princeton 's story and all of  Syrus Truesdale 's.
 During the last story event for Tier 2 and Tier 3 characters, the player and their partner Tag Duel against Amnael and  Adrian Gecko . If the player is partnering with Adrian, they duel against Amnael and Jaden. If either Lyman or Amnael is the player's partner, Jaden teams up with Adrian.
 
 Decks 
 Lyman, as himself, uses Decks focused on forcing players to  pay   Life Points  in order to perform actions while he uses a variety of effects to disrupt them and damage them further and to summon monsters in unusual ways, such as  Tokens  or  Trap Monsters . Many of the cards he uses can be related directly or indirectly to alchemy, such as " Jar " monsters or mirror-themed cards like " Copycat " and " Reflect Bounder ".
 As Amnael, he uses Decks focused on removing cards from play.
 
 Tag Force 
 Lyman does not use his second Deck,  "Finite Entity" , at any point during Story Mode. It can only be dueled against in Free Duel mode, and its  Deck Recipe  is given to the player upon beating Part 3 with Lyman as their partner.
