 " Secret Six Samurai ", known in Japan as the " Shadow Six Warmen " ( 影 六 武 衆 ,  Kage Roku Bushū ), are a sub- archetype  of the " Six Samurai " archetype introduced in  Deck Build Pack: Spirit Warriors .  
 The inclusion of " Secret Six Samurai - Hatsume " will likely be implicit in the Italian  TCG  when the Italian version of " Legendary Secret of the Six Samurai " comes out, as with other Italian archetype names, because her name doesn't contain "Sei Samurai Segreto". Oddly enough, one would expect the generic name for a group of individuals of both genders to be masculine by default, but her name alone, "Hatsume - Sei Samurai Segreta", for some reason, implies that all other members of the "Secret Six Samurai" are female, which conflicts with their own names. However, whether it is "Sei Samurai Segreto" or "Sei Samurai Segreta", both of those names are odd by themselves because the adjective for "secret" is singular in both cases, even though the noun phrases it modifies are plural. This peculiarity's also the case for the word for "legendary" used for the Italian name of the " Legendary Six Samurai ", although the word remains masculine in the name of " Legendary Six Samurai - Mizuho ".

 Design 
 They are improved versions of the "Six Samurai."  Much like the originally released "Six Samurai" monsters, there is one for every  Attribute  (except  DIVINE ). Their crest is different from the others, with kunai-like shapes, keeping the ninja style of the series.
 
 Playing style 
 Unlike other "Six Samurai" monsters who protect themselves from battle destruction by destroying another Six Samurai; the "Secret Six Samurai", fitting their ninja-styled theme, protect other "Six Samurai" monsters from effect destruction by banishing themselves from the Graveyard.
