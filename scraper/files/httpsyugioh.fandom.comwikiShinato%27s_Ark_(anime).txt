 Shinato's Ark 
 
 Japanese:   奇跡の方舟 
 Romaji:   Kiseki no Hakobune 
 Translated:  Ark of Miracles 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 Every  monster   sent  to the  Graveyard  is trapped in the ark. During the Battle Phase of your opponent you can summon monsters within the ark in defense position up to the number of monsters your opponent has on the field in attack position. You can  destroy  all monsters within the ark to gain 500  Life Points  for each monster destroyed. When this card is  removed from the field , you can  Special Summon  1 " Shinato, King of a Higher Plane " from your  hand .
