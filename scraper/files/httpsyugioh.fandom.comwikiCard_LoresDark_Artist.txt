 In the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Card Almanac , this card lore was " The DEF of this monster is decreased by half when attacked by LIGHT monsters. " 
 In the French version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was " La DEF de ce monstre est réduite de moitié lorsqu'il est attaqué par des monstres LUMIÈRE. " 
 In the German version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Der DEF-Wert dieses Monsters verringert sich um die Halfte, wenn es von Monstern des LICHTS angegriffen wird. " 
 In the Italian version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was " Il valore di DEF di questa carta viene dimezzato quando viene attaccato da mostri di LUCE. " 
 In the Spanish version of  Card Almanac , this card lore was " El DEF de este monstruo se reduce a la mitad cuando es atacado por monstruos LIZ. "
