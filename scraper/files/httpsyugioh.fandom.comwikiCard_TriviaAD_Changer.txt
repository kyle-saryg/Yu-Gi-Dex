 The "AD" in its name stands for "Attack" & "Defense".
 Interestingly enough, the "A/D" hasn't been 'translated' to other language's words for Attack or Defense, IE in Greek, it would be E (Επίθεση) and A (Άμυνα). 
 This monster appears in the artwork of " Surprise Inspection ".
