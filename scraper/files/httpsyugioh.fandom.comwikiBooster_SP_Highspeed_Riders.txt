 Booster SP: Highspeed Riders  is a Special  Booster  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It was first made available on August 16, 2015, at the venue of  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2015 , but was officially released (and its contents made legal for tournament play) on August 22, 2015. [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugo ,  Officer 227  and Jack Atlas ( 5D's  anime ,  manga  and  ARC-V  anime  versions). 
 Contains members of the " Resonator " archetype. 
 Introduces the " PSY-Frame " archetype and the first support for the " Speedroid " and " Red Dragon Archfiend " archetypes.
