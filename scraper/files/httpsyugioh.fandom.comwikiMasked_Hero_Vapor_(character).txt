 Masked Hero Vapor  is a character version of the card, " Masked HERO Vapor ". It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus  as a Single Duel opponent.
 This opponent can be unlocked by clearing chapter 2 of story mode.
 
 
 Deck 
 Over the Nexus 
 In  Over the Nexus , Masked Hero Vapor uses a Deck titled "Masked Hero".
