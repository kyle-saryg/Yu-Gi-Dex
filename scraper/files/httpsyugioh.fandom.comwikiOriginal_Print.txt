The  Original Print  of a card refers to the first set in which it was printed for a given language or territory.
 For example, a  LOB-000  " Tri-Horned Dragon " is a  TCG  Original Print, whereas a  LCYW-EN157  "Tri-Horned Dragon" is a  TCG   reprint .
