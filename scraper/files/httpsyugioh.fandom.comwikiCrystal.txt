 " Crystal ", known as " Gem " ( 宝 玉   Hōgyoku ) in Japan, is an  archetype  that includes the " Crystal Beast " and " Ultimate Crystal " archetypes and their support cards. It is used in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime by  Jesse Anderson .
 " Violet Crystal " is not a "Crystal" card because it does not have 「 宝 玉 」 in its Japanese name.
