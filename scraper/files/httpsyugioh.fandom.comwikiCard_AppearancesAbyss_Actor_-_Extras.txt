 Anime 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 72 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  plays this card during his  Turbo Duel  against  Yugo . Sylvio sets the  Pendulum Scales  on the first turn using this card and " Abyss Actor - Funky Comedian ". 
 In  episode 142 , Sylvio plays this card during the Battle Royal against  Yuya Sakaki ,  Gong Strong  and  Moon Shadow . On the sixth turn, Sylvio sets the Pendulum Scales using this card and "Abyss Actor - Funky Comedian". 
 In the anime, this card was never summoned as a monster, nor were its effects activated.
