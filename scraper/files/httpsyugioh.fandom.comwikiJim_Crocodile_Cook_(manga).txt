 Jim Crocodile Cook  (ジム・クロコダイル・クック,  Jimu Kurokodairu Kukku ) or  James Crocodile Cook  (ジェームス・クロコダイル・クック) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga.

 Biography 
 Jim attends the  American Duel Academy  alongside the other  Overseas Champions .
 His role is much more malicious than in the anime. He is possessed by  Tragoedia , thus making him an antagonist. However, he's also aware of Tragoedia's existence, and actively assists Tragoedia's goals. James seeks out  Atticus Rhodes , catching him in the forest near the  Abandoned Dorm  and challenges him to a Duel, which Atticus wins, despite Jim's use of the  Legendary Planet , " The Tyrant Neptune ". Jim had been rivals with  David Rabb  when they were in America, and seeks out Atticus because he defeated Rabb before Jim ever got a chance to. He is then selected to tag with  Axel Brodie  against  Chazz Princeton  and  Syrus Truesdale , and his team ultimately loses, despite working together much better than Adrain and Jesse before them. During the match, he attempts to make it a Shadow Game, but Principle MacKenzie prevents him from doing so.
 In the singles match-up, he Duels  Bastion Misawa  in the forest, and is the only one of the American Duelists to make the match into a  Shadow Game . He cruelly defeats Bastion with "Neptune", laughing afterwards at the closeness of the match. Tragoedia later recalls him to reclaim Neptune, and he is found in the warehouse where Tragoedia has stored his tablet by Aster Phoenix, and later Jaden and the others. He recovers after Tragoedia's defeat, seen before Jaden's Duel with Koyo alongside Axel.
 
 Deck 
 Jim plays an  " Alligator " Deck , which focuses on  swarming  and  Beatdown  tactics and later on,  Tributing  monsters for his  ace card , " The Tyrant Neptune ". Jim's Deck also contains one  unnamed monster .
