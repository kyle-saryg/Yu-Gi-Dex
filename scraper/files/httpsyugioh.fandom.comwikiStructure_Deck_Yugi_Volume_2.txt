 Structure Deck: Yugi Volume 2  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the fifth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Pegasus .

 Features 
 The deck features cards used by  Yugi Muto . It also contains new cards like  Magical Dimension  as well as new artworks for  Dark Paladin  and  Dark Magician .
