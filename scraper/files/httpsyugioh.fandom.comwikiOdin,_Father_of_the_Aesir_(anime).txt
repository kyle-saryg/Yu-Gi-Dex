 Notes 
 
 ↑   Despite the wording implying otherwise, the first effect is an  Ignition Effect  that activates during the  Main Phase  of its controller. Once activated, until the  End Phase  of the  turn , Spell/Trap effects that affects Divine-Beast-Type monsters are negated while "Odin" is face-up on the field.
