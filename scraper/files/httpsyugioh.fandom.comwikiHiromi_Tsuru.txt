 Hiromi Tsuru  was a Japanese voice actress.
 
 Work 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX , she voiced  Yubel . She would later go on to reprise the role in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time .
 
 Death 
 On November 16, 2017, she passed away from  Aortic dissection .
