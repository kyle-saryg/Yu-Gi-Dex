 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
When this card  resolves , you can  add  1 " Amazoness "  monster  from your  Deck  to your  hand  or, if it is a  Pendulum Monster , you can place it in your  Pendulum Zone  instead. When you take  battle damage  and you  control  an "Amazoness" Monster Card: You can gain  LP  equal to the damage you took. You can only use this  effect  of "Amazoness Hot Spring"  once per turn .
