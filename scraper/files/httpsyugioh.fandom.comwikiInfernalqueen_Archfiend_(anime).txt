 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] The  controller  of this card  pays  500  Life Points  during each of his/her  Standby Phases  (this is not optional). When this card is specifically designated as a  target  of the  effect  of a card controlled by your opponent, when  resolving  the effect, roll a six-sided die. If the result is 2 or 5,  negate  the effect and  destroy  the opponent's card. As long as this card remains on the  field , increase the  ATK  of all face-up  Archfiend   monsters  by 1000 points.
