Flying  is an ability used in two games,  Dungeon Dice Monsters  and  Capsule Monsters .
 
 Dungeon Dice Monsters 
 Monsters with the Flying Ability can only be attacked by monsters that can attack Flying monsters or other Flying Monsters. You have to pay two movement crests instead of one per space to move Flying monsters.
 
 Capsule Monsters 
 Monsters with the Flying ability can move over opposing Monsters that block their path. However they must also obey their Move pattern and range.
