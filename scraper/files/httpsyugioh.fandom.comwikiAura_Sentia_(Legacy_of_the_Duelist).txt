 Aura Sentia , known as  Mieru Hochun  ( 方 中  ミエル,  Hōchun Mieru ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Aura Sentia , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
