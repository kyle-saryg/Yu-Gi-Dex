 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Fusion Monsters  and " Tearlaments "  monsters  you  control  gain 500  ATK . If an  Aqua  "Tearlaments" monster is  sent  to your  GY  by  card effect : You can send 1  Level  4 or lower Aqua monster from your  Deck  to the GY, but if it is not a "Tearlaments" monster, you cannot activate cards, or the effects of cards, with that name for the rest of this  turn . You can only use this effect of "Tearlaments Perlegia"  once per turn .
