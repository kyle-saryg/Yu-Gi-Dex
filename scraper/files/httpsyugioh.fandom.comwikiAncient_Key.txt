 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Ancient "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 古代の 」 .   As its English name contains "Ancient", it  may  be treated a member of the "Ancient" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Property 
 
 
   Continuous 
 
 When you  activate  this card, you can  Special Summon  up to 2 " Stone Giants " from your  hand  or  Deck  in  Attack Position . During your  Main Phase , you can  send  this  face-up  card and 2 "Stone Giants" (whose  battle positions  have been changed twice each) you  control  to the  Graveyard  to  add  1 " Ancient Gate " from your Deck or Graveyard to your hand.
