 Fraudulent psychic,  Kokurano , has his eyes on  Anzu Mazaki . Although Anzu does not believe his predictions at first, her desire to see her mysterious savior,  Dark Yugi , leads her right into Kokurano's grasp. When Yugi finds out someone is keeping an eye on Anzu, he gets a bit jealous. Along with that, Dark Yugi knows it was all fake, and wants to protect Anzu away from Kokurano. Then Dark Yugi challenges him to a  Game of Darkness .
