 Notes 
 
 ↑   In terms of gameplay, this card  targeted  1 card in the opponent's  Graveyard  and treated itself as that card. If the targeted card was a  Spell / Trap Card , this card's  effects  were treated as that Spell/Trap Card's effects. If the targeted card was a  monster , this card was treated as that monster and  Special Summoned  to this card's  controller 's side of the  field . (Even if this card was treated as a monster, it still remained in the  Spell & Trap Zone .)
