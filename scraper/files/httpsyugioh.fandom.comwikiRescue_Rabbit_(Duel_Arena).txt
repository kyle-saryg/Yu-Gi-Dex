 Rescue Rabbit  is a character version of the card " Rescue Rabbit ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P100.

 Description 
 A rabbit who's an expert in saving  monsters . Apparently has a  cat  for a boss.
 
 Decks 
 An  Xyz   Deck  containing many low- Level   Normal Monsters  that can perform rescues. Use cards like " Gold Sarcophagus " to make sure you bring "Rescue Rabbit" into your  hand . Then line up monsters with the same name to perform an Xyz Summon!
