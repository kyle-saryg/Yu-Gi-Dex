 Goldd, Wu-Lord of Dark World  is a character version of the card, " Goldd, Wu-Lord of Dark World ", appearing in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime as a member of the  Dark World Army . He also appears as a Tag Duel opponent in the video game,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2008 . His Tag partner is  Sillva, Warlord of Dark World  and their team name is  Gold and Silver .
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 Goldd, along with his partner, Sillva, appeared as members of the  Dark World Army . They offered to take  Chazz Princeton ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Atticus Rhodes  and  Tyranno Hassleberry  to  Brron's  fortress. Chazz challenged them to a Duel, but the Dark World spirits said that they must bring in Jaden's friends alive, then knocked them unconscious, bringing them to the fortress. However, Goldd and Sillva later realized that there were supposed to be five sacrifices-- Syrus Truesdale  was missing. They later tracked Syrus down, only to find him plunging off a cliff to his apparent "death". Goldd and Silvva opted to retreat and not mention any of what they saw to Brron.
 
 Deck 
 Goldd, Wu-Lord of Dark World uses a " Dark World " Deck titled  "Logic of the Underworld" ,  "Fiend Family" ,  "Pure Gold" , and  "World of Darkness" .
