 " Subterror Behemoth ", known as " Subterror Malice " (サブテラーマリス  Sabuterā Marisu ) in the  OCG , is an  archetype  of high- Level   EARTH   Flip monsters . It is a sub-archetype of the " Subterror " archetype.
 All "Subterror Behemoth" monsters share the following effects, except for " Subterror Behemoth Fiendess ".
 
 When a  face-up   monster  you control is flipped  face-down , if you control no face-up monsters: You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand  in  Defense Position . 
 Once per turn: You can change this card to face-down Defense Position.
