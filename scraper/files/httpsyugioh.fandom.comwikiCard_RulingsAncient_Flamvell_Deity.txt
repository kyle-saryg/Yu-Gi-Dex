 OCG Rulings 
 The effect of " Ancient Flamvell Deity " does not  target . [1] [2]   You choose the cards to be removed from play when  resolving  the effect. [2] 
 The effect of " Ancient Flamvell Deity " which increases its ATK by the number of cards removed x200 resolves as part of the same effect which removes from play cards from the opponent's Graveyard.  This effect does not start an additional Chain Link when it is applied. [1]
