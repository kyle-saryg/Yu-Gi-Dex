 D.D. Survivor  is a character version of the card  D.D. Survivor . He appears in the video games,  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2007 ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , and  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator , this and  Raiza the Storm Monarch  can be unlocked if the person  Turbo Duels  for 200 times. Their Tag Team name is  Tribute Emperor!? . In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , this character and  Cyber Valley  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents during Story Mode (clear chapter 5). Their Tag Team name is  Removal Guys . In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus , He partners with  D.D. Scout Plane . Their team name is  Dimension Walkers
