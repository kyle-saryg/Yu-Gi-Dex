 Rica Matsumoto  is a Japanese voice actress.
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters  ( Yu-Gi-Oh! ), she voiced Ryo Bakura ( Bakura Ryou ) (from  episode 42  onwards), Dark Bakura ( Yami Bakura ) and Bakura, King of Thieves ( Bandit King Bakura ), following  Yo Inoue 's step down from Bakura roles due to her poor health condition. [2] 
 Outside of  Yu-Gi-Oh! , she is best known for voicing Satoshi (Ash Ketchum), the protagonist of the  Pokémon  anime.
