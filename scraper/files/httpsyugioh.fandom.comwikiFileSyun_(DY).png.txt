 Syun Kurosaki  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate version of  Shay Obsidian , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime . 
 
 Deck 
 Like his anime counterpart, Syun runs a  " Raidraptor " Deck .
