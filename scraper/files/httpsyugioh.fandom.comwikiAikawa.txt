 Aikawa  is an editor of  V Jump  in real life. He has also appeared as a character in V Jump alongside a number of  cosplay  characters. [1] 
 The video game character  Sergeant Aikawa  is named after him.
 
 Character biography 
 Aikawa took part in the  The Third Charisma Duelist Determining Fight Tournament . He defeated  Senjome  in the first round [2] ,  Dueluka  in the second round [3]  and  Duelua  in the finals, winning the tournament. [4] 
 
 Deck 
 Aikawa uses a " Machina Gadget ", referred to as " Machina  &  Gadget " (マシンナーズ ＆ ガジェット), Deck. [5]
