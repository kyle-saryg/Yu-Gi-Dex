 " Riding Rush Duel! " is the fifty-fourth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on June 27, 2021.
 A giant spaceship suddenly shows up in the sky! This spaceship is the fortress of the six Goha Siblings, the real presidents of  Goha Corporation ! In order to protect the  Rush Duel Robot  that contains data for the  Rush Duel  program from destruction,  Yuga  and his friends must Duel against the siblings. The first match is against the eldest brother, the speed demon  Yuro Goha ! Riding Rush Duel, Acceleration!
 
 Featured Duel: Yuga Ohdo VS. Yuro Goha 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
