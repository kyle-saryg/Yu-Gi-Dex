 [  Fairy  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except with " The Sky Lord ". When this card is Summoned, toss a coin. If Heads, this card gains the Right Side Up  effect , if Tails, this card gains the Upside Down effect ● Right Side Up: When this card  destroys  an opponent's  monster   by battle  and  sends  it to the  Graveyard ,  add  1 card from your Graveyard to your  hand . You cannot  activate  that card this  turn . ● Upside Down: During either player's turn, when a  Spell ,  Trap , or  Effect Monster 's effect is activated that targets this card, this card loses 1000  ATK  and your opponent  draws  1 card. Then,  negate  the activation of that card, and destroy it.
