 The  chairman  is an official at  Duel Academy . She leads the Disciplinary Action Squad, which is a group of security agents that intervene when a student has committed a serious crime. She escorts  Jaden Yuki  and  Syrus Truesdale  to  Chancellor Sheppard 's office after their trespass at the  Abandoned Dorm . She later appears protecting Chancelor Sheppard from  Pierre the Gambler  but fails.
 
 Non-canon appearances 
       The chairman in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force .     
 The chairman appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , where she can usually be found in  Chancellor Sheppard 's office. She claims she doesn't duel and states that she needs to increase the security at Duel Academy for the Tag Force tournament, as there will be duelists from outside Duel Academy participating.
 
 Triva 
 Out of all the staff she has the most minor role.
