 Z-ARC , known as  Zarc  (ズァーク,  Zāku ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Z-ARC , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
