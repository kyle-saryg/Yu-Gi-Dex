 D.D. Crow  is a character version of the card " D.D. Crow ".
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P085.

 Description 
 A crow from  another dimension  that  banishes  cards from the  Graveyard .
 
 Deck 
 A Low  Level   Deck  intended to obstruct your opponent. None of the  monsters  have especially high  ATK , but they all possess powerful  effects . Obstruct your opponent's moves completely with "use of Graveyard" or " Special Summons " and keep control of the  Duel  in your hands!
