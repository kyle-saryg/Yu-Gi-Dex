 Anime 
 
 In  episode 61 ,  Misty Tredwell  uses this card during her Ground Duel against  Akiza Izinski . After she activates this card, Misty Tributes " Dragon Queen of Tragic Endings " in order to Tribute Summon " Earthbound Immortal Ccarayhua " (as this card's effect decreases the necessary Tributes for the Tribute Summon of "Ccarayhua" by one). This card remained on the field until the Duel was over.
