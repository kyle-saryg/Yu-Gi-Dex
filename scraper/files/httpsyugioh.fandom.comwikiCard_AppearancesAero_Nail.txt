 In  episode 172 ,  Mai Valentine  uses this card during her Duel against  Joey Wheeler . She equips this card onto " Cyber Harpie Lady " (her first copy), increasing its ATK by 300. Later "Cyber-Tech Alligator" attacks and destroys the "Cyber Harpie Lady" equipped with this card, but Mai activates this card's effect to equip it onto her other "Cyber Harpie Lady" instead of sending it to the Graveyard. This increases the other "Cyber Harpie Lady's" ATK by 300. In  the next episode , this card is destroyed when Mai's "Cyber Harpie Lady" is destroyed by the effect of " Gilford the Lightning ".
