 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 This card can be  searched  by " Dark Sage ", " Ancient Gear Drill ", " Angmarl the Fiendish Monarch ", " Advanced Crystal Beast Cobalt Eagle ", " Left Arm Offering ", " Terraforming ", " An Owl of Luck ", " Demise of the Land ", " Madolche Butlerusk ", " Planet Pathfinder ", " Pop-Up ", " Metaverse ", and " Set Rotation ". 
 This card makes it easier for " Crystal Beast " decks to fill the Graveyard for " Rainbow Dragon ".
 This card also allows "Crystal Beast" decks to fulfill the Summoning condition of " Rainbow Dark Dragon ", and by extension, the activation requirement of " Rainbow Gravity ". 
 This card allows you to utilize  DARK  monster support in "Crystal Beast" decks, including " Dark Armed Dragon ", " Dark Nephthys ", " Doomsday Horror ", " Creeping Darkness " and " The Beginning of the End ". 
 Traditional Format 
 This card can be searched by " Ancient Fairy Dragon ".
