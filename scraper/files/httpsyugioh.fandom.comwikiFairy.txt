 Fairy  ( 天 使   Tenshi  "Angel") is a  Type  of  monster  often represented by beings such as sprites, angels, or in some occasions, anything that represents organic and mechanical beings of advanced extraterrestrial origin. They're primarily  LIGHT , although there are also those of all six common  Attributes .
 Fairies have their own  Field Spell Card  " The Sanctuary in the Sky ",  searcher  " Nova Summoner " and double  drawer  " Cards from the Sky ". It is very easy to summon powerful, high-Level Fairies with cards like " Valhalla, Hall of the Fallen ", " Court of Justice ", " Celestial Transformation " and " Gellenduo ".
 Fairies have three of their own Structure Decks:  Structure Deck: Surge of Radiance  ( OCG -only),  Structure Deck: Lost Sanctuary / Lost Sanctuary Structure Deck  and  Structure Deck R: Surge of Divine Light / Structure Deck: Wave of Light .
 A large proportion of this type is classed as  female , and as such, many female duelists have used Fairy monsters in both the anime and manga. In the anime, lead female characters  Téa Gardner ,  Alexis Rhodes ,  Zuzu Boyle  and  Skye Zaizen  (as Blue Angel) have used Decks consisting of mainly Fairy monsters. Fairy monsters have also been used in the anime by  Zigfried von Schroeder ,  Sartorius ,  Z-one ,  Dumon  and  Aura Sentia . 
 Prominent Fairy monsters include: " Arcana Force EX - The Light Ruler ", " Archlord Kristya ", " Athena ", " Banisher of the Radiance ", " Bountiful Artemis ", " Darklord Morningstar ", " Guiding Ariadne ", " Herald of Perfection ", " Honest ", “ Tethys, Goddess of Light ”, “ Splendid Venus ”, " Master Hyperion ", " Odin, Father of the Aesir ", " Vylon Omega ", " Number 16: Shock Master ", " Artifact Durendal ", and " Sacred Arch-Airknight Parshath ".
