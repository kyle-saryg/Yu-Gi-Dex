 Summary 
 Anzu  clashes with the beautiful but sadistically strict  Ms. Chono , and organizes a petition to change the school rules. But when Ms. Chono's underhanded counterattack threatens to ruin the petition,  Dark Yugi  steps in and challenges her to a  Game of Darkness .
