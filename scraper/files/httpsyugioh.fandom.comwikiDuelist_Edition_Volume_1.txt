 Duelist Edition Volume 1  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ) in the  Duelist Edition  series.
 Precious Pack Volume 1  and  Volume 2  are the Korean equivalents of this Booster Pack.

 Features 
 A variety of cards have been specially selected, all of which have been renewed with a new card and text format. 
 Reprint cards from 2006 Booster Packs series:  Power of the Duelist ,  Cyberdark Impact ,  Strike of Neos  and  Force of the Breaker . 
 Additionally, 3  Secret Rare  cards have also been included. 
 Includes one new card, " Miracle Contact ".
