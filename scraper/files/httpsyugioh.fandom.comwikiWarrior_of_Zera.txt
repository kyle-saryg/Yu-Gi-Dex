 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Warrior "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ウォリアー」 .   As its English name contains "Warrior", it  may  be treated a member of the "Warrior" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 The  Arabic, Croatian  and  Greek                      names given are not official.  ( card names )

 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 A wandering  warrior  who seeks the  sanctuary  where he can gain the power of the Archlords. To escape the temptation of  evil fiends , he fights solo day by day. 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Un guerrier errant à la recherche du sanctuaire où obtenir le pouvoir des seigneurs ancestraux. Jour après jour, solitaire, il combat pour échapper aux tentations des démons maléfiques. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Ein wandernder Krieger, der nach der Zuflucht sucht, in der er die Macht der Erzlords erlangen kann. Um der Verlockung der bösen Unterweltler zu entgehen, kämpft er Tag für Tag allein.
