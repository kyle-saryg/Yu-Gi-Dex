 Summoned Skull  is a character version of the card " Summoned Skull ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P059. It also appears as an opponent at Stage 11-2 under Quest mode.

 Description 
 An " Archfiend " that uses dark powers to control people's minds. Can be pretty tricky to  Summon !
 
 Opponent Quote 
 My name is Summoned Skull. If you want to Summon me then you must chant " Summon  Summoned Skull!" Do you understand? Promise me you will do so!
 
 Decks 
 A  Synchro   Deck  based on  monster   effects  that use Dice and " Archfiend "  archetype  monsters, which require  Life Points  to Summon. Once you have an "Archfiend" card in the  field  you can  Activate  an array of  Spells  and  Traps . Don't worry about the cost to your Life Points, just Summon it and start the  attack !

 Trivia 
 This character's pose is based on the artwork of " Skull Archfiend of Lightning ", which is considered a  retrained  version of " Summoned Skull ". 
 " Rainbow Life " is included in this character's Deck to possibly counter the LP maintenance effects of the chess-themed " Archfiend " monsters, and/or because " Terrorking Archfiend " appears in its artwork.
