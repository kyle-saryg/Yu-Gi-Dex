 Ruff Ruff McDogg  is a nested fictional character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga  and  anime . He is from the cartoon  Funny Bunny . Ruff Ruff is a bulldog police officer and antagonist to  Funny Bunny .
 In the entrance to the cartoon, Ruff Ruff fires 26,000 bullets at Funny Bunny, all of which were dodged. In 3013 episodes, he never managed to catch Funny Bunny.
