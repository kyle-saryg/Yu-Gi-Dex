 Dante , known as  Atsumi Onda  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  series . He is an  Obelisk Blue  student.

 Appearances 
 Dante is a member of the  Six Attribute Deck Masters , a group of Obelisk Blue students who uses Attribute-specific Decks. Dante is the  FIRE  user.
 In  Tag Force 1 , Dante can always be found with the other members of the group:  Seydina ,  Truly ,  Brown ,  Rock , and  Celia .
 
 Tag Force 3 
 In Obelisk Blue  Syrus ' third event, Dante and Brown Tag Duel against the player and Syrus.
