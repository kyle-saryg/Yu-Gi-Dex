 This card can be searched by " Advanced Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ", " Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus ", " Crystal Bond ", " Crystal Beast Rainbow Dragon ", " Crystal Beacon ", " Rainbow Bridge of Salvation ", " Awakening of the Crystal Ultimates ", " Rainbow Refraction ", " Crystal Tree ", " Crystal Conclave ", " Crystal Master ", " Crystal Miracle ", " Crystal Release ", " Crystal Pair ", " Cyber Angel Benten ", " Sky Scourge Cidhels ", " Celestial Knightlord Parshath ", " Victorica, Angel of Bravery ", " Power Angel Valkyria ", " Nova Summoner ", " Shining Angel ", " Lyna the Light Charmer, Lustrous ", " Photon Veil ", " Chaos Zone ", " Sphere of Chaos ", " Serpentine Princess ", " Battle Royal Mode - Joining ", " Vampire Dragon ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Umbramirage the Elemental Lord ", " Sangan " and " Witch of the Black Forest ".
 Placing this card in your  Spell & Trap Zone  via either "Crystal Bond" or the "Crystal Beast Sapphire Pegasus" that it searches will allow this card to Special Summon the other (non-Advanced) " Crystal Beast " monster that was placed in another of your Spell & Trap Zones, setting up a  Link 2   Link Monster  or  Rank 4   Xyz Monster  with just one card. 
 If you control " Advanced Dark ", this card becomes an ideal target for effects that place it in your Spell & Trap Zone during your turn. Apart from some of the aforementioned cards which place them from your Deck, " Crystal Blessing " places them from your Graveyard only.
