 Quattro , known as  IV  ( Ⅳ ,  Fō ) in the Japanese and Italian versions, is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL World Duel Carnival  video game. This is a video game depiction of  Quattro , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  anime.
