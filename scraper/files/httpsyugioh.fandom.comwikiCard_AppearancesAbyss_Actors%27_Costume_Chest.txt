 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 101 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  uses this card during his duel with  Gong Strong  against  Kite Tenjo . On the fourth turn of the duel, he activates this card. He then activates the effect of this card to target his " Abyss Actor - Superstar " and  Equip  " Abyss Costume - Hero's Sword " from his Deck to it, giving "Superstar" 300 ATK. During Gong's next turn, Sylvio activate this card's effect to Equip " Abyss Costume - Dwarf's Shield " from his Deck to Gong's  Superheavy Samurai Ninja Sarutobi ", which increases "Ninja Sarutobi"'s DEF by 300. This card remains on the field until the Duel is over.
