 Premium Pack  is a pack in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game . It has been released in English, French, German, and Italian.
 Premium Pack  was pre-released at Comic Con from August 26–28 2007. It was released to retail on October 10, 2007. [1] [2] 
 Premium Pack  contains cards that had not previously been released in the  TCG  (except " Metalmorph " and " Red-Eyes Black Metal Dragon ", which had previously only been released in the  TCG  in North America). This pack featured all the cards from the Japanese  Premium Pack 6 , plus 9 additional cards which had previously been released in Japanese in other  Premium Packs .

 Breakdown 
 Each pack contained 5 cards, 2  Secret Rares  and 3  Super Rares . There are mainly 7 Super and 8 Secret Rares, but early prints of the set, printed the 7 Super Rares as additional Secret Rares making the  Master Set  22, instead of 15.
