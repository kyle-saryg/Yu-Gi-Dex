 Green Gadget  is a character version of the card " Green Gadget ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P073.
 In the beta, its partner ID was P033.

 Description 
 One of the Green,  Red  and  Yellow  " Gadget " Trio. A little rusty but still very much on active duty.
 
 Decks 
 A  Deck  that mixes "Gadget," " Machina " and " Geargia "  Machine - Type   monsters . Fight using the "Gadget"  hand  replenishing  effect , "Geargia"  Special Summon  effects and "Machina"  Equip Card  conversion effects!
