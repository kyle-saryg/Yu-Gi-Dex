 If this card is  Xyz Summoned  using an  Xyz Monster  that was treated as a Level 7 monster as  Material , it gains these  effects . ● It can make a second  attack  during each  Battle Phase . ●  Once per turn : You can  detach  all  Xyz Materials  from it;  negate  the effects of as many  face-up   Spell  and  Trap Cards  currently on the  field  as possible, and if you do,  destroy  as many cards on the field as possible (other than this card), then this card gains 200  ATK  for each, until the end of this  turn .
