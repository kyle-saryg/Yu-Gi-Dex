 The  Duel Computer  (ﾃﾞｭｴﾙｺﾝﾋﾟｭｰﾀｰ  Dyueru Konpyūtā ), also known as the  Duel Machine  (決闘マシーン) in the Japanese version, is a computer used by  Seto Kaiba  to practice dueling against. It appears in the anime when Kaiba tests his new  Duel Disks  and " Obelisk the Tormentor ". Giving the computer his old deck, he defeats it using "Obelisk". Due to the excessive power of "Obelisk", the computer overloaded and exploded.
