 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
While  face-up  on the  field , this card is also  DARK - Attribute . You can  discard  1  LIGHT  or DARK  monster ;  send  1 LIGHT or DARK monster with a different Attribute than the discarded monster from your  Deck  to the  GY , but for the rest of this  turn , you cannot  Special Summon  monsters with the same name as the one sent to the GY by this  effect . You can only use this effect of "Chaos Grepher"  once per turn .
