 This card can be  searched  by " Cosmic Slicer Zer'oll ", " Spell Calling ", " Quick Booster " and " Mecha Phantom Beast Jaculuslan ". 
 This card cannot destroy " Alien Shocktrooper M-Frame " because it does not have a Level. 
 If your " Alien " monster would be destroyed by your opponent's card, Chain this card in response. Your opponent must control at least one face-up monster. 
 Follow up this card with cards that make use of  A-Counters  such as " Mysterious Triangle ", " Alien Overlord ", " Brainwashing Beam " or " Detonator Circle "A" ". 
 Use of this card will not miss the timing on " Snake Whistle ".
