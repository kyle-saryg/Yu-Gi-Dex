 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Pay  half your  Life Points .  Special Summon  as many of your  removed from play   monsters  as possible. During the  End Phase , remove from play all monsters that were Special Summoned by this  effect . 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Payez la moitié de vos Life Points. Invoquez Spécialement autant de vos monstres retirés du jeu que possible. Durant la End Phase, retirez du jeu tous les monstres ayant été Invoqués Spécialement par cet effet.
