 Structure Deck: Kaiba Volume 2  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the seventh deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Joey Volume 2 .

 2   Breakdown

 Features 
 The deck features cards used by  Kaiba  towards the end of the  Battle City  arc, including support for his  Blue-Eyes White Dragons , his infamous  Virus Cards  as well as the first monsters in the  VWXYZ  series he used in his duel against  Yugi Muto  in the Semi Finals off his  Battle City  tournament. It contains one previously-unreleased card, " Shrink ".
