 Arcana Force EX - The Dark Ruler  is a character version of the card, " Arcana Force EX - The Dark Ruler ". He appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator . In order to unlock him, you must unlock the "Arcana Force EX - The Dark Ruler" Achievement. To do that, you must successfully  summon  him in a duel.
 
 Deck 
 Arcana Force EX - The Dark Ruler uses an " Arcana Force " Deck titled, "Test of Fate".
