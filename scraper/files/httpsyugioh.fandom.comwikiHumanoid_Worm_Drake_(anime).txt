Main card page: " Humanoid Worm Drake " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Worm "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「ワーム」 .   As its English name contains "Worm", it  may  be treated a member of the "Worm" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.
