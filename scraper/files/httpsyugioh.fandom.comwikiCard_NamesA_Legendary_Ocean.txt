 Anime Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM 
 
 In the Arabic anime dub, this card was called (البحر الأسطوريّ) literally  Legendary Sea . 
 The given approximated Arabic name (مدينة أطلنتس الأسطوريّة) translates to  Legendary City of Atlantis .
