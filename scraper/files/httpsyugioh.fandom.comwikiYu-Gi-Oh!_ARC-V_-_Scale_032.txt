 "What Adam Saw!!"  is the thirty-second chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga .
 This chapter was first printed in March in the May 2018 issue of  V Jump , released on March 21, 2018.
 
 Summary 
 With the Duel over,  Yuya  leaves  Shun  and  Shingo  to tend to  Yuzu  and  Sora . He goes to face  Eve , with  Yuto  backing him up. Meanwhile,  Reiji  meets  Adam , who speaks about  G.O.D. 
 
 Featured Duel: Yuya Sakaki and Yuri vs. Sora Shiunin 
 Duel continues from the previous Scale . 
 Turn 6: Yuri 
" Starving Venemy Lethal Dose Dragon " attacks " Dangerous Frightfur Nightmary " ( Sora : 4000 → 0 LP).
