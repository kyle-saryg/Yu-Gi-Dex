 Gold Pack 2016 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Gold Pack 2016  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ), and part of the  Gold Series  series.
 This is currently the only set in the  OCG   Gold Series  series to be released in Japan only. 
 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack (1 Gold Secret Rare and 4 Gold Rares) and 10 packs per box. 
 The set includes 20 cards, 40 in the  Master Set . This is comprised of:
 
 20  Gold Rares 
 20  Gold Secret Rares
