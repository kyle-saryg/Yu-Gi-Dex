 Photon Wing 
 
 Japanese:   フォトン・ウィング 
 Romaji:   Foton Wingu 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Spell   
 
 Property 
 
 
   Equip 
 
 Equip  only to a " Photon "  monster . Once per  turn : You can  target  1  Xyz Monster  you  control ;  detach  all of its  Xyz Materials , and until the  End Phase , the equipped monster gains  ATK  equal to that target's  Rank  x 200. The equipped monster can  attack  your opponent  directly  during the turn you  activate  this  effect .
