 Chimeratech Overdragon  is a character version of the card " Chimeratech Overdragon " and upgraded form of  Cyber Dragon .
 It appears in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2009: Stardust Accelerator  where it and  Cyber End Dragon  are a team for Tag Duels. The team is unlockable by summoning a monster in a duel with at least 20,000 ATK.
 
 Deck 
 Chimeratech Overdragon uses a Deck titled, "Go Polymerize!", which focuses on Summoning " Cyber Dragon 's"  Fusion  components.
