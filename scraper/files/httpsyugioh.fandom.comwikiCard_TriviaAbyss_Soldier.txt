 This card has a  counterpart , " Abyss Warrior "; they both have the same  Attribute ,  Type ,  ATK ,  DEF , similar weapons, their effects are activated by  discarding  a  WATER  monster and both are  whales . 
 This monster is based on a  blue whale  or a  humpback whale .
