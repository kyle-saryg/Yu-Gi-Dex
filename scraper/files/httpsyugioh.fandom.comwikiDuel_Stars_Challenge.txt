        Image of a Duel Star.     
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! BAM , a Duel Star can be obtained by replaying existing Duels with additional challenging rules. Every Duel node contains 3 set challenges.
 The maximum number of Duel Stars obtainable is 1602 after the Series 2 Chapter 8.2 update on 27 February 2014, with 876 Duel Stars in  Alba Litora  and 726 in  Tropica City .
