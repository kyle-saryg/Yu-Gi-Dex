 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Ancient "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 古代の 」 .   As its English name contains "Ancient", it  may  be treated a member of the "Ancient" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.

 Translated:  Ancient Capital - Ancient City

 Property 
 
 
   Field 
 
 If you  control  1  face-up  " Ancient Gate ", " Ancient Tome " and " Ancient Giant ", you can  send  them to the  Graveyard  to  Special Summon  1 " Ancient Dragon " from your  hand ,  Deck , or Graveyard. If an "Ancient Dragon" you control is  destroyed , Special Summon it during the  End Phase .
