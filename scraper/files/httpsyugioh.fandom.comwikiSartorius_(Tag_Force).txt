 Sartorius , known as  Takuma Saiou  (斎王 琢磨,  Saiō Takuma ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Sartorius , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 In the last event of  Aster Phoenix 's story, the player and Aster duel Sartorius and  Sarina .
 Aster and Sartorius Tag Duel against the player and  Jaden Yuki  during Jaden's seventh story event.
 
 
 Tag Force 3 
 During the first story event for Normal  Blair Flannigan , Sartorius partners with  Jesse Anderson  to duel the player and Blair.
 In the second event of Excited  Bastion Misawa 's story, Bastion and the player duel Sartorius and Sarina.
