 " Journey's Companions " is the eighty-third episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on January 23, 2022.
 Rino  goes all-out against  Gavin  in a  Rush Duel  to help him regain his memories. Inspired by Rino,  Galient  and  Sebastian   Duel  against their masters,  Asana  and  Nail , who have also lost their memories like Gavin.
