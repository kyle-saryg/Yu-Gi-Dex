 A  Clock Counter  is a type of  counter  that can only be placed on " Clock Tower Prison ". Two other cards that support Clock Counters are " Eternal Dread " and " Destiny HERO - Dread Servant ". These cards are all used by  Aster Phoenix  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
 
 Appearance 
 A Clock Counter as it appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus . A Clock Counter as it appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 6 . A Clock Counter as it appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , after being placed on "Clock Tower Prison". A Clock Counter as it appears in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Generation .
