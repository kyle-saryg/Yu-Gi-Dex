 Extra Pack 2012  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -exclusive cards to the  OCG .

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive  cards from  Legendary Collection 3: Yugi's World ,  Samurai Warlords Structure Deck  and the  Booster Packs   Extreme Victory ,  Generation Force ,  Photon Shockwave , and  Order of Chaos .
