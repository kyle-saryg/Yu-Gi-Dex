 EX Starter Box 
 EX-R Starter Box 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The  EX-R  Starter Box  is the first  Series 2   Starter Deck , released on November 23,  2000 . Like all Series 2 sets, it was only released in Japan, though it formed the basis for the later  Yugi  and  Kaiba  Starter Decks in  South Korea  and the  TCG , with all 87 cards being released in these sets. It includes all 85 cards from the previous  EX Starter Box , and also includes two new cards (" Soul Exchange " and " Card Destruction "). Sealed copies of this set also contained a "Duel Master's Guide" VHS.
