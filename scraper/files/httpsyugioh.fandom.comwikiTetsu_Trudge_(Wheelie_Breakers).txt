 Tetsu Trudge , known as  Tetsu Ushio  ( 牛尾 哲 ,  Ushio Tetsu ) in the Japanese version, is a character in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Wheelie Breakers . This is a video game depiction of  Tetsu Trudge , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.

 Unlockables 
 The player can unlock " Power Bind " by defeating Trudge for the first time. 
 The player can unlock " Des Counterblow " by defeating Trudge for the second time. 
 The player can unlock " Jutte Fighter " by defeating Trudge for the third time. 
 The player can unlock " Solemn Judgment " by defeating Trudge for the fourth time. 
 The player can unlock " Discord Counter " by defeating Trudge for the fifth time. 
 The player can unlock " Graceful Dice " by defeating Trudge for the sixth time. 
 The player can unlock " Sonic Buster " by defeating Trudge for the seventh time. 
 The player can unlock " Gate Blocker " by defeating Trudge for the eighth time. 
 The player can unlock  Trudge's Duel Runner  by defeating him for the first time. 
 The player can unlock Trudge's Decklist by winning the  Silver Cup .
