 This card can be searched by " Archfiend Heiress ", " Quick Booster ", " Spell Calling " and " Mecha Phantom Beast Jaculuslan ". 
 Many Level 8 or higher monsters can easily  Special Summon themselves from the hand . If they are unable to destroy themselves or send themselves to the Graveyard, Tribute one such monster for a  Tribute Summon , or use it as  Synchro  or  Link  material. 
 In an " Archfiend " Deck, " Imprisoned Queen Archfiend ", " Archfiend Emperor, the First Lord of Horror ", " Archfiend Empress " and " Annihilator Archfiend " are Level 8 or higher members that belong to the Main Deck that can meet this card's activation requirements.
