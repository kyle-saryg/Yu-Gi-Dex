 Anime Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses 
 
 This card can be searched by " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Temple of the Kings ", " Ties of the Brethren ", " Painful Escape ", " Transmodify ", " Rescue Ferret ", " Chaos Zone ", " Cyber Angel Benten ", " Power Angel Valkyria ", " Nova Summoner ", " Sacred Arch-Airknight Parshath ", " Witch of the Black Forest ", and " Rebirth of Parshath ". 
 This card can be Special Summoned from the hand by " Court of Justice ", " Valhalla, Hall of the Fallen " and " Celestial Transformation ". 
 " Action Magic - Double Banking ",  "Infernoble Arms - Hauteclere" , " Shark Fortress " and " Twin Swords of Flashing Light - Tryce " all can allow this card to attack more than once in one Battle Phase, and thus allow you to draw more than once, as battle damage is guaranteed through its piercing ability.
