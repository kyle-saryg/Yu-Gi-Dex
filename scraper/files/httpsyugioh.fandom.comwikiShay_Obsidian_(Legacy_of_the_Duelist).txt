 Shay Obsidian , known as  Syun Kurosaki ( 黒 咲   隼 ,  Kurosaki Shun ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Shay Obsidian , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
