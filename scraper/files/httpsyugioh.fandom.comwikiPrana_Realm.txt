      The Prana Realm     
 The  Prana Realm  (プラナ 達 の 領 域   Purana-tachi no Ryōik ) [1]  is a location in  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Dark Side of Dimensions . The  Prana  reside here.
 The realm contains a plateau above the clouds. In the middle of it are seven pillars, each depicting one of the  Millennium Items .
