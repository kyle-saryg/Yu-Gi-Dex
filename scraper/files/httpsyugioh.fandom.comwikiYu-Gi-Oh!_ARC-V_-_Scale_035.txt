 "The Four Brothers' Bond!"  is the thirty-fifth chapter of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  manga .
 This chapter was first printed in June in the August 2018 issue of  V Jump , released on June 21, 2018.
 
 Summary 
 Yuya  sheds the truth behind his brothers -  Yuri ,  Yugo  and  Yuto  - and reveals the events of the World Illusion.
