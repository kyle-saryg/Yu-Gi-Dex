The  Duel Monsters World Champion  title is exclusive to the English version of  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters . 
 
 History 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! 
 This title was held by  Seto Kaiba  (In the Japanese version Seto Kaiba held the  Japanese National Duel Monsters Championship ) and the title is equivalent or synonymous to the title  King of Games .
