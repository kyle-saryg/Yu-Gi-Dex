 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You can  activate  1 of these  effects  based on the  Attributes  in your  GY ; ●  LIGHT :  Send  1 " Blue-Eyes White Dragon " or 1 card that mentions it from your  Deck  to the GY, except "Chronicle Sorceress". ●  DARK : Send 1 " Dark Magician " or 1 card that mentions it from your Deck to the GY, except "Chronicle Sorceress". You can only use the effect of "Chronicle Sorceress"  once per turn .
