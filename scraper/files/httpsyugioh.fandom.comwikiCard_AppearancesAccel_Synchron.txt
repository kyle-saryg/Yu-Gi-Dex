 In  chapter 11 ,  Ms. Guide  uses this card during her duel against  Shoma Yusa .Â She  Synchro Summons  this card by tuning " Sentry Soldier of Stone " with " Adamancipator Seeker ".  Scripted Duels 
 In  North American WCQ 2016 Special: Yusei vs. Yuya , Yusei  Synchro Summons  this card by tuning " Jet Synchron " with " Synchron Carrier " and " Tuningware ". Yusei then activates this card's effect, sending " Turbo Synchron " from his Deck to the Graveyard to reduce this card's Level by 1. Yusei then returns this card to his  Extra Deck  for the effect of " Limit Overdrive ".
