 Pendulum Domination Structure Deck  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ). It is the thirty-sixth Deck in the  TCG ' s Structure Deck series, following  Structure Deck: Seto Kaiba . This Deck is the  TCG  equivalent of  Structure Deck: Pendulum Domination  in the  OCG .
 The set was originally scheduled to be released on July 15, 2016, [1]  but was replaced by  Rise of the True Dragons Structure Deck , [2]  and delayed until 2017. [3] 
 
 Make a New Year’s resolution to crush your opposition with the  Pendulum Domination Structure Deck ! The  Pendulum Domination Structure Deck  gives you full command of Fusion, Synchro, and Xyz Monsters by employing a unique Pendulum Summoning strategy based around “D/D” monsters and “Dark Contract” cards. 
 This 43-card Deck is all about planning and executing the perfect strategy for every Duel. The “Dark Contract” cards allow you to put the pieces of the puzzle in place by moving your “D/D” monsters to your hand, field, or even your Pendulum Zones. These cards can even give your monsters a massive ATK boost, prevent interference from Trap Cards, or break the basic rules of Fusion Summoning, but be careful! Prolonged reliance on Dark Contracts has an adverse effect on your Life Points, so be sure to have a goal in mind before you sign on the dotted line!
 In addition to offering an effectively infinite source of monsters thanks to the power of Pendulum Summoning, the  Pendulum Domination Structure Deck  includes reusable “D/D” Tuner monsters for Synchro Summoning, monsters that can alter the Levels of your other monsters to help with Xyz Summoning, and Spell Cards that let you Fusion Summon every single turn or even use monsters in your Graveyard as Fusion Material!
 With all these new “D/D” monsters, Duelists who picked up the  2016 Mega-Tins  can now unleash the maximum potential of  D/D/D Flame King Genghis  and  D/D/D Gust King Alexander  to create even more exciting and powerful card combinations. Duelists can add even more “D/D” cards to their Extra Decks with cards from the  2016 Mega-Tin Mega-Packs  like  D/D/D Wave Oblivion King Caesar Ragnarok  and  D/D/D Duo-Dawn King Kali Yuga . “D/D” Pendulum Monsters can also be found in  The Dark Illusion , and more new cards that enhance this strategy will become available as the 2017 year continues!
