 Duelist Pack: Memories of the Pharaoh  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the seventeenth set in the  OCG ' s Duelist Pack series, following  Duelist Pack: Battle City .

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yugi Muto ,  Seto Kaiba ,  Yami Marik ,  Maximillion Pegasus ,  Yami Bakura ,  Ishizu Ishtar , and  Odion . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Blue-Eyes ", " LV " and " Spirit Message " archetypes, as well as the " A-to-Z " series. 
 Introduces the first support for the " Silent Magician " and " Silent Swordsman " archetypes. 
 Breakdown 
 Each pack of  Duelist Pack: Memories of the Pharaoh  contains 5 cards. Each Japanese box contains 15 packs and each Korean box contains 30 packs. The set contains 45 cards:
