 Property 
 
 
   Counter 
 
 Activate  only when a  face-up   Attack Position   monster  you  control   battles . The  ATK  of that monster becomes 0, and the  targeted  monster cannot be  destroyed by battle . After  damage calculation ,  remove  the targeted monster from play and  draw  1 card.
