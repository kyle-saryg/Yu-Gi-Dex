 Pendulum Evolution 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Pendulum Evolution  is a  Booster Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Yuya Sakaki  and  Z-ARC . 
 Includes members/support for the " Archfiend ", " Magician ", " Metalfoes ", " Odd-Eyes ", " Pendulum ", " Performapal ", " Qli ", " Ritual Beast ", " tellarknight ", " Yang Zing ", and " Zefra " archetypes, and the " Rescue Squad ", " Counter Fairy ", and " Four Dimension Dragons " series. 
 Breakdown 
 There are 5 cards per pack (2 Ultra Rares and 3 Super Rares) and 24 packs per box. The set contains 60 cards:
 
 10  Ultra Rares 
 50  Super Rares
