 Dartz,  is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Dartz , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  Second Series Anime.
