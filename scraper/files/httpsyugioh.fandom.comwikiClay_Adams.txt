 Clay Adams  (also known as  Christopher C. Adams ) is a voice actor for  4Kids Entertainment  whose provided voices for the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime series.
 
 Roles 
 Adams voiced  Reginald Van Howell III ,  Bob Banter  and  Jesse Anderson  in  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , he provided the first voice for  Crow Hogan , eventually [ citation needed ]  being replaced by  Tom Wayland  in  episode 94 . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , Adams reprises his role as  Crow Hogan .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links , Adams reprises his roles as  Jesse Anderson  and  Crow Hogan .
