 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 72 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  uses this card during his  Turbo Duel  against  Yugo . He adds this card from his Deck to his hand via the effect of " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", then activates it, gaining 1000 ATK (500 for each " Abyss Actor " he controls). 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! 
 In  chapter 9 ,  Shingo Sawatari  uses this card during his third duel against  Yuya Sakaki . He Sets this card directly from his Deck via the effect of " Abyss Actor - Superstar ", then activates it, gaining 500 ATK. On Shingo's next turn, he Sets another copy of this card from his Deck using "Superstar's" effect, then activates it when he controls three "Abyss Actors", and gains 1500 ATK.
