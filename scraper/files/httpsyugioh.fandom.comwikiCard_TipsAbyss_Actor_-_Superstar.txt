 This card can be  searched  by " Temple of the Kings ", " Dogu ", " Single Purchase ", " Transmodify ", " Painful Escape ", " Pendulum Rising ", " Odd-Eyes Rebellion Dragon ", " Odd-Eyes Raging Dragon ", " Luster Pendulum, the Dracoslayer ", " Magical Abductor ", " Majester Paladin, the Ascending Dracoslayer ", " Performapal Handsamuraiger ", " Chaos Zone ", " Dwarf Star Dragon Planeter ", " Magical Contract Door ", " Lucent, Netherlord of Dark World ", " Abyss Actor - Extras ", " Abyss Actor - Wild Hope ", " Abyss Script - Rise of the Abyss King ", and " Abyss Script - Abysstainment ". 
 This card's first effect specifically prevents your opponent from activating Spells/Traps (or their effects)  in response to its summon  - they can be activated any time after the summon, or to negate said summon. 
 This card's other effect is best used to search any " Abyss Script "  Spell Card  other than " Abyss Script - Romantic Terror " because of its  Quick-Play Spell Card  designation preventing its activation on the turn it is Set.
