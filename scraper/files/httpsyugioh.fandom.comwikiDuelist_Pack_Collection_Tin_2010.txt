 The brand-new Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game 2010 Duelist Pack Collectible Mini-Tin features cards used by  Yusei  on the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  animated series.

 Features 
 Each Duelist Pack Collection Tin 2010 includes 1  Secret Rare  copy of " Starlight Road " as well as 3 packs of the latest Yusei set:  Duelist Pack: Yusei 2 , plus 1 pack of  Ancient Prophecy . Also includes 1 Yusei all-foil 3-card promo pack. There are 3 different Yusei all-foil 3-card promo packs, each with 3 cards used by Yusei on the Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D’s animated series (1  Ultra Rare  and 2  Super Rares  per pack); these cards have been released in previous sets, but are now being offered as foil cards for the first time.
 
 Breakdown 
 Each Tin contains:
 
 1 5D's Beginner's Guide 
 3  Duelist Pack: Yusei 2 
 1  Ancient Prophecy 
 1  Secret Rare  " Starlight Road " 
 1 Special Yusei 3-Card Promo Pack 
 In Red Tin, the Promo Pack contains:
 
 1  Ultra Rare  " Junk Synchron " 
 2  Super Rare  cards: " Quillbolt Hedgehog " and " Synchro Blast Wave " 
 In Yellow Tin, the Promo Pack contains:
 
 1  Ultra Rare  " Drill Synchron " 
 2  Super Rare  cards: " Speed Warrior " and " Advance Draw " 
 In Purple Tin, the Promo Pack contains:
 
 1  Ultra Rare  " Scrap-Iron Scarecrow " 
 2  Super Rare  cards: " Level Eater " and " One for One " 
 In a set, there are:
 
 1  Secret Rare 
 3  Ultra Rare 
 6  Super Rare
