 Toru  is a character appearing in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2011: Over the Nexus .  He, along with the game's protagonist, dreams of one day going to  New Domino City  to participate in the  World Racing Grand Prix . In chapter four the player forms a team with him and  Misaki .
