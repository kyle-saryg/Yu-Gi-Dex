 Duelist Pack: Legend Duelist  is a  Duelist Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . [1]

 Features 
 Includes cards used by  Joey Wheeler ,  Mai Valentine ,  Mako Tsunami ,  The Big Five ,  Syrus Truesdale ,  Zane Truesdale ,  Bastion Misawa ,  Atticus Rhodes ,  Nightshroud ,  Broder ,  Yuya Sakaki  and  Trout . 
 Includes members and/or support for the " Amazoness ", " Cyber ", " Darkness ", " Gearfried ", " Kuriboh ", " Red-Eyes ", " roid " and " Vehicroid " archetypes, as well as the  Chemical ,  Signature move ,  with Chain  and " Umi " series.
 Introduces the first support for the " Bonding " and " Cyberdark " archetypes to the  OCG .
