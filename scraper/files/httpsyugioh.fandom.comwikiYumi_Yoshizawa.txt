 Appearances 
 Tag Force 1-3 
 In Tag Force 1-3, she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force , upon acquiring her  Deck Recipe , she also gifts the player with a " Gravekeeper's Guard " card.
 
 Tag Force 5 
 In  Kalin Kessler 's second story event, Yumi and  Kaia  Tag Duel against the player and Kalin.
