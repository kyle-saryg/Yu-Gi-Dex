 Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 
 
 
 Masao  is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  manga , and a childhood friend of  Syrus Truesdale . He appears in  Zane's  flashback to his and Syrus' childhood, while he watched Syrus Duel  Chazz  in the first round of the  Duel Academy  Tournament Finals. Masao Summons " Angel 07 ", which Syrus destroys with " Trap Hole ". Feeling bad for his friend, Syrus allows Masao to win the Duel despite having a card that could defeat him in his hand.
 
 Deck 
 Masao's only shown card is " Angel 07 ".
