 Yubel  (ユベル,  Yuberu ) is a character in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Tag Force  video game series. This is a video game depiction of  Yubel , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.

 Appearances 
 In Season 4  Jaden 's second story event, Yubel and  Adrian  Tag Duel against the player and Jaden.
 
 
 Decks 
 Yubel uses Decks that revolve around the use of  its own card , as well as facilitating its destruction so as to summon  its more   powerful forms .
