 " Half-Deck: DARK ", is a Half-Deck in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is part of the "Half-Deck Free Gift" campaign, where one can either get a " Half-Deck: LIGHT ", "Half-Deck: DARK" or both for free.
