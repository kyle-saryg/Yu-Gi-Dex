 Oda  is a character appearing in the World Championship video games. His name is a reference to  Nobunaga Oda , who was a Japanese feudal lord, until his death in 1582. Oda's Deck is a reference to samurai as well, especially since one of his cards "Great Shougun Shien" is modeled  after is Nobunaga Oda.
