 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Place 1 " Vaylantz "  Pendulum Monster  from your  Deck  in your  Pendulum Zone . If there is a card in the  Field Zone : You can  banish  this card from your  GY , then  target  1 "Vaylantz"  monster  in your  Main Monster Zone ; move it to an adjacent (horizontally)  Monster Zone . You can only use each  effect  of "Vaylantz Wakening - Solo Activation"  once per turn .
