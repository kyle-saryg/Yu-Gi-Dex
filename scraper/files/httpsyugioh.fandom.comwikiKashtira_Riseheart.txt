 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
If you  control  a " Kashtira "  monster : You can  Special Summon  this card from your  hand , also you cannot Special Summon monsters from the  Extra Deck  for the rest of this  turn , except  Xyz Monsters . If this card was  Normal  or Special Summoned this turn: You can  banish  1 "Kashtira" card from your  Deck , except "Kashtira Riseheart"; banish the top 3 cards of your opponent's Deck,  face-down , and if you do, this card's  Level  becomes 7. You can only use each  effect  of "Kashtira Riseheart"  once per turn .
