 Bond’s Reward 
 
 Japanese:   絆の結晶 
 Kana:   きずなのけっしょう 
 Romaji:   Kizuna no Kesshō 
 Translated:  Crystal of Bonds 
 
 
 
 
 en 
 ja 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Trap 
 
 
 
 If there is a  Synchro Monster  on your  field ,  exclude  Synchro Monsters from your  graveyard  to raise its  ATK  by their  Level  x 200. When the  turn   ends , you'll take  damage  equal to the amount of the ATK increase.
