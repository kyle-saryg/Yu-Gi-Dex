 Structure Deck: Warrior's Strike  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the seventeenth deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-fifth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Lord of the Magician . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Warriors' Strike Structure Deck .

 Features 
 The deck features  Warrior - Type  monsters. It is designed to bring Warrior Decks up to date and includes many reprinted cards. The Deck puts forward a new Warrior Deck strategy utilizing the  Dual System  ( Gemini monsters ).
