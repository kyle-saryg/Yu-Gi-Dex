 Wisteria , known as  Yukino Fujiwara  in the Japanese version, is a character appearing in the  Tag Force  games . She is named after  wisteria , a type of climbing plant.

 Appearances 
 Tag Force 2-3 
       Wisteria, in  Tag Force 2 - 3     .
 In Tag Force 2-3, she is an  Obelisk Blue  student at  Duel Academy , and most notable for being highly condescending and arrogant.
 
 Tag Force 4-6 
 In Tag Force 4-6, she is a student at  Duel Academy  in  New Domino City . Somewhat less arrogant than before, she is still largely vain and happily calls herself a drama queen.
 
 Tag Force 4 
       Wisteria, in  Tag Force 4     
 In her own story in Tag Force 4, she competes with the  Player  against  Carly Carmine  and  Luna  in the Preliminaries,  Misty Tredwell  and  Leo  in the quarter finals,  Crow Hogan  and  Akiza Izinski  in the semi-finals and  Jack Atlas  and  Yusei Fudo  in the finals. For completing the story, the Player will receive a photo of himself,  Cali ,  Naomi , Wisteria,  Kami  and  Bright  called "Tops Student".

 Deck 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 2  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX Tag Force 3 , Wisteria's Decks focus on using  " Reasoning "  and  " Monster Gate "  to Special Summon a variety of powerful high-Level monsters straight from her Deck.
 From  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's Tag Force 4  onwards, Wisteria's Decks shift their focus towards  Ritual Monsters . Many of her Decks  Ritual Summon  via " Advanced Ritual Art " and then use the Ritual fodder to Special Summon powerful monsters as a follow-up, making her capable of dealing massive amounts of damage quickly after clearing the field with " Demise, King of Armageddon ". She has several Decks dedicated to the  Demise/Doom OTK  as well.
