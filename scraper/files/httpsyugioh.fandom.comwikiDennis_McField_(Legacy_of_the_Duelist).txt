 Dennis McField , known as  Dennis Mackfield  (デニス・マックフィールド,  Denisu Makkufīrudo ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Dennis McField , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime.
