 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
1  Level  4 or lower " Prank-Kids "  monsters You can only  Link Summon  "Prank-Kids Meow-Meow-Mu"  once per turn . If a "Prank-Kids" monster you  control  would  Tribute  itself to activate its effect during your opponent’s turn, you can  banish  this card you control or from your  GY  instead. You can only use this effect of "Prank-Kids Meow-Meow-Mu" once per turn.

   German
