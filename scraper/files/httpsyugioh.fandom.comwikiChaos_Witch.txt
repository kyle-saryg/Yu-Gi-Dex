 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
You cannot  Special Summon   monsters  from the  Extra Deck  the  turn  you  activate  either of this card's  effects , except  LIGHT  and  DARK   Synchro Monsters . You can only use 1 of the following effects of "Chaos Witch" per turn, and only  once that turn . You can  Tribute  this card; Special Summon 2 " Black Beast Tokens " ( Fiend /DARK/ Level  2/ ATK  1000/ DEF  500). If this card is  banished  from the  hand  or  GY : You can Special Summon 2 " White Beast Tokens " ( Fairy / Tuner /LIGHT/Level 2/ATK 500/DEF 1000).
