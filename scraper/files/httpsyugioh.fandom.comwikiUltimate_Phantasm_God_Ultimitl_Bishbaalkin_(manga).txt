 Once per turn , during either player's  turn , you can  Special Summon  as many  Uchatsuimiime Tokens  in  Defense Position  as possible to each player's  field . These Tokens cannot be  Tributed . This card gains 1000  ATK  for each  monster  on the field. If this card would be  destroyed , you can  negate  its destruction, then destroy all monsters your opponent  controls  and inflict 200  damage  to your opponent for each monster destroyed by this effect.
