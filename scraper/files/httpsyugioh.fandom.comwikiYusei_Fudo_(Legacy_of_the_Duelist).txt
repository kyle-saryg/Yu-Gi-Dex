 Yusei Fudo 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 English name 
 Yusei Fudo 
 
 
 Japanese name  
 不動 遊星 不動遊星 ふどうゆうせい Rōmaji: ( Fudō Yūsei ) 
 
 
 
 
 Gender 
 Male 
 
 
 
 
 Video game debut 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist 
 
 
 
 Yusei Fudo , known as ( 不動 遊星 ,  Fudō Yūsei ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Legacy of the Duelist: Link Evolution . This is a video game depiction of  Yusei Fudo , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime.
 
 Decks 
 Yusei's Decks are based on his Duels throughout the anime.
 
 "Junk Warrior" -  Yusei and Jack's reunion Duel  (Pre-Fortune Cup)

 "Stardust Junk" -  Yusei Fudo and Jack Atlas's Fortune Cup Duel 
 "Stardust Majestic" -  Yusei Fudo and Kalin Kessler's Second Duel 
 "Stardust Road" -  Yusei Fudo and Roman Goodwin's Duel 
 "Archer Stardust" -  Yusei Fudo and Rex Goodwin's Duel 
 "Stardust Starlight" -  Yusei Fudo and Sherry LeBlanc's Duel 
 "Stardust Breaker" -  Yusei Fudo and Andre's Duel 
 "Stardust Destroyer" -  Yusei Fudo and Breo's Duel 
 "Stardust Dragon Knight" -  Yusei Fudo and Jean's Duel 
 "Stardust Shooting Star" -  Yusei Fudo and Halldor's Duel
