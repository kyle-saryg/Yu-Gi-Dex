 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
 Printed Effect Cannot be  Normal Summoned / Set . The  ATK  of all other " Z-ONE " and " Timelord "  monsters  you  control  become 4000. The ATK of this card is equal to the combined ATK of all "Z-ONE" and "Timelord" monsters you control. -I  Summon  " Sephylon, the Ultimate Timelord "!- Hidden Effect If this card would be  destroyed , you can destroy 1 other "Z-ONE" or "Timelord" monster you control instead.
