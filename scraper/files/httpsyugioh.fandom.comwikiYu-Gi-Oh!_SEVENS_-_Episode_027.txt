 " Luke Club " known in Japan as " The Luke Club Is Born! " is the twenty-seventh episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It first aired in Japan on December 5, 2020.
 Luke  creates the "Luke Club" to turn the  7th Elementary  into the holy land of  Rush Duels . However,  Yuga  and co. deserted him, and no one else is interested in joining. Tears streak down his face as he Duels himself... when a mysterious Duelist appears before him.
