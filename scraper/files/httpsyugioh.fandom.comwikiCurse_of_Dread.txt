 Curse of Dread  is an original pack in the video game  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links . It was first released in-game on August 12, 2019.
 
 Description  
 Vendreads  stalk the night, preying on the souls of the wicked. Evil is crushed by their immortal might! 
 
 Featuring: 
" Revendread Slayer ", An ace monster of " Vendread " Deck which can be Ritual Summoned from your Graveyard! When this monster is sent to the Graveyard after being Ritual Summoned, you can add 1 Ritual Spell Card from your Deck to your hand and send 1 "Vendread" monster to the Graveyard! Add " Revendread Origin " to your hand with this effect and follow it up with another Ritual Summon using "Vendread" monsters in your Graveyard! 
" Plaguespreader Zombie ", If this Tuner Monster is in your Graveyard, you can Special Summon it by returning 1 card from your hand to the top of your Deck! Use this monster as Synchro Material and send it to the Graveyard, then Special Summon it with this effect to bring it back. You can then use this monster and the Synchro Monster on the field for another Synchro Summon! 
" Glow-Up Bloom ", TWhen this Tuner Monster is sent to your Graveyard, you can banish this card to add 1 Level 5 or higher  Zombie -Type monster from your Deck to your hand. You can even Special Summon it without adding it to your hand by using " Zombie World ," a  Bonz  Level Up Reward! 
" Red-Eyes Zombie Necro Dragon ", The ATK of this Synchro Monster increases based on the number of Zombie-Type monsters on the field and in the Graveyard! When another Zombie-Type monster is destroyed by battle, you can Special Summon 1 Zombie-Type monster from the Graveyard! 
A total of 50 types of Cards: 3  UR , 10  SR , 17  R , and 20  N .
 
 
 
 Each box of  Curse of Dread  contains 100 packs. Each pack contains 3 different cards, which will always consist of 2 Normal (aka Common) cards and 1 Rare, Super Rare, or Ultra Rare card. There is a guaranteed number of copies of each individual card per box, depending on its rarity:
 
 Ultra Rare: 1 copy 
 Super Rare: 1 copy 
 Rare: 6 copies 
 Normal (aka Common): 9 copies
 The exceptions to this are the following Normal cards, with there being 10 copies of each instead: " The 13th Grave ", " Mech Mole Zombie ", " Fabled Topi ", " Bone Crusher ", and " Attack Pheromones ". 
 Reprinted cards from previous boxes include: " The Fabled Ganashia ", " Fabled Topi ", " Bone Crusher ", and " Attack Pheromones ".
