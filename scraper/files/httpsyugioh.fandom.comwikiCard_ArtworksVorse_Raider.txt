 "Vorse Raider" has two different artworks featuring it in different positions and with different backgrounds. As with all cards released in  Dungeon Dice Monsters  "Vorse Raider" is drawn from a different background and from a different angle, while in the English  Dungeon Dice Monsters  it was drawn further away. When "Vorse Raider" was seen in  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  anime it was featured facing a different direction and with a different coloration. Also in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Monsters 4: Battle of Great Duelist , "Vorse Raider" was drawn with a different artwork and different color.
