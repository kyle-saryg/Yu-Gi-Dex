 Extra Pack: Knights of Order  is an  Extra Pack  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . It introduces previously  TCG -exclusive cards to the  OCG , and introduces previously  OCG  region-exclusive  Booster Pack  cards to the other region.

 Features 
 Features previously  TCG -exclusive  cards from  Legendary Collection 4: Joey's World  and the Booster Packs  Judgment of the Light ,  Shadow Specters ,  Legacy of the Valiant , and  Primal Origin ; as well as reprints of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! World Championship 2013 prize cards  as  Extra Secret Rare  cards.
 The Japanese version also includes the Korean  OCG -exclusive  cards from  Legacy of the Valiant  and  Primal Origin . 
 The Korean version also includes the Japanese  OCG -exclusive cards from  Legacy of the Valiant  and  Primal Origin .
