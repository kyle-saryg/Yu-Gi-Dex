 These are the  Worldwide Forbidden and Limited Lists  for the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  World Championship 2014 , effective August 9, 2014. [1] [2] [3] 
 The list is constructed by giving each card the maximum restriction it has between the  July 2014  OCG  and the  July 2014  TCG  Forbidden/Limited Lists. Cards that were not available in either the Japanese  OCG  before July 1, 2014 or the North American  TCG  at the time  are  Forbidden ; the cards from  V Jump Edition Pendulum Victory Pack , for which subscriptions were completed before July 1, 2014, are Forbidden. [3]  Cards that had only been released in the  TCG  as  Shonen Jump  or  manga promotional cards  or as tournament prize cards are also Forbidden. Finally, " Emergency Provisions ", " Mystik Wok ", " Rainbow Life ", and " Tri-and-Guess " are also Forbidden, as they encourage strategies that force a win right before the round ends. [1] [2]
