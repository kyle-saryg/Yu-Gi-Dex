In several  Yu-Gi-Oh!  series,  Duel Academy  is the name of a school that trains young Duelists.
 Duel Academy may refer to:
 
 The original  Duel Academy  from  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX 
 The  Duel Academy  from  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's 
 The  Duel Academy  (aka the "Academia") from  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V
