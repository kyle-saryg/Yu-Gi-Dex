 Contrast HERO Chaos  is a character version of the card " Contrast HERO Chaos ". 
 He appears as a Super Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 5 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P142.
 He was only obtainable by achieving any Rank from 1 to 100 on the Arena Weekly Win Ratings (Single) for the time period of February 9, 2015, 00:00 (UTC) through February 15, 2015, 23:59 (UTC). Applicable players received him in their Gift Boxes after maintenance on February 18, 2015.

 Description 
 New  hero  bestowed with power of  Light  and  Darkness . This hero has reported to  Jaden's  service in order to fight the battle beyond destiny.
 
 Deck 
 This  deck  features various heroes. Utilize  Fusion Summoning  when  dueling  with this deck. Heroes shall never lose hope even in the tough corners!

 Trivia 
 Despite not being related to the " HERO " archetype, " Thunder King Rai-Oh " is most likely included in this partner's Deck as a reference to how  Jaden Yuki  used this card in his Deck alongside his other "HERO" monsters in the  manga .
