 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2  Level  4  monsters This card with  material  is  unaffected  by  Trap   effects  and by  activated  effects from any other monster with the same  Type  as any of those materials. You can only use each of the following effects of "Traptrix Pinguicula"  once per turn . You can  detach  1 material from this card;  add  1 " Traptrix " monster from your  Deck  to your  hand . If a monster(s) that is owned by your opponent is  sent  to the  GY , or  banished , by card effect: You can  attach  1 of them to this card as material.
