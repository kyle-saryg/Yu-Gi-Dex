 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
2 " Ninja "  monsters  with different  Types Must first be either  Fusion Summoned , or  Special Summoned  by  Tributing  the above cards you  control . If this card is Special Summoned or  flipped   face-up : You can  banish  1 other "Ninja" or " Ninjitsu Art " card from your  hand ,  GY , or face-up  field , then  target  1 card on the field; banish it. You can only use this  effect  of "Yaguramaru the Armored Ninja"  once per turn .
