 Syrus Truesdale , known as  Sho Marufuji  (丸藤翔,  Marufuji Shō ) in the Japanese version, is an opponent in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium Duels . This is a video game depiction of  Syrus Truesdale , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! GX  anime.
