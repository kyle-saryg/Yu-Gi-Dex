 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 In  episode 72 ,  Sylvio Sawatari  uses this card during his  Turbo Duel  against  Yugo . He activates this card to prevent his " Abyss Actor - Leading Lady " from being destroyed by " Clear Wing Synchro Dragon 's" attack. "Leading Lady's" effect then activates, with this card also protecting "Leading Lady" from destruction by card effects during the Battle Phase, allowing the effect to weaken "Clear Wing".
