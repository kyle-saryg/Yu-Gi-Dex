 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
Roll a six-sided die. All  monsters  you currently  control  gain  ATK / DEF  equal to the result x 100, until the end of this  turn . 
 
 
 
 
 
   French 
 
 
 Lancez un dé à six faces. Jusqu'à la fin de ce tour, tous les monstres que vous contrôlez actuellement gagnent une ATK/DEF égales au résultat x 100. 
 
 
 
 
 
   German 
 
 
 Wirf einen sechsseitigen Würfel. Alle Monster, die du derzeit kontrollierst, erhalten bis zum Ende dieses Spielzugs ATK/DEF in Höhe des Ergebnisses x 100. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Italian 
 
 
 Tira un dado a sei facce. Tutti i mostri che controlli attualmente guadagnano ATK/DEF pari al risultato x 100, fino alla fine di questo turno. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Portuguese 
 
 
 Lance um dado de seis faces. Até o final deste turno, todos os  monstros  que você  controla  atualmente ganham  ATK / DEF  igual ao resultado x 100. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Spanish 
 
 
 Lanza un dado de seis caras. Hasta el final de este turno, todos los monstruos que controles en este momento ganan ATK/DEF igual al resultado x 100. 
 
 
 
 
 
   Japanese 
 
 
 ①： サイコロ を１ 回 振 る。 自 分 フィールド の モンスター の 攻 撃 力 ・ 守 備 力 は、 ターン 終 了 時 まで 出 た 目 の 数 ×１００アップする。 
 
 
 
 
 
   Korean 
 
 
 주사위를 1회 던진다. 자신이 컨트롤하고 있는 모든 앞면 표시 몬스터의 공격력 / 수비력은, 엔드 페이즈까지 '나온 눈 x 100포인트' 올린다. 
 
 
 
 
 
   The Eternal Duelist Soul 
 
 
Roll a die. The result is multiplied by 100 points and added to the ATK and DEF of all monsters you control until the end of your turn. 
 
 
 
 
 
   GX Duel Academy 
 
 
Roll 1 six-sided die. The result is multiplied by 100 points and added to the ATK and DEF of all monsters you control until the end of the turn. Monsters summoned after this card's activation are excluded.
