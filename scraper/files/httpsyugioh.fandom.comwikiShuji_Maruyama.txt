 Shuji Maruyama  is a Japanese animation director. The video game character,  Shuji Maruyama  is named after him.
 
Maruyama is credited as animation director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's  episodes. #028 " A Web of Deceit, Part 2 " #154 " Shining Forth to the Future " 
Maruyama is credited as storyboard artist in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  episodes. #102 " The Dark Mist Rises: Part 2 " 
Maruyama is credited as animation director in the following  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL  episodes. #025 " Frozen in Time " #076 " Hard Knox " #085 " Counter Offensive: Part 1 " #093 " An Imperfect Couple: Part 2 "
