 This card can be searched by " A Cat of Ill Omen ", " The Despair Uranus " and " Pikari @Ignister ". 
 This card's first effect is cumulative. If you control all three copies of this card face-up, monsters your opponent controls will lose 900 ATK plus another 300 for each other card you control. 
 This card's first effect helps to increase the range of opposing monsters affected by " Water Leviathan @Ignister ".
