 D.D. Warrior Lady  is a character version of the card, " D.D. Warrior Lady ". She appears in several  Yu-Gi-Oh! video games .
 
 Appearances 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , this character and  Breaker the Magical Warrior  can be unlocked as Tag Duel opponents by defeating the Tag Team  Water & Fire  three times. Their Tag Team name is  Child of Chaos .
 
 Deck 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's World Championship 2010: Reverse of Arcadia , D.D. Warrior Lady uses a  LIGHT  Deck, titled "Legion of the Light". It is intended for Tag Duels, where it combines with Breaker the Magical Warrior's "Legion of Darkness" to create a  Chaos  Deck. Although cheat software can allow players to face D.D. Warrior Lady as a single Duel opponent, her Deck is not intended for this purpose and contains two " Chaos Sorcerer " with no other  DARK  monsters to use to Summon them.
