 In  episode 84 ,  Sartorius  uses this card during his duel against  Prince Ojin . He activates this card via the effect of " Intervention of Fate ". Sartorius then targets " Satellite Cannon " for this card's effect as this card begins to rotate. This card then lands upright, so this card inflicts damage to Ojin equal to " Satellite Cannon 's" ATK, which wins Sartorius the duel. 
 In  episode 171 , during  Jaden Yuki 's duel against Sartorius, Sartorius was forced to send this card from the top of his Deck to the Graveyard via the effect of " Sowing of the Fool ".
