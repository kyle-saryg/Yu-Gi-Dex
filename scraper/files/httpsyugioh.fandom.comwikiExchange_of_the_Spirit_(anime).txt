Main card page: " Exchange of the Spirit " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Spirit "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 精霊 」 .   As its English name contains "Spirit", it  may  be treated a member of the "Spirit" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 Exchange of the Spirit 
 
 Japanese:   現世と冥界の逆転 
 Romaji:   Gense to Meikai no Gyakuten 
 Translated:  Reversal of the Present World and Underworld 
 
 
 
<nomobile></nomobile> 
 
 
 
 Card type
 
 
 Trap   
 
 Property 
 
 
 Normal 
 
 Activate  only while there are 15 or more cards in your  Graveyard .  Pay  1000  Life Points . Both players swap the card(s) in their Graveyard with the card(s) in their  Deck . Then, both players  shuffle  their Decks.
 
 Appearances
 
 
 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! :  093 ,  094 
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  GX :  091
