 Michael Liscio Jr.  is a voice actor for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.

 Roles 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL 
 Liscio's first major role was as  Mach , later voicing  Chironex . [1] 
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V 
 Liscio's most notable roles were portraying  Yuya Sakaki  and his counterparts  Yuto ,  Yugo , and  Yuri , as well as their original self  Z-ARC , and  Yusho Sakaki .
 To differentiate Yuya from his dimensional counterparts, he voices Yuto very gruffly, Yugo with a deep voice and Yuri with a high pitch. For their original self, Z-ARC, he uses a high pitch snarling voice to portray Z-ARC's viciousness.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 Lisco provides the voice of  Ai  in VRAINS. 
 
 Scripted Duels 
 Liscio reprised his role of Yuya Sakaki at the  North American WCQ 2016 Special: Yusei vs. Yuya  scripted duel.
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links 
 Liscio reprises his role of Yuya Sakaki in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Links .
