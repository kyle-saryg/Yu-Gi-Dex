 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
(This card is always treated as a " Galaxy-Eyes " card.) Pay  half your  LP , then  target  2  monsters  in your  GY ;  Special Summon  both targeted monsters (but their effects are  negated ), and if you do, immediately after this effect resolves,  Xyz Summon  1 " Number " monster using only those 2 monsters, also you can only  Special Summon  from the  Extra Deck  once for the rest of this  turn . You can only  activate  1 "Last Hope Numbers" per turn.
