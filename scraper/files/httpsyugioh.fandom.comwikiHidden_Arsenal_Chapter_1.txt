 Hidden Arsenal: Chapter 1 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Hidden Arsenal: Chapter 1  is a reprint set in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game  ( TCG ).

 Features 
 Contains cards from  Hidden Arsenal ,  Hidden Arsenal 2 ,  Hidden Arsenal 3 , and  Hidden Arsenal 4: Trishula's Triumph . 
 Contains one of five possible collectible dice, each designed after a different card in the Duel Terminal storyline; the imps that appear in the artwork of several  Fabled  cards,  Trishula, Dragon of the Ice Barrier ,  Naturia Cosmobeet ,  Flamvell Baby , and  Ally of Justice Catastor 
 Contains an insert with the first official English release of part of the  Duel Terminal storyline . 
 Breakdown 
 Each  Hidden Arsenal: Chapter 1  box contains:
 
 1  Secret Rare 
 6  Duel Terminal Technology Ultra Rares 
 2  Duel Terminal Technology Commons 
 28 Standard  Commons 
 1 Hidden Arsenal Storyline Insert with Game Mat 
 1 Special Collectible Dice 
 The set contains 178 cards:
 
 7 Secret Rares variant cards 
 32 Duel Terminal Technology Ultra Rares 
 139 Standard Commons/Duel Terminal Technology Commons
