 " Clash of Secret Techniques! " is the fourty-eighth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on May 16, 2021.
 Deep in the tunnel of Maximum Mountain,  Asana  and  Tiger  are fighting against each other with their secret techniques on a snowy Duel Field. Their childhood feelings and memories intersect for one fleeting moment. They used to be very close, and even called each other "Harucchi" and "Asacchi". But one day, an incident caused them to drift apart. On that day, it was also snowing...
 
 Featured Duel: 
 The duel is conducted as a  Rush Duel .
