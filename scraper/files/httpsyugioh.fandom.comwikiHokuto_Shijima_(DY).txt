 Hokuto Shijima  is a character in the spin-off manga  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V The Strongest Duelist Yuya!! . This is an alternate version of  Dipper O'rion , a character from the  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V  anime . Hokuto is a student from the famous  Leo Duel School .
