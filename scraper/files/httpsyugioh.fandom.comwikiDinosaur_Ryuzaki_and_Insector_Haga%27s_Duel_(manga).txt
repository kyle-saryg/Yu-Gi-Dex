 Dinosaur Ryuzaki  and  Insector Haga  faced each other in the final Duel of the the  Japanese National Duel Monsters Championship  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  manga .
 Ryuzaki, the champion of West Japan was 15 at the time, while Haga, the champion of East Japan was 14. The Duel took place in a  Battle Box  and was televised.

 Events 
 The Duel 
 Ryuzaki's turn 
 Ryuzaki  Summoned  " Two-Headed King Rex " and Haga countered with " Basic Insect ".
 "Two-Headed King Rex"  attacked  "Basic Insect", but Weevil activated " Infinite Dismissal ",  negating  the attack.
 Weevil's turn 
 Weevil  equipped  "Basic Insect" with " Insect Gun Armor ", raising its ATK to 2000.
 "Basic Insect" attacked and  destroyed  "Two-Headed King Rex" (Ryuzaki: 0  Life Points ), winning the Duel for Haga.
 
 Aftermath 
 Maximillion J. Pegasus  presented Haga with the trophy for winning the tournament and invited him to take part in his upcoming  Duelist Kingdom  tournament.
