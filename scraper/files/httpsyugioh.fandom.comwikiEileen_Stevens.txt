 Eileen Stevens  is a voice actress for the English-language dub of the  Yu-Gi-Oh!  anime.
 
 Roles 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 5D's , Eileen voiced  Leo [ citation needed ]  and  Luna [ citation needed ]  after the original actress,  Cassandra Morris , moved to Los Angeles. [ citation needed ]  She also voiced  Sherry LeBlanc . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D Bonds Beyond Time , she portrayed Leo [ citation needed ]  and Luna [ citation needed ]  and also took over Cassandra Morris' role as the voice of  Yubel . [ citation needed ] 
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL , she voiced  Tori Meadows [1]  and  Mrs. Meadows .
 In  Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V , she voices  Sora Perse [ citation needed ]  and  Olga . [ citation needed ]
