 Structure Deck: Devil's Gate  is a  Structure Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It is the twenty-first deck in the  OCG ' s Structure Deck series (twenty-ninth including the Character Structure Decks), following  Structure Deck: Lost Sanctuary . The  TCG  equivalent of this deck is  Gates of the Underworld Structure Deck .

 Features 
 The deck features the  Fiend -Type cards from the " Dark World " series. It includes 5 new cards and many reprints.
