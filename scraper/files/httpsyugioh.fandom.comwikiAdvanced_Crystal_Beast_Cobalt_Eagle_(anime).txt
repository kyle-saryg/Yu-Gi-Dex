 [  Winged Beast  /  Effect  ] This card's name is also treated as " Crystal Beast Cobalt Eagle ". If there is no  face-up  " Advanced Dark " on the  field ,  destroy  this card. Once per turn, you can  return  1 face-up " Crystal Beast " card you  control  to the top of its  owner 's  Deck . If this card is destroyed while it is in a  Monster Card Zone , you can place it face-up in your  Spell & Trap Card Zone  as a  Continuous Spell Card , instead of sending it to the  Graveyard .
