 " Order Button " is the fifty-ninth episode of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! SEVENS  anime. It will first air in Japan on August 1st, 2021.
 At the  A.I. Restaurant , the student councils of  Goha 6th  and  Goha 7th  hold a meeting to talk about how to fight against the  Goha Siblings . However, strange things keep happening during the meeting. The food that was brought to  Botan Kenzaki , the vice president of Goha 6th's student council, just keeps disappearing. The meeting isn't moving forward at all. It just dances.
