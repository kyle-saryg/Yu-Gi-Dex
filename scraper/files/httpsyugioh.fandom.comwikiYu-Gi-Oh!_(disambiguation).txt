Yu-Gi-Oh!  or  Yu-Gi-Oh , or its English translation  King of Games , may refer to any of the following:
 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! : The entire franchise. 
 The series:
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (manga) : The original manga, divided into  Yu-Gi-Oh! ,  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duelist  and  Yu-Gi-Oh! Millennium World  in its English adaptation.
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (subseries) : The first division of the English manga. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (Toei anime) : An anime series by Toei Animation. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (anime) : An anime series by Studio Gallop and Nihon Ad Systems. 
 The card games:
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Bandai's Official Card Game : The card game by Bandai. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game : The card game by Konami distributed in Asian countries. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game : The international version of the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game . 
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (novel) : A novel. 
 Yu-Gi-Oh!  (soundtrack) : The German anime soundtrack. 
 " King of Games ": The final chapter of the original manga. 
 Yami Yugi  and  Pharaoh Atem : A character sometimes referred to as Yu-Gi-Oh.
