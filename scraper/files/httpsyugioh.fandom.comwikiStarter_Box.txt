 Starter Box  is the first  Starter Deck  in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Official Card Game  ( OCG ). It has only been released in Japanese.
 It was  initially released  in theaters on March 6, 1999, coinciding with the Japanese premiere of  Yu-Gi-Oh! (1999 flim) . The official, widespread release date was twelve days later, on March 18. In addition to these cards, both releases of the  Starter Box  included a Deck box with belt clip, a  Life Points  calculator, six  Star Chips  and a  playmat .
 Konami  substituted cards in the  Starter Box  between its theatrical and official releases. Those who purchased the  Starter Box  at screenings of  Yu-Gi-Oh! The Movie  received " Aqua Madoor ", " Trial of Nightmare ", " The 13th Grave ", " Dark King of the Abyss ", and " Turtle Tiger ", while in the official release version these cards were replaced by " Genin ", " Kageningen ", " Rock Ogre Grotto #2 ", " King Fog ", and " Spiked Snail ".
 This set introduced  Fusion Monsters  and  Field Spell Cards  to the  OCG .
 Like other cards printed early on in the game, the cards in  Starter Box  have no printed set numbers, and lack the holographic  Eye of Anubis  in the lower right-hand corner of each card.
 Of the fifty cards included in the retail release, all but 11 (" Ancient Tree of Enlightenment ", " Genin ", " Lunar Queen Elzaim ", " Madjinn Gunn ", " Masked Clown ", " Monsturtle ", " Mountain Warrior ", " Mystery Hand ", " Serpent Marauder ", " Spiked Snail " and  Rock Ogre Grotto #2  ) have been since been released in the  Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game ; none of those 14 have ever been re-released in the Japanese  OCG  after  Series 1 .
 Customers who pre-ordered the  Starter Box  also received a  Super Rare  " Celtic Guardian "  promotional card .
