 Gogogo Golem  is a character version of the card " Gogogo Golem ". 
 It appears as a Normal Partner available in  Yu-Gi-Oh! Duel Arena , with a default of 3 Rental Points. Its partner ID is P099. It is also available as an opponent at Stage 5-3 under Quest mode.

 A tough guy golem who gets  Summoned  on the go-go-go. Also works well if  Set  in  Face-down   Defense Position .

 Partner 
 An  Xyz   Deck  that takes  EARTH   Rock - Type   monsters  as its main cards. Use the  effect  of " Gogogo Giant " to  Special Summon  "Gogogo Golem" from the  Graveyard  and link that into Xyz Summon.  Send  " Catapult Zone " and " Rock Bombardment " to the Graveyard in readiness!
