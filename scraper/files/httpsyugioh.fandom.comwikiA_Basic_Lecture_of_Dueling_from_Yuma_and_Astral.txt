 "Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL II Special"  is a half-hour, special episode of  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL . It first aired in Japan on December 6, 2012, at 7:30 AM.
 It's a thorough dissection and analysis of the world  Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL II  that started in October. Furthermore,  Yuma Tsukumo  and  Astral  will teach the basic rules of  Dueling  to beginners. 
 
 Summary 
 Appearing before Yuma and Astral is a new enemy: the  Barians . What is the true nature of these beings shrouded in mystery? And what is the mysterious power of the strange " CXyz " monsters!? You'll completely understand "Yu-Gi-Oh! ZEXAL II" if you watch this program!
