Main card page: " Guardian Dreadscythe " 
 The Japanese (OCG) version of this card is not a member of the " Guard "  archetype , because its Japanese name does not contain  「 Ｇ 」 .   As its English name contains "Guard", it  may  be treated a member of the "Guard" archetype at a TCG event, subject to local rulings.     
 This card's written lore in the anime was not shown, was too small to read, or was partially or completely obstructed; the lore listed here is a reconstruction based on how it was used, character descriptions of the card, and/or its  OCG / TCG  lore. It may not be accurate or complete.

 [  Fiend  /  Effect  ] This card cannot be  Normal Summoned  or  Set . This card cannot be  Special Summoned  except by its own  effect . When a " Guardian Eatos " you  control  is  destroyed by battle  and  sent  to your  Graveyard , you can Special Summon this card from your  Deck  in  face-up   Defense Position . When this card is Special Summoned by this effect, you can  equip  this card with 1 " Reaper Scythe - Dreadscythe " from your Deck. As long as this card remains face-up on the  field , you cannot Normal or Special Summon  monsters . While this card is in face-up  Attack Position ,  destroy  all monsters you control other than this card. If this card would be destroyed, you must  discard  1 card instead.

 Lore 
 
 
 Japanese 
 ガーディアン・デスサイス 
 このカードは通常召喚できない。「ガーディアン・エアトス」の効果でのみ特殊召喚する事ができる。このカードの特殊召喚に成功した時、デッキから「死神の大鎌-デスサイス」１枚をこのカードに装備させる事が出来る。このカードがフィールドに表側表示で存在する限り、このカードのコントローラーはモンスターを召喚出来ない。このカードが表側攻撃表示になった時、このカード以外の自分フィールドのモンスターを全て破壊する。このカードが破壊される場合、代わりに自分の手札を１枚捨てる。
