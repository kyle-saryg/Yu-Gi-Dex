 Kenichi Hara  is a Japanese animation director. The episodes that he has directed have a reputation for having high-quality animation, possibly the highest in comparison to the episodes where he was not involved. This has led to the fans naming his contributions as  Haranimation , used to denote said quality. Most of the episodes where he directed are climactic points in the series.
