 Exodia the Forbidden One 
 
 Japanese:   封印されしエクゾディア 
 Romaji:   Fūin-sareshi Ekuzodia 
 Translated:  Exodia the Sealed One 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Level 
 
 
 8  
 
 ATK / DEF 
 
 
 1000 / 1000
 
 Lore
 
 
 The 4 limb cards of this god were separated. By assembling the 5 pieces He revives, and all enemies are obliterated instantaneously. 
 Rule 
 
 
 
 ~Special Rule~ 
 When the 5 different parts of  Exodia  are all in your hand, you are declared winner at that exact moment.
