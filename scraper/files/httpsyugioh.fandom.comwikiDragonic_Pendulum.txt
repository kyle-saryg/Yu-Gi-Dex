 Card descriptions 
 
 
 
   English 
 
 
All " Dracoslayer "  monsters  on the  field  gain 300  ATK / DEF . You can only use the following  effects  of "Dragonic Pendulum"  once per turn . If a  Dragon  "Dracoslayer" monster you  control   activates  its effect: You can  target  1 card on the  field ;  destroy  it. If this card on the field is  destroyed : You can take 1 "Dracoslayer" or " Dracoverlord " monster from your  Deck , and either  add  it to your  hand  or  Special Summon  it.
