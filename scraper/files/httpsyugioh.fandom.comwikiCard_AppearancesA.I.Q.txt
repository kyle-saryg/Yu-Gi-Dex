 Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS 
 In  episode 120 ,  Ai  uses this card during his  Master Duel  against  Playmaker . He activates this card to prevent Playmaker from  Link Summoning  more than once during his turn. Playmaker overcomes this by using the effects of " Drastic Draw " and " Borreload Furious Dragon " to set up the Link Summon of the only monster left in his  Extra Deck , " Accesscode Talker ".
